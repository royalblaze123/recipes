Chicken Fajita Quesadilla Plates

Nutrition (approx)

Frank: 645 kcal • P 58g • C 45g • F 22g
Susan/Elise (each): 484 kcal • P 44g • C 34g • F 16g

Ingredients (purchase-clean)

  • 1.25 lb boneless skinless chicken breast
  • 1 bag frozen fajita-style pepper blend
  • 1 package whole-grain tortillas
  • 1 bag reduced-fat shredded Mexican cheese blend
  • 1 bag spring mix
  • 1 each avocado
  • 1/2 jar salsa
  • 1 each lime
  • 6 tsp extra-virgin olive oil
  • 2 tsp chili powder
  • 2 tsp cumin
  • 2 tsp garlic powder
  • 1 tsp smoked paprika
  • 1 tsp black pepper
  • 1 tsp kosher salt

Steps

  1. Chicken: pat dry and cut into 1/2-inch pieces. Season with the spices.
  2. Cook chicken in a skillet over medium-high 8–10 minutes until browned and 165°F.
  3. Veg: cook frozen fajita pepper blend in the same skillet with 2 tsp olive oil 6–8 minutes until browned at edges.
  4. Combine: stir peppers into the chicken and warm 1 minute.
  5. Assembly: lay tortillas on a board. Distribute the filling evenly and top with a measured layer of shredded cheese. Add the second tortilla on top for Frank’s quesadilla; for Susan/Elise, fold a single tortilla over the filling.
  6. Cook: heat a large nonstick skillet over medium. Cook quesadillas 2–3 minutes per side until tortillas are browned and cheese is melted. (Work in batches if needed.)
  7. Side salad: add spring mix to a large bowl. Drizzle with 2 tsp lime juice and 2 tsp olive oil, then toss. Add diced cucumber/tomato if listed.
  8. Avocado: halve, pit, and dice. Use about 1/2 avocado total; refrigerate remaining avocado with the pit to slow browning.
  9. Serve: slice quesadillas into wedges. Serve with salad, salsa, and avocado. Portion per Notes.

Notes

Cheesy fajita quesadillas with big salad, salsa, and avocado to keep the plate balanced. Portions (per person): Frank: Frank: 1 large quesadilla (made with 2 tortillas) + 1/3 of the salad/veg side + salsa + avocado Susan: Susan: 1 medium quesadilla (made with 1 tortilla folded) + 1/4 of the salad/veg side + salsa + avocado Elise: Elise: 1 medium quesadilla (made with 1 tortilla folded) + 1/4 of the salad/veg side + salsa + avocado
