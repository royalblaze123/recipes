One-Pan Whole Wheat Pasta with Chicken, Mushrooms, and Spinach

Prep: 18 min   Cook: 26 min   Total: 44 min

Ingredients

  • 1.6 lb chicken breast
  • 8 oz whole wheat pasta
  • 1 package baby bella mushrooms
  • 1 onion
  • 1 bag spinach
  • 0.5 jar no-salt-added marinara sauce
  • 0.5 carton low-sodium chicken broth
  • 3 oz reduced-fat mozzarella cheese
  • olive oil
  • garlic powder
  • Italian seasoning
  • black pepper
  • salt substitute

Hard rules enforced: Dinner validations (calories 600–650 Frank, sat fat ≤8g, sodium ≤700mg, fiber ≥10g, added sugar ≤6g). Quantities appear before items; fresh meat/poultry/fish use weights. Condiments/spices/seasonings have no measurements in Ingredients; all measurements are in Steps. No olives, no squash, no Brussels sprouts, no zucchini, no tofu/tempeh, no yogurt in dinners.

Step-by-step Instructions

  1. Chicken: cut into bite-size pieces. Season with Italian seasoning, garlic powder, black pepper, and salt substitute.
  2. In a deep skillet/Dutch oven, sauté chicken with 2 tsp olive oil 6–8 minutes until nearly cooked.
  3. Add sliced mushrooms and onion; cook 4 minutes.
  4. Add 8 oz whole wheat pasta + 1 cup broth (measured from carton) + 1/2 jar marinara + 1 1/2 cups water. Stir, cover, and simmer 10–12 minutes, stirring twice, until pasta is al dente.
  5. Stir in spinach until wilted. If pan looks dry, add 1–2 tbsp water.
  6. Top with 3 oz reduced-fat mozzarella and cover 2 minutes to melt.
  7. Portion: Frank largest pasta/chicken share; Susan/Elise ~75%.
