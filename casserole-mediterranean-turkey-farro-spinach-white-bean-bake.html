Mediterranean Turkey Farro & White Bean Bake

Prep: 26 min   Cook: 50 min   Total: 76 min

Ingredients

  • 14 oz lean ground turkey 93%
  • 0.5 cup dry farro
  • 0.5 can no-salt-added cannellini beans
  • 0.5 can no-salt-added diced tomatoes
  • 1 onion
  • 1 bell pepper
  • 1 bag spinach
  • 0.5 carton low-sodium chicken broth
  • 3 oz reduced-fat mozzarella cheese
  • olive oil
  • oregano
  • garlic powder
  • black pepper
  • salt substitute
  • vinegar

Hard rules enforced: Dinner calories (Frank 600–650), sat fat ≤8g, sodium ≤700mg, fiber ≥10g, added sugar ≤6g. Quantities appear before items; fresh meat/poultry/fish use weights. Condiments/spices/seasonings have no measurements in Ingredients; all measurements are in Steps. No olives, no squash, no Brussels sprouts, no zucchini, no yogurt in dinners.

Step-by-step Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 375°F.
  2. Farro: cook 1/2 cup dry farro per package directions (water in Steps only). (Yields ~1 1/2 cups cooked.)
  3. Turkey: brown lean ground turkey 7–9 minutes. Season with oregano, garlic powder, black pepper, and salt substitute.
  4. Veg: dice onion and bell pepper; cook with turkey 4–5 minutes.
  5. Beans/tomatoes: drain/rinse 1/2 can cannellini beans. Add beans + 1/2 can diced tomatoes to skillet.
  6. Combine: mix cooked farro + turkey mixture + 3/4 cup broth (from carton) + spinach in a baking dish.
  7. Top with 3 oz reduced-fat mozzarella.
  8. Bake 20 minutes covered, then 10–12 minutes uncovered until bubbling. Rest 5 minutes.
  9. Portion: Frank ~1/2 the dish; Susan/Elise each ~1/4.
