Mediterranean Chicken-Hummus Sandwiches with Cucumber-Tomato Side

Prep: 12 min   Cook: 0 min   Total: 12 min

Ingredients

  • 6 slices whole wheat bread
  • 16 oz cooked rotisserie chicken
  • 0.5 tub hummus
  • 1 cucumber
  • 1 tomato
  • 1 lemon
  • 1 bag microgreens
  • black pepper
  • salt substitute
  • oregano

RBM Sandwich rules: 3 full sandwiches total (6 slices bread). Deli meats/cheeses listed as slice counts; slicing guidance lives in Steps. Condiments/spices are name-only in Ingredients; all measurements are in Steps. Produce is listed in shoppable units (fractional OK). Microgreens preferred.

Step-by-step Instructions

  1. Chicken: pull 16 oz cooked rotisserie chicken (about 1/2 rotisserie chicken). Chop small.
  2. Season chicken: toss with 4 tsp lemon juice + 1 tsp oregano + 1 tsp black pepper + 1 tsp salt substitute.
  3. Assemble 3 sandwiches: spread hummus on both bread slices (about 2 tbsp per sandwich total). Add chicken, then cucumber slices, then tomato slices, then microgreens. Close and press.
  4. Portioning: Frank gets ~7 oz chicken and ~3 tbsp hummus. Susan and Elise each get ~4.5 oz chicken and ~2 tbsp hummus, while each still gets one full sandwich.
  5. Veg side: chop extra cucumber and tomato. Frank gets ~2 cups total veg. Susan and Elise each get ~1 1/2 cups.
