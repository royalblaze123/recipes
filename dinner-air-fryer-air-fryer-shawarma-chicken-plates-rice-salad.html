Air Fryer Shawarma Chicken Plates + Rice + Salad

Nutrition (approx)

Frank: 640 kcal • P 60g • C 70g • F 14g • Sat 3g • Na 700mg • Fiber 10g
Susan/Elise (each): 480 kcal • P 45g • C 52g • F 10g • Sat 2g • Na 525mg • Fiber 8g

Ingredients (purchase-clean)

  • 1.75 lb boneless skinless chicken breast
  • 1 cup dry brown rice
  • 1 each cucumber
  • 2 each tomatoes
  • 1 bag baby spinach
  • 1 each lemon
  • kosher salt
  • black pepper
  • garlic powder
  • onion powder
  • smoked paprika
  • Italian seasoning
  • ground cumin
  • chili powder
  • red pepper flakes
  • extra-virgin olive oil
  • low-sodium soy sauce
  • hot sauce
  • Dijon mustard
  • balsamic vinegar
  • apple cider vinegar
  • cornstarch

Steps (Cosori dual-basket)

  1. Rice: cook 1 cup dry brown rice with 2 cups water 35–40 minutes; rest 5 minutes.
  2. Preheat Cosori to 400°F. Basket A: chicken.
  3. Chicken: cut into bite-size pieces. Toss with 2 tsp olive oil, 2 tsp cumin, 2 tsp smoked paprika, 1 tsp garlic powder, 1/2 tsp kosher salt, and black pepper.
  4. Air fry chicken 10–12 minutes at 400°F with Shake Reminder; shake at reminder; cook to 165°F.
  5. Salad: chop cucumber and tomatoes; toss with spinach, 2 tbsp lemon juice, 1 tsp olive oil, black pepper, and a small pinch of kosher salt.
  6. Plate: rice + chicken + salad per Notes (add 2 tbsp salsa if you want extra sauce).

Notes

Shawarma comfort with air-fryer browning and a fresh lemon salad (no creamy sauce). Portions (per person): Frank: 7 oz chicken + 1 cup cooked rice + 2 cups salad Susan: 5 oz protein + 1 cup cooked rice + 3/4 cup veg Elise: 5 oz protein + 1 cup cooked rice + 3/4 cup veg Tips (recipe-specific): Shake chicken at the reminder for better browning. Use lemon on the salad so the whole plate tastes brighter.
