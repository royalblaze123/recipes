Air Fryer Ginger-Soy Salmon with Roasted Broccoli and Cauliflower Rice

Prep: 12 min   Cook: 22 min   Total: 34 min

Ingredients

  • 1.25 lb salmon fillets
  • 1 bag frozen broccoli florets
  • 1 bag frozen cauliflower rice
  • 1 knob fresh ginger
  • 1 bulb garlic
  • low-sodium soy sauce
  • rice vinegar
  • sesame oil
  • olive oil
  • black pepper
  • salt substitute
  • 1 bag microgreens

RBM rules: quantities appear before items; fresh proteins use weight. Condiments/spices/seasonings are listed name-only; all measurements are in Steps.

Step-by-step Instructions

  1. Prep sauce: grate ginger (about 2 tsp) and mince 2 garlic cloves. Whisk 10 tsp low-sodium soy sauce + 8 tsp rice vinegar + 4 tsp sesame oil + 1 tsp black pepper + the ginger + garlic.
  2. Pat salmon dry. Spoon half the sauce over the salmon; rest 10 minutes. Reserve remaining sauce for finishing.
  3. Heat oven to 425°F and line a sheet pan. Spread frozen broccoli out. Drizzle 8 tsp olive oil + 2 tsp salt substitute + 1 tsp black pepper. Roast 16–20 minutes, tossing once, until browned at edges.
  4. Heat cauliflower rice: in a nonstick skillet over medium, add 2 tsp olive oil. Add frozen cauliflower rice; cook 6–8 minutes, stirring, until hot and steam-dried. Season with 1 tsp salt substitute + 1 tsp black pepper.
  5. Air fry salmon (Cosori): preheat 390°F for 3 minutes. Cook salmon skin-side down 8–11 minutes until flaky and cooked to preference.
  6. Finish: warm reserved sauce 15–20 seconds. Drizzle on plates: Frank 4 tsp; Susan 3 tsp; Elise 3 tsp. Top with microgreens if using.
