Shrimp Taco Bowl with Cabbage Slaw, Black Beans, and Quinoa

Prep: 18 min   Cook: 18 min   Total: 36 min

Ingredients

  • 1.5 lb shrimp
  • 0.5 cup dry quinoa
  • 0.5 can no-salt-added black beans
  • 0.5 bag shredded cabbage
  • 1 lime
  • 1 pint cherry tomatoes
  • 1 bag romaine hearts
  • olive oil
  • cumin
  • smoked paprika
  • black pepper
  • salt substitute
  • vinegar
  • salsa

Hard rules enforced: Dinner validations (calories 600–650 Frank, sat fat ≤8g, sodium ≤700mg, fiber ≥10g, added sugar ≤6g). Quantities appear before items; fresh meat/poultry/fish use weights. Condiments/spices/seasonings have no measurements in Ingredients; all measurements are in Steps. No olives, no squash, no Brussels sprouts, no zucchini, no tofu/tempeh, no yogurt in dinners.

Step-by-step Instructions

  1. Cook quinoa: rinse 1/2 cup dry quinoa. Simmer with 1 cup water (Steps only) 15 minutes; rest 5 minutes.
  2. Beans: drain/rinse 1/2 can black beans; warm 1–2 minutes.
  3. Slaw: toss cabbage with 2 tsp lime juice + 1 tsp vinegar + 1 tsp olive oil + black pepper + salt substitute.
  4. Shrimp: season with cumin, smoked paprika, black pepper, and salt substitute. Sauté in a hot skillet with 2 tsp olive oil 3–4 minutes until pink and curled.
  5. Build bowls: romaine + quinoa + beans + tomatoes + shrimp + slaw. Top with measured salsa (1 tbsp Frank; 2 tsp Susan; 2 tsp Elise).
  6. Portion: Frank largest shrimp/quinoa share; Susan/Elise ~75%.
