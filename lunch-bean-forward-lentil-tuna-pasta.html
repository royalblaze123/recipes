Bean-Forward Lunch: Lentil-Tuna Whole Wheat Pasta Bowl

Prep: 18 min   Cook: 16 min   Total: 34 min

Ingredients

  • 2 cans tuna in water
  • 0.5 bag cooked lentils
  • 8 oz whole wheat penne
  • 1 bag spinach
  • 0.5 carton low-sodium chicken broth
  • 1 lemon
  • Italian seasoning
  • black pepper
  • salt substitute
  • tomato paste
  • olive oil

Rules enforced: quantities before items; fresh meat/poultry/fish use weights; packaged items use fractional containers when partial. Condiments/spices/seasonings have no measurements in Ingredients; all measurements are in Steps. No olives, no squash, no Brussels sprouts, no zucchini, no tofu/tempeh. Lunch validations included in Notes.

Step-by-step Instructions

  1. Pasta: cook 8 oz whole wheat penne until al dente; drain, reserving 1/2 cup water.
  2. Tuna: drain 2 cans tuna well.
  3. Sauce: simmer 1/2 cup broth + 1/2 cup pasta water + 1 tbsp tomato paste + 1 tsp Italian seasoning + pepper/salt substitute 2 minutes.
  4. Add lentils (1/2 bag cooked) and tuna; warm 2 minutes.
  5. Stir in 1 bag spinach until wilted.
  6. Toss in pasta; add 1 tsp olive oil if needed for sheen.
  7. Finish: add 2 tsp lemon juice per plate.
