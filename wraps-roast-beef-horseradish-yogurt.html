Roast Beef & Horseradish Yogurt Wrap (Frank Household – Occasional)

High-protein roast beef wrap with light horseradish-yogurt sauce and crunchy veggies for a full-flavor but still portion-controlled lunch.

Servings

3 servings (Frank, Susan, Elise).

Times

  • Prep time: 15 minutes
  • Cook time: 0 minutes (no cooking required)
  • Total time: 15 minutes

Ingredients

  • 1 large (10-inch) high-fiber whole-wheat tortilla
  • 2 medium (8-inch) high-fiber whole-wheat tortillas
  • 10 oz lean deli roast beef, sliced (about 4 oz for Frank, 3 oz each for Susan and Elise)
  • 2 1/2 slices part-skim provolone cheese (about 1 slice for Frank, 3/4 slice each for Susan and Elise)
  • 6 tbsp 2% plain Greek yogurt
  • 1 1/2 tsp prepared horseradish (adjust to taste and tolerance)
  • 1 tsp Dijon mustard
  • 1 tsp extra-virgin olive oil
  • 1/2 tsp garlic powder
  • 1/4 tsp ground black pepper (or to taste)
  • 1/8 to 1/4 tsp kosher or sea salt (keep total modest; season to taste)
  • 1 1/2 cups mixed baby greens or romaine, loosely packed
  • 1/2 cup very thinly sliced cucumber
  • 1/4 cup thinly sliced red onion (optional, for tolerance)

Preparation Steps

  1. Rinse and pat dry the baby greens and cucumber. Slice the cucumber into very thin rounds and thinly slice the onion if using.
  2. In a small bowl, whisk together the Greek yogurt, prepared horseradish, Dijon mustard, olive oil, garlic powder, black pepper, and a pinch of salt until smooth. Taste and adjust horseradish level to your heat tolerance.
  3. Gently warm each tortilla in a dry skillet over low heat for 15–20 seconds per side, or in the microwave for about 10 seconds, until flexible.
  4. Place the large (10-inch) tortilla on a cutting board. Spread about 2 tbsp horseradish yogurt sauce down the center. Layer about 4 oz lean deli roast beef over the sauce. Top with 1 slice part-skim provolone, a generous handful of mixed greens, cucumber slices, and a few onion slices if desired.
  5. Fold the sides inward, then roll up tightly from the bottom into a burrito-style wrap. Wrap in foil or parchment and slice in half if desired.
  6. Place one medium (8-inch) tortilla on the board. Spread about 2 tbsp sauce down the center. Add about 3 oz roast beef, 3/4 slice provolone (cut into pieces and distributed), a moderate handful of greens, cucumber, and onion if desired.
  7. Fold in the sides and roll firmly from the bottom. Wrap in foil or parchment and slice in half if desired.
  8. Repeat the process with the remaining medium (8-inch) tortilla, using about 2 tbsp sauce, 3 oz roast beef, 3/4 slice provolone, greens, cucumber, and optional onion.
  9. Serve immediately or refrigerate the wrapped sandwiches for up to 24 hours. Keep tightly wrapped for grab-and-go lunches. Consider this a full-flavor occasional option within your lunch rotation.
