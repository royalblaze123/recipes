Salsa-Yogurt Pink Sauce

Category: Sauce • Yield: About 1 cup (16 tbsp) • AnyList-ready

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup no-sugar-added salsa
  • 1/2 cup 2% plain Greek yogurt

Steps

  1. Stir salsa and Greek yogurt until uniform.
  2. If too sharp for dinner, stir in 1–2 tbsp additional Greek yogurt.
