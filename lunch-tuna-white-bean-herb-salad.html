Tuna White Bean Herb Salad Box

NOTES: Designed as a portable grab-and-go lunch using lean protein, high-fiber vegetables, and controlled smart carbs. Use low-sodium ingredients and avoid added sugars where possible. PORTION SIZES: Frank: largest portion (~1/3 of the total volume); Susan: medium portion (~1/4 of the total volume); Elise: medium portion (~1/4 of the total volume) (Frank = 1.0×, Susan/Elise ≈0.75× portions).

Ingredients

  • 3 (5 oz) cans tuna in water, drained
  • 1 can (15 oz) no-salt-added cannellini or great northern beans, drained and rinsed
  • 1 1/2 cups chopped celery
  • 1/2 small red onion, finely diced
  • 1 red bell pepper, diced
  • 3 cups chopped romaine or spring mix
  • 3 tbsp extra-virgin olive oil
  • 3 tbsp lemon juice
  • 1 tsp dried dill or 2 tbsp fresh dill, chopped
  • 1/2 tsp garlic powder
  • Freshly ground black pepper, to taste

Step-by-Step Instructions

  1. In a large bowl, combine drained tuna, white beans, chopped celery, diced red onion, and diced bell pepper.
  2. In a small bowl, whisk together olive oil, lemon juice, dried or fresh dill, garlic powder, and black pepper.
  3. Pour dressing over the tuna mixture and gently fold until everything is coated.
  4. Place chopped romaine or spring mix into the bottom of three grab-and-go containers.
  5. Top each container with a generous scoop of tuna white bean salad, giving Frank the largest share and Susan/Elise slightly smaller portions.
  6. Cover and refrigerate up to 3 days. Toss gently before eating.

Nutrition per Person

  • Frank: ~560 kcal, ~52 g protein, ~35 g carbs, ~19 g fat.
  • Susan: ~420 kcal, ~39 g protein, ~26 g carbs, ~14 g fat.
  • Elise: ~420 kcal, ~39 g protein, ~26 g carbs, ~14 g fat.
