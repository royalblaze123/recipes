Garlic Herb Parmesan Jalapeño Poppers

Air Fryer or Sheet Pan • Yield: 24 popper halves

Ingredients (purchase-clean)

  • 12 each fresh jalapeños
  • 8 oz 1% cottage cheese
  • 2 oz grated parmesan
  • 2 cloves garlic
  • dried Italian seasoning
  • kosher salt
  • black pepper

Steps

  1. Preheat air fryer to 370°F (or oven to 425°F).
  2. Blend filling: blend 8 oz cottage cheese with 2 cloves garlic, 2 tsp Italian seasoning, 1/2 tsp kosher salt, and 1/2 tsp black pepper until smooth; stir in 2 oz parmesan.
  3. Halve and seed 12 jalapeños; pat dry.
  4. Pipe/spoon filling into halves.
  5. Cook: air-fry 370°F for 7–9 min (or bake 425°F for 12–14 min) until browned at edges and hot.

Notes

Flavor-forward holiday poppers designed for snacking or a light meal. Portions (guidance): These are lower-fat but still rich; serve with fresh cucumber and bell pepper strips. If you want extra browning, finish 1 minute under the broiler (sheet-pan version).
