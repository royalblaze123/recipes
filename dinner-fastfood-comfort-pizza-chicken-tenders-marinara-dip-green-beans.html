Pizza Chicken Tenders + Marinara Dip + Green Beans

Ingredients (purchase-clean)

  • 1.75 lb chicken tenderloins
  • 1 carton egg whites
  • 1 package whole-wheat panko breadcrumbs
  • 2 oz grated Parmesan cheese
  • 6 oz reduced-fat shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1 jar marinara sauce
  • 1 bag frozen green beans
  • kosher salt
  • black pepper

  • Italian seasoning

Steps

  1. Air fryer: preheat to 400°F.
  2. Green beans: Basket A, air fry frozen green beans with 2 tsp olive oil, black pepper, and a pinch of kosher salt at 400°F for 10–12 minutes (shake once).
  3. Breading: Bowl 1: 1/2 cup egg whites. Bowl 2: 1 1/2 cups panko + 2 oz Parmesan + 2 tsp Italian seasoning + 1 tsp garlic powder + 1/2 tsp black pepper.
  4. Coat tenders and cook in Basket B at 400°F for 10–12 minutes with shake reminder ON; flip at prompt; cook to 165°F.
  5. Cheese finish: sprinkle 6 oz reduced-fat mozzarella over cooked tenders and air fry 400°F for 1 minute to melt.
  6. Warm 1/2 cup marinara for dipping (measured).
  7. Serve per Notes.

Notes

Pizza-night comfort as tenders with marinara dip and melted mozz. Portions (per person): Frank: 6 tenders + 1 cup green beans + 2 tbsp marinara dip side + dip Susan: 4 tenders + 3/4 cup green beans + 1 1/2 tbsp marinara dip side + dip Elise: 4 tenders + 3/4 cup green beans + 1 1/2 tbsp marinara dip side + dip Tips (recipe-specific): Melt mozzarella at the end so you use less but still get the ‘pizza’ hit. Keep marinara measured; flavor comes from Italian crumb + parm.
