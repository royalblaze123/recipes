Air Fryer Garlic Paprika Cod with Potatoes and Broccoli

Garlic-paprika cod with crispy potatoes and broccoli, timed with Sync Finish so everything hits the plate together.

Servings

3 servings.

Times

  • Prep time: 20 minutes
  • Cook time: 20 minutes
  • Total time: 40 minutes

Ingredients

Main Dish

  • 1 lb cod fillets
  • 2 each russet potatoes
  • 1 head broccoli
  • 1 each lemon
  • 1 bottle extra virgin olive oil
  • 1 bag reduced fat shredded cheese
  • 1 container nonfat Greek yogurt
  • 1 jar garlic powder
  • 1 jar paprika
  • 1 jar black pepper
  • 1 jar cornstarch
    • 

    Preparation Steps

    1. Prep (potatoes): cut potatoes into wedges. Measure out about 2 cups wedges for this recipe; refrigerate remaining potato for another meal. Toss wedges with 1 tbsp olive oil, paprika, black pepper, and 1 tsp cornstarch (helps crisp).
    2. Prep (broccoli): cut florets. Measure out 1/2 head broccoli for this recipe; refrigerate remaining. Toss with 1 tsp olive oil and black pepper.
    3. Prep (cod): pat cod dry. Lightly coat with 1 tsp olive oil, garlic powder, paprika, and black pepper. Cut into 3 portions.
    4. Cosori setup: preheat both baskets to 390°F for 3 minutes. Put potato wedges in Basket A (single layer). Put broccoli in Basket B.
    5. Sync Finish: set Basket A to 390°F for 20 minutes (shake reminder ON). Set Basket B to 390°F for 12 minutes (shake reminder ON). Press Sync Finish.
    6. Shake reminders: shake/flip potato wedges and broccoli when prompted.
    7. Add cod: when Basket B has 8 minutes left, push broccoli to the side and add cod pieces. Cook until cod flakes easily and reaches 145°F.
    8. Yogurt drizzle: stir 1/4 cup Greek yogurt with 1 tsp lemon juice and a pinch of garlic powder.
    9. Serve (40/30/30): plate cod with potato wedges and broccoli. Sprinkle 1/2 oz reduced-fat cheese total across all plates and add a small yogurt drizzle.

      Notes

      Portions (per person):


      Frank – Cod: largest piece. Potato wedges: about 1 cup. Broccoli: about 1 1/2 cups. Yogurt drizzle + light cheese sprinkle.


      Susan – Cod: medium piece. Potato wedges: about 3/4 cup. Broccoli: about 1 1/4 cups. Yogurt drizzle + light cheese sprinkle.


      Elise – Cod: medium piece. Potato wedges: about 3/4 cup. Broccoli: about 1 1/4 cups. Yogurt drizzle + light cheese sprinkle.


      Tips (recipe-specific):


      Add cod near the end so it stays flaky and doesn’t dry out.


      Cornstarch on potatoes adds crisp without extra oil.


      If cod is thin, reduce its cook window to 5–6 minutes.
