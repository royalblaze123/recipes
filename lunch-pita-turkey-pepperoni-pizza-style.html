High-Protein Pitas: Pizza-Style Turkey Pepperoni Pitas

Prep: 16 min   Cook: 8 min   Total: 24 min

Ingredients

  • 3 whole wheat pitas
  • 12 slices deli turkey
  • 18 slices turkey pepperoni
  • 6 slices part-skim mozzarella
  • 0.5 jar marinara sauce
  • 1 bag spinach
  • Italian seasoning
  • garlic powder
  • black pepper
  • salt substitute

Rules enforced: quantities before items; fresh meat/poultry/fish use weights; packaged items use fractional containers when partial. Condiments/spices/seasonings have no measurements in Ingredients; all measurements are in Steps. No olives, no squash, no Brussels sprouts, no zucchini, no tofu/tempeh. Lunch validations included in Notes.

Step-by-step Instructions

  1. Oven: preheat broiler on high.
  2. Prep pitas: split 3 pitas open and lay on a sheet pan.
  3. Sauce: spread marinara in measured amounts (Frank 2 tbsp; Susan 1.5 tbsp; Elise 1.5 tbsp) across pitas.
  4. Cheese: add mozzarella slices (Frank 2; Susan 2; Elise 2).
  5. Protein: add deli turkey (Frank 5 slices; Susan 3–4; Elise 3–4) and turkey pepperoni (Frank 8 slices; Susan 5; Elise 5).
  6. Spinach: pile spinach on top (divide 1 bag; it will wilt). Season with Italian seasoning, garlic powder, pepper, salt substitute.
  7. Broil 2–3 minutes until cheese melts; cool 2 minutes; fold and serve.
