Pork Tenderloin Tostada Plates

Nutrition (approx)

Frank: 645 kcal • P 58g • C 48g • F 22g
Susan/Elise (each): 484 kcal • P 44g • C 36g • F 16g

Ingredients (purchase-clean)

  • 1.50 lb pork tenderloin
  • 1 package baked tostada shells
  • 1 each avocado
  • 1/2 jar salsa
  • 6 tsp extra-virgin olive oil
  • 1 each lime
  • 2 tsp chili powder
  • 2 tsp cumin
  • 2 tsp garlic powder
  • 1 tsp smoked paprika
  • 1 tsp black pepper
  • 1 tsp kosher salt

Steps

  1. Tostadas: warm in a 350°F oven for 3–4 minutes to crisp and heat through.
  2. Pork: pat tenderloin dry and slice into 1/2-inch medallions. Season with the spices.
  3. Cook pork in a hot nonstick skillet with 2 tsp olive oil for 3–4 minutes per side until 145°F. Rest 3 minutes, then slice into bite-size pieces. Finish with 2 tsp lime juice.
  4. Avocado: halve, pit, and dice. Use about 1/2 avocado total; refrigerate remaining avocado with the pit to slow browning.
  5. Serve: top tostadas with pork, then salsa and avocado. Portion per Notes.

Notes

Juicy pork tenderloin tostadas with salsa and avocado; crisp shell keeps it fun and portionable. Portions (per person): Frank: 2 tostadas + 1/3 of the protein topping + 1/3 of the avocado + salsa Susan: 1 tostada + 1/4 of the protein topping + 1/4 of the avocado + salsa Elise: 1 tostada + 1/4 of the protein topping + 1/4 of the avocado + salsa
