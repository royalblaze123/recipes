Garlic Shrimp with Tomatoes and Spinach over Whole Wheat Orzo

Prep: 18 min   Cook: 22 min   Total: 40 min

Ingredients

  • 1.5 lb shrimp
  • 8 oz whole wheat orzo
  • 1 pint cherry tomatoes
  • 1 bag spinach
  • 0.5 carton low-sodium chicken broth
  • olive oil
  • garlic
  • black pepper
  • salt substitute
  • Italian seasoning
  • lemon

Hard rules enforced: Dinner validations (calories 600–650 Frank, sat fat ≤8g, sodium ≤700mg, fiber ≥10g, added sugar ≤6g). Quantities appear before items; fresh meat/poultry/fish use weights. Condiments/spices/seasonings have no measurements in Ingredients; all measurements are in Steps. No olives, no squash, no Brussels sprouts, no zucchini, no tofu/tempeh, no yogurt in dinners.

Step-by-step Instructions

  1. Orzo: simmer 8 oz whole wheat orzo in 2 cups broth + 1 cup water, stirring often, 9–10 minutes until al dente.
  2. Shrimp: pat dry 1.5 lb shrimp. Season with 1 tsp Italian seasoning + 1/4 tsp black pepper + 1/4 tsp salt substitute.
  3. Sauté shrimp in a skillet with 2 tsp olive oil 3 minutes. Add 1 tsp minced garlic and cook 30 seconds.
  4. Add 1 pint cherry tomatoes; cook 2 minutes until blistered.
  5. Stir in 1 bag spinach to wilt, about 1 minute.
  6. Serve shrimp/tomato/spinach over orzo. Finish with 2 tsp lemon juice per plate.
  7. Portion: Frank largest orzo share; Susan/Elise ~75%.
