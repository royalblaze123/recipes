Turkey Taco Bowl with Black Beans, Brown Rice, and Romaine

Prep: 18 min   Cook: 22 min   Total: 40 min

Ingredients

  • 1.5 lb lean ground turkey 93%
  • 0.5 cup dry brown rice
  • 0.5 can no-salt-added black beans
  • 1 bag romaine hearts
  • 1 pint cherry tomatoes
  • 1 onion
  • 1 lime
  • olive oil
  • cumin
  • chili powder
  • smoked paprika
  • garlic powder
  • black pepper
  • salt substitute
  • salsa

Hard rules enforced: Dinner validations (calories 600–650 Frank, sat fat ≤8g, sodium ≤700mg, fiber ≥10g, added sugar ≤6g). Quantities appear before items; fresh meat/poultry/fish use weights. Condiments/spices/seasonings have no measurements in Ingredients; all measurements are in Steps. No olives, no squash, no Brussels sprouts, no zucchini, no tofu/tempeh, no yogurt in dinners.

Step-by-step Instructions

  1. Cook brown rice: cook 1/2 cup dry brown rice per package directions (water in Steps only).
  2. Turkey: brown turkey with 2 tsp olive oil 7–9 minutes. Season with cumin, chili powder, smoked paprika, garlic powder, black pepper, and salt substitute.
  3. Beans: drain/rinse 1/2 can black beans; warm in the skillet 1–2 minutes with a splash of water.
  4. Build bowls: chopped romaine + rice + turkey + beans + tomatoes.
  5. Top each bowl with a measured salsa spoon (1 tbsp Frank; 2 tsp Susan; 2 tsp Elise) and a squeeze of lime.
  6. Portion: Frank largest rice/turkey share; Susan/Elise ~75%.
