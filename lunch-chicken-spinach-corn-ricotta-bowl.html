Chicken Spinach Corn Ricotta Bowl

NOTES: Designed as a portable grab-and-go lunch using lean protein, high-fiber vegetables, and controlled smart carbs. Use low-sodium ingredients and avoid added sugars where possible. PORTION SIZES: Frank: largest portion (~1/3 of the total volume); Susan: medium portion (~1/4 of the total volume); Elise: medium portion (~1/4 of the total volume) (Frank = 1.0×, Susan/Elise ≈0.75× portions).

Ingredients

  • 12 oz cooked boneless, skinless chicken breast, chopped
  • 1 cup frozen corn (no added salt), thawed and drained
  • 3 cups fresh baby spinach, roughly chopped
  • 1 cup halved cherry tomatoes
  • 3/4 cup part-skim ricotta cheese
  • 2 tbsp extra-virgin olive oil
  • 2 tbsp lemon juice
  • 1 tsp dried Italian seasoning
  • Freshly ground black pepper, to taste

Step-by-Step Instructions

  1. In a large bowl, combine chopped chicken, thawed corn, chopped spinach, and halved cherry tomatoes.
  2. In a small bowl, whisk together ricotta, olive oil, lemon juice, Italian seasoning, and black pepper to create a creamy dressing.
  3. Pour dressing over the bowl and toss until everything is evenly coated.
  4. Divide mixture into three containers, giving Frank the largest portion.
  5. Cover and refrigerate up to 3 days. Eat chilled or slightly warmed at home if preferred.

Nutrition per Person

  • Frank: ~560 kcal, ~48 g protein, ~36 g carbs, ~20 g fat.
  • Susan: ~420 kcal, ~36 g protein, ~27 g carbs, ~15 g fat.
  • Elise: ~420 kcal, ~36 g protein, ~27 g carbs, ~15 g fat.
