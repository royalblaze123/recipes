Fraction Test – Southwest Chicken Yogurt Salad Sandwiches + Air-Fried Broccoli

Lunch • Prep 15 min • Cook 12 min • Total 27 min

Ingredients

  • 6 slices whole wheat bread
  • 12.5 oz chicken breast
  • 1/4 tub 2% plain Greek yogurt
  • 1/3 jar salsa
  • 1/2 bag spring mix
  • 1/2 bag frozen broccoli florets
  • 1 tsp olive oil
  • 1 tsp ground cumin
  • 1 tsp chili powder
  • 1/2 tsp garlic powder
  • 1/2 tsp kosher salt
  • 1/4 tsp black pepper
  • 1 each lime

Step-by-step Instructions

  1. Air-fry broccoli (side): Preheat air fryer to 400°F. Add the frozen broccoli florets to a bowl. Drizzle with 1 tsp olive oil and toss until glossy. Sprinkle with 1/4 tsp kosher salt and 1/4 tsp black pepper. Air-fry in a single layer for 10–12 minutes, shaking at 6 minutes, until edges are lightly browned. (This uses about 1/2 of a standard 12 oz bag; reseal and freeze the rest.)
  2. Cook chicken: Pat the chicken breast dry. Season both sides with 1 tsp ground cumin, 1 tsp chili powder, 1/2 tsp garlic powder, and 1/4 tsp kosher salt. Cook in a nonstick skillet over medium heat for 5–7 minutes per side (internal temp 165°F). Rest 5 minutes, then chop finely.
  3. Make the southwest yogurt dressing: In a bowl, combine 2% plain Greek yogurt and salsa. Squeeze in juice from 1/2 the lime. Stir until smooth.
  4. Make the chicken salad filling: Add the chopped chicken to the yogurt dressing and mix until evenly coated.
  5. Build 3 sandwiches (one each): Lay out 6 slices bread. Divide spring mix into 3 equal piles and place on 3 bread slices.
  6. Portion the filling by person: Put about 2/5 of the chicken salad on Frank’s sandwich, and about 3/10 on Susan’s and 3/10 on Elise’s. Close each sandwich with the remaining bread slices.
  7. Plate and serve: Serve each person 1 sandwich. Divide the air-fried broccoli into 3 equal portions. If you have extra spring mix, add a small side salad and squeeze the remaining lime over greens if desired.
