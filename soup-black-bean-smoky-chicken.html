Smoky Black Bean & Chicken Soup (Stovetop)

NOTES: Designed as a complete meal soup using lean protein, high-fiber vegetables, and controlled starch. Use low-sodium broth and avoid extra salt when possible to support blood pressure and kidney health. PORTION SIZES: Frank: largest portion (~2 cups); Susan: medium portion (~1.5 cups); Elise: medium portion (~1.5 cups).

Ingredients

  • 1 tbsp extra-virgin olive oil
  • 1 medium yellow onion, diced
  • 1 red bell pepper, diced
  • 2 celery stalks, diced
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 cans (15 oz each) no-salt-added black beans, drained and rinsed
  • 4 cups low-sodium chicken broth
  • 1 cup water
  • 1 lb boneless, skinless chicken breast
  • 2 tsp smoked paprika
  • 1 tsp ground cumin
  • 1/2 tsp chili powder
  • 1/2 tsp dried oregano
  • 1/2 tsp kosher salt (or to taste)
  • Freshly ground black pepper, to taste
  • Juice of 1 lime
  • Chopped cilantro and sliced green onions, for garnish (optional)

Step-by-Step Instructions

  1. Heat olive oil in a large soup pot over medium heat.
  2. Add diced onion, red bell pepper, and celery. Cook 5–6 minutes until vegetables soften.
  3. Stir in minced garlic and cook 30–60 seconds until fragrant.
  4. Stir in smoked paprika, ground cumin, chili powder, dried oregano, salt, and black pepper.
  5. Add drained black beans, low-sodium chicken broth, and water. Stir well.
  6. Nestle chicken breasts into the liquid so they are fully submerged.
  7. Bring to a gentle boil, then reduce heat to low, cover partially, and simmer 20–25 minutes until chicken is cooked through and beans are very tender.
  8. Remove chicken to a cutting board and let cool slightly, then shred with two forks.
  9. Use an immersion blender to briefly blend a portion (about 10–20%) of the soup in the pot to thicken, leaving plenty of whole beans.
  10. Return shredded chicken to the pot and stir to combine.
  11. Stir in lime juice and taste, adjusting salt and pepper as needed.
  12. Simmer on low 5 more minutes, then remove from heat and let rest a few minutes.
  13. Ladle into bowls for Frank, Susan, and Elise and garnish with cilantro and green onions if desired.

Nutrition per Person

  • Frank: ~490 kcal, ~42 g protein, ~46 g carbs, ~11 g fat.
  • Susan: ~370 kcal, ~32 g protein, ~34 g carbs, ~8 g fat.
  • Elise: ~370 kcal, ~32 g protein, ~34 g carbs, ~8 g fat.
