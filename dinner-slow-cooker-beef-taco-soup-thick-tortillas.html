Beef Taco Soup (Thick) + Tortillas

Nutrition (approx)

Frank: 650 kcal • P 55g • C 60g • F 20g
Susan/Elise (each): 488 kcal • P 41g • C 45g • F 15g

Ingredients (purchase-clean)

  • 1.50 lb 90% lean ground beef
  • 1 can black beans
  • 1 jar salsa
  • 2 cans no-salt-added diced tomatoes
  • 3 each whole-wheat tortillas
  • low-sodium beef broth
  • kosher salt
  • black pepper
  • garlic powder
  • onion powder
  • smoked paprika
  • Italian seasoning
  • ground cumin
  • chili powder
  • red pepper flakes
  • extra-virgin olive oil
  • low-sodium chicken broth
  • low-sodium beef broth
  • low-sodium soy sauce
  • hot sauce
  • Dijon mustard
  • balsamic vinegar
  • apple cider vinegar
  • cornstarch

Steps (slow cooker)

  1. Brown beef with chili powder, cumin, garlic powder, pepper; modest salt (8–10 min).
  2. Add salsa (1 1/2 cups) + tomatoes (2x 14.5 oz) + broth (2 cups).
  3. LOW 4–5 hr or HIGH 2–3 hr.
  4. Add black beans (15 oz; 1 1/2 cups) for last 25 min on HIGH.
  5. Thicken: stir in strips from 2 tortillas; cook 15 min.
  6. Warm remaining tortilla; slice for serving.

Notes

Taco-soup comfort that eats like chili (tortillas thicken it). Portions (per person): Frank: About 2 1/2 cups (heaping bowl) Susan: About 2 cups (bowl) Elise: About 2 cups (bowl) Tips (recipe-specific): Beans go in late for better texture. Tortilla strips thicken without chips.
