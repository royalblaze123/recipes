Chicken Posole-Style Soup (Thick)

Nutrition (approx)

Frank: 610 kcal • P 58g • C 50g • F 14g • Sat 3g • Na 670mg • Fiber 10g
Susan/Elise (each): 458 kcal • P 44g • C 38g • F 10g • Sat 2g • Na 502mg • Fiber 8g

Ingredients (purchase-clean)

  • 1.75 lb boneless skinless chicken breast
  • 1 can hominy
  • 1 can no-salt-added diced tomatoes
  • 1 can diced green chiles
  • 1 each onion
  • 1 carton low-sodium chicken broth
  • 1 each lime
  • extra-virgin olive oil
  • kosher salt
  • black pepper
  • chili powder
  • ground cumin
  • garlic powder
  • onion powder
  • smoked paprika
  • Italian seasoning
  • crushed red pepper
  • bay leaves
  • oregano

Steps

  1. Pot: heat 2 tsp olive oil. Cook 1 diced onion 6–8 minutes until softened.
  2. Chicken: add diced chicken; cook 8–10 minutes until 165°F.
  3. Season with oregano, cumin, garlic powder, onion powder, kosher salt, black pepper.
  4. Hominy: Can size note—use a standard 15 oz can hominy.. Drain/rinse and add.
  5. Tomatoes: Can size note—use a standard 14.5 oz can no-salt-added diced tomatoes.. Add 1 can.
  6. Green chiles: Can size note—use a standard 4 oz can diced green chiles.. Add 1 can.
  7. Add 2 cups low-sodium broth. Simmer uncovered 15–18 minutes.
  8. Finish with 2 tsp lime juice. Portion per Notes.

Notes

Posole vibes: hearty, brothy-but-thick, very reheatable. Portions (per person): Frank: About 2 1/2 cups soup Susan: About 2 cups soup Elise: About 2 cups soup Tips (recipe-specific): Drain hominy well so the soup stays thick. Lime at the end makes it taste ‘fresh’ even on day 3.
