Pesto Chicken Protein Mac (Herby Comfort)

Nutrition (approx)

Frank: 645 kcal • P 60g • C 60g • F 16g • Sat 6g • Na 690mg • Fiber 10g
Susan/Elise (each): 484 kcal • P 45g • C 45g • F 12g • Sat 4g • Na 518mg • Fiber 8g

Ingredients (purchase-clean)

  • 1 box Barilla Protein+ pasta
  • 1.75 lb boneless skinless chicken breast
  • 1 jar basil pesto
  • 1 carton skim milk
  • 1 container reduced-fat shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1 bag frozen broccoli florets
  • kosher salt
  • black pepper
  • garlic powder
  • onion powder
  • smoked paprika
  • Italian seasoning
  • chili powder
  • ground cumin
  • crushed red pepper
  • cornstarch
  • extra-virgin olive oil
  • Dijon mustard
  • hot sauce
  • Worcestershire sauce
  • low-sodium soy sauce
  • rice vinegar
  • Italian seasoning

Steps

  1. Pasta: cook Protein+ pasta al dente. Drain.
  2. Chicken: sauté diced chicken with 2 tsp olive oil, 1 tsp garlic powder, 1 tsp Italian seasoning, 1/2 tsp kosher salt, 1/2 tsp black pepper 8–10 minutes to 165°F.
  3. Broccoli: steam 3 cups frozen broccoli; drain well.
  4. Sauce base: whisk 2 tbsp cornstarch into 2 cups skim milk. Simmer 3–5 minutes until thick.
  5. Cheese: stir in 4 oz reduced-fat mozzarella until melted.
  6. Pesto: stir in 2 tbsp basil pesto (measured) to flavor the sauce.
  7. Combine: fold in pasta + chicken + broccoli.
  8. Serve per Notes.

Notes

Herby pesto mac comfort without cream or heavy oil load (pesto is measured). Portions (per person): Frank: About 2 1/2 cups mac (1/3 pan) + extra veg Susan: About 2 cups mac (1/4 pan) + extra veg Elise: About 2 cups mac (1/4 pan) + extra veg Tips (recipe-specific): Measure pesto (2 tbsp) so fats stay controlled. Drain broccoli well so the sauce stays thick.
