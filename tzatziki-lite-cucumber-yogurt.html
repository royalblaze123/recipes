Tzatziki-Lite Cucumber Yogurt

Category: Dip • Yield: About 3/4 cup (12–13 tbsp) • AnyList-ready

Ingredients

  • 3/4 cup 2% plain Greek yogurt
  • 1/2 cucumber
  • 1 tsp extra-virgin olive oil
  • 1 tsp lemon juice
  • 2 tsp fresh dill
  • 1/4 tsp black pepper

Steps

  1. Grate 1/2 cucumber and squeeze it very dry in paper towels.
  2. Stir together Greek yogurt, cucumber, olive oil, lemon juice, dill, and black pepper.
  3. Chill 10 minutes before serving.
