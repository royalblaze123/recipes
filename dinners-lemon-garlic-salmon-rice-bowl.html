Lemon Garlic Salmon Rice Bowls

Baked lemon-garlic salmon over brown rice with steamed-style roasted broccoli and carrots for a fast, heart-healthy dinner.

Servings

3 servings.

Times

  • Prep time: 10 minutes
  • Cook time: 18 minutes
  • Total time: 28 minutes

Ingredients

  • 1 cup cooked brown rice
  • 14 oz raw salmon fillets
  • 2 cups small broccoli florets
  • 1 cup thinly sliced carrots
  • 1 tbsp extra-virgin olive oil
  • 1 1/2 tbsp lemon juice
  • 1 tsp lemon zest
  • 1 tsp garlic powder
  • 1/2 tsp dried dill or parsley
  • 1/4 tsp ground black pepper
  • 1/4 tsp kosher or sea salt
  • Olive oil spray

Preparation Steps

  1. Heat the oven to 400°F (about 205°C). Line a baking sheet with parchment paper and lightly spray with olive oil.
  2. Cook the brown rice if not already prepared. Use leftover brown rice, cook 1/3 cup dry brown rice according to package directions, or heat a microwave-ready pouch. You need about 1 cup cooked rice total. Cover and keep warm.
  3. Rinse and dry the broccoli and carrots. Cut the broccoli into small florets and thinly slice the carrots so they cook quickly.
  4. Pat the salmon fillets dry with a paper towel. Cut into three portions, aiming for about 5 oz for Frank and slightly smaller pieces for Susan and Elise.
  5. In a small bowl, stir together the olive oil, lemon juice, lemon zest (if using), garlic powder, dried dill or parsley, black pepper, and salt. Brush or spoon most of this mixture over the tops of the salmon portions.
  6. Place the salmon portions on one side of the prepared baking sheet. On the other side, add the broccoli florets and carrot slices in a single layer. Drizzle or brush the remaining lemon-garlic mixture lightly over the vegetables. Bake for 12–15 minutes, until the salmon flakes easily with a fork (145°F internal) and the vegetables are tender-crisp.
  7. If the vegetables need a bit more time, you can remove the salmon to a plate and return the vegetables to the oven for 2–3 extra minutes, but this is usually not needed when the pieces are small.
  8. Add 1/2 cup cooked brown rice to a large bowl for Frank. Top with about 5 oz cooked salmon and about 1 1/2 cups of the roasted broccoli and carrots, including a light drizzle of any juices from the pan.
  9. Divide the remaining rice between two bowls for Susan and Elise, about 1/3 cup each. Top each with about 3 3/4 oz salmon and about 1 cup vegetables, drizzling with any remaining pan juices.
  10. Serve immediately with lemon wedges if desired, keeping acidity modest for tolerance.

Portion Sizes (Per Person, Approximate)

  • Frank (F · 1.0×): Bowl with ~5 oz cooked salmon, ~1/2 cup cooked brown rice, ~1 1/2 cups vegetables, and about 2 tsp olive oil total across fish and vegetables.
  • Susan (S · 0.75×): Bowl with ~3 3/4 oz salmon, ~1/3 cup rice, ~1 cup vegetables, and about 1 1/2 tsp olive oil total.
  • Elise (E · 0.75×): Bowl with ~3 3/4 oz salmon, ~1/3 cup rice, ~1 cup vegetables, and about 1 1/2 tsp olive oil total.
