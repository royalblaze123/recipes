Chicken Sausage + Peppers Skillet + Potatoes

Nutrition (approx)

Frank: 650 kcal • P 45g • C 60g • F 26g
Susan/Elise (each): 488 kcal • P 34g • C 45g • F 20g

Ingredients (purchase-clean)

  • 1.25 lb chicken sausage
  • 2 each bell pepper
  • 1 each onion
  • 1.50 lb baby potatoes
  • low-sodium chicken broth
  • kosher salt
  • black pepper
  • garlic powder
  • onion powder
  • smoked paprika
  • Italian seasoning
  • ground cumin
  • chili powder
  • red pepper flakes
  • extra-virgin olive oil
  • low-sodium chicken broth
  • low-sodium beef broth
  • low-sodium soy sauce
  • hot sauce
  • Dijon mustard
  • balsamic vinegar
  • apple cider vinegar
  • cornstarch

Steps (stovetop)

  1. Potatoes: boil 12–14 minutes; drain and smash. Sear with olive oil 6–8 minutes; season modest salt/pepper/garlic powder.
  2. Sausage: slice chicken sausage; brown 4–5 minutes.
  3. Peppers/onion: add sliced peppers/onion; cook 6–8 minutes tender-crisp.
  4. Deglaze: add 1/4 cup broth; season with smoked paprika, black pepper, and a small pinch of salt; simmer 2 minutes.
  5. Serve per Notes.

Notes

Sausage-and-peppers vibe with controlled portions and crispy potatoes. Portions (per person): Frank: 6 oz sausage + 1 1/2 cups peppers/onion + 1 cup potatoes Susan: 4.5 oz sausage + 1 cup peppers/onion + 3/4 cup potatoes Elise: 4.5 oz sausage + 1 cup peppers/onion + 3/4 cup potatoes Tips (recipe-specific): Pick the lowest-sodium chicken sausage you can find. Deglaze with broth so it stays juicy.
