Sugar-Free BBQ Glazed Meatloaf Plate

Nutrition (approx)

Frank: 640 kcal • P 55g • C 40g • F 24g
Susan/Elise (each): 480 kcal • P 41g • C 30g • F 18g

Ingredients (purchase-clean)

  • 1.50 lb 93% lean ground beef
  • 1 each yellow onion
  • 2 each large eggs
  • 3/4 cup whole wheat panko breadcrumbs
  • 6 tsp sugar-free BBQ sauce
  • 4 tsp ketchup
  • 2 tsp apple cider vinegar
  • 2 tsp smoked paprika
  • 2 tsp garlic powder
  • 1 tsp black pepper
  • 1 tsp kosher salt
  • 1.50 lb sweet potatoes
  • 12 oz fresh broccoli florets
  • 4 tsp extra-virgin olive oil

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 400°F. Line a sheet pan with parchment.
  2. Dice the yellow onion very small.
  3. Meatloaf mix: combine ground beef, onion, eggs, panko, 4 tsp ketchup, 2 tsp apple cider vinegar, 2 tsp smoked paprika, 2 tsp garlic powder, 1 tsp black pepper, and 1 tsp kosher salt. Mix just until combined.
  4. Form loaf on the sheet pan. Bake 25 minutes.
  5. Glaze: stir 6 tsp sugar-free BBQ sauce with 2 tsp water (from tap; do not add to Ingredients) to thin slightly. Brush over the top of the meatloaf.
  6. Sweet potatoes: peel if desired; cut into 3/4-inch cubes. Toss with 2 tsp olive oil and spread on the pan. Roast 25–30 minutes, turning once.
  7. Broccoli: toss florets with 2 tsp olive oil; add for the last 12–15 minutes until browned at edges.
  8. Meatloaf doneness: bake until center reaches 160°F. Rest 10 minutes before slicing.

Notes

BBQ-style meatloaf without added sugar, paired with sweet potatoes and roasted broccoli.
