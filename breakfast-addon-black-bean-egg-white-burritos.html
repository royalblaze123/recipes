Freezer Black Bean & Egg White Breakfast Burritos

Freezer burritos with beans + whole grains for slow carbs and steady energy alongside your shake.

Servings

12 burritos.

Times

  • Prep time: 25 minutes
  • Cook time: 15 minutes
  • Total time: 40 minutes

Ingredients

Main Dish

  • 1 package whole wheat tortillas
  • 1 carton egg whites
  • 2 cans black beans
  • 1 jar salsa
  • 1 bag reduced-fat shredded cheese
  • 1 each bell pepper
  • 1 jar ground cumin
  • 1 jar black pepper
    • 

    Preparation Steps

    1. Dice 1/2 bell pepper; store the rest for another recipe.
    2. Drain and rinse both cans of black beans. Measure out 2 cups beans for the filling; refrigerate the rest for another recipe.
    3. Scramble 4 cups egg whites in a nonstick skillet until just set. Season with cumin and black pepper.
    4. Stir beans, eggs, diced pepper, 1/2 cup salsa, and 1 cup reduced-fat shredded cheese. Reseal the cheese bag.
    5. Divide filling across 12 whole wheat tortillas and roll into burritos.
    6. Wrap each burrito tightly and freeze in a labeled bag for up to 1 month.
    7. Breakfast use: microwave from frozen until hot; eat with your morning shake.

      Notes

      Portions (per person):


      Frank – 1 burrito with your morning shake (slow-carb add-on).


      Susan – Optional: 1 burrito with breakfast if desired.


      Elise – Optional: 1 burrito with breakfast if desired.


      Tips (recipe-specific):


      Choose a lower-sodium salsa to keep this breakfast add-on in bounds.


      For less sodium and more volume, add spinach at the end of scrambling.


      If lunch is early, you can split 1 burrito into two smaller halves.

      • Susan: 250 kcal; 22 g protein; 32 g carbs; 6 g fat; 7 g fiber; 520 mg sodium
      • Elise: 250 kcal; 22 g protein; 32 g carbs; 6 g fat; 7 g fiber; 520 mg sodium
