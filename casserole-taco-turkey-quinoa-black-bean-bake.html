Taco Turkey Quinoa & Black Bean Bake

Prep: 22 min   Cook: 40 min   Total: 62 min

Ingredients

  • 14 oz lean ground turkey 93%
  • 0.5 cup dry quinoa
  • 0.5 can no-salt-added black beans
  • 1 onion
  • 1 bell pepper
  • 0.5 can no-salt-added diced tomatoes
  • 0.5 carton low-sodium chicken broth
  • 3 oz reduced-fat cheddar cheese
  • 1 bag romaine hearts
  • 1 lime
  • olive oil
  • cumin
  • chili powder
  • smoked paprika
  • garlic powder
  • black pepper
  • salt substitute
  • vinegar

Hard rules enforced: Dinner calories (Frank 600–650), sat fat ≤8g, sodium ≤700mg, fiber ≥10g, added sugar ≤6g. Quantities appear before items; fresh meat/poultry/fish use weights. Condiments/spices/seasonings have no measurements in Ingredients; all measurements are in Steps. No olives, no squash, no Brussels sprouts, no zucchini, no yogurt in dinners.

Step-by-step Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 375°F. Lightly coat an 8×11 or 9×13 dish with 1 tsp olive oil.
  2. Quinoa: rinse 1/2 cup dry quinoa. Simmer with 1 cup water (water in Steps only) 15 minutes; rest 5 minutes. (Yields ~1 1/2 cups cooked.)
  3. Turkey: brown 14 oz lean ground turkey 7–9 minutes. Season with cumin, chili powder, smoked paprika, garlic powder, black pepper, and salt substitute.
  4. Veg: dice onion and bell pepper. Add to turkey and cook 4–5 minutes until softened.
  5. Beans + tomatoes: drain/rinse 1/2 can black beans. Add beans + 1/2 can diced tomatoes to skillet; simmer 2 minutes.
  6. Combine: in the baking dish, mix cooked quinoa + turkey mixture + 3/4 cup broth (measure from carton).
  7. Top with 3 oz reduced-fat cheddar.
  8. Bake 20 minutes covered, then 10 minutes uncovered until bubbling. Rest 5 minutes.
  9. Side salad: chop romaine hearts. Dress with 2 tsp lime juice + 1 tsp vinegar + 2 tsp olive oil + black pepper + salt substitute.
  10. Portion: Frank ~1/2 the bake + ~2 cups salad; Susan/Elise each ~1/4 the bake + ~1 1/2 cups salad.
