Neutral Magnesium Hydration Mix (3 Bottles, No Citrus)

NOTES: Built for evenings or GERD-sensitive times when you want electrolytes without lemon or other acidic flavors. Keeps sodium moderate while still supporting hydration and muscle function.

PORTION SIZES (each recipe makes 3 bottles): Frank: 1 full 20 oz bottle; Susan: 1 full 20 oz bottle; Elise: 1 full 20 oz bottle.

Ingredients

  • 60 fl oz room-temperature or cool filtered water (3 x 20 fl oz bottles)
  • 3/8 tsp lite salt (divided, 1/8 tsp per bottle)
  • 3 scoops magnesium glycinate powder (about 150–200 mg elemental Mg per scoop, divided)
  • 9–12 drops neutral, non-acidic flavor drops (e.g., vanilla or mixed berry, 3–4 drops per bottle)
  • Liquid stevia or monk fruit sweetener, to taste (optional)

Step-by-Step Instructions

  1. Line up three 20 fl oz bottles. Add 1/8 teaspoon lite salt to each bottle.
  2. Divide the magnesium glycinate powder evenly across the three bottles.
  3. Add 3–4 drops of neutral, non-acidic flavor drops to each bottle.
  4. Add a few drops of stevia or monk fruit sweetener if desired.
  5. Fill each bottle with filtered water up to about 20 fl oz total volume.
  6. Cap tightly, then shake each bottle for 20–30 seconds until the powders dissolve.
  7. Chill in the refrigerator or keep at room temperature based on preference. Shake gently before sipping.

Nutrition per Person (1 bottle)

  • Frank: ~5 kcal, 0 g protein, 0 g fat, 1 g carbs, ~250–300 mg sodium, ~300–350 mg potassium.
  • Susan: ~5 kcal, 0 g protein, 0 g fat, 1 g carbs, ~250–300 mg sodium, ~300–350 mg potassium.
  • Elise: ~5 kcal, 0 g protein, 0 g fat, 1 g carbs, ~250–300 mg sodium, ~300–350 mg potassium.
