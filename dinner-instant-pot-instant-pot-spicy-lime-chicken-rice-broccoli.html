Instant Pot Spicy Lime Chicken + Rice + Broccoli

Nutrition (approx)

Frank: 650 kcal • P 60g • C 70g • F 14g
Susan/Elise (each): 488 kcal • P 45g • C 52g • F 10g

Ingredients (purchase-clean)

  • 1.75 lb boneless skinless chicken breast
  • 1 each lime
  • low-sodium soy sauce
  • 1 cup dry brown rice
  • 1 bag frozen broccoli florets
  • low-sodium chicken broth
  • cornstarch
  • kosher salt
  • black pepper
  • garlic powder
  • onion powder
  • smoked paprika
  • Italian seasoning
  • ground cumin
  • chili powder
  • red pepper flakes
  • extra-virgin olive oil
  • low-sodium chicken broth
  • low-sodium beef broth
  • low-sodium soy sauce
  • hot sauce
  • Dijon mustard
  • balsamic vinegar
  • apple cider vinegar
  • cornstarch

Steps (Instant Pot)

  1. Rice: cook brown rice (stovetop 35–40 minutes or pot-in-pot High 22; NR 10).
  2. Chicken: add chicken + broth (1/2 cup) + soy sauce (4 tbsp) + hot sauce (2 tsp). Season garlic powder, black pepper, modest salt.
  3. Pressure cook: High 10 minutes; NR 10 then Quick Release. Shred chicken.
  4. Thicken: stir in cornstarch slurry (1 tbsp + cold water). Sauté 2 minutes.
  5. Broccoli: microwave broccoli 4–5 minutes; drain/pat dry. Fold into chicken for 2 minutes.
  6. Finish: add lime juice (2 tbsp measured). Serve over rice per Notes.

Notes

Bright lime heat with a thick sauce—no sugar, no cream. Portions (per person): Frank: 7 oz chicken + 1 cup cooked rice + 1 cup broccoli Susan: 5 oz protein + 1 cup cooked rice (smaller) + 1 cup broccoli (smaller) Elise: 5 oz protein + 1 cup cooked rice (smaller) + 1 cup broccoli (smaller) Tips (recipe-specific): Add lime after thickening so it stays bright. Pat broccoli dry so the sauce stays thick.
