Lean Beef Veggie Rice Bowl

93% lean beef with brown rice and steamed mixed vegetables.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb 93% lean ground beef
  • 2 cups cooked brown rice
  • 1Â½ cups steamed mixed vegetables
  • 1 tsp garlic powder
  • 1 tsp onion powder
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1/4 tsp black pepper

Instructions

  1. Brown beef and season.
  2. Steam mixed vegetables.
  3. Combine beef, vegetables, and rice.
  4. Portion and reheat 60 seconds before eating.

Serves: 3 servings
