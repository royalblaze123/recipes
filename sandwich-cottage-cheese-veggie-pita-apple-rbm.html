Cottage Cheese Veggie Pita & Apple Slices

High-protein cottage cheese stuffed into a whole-wheat pita with crunchy veggies, served with apple slices on the side.

Servings

3 servings.

Times

  • Prep time: 10 minutes
  • Cook time: 0 minutes
  • Total time: 10 minutes

Ingredients

Sandwich

  • 3 small whole-wheat pitas
  • 1 1/2 cups low-fat cottage cheese
  • 1/4 cup finely diced cucumber
  • 1/4 cup finely diced red bell pepper
  • 1/4 cup finely diced carrot
  • 1/4 tsp garlic powder
  • 1/4 tsp black pepper

Side

  • 1 1/2 medium apples, cored and sliced (leave peel on)

Preparation Steps

  1. Place the cottage cheese in a medium mixing bowl.
  2. Add the diced cucumber, diced red bell pepper, diced carrot, garlic powder, and black pepper to the bowl and stir until the vegetables are evenly distributed in the cottage cheese.
  3. Cut each whole-wheat pita in half to make two pockets, for a total of six pita halves.
  4. Open each pita half gently with your fingers to create a pocket for filling.
  5. Spoon the cottage cheese and vegetable mixture into the pita halves, dividing it so Frank receives a slightly larger amount and Susan and Elise slightly smaller amounts as listed in the portions section.
  6. Arrange the stuffed pita halves on plates.
  7. Core the apples and slice them thinly, leaving the peel on for extra fiber, then divide the slices onto three plates according to the portions listed.
  8. Serve each person their stuffed pita halves with their portion of apple slices.

Notes

Portions (per person):

Frank – 2 stuffed pita halves filled with about 2/3 cup cottage cheese veggie mixture, plus about 2/3 of a medium apple in slices.

Susan – 2 stuffed pita halves filled with about 1/2 cup cottage cheese veggie mixture, plus about 1/2 of a medium apple in slices.

Elise – 2 stuffed pita halves filled with about 1/2 cup cottage cheese veggie mixture, plus about 1/2 of a medium apple in slices.

Nutrition (per person)

  • Frank: 390 kcal; 36 g protein; 40 g carbs; 8 g fat; 6 g fiber; ~520 mg sodium.
  • Susan: 350 kcal; 31 g protein; 36 g carbs; 7 g fat; 5 g fiber; ~480 mg sodium.
  • Elise: 350 kcal; 31 g protein; 36 g carbs; 7 g fat; 5 g fiber; ~480 mg sodium.
