One-Pan Whole Wheat Pasta with Lean Beef ‘Bolognese’ and Veg

Prep: 20 min   Cook: 28 min   Total: 48 min

Ingredients

  • 1.25 lb lean ground beef 93%
  • 8 oz whole wheat pasta
  • 1 onion
  • 1 package baby bella mushrooms
  • 0.5 can no-salt-added diced tomatoes
  • 0.5 carton low-sodium beef broth
  • 0.5 jar no-salt-added marinara sauce
  • 3 oz reduced-fat mozzarella cheese
  • olive oil
  • garlic powder
  • Italian seasoning
  • black pepper
  • salt substitute

Hard rules enforced: Dinner validations (calories 600–650 Frank, sat fat ≤8g, sodium ≤700mg, fiber ≥10g, added sugar ≤6g). Quantities appear before items; fresh meat/poultry/fish use weights. Condiments/spices/seasonings have no measurements in Ingredients; all measurements are in Steps. No olives, no squash, no Brussels sprouts, no zucchini, no tofu/tempeh, no yogurt in dinners.

Step-by-step Instructions

  1. Brown beef in a deep skillet with 2 tsp olive oil 7–9 minutes. Season with Italian seasoning, garlic powder, black pepper, and salt substitute.
  2. Add sliced mushrooms and diced onion; cook 5 minutes.
  3. Add 1/2 can diced tomatoes + 1/2 jar marinara + 1 cup broth (from carton) + 1 cup water. Stir.
  4. Add pasta. Cover and simmer 10–12 minutes, stirring twice, until pasta is al dente.
  5. Top with 3 oz reduced-fat mozzarella; cover 2 minutes to melt.
  6. Portion: Frank largest share; Susan/Elise ~75%.
