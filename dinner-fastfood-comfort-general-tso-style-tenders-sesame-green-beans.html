General Tso-Style Tenders + Sesame Green Beans

Nutrition (approx)

Frank: 635 kcal • P 62g • C 45g • F 18g • Sat 4g • Na 700mg • Fiber 10g
Susan/Elise (each): 476 kcal • P 46g • C 34g • F 14g • Sat 3g • Na 525mg • Fiber 8g

Ingredients (purchase-clean)

  • 1.75 lb chicken tenderloins
  • 1 carton egg whites
  • cornstarch
  • low-sodium soy sauce
  • rice vinegar
  • 1 each lime
  • 1 bag frozen green beans
  • kosher salt
  • black pepper
  • garlic powder
  • onion powder
  • smoked paprika
  • chili powder
  • ground cumin
  • Italian seasoning
  • crushed red pepper
  • cornstarch
  • low-sodium soy sauce
  • rice vinegar
  • hot sauce
  • Dijon mustard
  • extra-virgin olive oil
  • sesame seeds
  • ginger

Steps

  1. Air fryer: preheat to 400°F.
  2. Tenders: lightly dust with 3 tbsp cornstarch + 1/2 tsp garlic powder + 1/2 tsp black pepper. Dip in 1/2 cup egg whites and re-dust with 2 tbsp cornstarch.
  3. Cook tenders: Basket A, 400°F for 10–12 minutes with shake reminder ON; flip at prompt; cook to 165°F.
  4. Green beans: Basket B, air fry frozen green beans with 2 tsp olive oil, black pepper, and a pinch of kosher salt at 400°F for 10–12 minutes (shake once).
  5. Sauce: whisk 3 tbsp low-sodium soy sauce + 2 tbsp rice vinegar + 2 tsp lime juice + 1 tsp ginger + 1 tsp cornstarch + 1/4 cup water + 1 tsp hot sauce. Simmer 2–3 minutes until thick.
  6. Toss tenders in sauce. Sprinkle 1 tsp sesame seeds over green beans.
  7. Serve per Notes.

Notes

General Tso energy, but sauce is measured and thick (not sugary). Portions (per person): Frank: 6 tenders + 1 cup green beans side + dip Susan: 4 tenders + 3/4 cup green beans side + dip Elise: 4 tenders + 3/4 cup green beans side + dip Tips (recipe-specific): Thicken sauce with cornstarch so a small amount coats well. Air fry green beans until browned for ‘takeout’ flavor.
