Oven-Baked Chicken Parmesan + Barilla Protein+

Nutrition (approx)

Frank: 650 kcal • P 60g • C 75g • F 14g
Susan/Elise (each): 488 kcal • P 45g • C 56g • F 10g

Ingredients (purchase-clean)

  • 1.75 lb boneless skinless chicken breast
  • 1 jar marinara sauce
  • 2 cups panko breadcrumbs
  • 6 oz grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 box Barilla Protein+ pasta
  • kosher salt
  • black pepper
  • garlic powder
  • onion powder
  • smoked paprika
  • Italian seasoning
  • ground cumin
  • chili powder
  • red pepper flakes
  • extra-virgin olive oil
  • low-sodium chicken broth
  • low-sodium beef broth
  • low-sodium soy sauce
  • hot sauce
  • Dijon mustard
  • balsamic vinegar
  • apple cider vinegar
  • cornstarch

Steps (oven)

  1. Oven: heat to 425°F. Line a sheet with a rack (or foil + spray).
  2. Chicken: slice into cutlets. Season with garlic powder, Italian seasoning, black pepper, and a modest pinch of kosher salt.
  3. Crumb: toast panko in a skillet with 2 tsp olive oil 3–4 minutes. Stir in 1/2 of Parmesan.
  4. Bake: place chicken on rack; bake 14–18 minutes to 165°F.
  5. Sauce: warm marinara (2 cups measured) in a saucepan 3 minutes.
  6. Pasta: cook Barilla Protein+ al dente; drain.
  7. Serve: pasta bowl + chicken + marinara + remaining Parmesan + toasted crumbs per Notes.

Notes

Crispy-topped chicken parm feel without frying; Protein+ pasta keeps it balanced. Portions (per person): Frank: 7 oz chicken + 2 cups pasta bowl + 2 tbsp crumbs Susan: 5 oz chicken + 1 1/2 cups pasta bowl + 1 1/2 tbsp crumbs Elise: 5 oz chicken + 1 1/2 cups pasta bowl + 1 1/2 tbsp crumbs Tips (recipe-specific): Toast panko first so it stays crunchy on leftovers (add at serving). Bake on a rack for drier surface and better texture.
