Turkey Wild Rice Mushroom Soup (Stovetop)

NOTES: Designed as a complete meal soup using lean protein, high-fiber vegetables, and controlled starch. Use low-sodium broth and avoid extra salt when possible to support blood pressure and kidney health. PORTION SIZES: Frank: largest portion (~2 cups); Susan: medium portion (~1.5 cups); Elise: medium portion (~1.5 cups).

Ingredients

  • 1 tbsp extra-virgin olive oil
  • 1 medium yellow onion, diced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 cups sliced mushrooms
  • 1 medium carrot, diced
  • 1 celery stalk, diced
  • 1/2 cup uncooked wild rice, rinsed
  • 5 cups low-sodium chicken broth
  • 1 cup water
  • 1 lb cooked turkey breast, diced or shredded (or leftover roasted turkey)
  • 1 tsp dried thyme
  • 1/2 tsp dried rosemary, crushed
  • 1/2 tsp kosher salt (or to taste)
  • Freshly ground black pepper, to taste

Step-by-Step Instructions

  1. Heat olive oil in a large soup pot over medium heat.
  2. Add diced onion and cook 4–5 minutes until softened.
  3. Stir in minced garlic and cook 30–60 seconds until fragrant.
  4. Add sliced mushrooms, diced carrot, and diced celery and cook 4–5 minutes, stirring occasionally, until vegetables soften and mushrooms release some liquid.
  5. Add rinsed wild rice and stir to coat with the vegetable mixture.
  6. Pour in low-sodium chicken broth and water and stir.
  7. Stir in dried thyme, dried rosemary, salt, and black pepper.
  8. Bring to a gentle boil, then reduce heat to low, cover partially, and simmer 40–45 minutes, stirring occasionally, until wild rice is tender and grains have burst open.
  9. Stir in cooked turkey and simmer another 5–10 minutes on low to warm through.
  10. Taste and adjust seasoning with a bit more pepper or a small pinch of salt if needed.
  11. Remove from heat, let rest a few minutes, then ladle into bowls for Frank, Susan, and Elise.

Nutrition per Person

  • Frank: ~490 kcal, ~45 g protein, ~45 g carbs, ~11 g fat.
  • Susan: ~370 kcal, ~34 g protein, ~34 g carbs, ~8 g fat.
  • Elise: ~370 kcal, ~34 g protein, ~34 g carbs, ~8 g fat.
