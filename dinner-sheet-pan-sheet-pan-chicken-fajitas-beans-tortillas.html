Sheet-Pan Chicken Fajitas + Beans + Tortillas

Nutrition (approx)

Frank: 650 kcal • P 60g • C 60g • F 18g • Sat 3g • Na 700mg • Fiber 14g
Susan/Elise (each): 488 kcal • P 45g • C 45g • F 14g • Sat 2g • Na 525mg • Fiber 10g

Ingredients (purchase-clean)

  • 1.75 lb boneless skinless chicken breast
  • 1 bag frozen fajita-style pepper blend
  • 1 can black beans
  • 3 each whole-wheat tortillas
  • 1 jar salsa
  • kosher salt
  • black pepper
  • garlic powder
  • onion powder
  • smoked paprika
  • Italian seasoning
  • ground cumin
  • chili powder
  • red pepper flakes
  • extra-virgin olive oil
  • Dijon mustard
  • balsamic vinegar
  • apple cider vinegar
  • low-sodium soy sauce
  • cornstarch
  • hot sauce

Steps

  1. Oven: heat to 450°F. Line sheet pan.
  2. Peppers: toss pepper blend with 2 tsp olive oil, chili powder, cumin, garlic powder, 1/2 tsp kosher salt, black pepper. Roast 16 minutes, stirring once.
  3. Chicken: slice into strips; toss with 2 tsp olive oil, chili powder, cumin, garlic powder, 1/2 tsp kosher salt, black pepper. Add to pan; roast 10–12 minutes to 165°F.
  4. Beans: drain/rinse black beans (15 oz). Warm 1 1/2 cups beans with 1/2 cup salsa (measured) on stovetop 4 minutes.
  5. Tortillas: warm on skillet 20–30 seconds per side.
  6. Assemble: 2 tbsp salsa per taco (measured) plus beans on the side per Notes.
  7. Serve per Notes.

Notes

Fajita night with big roasted flavor; beans + salsa add moisture and fiber. Portions (per person): Frank: 3 tacos + 1/2 cup beans Susan: 3 tacos + 1/3 cup beans Elise: 3 tacos + 1/3 cup beans Tips (recipe-specific): Roast peppers first so they char instead of steam. Warm beans with salsa so it feels like a full dinner.
