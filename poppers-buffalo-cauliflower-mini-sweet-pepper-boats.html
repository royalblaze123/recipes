Buffalo Cauliflower Mini Sweet Pepper Boats

Sheet Pan • Yield: 36 pepper boats

Ingredients (purchase-clean)

  • 18 each fresh mini sweet peppers
  • 12 oz fresh cauliflower florets
  • 6 oz reduced-fat cream cheese
  • 2 oz part-skim shredded mozzarella
  • hot sauce
  • garlic powder
  • kosher salt
  • black pepper

Steps

  1. Heat oven to 425°F. Line a sheet pan with parchment.
  2. Chop 12 oz cauliflower very small. Microwave 2 minutes; squeeze dry in a towel.
  3. Mix filling: soften 6 oz cream cheese; stir in 6 tbsp hot sauce (18 tsp), 1 tsp garlic powder, 1/2 tsp kosher salt, 1/2 tsp black pepper; fold in cauliflower and 2 oz mozzarella.
  4. Halve 18 mini sweet peppers to 36 boats; remove seeds; fill evenly.
  5. Bake 10–12 min until peppers are tender-crisp and filling is hot.

Notes

Flavor-forward holiday poppers designed for snacking or a light meal. Portions (guidance): Great for guests who want a lighter option. Snack portion: 8–10 boats.
