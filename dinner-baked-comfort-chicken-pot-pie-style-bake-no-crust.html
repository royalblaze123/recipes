Chicken Pot Pie-Style Bake (No Crust)

Nutrition (approx)

Frank: 635 kcal • P 58g • C 58g • F 16g • Sat 5g • Na 690mg • Fiber 10g
Susan/Elise (each): 476 kcal • P 44g • C 44g • F 12g • Sat 4g • Na 518mg • Fiber 8g

Ingredients (purchase-clean)

  • 1.75 lb boneless skinless chicken breast
  • 1 bag frozen mixed vegetables
  • 1 each onion
  • 1 carton low-sodium chicken broth
  • 1 tub low-fat cottage cheese
  • 1 box instant mashed potatoes
  • kosher salt
  • black pepper
  • garlic powder
  • onion powder
  • smoked paprika
  • chili powder
  • ground cumin
  • Italian seasoning
  • extra-virgin olive oil

Steps

  1. Oven: heat to 400°F. Lightly oil a baking dish.
  2. Chicken: cube and sauté with 2 tsp olive oil 8–10 minutes until 165°F.
  3. Add 1 diced onion and cook 3–4 minutes until softened.
  4. Season with 2 tsp garlic powder, 1 tsp onion powder, 1 tsp black pepper, 1/2 tsp kosher salt.
  5. Veg: add 3 cups frozen mixed vegetables; cook 6–8 minutes until hot.
  6. Sauce base: stir 1 cup low-fat cottage cheese with 3/4 cup warm broth until smooth; add to skillet and simmer 2 minutes.
  7. Top: prepare instant mashed potatoes per box for 3 servings (use water/broth). Spread over the chicken mixture.
  8. Bake: 12–15 minutes until bubbly. Broil 1–2 minutes to brown peaks. Rest 10 minutes.
  9. Portion per Notes.

Notes

Pot-pie comfort without crust—creamy chicken/veg under mashed potato topping. Portions (per person): Frank: 1/3 of pan Susan: 1/4 of pan Elise: 1/4 of pan Tips (recipe-specific): Use cottage + broth for a creamy sauce without heavy cream. Brown the potato top under the broiler for ‘real comfort’ flavor.
