Chicken Avocado Smash Sandwiches - RBM Lunch

Lean chicken with avocado yogurt smash plus a cucumber and carrot side.

Portions (per person):

Frank – 4 sandwich halves (2 sandwiches) + veg side

 

Susan – 3 sandwich halves (1.5 sandwiches) + veg side

 

Elise – 3 sandwich halves (1.5 sandwiches) + veg side

Ingredients

  • 10 slices thin whole wheat bread
  • 15 slices deli chicken breast
  • 2 avocados
  • 1 container plain Greek yogurt
  • 1 lime
  • 1 romaine heart
  • 2 tomatoes
  • 1 cucumber
  • 1 lb bag baby carrots
  • 1 bottle olive oil spray

Step-by-step Instructions

  1. Deli order: ask for chicken breast sliced thin for sandwiches not shaved.
  2. Cut the avocados in half, remove pits, and scoop flesh into a bowl. Mash until mostly smooth.
  3. Stir 4 tablespoons plain Greek yogurt into the mashed avocado. Zest the lime and squeeze 1 tablespoon lime juice into the bowl; mix.
  4. Wash the romaine heart. Chop enough to measure about 4 cups chopped romaine for the sandwiches; refrigerate remaining romaine if any.
  5. Wash the tomatoes. Slice enough to make 10 tomato slices total for the sandwiches; refrigerate any extra slices.
  6. Wash the cucumber. Slice enough to make 20 thin rounds for the sandwiches; reserve remaining cucumber for the veg sides.
  7. Toast option: lightly mist 10 bread slices with olive oil spray and toast 2–4 minutes, flipping once, until lightly crisp.
  8. Assemble 5 sandwiches: spread avocado mixture across 5 bread slices. Layer romaine, tomato, 3 slices deli chicken, and cucumber rounds. Top with remaining bread slices.
  9. Cut each sandwich in half to make 10 halves. Portion: Frank gets 4 halves; Susan 3 halves; Elise 3 halves.
  10. Veg sides: measure 1 cup cucumber slices for Frank and 3/4 cup for Susan and Elise. Measure baby carrots: 1/2 cup for Frank and 1/3 cup for Susan and Elise. Pack separately.
