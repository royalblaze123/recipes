Teriyaki-Style Chicken Meatballs + Broccoli

Nutrition (approx)

Frank: 645 kcal • P 60g • C 55g • F 18g • Sat 5g • Na 690mg • Fiber 10g
Susan/Elise (each): 484 kcal • P 45g • C 41g • F 14g • Sat 4g • Na 518mg • Fiber 8g

Ingredients (purchase-clean)

  • 2.00 lb 99% lean ground chicken
  • 1 package whole-wheat breadcrumbs
  • 2 each eggs
  • 1 bottle low-sugar teriyaki sauce
  • 1 bag frozen broccoli florets
  • 1 cup dry brown rice
  • kosher salt
  • black pepper
  • garlic powder
  • onion powder
  • Italian seasoning
  • smoked paprika
  • chili powder
  • ground cumin
  • crushed red pepper
  • extra-virgin olive oil
  • ginger
  • rice vinegar

Steps

  1. Oven: heat to 425°F. Line a sheet pan and lightly oil.
  2. Rice: cook 1 cup dry brown rice with 2 cups water 35–40 minutes. Rest 5 minutes, fluff.
  3. Meatballs: mix chicken with 1/2 cup breadcrumbs, 2 eggs, 2 tsp garlic powder, 1 tsp onion powder, 1 tsp ginger, 1/2 tsp kosher salt, 1/2 tsp black pepper. Form 18–20 meatballs.
  4. Bake 13–15 minutes to 165°F.
  5. Broccoli: roast frozen broccoli florets with 2 tsp olive oil at 425°F for 16–20 minutes.
  6. Sauce: warm 1/4 cup low-sugar teriyaki + 2 tsp rice vinegar. Toss meatballs in sauce.
  7. Serve: portion rice + meatballs + broccoli per Notes.

Notes

Sticky-sweet vibe without loading sugar. Portions (per person): Frank: 8–9 meatballs + 1 cup cooked rice + 1 cup broccoli Susan: 6–7 meatballs + 3/4 cup cooked rice + 3/4 cup broccoli Elise: 6–7 meatballs + 3/4 cup cooked rice + 3/4 cup broccoli Tips (recipe-specific): Measure teriyaki; vinegar helps it taste bigger. Roast broccoli until browned for ‘takeout’ flavor.
