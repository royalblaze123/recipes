Sirloin + Peppers Skillet + Sweet Potatoes

Nutrition (approx)

Frank: 650 kcal • P 55g • C 60g • F 18g
Susan/Elise (each): 488 kcal • P 41g • C 45g • F 14g

Ingredients (purchase-clean)

  • 1.50 lb lean sirloin steak
  • 2 each bell pepper
  • 1 each onion
  • 2.00 lb sweet potatoes
  • low-sodium beef broth
  • kosher salt
  • black pepper
  • garlic powder
  • onion powder
  • smoked paprika
  • Italian seasoning
  • ground cumin
  • chili powder
  • red pepper flakes
  • extra-virgin olive oil
  • low-sodium chicken broth
  • low-sodium beef broth
  • low-sodium soy sauce
  • hot sauce
  • Dijon mustard
  • balsamic vinegar
  • apple cider vinegar
  • cornstarch

Steps (stovetop)

  1. Sweet potatoes: roast cubes 425°F 22–26 minutes with olive oil, smoked paprika, garlic powder, modest salt, black pepper.
  2. Steak: slice sirloin thin across grain. Sear in hot skillet with olive oil 2–3 minutes.
  3. Peppers/onion: add sliced peppers/onion; cook 6–8 minutes tender-crisp.
  4. Deglaze: add 1/4 cup beef broth; season black pepper, garlic powder, onion powder, red pepper flakes (optional). Simmer 2 minutes.
  5. Serve per Notes.

Notes

Steak-and-peppers comfort with sweet potato as the controlled carb. Portions (per person): Frank: 7 oz steak + 1 1/2 cups peppers/onion + 1 cup roasted sweet potatoes Susan: 5 oz protein + 1 cup veg + 3/4 cup Elise: 5 oz protein + 1 cup veg + 3/4 cup Tips (recipe-specific): Slice steak across grain for tenderness. Roast sweet potatoes hot for edges instead of soft cubes.
