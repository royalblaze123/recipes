Instant Pot Chicken Soup Base + Protein Pasta (Cooked Separately)

Nutrition (approx)

Frank: 650 kcal • P 60g • C 70g • F 14g
Susan/Elise (each): 488 kcal • P 45g • C 52g • F 10g

Ingredients (purchase-clean)

  • 1.75 lb boneless skinless chicken breast
  • 1 box Barilla Protein+ pasta
  • 1 lb carrots
  • 2 stalks celery
  • 1 each onion
  • low-sodium chicken broth
  • kosher salt
  • black pepper
  • garlic powder
  • onion powder
  • smoked paprika
  • Italian seasoning
  • ground cumin
  • chili powder
  • red pepper flakes
  • extra-virgin olive oil
  • low-sodium chicken broth
  • low-sodium beef broth
  • low-sodium soy sauce
  • hot sauce
  • Dijon mustard
  • balsamic vinegar
  • apple cider vinegar
  • cornstarch

Steps (Instant Pot)

  1. Sauté: olive oil + onion 3 minutes; add carrots/celery 2 minutes.
  2. Load: add broth (10 cups) + chicken. Season garlic powder, black pepper, modest salt, Italian seasoning.
  3. Pressure cook: High 10 minutes; NR 10 then Quick Release.
  4. Shred chicken. Reduce: Sauté 8–10 minutes to concentrate into a thick ‘soup base.’
  5. Pasta: cook Barilla Protein+ separately al dente; drain.
  6. Serve: ladle thick base over measured pasta per Notes.

Notes

Chicken ‘noodle’ comfort without mush: cook pasta separately and combine at serving. Portions (per person): Frank: 7 oz chicken base + 2 cups pasta bowl + (veg in base) Susan: 5 oz protein + 2 cups pasta bowl (smaller) + (veg in base) (smaller) Elise: 5 oz protein + 2 cups pasta bowl (smaller) + (veg in base) (smaller) Tips (recipe-specific): Always cook pasta separately for best leftovers. Reduce base so it stays thick when reheated.
