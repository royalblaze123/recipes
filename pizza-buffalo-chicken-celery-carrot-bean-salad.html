Buffalo Chicken Pizza with Celery-Carrot Bean Salad

Prep: 24 min   Cook: 18 min   Total: 42 min

Ingredients

  • 1 pizza dough ball
  • 0.25 jar no-salt-added pizza sauce
  • 5 oz reduced-fat mozzarella cheese
  • 14 oz chicken breast
  • 1 celery bunch
  • 1 bag baby carrots
  • 0.5 can no-salt-added cannellini beans
  • 1 lemon
  • olive oil
  • hot sauce
  • garlic powder
  • black pepper
  • salt substitute
  • vinegar

Hard rules enforced: Dinner calories (Frank 600–650), sat fat ≤8g, sodium ≤700mg, fiber ≥10g, added sugar ≤6g. Quantities appear before items; fresh meat/poultry/fish use weights. Condiments/spices/seasonings are listed name-only (no tsp/tbsp in Ingredients); all measurements are in Steps. No olives, no squash, no Brussels sprouts, no zucchini, no yogurt in dinners.

Step-by-step Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 475°F.
  2. Chicken: season chicken breast with 2 tsp garlic powder + 1 tsp black pepper + 2 tsp salt substitute. Cook in a skillet with 2 tsp olive oil 6–7 minutes per side to 165°F. Slice thin.
  3. Buffalo drizzle: mix 2 tbsp hot sauce + 1 tbsp vinegar + 1 tsp olive oil + black pepper. (Keep it thin; drizzle after baking.)
  4. Shape dough into 12-inch pizza. Spread 1/4 jar sauce thinly. Add mozzarella and chicken. Bake 10–12 minutes. Drizzle lightly with buffalo mix. Cut into 10 slices.
  5. Bean crunch salad: drain and rinse 1/2 can cannellini beans. Dice 2 celery stalks and slice carrots. Toss with 2 tsp olive oil + 2 tsp lemon juice + 1 tsp vinegar + black pepper + salt substitute.
  6. Portion: Frank 4 slices + ~2 cups salad. Susan/Elise 3 slices + ~1 1/2 cups each.
