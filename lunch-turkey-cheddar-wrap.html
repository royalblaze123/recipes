Turkey Cheddar Grab-and-Go Wrap

Whole-grain turkey and cheddar wraps that can be eaten cold or lightly warmed in the microwave.

Ingredients

  • 3 whole-grain tortillas (8-inch)
  • 9 oz sliced low-sodium turkey breast
  • 3 slices reduced-fat cheddar cheese
  • 1 cup shredded romaine lettuce
  • 3 tbsp light mayonnaise or Greek yogurt
  • 1/4 tsp black pepper

Instructions

  1. Lay the tortillas flat on a cutting board.
  2. Spread 1 tbsp of light mayonnaise or Greek yogurt over each tortilla.
  3. Layer turkey slices and one slice of cheddar on each tortilla.
  4. Top with shredded romaine and a pinch of black pepper.
  5. Roll each tortilla tightly into a wrap, then wrap in foil or place in containers.
  6. Eat cold, or unwrap and microwave for 20â€“30 seconds to slightly warm before serving.

Serves: 3 servings
