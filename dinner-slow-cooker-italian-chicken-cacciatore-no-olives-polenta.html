Italian Chicken Cacciatore (No Olives) + Polenta

Nutrition (approx)

Frank: 645 kcal • P 58g • C 60g • F 16g
Susan/Elise (each): 484 kcal • P 44g • C 45g • F 12g

Ingredients (purchase-clean)

  • 1.75 lb boneless skinless chicken breast
  • 2 cans no-salt-added diced tomatoes
  • 1 each onion
  • 1 each bell pepper
  • 1 tube polenta
  • low-sodium chicken broth
  • kosher salt
  • black pepper
  • garlic powder
  • onion powder
  • smoked paprika
  • Italian seasoning
  • ground cumin
  • chili powder
  • red pepper flakes
  • extra-virgin olive oil
  • low-sodium chicken broth
  • low-sodium beef broth
  • low-sodium soy sauce
  • hot sauce
  • Dijon mustard
  • balsamic vinegar
  • apple cider vinegar
  • cornstarch

Steps (slow cooker)

  1. Add onion + bell pepper + tomatoes (2x 14.5 oz) + 1/2 cup broth.
  2. Nestle chicken; season (Italian seasoning, garlic powder, pepper, red pepper flakes; modest salt).
  3. LOW 4–5 hr or HIGH 2–3 hr to 165°F.
  4. Remove chicken; reduce sauce HIGH lid ajar 15–20 min.
  5. Polenta: slice rounds; sear 3–4 min/side OR bake 425°F 18–20 min.
  6. Serve: chicken + polenta + thick sauce per Notes.

Notes

Cacciatore comfort without olives; polenta makes it a full plate. Portions (per person): Frank: 7 oz chicken + 4 polenta rounds + 1 cup sauce/veg Susan: 5 oz chicken + 3 rounds + 3/4 cup sauce/veg Elise: 5 oz chicken + 3 rounds + 3/4 cup sauce/veg Tips (recipe-specific): Reduce sauce briefly so leftovers don’t turn watery. For firmer peppers, add pepper for last 60 min on LOW.
