Baked Cod Red Enchilada Bake (No-Roll)

Nutrition (approx)

Frank: 635 kcal • P 58g • C 48g • F 19g
Susan/Elise (each): 476 kcal • P 44g • C 36g • F 14g

Ingredients (purchase-clean)

  • 1.50 lb cod fillets
  • 1 package corn tortillas
  • 1 bag reduced-fat shredded Mexican cheese blend
  • 1 each avocado
  • 1/2 jar salsa
  • 1 can enchilada sauce
  • 1 bag frozen fajita-style pepper blend
  • 6 tsp extra-virgin olive oil
  • 2 tsp chili powder
  • 2 tsp cumin
  • 2 tsp garlic powder
  • 1 tsp smoked paprika
  • 1 tsp black pepper
  • 1 tsp kosher salt

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 400°F.
  2. Fish: pat cod dry. Rub with 2 tsp olive oil and season with the spices.
  3. Bake fish at 425°F for 10–12 minutes until 145°F and flaky. Rest 3 minutes, then break into large flakes.
  4. Veg: cook frozen fajita pepper blend with 4 tsp olive oil 6–8 minutes until browned.
  5. Combine: fold flaked cod into the peppers and warm 1 minute.
  6. Sauce: can size note—use a standard 10 oz can red enchilada sauce. Use all sauce for layering.
  7. Assemble: spread 1/2 cup sauce in the bottom of a 9x13 dish. Layer 1/2 tortillas, then 1/2 filling, then 1/3 cheese. Repeat. Top with remaining sauce and last 1/3 cheese.
  8. Bake 16–20 minutes until bubbling. Rest 10 minutes before slicing.
  9. Toppings: dice avocado and serve salsa on the side. Top each serving with measured avocado and salsa.

Notes

Fish enchilada bake: flaky cod + peppers under red sauce, finished with avocado + salsa. Portions (per person): Frank: 1/3 of the casserole + 1/3 of the avocado + salsa topping Susan: 1/4 of the casserole + 1/4 of the avocado + salsa topping Elise: 1/4 of the casserole + 1/4 of the avocado + salsa topping
