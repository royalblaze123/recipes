French Toast Plates + Turkey + Berries

Nutrition (approx)

Frank: 645 kcal • P 55g • C 75g • F 14g • Sat 4g • Na 650mg • Fiber 10g
Susan/Elise (each): 484 kcal • P 41g • C 56g • F 10g • Sat 3g • Na 488mg • Fiber 8g

Ingredients (purchase-clean)

  • 9 slices whole-wheat sandwich bread
  • 6 each eggs
  • 1 carton skim milk
  • 12 slices deli turkey
  • 1 pint blueberries
  • kosher salt
  • black pepper
  • garlic powder
  • onion powder
  • smoked paprika
  • Italian seasoning
  • hot sauce
  • Dijon mustard
  • extra-virgin olive oil
  • salsa
  • cinnamon
  • sugar-free syrup

Steps

  1. Whisk custard: 6 eggs + 1/2 cup skim milk + cinnamon.
  2. Dip bread quickly and cook in nonstick skillet 2–3 minutes per side.
  3. Warm turkey slices in the pan 30 seconds per side.
  4. Serve with blueberries and measured sugar-free syrup per Notes.

Notes

French toast comfort with protein sides and fruit, without heavy butter/sugar. Portions (per person): Frank: 3 slices french toast + 4 slices turkey + 1 cup blueberries Susan: 2 slices french toast + 4 slices turkey + 3/4 cup blueberries Elise: 2 slices french toast + 4 slices turkey + 3/4 cup blueberries Tips (recipe-specific): Quick dip prevents soggy toast. Use cinnamon + berries for sweetness; keep syrup measured.
