Turkey Meatball Marinara Pizza + Caesar-Style Salad

Thin-crust whole-wheat pizza with turkey meatballs and marinara flavors, served with a creamy Greek-yogurt Caesar-style salad.

Servings

3 servings (Frank, Susan, Elise).

Times

  • Prep time: 35 minutes
  • Cook time: 15 minutes
  • Total time: 50 minutes

Ingredients

Pizza

  • 1 dough ball whole wheat pizza dough
  • 1 can no-salt-added crushed tomatoes
  • 1 package reduced-fat mozzarella
  • 1 lb lean ground turkey
  • 1 package mushrooms
  • 1 tsp garlic powder
  • 1 tsp dried oregano
  • 1 tsp dried basil
  • 1 tsp ground black pepper

Side salad

  • 2 hearts romaine hearts
  • 1 cup nonfat Greek yogurt
  • 2 tbsp extra-virgin olive oil
  • 2 tbsp apple cider vinegar
  • 1 tsp garlic powder
  • 1 tsp ground black pepper

Step-by-step Instructions

  1. Thaw and warm dough: Thaw 1 dough ball overnight in the refrigerator. Bring to room temperature 60–90 minutes.
  2. Preheat: Place a pizza stone/steel or inverted sheet pan in the oven and preheat to 500°F for 30 minutes.
  3. Make meatballs: Mix ground turkey with 1/2 tsp garlic powder, 1/2 tsp oregano, 1/2 tsp basil, and black pepper. Form into small 1-inch meatballs and bake at 425°F for 10–12 minutes until 165°F.
  4. Make sauce: Measure 1 1/2 cups crushed tomatoes into a bowl and stir in 1/2 tsp oregano and 1/2 tsp basil. Refrigerate remaining tomatoes.
  5. Prep mushrooms: Slice 4 oz mushrooms for the pizza; reseal remaining mushrooms.
  6. Stretch and top: Stretch dough into a thin 10–12 inch round on parchment. Spread sauce. Sprinkle 6 oz mozzarella (save remaining). Add baked meatballs and mushrooms.
  7. Bake: Slide pizza (on parchment) onto the hot surface. Bake 8–12 minutes until browned and bubbling.
  8. Make salad dressing: Whisk 1/2 cup Greek yogurt with 1 tbsp olive oil, 1 tbsp vinegar, 1/2 tsp garlic powder, and black pepper. Refrigerate remaining yogurt.
  9. Side salad: Chop romaine, toss with dressing, and portion per person.
  10. Portion dinner: Cut pizza into 10 slices and serve with salad.

Portion Sizes

Portions (per person):
Pizza (1 dough ball): cut into 10 slices.
Frank: 4 slices pizza + 1 generous bowl Caesar-style romaine salad

Susan: 3 slices pizza + 3/4 bowl salad

Elise: 3 slices pizza + 3/4 bowl salad
