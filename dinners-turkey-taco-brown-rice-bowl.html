Turkey Taco Brown Rice Bowls

Lean turkey taco bowls with brown rice, black beans, and veggies for a fast, taco-night style dinner that fits Frank Household targets.

Servings

3 servings.

Times

  • Prep time: 10 minutes
  • Cook time: 18 minutes
  • Total time: 28 minutes

Ingredients

  • 1 cup cooked brown rice
  • 1 lb 93% lean ground turkey
  • 3/4 cup canned black beans
  • 1 cup canned no-salt-added diced tomatoes
  • 3/4 cup frozen corn
  • 1 cup chopped bell peppers
  • 1/2 cup chopped onion
  • 1 tbsp extra-virgin olive oil
  • 6 tbsp 2% plain Greek yogurt
  • 2 tbsp water
  • 1 1/2 tsp chili powder
  • 1 tsp ground cumin
  • 1/2 tsp smoked paprika or sweet paprika
  • 1/2 tsp garlic powder
  • 1/4 tsp dried oregano
  • 1/4 tsp ground black pepper
  • 1/4 tsp kosher or sea salt

Preparation Steps

  1. Cook the brown rice if not already prepared. Use leftover brown rice, cook 1/3 cup dry brown rice according to package directions, or heat a microwave-ready pouch. You need about 1 cup cooked rice total. Set aside.
  2. Heat a large nonstick skillet over medium-high heat. Add the olive oil and the ground turkey. Cook, breaking the turkey into small crumbles, for 5–6 minutes until mostly browned.
  3. Add the chopped onion and bell peppers to the skillet. Cook for 3–4 minutes, stirring occasionally, until the vegetables begin to soften and the turkey is fully cooked (165°F internal).
  4. Stir in the chili powder, ground cumin, smoked or sweet paprika, garlic powder, dried oregano, black pepper, and salt. Cook for 1 minute, stirring, to bloom the spices.
  5. Add the drained diced tomatoes, black beans, corn, and 2 tbsp water to the skillet. Stir well and reduce the heat to medium-low. Simmer for 3–4 minutes, stirring occasionally, until everything is heated through and most of the liquid is absorbed.
  6. In a small bowl, stir the Greek yogurt until smooth. If you prefer a thinner topping, you can add a teaspoon or two of water and mix until spoonable.
  7. Add about 1/2 cup cooked brown rice to a large bowl for Frank. Top with about 2 to 2 1/4 cups of the turkey and vegetable mixture, making sure his portion includes roughly 6 oz cooked turkey, 1/3 cup black beans, and a generous amount of peppers, tomatoes, and corn. Spoon about 2 tbsp Greek yogurt over the top.
  8. Divide the remaining rice between two bowls for Susan and Elise, about 1/3 cup each. Top each with about 1 1/2 to 1 3/4 cups of the turkey mixture, ensuring each portion has about 4 1/2 oz turkey and 1/4 cup black beans. Spoon about 2 tbsp Greek yogurt over each bowl.
  9. Serve immediately. If packing for later, keep the Greek yogurt in a small container and add just before eating.

  • Frank (F · 1.0×): ~2 to 2 1/4 cups turkey mixture over 1/2 cup cooked brown rice with ~6 oz cooked lean turkey, ~1/3 cup black beans, ~1 cup vegetables, and ~2 tbsp Greek yogurt.
  • Susan (S · 0.75×): ~1 1/2 to 1 3/4 cups turkey mixture over 1/3 cup rice with ~4 1/2 oz turkey, ~1/4 cup black beans, ~3/4 cup vegetables, and ~2 tbsp Greek yogurt.
  • Elise (E · 0.75×): ~1 1/2 to 1 3/4 cups turkey mixture over 1/3 cup rice with ~4 1/2 oz turkey, ~1/4 cup black beans, ~3/4 cup vegetables, and ~2 tbsp Greek yogurt.
