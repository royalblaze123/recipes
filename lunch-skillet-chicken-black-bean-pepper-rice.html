Warm Lunch Skillet: Chicken, Black Beans, Peppers, and Brown Rice

Prep: 16 min   Cook: 14 min   Total: 30 min

Ingredients

  • 1.1 lb chicken breast
  • 0.5 cup dry brown rice
  • 0.5 can no-salt-added black beans
  • 1 bell pepper
  • 1 onion
  • 1 lime
  • olive oil
  • cumin
  • chili powder
  • smoked paprika
  • garlic powder
  • black pepper
  • salt substitute
  • salsa

Rules enforced: Clean AnyList Ingredients (quantities before items; fresh meat/poultry/fish use weights; packaged items use fractional containers when partial). Condiments/spices/seasonings have no measurements in Ingredients; all measurements are in Steps. No olives, no squash, no Brussels sprouts, no zucchini, no tofu/tempeh. Lunch validations: Frank calories 500–550, sat fat ≤6g, sodium ≤650mg, fiber ≥8g, added sugar ≤6g.

Step-by-step Instructions

  1. Cook rice: cook 1/2 cup dry brown rice with 1 cup water; simmer 35–40 minutes; rest 5 minutes.
  2. Chicken: dice 1.1 lb chicken breast. Season with 2 tsp cumin + 1 tsp chili powder + 1/2 tsp smoked paprika + 1/2 tsp garlic powder + 1/2 tsp black pepper + 1/2 tsp salt substitute.
  3. Sauté chicken with 2 tsp olive oil 6–8 minutes until 165°F.
  4. Add sliced 1 bell pepper and 1 onion; cook 4 minutes.
  5. Beans: drain and rinse 1/2 can black beans; stir in with 2 tbsp water; warm 2 minutes.
  6. Stir in cooked rice and heat 2 minutes.
  7. Finish: add salsa measured (Frank 1 tbsp; Susan 2 tsp; Elise 2 tsp) + 1 tsp lime juice per plate.
