Chicken + Lentil Skillet Bowls

Nutrition (approx)

Frank: 630 kcal • P 58g • C 60g • F 16g • Sat 4g • Na 670mg • Fiber 16g
Susan/Elise (each): 472 kcal • P 44g • C 45g • F 12g • Sat 3g • Na 502mg • Fiber 12g

Ingredients (purchase-clean)

  • 1.50 lb boneless skinless chicken breast
  • 1 cup dry brown lentils
  • 1 bag frozen fajita-style pepper blend
  • 1 each avocado
  • 1/2 jar salsa
  • 1 each lime
  • kosher salt
  • black pepper
  • chili powder
  • ground cumin
  • garlic powder
  • smoked paprika
  • extra-virgin olive oil

Steps

  1. Lentils: rinse 1 cup dry brown lentils. Simmer in 3 cups water 18–22 minutes until tender. Drain well.
  2. Chicken: cook 1.50 lb diced chicken in a skillet with 2 tsp olive oil 8–10 minutes to 165°F.
  3. Season with 2 tsp chili powder, 2 tsp cumin, 2 tsp garlic powder, 1/2 tsp kosher salt, 1/2 tsp black pepper.
  4. Add frozen fajita pepper blend; cook 6–8 minutes until browned.
  5. Stir in cooked lentils + 1/2 cup salsa; simmer 2 minutes. Finish with 2 tsp lime juice.
  6. Bowls: divide into bowls. Top with salsa and avocado. Portion per Notes.

Notes

High-fiber comfort bowls that still feel like a big, saucy skillet meal. Portions (per person): Frank: 1/3 skillet mix + 1/3 avocado + salsa Susan: 1/4 skillet mix + 1/4 avocado + salsa Elise: 1/4 skillet mix + 1/4 avocado + salsa Tips (recipe-specific): Drain lentils well so the skillet stays thick, not watery. Salsa at the end keeps it tasting fresh after reheating.
