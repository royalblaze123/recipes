Roast Beef Horseradish Provolone Sandwich + Broccoli Slaw

Roast beef with a creamy horseradish-yogurt spread and provolone, paired with a crunchy broccoli slaw side.

Servings

3 servings (Frank, Susan, Elise).

Times

  • Prep time: 15 minutes
  • Cook time: 0 minutes
  • Total time: 15 minutes

Ingredients

Sandwich

  • 6 slices whole wheat bread
  • 12 slices deli roast beef
  • 3 slices reduced-fat provolone cheese
  • 1 cup nonfat Greek yogurt
  • 1 each lemon
  • 1 each garlic clove

Side

  • 1 bag broccoli slaw
  • 1 each apple

Step-by-step Instructions

  1. Deli guidance: Ask for roast beef sliced medium-thin for sandwiches (about ~2 mm).
  2. Make the spread: Mix 1/2 cup Greek yogurt with 1 tsp lemon juice and a small grated garlic clove. Stir in prepared horseradish if you choose to add it (optional at serving).
  3. Assemble sandwiches: Spread yogurt on bread. Portion roast beef and provolone per person (below). Close into 3 full sandwiches.
  4. Make slaw side: Toss broccoli slaw with 2 tbsp of the yogurt spread (or plain yogurt if you prefer). Portion per person. Slice apple and portion as listed (pack apple separately so it stays crisp).

Portion Sizes

Portions (per person):

Frank: 2 slices bread, 3 slices roast beef, 1 slice provolone, 2 tbsp yogurt spread, 1 cup broccoli slaw, 1/2 apple

Susan: 2 slices bread, 2 slices roast beef, 3/4 slice provolone, 1 1/2 tbsp yogurt spread, 3/4 cup broccoli slaw, 1/4 apple

Elise: 2 slices bread, 2 slices roast beef, 3/4 slice provolone, 1 1/2 tbsp yogurt spread, 3/4 cup broccoli slaw, 1/4 apple
