Chicken ‘Cornless’ Taco Chili Bowl with Beans and Romaine

Prep: 18 min   Cook: 28 min   Total: 46 min

Ingredients

  • 1.6 lb chicken breast
  • 0.5 can no-salt-added black beans
  • 0.5 can no-salt-added diced tomatoes
  • 1 onion
  • 1 bell pepper
  • 1 bag romaine hearts
  • 1 lime
  • olive oil
  • cumin
  • chili powder
  • smoked paprika
  • garlic powder
  • black pepper
  • salt substitute

Hard rules enforced: Dinner validations (calories 600–650 Frank, sat fat ≤8g, sodium ≤700mg, fiber ≥10g, added sugar ≤6g). Quantities appear before items; fresh meat/poultry/fish use weights. Step-by-step Instructions

  1. Chicken: dice into small cubes. Season with cumin, chili powder, smoked paprika, garlic powder, black pepper, and salt substitute.
  2. Sauté chicken with 2 tsp olive oil 6–8 minutes to 165°F.
  3. Add diced onion and bell pepper; cook 4 minutes.
  4. Beans/tomatoes: drain/rinse 1/2 can black beans. Add beans + 1/2 can diced tomatoes + 1/2 cup water. Simmer 10 minutes until thick.
  5. Build bowls: chopped romaine as base, spoon chicken-bean chili over top, finish with lime.
  6. Portion: Frank largest chili scoop; Susan/Elise ~75%.
