One-Pan Salmon + Sweet Potato + Broccoli

Nutrition (approx)

Frank: 645 kcal • P 55g • C 50g • F 20g • Sat 5g • Na 650mg • Fiber 10g
Susan/Elise (each): 484 kcal • P 41g • C 38g • F 15g • Sat 4g • Na 488mg • Fiber 8g

Ingredients (purchase-clean)

  • 1.25 lb salmon fillets
  • 2.00 lb sweet potatoes
  • 1 bag frozen broccoli florets
  • 1 each lemon
  • kosher salt
  • black pepper
  • garlic powder
  • onion powder
  • smoked paprika
  • Italian seasoning
  • ground cumin
  • chili powder
  • red pepper flakes
  • extra-virgin olive oil
  • low-sodium chicken broth

Steps

  1. Oven: heat to 425°F. Line a sheet pan.
  2. Sweet potatoes: cube. Toss with 2 tsp olive oil, 1 tsp smoked paprika, 1 tsp garlic powder, 1/2 tsp kosher salt, 1/2 tsp black pepper. Roast 18 minutes.
  3. Broccoli: add frozen broccoli tossed with 2 tsp olive oil and black pepper. Roast 10 minutes.
  4. Salmon: season salmon with 1 tsp garlic powder, 1 tsp Italian seasoning, black pepper, and 2 tsp lemon juice.
  5. Add salmon to pan; roast 8–10 minutes until flaky.
  6. Finish: squeeze lemon over everything. Serve per Notes.

Notes

High-flavor sheet-pan salmon with sweet potatoes back in the rotation. Portions (per person): Frank: About 6 oz salmon + 1 cup sweet potato + 1 cup broccoli Susan: About 4 oz salmon + 3/4 cup sweet potato + 3/4 cup broccoli Elise: About 4 oz salmon + 3/4 cup sweet potato + 3/4 cup broccoli Tips (recipe-specific): Roast sweet potatoes first so they caramelize. Lemon at the end makes salmon taste brighter without extra salt.
