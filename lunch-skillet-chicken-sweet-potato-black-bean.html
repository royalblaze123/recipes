Warm Lunch Skillet: Chicken, Sweet Potato, and Black Beans

Prep: 18 min   Cook: 20 min   Total: 38 min

Ingredients

  • 1.1 lb chicken breast
  • 1.25 lb sweet potatoes
  • 0.5 can no-salt-added black beans
  • 1 onion
  • 1 lime
  • olive oil
  • cumin
  • smoked paprika
  • garlic powder
  • black pepper
  • salt substitute
  • salsa

Rules enforced: Clean AnyList Ingredients (quantities before items; fresh meat/poultry/fish use weights; packaged items use fractional containers when partial). Condiments/spices/seasonings have no measurements in Ingredients; all measurements are in Steps. No olives, no squash, no Brussels sprouts, no zucchini, no tofu/tempeh. Lunch validations: Frank calories 500–550, sat fat ≤6g, sodium ≤650mg, fiber ≥8g, added sugar ≤6g.

Step-by-step Instructions

  1. Sweet potatoes: dice 1.25 lb sweet potatoes into 1/2-inch cubes. Microwave with 2 tbsp water, covered, 5 minutes to par-cook; drain.
  2. Chicken: dice 1.1 lb chicken breast. Season with 2 tsp cumin + 1 tsp smoked paprika + 1/2 tsp garlic powder + 1/2 tsp black pepper + 1/2 tsp salt substitute.
  3. Sauté chicken with 2 tsp olive oil 6–8 minutes until 165°F.
  4. Add diced 1 onion and par-cooked sweet potato; cook 8–10 minutes until browned and tender.
  5. Beans: drain and rinse 1/2 can black beans; stir in with 2 tbsp water; warm 2 minutes.
  6. Finish: salsa measured (Frank 1 tbsp; Susan 2 tsp; Elise 2 tsp) + 1 tsp lime juice per plate.
  7. Reheat tip: microwave 1–2 minutes; add salsa after reheating.
