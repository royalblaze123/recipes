Air Fryer BBQ Chicken + Sweet Potatoes + Broccoli

Nutrition (approx)

Frank: 650 kcal • P 60g • C 55g • F 18g • Sat 3g • Na 700mg • Fiber 10g
Susan/Elise (each): 488 kcal • P 45g • C 41g • F 14g • Sat 2g • Na 525mg • Fiber 8g

Ingredients (purchase-clean)

  • 1.75 lb boneless skinless chicken breast
  • 2.00 lb sweet potatoes
  • 1 bag frozen broccoli florets
  • low-sugar BBQ sauce

Steps (Cosori dual-basket)

  1. Preheat Cosori to 390°F. Basket A: sweet potatoes. Basket B: chicken later. Use Sync Finish.
  2. Sweet potatoes (Basket A): cube sweet potatoes. Toss with 2 tsp olive oil, 1 tsp smoked paprika, 1 tsp garlic powder, 1/2 tsp kosher salt, black pepper. Air fry 18–22 minutes at 390°F with Shake Reminder.
  3. Chicken: season with 1 tsp garlic powder, 1 tsp smoked paprika, 1/2 tsp kosher salt, and black pepper. Air fry 10–12 minutes at 390°F to 165°F.
  4. BBQ glaze: brush chicken with 3 tbsp low-sugar BBQ sauce total (measured). Return to air fryer 1 minute to tack up.
  5. Broccoli: microwave, drain and pat dry. Toss with 2 tsp olive oil, 1 tsp garlic powder, 1/2 tsp kosher salt, black pepper. Crisp 6 minutes at 400°F if space or roast 425°F for 10–12 minutes.
  6. Serve per Notes.

Notes

BBQ flavor without BBQ calories: glaze is measured and tacked on at the end. Portions (per person): Frank: 7 oz chicken + 1 cup sweet potato + 1 cup broccoli Susan: 5 oz protein + 1 cup sweet potato + 3/4 cup veg Elise: 5 oz protein + 1 cup sweet potato + 3/4 cup veg Tips (recipe-specific): Glaze at the end so you use less sauce. Pat broccoli dry so it actually crisps.
