Italian Turkey Vegetable Soup (Thick, Stew-Like)

Nutrition (approx)

Frank: 615 kcal • P 58g • C 45g • F 18g • Sat 5g • Na 680mg • Fiber 12g
Susan/Elise (each): 461 kcal • P 44g • C 34g • F 14g • Sat 4g • Na 510mg • Fiber 9g

Ingredients (purchase-clean)

  • 1.75 lb 93% lean ground turkey
  • 1 can cannellini beans
  • 1 can no-salt-added diced tomatoes
  • 1 bag frozen mixed vegetables
  • 1 carton low-sodium chicken broth
  • 1 each onion
  • extra-virgin olive oil
  • kosher salt
  • black pepper
  • chili powder
  • ground cumin
  • garlic powder
  • onion powder
  • smoked paprika
  • Italian seasoning
  • crushed red pepper
  • bay leaves
  • Italian seasoning
  • bay leaves

Steps

  1. Pot: heat 2 tsp olive oil. Cook 1 diced onion 6–8 minutes until browned.
  2. Add turkey; cook 7–9 minutes until browned.
  3. Season with Italian seasoning, garlic powder, onion powder, kosher salt, black pepper.
  4. Tomatoes: Can size note—use a standard 14.5 oz can no-salt-added diced tomatoes.. Add 1 can.
  5. Beans: Can size note—use a standard 15 oz can cannellini beans.. Drain/rinse and add.
  6. Veg: add 3 cups frozen mixed vegetables.
  7. Add 2 cups low-sodium broth. Simmer uncovered 15–18 minutes until thick and stew-like.
  8. Optional: add 1 bay leaf during simmer; remove before serving.
  9. Portion per Notes.

Notes

Cozy Italian-style thick soup—like minestrone without pasta overload. Portions (per person): Frank: About 2 1/2 cups soup Susan: About 2 cups soup Elise: About 2 cups soup Tips (recipe-specific): Uncovered simmer keeps it stew-like instead of watery. If you miss pasta, serve with a small measured tortilla or bread on the side (optional).
