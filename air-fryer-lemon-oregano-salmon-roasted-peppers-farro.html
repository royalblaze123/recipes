Air Fryer Lemon-Oregano Salmon with Roasted Peppers and Quick Farro

Prep: 12 min   Cook: 24 min   Total: 36 min

Ingredients

  • 1.25 lb salmon fillets
  • 3 bell peppers
  • 1 red onion
  • 1 pint cherry tomatoes
  • 1 pouch microwave farro
  • 1 lemon
  • olive oil
  • oregano
  • garlic powder
  • black pepper
  • salt substitute
  • balsamic vinegar
  • 1 bag microgreens

RBM rules: quantities appear before items; fresh proteins use weight. Condiments/spices/seasonings are listed name-only; all measurements are in Steps.

Step-by-step Instructions

  1. Heat oven to 425°F and line a sheet pan. Slice bell peppers into strips and red onion into wedges.
  2. Toss peppers + onion with 10 tsp olive oil + 3 tsp oregano + 2 tsp garlic powder + 2 tsp salt substitute + 1 tsp black pepper. Roast 18–22 minutes, tossing once.
  3. Add cherry tomatoes for the last 6 minutes to blister.
  4. Season salmon: pat dry. Rub with 4 tsp olive oil + zest of 1 lemon + 2 tsp oregano + 1 tsp black pepper.
  5. Air fry salmon (Cosori): preheat 390°F for 3 minutes. Cook skin-side down 8–11 minutes until flaky and cooked to preference.
  6. Heat farro pouch per package directions; finish with 4 tsp balsamic vinegar + 1 tsp black pepper.
  7. Portion: Frank gets 6 oz salmon + ~3/4 cup farro + ~2 cups roasted veg + microgreens (optional). Susan and Elise each get ~4.5 oz salmon + ~1/2 cup farro + ~1 1/2 cups veg.
