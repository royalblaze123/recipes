Air Fryer Lemon-Oregano Salmon with Roasted Peppers and Quick Farro

Prep: 12 min   Cook: 24 min   Total: 36 min

Ingredients

  • 1 package salmon fillets (~1–1.25 lb total)
  • 1 bag bell peppers
  • 1 bag red onions
  • 1 container cherry tomatoes
  • 1 bag microgreens (optional)
  • 1 pouch microwave farro (or whole grain blend)
  • 1 lemon
  • olive oil (tsp) — pantry
  • oregano (tsp) — pantry
  • garlic powder (tsp) — pantry
  • black pepper (tsp) — pantry
  • salt substitute (tsp) — pantry
  • balsamic vinegar (tsp) — pantry

Ingredient list is purchase-clean (AnyList aggregation). All measuring/used amounts and remainder handling are in Steps.

Step-by-step Instructions

  1. Heat oven to 425°F and line a sheet pan with parchment. Slice bell peppers into strips and red onion into wedges; keep pieces fairly uniform.
  2. Measure olive oil: use 14 tsp total for this recipe. Toss peppers + onions with 10 tsp olive oil + 3 tsp oregano + 2 tsp garlic powder + 2 tsp salt substitute + 1 tsp black pepper. Roast 18–22 minutes, tossing once, until edges char slightly.
  3. Add cherry tomatoes to the pan for the last 6 minutes (so they blister but don’t collapse fully).
  4. Season salmon: pat dry. Mix 4 tsp olive oil + zest of 1 lemon + 2 tsp oregano + 1 tsp black pepper. Rub over salmon.
  5. Air fry salmon (Cosori): preheat 390°F for 3 minutes. Cook skin-side down 8–11 minutes until flaky.
  6. Heat farro pouch per package directions. Finish farro with 4 tsp balsamic vinegar and 1 tsp black pepper.
  7. Portion: Frank gets 6 oz salmon + ~3/4 cup farro + ~2 cups roasted peppers/onions/tomatoes + microgreens on top if using. Susan and Elise each get ~4.5 oz salmon + ~1/2 cup farro + ~1 1/2 cups veg.
