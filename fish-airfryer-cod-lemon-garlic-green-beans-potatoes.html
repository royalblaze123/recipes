Air Fryer Lemon-Garlic Cod with Green Beans and Baby Potatoes

Prep: 16 min   Cook: 24 min   Total: 40 min

Ingredients

  • 1.5 lb cod fillets
  • 1 lb green beans
  • 1.25 lb baby potatoes
  • olive oil
  • lemon
  • garlic
  • black pepper
  • salt substitute
  • Italian seasoning

Hard rules enforced: Dinner validations (calories 600–650 Frank, sat fat ≤8g, sodium ≤700mg, fiber ≥10g, added sugar ≤6g). Quantities appear before items; fresh meat/poultry/fish use weights. Condiments/spices/seasonings have no measurements in Ingredients; all measurements are in Steps. No olives, no squash, no Brussels sprouts, no zucchini, no tofu/tempeh, no yogurt in dinners.

Step-by-step Instructions

  1. Potatoes: halve 1.25 lb baby potatoes. Toss with 2 tsp olive oil + 1 tsp Italian seasoning + 1/2 tsp black pepper + 1/2 tsp salt substitute.
  2. Air fry potatoes at 400°F for 14 minutes, shaking at 7 minutes.
  3. Green beans: toss 1 lb green beans with 1 tsp olive oil + 1/4 tsp black pepper + 1/4 tsp salt substitute. Add to basket and air fry 6 minutes.
  4. Cod: pat dry 1.5 lb cod. Mix a brush-on: 1 tsp olive oil + 2 tsp lemon juice + 1 tsp minced garlic + 1/2 tsp Italian seasoning + 1/4 tsp black pepper + 1/4 tsp salt substitute.
  5. Add cod and air fry at 400°F for 8–10 minutes until cod flakes easily.
  6. Finish: squeeze 1 tsp lemon juice per plate.
  7. Portion: Frank largest cod/potato share; Susan/Elise ~75%.
