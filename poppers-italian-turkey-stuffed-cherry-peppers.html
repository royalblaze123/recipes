Italian Turkey Stuffed Cherry Peppers

Sheet Pan • Yield: 24 stuffed peppers

Ingredients (purchase-clean)

  • 24 each fresh cherry peppers
  • 16 oz fresh 93% lean ground turkey
  • 4 oz 1% cottage cheese
  • 2 oz grated parmesan
  • 2 tsp fennel seed
  • 2 tsp garlic powder
  • kosher salt
  • black pepper

Steps

  1. Heat oven to 425°F. Line a sheet pan with parchment.
  2. Core 24 cherry peppers (slice tops, remove seeds).
  3. Brown turkey with 2 tsp fennel seed, 2 tsp garlic powder, 1 tsp kosher salt, and 1/2 tsp black pepper for 7–9 min; cool 5 min.
  4. Blend 4 oz cottage cheese until smooth; stir into turkey with 2 oz parmesan.
  5. Stuff peppers; stand upright (muffin tin helps).
  6. Bake 16–20 min until peppers are tender and filling is hot.

Notes

Flavor-forward holiday poppers designed for snacking or a light meal. Portions (guidance): Flavor reads like ‘Italian sausage’ without the fat. Snack portion: 3–5 stuffed peppers.
