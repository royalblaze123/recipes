White Chicken Chili (No Cream, Still Thick)

Nutrition (approx)

Frank: 615 kcal • P 60g • C 42g • F 16g • Sat 4g • Na 670mg • Fiber 12g
Susan/Elise (each): 461 kcal • P 45g • C 32g • F 12g • Sat 3g • Na 502mg • Fiber 9g

Ingredients (purchase-clean)

  • 1.75 lb boneless skinless chicken breast
  • 1 can cannellini beans
  • 1 can diced green chiles
  • 1 each onion
  • 1 carton low-sodium chicken broth
  • extra-virgin olive oil
  • kosher salt
  • black pepper
  • chili powder
  • ground cumin
  • garlic powder
  • onion powder
  • smoked paprika
  • Italian seasoning
  • crushed red pepper
  • bay leaves
  • oregano

Steps

  1. Pot: heat 2 tsp olive oil. Add 1 diced onion; cook 6–8 minutes until softened and lightly browned.
  2. Chicken: add 1.75 lb diced chicken; cook 8–10 minutes until 165°F.
  3. Season with cumin, garlic powder, onion powder, oregano, kosher salt, and black pepper.
  4. Chiles: Can size note—use a standard 4 oz can diced green chiles.. Add 1 can.
  5. Beans: Can size note—use a standard 15 oz can cannellini beans.. Drain/rinse. Add beans.
  6. Add 2 cups low-sodium broth. Simmer uncovered 15–18 minutes.
  7. Thicken: mash 1 cup beans against the pot wall with a spoon, then stir back in.
  8. Portion per Notes.

Notes

White chicken chili without dairy, thickened by mashed beans. Portions (per person): Frank: About 2 1/2 cups chili Susan: About 2 cups chili Elise: About 2 cups chili Tips (recipe-specific): Mash beans to thicken—no flour, no cream. Add extra green chiles at serving time if you want more kick.
