Open-Face Melt: Chicken Pesto Mozzarella with Roasted Peppers

Prep: 18 min   Cook: 10 min   Total: 28 min

Ingredients

  • 6 slices whole wheat bread
  • 1.0 lb chicken breast
  • 6 slices part-skim mozzarella
  • 0.25 jar roasted red peppers
  • 1 bag spinach
  • olive oil
  • basil pesto
  • black pepper
  • salt substitute
  • garlic powder

Rules enforced: quantities before items; fresh meat/poultry/fish use weights; packaged items use fractional containers when partial. Condiments/spices/seasonings have no measurements in Ingredients; all measurements are in Steps. No olives, no squash, no Brussels sprouts, no zucchini, no tofu/tempeh. Lunch validations included in Notes.

Step-by-step Instructions

  1. Chicken: cook 1.0 lb chicken breast with 2 tsp olive oil, 6–8 minutes until 165°F; rest 3 minutes; slice thin.
  2. Broiler: set to high. Line sheet pan.
  3. Toast bread: broil 1 minute.
  4. Spread: pesto measured in Steps (Frank 2 tsp total; Susan 1.5 tsp; Elise 1.5 tsp).
  5. Layer spinach across bread slices (divide 1 bag). Add chicken slices.
  6. Add roasted red pepper strips (Frank 2 tbsp; Susan 1.5 tbsp; Elise 1.5 tbsp).
  7. Top with mozzarella slices; broil 2–3 minutes until melted; rest 2 minutes.
