Smoky Chipotle Cream Dip

Smoky, creamy dip for meatballs, burgers, tacos, roasted potatoes, and air-fryer proteins.
Servings: 10
Prep: 5 min | Cook: 0 min | Total: 5 min

Ingredients

  • 1 cup 2% Greek yogurt
  • 1/2 cup light mayonnaise
  • 1 can chipotle peppers in adobo
  • 1 lime
  • 2 tsp honey
  • 2 tsp garlic powder
  • 1 tsp cumin
  • 1 tsp smoked paprika
  • 1 tsp black pepper

Steps

  1. Open 1 can chipotle peppers in adobo (typically 7 oz). Remove 1 pepper plus 2 tsp adobo sauce for mild-medium heat (use 2 peppers for hotter). Refrigerate the remaining peppers/sauce in an airtight container for up to 1 week or freeze in small portions.
  2. Zest and juice the lime. Whisk Greek yogurt and light mayonnaise until smooth.
  3. Finely mince the measured chipotle pepper(s) (or blend briefly) and whisk into the yogurt mix with the measured adobo sauce, lime juice (about 2 tsp), lime zest (about 1/2 tsp), honey, garlic powder, cumin, smoked paprika, and black pepper.
  4. Chill 10 minutes if possible. Store refrigerated up to 7 days; stir before serving.

Notes

Optional one-line dish description: Smoky chipotle-lime cream dip with controllable heat. Portions (per person): Frank: 1/3 of recipe (about 1/2 cup) Susan: 1/4 of recipe (about 6 tbsp) Elise: 1/4 of recipe (about 6 tbsp)
