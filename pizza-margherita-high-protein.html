Margherita-Style High-Protein Pizza

NOTES: Built as a full dinner using ready-made whole-grain or thin crust, lean protein, plenty of vegetables, and controlled cheese to support A1c, cholesterol, and blood pressure goals. Use low-sodium sauce and minimal added salt. PORTION SIZES: Frank: ~40% of the pizza; Susan: ~30% of the pizza; Elise: ~30% of the pizza (Frank = 1.0×, Susan/Elise = 0.75× portions).

Ingredients

  • 1 ready-made 12-inch thin whole-wheat pizza crust
  • 1/2 cup no-salt-added tomato or pizza sauce
  • 8 oz cooked boneless, skinless chicken breast, thinly sliced
  • 1 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 3/4 cup shredded part-skim mozzarella cheese
  • 2–3 tbsp grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1/4 cup fresh basil leaves, torn
  • 1 tsp dried oregano
  • Freshly ground black pepper, to taste

Step-by-Step Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 425°F. Place the crust on a pizza pan or parchment-lined baking sheet.
  2. Spread tomato or pizza sauce evenly over the crust.
  3. Arrange sliced chicken evenly over the sauce.
  4. Top with halved cherry tomatoes.
  5. Sprinkle shredded mozzarella and grated Parmesan evenly over the pizza.
  6. Season with dried oregano and a few grinds of black pepper.
  7. Bake 12–15 minutes, until the crust is crisp and the cheese is melted and lightly browned.
  8. Immediately after baking, scatter torn fresh basil leaves over the top.
  9. Cool 3–5 minutes, then slice and portion for Frank (~40%) and Susan/Elise (~30% each).

Nutrition per Person

  • Frank: ~620 kcal, ~55 g protein, ~52 g carbs, ~18 g fat.
  • Susan: ~465 kcal, ~41 g protein, ~39 g carbs, ~13 g fat.
  • Elise: ~465 kcal, ~41 g protein, ~39 g carbs, ~13 g fat.
