One-Pot Chicken Tortilla Soup (No Chips Needed)

Nutrition (approx)

Frank: 640 kcal • P 58g • C 55g • F 16g • Sat 3g • Na 700mg • Fiber 12g
Susan/Elise (each): 480 kcal • P 44g • C 41g • F 12g • Sat 2g • Na 525mg • Fiber 9g

Ingredients (purchase-clean)

  • 1.50 lb boneless skinless chicken breast
  • 1 can black beans
  • 1 can no-salt-added diced tomatoes
  • 1 jar salsa
  • 1 bag frozen mixed vegetables
  • low-sodium chicken broth
  • 3 each whole-wheat tortillas
  • kosher salt
  • black pepper
  • garlic powder
  • onion powder
  • smoked paprika
  • Italian seasoning
  • ground cumin
  • chili powder
  • red pepper flakes
  • extra-virgin olive oil
  • low-sodium chicken broth

Steps

  1. Soup base: in a pot, combine 6 cups low-sodium chicken broth, 1 can diced tomatoes (14.5 oz), and 1 cup salsa (measured). Bring to a simmer.
  2. Chicken: add chicken breast (whole) plus 1 tsp chili powder, 1 tsp cumin, 1 tsp garlic powder, black pepper, and a modest pinch of kosher salt. Simmer 14–16 minutes to 165°F.
  3. Shred chicken in the pot with forks.
  4. Beans/veg: stir in 1 1/2 cups drained/rinsed black beans and 2 cups frozen mixed vegetables. Simmer 6 minutes.
  5. Tortilla strips: slice 3 tortillas into strips and bake at 425°F for 6–8 minutes until crisp (optional).
  6. Serve per Notes.

Notes

Big bowl comfort soup with tortilla crunch as an optional, measured add-on. Portions (per person): Frank: About 3 cups soup + 1/2 tortilla worth of strips Susan: About 2 1/2 cups soup + 1/3 tortilla strips Elise: About 2 1/2 cups soup + 1/3 tortilla strips Tips (recipe-specific): Keep tortilla strips optional so the soup still works without chips. Salsa + spices give depth without heavy salt.
