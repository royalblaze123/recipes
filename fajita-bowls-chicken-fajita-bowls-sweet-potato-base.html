Chicken Fajita Bowls (Sweet Potato Base)

Nutrition (approx)

Frank: 650 kcal • P 58g • C 46g • F 20g
Susan/Elise (each): 488 kcal • P 44g • C 34g • F 15g

Ingredients (purchase-clean)

  • 1.50 lb boneless skinless chicken breast
  • 2 each bell peppers
  • 1 each yellow onion
  • 1.75 lb sweet potatoes
  • 1 each avocado
  • 1/2 jar salsa
  • 1/2 tub 2% plain Greek yogurt
  • 6 tsp extra-virgin olive oil
  • 1 each lime
  • 2 tsp chili powder
  • 2 tsp cumin
  • 2 tsp garlic powder
  • 1 tsp smoked paprika
  • 1 tsp black pepper
  • 1 tsp kosher salt

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 425°F. Line two sheet pans with parchment.
  2. Sweet potatoes: cube into 3/4-inch pieces. Toss with 2 tsp olive oil and 1/2 tsp kosher salt. Roast 22–26 minutes, turning once, until browned and tender.
  3. Veg: slice bell peppers and onion into strips.
  4. Chicken: pat dry and slice into strips. Season with fajita spices.
  5. Roast chicken + veg: toss with remaining 4 tsp olive oil and roast 16–22 minutes, stirring once, until chicken reaches 165°F and veg is browned at edges.
  6. Yogurt crema: stir 6 tbsp Greek yogurt with 2 tsp lime juice and 1 tsp lime zest until smooth.
  7. Avocado: halve, pit, and dice. For this recipe, plan on using about 1/2 avocado total; refrigerate the remaining avocado with the pit to slow browning.
  8. Build bowls: layer roasted sweet potatoes, then fajita chicken/veg, then salsa + avocado + yogurt crema. Portion per Notes.

Notes

Fajita bowls using roasted sweet potatoes as the base (still portioned to stay in the dinner band). Portions (per person): Frank: 1/3 of the sweet potatoes + 1/3 of the fajita protein/veg + 1/3 of salsa + avocado + yogurt crema Susan: 1/4 of the sweet potatoes + 1/4 of the fajita protein/veg + 1/4 of salsa + avocado + yogurt crema Elise: 1/4 of the sweet potatoes + 1/4 of the fajita protein/veg + 1/4 of salsa + avocado + yogurt crema
