Chicken Broccoli Garlic Parmesan Pizza

NOTES: Built as a full dinner using ready-made whole-grain or thin crust, lean protein, plenty of vegetables, and controlled cheese to support A1c, cholesterol, and blood pressure goals. Use low-sodium sauce and minimal added salt. PORTION SIZES: Frank: ~40% of the pizza; Susan: ~30% of the pizza; Elise: ~30% of the pizza (Frank = 1.0×, Susan/Elise = 0.75× portions).

Ingredients

  • 1 ready-made 12-inch thin whole-wheat pizza crust
  • 1/2 cup plain 2% Greek yogurt
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • 2 tbsp grated Parmesan cheese
  • 8 oz cooked boneless, skinless chicken breast, chopped
  • 1 cup small broccoli florets, lightly steamed
  • 1/4 red onion, thinly sliced
  • 1/2 cup shredded reduced-fat mozzarella cheese
  • Freshly ground black pepper, to taste

Step-by-Step Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 425°F and place the crust on a pizza pan or parchment-lined baking sheet.
  2. In a small bowl, mix Greek yogurt, minced garlic, and grated Parmesan until smooth.
  3. Spread the yogurt mixture evenly over the crust as a white sauce.
  4. Distribute chopped chicken, steamed broccoli florets, and sliced red onion evenly over the sauce.
  5. Sprinkle shredded mozzarella over the toppings and add a few grinds of black pepper.
  6. Bake 12–15 minutes, until the crust is crisp and the cheese is melted and lightly browned.
  7. Cool 3–5 minutes before slicing and portioning for Frank (~40%) and Susan/Elise (~30% each).

Nutrition per Person

  • Frank: ~600 kcal, ~54 g protein, ~50 g carbs, ~18 g fat.
  • Susan: ~450 kcal, ~40 g protein, ~38 g carbs, ~13 g fat.
  • Elise: ~450 kcal, ~40 g protein, ~38 g carbs, ~13 g fat.
