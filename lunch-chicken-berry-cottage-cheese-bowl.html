Chicken Berry Cottage Cheese Bowl (Savory-Sweet)

NOTES: Designed as a portable grab-and-go lunch using lean protein, high-fiber vegetables, and controlled smart carbs. Use low-sodium ingredients and avoid added sugars where possible. PORTION SIZES: Frank: largest portion (~1/3 of the total volume); Susan: medium portion (~1/4 of the total volume); Elise: medium portion (~1/4 of the total volume) (Frank = 1.0×, Susan/Elise ≈0.75× portions).

Ingredients

  • 12 oz cooked boneless, skinless chicken breast, chopped
  • 2 cups low-fat cottage cheese (1–2%)
  • 1 cup mixed berries (blueberries and strawberries, fresh or thawed frozen, no added sugar)
  • 1 small apple, chopped
  • 1/4 cup chopped walnuts or almonds (unsalted)
  • 1 tsp ground cinnamon

Step-by-Step Instructions

  1. In a large bowl, combine chopped chicken and cottage cheese and stir gently to combine.
  2. Fold in mixed berries and chopped apple.
  3. Sprinkle chopped nuts and cinnamon over the top and fold gently again.
  4. Divide mixture into three containers, making Frank’s portion largest while Susan and Elise share the remaining volume equally.
  5. Cover and refrigerate up to 2 days. Eat chilled straight from the container.

Nutrition per Person

  • Frank: ~560 kcal, ~52 g protein, ~40 g carbs, ~18 g fat.
  • Susan: ~420 kcal, ~39 g protein, ~30 g carbs, ~13 g fat.
  • Elise: ~420 kcal, ~39 g protein, ~30 g carbs, ~13 g fat.
