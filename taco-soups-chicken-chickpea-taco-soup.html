Chicken Chickpea Taco Soup

Nutrition (approx)

Frank: 645 kcal • P 58g • C 48g • F 19g
Susan/Elise (each): 484 kcal • P 44g • C 36g • F 14 g

Ingredients (purchase-clean)

  • 1.50 lb boneless skinless chicken breast
  • 1 can chickpeas
  • 1 can fire-roasted diced tomatoes
  • 1 carton low-sodium chicken broth
  • 1 each avocado
  • 1/2 jar salsa
  • 1 each lime
  • 2 tsp extra-virgin olive oil
  • 2 tsp chili powder
  • 2 tsp cumin
  • 2 tsp garlic powder
  • 1 tsp smoked paprika
  • 1 tsp black pepper
  • 1 tsp kosher salt

Steps

  1. Chicken: cut into 1/2-inch pieces. Brown in a pot with 2 tsp olive oil 8–10 minutes until 165°F.
  2. Season: stir in spices for 60 seconds.
  3. Tomatoes: Can size note—use a standard 14.5 oz can fire-roasted diced tomatoes.. Add the full can.
  4. Chickpeas: Can size note—use a standard 15 oz can chickpeas.. Drain/rinse. Add 1 1/2 cups chickpeas; refrigerate remainder.
  5. Broth: carton size note—use a 32 oz carton low-sodium chicken broth. Add 3 cups broth; refrigerate remainder.
  6. Simmer: simmer 12–15 minutes.
  7. Acid: add 2 tsp lime juice.
  8. Finish: taste and adjust seasoning with additional black pepper if needed.
  9. Toppings: dice avocado and serve salsa on the side. Top each bowl with measured avocado + salsa. Portion per Notes.

Notes

Bean-forward taco soup using chickpeas for a different texture, finished with avocado + salsa. Portions (per person): Frank: 1/3 of the pot + 1/3 of the avocado + 1/3 of the salsa Susan: 1/4 of the pot + 1/4 of the avocado + 1/4 of the salsa Elise: 1/4 of the pot + 1/4 of the avocado + 1/4 of the salsa
