Slow Cooker Salsa Verde Chicken Burrito Bowls

Tender salsa verde chicken served over brown rice with beans and crunchy toppings for an easy, high-protein slow-cooker dinner.

Servings

3 servings.

Times

  • Prep time: 20 minutes
  • Cook time: 360 minutes
  • Total time: 380 minutes

Ingredients

Main Dish

  • 2 lb boneless skinless chicken breast
  • 1 jar salsa verde
  • 1 carton low sodium chicken broth
  • 1 each yellow onion
  • 1 each bell pepper
  • 2 cans black beans
  • 1 bag brown rice
  • 1 head romaine hearts
  • 1 each lime
  • 1 bunch cilantro
  • 1 bottle extra virgin olive oil
  • 1 jar ground cumin
  • 1 jar chili powder
  • 1 jar garlic powder
  • 1 jar black pepper
  • 1 bag reduced fat shredded cheese
  • 1 container nonfat Greek yogurt
    • 

    Preparation Steps

    1. Slow cooker (6 qt): add chicken, 1 cup salsa verde, and 1/2 cup low sodium chicken broth. Season with cumin, chili powder, garlic powder, and black pepper. Cook LOW 6 hours (or HIGH 3–4 hours) until chicken shreds easily.
    2. Shred + tighten texture: shred chicken in the slow cooker. Leave the lid off 10 minutes on HIGH to reduce excess liquid (keeps bowls from getting watery).
    3. Beans: drain and rinse both cans of black beans. Stir 2 cups beans into the chicken. Refrigerate remaining beans for another recipe.
    4. Rice side: cook 1 cup dry brown rice. Measure out 1 1/4 cups cooked rice for this dinner; refrigerate remaining rice.
    5. Crunchy toppings: chop 1 romaine heart into bite-size pieces (store remaining romaine). Dice 1/2 onion and 1/2 bell pepper (store remaining). Chop cilantro. Cut lime into wedges.
    6. Serve: build bowls with rice, salsa verde chicken + beans, romaine, onion, bell pepper, a small sprinkle of reduced fat shredded cheese, and a dollop of Greek yogurt. Squeeze lime over top.
    7. Portioning: serve the full recipe using the 40/30/30 split (Frank 40%, Susan 30%, Elise 30%).

      Notes

      Portions (per person):


      Frank – About 2 1/2 cups bowl total (40% of the cooked chicken + beans + rice + toppings).


      Susan – About 2 cups bowl total (30% of the cooked chicken + beans + rice + toppings).


      Elise – About 2 cups bowl total (30% of the cooked chicken + beans + rice + toppings).


      Tips (recipe-specific):


      Choose a lower-sodium salsa verde and broth to keep the day’s sodium in bounds.


      Leaving the lid off briefly after shredding prevents a soupy texture.


      If you want less carbs one night, reduce rice in the bowls and add extra romaine.

      • Susan: 476 kcal; 44 g protein; 28 g carbs; 17 g fat; 8 g fiber; 390 mg sodium
      • Elise: 476 kcal; 44 g protein; 28 g carbs; 17 g fat; 8 g fiber; 390 mg sodium
