Lunch Sandwich Turkey Avocado Yogurt Ranch

High-protein turkey-avocado sandwiches with a Greek-yogurt ranch spread and big veg volume built into the sandwich (no side needed).

Servings

3 servings.

Times

  • Prep time: 12 minutes
  • Cook time: 0 minutes
  • Total time: 12 minutes

Ingredients

Main Dish

  • 6 slices whole wheat bread
  • 14 slices deli turkey
  • 3 slices provolone cheese
  • 1 each avocado
  • 1 container nonfat Greek yogurt
  • 1 jar ranch seasoning
  • 1 bag spring mix
  • 1 each cucumber
  • 2 each tomatoes
  • 1 jar black pepper
    • 

    Preparation Steps

    1. Set up: lay out 6 slices whole wheat bread (3 sandwiches).
    2. Spread: in a small bowl, mix 3 tbsp Greek yogurt with 1 tsp ranch seasoning and black pepper. Measure out 3 tbsp spread for this recipe; refrigerate the rest.
    3. Veg: wash spring mix. Measure out 6 packed cups spring mix for these sandwiches; refrigerate remaining spring mix.
    4. Slice: slice cucumber and tomatoes. Use about 1/2 cucumber and 1 tomato for these sandwiches; refrigerate the rest.
    5. Avocado: slice 1 avocado into 12 thin slices.
    6. Build (Frank): 2 slices bread + 6 slices deli turkey + 1 slice provolone + 4 avocado slices + about 2 cups spring mix + several cucumber and tomato slices + about 1 tbsp yogurt ranch.
    7. Build (Susan): 2 slices bread + 4 slices deli turkey + 1 slice provolone + 4 avocado slices + about 2 cups spring mix + several cucumber and tomato slices + about 1 tbsp yogurt ranch.
    8. Build (Elise): 2 slices bread + 4 slices deli turkey + 1 slice provolone + 4 avocado slices + about 2 cups spring mix + several cucumber and tomato slices + about 1 tbsp yogurt ranch.
    9. Close sandwiches, press gently, and cut in half if desired (each person still gets 1 whole sandwich).
      10. 

      Notes

      Portions (per person):


      Frank – 1 whole sandwich: 6 slices turkey, 1 slice provolone, 4 avocado slices, ~2 cups spring mix, cucumber + tomato, ~1 tbsp yogurt ranch.


      Susan – 1 whole sandwich: 4 slices turkey, 1 slice provolone, 4 avocado slices, ~2 cups spring mix, cucumber + tomato, ~1 tbsp yogurt ranch.


      Elise – 1 whole sandwich: 4 slices turkey, 1 slice provolone, 4 avocado slices, ~2 cups spring mix, cucumber + tomato, ~1 tbsp yogurt ranch.


      Tips (recipe-specific):


      Ask for thin sandwich slices (not shaved) so the turkey stacks cleanly.


      Use spring mix as the volume anchor—pack it tight so the sandwich still closes.


      If avocado browns, squeeze a little tomato juice on it and keep the sandwich wrapped until lunch.
