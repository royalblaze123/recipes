Freezer Turkey Meatball Marinara Bake + Barilla Protein+

Nutrition (approx)

Frank: 650 kcal • P 60g • C 70g • F 14g • Sat 6g • Na 700mg • Fiber 10g
Susan/Elise (each): 488 kcal • P 45g • C 52g • F 10g • Sat 4g • Na 525mg • Fiber 8g

Ingredients (purchase-clean)

  • 1.75 lb 93% lean ground turkey
  • 1 box Barilla Protein+ pasta
  • 1 jar marinara sauce
  • 1 can no-salt-added diced tomatoes
  • 6 oz grated Parmesan cheese
  • kosher salt
  • black pepper
  • garlic powder
  • onion powder
  • smoked paprika
  • Italian seasoning
  • ground cumin
  • chili powder
  • red pepper flakes
  • extra-virgin olive oil
  • low-sodium chicken broth
  • cornstarch

Steps

  1. Oven: heat to 425°F. Line a sheet pan.
  2. Meatballs: mix turkey with Italian seasoning, garlic powder, onion powder, black pepper, and a modest pinch of kosher salt. Form 18 meatballs.
  3. Bake meatballs 14–16 minutes to 165°F.
  4. Sauce: simmer marinara (2 cups measured) with diced tomatoes (14.5 oz can) 10 minutes uncovered to thicken.
  5. Pasta: cook Barilla Protein+ pasta to al dente; drain well.
  6. Assemble: toss pasta with sauce; fold in meatballs; top with Parmesan (measured).
  7. Bake 12 minutes at 400°F to meld; rest 10 minutes.
  8. Freeze: cool fully; portion; freeze. Reheat covered at 350°F 25–35 minutes or microwave until hot.

Notes

Meatball marinara comfort engineered for freezer portions that reheat like fresh baked pasta. Portions (per person): Frank: About 2 1/2 cups (heaping scoop) Susan: About 2 cups (scoop) Elise: About 2 cups (scoop) Tips (recipe-specific): Cook pasta al dente so it doesn’t go mushy after freezing. Simmer sauce uncovered so it doesn’t water out on reheat.
