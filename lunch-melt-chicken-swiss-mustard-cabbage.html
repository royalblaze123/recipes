Open-Face Melt: Chicken Swiss with Mustard Cabbage Crunch

Prep: 18 min   Cook: 10 min   Total: 28 min

Ingredients

  • 6 slices whole wheat bread
  • 1.0 lb chicken breast
  • 6 slices Swiss cheese
  • 0.5 bag shredded cabbage
  • 1 cucumber
  • 1 lemon
  • olive oil
  • Dijon mustard
  • black pepper
  • salt substitute
  • vinegar
  • garlic powder

Rules enforced: quantities before items; fresh meat/poultry/fish use weights; packaged items use fractional containers when partial. Condiments/spices/seasonings have no measurements in Ingredients; all measurements are in Steps. No olives, no squash, no Brussels sprouts, no zucchini, no tofu/tempeh. Lunch validations included in Notes.

Step-by-step Instructions

  1. Chicken: season 1.0 lb chicken breast with 1 tsp garlic powder + 1/2 tsp pepper + 1/2 tsp salt substitute. Cook with 2 tsp olive oil 6–8 minutes until 165°F; rest 3 minutes; slice.
  2. Crunch: toss 1/2 bag shredded cabbage + sliced 1 cucumber with 2 tsp vinegar + 2 tsp lemon juice + pepper; set aside.
  3. Broiler: set to high. Line sheet pan.
  4. Toast bread: broil 1 minute.
  5. Spread Dijon measured (Frank 2 tsp; Susan 1.5 tsp; Elise 1.5 tsp). Add sliced chicken.
  6. Top with Swiss slices; broil 2–3 minutes until melted; rest 2 minutes.
  7. Serve crunch on top or alongside (keeps melt crisp).
