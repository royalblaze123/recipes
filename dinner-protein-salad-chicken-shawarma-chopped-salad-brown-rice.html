Chicken Shawarma Chopped Salad + Brown Rice

Nutrition (approx)

Frank: 645 kcal • P 58g • C 60g • F 16g • Sat 3g • Na 650mg • Fiber 10g
Susan/Elise (each): 484 kcal • P 44g • C 45g • F 12g • Sat 2g • Na 488mg • Fiber 8g

Ingredients (purchase-clean)

  • 1.75 lb boneless skinless chicken breast
  • 1 head romaine lettuce
  • 1 each cucumber
  • 2 each tomatoes
  • 1 cup dry brown rice
  • 1 each lemon
  • kosher salt
  • black pepper
  • garlic powder
  • onion powder
  • smoked paprika
  • Italian seasoning
  • ground cumin
  • chili powder
  • red pepper flakes
  • extra-virgin olive oil
  • balsamic vinegar
  • apple cider vinegar
  • Dijon mustard

Steps

  1. Rice: cook 1 cup dry brown rice with 2 cups water 35–40 minutes; rest 5 minutes.
  2. Chicken: toss with cumin, smoked paprika, garlic powder, black pepper, and lemon juice. Roast at 450°F 10–12 minutes to 165°F; rest; slice.
  3. Salad: chop romaine, cucumber, and tomatoes.
  4. Dressing: whisk lemon juice, apple cider vinegar, 2 tbsp olive oil, Dijon mustard, black pepper, and a small pinch of kosher salt.
  5. Toss salad with dressing. Plate with chicken and a side of rice per Notes.

Notes

Shawarma chopped salad with rice on the side—big flavor, no creamy sauce. Portions (per person): Frank: 4 cups salad + 7 oz chicken + 1 cup cooked rice Susan: 3 cups salad + 5 oz chicken + 3/4 cup cooked rice Elise: 3 cups salad + 5 oz chicken + 3/4 cup cooked rice Tips (recipe-specific): Roast chicken at 450°F for browning that tastes like street food. Keep rice measured; the salad volume does the fullness.
