Taco-Spiced Beef Pizza with Lime-Chili Wings

Prep: 22 min   Cook: 32 min   Total: 54 min

Ingredients

  • 1 pizza dough ball
  • 0.5 jar low-sodium pizza sauce
  • 6 oz reduced-fat mozzarella cheese
  • 10 oz lean ground beef 93%
  • 1 onion
  • 1 bell pepper
  • 1 lime
  • 1 bag shredded cabbage
  • 2.25 lb chicken wings
  • olive oil
  • cumin
  • chili powder
  • smoked paprika
  • black pepper
  • salt substitute
  • hot sauce

RBM rules: quantities appear before items; fresh meat/poultry/fish use weights. Condiments/spices/seasonings are listed name-only (no tsp/tbsp in Ingredients); all measurements are in Steps. Water is referenced in Steps only. No olives, no squash, no Brussels sprouts, no yogurt in dinners.

Step-by-step Instructions

  1. Cook beef topping: brown lean ground beef in a skillet 7–9 minutes. Season with 2 tsp cumin + 2 tsp chili powder + 1 tsp smoked paprika + 1 tsp black pepper + 2 tsp salt substitute. Set aside.
  2. Pizza: preheat oven to 475°F. Stretch dough into 12-inch round on parchment.
  3. Top pizza: spread ~1/2 jar pizza sauce, add mozzarella, then seasoned beef, sliced onion, and sliced bell pepper. Bake 10–12 minutes. Cut into 10 slices.
  4. Quick slaw side (for freshness): toss shredded cabbage with 6 tsp lime juice + 4 tsp olive oil + black pepper + salt substitute. (Served on the plate alongside pizza/wings.)
  5. Wings: pat dry. Toss with 2 tsp olive oil + 2 tsp salt substitute + 1 tsp chili powder + 1 tsp smoked paprika + 1 tsp black pepper + zest of 1 lime.
  6. Air fry wings: 380°F for 24–26 minutes; then 400°F for 4–6 minutes. Finish with a small hot sauce drizzle.
  7. Portion: Frank 4 slices + 8 wings (+ ~1 cup slaw). Susan/Elise 3 slices + 6 wings (+ ~3/4 cup slaw).
