Air Fryer Italian Beef Tips with Green Beans and Quinoa

Italian-seasoned beef tips with green beans and quinoa—lean, high-protein, and fully timed for the dual-basket air fryer.

Servings

3 servings.

Times

  • Prep time: 25 minutes
  • Cook time: 16 minutes
  • Total time: 41 minutes

Ingredients

Main Dish

  • 1 lb sirloin steak
  • 1 bag quinoa
  • 1 bag fresh green beans
  • 1 bottle extra virgin olive oil
  • 1 jar Italian seasoning
  • 1 jar garlic powder
  • 1 jar black pepper
  • 1 jar parmesan cheese
  • 1 container nonfat Greek yogurt
  • 1 each lemon
    • 

    Preparation Steps

    1. Quinoa: cook 3/4 cup dry quinoa. Measure out 1 1/2 cups cooked quinoa for this dinner; refrigerate remaining quinoa.
    2. Prep beef: cut sirloin into 1-inch cubes. Toss with 2 tsp olive oil, Italian seasoning, garlic powder, and black pepper.
    3. Prep green beans: trim. Toss 1/2 bag green beans with 1 tsp olive oil, garlic powder, and black pepper. Refrigerate remaining green beans.
    4. Cosori setup: preheat both baskets to 400°F for 3 minutes.
    5. Basket B (beef): cook beef tips 400°F for 7–9 minutes, shaking once, to desired doneness. Rest 3 minutes.
    6. Basket A (green beans): cook green beans 400°F for 6–7 minutes, shaking once.
    7. Finish: sprinkle 1 tbsp parmesan total across all plates.
    8. Yogurt lemon drizzle: stir 1/4 cup Greek yogurt with 1 tsp lemon juice and a pinch of black pepper.
    9. Serve (40/30/30): plate beef tips over quinoa with green beans and add a light yogurt drizzle.

      Notes

      Portions (per person):


      Frank – Quinoa: about 1/2 cup cooked. Beef tips: largest share. Green beans: about 1 1/2 cups. Yogurt drizzle + parmesan sprinkle.


      Susan – Quinoa: about 3/8 cup cooked. Beef tips: medium share. Green beans: about 1 1/4 cups. Yogurt drizzle + parmesan sprinkle.


      Elise – Quinoa: about 3/8 cup cooked. Beef tips: medium share. Green beans: about 1 1/4 cups. Yogurt drizzle + parmesan sprinkle.


      Tips (recipe-specific):


      Cook beef tips in a single layer for browning; do a second batch if needed.


      Keep green beans crisp by cooking them separately and serving immediately.


      Quinoa is the slow-carb anchor—reduce it if you want a lighter carb night.
