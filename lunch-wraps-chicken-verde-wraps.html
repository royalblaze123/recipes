Chicken Verde Wraps

Nutrition (approx)

Frank: 530 kcal • P 48g • C 52g • F 15g
Susan/Elise (each): 398 kcal • P 36g • C 39g • F 11g

Ingredients (purchase-clean)

  • 1.25 lb boneless skinless chicken breast
  • 1 jar salsa verde
  • 1 package large whole-wheat tortillas
  • 1 bag shredded lettuce
  • 1 each avocado
  • 1/2 jar salsa
  • 1 each cucumber
  • 1 bag mini sweet peppers
  • 1 tub plain nonfat Greek yogurt
  • 1 each lime
  • 2 tsp cumin
  • 2 tsp garlic powder
  • 1 tsp black pepper
  • 1 tsp kosher salt

Steps

  1. Chicken: cook diced chicken 8–10 minutes until 165°F. Season with cumin, garlic powder, black pepper, and kosher salt.
  2. Verde: jar size note—use a 16 oz jar salsa verde. Stir 1/2 cup verde into the chicken; refrigerate remainder.
  3. Sauce: mix 1/2 cup Greek yogurt with 2 tsp lime juice.
  4. Wrap build: warm tortillas 15 seconds so they roll easily. Spread the sauce on each tortilla. Add the protein, then add shredded lettuce or slaw mix if listed.
  5. Roll: fold in the sides and roll tightly. Slice in half for serving if desired.
  6. Crunchy side: slice cucumber and sweet peppers into sticks. Sprinkle with black pepper. Serve alongside the wrap.
  7. Avocado: halve, pit, and slice. Use about 1/2 avocado total; refrigerate remaining avocado with the pit to slow browning.
  8. Serve with salsa and portion per Notes.

Notes

Green-style chicken wraps (verde inside) with salsa and avocado on the side. Portions (per person): Frank: 1 large wrap + 1/3 of the crunchy veg side + salsa + avocado Susan: 3/4 wrap + 1/4 of the crunchy veg side + salsa + avocado Elise: 3/4 wrap + 1/4 of the crunchy veg side + salsa + avocado
