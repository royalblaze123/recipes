Morning Lite Hydration Primer (3 Bottles)

NOTES: Designed to pair with your existing morning shake and early hydration windows. Very mild flavor to avoid triggering morning nausea, PPPD, or anxiety flares. Rotate this in on days when you want electrolytes but a more subtle taste.

PORTION SIZES (each recipe makes 3 bottles): Frank: 1 full 20 oz bottle; Susan: 1 full 20 oz bottle; Elise: 1 full 20 oz bottle.

Ingredients

  • 60 fl oz cool filtered water (3 x 20 fl oz bottles)
  • 1/4 tsp lite salt total (about 1/12 tsp per bottle; a very small pinch each)
  • 1.5 scoops magnesium glycinate powder total (about 0.5 scoop per bottle)
  • 6–9 drops very mild flavor drops (e.g., light lemon, cucumber, or plain mineral flavor, 2–3 drops per bottle)
  • Liquid stevia or monk fruit sweetener, to taste (optional)

Step-by-Step Instructions

  1. Line up three 20 fl oz bottles.
  2. Add a very small pinch of lite salt (about 1/12 teaspoon) to each bottle so the total is 1/4 teaspoon across all three.
  3. Divide 1.5 scoops of magnesium glycinate powder evenly across the three bottles (about 1/2 scoop per bottle).
  4. Add 2–3 drops of very mild flavor drops to each bottle.
  5. Add stevia or monk fruit only if you want a slightly sweeter taste; keep it very light.
  6. Fill each bottle with filtered water up to about 20 fl oz.
  7. Cap tightly and shake each bottle for 20–30 seconds until the powders fully dissolve.
  8. Use these bottles earlier in the day alongside your normal hydration plan. Shake gently before sipping.

Nutrition per Person (1 bottle)

  • Frank: ~0–5 kcal, 0 g protein, 0 g fat, 0–1 g carbs, ~90–120 mg sodium, ~150–200 mg potassium.
  • Susan: ~0–5 kcal, 0 g protein, 0 g fat, 0–1 g carbs, ~90–120 mg sodium, ~150–200 mg potassium.
  • Elise: ~0–5 kcal, 0 g protein, 0 g fat, 0–1 g carbs, ~90–120 mg sodium, ~150–200 mg potassium.
