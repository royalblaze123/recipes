Cajun-Style Chicken Protein Mac (Spicy)

Nutrition (approx)

Frank: 645 kcal • P 60g • C 60g • F 16g • Sat 6g • Na 700mg • Fiber 11g
Susan/Elise (each): 484 kcal • P 45g • C 45g • F 12g • Sat 4g • Na 525mg • Fiber 8g

Ingredients (purchase-clean)

  • 1 box Barilla Protein+ pasta
  • 1.75 lb boneless skinless chicken breast
  • 1 carton skim milk
  • 1 container reduced-fat shredded cheddar cheese
  • 1 bag frozen mixed vegetables
  • kosher salt
  • black pepper
  • garlic powder
  • onion powder
  • smoked paprika
  • Italian seasoning
  • chili powder
  • ground cumin
  • crushed red pepper
  • cornstarch
  • extra-virgin olive oil
  • Dijon mustard
  • hot sauce
  • Worcestershire sauce
  • low-sodium soy sauce
  • rice vinegar
  • cayenne pepper
  • paprika

Steps

  1. Pasta: cook Protein+ pasta al dente. Drain.
  2. Chicken: sauté diced chicken with 2 tsp olive oil and a Cajun mix (garlic powder, onion powder, smoked paprika, a pinch of cayenne, black pepper, modest kosher salt). Cook 8–10 minutes to 165°F.
  3. Veg: sauté 3 cups frozen mixed vegetables 6–8 minutes until browned and water evaporates.
  4. Sauce base: whisk 2 tbsp cornstarch into 2 cups skim milk. Simmer 3–5 minutes until thick.
  5. Cheese: stir in 6 oz reduced-fat cheddar until melted.
  6. Combine: fold in pasta + chicken + veg. Rest 5 minutes.
  7. Serve per Notes.

Notes

Spicy Cajun comfort with a thick cheese sauce (no cream needed). Portions (per person): Frank: About 2 1/2 cups mac (1/3 pan) + extra veg Susan: About 2 cups mac (1/4 pan) + extra veg Elise: About 2 cups mac (1/4 pan) + extra veg Tips (recipe-specific): Brown the frozen veg so you don’t water down the sauce. Use spice heat + smoke instead of extra cheese.
