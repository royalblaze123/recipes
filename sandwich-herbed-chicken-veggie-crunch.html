Herbed Chicken & Veggie Crunch Sandwich Plate

Whole-grain chicken sandwich with Greek yogurt herb spread and carrot-cucumber crunch on the side, designed as a full RBM lunch.

Servings

3 servings.

Times

  • Prep time: 10 minutes
  • Cook time: 0 minutes
  • Total time: 10 minutes

Ingredients

Sandwich

  • 6 slices high-fiber whole-grain sandwich bread
  • 9 oz leftover home-cooked chicken breast
  • 6 tbsp low-fat plain Greek yogurt
  • 1 tsp dried Italian herb blend
  • 1/4 tsp garlic powder
  • 1/4 tsp black pepper
  • 2 cups fresh lettuce or spring mix
  • 1 cup fresh tomato slices

Side

  • 1 cup fresh cucumber slices
  • 1 cup fresh carrot sticks

Preparation Steps

  1. In a small bowl, stir together the Greek yogurt, dried Italian herb blend, garlic powder, and black pepper to make an herbed sandwich spread.
  2. Place the chicken breast on a cutting board and thinly slice it across the grain into bite-size strips.
  3. Lay out all six slices of whole-grain bread. Spread the herbed yogurt mixture evenly over one side of each slice.
  4. Divide the sliced chicken over three of the bread slices, giving Frank a slightly larger share and Susan and Elise slightly smaller shares according to the portions listed.
  5. Top the chicken on each sandwich with lettuce or spring mix and tomato slices.
  6. Close each sandwich with a remaining slice of bread, press gently, and cut in half if desired.
  7. On a clean cutting board, slice the cucumber and cut the carrots into sticks if not already prepared.
  8. Divide the cucumber slices and carrot sticks onto three plates to match the portions listed for Frank, Susan, and Elise.
  9. Serve each plate with one sandwich and its portion of carrot and cucumber sticks.

Notes

Portions (per person):

Frank – 1 full sandwich made with about 3.5 oz chicken and a generous layer of herbed yogurt spread, plus about 1 cup total carrot and cucumber sticks.

Susan – 1 full sandwich made with about 3 oz chicken and a moderate layer of herbed yogurt spread, plus about 3/4 cup total carrot and cucumber sticks.

Elise – 1 full sandwich made with about 2.5 oz chicken and a moderate layer of herbed yogurt spread, plus about 3/4 cup total carrot and cucumber sticks.

Nutrition (per person)

  • Frank: 410 kcal; 38 g protein; 34 g carbs; 11 g fat; 8 g fiber; ~560 mg sodium.
  • Susan: 350 kcal; 31 g protein; 30 g carbs; 9 g fat; 7 g fiber; ~500 mg sodium.
  • Elise: 350 kcal; 31 g protein; 30 g carbs; 9 g fat; 7 g fiber; ~500 mg sodium.
