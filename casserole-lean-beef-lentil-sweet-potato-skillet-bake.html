Lean Beef & Lentil Sweet Potato Bake

Prep: 25 min   Cook: 55 min   Total: 80 min

Ingredients

  • 14 oz lean ground beef 93%
  • 0.5 can no-salt-added lentils
  • 2 sweet potatoes
  • 1 onion
  • 2 carrots
  • 0.5 carton low-sodium beef broth
  • 1 bag frozen peas
  • olive oil
  • tomato paste
  • Worcestershire sauce
  • garlic powder
  • black pepper
  • salt substitute
  • smoked paprika

Hard rules enforced: Dinner calories (Frank 600–650), sat fat ≤8g, sodium ≤700mg, fiber ≥10g, added sugar ≤6g. Quantities appear before items; fresh meat/poultry/fish use weights. Condiments/spices/seasonings have no measurements in Ingredients; all measurements are in Steps. No olives, no squash, no Brussels sprouts, no zucchini, no yogurt in dinners.

Step-by-step Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 400°F.
  2. Sweet potato topping: peel and cube sweet potatoes. Boil in water (Steps only) 12–15 minutes until very tender; drain well. Mash with 2 tsp olive oil + garlic powder + black pepper + salt substitute.
  3. Beef base: brown lean ground beef 7–9 minutes. Add diced onion and diced carrots; cook 5 minutes.
  4. Stir in 2 tbsp tomato paste + Worcestershire sauce + smoked paprika + black pepper + salt substitute. Add 3/4 cup broth (measured from carton) and simmer 3 minutes.
  5. Lentils + peas: drain/rinse 1/2 can lentils. Stir into beef mixture. Microwave frozen peas 3 minutes; measure 1 cup peas into mixture and refreeze remaining peas.
  6. Transfer beef mixture to a baking dish. Spread sweet potato mash evenly over the top.
  7. Bake 20 minutes until hot and lightly browned on edges. Rest 5 minutes.
  8. Portion: Frank ~1/2 the dish; Susan/Elise each ~1/4.
