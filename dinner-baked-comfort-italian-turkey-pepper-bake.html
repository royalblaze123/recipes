Italian Turkey + Pepper Bake

Nutrition (approx)

Frank: 640 kcal • P 60g • C 60g • F 16g • Sat 6g • Na 690mg • Fiber 11g
Susan/Elise (each): 480 kcal • P 45g • C 45g • F 12g • Sat 4g • Na 518mg • Fiber 8g

Ingredients (purchase-clean)

  • 1.75 lb 93% lean ground turkey
  • 2 each bell peppers
  • 1 each onion
  • 1 jar marinara sauce
  • 1 bag baby spinach
  • 6 oz reduced-fat shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1 box Barilla Protein+ pasta
  • kosher salt
  • black pepper
  • garlic powder
  • onion powder
  • smoked paprika
  • chili powder
  • ground cumin
  • Italian seasoning
  • extra-virgin olive oil

Steps

  1. Oven: heat to 425°F. Lightly oil a baking dish.
  2. Pasta: cook Barilla Protein+ pasta al dente; drain.
  3. Veg: slice bell peppers and onion. Sauté with 2 tsp olive oil 6–8 minutes until softened and browned.
  4. Turkey: add turkey and cook 7–9 minutes until browned. Season with 2 tsp garlic powder, 1 tsp onion powder, 1 tsp Italian seasoning, 1/2 tsp kosher salt, 1/2 tsp black pepper.
  5. Sauce: stir in 2 cups marinara and simmer 3 minutes. Stir in 4 cups spinach until wilted.
  6. Assemble: fold pasta into the turkey-pepper sauce. Spread in dish. Top with 6 oz reduced-fat mozzarella.
  7. Bake: 12–14 minutes until bubbly. Rest 8 minutes.
  8. Portion per Notes.

Notes

Baked pasta comfort with peppers and turkey—simple, saucy, and reheat-proof. Portions (per person): Frank: 1/3 of pan Susan: 1/4 of pan Elise: 1/4 of pan Tips (recipe-specific): Brown peppers first for sweetness without added sugar. Keep it saucy: if leftovers seem dry, add a spoon of marinara when reheating.
