Parmesan Chicken + Roasted Broccoli + Potatoes

Nutrition (approx)

Frank: 650 kcal • P 60g • C 55g • F 18g • Sat 6g • Na 700mg • Fiber 10g
Susan/Elise (each): 488 kcal • P 45g • C 41g • F 14g • Sat 4g • Na 525mg • Fiber 8g

Ingredients (purchase-clean)

  • 1.75 lb boneless skinless chicken breast
  • 1.50 lb baby potatoes
  • 1 bag frozen broccoli florets
  • 6 oz grated Parmesan cheese
  • kosher salt
  • black pepper
  • garlic powder
  • onion powder
  • smoked paprika
  • Italian seasoning
  • ground cumin
  • chili powder
  • red pepper flakes
  • extra-virgin olive oil
  • Dijon mustard
  • balsamic vinegar
  • apple cider vinegar
  • low-sodium soy sauce
  • cornstarch
  • hot sauce

Steps

  1. Oven: heat to 425°F. Line sheet pan.
  2. Potatoes: halve; toss with 2 tsp olive oil, garlic powder, smoked paprika, 1/2 tsp kosher salt, black pepper. Roast 18 minutes.
  3. Broccoli: microwave; drain/pat dry. Toss with 2 tsp olive oil, garlic powder, 1/2 tsp kosher salt, black pepper. Add; roast 10 minutes.
  4. Chicken: season with garlic powder, Italian seasoning, black pepper, 1/2 tsp kosher salt. Add to pan; roast 12–15 minutes to 165°F.
  5. Finish: sprinkle 3 oz Parmesan total across chicken/veg (measured); return 2 minutes to melt.
  6. Serve per Notes.

Notes

Chicken parm energy without heavy layering—Parmesan is a measured finish. Portions (per person): Frank: 7 oz chicken + 1 cup potatoes + 1 1/2 cups broccoli Susan: 5 oz protein + 3/4 cup starch + 1 cup veg Elise: 5 oz protein + 3/4 cup starch + 1 cup veg Tips (recipe-specific): Use Parmesan as a topper, not a blanket. Pat broccoli dry so it actually browns.
