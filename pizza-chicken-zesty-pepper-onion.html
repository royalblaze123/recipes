Chicken Zesty Bell Pepper Onion Pizza

NOTES: Built as a full dinner using ready-made whole-grain or thin crust, lean protein, plenty of vegetables, and controlled cheese to support A1c, cholesterol, and blood pressure goals. Use low-sodium sauce and minimal added salt. PORTION SIZES: Frank: ~40% of the pizza; Susan: ~30% of the pizza; Elise: ~30% of the pizza (Frank = 1.0×, Susan/Elise = 0.75× portions).

Ingredients

  • 1 ready-made 12-inch thin whole-wheat pizza crust
  • 1/2 cup no-salt-added tomato or pizza sauce
  • 8 oz cooked boneless, skinless chicken breast, sliced or chopped
  • 1/2 red bell pepper, thinly sliced
  • 1/2 green bell pepper, thinly sliced
  • 1 small yellow onion, thinly sliced
  • 3/4 cup shredded reduced-fat mozzarella cheese
  • 1 tsp dried Italian seasoning
  • 1/4 tsp crushed red pepper flakes (optional, for mild heat)
  • Freshly ground black pepper, to taste

Step-by-Step Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 425°F and place the crust on a pizza pan or parchment-lined baking sheet.
  2. Spread tomato or pizza sauce evenly over the crust.
  3. Arrange sliced or chopped chicken evenly over the sauce.
  4. Top with sliced bell peppers and onion.
  5. Sprinkle shredded mozzarella over the toppings.
  6. Season with Italian seasoning, optional crushed red pepper flakes, and black pepper.
  7. Bake 12–15 minutes, until the crust is crisp and the cheese is melted and lightly golden.
  8. Cool 3–5 minutes, then slice and portion for Frank (~40%) and Susan/Elise (~30% each).

Nutrition per Person

  • Frank: ~590 kcal, ~52 g protein, ~50 g carbs, ~17 g fat.
  • Susan: ~445 kcal, ~39 g protein, ~38 g carbs, ~13 g fat.
  • Elise: ~445 kcal, ~39 g protein, ~38 g carbs, ~13 g fat.
