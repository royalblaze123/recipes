Lunch Sandwich Turkey Curry Yogurt Crunch

Mild curry-yogurt turkey sandwiches with cucumber and slaw for crunch and volume—full lunch in one sandwich.

Servings

3 servings.

Times

  • Prep time: 12 minutes
  • Cook time: 0 minutes
  • Total time: 12 minutes

Ingredients

Main Dish

  • 6 slices whole wheat bread
  • 14 slices deli turkey
  • 3 slices provolone cheese
  • 1 container nonfat Greek yogurt
  • 1 jar curry powder
  • 1 bag coleslaw mix
  • 1 each cucumber
  • 1 each apple
  • 1 jar black pepper
    • 

    Preparation Steps

    1. Spread: mix 4 tbsp Greek yogurt with 1/2 tsp curry powder and black pepper. Use all of this spread for the sandwiches; refrigerate remaining yogurt and curry powder.
    2. Veg: measure out 6 packed cups coleslaw mix for these sandwiches; refrigerate remaining slaw.
    3. Slice cucumber and apple. Use about 1/2 cucumber and 1/2 apple for these sandwiches; refrigerate the rest.
    4. Build (Frank): 2 slices bread + 6 slices turkey + 1 slice provolone + ~2 cups slaw + cucumber + a few apple slices + ~1 tbsp curry-yogurt spread.
    5. Build (Susan): 2 slices bread + 4 slices turkey + 1 slice provolone + ~2 cups slaw + cucumber + a few apple slices + ~1 tbsp curry-yogurt spread.
    6. Build (Elise): 2 slices bread + 4 slices turkey + 1 slice provolone + ~2 cups slaw + cucumber + a few apple slices + ~1 tbsp curry-yogurt spread.
    7. Close sandwiches, press gently, and wrap.
      8. 

      Notes

      Portions (per person):


      Frank – 1 whole sandwich: 6 slices turkey, 1 slice provolone, ~2 cups slaw, cucumber, apple slices, ~1 tbsp curry-yogurt spread.


      Susan – 1 whole sandwich: 4 slices turkey, 1 slice provolone, ~2 cups slaw, cucumber, apple slices, ~1 tbsp curry-yogurt spread.


      Elise – 1 whole sandwich: 4 slices turkey, 1 slice provolone, ~2 cups slaw, cucumber, apple slices, ~1 tbsp curry-yogurt spread.


      Tips (recipe-specific):


      Keep curry powder light so the flavor stays mild and PPPD-friendly.


      Coleslaw mix adds big volume without making the sandwich soggy.


      If you want fewer carbs, use fewer apple slices and add more slaw.
