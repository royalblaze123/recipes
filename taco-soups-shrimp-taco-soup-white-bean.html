Shrimp Taco Soup (White Bean)

Nutrition (approx)

Frank: 625 kcal • P 56g • C 42g • F 20g
Susan/Elise (each): 469 kcal • P 42g • C 32g • F 15 g

Ingredients (purchase-clean)

  • 1.50 lb raw shrimp
  • 1 can cannellini beans
  • 1 can fire-roasted diced tomatoes
  • 1 carton low-sodium chicken broth
  • 1 bag frozen fajita-style pepper blend
  • 1 each avocado
  • 1/2 jar salsa
  • 1 each lime
  • 4 tsp extra-virgin olive oil
  • 2 tsp chili powder
  • 2 tsp garlic powder
  • 1 tsp black pepper
  • 1 tsp kosher salt

Steps

  1. Veg: cook frozen fajita pepper blend in a pot with 2 tsp olive oil 6–8 minutes until browned at edges.
  2. Beans: Can size note—use a standard 15 oz can cannellini beans.. Drain/rinse. Add 1 1/2 cups; refrigerate remainder.
  3. Tomatoes: Can size note—use a standard 14.5 oz can fire-roasted diced tomatoes.. Add the full can.
  4. Broth: carton size note—use a 32 oz carton low-sodium chicken broth. Add 3 cups broth; refrigerate remainder.
  5. Simmer: simmer 10 minutes.
  6. Shrimp: pat dry, season with chili powder, garlic powder, black pepper, and kosher salt. Add shrimp and cook 2–3 minutes until just opaque.
  7. Acid: add 2 tsp lime juice.
  8. Finish: taste and adjust seasoning with additional black pepper if needed.
  9. Toppings: dice avocado and serve salsa on the side. Top each bowl with measured avocado + salsa. Portion per Notes.

Notes

Fast shrimp taco soup with peppers and white beans—big flavor finish with avocado + salsa. Portions (per person): Frank: 1/3 of the pot + 1/3 of the avocado + 1/3 of the salsa Susan: 1/4 of the pot + 1/4 of the avocado + 1/4 of the salsa Elise: 1/4 of the pot + 1/4 of the avocado + 1/4 of the salsa
