Italian Chicken Bake + Polenta Triangles

Nutrition (approx)

Frank: 645 kcal • P 60g • C 55g • F 16g • Sat 6g • Na 700mg • Fiber 10g
Susan/Elise (each): 484 kcal • P 45g • C 41g • F 12g • Sat 4g • Na 525mg • Fiber 8g

Ingredients (purchase-clean)

  • 1.75 lb boneless skinless chicken breast
  • 1 tube prepared polenta
  • 1 jar marinara sauce
  • 1 container reduced-fat shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1 bag frozen broccoli florets
  • kosher salt
  • black pepper
  • garlic powder
  • onion powder
  • Italian seasoning
  • smoked paprika
  • cornstarch
  • extra-virgin olive oil
  • low-sodium chicken broth
  • Italian seasoning

Steps

  1. Oven: heat to 425°F.
  2. Polenta: slice polenta into 1/2-inch triangles. Brush with 2 tsp olive oil and black pepper. Bake 18–22 minutes, flipping once, until crisp.
  3. Chicken: sauté diced chicken with 2 tsp olive oil, garlic powder, Italian seasoning, modest kosher salt, black pepper 8–10 minutes to 165°F.
  4. Broccoli: roast frozen broccoli at 425°F with 2 tsp olive oil and black pepper 16–20 minutes.
  5. Assemble: spread 1 cup marinara (measured) in dish. Add chicken. Top with 6 oz reduced-fat mozzarella.
  6. Bake 10–12 minutes until bubbly; broil 1 minute.
  7. Serve: chicken bake with polenta triangles and broccoli per Notes.

Notes

Chicken-parm-ish comfort with crispy polenta instead of bread. Portions (per person): Frank: 2 cups chicken bake + 6 polenta triangles + 1 cup broccoli Susan: 1 1/2 cups chicken bake + 4 polenta triangles + 3/4 cup broccoli Elise: 1 1/2 cups chicken bake + 4 polenta triangles + 3/4 cup broccoli Tips (recipe-specific): Crisp polenta gives that ‘carb comfort’ without pasta or bread. Broil 1 minute for a browned cheese top.
