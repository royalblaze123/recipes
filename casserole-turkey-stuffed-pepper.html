Turkey Stuffed Pepper Casserole

NOTES: Built as a complete dinner using lean protein, high-fiber vegetables, and controlled starch portions. Use low-sodium broth and minimal added salt to support blood pressure and kidney health. PORTION SIZES: Frank: largest portion (~1/3 of pan); Susan: medium portion (~1/4 of pan); Elise: medium portion (~1/4 of pan).

Ingredients

  • 1 lb 93% lean ground turkey
  • 1 tbsp extra-virgin olive oil
  • 1 medium yellow onion, diced
  • 3 bell peppers (mixed colors), diced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 cup cooked brown rice
  • 1 can (14.5 oz) no-salt-added diced tomatoes
  • 1 can (8 oz) no-salt-added tomato sauce
  • 1 tsp dried oregano
  • 1 tsp dried basil
  • 1/2 tsp smoked paprika
  • 1/2 tsp kosher salt (or to taste)
  • Freshly ground black pepper, to taste
  • 1/2 cup shredded reduced-fat mozzarella cheese
  • Olive oil spray for baking dish

Step-by-Step Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 375°F. Lightly spray a 9x13-inch baking dish with olive oil spray.
  2. Heat olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat.
  3. Add diced onion and diced bell peppers and cook 5–6 minutes until slightly softened.
  4. Stir in minced garlic and cook 30–60 seconds until fragrant.
  5. Add ground turkey, breaking it into crumbles, and cook 6–8 minutes until no pink remains.
  6. Stir in cooked brown rice, diced tomatoes with juices, tomato sauce, dried oregano, dried basil, smoked paprika, salt, and black pepper.
  7. Simmer 3–4 minutes to slightly thicken the mixture.
  8. Transfer the turkey and rice mixture to the prepared baking dish and spread into an even layer.
  9. Sprinkle reduced-fat mozzarella cheese evenly over the top.
  10. Bake uncovered 20–25 minutes until bubbly and the cheese is melted and lightly browned.
  11. Let casserole rest 10 minutes before slicing into portions for Frank, Susan, and Elise.

Nutrition per Person

  • Frank: ~580 kcal, ~48 g protein, ~55 g carbs, ~17 g fat.
  • Susan: ~435 kcal, ~36 g protein, ~41 g carbs, ~13 g fat.
  • Elise: ~435 kcal, ~36 g protein, ~41 g carbs, ~13 g fat.
