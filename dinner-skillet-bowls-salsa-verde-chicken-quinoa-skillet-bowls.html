Salsa Verde Chicken Quinoa Skillet Bowls

Nutrition (approx)

Frank: 625 kcal • P 58g • C 54g • F 17g • Sat 5g • Na 680mg • Fiber 12g
Susan/Elise (each): 469 kcal • P 44g • C 40g • F 13g • Sat 4g • Na 510mg • Fiber 9g

Ingredients (purchase-clean)

  • 1.50 lb boneless skinless chicken breast
  • 1 bag frozen fajita-style pepper blend
  • 1 can cannellini beans
  • 3/4 cup dry quinoa
  • 1 jar salsa verde
  • 1 each avocado
  • kosher salt
  • black pepper
  • chili powder
  • ground cumin
  • garlic powder
  • smoked paprika
  • extra-virgin olive oil

Steps

  1. Quinoa: rinse 3/4 cup dry quinoa. Cook with 1 1/2 cups water: bring to boil, cover, simmer 14–16 minutes. Rest 5 minutes, fluff.
  2. Chicken: heat 2 tsp olive oil in a skillet. Add 1.50 lb diced chicken; cook 8–10 minutes to 165°F.
  3. Season with 2 tsp cumin, 2 tsp garlic powder, 1 tsp smoked paprika, 1/2 tsp kosher salt, 1/2 tsp black pepper.
  4. Add 1 bag frozen fajita pepper blend; cook 6–8 minutes until browned.
  5. Beans: use one 15 oz can cannellini beans. Drain/rinse. Stir 1 1/2 cups beans into skillet; cook 2 minutes.
  6. Stir in 1/2 cup salsa verde; simmer 2 minutes.
  7. Bowls: divide quinoa + skillet mix into bowls. Top with diced avocado. Portion per Notes.

Notes

Green-chile comfort bowls with quinoa for a different texture than rice. Portions (per person): Frank: 1/3 quinoa + 1/3 skillet mix + 1/3 avocado Susan: 1/4 quinoa + 1/4 skillet mix + 1/4 avocado Elise: 1/4 quinoa + 1/4 skillet mix + 1/4 avocado Tips (recipe-specific): Use salsa verde as the ‘sauce’ instead of extra oil. Let the skillet simmer 2 minutes after adding verde so it tastes cooked-in, not raw.
