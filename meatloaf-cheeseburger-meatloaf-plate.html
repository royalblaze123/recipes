Cheeseburger Meatloaf Plate

Nutrition (approx)

Frank: 650 kcal • P 57g • C 38g • F 26g
Susan/Elise (each): 488 kcal • P 43g • C 28g • F 20g

Ingredients (purchase-clean)

  • 1.50 lb 93% lean ground beef
  • 1 each yellow onion
  • 2 each large eggs
  • 3/4 cup whole wheat panko breadcrumbs
  • 2 tsp yellow mustard
  • 6 tsp ketchup
  • 4 oz reduced-fat shredded cheddar
  • 2 tsp garlic powder
  • 1 tsp black pepper
  • 1 tsp kosher salt
  • 1.50 lb baby potatoes
  • 1 bag spring mix
  • 1 each cucumber
  • 8 tsp light salad dressing
  • 4 tsp extra-virgin olive oil

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 400°F. Line a sheet pan with parchment.
  2. Dice the yellow onion small.
  3. Meatloaf mix: combine ground beef, onion, eggs, panko, mustard, 4 tsp ketchup, garlic powder, black pepper, and kosher salt. Mix just until combined.
  4. Form loaf on sheet pan. Bake 25 minutes.
  5. Cheese top: sprinkle cheddar over the top (keep it centered). Bake 10 more minutes until melted. Verify center is 160°F.
  6. Potato wedges: cut baby potatoes into quarters. Toss with 2 tsp olive oil; roast 25–30 minutes, flipping once, until browned and tender.
  7. Salad: slice cucumber. Toss spring mix with cucumber and 8 tsp dressing right before serving.
  8. Rest meatloaf 10 minutes before slicing. Serve with remaining 2 tsp ketchup as a dip if desired.

Notes

Cheeseburger-style meatloaf with measured cheddar, plus potato wedges and a simple salad.
