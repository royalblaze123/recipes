Creamy Onion Gravy Dip

Category: Sauce • Yield: About 1 3/4 cups (30 tbsp) • AnyList-ready

Ingredients

  • 1 onion
  • 1 carton low-sodium beef broth
  • 3/4 cup 2% plain Greek yogurt
  • 2 tsp extra-virgin olive oil
  • 2 tsp low-sodium soy sauce
  • 1 tsp cornstarch
  • 1 tsp dried thyme
  • 1/4 tsp black pepper

Steps

  1. Thinly slice 1 onion.
  2. In a small saucepan over medium-low heat, warm 2 tsp olive oil. Add onion and cook 12–15 minutes, stirring often, until soft and golden.
  3. Add thyme and black pepper; stir 15 seconds.
  4. Pour in 1 cup broth from the 32 fl oz carton (reserve remaining broth refrigerated). Bring to a gentle simmer.
  5. Whisk cornstarch with 2 tsp water until smooth. Whisk slurry into the pan; simmer 60–90 seconds until lightly thickened.
  6. Remove from heat and cool 2 minutes. Whisk in Greek yogurt and soy sauce until smooth. Do not boil after adding yogurt.
