Skillet Chicken & Broccoli Stir-Fry with Brown Rice

Prep: 18 min   Cook: 22 min   Total: 40 min

Ingredients

  • 1.6 lb chicken breast
  • 1 bag frozen broccoli florets
  • 0.5 cup dry brown rice
  • 1 bell pepper
  • 1 onion
  • olive oil
  • low-sodium soy sauce
  • rice vinegar
  • sesame oil
  • garlic
  • ginger
  • black pepper
  • salt substitute
  • cornstarch

Hard rules enforced: Dinner validations (calories 600–650 Frank, sat fat ≤8g, sodium ≤700mg, fiber ≥10g, added sugar ≤6g). Quantities appear before items; fresh meat/poultry/fish use weights. Condiments/spices/seasonings have no measurements in Ingredients; all measurements are in Steps. No olives, no squash, no Brussels sprouts, no zucchini, no tofu/tempeh, no yogurt in dinners.

Step-by-step Instructions

  1. Cook brown rice: cook 1/2 cup dry brown rice per package directions (water in Steps only).
  2. Broccoli: microwave 1 bag frozen broccoli 3–4 minutes to thaw; pat very dry. Measure 4 cups; refreeze the rest.
  3. Sauce (low sodium): whisk 2 tbsp low-sodium soy sauce + 1 tbsp rice vinegar + 1 tsp sesame oil + 1 tsp cornstarch + 1 tbsp water. Add grated ginger and minced garlic.
  4. Chicken: slice into thin strips. Season with black pepper and salt substitute. Sear in a skillet with 2 tsp olive oil over medium-high 6–8 minutes until 165°F.
  5. Veg: add sliced pepper and onion; stir-fry 3 minutes. Add broccoli; stir-fry 2 minutes.
  6. Finish: pour in sauce and toss 1–2 minutes until glossy and thickened.
  7. Serve over brown rice. Portion: Frank gets the largest chicken/rice share; Susan/Elise each ~75%.
