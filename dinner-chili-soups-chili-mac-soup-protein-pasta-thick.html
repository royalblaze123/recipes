Chili-Mac Soup (Protein Pasta, Thick)

Nutrition (approx)

Frank: 645 kcal • P 60g • C 60g • F 18g • Sat 6g • Na 690mg • Fiber 12g
Susan/Elise (each): 484 kcal • P 45g • C 45g • F 14g • Sat 4g • Na 518mg • Fiber 9g

Ingredients (purchase-clean)

  • 1.75 lb 93% lean ground turkey
  • 1 box Barilla Protein+ pasta
  • 1 can black beans
  • 1 can no-salt-added diced tomatoes
  • 1 carton low-sodium chicken broth
  • extra-virgin olive oil
  • kosher salt
  • black pepper
  • chili powder
  • ground cumin
  • garlic powder
  • onion powder
  • smoked paprika
  • Italian seasoning
  • crushed red pepper
  • bay leaves

Steps

  1. Pot: heat 2 tsp olive oil. Brown turkey 7–9 minutes until cooked through.
  2. Season with chili powder, cumin, garlic powder, onion powder, smoked paprika, kosher salt, and black pepper.
  3. Tomatoes: Can size note—use a standard 14.5 oz can no-salt-added diced tomatoes.. Add 1 can.
  4. Beans: Can size note—use a standard 15 oz can black beans.. Drain/rinse and add.
  5. Broth: add 2 cups low-sodium broth and bring to a simmer.
  6. Pasta: add 2 cups dry Barilla Protein+ pasta (from the box). Simmer 8–10 minutes, stirring, until pasta is al dente and soup is thick.
  7. Rest 5 minutes (it thickens more). Portion per Notes.

Notes

Chili mac comfort, but in a thick soup form with protein pasta. Portions (per person): Frank: About 2 1/2 cups soup Susan: About 2 cups soup Elise: About 2 cups soup Tips (recipe-specific): Only cook the pasta al dente—overcooked pasta turns it gummy. Let it rest before portioning; it thickens naturally.
