Chicken & Corn Fajita Bowls (Cauliflower Rice)

Nutrition (approx)

Frank: 645 kcal • P 58g • C 44g • F 21g
Susan/Elise (each): 484 kcal • P 44g • C 33g • F 16g

Ingredients (purchase-clean)

  • 1.25 lb boneless skinless chicken breast
  • 2 each bell peppers
  • 1 each yellow onion
  • 1 can corn
  • 1 bag frozen cauliflower rice
  • 1 each avocado
  • 1/2 jar salsa
  • 1/2 tub 2% plain Greek yogurt
  • 4 tsp extra-virgin olive oil
  • 1 each lime
  • 2 tsp chili powder
  • 2 tsp cumin
  • 2 tsp garlic powder
  • 1 tsp smoked paprika
  • 1 tsp black pepper
  • 1 tsp kosher salt

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 425°F. Line a sheet pan with parchment.
  2. Veg: slice peppers and onion into strips.
  3. Chicken: pat dry and slice into strips. Season with fajita spices.
  4. Roast chicken + veg with 4 tsp olive oil for 16–22 minutes, stirring once, until chicken reaches 165°F.
  5. Corn: can size note—use a standard 15 oz can corn. Drain well. Measure out 1 1/2 cups corn for this recipe; refrigerate any remainder.
  6. Fold drained corn into the hot chicken/veg mix for 1 minute to warm through.
  7. Cauliflower rice: microwave the frozen cauliflower rice per bag directions. Drain any excess moisture. Season with a pinch of black pepper and a squeeze of lime if using.
  8. Yogurt crema: stir 6 tbsp Greek yogurt with 2 tsp lime juice and 1 tsp lime zest until smooth.
  9. Avocado: halve, pit, and dice. For this recipe, plan on using about 1/2 avocado total; refrigerate the remaining avocado with the pit to slow browning.
  10. Build bowls and portion per Notes.

Notes

Chicken fajita bowls with a controlled corn add-in for sweetness and variety. Portions (per person): Frank: 1/3 of the cauliflower rice + 1/3 of the fajita protein/veg + 1/3 of salsa + avocado + yogurt Susan: 1/4 of the cauliflower rice + 1/4 of the fajita protein/veg + 1/4 of salsa + avocado + yogurt Elise: 1/4 of the cauliflower rice + 1/4 of the fajita protein/veg + 1/4 of salsa + avocado + yogurt
