White Turkey Chili with Corn

NOTES: Each chili is designed as a full meal with lean protein, smart carbs, and plenty of vegetables. Use low-sodium, no-sugar-added canned goods, avoid added sugars, and keep portions aligned with your goals. PORTION SIZES: Frank: largest portion (~1/3 of the total pot); Susan: medium portion (~1/4 of the pot); Elise: medium portion (~1/4 of the pot) (Frank = 1.0×, Susan/Elise ≈0.75× portions).

Ingredients

  • 12 oz lean ground turkey (93%+ lean)
  • 1 small yellow onion, diced
  • 1 green bell pepper, diced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced or 1 tsp garlic powder
  • 1 can (15 oz) no-salt-added cannellini or great northern beans, drained and rinsed
  • 1 cup frozen corn kernels, no salt added
  • 2 cups low-sodium chicken broth
  • 3/4 cup 2% milk or unsweetened almond milk
  • 1/2 cup plain 2% Greek yogurt
  • 1 tbsp extra-virgin olive oil
  • 1 tsp ground cumin
  • 1/2 tsp mild chili powder
  • Freshly ground black pepper, to taste

Step-by-Step Instructions

  1. Dice onion and bell pepper into small pieces.
  2. Heat olive oil in a soup pot over medium heat for 1–2 minutes.
  3. Add ground turkey and cook 5–6 minutes, breaking it into small pieces, until browned and cooked through.
  4. Add onion, bell pepper, and garlic. Cook 4–5 minutes, stirring occasionally, until vegetables soften.
  5. Stir in cumin, chili powder, and black pepper. Cook 1 minute.
  6. Add white beans, corn, and chicken broth. Stir and bring to a gentle boil.
  7. Reduce heat to low, cover, and simmer 15–20 minutes, stirring once or twice, until flavors meld.
  8. Whisk milk and Greek yogurt together, then stir into the pot. Warm on low heat 3–4 minutes without boiling.
  9. Taste and adjust seasoning. Ladle into three bowls, giving Frank the largest portion. Serve with a side of steamed broccoli or green beans.

Nutrition per Person

  • Frank: ~630 kcal, ~48 g protein, ~55 g carbs, ~18 g fat.
  • Susan: ~475 kcal, ~36 g protein, ~41 g carbs, ~13 g fat.
  • Elise: ~475 kcal, ~36 g protein, ~41 g carbs, ~13 g fat.
