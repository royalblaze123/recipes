BBQ Pulled Chicken Jalapeño Poppers

Air Fryer or Sheet Pan • Yield: 24 popper halves

Ingredients (purchase-clean)

  • 12 each fresh jalapeños
  • 16 oz fresh chicken breast
  • 6 oz reduced-fat cream cheese
  • 3 tbsp sugar-free BBQ sauce
  • 2 oz reduced-fat shredded cheddar
  • kosher salt
  • black pepper
  • smoked paprika

Steps

  1. Preheat air fryer to 370°F (or oven to 425°F).
  2. Cook chicken with 1 tsp kosher salt, 1 tsp smoked paprika, and 1/2 tsp black pepper until 165°F; shred finely.
  3. Halve and seed 12 jalapeños; pat dry.
  4. Mix filling: soften 6 oz cream cheese; stir in 3 tbsp sugar-free BBQ sauce; fold in chicken and 2 oz cheddar.
  5. Stuff halves evenly.
  6. Cook: air-fry 370°F for 7–9 min (or bake 425°F for 12–15 min).

Notes

Flavor-forward holiday poppers designed for snacking or a light meal. Portions (guidance): Choose a BBQ sauce with 0–2 g added sugar per serving. Snack portion: 4–6 halves.
