Bean-Forward Lunch: White Bean Chicken Farro Bowl

Prep: 18 min   Cook: 16 min   Total: 34 min

Ingredients

  • 1.0 lb chicken breast
  • 0.5 cup dry farro
  • 0.5 can no-salt-added cannellini beans
  • 1 bag spinach
  • 1 lemon
  • 1 onion
  • olive oil
  • Italian seasoning
  • garlic powder
  • black pepper
  • salt substitute

Rules enforced: quantities before items; fresh meat/poultry/fish use weights; packaged items use fractional containers when partial. Condiments/spices/seasonings have no measurements in Ingredients; all measurements are in Steps. No olives, no squash, no Brussels sprouts, no zucchini, no tofu/tempeh. Lunch validations included in Notes.

Step-by-step Instructions

  1. Cook farro: cook 1/2 cup dry farro with 1 1/2 cups water per package directions (about 20 minutes).
  2. Chicken: dice 1.0 lb chicken breast. Season with 2 tsp Italian seasoning + 1 tsp garlic powder + 1/2 tsp pepper + 1/2 tsp salt substitute.
  3. Sauté chicken with 2 tsp olive oil 6–8 minutes until 165°F.
  4. Beans: drain and rinse 1/2 can cannellini beans; stir in with 2 tbsp water; warm 2 minutes.
  5. Spinach: stir in 1 bag spinach until wilted.
  6. Build bowls: farro base + chicken/bean/spinach mix.
  7. Finish: add 2 tsp lemon juice per bowl.
