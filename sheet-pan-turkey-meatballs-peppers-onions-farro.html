Sheet Pan Turkey Meatballs with Peppers and Farro

Prep: 22 min   Cook: 22 min   Total: 44 min

Ingredients

  • 1.5 lb lean ground turkey 93%
  • 1 egg
  • 1 onion
  • 2 bell peppers
  • 0.5 cup dry farro
  • olive oil
  • garlic powder
  • black pepper
  • salt substitute
  • Italian seasoning

Hard rules enforced: Dinner validations (calories, sat fat, sodium, fiber, added sugar). Quantities appear before items; fresh meat/poultry/fish use weights. Condiments/spices/seasonings have no measurements in Ingredients; all measurements are in Steps. No olives, no squash, no Brussels sprouts, no zucchini. Yogurt is not used in dinners.

Step-by-step Instructions

  1. Cook farro: cook 1/2 cup dry farro per package directions (water in Steps only).
  2. Preheat oven to 425°F. Line a sheet pan.
  3. Meatballs: mix turkey with 1 egg + Italian seasoning + garlic powder + black pepper + salt substitute. Form 18 small meatballs.
  4. Veg: slice peppers and onion. Toss with 2 tsp olive oil + black pepper + salt substitute. Spread on pan.
  5. Add meatballs to pan. Bake 15 minutes. Flip meatballs and stir veg, then bake 6–8 minutes more until meatballs hit 165°F.
  6. Serve over farro.
  7. Portion: Frank gets 8 meatballs; Susan/Elise 5 each, with farro portions scaled 1.0 / 0.75 / 0.75.
