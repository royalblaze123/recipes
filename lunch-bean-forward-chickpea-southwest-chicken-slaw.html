Bean-Forward Lunch: Southwest Chicken Chickpea Slaw Bowl

Prep: 18 min   Cook: 12 min   Total: 30 min

Ingredients

  • 1.0 lb chicken breast
  • 0.5 can no-salt-added chickpeas
  • 0.5 bag broccoli slaw
  • 1 bell pepper
  • 1 lime
  • 1 clamshell microgreens
  • olive oil
  • cumin
  • smoked paprika
  • garlic powder
  • black pepper
  • salt substitute
  • salsa

Rules enforced: quantities before items; fresh meat/poultry/fish use weights; packaged items use fractional containers when partial. Condiments/spices/seasonings have no measurements in Ingredients; all measurements are in Steps. No olives, no squash, no Brussels sprouts, no zucchini, no tofu/tempeh. Lunch validations included in Notes.

Step-by-step Instructions

  1. Chicken: slice 1.0 lb chicken breast thin. Season with 2 tsp cumin + 1 tsp smoked paprika + 1 tsp garlic powder + 1/2 tsp pepper + 1/2 tsp salt substitute.
  2. Cook chicken with 2 tsp olive oil 6–8 minutes until 165°F; cool 5 minutes.
  3. Chickpeas: drain and rinse 1/2 can chickpeas; measure 3/4 cup for bowls; refrigerate remainder.
  4. Slaw: toss 1/2 bag broccoli slaw with 2 tsp lime juice + pepper; keep crisp.
  5. Build bowls: slaw base + chicken + chickpeas + microgreens.
  6. Finish: salsa measured (Frank 1 tbsp; Susan 2 tsp; Elise 2 tsp).
  7. Pack salsa separately for crunch.
