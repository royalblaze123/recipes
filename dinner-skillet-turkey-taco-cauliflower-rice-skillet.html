Turkey Taco Cauliflower Rice Skillet

Nutrition (approx)

Frank: 630 kcal • P 58g • C 45g • F 18g
Susan/Elise (each): 472 kcal • P 44g • C 34g • F 14g

Ingredients (purchase-clean)

  • 1.50 lb 93% lean ground turkey
  • 1 bag frozen cauliflower rice
  • 1 can black beans
  • 1 jar salsa
  • 1 each lime
  • low-sodium chicken broth
  • kosher salt
  • black pepper
  • garlic powder
  • onion powder
  • smoked paprika
  • Italian seasoning
  • ground cumin
  • chili powder
  • red pepper flakes
  • extra-virgin olive oil
  • low-sodium chicken broth
  • low-sodium beef broth
  • low-sodium soy sauce
  • hot sauce
  • Dijon mustard
  • balsamic vinegar
  • apple cider vinegar
  • cornstarch

Steps (stovetop)

  1. Cauli rice: microwave 6–7 minutes; drain/pat dry.
  2. Turkey: brown with chili powder, cumin, garlic powder, pepper, modest salt (8–10 minutes).
  3. Beans: add black beans (15 oz; 1 1/2 cups) 2 minutes.
  4. Salsa: add salsa (1 cup measured) + broth (1/4 cup); simmer 2 minutes to thicken.
  5. Fold in cauliflower rice; cook 2 minutes; finish with lime juice (1 tbsp measured).

Notes

High-volume taco skillet that stays thick and reheats well. Portions (per person): Frank: 7 oz turkey mix + (cauli rice in mix) + (beans in mix) Susan: 5 oz protein + 1 cup veg + 3/4 cup Elise: 5 oz protein + 1 cup veg + 3/4 cup Tips (recipe-specific): Drain cauli rice well so it stays thick. Add lime at the end so it stays bright.
