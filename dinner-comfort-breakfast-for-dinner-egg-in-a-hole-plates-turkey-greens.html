Egg-in-a-Hole Plates + Turkey + Greens

Nutrition (approx)

Frank: 640 kcal • P 55g • C 60g • F 16g • Sat 6g • Na 700mg • Fiber 10g
Susan/Elise (each): 480 kcal • P 41g • C 45g • F 12g • Sat 4g • Na 525mg • Fiber 8g

Ingredients (purchase-clean)

  • 6 slices whole-wheat sandwich bread
  • 6 each eggs
  • 12 slices deli turkey
  • 1 bag baby spinach
  • kosher salt
  • black pepper
  • garlic powder
  • onion powder
  • smoked paprika
  • Italian seasoning
  • hot sauce
  • Dijon mustard
  • extra-virgin olive oil
  • salsa

Steps

  1. Spinach: sauté spinach with 1 tsp olive oil, black pepper, and a pinch of kosher salt 90 seconds.
  2. Egg-in-a-hole: cut a hole in each bread slice. Toast bread in nonstick pan 1 minute; crack an egg into each hole; cook 2–3 minutes; flip carefully and cook 1–2 minutes to set.
  3. Turkey: warm turkey slices 30 seconds per side.
  4. Serve per Notes.

Notes

Classic egg-in-a-hole comfort, built as a full plate with turkey and greens. Portions (per person): Frank: 2 egg-in-a-holes + 4 slices turkey + 1 cup spinach Susan: 2 egg-in-a-holes + 4 slices turkey + 3/4 cup spinach Elise: 2 egg-in-a-holes + 4 slices turkey + 3/4 cup spinach Tips (recipe-specific): Use nonstick + low heat so you don’t need butter. Add hot sauce at the table for a bigger ‘breakfast diner’ flavor.
