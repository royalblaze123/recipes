Turkey Meatballs + Roasted Peppers + Sweet Potatoes

Nutrition (approx)

Frank: 650 kcal • P 60g • C 55g • F 18g • Sat 4g • Na 680mg • Fiber 10g
Susan/Elise (each): 488 kcal • P 45g • C 41g • F 14g • Sat 3g • Na 510mg • Fiber 8g

Ingredients (purchase-clean)

  • 1.75 lb 93% lean ground turkey
  • 2.00 lb sweet potatoes
  • 1 bag frozen fajita-style pepper blend
  • kosher salt
  • black pepper
  • garlic powder
  • onion powder
  • smoked paprika
  • Italian seasoning
  • ground cumin
  • chili powder
  • red pepper flakes
  • extra-virgin olive oil
  • Dijon mustard
  • balsamic vinegar
  • apple cider vinegar
  • low-sodium soy sauce
  • cornstarch
  • hot sauce

Steps

  1. Oven: heat to 425°F. Line sheet pan.
  2. Sweet potatoes: cube; toss with 2 tsp olive oil, smoked paprika, garlic powder, 1/2 tsp kosher salt, black pepper. Roast 12 minutes.
  3. Meatballs: mix turkey with Italian seasoning, garlic powder, onion powder, 1/2 tsp kosher salt, black pepper. Form 18 meatballs.
  4. Peppers: toss frozen pepper blend with 2 tsp olive oil, garlic powder, black pepper, pinch kosher salt.
  5. Add meatballs and peppers to pan; roast 14–16 minutes to 165°F (flip meatballs once).
  6. Serve per Notes.

Notes

Meatball comfort without a skillet: roasted peppers add sweetness and moisture. Portions (per person): Frank: 6 meatballs + 1 cup sweet potatoes + 1 1/2 cups peppers Susan: 5 oz protein + 3/4 cup starch + 1 cup veg Elise: 5 oz protein + 3/4 cup starch + 1 cup veg Tips (recipe-specific): Roast peppers until edges brown; that’s the ‘sauce.’ Flip meatballs once for even browning.
