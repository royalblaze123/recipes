Pork Tenderloin + Sweet Potatoes + Broccoli (Dijon Finish)

Nutrition (approx)

Frank: 645 kcal • P 58g • C 55g • F 18g • Sat 4g • Na 700mg • Fiber 10g
Susan/Elise (each): 484 kcal • P 44g • C 41g • F 14g • Sat 3g • Na 525mg • Fiber 8g

Ingredients (purchase-clean)

  • 1.50 lb pork tenderloin
  • 2.00 lb sweet potatoes
  • 1 bag frozen broccoli florets
  • 1 jar Dijon mustard
  • kosher salt
  • black pepper
  • garlic powder
  • onion powder
  • smoked paprika
  • Italian seasoning

Steps

  1. Oven: heat to 425°F. Line sheet pan.
  2. Sweet potatoes: cube; toss with 2 tsp olive oil, smoked paprika, garlic powder, 1/2 tsp kosher salt, black pepper. Roast 12 minutes.
  3. Pork: slice tenderloin into thick medallions. Season with garlic powder, smoked paprika, 1/2 tsp kosher salt, black pepper. Brush with 2 tsp Dijon total (measured).
  4. Add pork; roast 12–14 minutes to 145°F; rest 3 minutes.
  5. Broccoli: microwave; drain/pat dry. Toss with 2 tsp olive oil, garlic powder, 1/2 tsp kosher salt, black pepper. Add; roast 10 minutes.
  6. Serve per Notes.

Notes

Juicy pork without sauce overload: a thin Dijon finish carries it. Portions (per person): Frank: 7 oz pork + 1 cup sweet potatoes + 1 1/2 cups broccoli Susan: 5 oz protein + 3/4 cup starch + 1 cup veg Elise: 5 oz protein + 3/4 cup starch + 1 cup veg Tips (recipe-specific): Rest pork 3 minutes so it stays juicy. Roast sweet potatoes first so they caramelize.
