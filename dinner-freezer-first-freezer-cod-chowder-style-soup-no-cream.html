Freezer Cod Chowder-Style Soup (No Cream)

Nutrition (approx)

Frank: 640 kcal • P 55g • C 55g • F 16g • Sat 3g • Na 650mg • Fiber 10g
Susan/Elise (each): 480 kcal • P 41g • C 41g • F 12g • Sat 2g • Na 488mg • Fiber 8g

Ingredients (purchase-clean)

  • 1.50 lb cod fillets
  • 1.50 lb baby potatoes
  • 1 each onion
  • 1 carton skim milk
  • low-sodium chicken broth
  • cornstarch
  • kosher salt
  • black pepper
  • garlic powder
  • onion powder
  • smoked paprika
  • Italian seasoning
  • ground cumin
  • chili powder
  • red pepper flakes
  • extra-virgin olive oil
  • low-sodium chicken broth
  • cornstarch

Steps

  1. Potatoes: dice potatoes; simmer in 5 cups low-sodium broth 12–15 minutes until just tender.
  2. Cod: add cod chunks with black pepper and garlic powder; simmer 4–6 minutes until flaky.
  3. Milk base: warm 2 cups skim milk. Whisk 2 tbsp cornstarch into 1/2 cup cold water; stir into milk; simmer 2 minutes until thick.
  4. Combine: pour thickened milk base into soup; simmer 2 minutes (do not boil hard).
  5. Cool + freeze: cool fully; portion; freeze. Reheat gently to steaming hot without boiling hard.

Notes

Chowder-style comfort that freezes well because it’s thickened milk—not heavy cream. Portions (per person): Frank: About 2 1/2 cups (heaping bowl) Susan: About 2 cups (bowl) Elise: About 2 cups (bowl) Tips (recipe-specific): Reheat gently; hard boiling can break the thickened milk base. Freeze in smaller portions for fast work-night reheats.
