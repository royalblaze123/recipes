Curry-Style Chicken Bake (No Coconut Milk)

Nutrition (approx)

Frank: 645 kcal • P 60g • C 62g • F 16g • Sat 4g • Na 690mg • Fiber 10g
Susan/Elise (each): 484 kcal • P 45g • C 46g • F 12g • Sat 3g • Na 518mg • Fiber 8g

Ingredients (purchase-clean)

  • 1.75 lb boneless skinless chicken breast
  • 1 bag frozen mixed vegetables
  • low-sodium chicken broth
  • cornstarch
  • 1 cup dry brown rice
  • kosher salt
  • black pepper
  • garlic powder
  • onion powder
  • Italian seasoning
  • smoked paprika
  • cornstarch
  • extra-virgin olive oil
  • low-sodium chicken broth
  • curry powder
  • turmeric

Steps

  1. Oven: heat to 400°F.
  2. Rice: cook 1 cup dry brown rice with 2 cups water 35–40 minutes; rest 5 minutes.
  3. Chicken: sauté diced chicken with 2 tsp olive oil, 2 tsp curry powder, 1 tsp turmeric, garlic powder, onion powder, modest kosher salt, black pepper 8–10 minutes to 165°F.
  4. Veg: add 3 cups frozen mixed vegetables; cook 6 minutes until hot and water reduces.
  5. Sauce: whisk 2 tbsp cornstarch into 1 1/2 cups low-sodium broth. Stir in and simmer 3 minutes until thick.
  6. Transfer to baking dish and bake 12 minutes to marry flavors.
  7. Serve: over measured rice per Notes.

Notes

Cozy curry bake comfort using thickened broth instead of coconut milk. Portions (per person): Frank: 2 cups bake over 1 cup cooked rice Susan: 1 1/2 cups bake over 3/4 cup cooked rice Elise: 1 1/2 cups bake over 3/4 cup cooked rice Tips (recipe-specific): Bake after thickening to deepen flavor without extra fat. Measure rice; comfort comes from sauce + spices.
