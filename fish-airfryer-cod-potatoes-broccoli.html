Air Fryer Cod with Roasted Potatoes and Broccoli (Cosori)

NOTES: Complete dinner with lean seafood, smart carbs, and 1–2 cups of non-starchy vegetables per person. Use low-sodium ingredients, avoid added sugars, and keep portions aligned with goals. PORTION SIZES: Frank: largest portion (~1/3 of the total volume); Susan: medium portion (~1/4 of the total volume); Elise: medium portion (~1/4 of the total volume) (Frank = 1.0×, Susan/Elise ≈0.75× portions).

Ingredients

  • 3 cod fillets (4–5 oz each)
  • 12 oz baby potatoes, quartered
  • 3 cups small broccoli florets
  • 1 1/2 tbsp extra-virgin olive oil
  • 1 tsp garlic powder
  • 1 tsp dried Italian seasoning
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice
  • Freshly ground black pepper, to taste

Step-by-Step Instructions

  1. Preheat the Cosori dual-basket air fryer to 380°F (193°C).
  2. In a bowl, toss baby potatoes with 1 tbsp olive oil, half the garlic powder, half the Italian seasoning, and black pepper until coated.
  3. In a separate bowl, toss broccoli florets with remaining 1/2 tbsp olive oil, remaining garlic powder, Italian seasoning, and black pepper.
  4. Pat cod fillets dry and brush with lemon juice. Lightly season with a pinch of Italian seasoning and black pepper.
  5. Load Basket 1 with seasoned potatoes in an even layer. Load Basket 2 with broccoli florets.
  6. Set both baskets to 16 minutes at 380°F using Sync Cook so they share the same time and temperature. Enable the shake reminder.
  7. At the shake reminder, shake both baskets. Move broccoli to one side of Basket 2 and place cod fillets on the open side. Continue cooking until potatoes are tender and cod flakes easily with a fork (about 7–8 minutes remaining).
  8. Divide cod, potatoes, and broccoli among three plates, giving Frank the largest portion of cod and potatoes.

Nutrition per Person

  • Frank: ~600 kcal, ~45 g protein, ~55 g carbs, ~17 g fat.
  • Susan: ~450 kcal, ~34 g protein, ~41 g carbs, ~13 g fat.
  • Elise: ~450 kcal, ~34 g protein, ~41 g carbs, ~13 g fat.
