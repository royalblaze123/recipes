Open-Face Melt: Turkey Pepperoni Marinara Melts

Prep: 16 min   Cook: 10 min   Total: 26 min

Ingredients

  • 6 slices whole wheat bread
  • 14 slices deli turkey
  • 18 slices turkey pepperoni
  • 6 slices part-skim mozzarella
  • 0.5 jar marinara sauce
  • 1 bag spinach
  • Italian seasoning
  • black pepper
  • salt substitute
  • garlic powder

Rules enforced: quantities before items; fresh meat/poultry/fish use weights; packaged items use fractional containers when partial. Condiments/spices/seasonings have no measurements in Ingredients; all measurements are in Steps. No olives, no squash, no Brussels sprouts, no zucchini, no tofu/tempeh. Lunch validations included in Notes.

Step-by-step Instructions

  1. Broiler: set to high. Line sheet pan.
  2. Toast bread: broil 1 minute.
  3. Sauce: spread marinara measured (Frank 2 tbsp total across 2 slices; Susan 1.5 tbsp; Elise 1.5 tbsp).
  4. Layer turkey (Frank 6 slices; Susan 4; Elise 4) and turkey pepperoni (Frank 8; Susan 5; Elise 5).
  5. Add spinach (divide 1 bag across slices).
  6. Top with mozzarella (1 slice per bread slice). Season with Italian seasoning, garlic powder, pepper/salt substitute.
  7. Broil 2–3 minutes until melted; rest 2 minutes.
