One-Pan Greek-Style Chicken + Potatoes + Broccoli (No Yogurt)

Nutrition (approx)

Frank: 640 kcal • P 58g • C 55g • F 16g • Sat 4g • Na 680mg • Fiber 10g
Susan/Elise (each): 480 kcal • P 44g • C 41g • F 12g • Sat 3g • Na 510mg • Fiber 8g

Ingredients (purchase-clean)

  • 1.75 lb boneless skinless chicken breast
  • 1.50 lb baby potatoes
  • 1 bag frozen broccoli florets
  • 1 each lemon
  • kosher salt
  • black pepper
  • garlic powder
  • onion powder
  • smoked paprika
  • Italian seasoning
  • ground cumin
  • chili powder
  • red pepper flakes
  • extra-virgin olive oil
  • low-sodium chicken broth

Steps

  1. Oven: heat to 425°F.
  2. Potatoes: toss with 2 tsp olive oil, 1 tsp garlic powder, 1 tsp Italian seasoning, black pepper, modest kosher salt. Roast 15 minutes.
  3. Chicken: season with garlic powder, onion powder, Italian seasoning, black pepper, and 2 tsp lemon juice. Add to pan; roast 10 minutes.
  4. Broccoli: add broccoli tossed with 2 tsp olive oil and black pepper. Roast 10–12 minutes until chicken is 165°F.
  5. Finish with lemon. Serve per Notes.

Notes

Greek-style weeknight plate with lemon + herbs, no creamy sauces. Portions (per person): Frank: About 7 oz chicken + 1 cup potatoes + 1 cup broccoli Susan: About 5 oz chicken + 3/4 cup potatoes + 3/4 cup broccoli Elise: About 5 oz chicken + 3/4 cup potatoes + 3/4 cup broccoli Tips (recipe-specific): Lemon at the end is what makes it taste ‘Greek’. Roast broccoli hard so it gets crispy edges.
