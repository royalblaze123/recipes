Air Fryer Herb Pork Tenderloin with Baby Potatoes and Broccoli

Lean pork tenderloin with an herb rub, paired with crisp baby potatoes and broccoli for a full, balanced plate.

Servings

3 servings (Frank, Susan, Elise).

Times

  • Prep time: 15 minutes
  • Cook time: 22 minutes
  • Total time: 37 minutes

Ingredients (purchase-clean)

  • 1 1/2 lb pork tenderloin
  • 1 1/2 lb baby potatoes
  • 1/2 bag frozen broccoli florets
  • 8 tsp olive oil
  • 3 tsp Dijon mustard
  • 4 tsp garlic paste
  • 3 tsp Italian seasoning
  • 2 tsp smoked paprika
  • 1 tsp black pepper

Preparation Steps

  1. Prep: cut 1 1/2 lb pork tenderloin into 1 1/2-inch medallions (about 12 pieces).
  2. Season pork: whisk 3 tsp Dijon mustard, 2 tsp olive oil, 4 tsp garlic paste, 3 tsp Italian seasoning, 2 tsp smoked paprika, and 1 tsp black pepper; coat pork medallions evenly.
  3. Cook potatoes (air fryer): halve 1 1/2 lb baby potatoes; toss with 4 tsp olive oil; air fry at 400°F for 14–16 minutes, shaking at 8 minutes, until browned and tender.
  4. Cook pork (air fryer): air fry pork medallions at 400°F for 8–10 minutes, flipping at 5 minutes, until 145°F internal; rest 3 minutes.
  5. Cook broccoli: microwave 1/2 bag frozen broccoli florets with 2 tbsp water for 4–6 minutes; toss with 2 tsp olive oil.
  6. Portion: divide pork, potatoes, and broccoli per the Portions notes.

Notes

Portions (per person):

Frank — 9 oz pork tenderloin; 3/4 cup baby potatoes; 1 1/2 cups broccoli.

 

Susan — 6 3/4 oz pork tenderloin; 1/2 cup baby potatoes; 1 1/4 cups broccoli.

 

Elise — 6 3/4 oz pork tenderloin; 1/2 cup baby potatoes; 1 1/4 cups broccoli.

 

Tips (recipe-specific):

 

Doneness: pork tenderloin stays juicy at 145°F with a short rest.

 

If potatoes finish first, keep them warm while pork cooks; avoid stacking hot food in a covered bowl (it softens the crisp).

 

Nutrition (per person)

  • Frank: 645 kcal; 16 g fat; 520 mg sodium; 38 g carbs; 7 g fiber; 57 g protein.
  • Susan: 520 kcal; 13 g fat; 450 mg sodium; 30 g carbs; 6 g fiber; 45 g protein.
  • Elise: 520 kcal; 13 g fat; 450 mg sodium; 30 g carbs; 6 g fiber; 45 g protein.
