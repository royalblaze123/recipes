Double-Veg Chicken Fajita Bowls (Cauliflower Rice)

Nutrition (approx)

Frank: 625 kcal • P 58g • C 40g • F 21g
Susan/Elise (each): 469 kcal • P 44g • C 30g • F 16g

Ingredients (purchase-clean)

  • 1.25 lb boneless skinless chicken breast
  • 2 each bell peppers
  • 1 each yellow onion
  • 12 oz fresh broccoli florets
  • 1 bag frozen cauliflower rice
  • 1 each avocado
  • 1/2 jar salsa
  • 1/2 tub 2% plain Greek yogurt
  • 6 tsp extra-virgin olive oil
  • 1 each lime
  • 2 tsp chili powder
  • 2 tsp cumin
  • 2 tsp garlic powder
  • 1 tsp smoked paprika
  • 1 tsp black pepper
  • 1 tsp kosher salt

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 425°F. Line a sheet pan with parchment.
  2. Veg: slice peppers and onion into strips. Cut broccoli into bite-size florets.
  3. Roast veg: toss peppers, onion, and broccoli with 6 tsp olive oil. Roast 12–15 minutes, stirring once, until browned at edges.
  4. Chicken: pat dry and cut into 1/2-inch pieces. Season with fajita spices.
  5. Cook chicken in a nonstick skillet over medium-high 8–10 minutes until browned and 165°F.
  6. Combine: fold cooked chicken into the roasted vegetables on the sheet pan and warm 2 minutes in the oven.
  7. Cauliflower rice: microwave the frozen cauliflower rice per bag directions. Drain any excess moisture. Season with a pinch of black pepper and a squeeze of lime if using.
  8. Yogurt crema: stir 6 tbsp Greek yogurt with 2 tsp lime juice and 1 tsp lime zest until smooth.
  9. Avocado: halve, pit, and dice. For this recipe, plan on using about 1/2 avocado total; refrigerate the remaining avocado with the pit to slow browning.
  10. Build bowls and portion per Notes.

Notes

Extra vegetables make these especially filling while keeping the toppings (avocado/salsa) measured. Portions (per person): Frank: 1/3 of the cauliflower rice + 1/3 of the fajita protein/veg + 1/3 of salsa + avocado + yogurt Susan: 1/4 of the cauliflower rice + 1/4 of the fajita protein/veg + 1/4 of salsa + avocado + yogurt Elise: 1/4 of the cauliflower rice + 1/4 of the fajita protein/veg + 1/4 of salsa + avocado + yogurt
