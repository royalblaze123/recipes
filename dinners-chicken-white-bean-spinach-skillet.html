Chicken, White Bean & Spinach Skillet

Quick one-pan chicken, white beans, and spinach with tomatoes over a small portion of brown rice for a cozy, high-protein dinner.

Servings

3 servings.

Times

  • Prep time: 10 minutes
  • Cook time: 18 minutes
  • Total time: 28 minutes

Ingredients

  • 3/4 cup cooked brown rice
  • 15 oz raw boneless skinless chicken breast
  • 3/4 cup canned white beans
  • 3 cups lightly packed fresh baby spinach
  • 1 cup halved grape tomatoes or drained canned diced tomatoes
  • 1/2 cup chopped onion
  • 1 tbsp extra-virgin olive oil
  • 1/4 cup low-sodium chicken broth
  • 2 tbsp 2% plain Greek yogurt
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice
  • 1 tsp garlic powder
  • 1/2 tsp dried Italian seasoning
  • 1/4 tsp ground black pepper
  • 1/4 tsp kosher or sea salt

Preparation Steps

  1. Cook the brown rice if not already prepared. Use leftover brown rice, cook 1/4 cup dry brown rice according to package directions, or heat a microwave-ready pouch. You need about 3/4 cup cooked rice total. Set aside and keep warm.
  2. Trim any visible fat from the chicken breast. Cut into small bite-size pieces about 3/4-inch thick so they cook quickly.
  3. Rinse and dry the spinach and tomatoes. Halve the grape tomatoes (or drain canned diced tomatoes). Chop the onion if using. Drain and rinse the white beans.
  4. In a small bowl, whisk together the chicken broth, Greek yogurt, lemon juice, garlic powder, Italian seasoning, black pepper, and salt until smooth.
  5. Heat a large nonstick skillet over medium-high heat. Add the olive oil. Add the chicken pieces in a single layer and cook for 5–6 minutes, stirring occasionally, until browned and cooked through (165°F internal).
  6. Add the onion (if using) and tomatoes to the skillet. Cook for 2–3 minutes until the onion softens slightly and the tomatoes begin to release some juices.
  7. Stir in the drained white beans and the sauce mixture. Reduce heat to medium-low and simmer for 2–3 minutes, stirring occasionally, until the beans are warm and the sauce slightly thickens.
  8. Add the baby spinach to the skillet. Stir gently for 1–2 minutes until the spinach wilts into the mixture. Turn off the heat and taste, adjusting seasoning with a small pinch of salt or pepper if needed.
  9. Add about 1/3 cup cooked brown rice to a large bowl or plate for Frank. Top with about 2 to 2 1/4 cups of the chicken, bean, and spinach mixture, making sure his portion includes roughly 6 oz chicken and 1/3 cup white beans.
  10. Divide the remaining rice between two bowls or plates for Susan and Elise, about 1/4 cup each. Top each with about 1 1/2 to 1 3/4 cups of the mixture, ensuring each has about 4 1/2 oz chicken and 1/4 cup white beans.

Portion Sizes (Per Person, Approximate)

  • Frank (F · 1.0×): ~2 to 2 1/4 cups chicken, bean, and spinach mixture over 1/3 cup cooked brown rice with ~6 oz chicken, ~1/3 cup white beans, and ~1 1/2 cups vegetables.
  • Susan (S · 0.75×): ~1 1/2 to 1 3/4 cups mixture over 1/4 cup rice with ~4 1/2 oz chicken, ~1/4 cup white beans, and ~1 cup vegetables.
  • Elise (E · 0.75×): ~1 1/2 to 1 3/4 cups mixture over 1/4 cup rice with ~4 1/2 oz chicken, ~1/4 cup white beans, and ~1 cup vegetables.
