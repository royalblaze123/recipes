Skillet Turkey & Snap Peas Stir-Fry over Cauliflower Rice

Prep: 18 min   Cook: 18 min   Total: 36 min

Ingredients

  • 1.5 lb lean ground turkey 93%
  • 1 lb snap peas
  • 1 bag frozen cauliflower rice
  • 1 onion
  • olive oil
  • low-sodium soy sauce
  • rice vinegar
  • sesame oil
  • garlic
  • ginger
  • black pepper
  • salt substitute

Hard rules enforced: Dinner validations (calories 600–650 Frank, sat fat ≤8g, sodium ≤700mg, fiber ≥10g, added sugar ≤6g). Quantities appear before items; fresh meat/poultry/fish use weights. Condiments/spices/seasonings have no measurements in Ingredients; all measurements are in Steps. No olives, no squash, no Brussels sprouts, no zucchini, no tofu/tempeh, no yogurt in dinners.

Step-by-step Instructions

  1. Cauliflower rice: microwave 1 bag cauliflower rice 5–6 minutes. Measure 3 cups cooked for dinner; refreeze remainder. Drain/pat dry if watery.
  2. Sauce: whisk 2 tbsp low-sodium soy sauce + 1 tbsp rice vinegar + 1 tsp sesame oil + grated ginger + minced garlic + black pepper.
  3. Turkey: brown turkey in a skillet with 2 tsp olive oil 7–9 minutes. Season with black pepper and salt substitute.
  4. Veg: add sliced onion and snap peas; stir-fry 4–5 minutes until snap peas are bright and crisp-tender.
  5. Finish: pour in sauce; toss 1–2 minutes.
  6. Serve over cauliflower rice. Portion: Frank largest serving; Susan/Elise ~75%.
