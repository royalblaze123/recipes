Apple Cinnamon Dessert Mug Tea (No Sugar)

NOTES: Caffeine- and reflux-aware tea designed around your NutriChef hot water boiler. Built to support hydration, blood pressure, anxiety reduction, and A1c goals with zero added sugar. Use optional non-nutritive sweeteners only if desired.

PORTION SIZES (serves 3 mugs): Brew 4 cups total, then divide into three mugs or travel cups. Frank: largest mug (≈1/3 of the batch). Susan and Elise: slightly smaller mugs (≈1/4 of the batch each). Frank = 1.0×; Susan = 0.75×; Elise = 0.75× portions.

Ingredients

  • 4 cups hot water (about 190°F from NutriChef boiler)
  • 2 apple-cinnamon herbal tea bags
  • 1 rooibos or chamomile tea bag
  • 1 cinnamon stick
  • 1/4 tsp pure vanilla extract

Step-by-Step Instructions

  1. Fill the NutriChef hot water boiler with fresh filtered water and heat it to the temperature shown in the ingredients.
  2. In a large teapot, French press, or heat-safe jug, place 2 apple-cinnamon herbal tea bags, 1 rooibos or chamomile tea bag, 1 cinnamon stick, and 1/4 tsp pure vanilla extract. Keep the measured hot water separate for now.
  3. Carefully pour the measured hot water over the tea bags, herbs, spices, and any citrus listed so everything is submerged.
  4. Let the tea steep for about 10 minutes, tasting after a few minutes to make sure the flavor is strong but not bitter. Remove tea bags and any large herb pieces or cinnamon sticks.
  5. Divide the finished tea into three mugs or travel cups, giving Frank the largest portion and Susan and Elise slightly smaller portions. For iced versions, pour over ice and top off with extra cold water if you want a gentler flavor.

Nutrition (approximate per person)

Frank (largest mug): ~0–5 kcal, 0 g protein, 0 g carbs, 0 g fat
Susan (medium mug): ~0–5 kcal, 0 g protein, 0 g carbs, 0 g fat
Elise (medium mug): ~0–5 kcal, 0 g protein, 0 g carbs, 0 g fat
