BBQ Turkey Meatballs + Sweet Potato Plate

Nutrition (approx)

Frank: 640 kcal • P 58g • C 55g • F 18g • Sat 6g • Na 690mg • Fiber 10g
Susan/Elise (each): 480 kcal • P 44g • C 41g • F 14g • Sat 4g • Na 518mg • Fiber 8g

Ingredients (purchase-clean)

  • 1.75 lb 93% lean ground turkey
  • 1 package whole-wheat breadcrumbs
  • 2 each eggs
  • 1 bottle low-sugar BBQ sauce
  • 1.50 lb sweet potatoes
  • 1 bag frozen green beans
  • kosher salt
  • black pepper
  • garlic powder
  • onion powder
  • Italian seasoning
  • smoked paprika
  • chili powder
  • ground cumin
  • crushed red pepper
  • extra-virgin olive oil
  • apple cider vinegar

Steps

  1. Oven: heat to 425°F. Line two sheet pans and lightly oil.
  2. Sweet potatoes: cube 1.50 lb. Toss with 2 tsp olive oil, 1 tsp smoked paprika, 1/2 tsp kosher salt, 1/2 tsp black pepper. Roast 22–26 minutes, flipping once.
  3. Green beans: roast 1 bag frozen green beans with 2 tsp olive oil, 1/4 tsp kosher salt, black pepper for 14–18 minutes.
  4. Meatballs: mix turkey with 1/2 cup breadcrumbs, 2 eggs, 2 tsp garlic powder, 1 tsp onion powder, 1/2 tsp kosher salt, 1/2 tsp black pepper. Form 18–20 meatballs.
  5. Bake meatballs 12–14 minutes to 165°F.
  6. Sauce: warm 1/3 cup low-sugar BBQ sauce with 2 tsp apple cider vinegar. Toss meatballs in sauce.
  7. Serve per Notes.

Notes

BBQ comfort plate without deep-fried anything. Portions (per person): Frank: 8–9 meatballs + 1 cup sweet potato + 1 cup green beans Susan: 6–7 meatballs + 3/4 cup sweet potato + 3/4 cup green beans Elise: 6–7 meatballs + 3/4 cup sweet potato + 3/4 cup green beans Tips (recipe-specific): Use a measured 1/3 cup BBQ sauce for the whole batch; vinegar makes it pop. Roast sweet potato until browned—browning tastes ‘holiday comfort’.
