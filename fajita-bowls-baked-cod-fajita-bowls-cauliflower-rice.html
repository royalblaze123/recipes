Baked Cod Fajita Bowls (Cauliflower Rice)

Nutrition (approx)

Frank: 620 kcal • P 58g • C 38g • F 20g
Susan/Elise (each): 465 kcal • P 44g • C 28g • F 15g

Ingredients (purchase-clean)

  • 1.50 lb cod fillets
  • 2 each bell peppers
  • 1 each yellow onion
  • 1 bag frozen cauliflower rice
  • 1 each avocado
  • 1/2 jar salsa
  • 1/2 tub 2% plain Greek yogurt
  • 6 tsp extra-virgin olive oil
  • 1 each lime
  • 2 tsp chili powder
  • 2 tsp cumin
  • 2 tsp garlic powder
  • 1 tsp smoked paprika
  • 1 tsp black pepper
  • 1 tsp kosher salt

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 425°F. Line a sheet pan with parchment.
  2. Veg: slice peppers and onion into strips. Toss with 4 tsp olive oil and roast 12 minutes.
  3. Fish: pat cod dry. Rub with remaining 2 tsp olive oil and season with chili powder, cumin, garlic powder, smoked paprika, black pepper, and kosher salt.
  4. Add cod to the sheet pan and roast 10–12 minutes until cod reaches 145°F and flakes easily. Rest 3 minutes, then break into large flakes.
  5. Cauliflower rice: microwave the frozen cauliflower rice per bag directions. Drain any excess moisture. Season with a pinch of black pepper and a squeeze of lime if using.
  6. Yogurt crema: stir 6 tbsp Greek yogurt with 2 tsp lime juice and 1 tsp lime zest until smooth.
  7. Avocado: halve, pit, and dice. For this recipe, plan on using about 1/2 avocado total; refrigerate the remaining avocado with the pit to slow browning.
  8. Build bowls with cauliflower rice + cod + fajita veg + salsa + avocado + crema. Portion per Notes.

Notes

Fajita-style baked cod bowls finished with salsa, avocado, and yogurt crema. Portions (per person): Frank: 1/3 of the cauliflower rice + 1/3 of the fajita protein/veg + 1/3 of salsa + avocado + yogurt Susan: 1/4 of the cauliflower rice + 1/4 of the fajita protein/veg + 1/4 of salsa + avocado + yogurt Elise: 1/4 of the cauliflower rice + 1/4 of the fajita protein/veg + 1/4 of salsa + avocado + yogurt
