Grilled Cheese Plates + Tomato-Basil Soup (Turkey Boost)

Nutrition (approx)

Frank: 650 kcal • P 55g • C 72g • F 16g • Sat 6g • Na 700mg • Fiber 10g
Susan/Elise (each): 488 kcal • P 41g • C 54g • F 12g • Sat 4g • Na 525mg • Fiber 8g

Ingredients (purchase-clean)

  • 12 slices whole-wheat sandwich bread
  • 6 slices reduced-fat cheese slices
  • 12 slices deli turkey
  • 1 jar marinara sauce
  • 1 carton low-sodium chicken broth
  • 1 bunch fresh basil
  • kosher salt
  • black pepper
  • garlic powder
  • onion powder
  • Italian seasoning
  • smoked paprika
  • extra-virgin olive oil
  • Dijon mustard
  • hot sauce
  • rice vinegar

Steps

  1. Soup: simmer 2 cups marinara + 2 cups low-sodium chicken broth 8 minutes. Stir in torn basil at the end.
  2. Sandwiches: make 3 grilled cheeses. Each: 4 slices turkey and 2 slices cheese total across the 3 sandwiches (use 1 slice on Frank’s, split 1 slice across the other two).
  3. Skillet: toast 3–4 minutes per side until melted.
  4. Serve per Notes.

Notes

Grilled cheese and tomato soup comfort, with turkey to keep protein high. Portions (per person): Frank: 1 sandwich + 1 1/2 cups soup side Susan: 1 sandwich + 1 1/4 cups soup side Elise: 1 sandwich + 1 1/4 cups soup side Tips (recipe-specific): Broth-boosted soup keeps marinara measured but still satisfying. Turkey inside the grilled cheese keeps it from being ‘just carbs’.
