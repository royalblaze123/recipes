Buffalo Chicken Pizza + Buffalo-Garlic Wings

A low-sodium 12-inch buffalo chicken pizza paired with crisp Cosori air-fryer wings (no butter-heavy sauce).

Ingredients

  • 1 dough ball low-sodium pizza dough
  • 1/2 jar low-sodium pizza sauce
  • 2 oz reduced-fat mozzarella cheese
  • 4 oz chicken breast
  • 1/2 each red onion
  • 1/2 each bell pepper
  • 1.5 lb chicken wings
  • 6 tsp extra-virgin olive oil
  • 10 tsp hot sauce
  • 4 tsp garlic powder
  • 2 tsp onion powder
  • 3 tsp smoked paprika
  • 2 tsp ground black pepper
  • 2 tsp apple cider vinegar
  • 1 tsp dried oregano

Step-by-step Instructions

  1. Preheat oven with a pizza steel/stone on the middle rack to 500°F for 45 minutes.
  2. Prep toppings: thin-slice 1/2 red onion and thin-slice 1/2 bell pepper. Set aside.
  3. Cook chicken for the pizza: heat 2 tsp extra-virgin olive oil in a nonstick skillet over medium-high. Add 4 oz chicken breast. Season with 1 tsp garlic powder, 1 tsp onion powder, 1 tsp smoked paprika, and 1 tsp ground black pepper. Cook 4–6 minutes, turning as needed, until fully cooked; then chop into bite-size pieces.
  4. Make buffalo chicken toss (from totals): in a bowl, mix 3 tsp hot sauce + 1 tsp apple cider vinegar + 1 tsp dried oregano. Toss the chopped cooked chicken in this mixture.
  5. Build pizza: stretch 1 dough ball low-sodium pizza dough to a 12-inch round. Spread 1/2 jar low-sodium pizza sauce evenly (leave a 1/2-inch rim). Top with the buffalo chicken, the sliced 1/2 red onion, and the sliced 1/2 bell pepper. Sprinkle 2 oz reduced-fat mozzarella cheese evenly over the top.
  6. Bake pizza: transfer to the hot steel/stone and bake 7–9 minutes until the crust is browned and the cheese is melted. Rest 2 minutes, then cut into 10 slices.
  7. Prep wings: pat 1.5 lb chicken wings very dry with paper towels. In a bowl, coat wings with 2 tsp extra-virgin olive oil, 3 tsp garlic powder, 1 tsp onion powder, 2 tsp smoked paprika, and 1 tsp ground black pepper.
  8. Air-fry wings (Cosori dual-basket): preheat both baskets to 380°F for 3 minutes. Divide wings evenly between Basket A and Basket B in a single layer. Cook 12 minutes at 380°F using Sync Cook; shake both baskets at the reminder.
  9. Crisp finish: increase to 400°F and cook 8–10 more minutes, shaking once halfway, until wings are deeply browned and at least 165°F internal.
  10. Buffalo-garlic wing sauce (from totals): whisk 7 tsp hot sauce + 2 tsp extra-virgin olive oil + 1 tsp apple cider vinegar + 1 tsp smoked paprika + 1 tsp ground black pepper in a large bowl. Toss the hot wings in the sauce until evenly coated.
  11. Serve portions: Frank gets 4 pizza slices + 3 wings; Susan gets 3 slices + 2 wings; Elise gets 3 slices + 2 wings.

Notes

Portions (per person):

Frank: 4 pizza slices (of 10) + 3 wings (whole wings; flat + drumette = 1 wing).

 

Susan: 3 pizza slices (of 10) + 2 wings (whole wings; flat + drumette = 1 wing).

 

Elise: 3 pizza slices (of 10) + 2 wings (whole wings; flat + drumette = 1 wing).

 

Tips (recipe-specific):

  • Wing count note: 1 wing = 1 whole wing (flat + drumette). If buying separated party wings, count 2 pieces = 1 wing.
  • Keep the pizza cheese at the listed amount; the wings carry the extra protein so sodium stays “topping-safe.”
  • For more buffalo punch without extra sodium, increase smoked paprika and black pepper (not hot sauce volume).

  • Susan: 480 kcal; Protein 42 g; Carbs 36 g; Fiber 4 g; Fat 18 g; Sodium 490 mg
  • Elise: 480 kcal; Protein 42 g; Carbs 36 g; Fiber 4 g; Fat 18 g; Sodium 490 mg
