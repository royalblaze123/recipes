Turkey Taco Burrito Bowls (Cauliflower Rice)

Nutrition (approx)

Frank: 610 kcal • P 60g • C 38g • F 19g
Susan/Elise (each): 458 kcal • P 45g • C 28g • F 14g

Ingredients (purchase-clean)

  • 1.50 lb 93% lean ground turkey
  • 1 can black beans
  • 1 bag frozen cauliflower rice
  • 1 bag frozen fajita-style pepper blend
  • 1 each avocado
  • 1/2 jar salsa
  • 1/2 tub 2% plain Greek yogurt
  • 1 each lime
  • 4 tsp extra-virgin olive oil
  • 2 tsp chili powder
  • 2 tsp cumin
  • 2 tsp garlic powder
  • 1 tsp smoked paprika
  • 1 tsp black pepper
  • 1 tsp kosher salt

Steps

  1. Cauliflower rice: microwave the frozen cauliflower rice per bag directions. Drain excess moisture and season with a squeeze of lime and a pinch of black pepper.
  2. Turkey: cook ground turkey in a nonstick skillet over medium-high 7–9 minutes until browned.
  3. Veg: cook the frozen fajita pepper blend with 4 tsp olive oil 6–8 minutes until browned at edges.
  4. Beans: can size note—use a standard 15 oz can black beans. Drain and rinse. Measure out 1 1/2 cups beans; refrigerate any remainder.
  5. Season: add chili powder, cumin, garlic powder, smoked paprika, black pepper, and kosher salt; stir 60 seconds. Add beans and simmer 2 minutes.
  6. Yogurt-salsa drizzle: stir 6 tbsp Greek yogurt with 6 tbsp salsa until smooth.
  7. Avocado: halve, pit, and dice. Use about 1/2 avocado total; refrigerate remaining avocado with the pit to slow browning.
  8. Build bowls and portion per Notes.

Notes

Lower-carb burrito bowls using cauliflower rice, still fully loaded with salsa and avocado. Portions (per person): Frank: 1/3 of the cauliflower rice + 1/3 of the protein mix + 1/3 of the toppings (salsa + avocado + yogurt-salsa drizzle) Susan: 1/4 of the cauliflower rice + 1/4 of the protein mix + 1/4 of the toppings (salsa + avocado + yogurt-salsa drizzle) Elise: 1/4 of the cauliflower rice + 1/4 of the protein mix + 1/4 of the toppings (salsa + avocado + yogurt-salsa drizzle)
