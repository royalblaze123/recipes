Honey-Garlic Style Turkey Meatballs + Peppers

Nutrition (approx)

Frank: 645 kcal • P 58g • C 55g • F 18g • Sat 6g • Na 700mg • Fiber 10g
Susan/Elise (each): 484 kcal • P 44g • C 41g • F 14g • Sat 4g • Na 525mg • Fiber 8g

Ingredients (purchase-clean)

  • 1.75 lb 93% lean ground turkey
  • 1 package whole-wheat breadcrumbs
  • 2 each eggs
  • 1 bottle low-sugar honey garlic sauce
  • 2 each bell peppers
  • 1 each onion
  • 1 cup dry brown rice
  • kosher salt
  • black pepper
  • garlic powder
  • onion powder
  • Italian seasoning
  • smoked paprika
  • chili powder
  • ground cumin
  • crushed red pepper
  • extra-virgin olive oil
  • soy sauce
  • rice vinegar

Steps

  1. Oven: heat to 425°F. Line a sheet pan and lightly oil.
  2. Rice: cook 1 cup dry brown rice with 2 cups water 35–40 minutes. Rest 5 minutes, fluff.
  3. Meatballs: mix turkey with 1/2 cup breadcrumbs, 2 eggs, 2 tsp garlic powder, 1 tsp onion powder, 1/2 tsp kosher salt, 1/2 tsp black pepper. Form 18–20 meatballs; bake 12–14 minutes to 165°F.
  4. Peppers: slice 2 bell peppers + 1 onion. Sauté with 2 tsp olive oil 8–10 minutes until browned.
  5. Sauce: warm 1/4 cup low-sugar honey-garlic sauce + 2 tsp rice vinegar + 1 tsp soy sauce. Toss meatballs in sauce.
  6. Serve: portion rice + peppers + meatballs per Notes.

Notes

Sweet-salty comfort, but controlled sauce and lots of peppers. Portions (per person): Frank: 8–9 meatballs + 1 cup rice + 1 cup peppers Susan: 6–7 meatballs + 3/4 cup rice + 3/4 cup peppers Elise: 6–7 meatballs + 3/4 cup rice + 3/4 cup peppers Tips (recipe-specific): Measure sauce; vinegar helps it taste bigger. Brown peppers well—sweetness without extra sugar.
