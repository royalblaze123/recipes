Garlic Mushroom Spinach White Pizza with Lentil Tomato Salad

Prep: 26 min   Cook: 18 min   Total: 44 min

Ingredients

  • 1 pizza dough ball
  • 4 oz reduced-fat mozzarella cheese
  • 1 oz grated parmesan cheese
  • 1 package baby bella mushrooms
  • 1 bulb garlic
  • 1 bag spinach
  • 0.5 can no-salt-added lentils
  • 1 pint cherry tomatoes
  • 1 cucumber
  • olive oil
  • black pepper
  • salt substitute
  • Italian seasoning
  • vinegar

Hard rules enforced: Dinner calories (Frank 600–650), sat fat ≤8g, sodium ≤700mg, fiber ≥10g, added sugar ≤6g. Quantities appear before items; fresh meat/poultry/fish use weights. Condiments/spices/seasonings are listed name-only (no tsp/tbsp in Ingredients); all measurements are in Steps. No olives, no squash, no Brussels sprouts, no zucchini, no yogurt in dinners.

Step-by-step Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 475°F.
  2. Mushrooms: slice and sauté in a dry skillet 5–6 minutes to drive off moisture. Set aside.
  3. Garlic oil: mix 2 tbsp olive oil with 2 minced garlic cloves + 1 tsp Italian seasoning + black pepper.
  4. Shape dough into 12-inch pizza. Brush with 1 tbsp garlic oil (reserve the rest). Add mozzarella and parmesan, then mushrooms. Bake 10–12 minutes.
  5. Finish pizza: add spinach on top immediately after baking to wilt. Cut into 10 slices.
  6. Lentil salad: drain and rinse 1/2 can lentils. Mix lentils with halved cherry tomatoes and diced cucumber. Dress with 1 tbsp reserved garlic oil + 2 tsp vinegar + black pepper + salt substitute.
  7. Portion: Frank 4 slices + ~2 cups lentil salad. Susan/Elise 3 slices + ~1 1/2 cups each.
