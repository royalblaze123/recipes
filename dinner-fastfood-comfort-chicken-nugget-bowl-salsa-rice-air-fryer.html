Chicken Nugget Bowl + Salsa Rice (Air Fryer)

Steps

  1. Rice: cook 1 cup dry brown rice with 2 cups water 35–40 minutes. Rest 5 minutes.
  2. Stir 1/2 cup salsa into cooked rice (measured).
  3. Chicken: cut into 1-inch nugget pieces. Bowl 1: 1/2 cup egg whites. Bowl 2: 1 1/2 cups panko + 2 tsp chili powder + 2 tsp cumin + 1 tsp garlic powder + 1/2 tsp black pepper.
  4. Air fryer: 400°F. Cook nuggets in single layer 10–12 minutes with shake reminder ON; shake/flip at prompt; cook to 165°F.
  5. Beans: Can size note—use a standard 15 oz can black beans.. Drain/rinse. Use 1 1/2 cups beans; refrigerate remainder.
  6. Bowls: rice + beans + nuggets + avocado slices. Portion per Notes.

Notes

Nugget-bowl comfort with salsa rice and avocado (nacho vibes without chips). Portions (per person): Frank: Nuggets + 1 cup salsa rice + 1/2 cup beans + 1/3 avocado Susan: Nuggets + 3/4 cup salsa rice + 1/3 cup beans + 1/4 avocado Elise: Nuggets + 3/4 cup salsa rice + 1/3 cup beans + 1/4 avocado Tips (recipe-specific): Salsa in the rice gives big flavor with minimal extra calories. Keep nuggets single-layer so they crisp, not steam.
