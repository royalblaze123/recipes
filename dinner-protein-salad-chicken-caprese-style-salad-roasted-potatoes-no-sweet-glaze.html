Chicken Caprese-Style Salad + Roasted Potatoes (No Sweet Glaze)

Nutrition (approx)

Frank: 650 kcal • P 58g • C 55g • F 20g • Sat 6g • Na 650mg • Fiber 10g
Susan/Elise (each): 488 kcal • P 44g • C 41g • F 15g • Sat 4g • Na 488mg • Fiber 8g

Ingredients (purchase-clean)

  • 1.75 lb boneless skinless chicken breast
  • 1 head romaine lettuce
  • 2 each tomatoes
  • 8 oz part-skim mozzarella cheese
  • 1.50 lb baby potatoes
  • kosher salt
  • black pepper
  • garlic powder
  • onion powder
  • smoked paprika
  • Italian seasoning
  • ground cumin
  • chili powder
  • red pepper flakes
  • extra-virgin olive oil
  • balsamic vinegar
  • apple cider vinegar
  • Dijon mustard

Steps

  1. Oven: heat to 425°F.
  2. Potatoes: roast halved potatoes with 2 tsp olive oil, black pepper, garlic powder, modest kosher salt 22–26 minutes.
  3. Chicken: roast chicken with Italian seasoning, black pepper, and garlic powder 12–15 minutes to 165°F; rest; slice.
  4. Dressing: whisk 2 tbsp balsamic vinegar, 2 tbsp olive oil, Dijon mustard, black pepper, and a small pinch of kosher salt.
  5. Salad: chop romaine and tomatoes; toss with dressing.
  6. Top with chicken and sliced mozzarella (measured). Serve with potatoes per Notes.

Notes

Caprese vibe without sugary balsamic glaze—vinegar + olive oil does the job. Portions (per person): Frank: 4 cups salad + 7 oz chicken + 1 oz mozzarella + 1 cup potatoes Susan: 3 cups salad + 5 oz chicken + 3/4 oz mozzarella + 3/4 cup potatoes Elise: 3 cups salad + 5 oz chicken + 3/4 oz mozzarella + 3/4 cup potatoes Tips (recipe-specific): Keep mozzarella as a measured topper so it stays in range. Use warm potatoes to make the salad feel like dinner.
