Turkey Black Bean Classic Chili

NOTES: Each chili is designed as a full meal with lean protein, smart carbs, and plenty of vegetables. Use low-sodium, no-sugar-added canned goods, avoid added sugars, and keep portions aligned with your goals. PORTION SIZES: Frank: largest portion (~1/3 of the total pot); Susan: medium portion (~1/4 of the pot); Elise: medium portion (~1/4 of the pot) (Frank = 1.0×, Susan/Elise ≈0.75× portions).

Ingredients

  • 12 oz lean ground turkey (93%+ lean)
  • 1 small yellow onion, diced
  • 1 red bell pepper, diced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced or 1 tsp garlic powder
  • 1 can (15 oz) no-salt-added black beans, drained and rinsed
  • 1 can (15 oz) no-salt-added diced tomatoes
  • 1 cup low-sodium chicken broth
  • 1 tbsp tomato paste (no sugar added)
  • 1 tbsp extra-virgin olive oil
  • 1 1/2 tsp mild chili powder
  • 1 tsp ground cumin
  • 1/2 tsp smoked paprika
  • Freshly ground black pepper, to taste

Step-by-Step Instructions

  1. Dice onion and red bell pepper into small, even pieces so they soften quickly.
  2. Heat olive oil in a medium soup pot over medium heat for 1–2 minutes.
  3. Add ground turkey and cook 5–6 minutes, breaking it into small pieces, until browned and no longer pink.
  4. Add diced onion, bell pepper, and garlic. Cook 4–5 minutes, stirring occasionally, until vegetables are softened.
  5. Stir in chili powder, cumin, smoked paprika, and black pepper. Cook 1 minute to bloom the spices.
  6. Add black beans, diced tomatoes with their juice, tomato paste, and chicken broth. Stir to combine.
  7. Bring to a gentle boil over medium-high heat, then reduce heat to low. Cover and simmer 20–25 minutes, stirring once or twice, until flavors are blended and chili is thickened.
  8. Taste and adjust seasonings if needed. Ladle into three bowls, giving Frank the largest portion. Serve with an optional side of steamed broccoli or a small side salad.

Nutrition per Person

  • Frank: ~620 kcal, ~48 g protein, ~55 g carbs, ~18 g fat.
  • Susan: ~465 kcal, ~36 g protein, ~41 g carbs, ~13 g fat.
  • Elise: ~465 kcal, ~36 g protein, ~41 g carbs, ~13 g fat.
