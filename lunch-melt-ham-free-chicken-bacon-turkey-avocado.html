Open-Face Melt: Turkey ‘Club’ Melts with Avocado

Prep: 18 min   Cook: 8 min   Total: 26 min

Ingredients

  • 6 slices whole wheat bread
  • 14 slices deli turkey
  • 6 slices part-skim mozzarella
  • 6 slices turkey bacon
  • 1 avocado
  • 1 tomato
  • 1 bag spinach
  • black pepper
  • salt substitute
  • Dijon mustard

Rules enforced: quantities before items; fresh meat/poultry/fish use weights; packaged items use fractional containers when partial. Condiments/spices/seasonings have no measurements in Ingredients; all measurements are in Steps. No olives, no squash, no Brussels sprouts, no zucchini, no tofu/tempeh. Lunch validations included in Notes.

Step-by-step Instructions

  1. Turkey bacon: cook 6 slices in a skillet 6–8 minutes until crisp; drain on paper towel; break in halves.
  2. Broiler: set to high. Line sheet pan.
  3. Toast bread: broil 1 minute.
  4. Spread: Dijon measured (Frank 2 tsp; Susan 1.5 tsp; Elise 1.5 tsp).
  5. Layer: turkey (Frank 6 slices; Susan 4; Elise 4) + spinach (divide 1 bag) + turkey bacon pieces.
  6. Top with mozzarella slices; broil 2–3 minutes until melted; rest 2 minutes.
  7. Avocado/tomato: slice 1 avocado and 1 tomato; top melts after broiling (Frank gets the most avocado).
