Turkey Avocado Spinach Wrap with Tomato-Cucumber Side

Prep: 14 min   Cook: 0 min   Total: 14 min

Ingredients

  • 3 whole wheat tortillas
  • 12 oz deli turkey slices
  • 1 avocado
  • 1 bag spinach
  • 1 bag microgreens
  • 1 pint cherry tomatoes
  • 1 cucumber
  • olive oil
  • Dijon mustard
  • black pepper
  • salt substitute
  • vinegar

Hard rules enforced: Lunch validations (calories, sat fat, sodium, fiber, added sugar). Quantities appear before items; fresh meat/poultry/fish use weights. Condiments/spices/seasonings have no measurements in Ingredients; all measurements are in Steps. No olives, no squash, no Brussels sprouts, no zucchini. Yogurt is not used in dinners.

Step-by-step Instructions

  1. Prep veg: halve cherry tomatoes and slice cucumber.
  2. Wrap spread: mix Dijon mustard with vinegar and black pepper (thin, no mayo needed).
  3. Assemble wraps: layer spinach, turkey slices, avocado slices, and microgreens on tortillas. Add a light Dijon-vinegar spread. Roll tightly.
  4. Side: serve tomatoes + cucumber with 2 tsp olive oil + vinegar + salt substitute + black pepper.
  5. Portion: 1 wrap each; Frank gets extra turkey slices and a larger avocado share; Susan/Elise get ~75% of Frank’s filling volume.
