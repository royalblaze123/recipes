Cajun Tilapia with Roasted Broccoli over Quinoa

Prep: 16 min   Cook: 18 min   Total: 34 min

Ingredients

  • 1.5 lb tilapia fillets
  • 1 bag frozen broccoli florets
  • 0.5 cup dry quinoa
  • olive oil
  • cajun seasoning
  • garlic powder
  • black pepper
  • salt substitute
  • lemon

Hard rules enforced: Dinner validations (calories 600–650 Frank, sat fat ≤8g, sodium ≤700mg, fiber ≥10g, added sugar ≤6g). Quantities appear before items; fresh meat/poultry/fish use weights. Condiments/spices/seasonings have no measurements in Ingredients; all measurements are in Steps. No olives, no squash, no Brussels sprouts, no zucchini, no tofu/tempeh, no yogurt in dinners.

Step-by-step Instructions

  1. Cook quinoa: rinse 1/2 cup dry quinoa. Simmer with 1 cup water 15 minutes; rest 5 minutes.
  2. Broccoli: microwave 1 bag frozen broccoli 3–4 minutes to thaw; pat dry. Measure 4 cups; refreeze remainder.
  3. Roast broccoli: toss measured broccoli with 2 tsp olive oil + 1/4 tsp black pepper + 1/4 tsp salt substitute. Roast at 425°F for 12 minutes.
  4. Tilapia: pat dry 1.5 lb tilapia. Season with 2 tsp cajun seasoning + 1/2 tsp garlic powder + 1/4 tsp black pepper (skip salt if cajun blend is salty).
  5. Bake tilapia at 425°F for 10–12 minutes until flaky.
  6. Finish: squeeze 2 tsp lemon juice per plate.
  7. Serve tilapia and broccoli over quinoa. Portion: Frank largest share; Susan/Elise ~75%.
