Chicken White Bean Rosemary Soup (Stovetop)

NOTES: Designed as a complete meal soup using lean protein, high-fiber vegetables, and controlled starch. Use low-sodium broth and avoid extra salt when possible to support blood pressure and kidney health. PORTION SIZES: Frank: largest portion (~2 cups); Susan: medium portion (~1.5 cups); Elise: medium portion (~1.5 cups).

Ingredients

  • 1 tbsp extra-virgin olive oil
  • 1 medium yellow onion, diced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 carrots, diced
  • 2 celery stalks, diced
  • 4 cups low-sodium chicken broth
  • 1 cup water
  • 2 cans (15 oz each) no-salt-added cannellini beans, drained and rinsed
  • 1 lb boneless, skinless chicken breast
  • 1 tsp dried rosemary, crushed
  • 1 tsp dried thyme
  • 1/2 tsp kosher salt (or to taste)
  • Freshly ground black pepper, to taste
  • 2 cups chopped baby spinach

Step-by-Step Instructions

  1. Heat olive oil in a large soup pot over medium heat.
  2. Add diced onion, carrots, and celery and cook 5–6 minutes, stirring occasionally, until vegetables soften.
  3. Stir in minced garlic and cook 30–60 seconds until fragrant.
  4. Pour in low-sodium chicken broth and water and stir.
  5. Add drained cannellini beans and place chicken breasts into the pot, submerging them in the liquid.
  6. Stir in dried rosemary, dried thyme, salt, and black pepper.
  7. Bring to a gentle boil, then reduce heat to low, cover partially, and simmer 20–25 minutes until chicken is cooked through and vegetables are very tender.
  8. Remove chicken to a cutting board and shred with two forks, then return shredded chicken to the pot.
  9. Use a potato masher or spoon to lightly mash a portion of the beans in the pot to thicken the broth slightly.
  10. Stir in chopped baby spinach and cook 2–3 minutes until wilted.
  11. Taste and adjust seasoning with a small pinch more salt or pepper if needed.
  12. Remove from heat, let rest a few minutes, then ladle into bowls for Frank, Susan, and Elise.

Nutrition per Person

  • Frank: ~460 kcal, ~44 g protein, ~40 g carbs, ~10 g fat.
  • Susan: ~345 kcal, ~33 g protein, ~30 g carbs, ~8 g fat.
  • Elise: ~345 kcal, ~33 g protein, ~30 g carbs, ~8 g fat.
