Mustard Chicken + Roasted Carrots + Potatoes

Nutrition (approx)

Frank: 640 kcal • P 60g • C 50g • F 18g • Sat 3g • Na 700mg • Fiber 12g
Susan/Elise (each): 480 kcal • P 45g • C 38g • F 14g • Sat 2g • Na 525mg • Fiber 9g

Ingredients (purchase-clean)

  • 1.75 lb boneless skinless chicken breast
  • 1 lb carrots
  • 1.50 lb baby potatoes
  • 1 jar Dijon mustard
  • kosher salt
  • black pepper
  • garlic powder
  • onion powder
  • smoked paprika
  • Italian seasoning
  • ground cumin
  • chili powder
  • red pepper flakes
  • extra-virgin olive oil
  • Dijon mustard
  • balsamic vinegar
  • apple cider vinegar
  • low-sodium soy sauce
  • cornstarch
  • hot sauce

Steps

  1. Oven: heat to 425°F. Line sheet pan.
  2. Potatoes: halve; toss with 2 tsp olive oil, garlic powder, 1/2 tsp kosher salt, black pepper. Roast 18 minutes.
  3. Carrots: slice on a bias. Toss with 2 tsp olive oil, smoked paprika, black pepper, 1/2 tsp kosher salt. Add to pan; roast 10 minutes.
  4. Chicken: brush with 2 tsp Dijon total (measured) and season with garlic powder, black pepper, 1/2 tsp kosher salt. Add to pan; roast 12–15 minutes to 165°F.
  5. Rest 5 minutes; serve per Notes.

Notes

‘Honey-mustard’ vibe without sugar—roasted carrots bring natural sweetness. Portions (per person): Frank: 7 oz chicken + 1 cup potatoes + 1 1/2 cups carrots Susan: 5 oz protein + 3/4 cup starch + 1 cup veg Elise: 5 oz protein + 3/4 cup starch + 1 cup veg Tips (recipe-specific): Roast carrots until browned for natural sweetness. Add Dijon as a thin smear so it doesn’t burn.
