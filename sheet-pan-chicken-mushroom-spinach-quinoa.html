Sheet Pan Chicken and Mushrooms with Spinach over Quinoa

Prep: 20 min   Cook: 24 min   Total: 44 min

Ingredients

  • 1.75 lb chicken breast
  • 1 package baby bella mushrooms
  • 1 onion
  • 1 bag spinach
  • 0.5 cup dry quinoa
  • olive oil
  • garlic powder
  • black pepper
  • salt substitute
  • Italian seasoning
  • lemon

Hard rules enforced: Dinner validations (calories, sat fat, sodium, fiber, added sugar). Quantities appear before items; fresh meat/poultry/fish use weights. Condiments/spices/seasonings have no measurements in Ingredients; all measurements are in Steps. No olives, no squash, no Brussels sprouts, no zucchini. Yogurt is not used in dinners.

Step-by-step Instructions

  1. Cook quinoa: rinse 1/2 cup dry quinoa. Simmer with 1 cup water (Steps only) 15 minutes; rest 5 minutes.
  2. Preheat oven to 425°F. Line a sheet pan.
  3. Chicken: cut into chunks. Season with Italian seasoning, garlic powder, black pepper, salt substitute. Toss with 2 tsp olive oil.
  4. Mushrooms/onion: slice mushrooms and onion. Toss with 2 tsp olive oil + black pepper + salt substitute. Spread veg and chicken on pan.
  5. Roast 18–20 minutes, stirring once at 10 minutes, until chicken is 165°F and mushrooms browned.
  6. Spinach: pile spinach into a bowl. Spoon hot chicken/mushroom mixture over spinach to wilt 1–2 minutes.
  7. Serve over quinoa and finish with lemon squeeze.
  8. Portion: Frank largest quinoa and chicken portion; Susan/Elise ~75%.
