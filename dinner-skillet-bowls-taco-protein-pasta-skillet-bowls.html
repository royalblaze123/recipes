Taco Protein-Pasta Skillet Bowls

Nutrition (approx)

Frank: 645 kcal • P 60g • C 62g • F 18g • Sat 7g • Na 690mg • Fiber 12g
Susan/Elise (each): 484 kcal • P 45g • C 46g • F 14g • Sat 5g • Na 518mg • Fiber 9g

Ingredients (purchase-clean)

  • 1.50 lb 93% lean ground turkey
  • 1 box Barilla Protein+ pasta
  • 1 jar salsa
  • 1 can black beans
  • 1 bag baby spinach
  • 3 oz reduced-fat shredded cheddar cheese
  • kosher salt
  • black pepper
  • chili powder
  • ground cumin
  • garlic powder
  • smoked paprika
  • extra-virgin olive oil
  • onion powder

Steps

  1. Pasta: cook Barilla Protein+ pasta in boiling water until al dente (about 8–10 minutes depending on shape). Drain, reserve 1/2 cup pasta water.
  2. Turkey: cook 1.50 lb lean ground turkey 7–9 minutes until browned. Season with 2 tsp chili powder, 2 tsp cumin, 2 tsp garlic powder, 1 tsp onion powder, 1/2 tsp kosher salt, and 1/2 tsp black pepper.
  3. Beans: drain/rinse one 15 oz can black beans. Add 1 1/2 cups beans to turkey; warm 2 minutes.
  4. Sauce: stir in 1 cup salsa + 1/4 cup reserved pasta water; simmer 2 minutes.
  5. Spinach: stir in 4 cups baby spinach until wilted (about 60–90 seconds).
  6. Combine: fold cooked pasta into the skillet. Sprinkle 3 oz reduced-fat cheddar over the pan; cover 2 minutes to melt.
  7. Bowls: portion per Notes.

Notes

Comforty ‘taco mac’ vibe using protein pasta and salsa instead of heavy sauce. Portions (per person): Frank: About 2 1/4 cups skillet pasta (roughly 1/3 of pan) Susan: About 1 3/4 cups skillet pasta (roughly 1/4 of pan) Elise: About 1 3/4 cups skillet pasta (roughly 1/4 of pan) Tips (recipe-specific): Use reserved pasta water so the salsa coats the pasta like a real sauce. Keep cheese measured; salsa + spices do the heavy lifting.
