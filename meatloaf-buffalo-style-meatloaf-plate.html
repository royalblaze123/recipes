Buffalo-Style Meatloaf Plate

Nutrition (approx)

Frank: 630 kcal • P 55g • C 35g • F 25g
Susan/Elise (each): 472 kcal • P 41g • C 26g • F 19g

Ingredients (purchase-clean)

  • 1.50 lb 93% lean ground beef
  • 1 each yellow onion
  • 2 each large eggs
  • 3/4 cup whole wheat panko breadcrumbs
  • 8 tsp hot sauce
  • 2 tsp garlic powder
  • 1 tsp black pepper
  • 1 tsp kosher salt
  • 1.50 lb baby potatoes
  • 12 oz baby carrots
  • 1 bunch celery
  • 4 tsp extra-virgin olive oil

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 400°F. Line a sheet pan with parchment.
  2. Dice the yellow onion small.
  3. Meatloaf mix: combine ground beef, onion, eggs, panko, 6 tsp hot sauce, garlic powder, black pepper, and kosher salt. Mix just until combined.
  4. Form loaf on the sheet pan. Bake 35–40 minutes until 160°F in the center.
  5. Finish sauce: brush the top with remaining 2 tsp hot sauce right after baking.
  6. Potatoes: quarter baby potatoes; toss with 2 tsp olive oil and roast 25–30 minutes, flipping once, until browned and tender.
  7. Veg crunch: cut celery into sticks. Serve baby carrots and celery as a crisp side alongside the plate.
  8. Rest meatloaf 10 minutes before slicing.

Notes

Buffalo-style meatloaf for big flavor, paired with roasted potatoes and crunchy veg.
