Skillet Steak Fajitas + Baby Potatoes

Nutrition (approx)

Frank: 650 kcal • P 58g • C 40g • F 23g
Susan/Elise (each): 488 kcal • P 44g • C 30g • F 17g

Ingredients (purchase-clean)

  • 1.50 lb sirloin steak
  • 2 each bell peppers
  • 1 each yellow onion
  • 4/5 package whole-grain small tortillas
  • 1.50 lb baby potatoes
  • 6 tsp extra-virgin olive oil
  • 1 each lime
  • 1 jar salsa
  • 2 tsp chili powder
  • 2 tsp cumin
  • 2 tsp garlic powder
  • 1 tsp smoked paprika
  • 1 tsp black pepper
  • 1 tsp kosher salt

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 425°F. Line a sheet pan with parchment for the potatoes.
  2. Baby potatoes: quarter potatoes. Toss with 2 tsp olive oil and 1/2 tsp kosher salt. Roast 25–30 minutes, flipping once, until browned and tender.
  3. Steak: pat sirloin dry. Slice thin against the grain. Season with 2 tsp chili powder, 2 tsp cumin, 2 tsp garlic powder, 1 tsp smoked paprika, 1 tsp black pepper, and 1 tsp kosher salt.
  4. Veg: slice bell peppers and onion into thin strips.
  5. Skillet cook: heat a large skillet over medium-high. Add 4 tsp olive oil. Cook steak 2–3 minutes, stirring often, until just cooked. Transfer steak to a plate.
  6. Cook veg: in the same skillet, cook peppers/onion 5–7 minutes until tender-crisp and browned at edges.
  7. Finish: return steak to skillet with 2 tsp lime juice; toss 30 seconds.
  8. Tortillas: warm tortillas 20–30 seconds per side.
  9. Serve with salsa and potatoes. Portion per Notes.

Notes

Steak fajitas with roasted baby potatoes and salsa on the side. Portions (per person): Frank: Build a plate: 3 tortillas + 1/3 of the fajita mix + 1/3 of the side Susan: Build a plate: 2 tortillas + 1/4 of the fajita mix + 1/4 of the side Elise: Build a plate: 2 tortillas + 1/4 of the fajita mix + 1/4 of the side
