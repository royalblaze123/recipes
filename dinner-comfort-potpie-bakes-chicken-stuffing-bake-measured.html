Chicken & Stuffing Bake (Measured)

Nutrition (approx)

Frank: 645 kcal • P 60g • C 58g • F 16g • Sat 5g • Na 700mg • Fiber 10g
Susan/Elise (each): 484 kcal • P 45g • C 44g • F 12g • Sat 4g • Na 525mg • Fiber 8g

Ingredients (purchase-clean)

  • 1.75 lb boneless skinless chicken breast
  • 1 box stuffing mix
  • 1 bag frozen green beans
  • low-sodium chicken broth
  • 1 carton skim milk
  • kosher salt
  • black pepper
  • garlic powder
  • onion powder
  • Italian seasoning
  • smoked paprika
  • cornstarch
  • extra-virgin olive oil
  • low-sodium chicken broth

Steps

  1. Oven: heat to 400°F.
  2. Chicken: dice and sauté chicken with 2 tsp olive oil, 1 tsp garlic powder, 1 tsp onion powder, 1/2 tsp kosher salt, black pepper 8–10 minutes to 165°F.
  3. Green beans: roast 1 bag frozen green beans at 425°F with 2 tsp olive oil and black pepper for 14–18 minutes.
  4. Stuffing: prepare stuffing using 1 1/2 cups low-sodium broth (measured) per box directions.
  5. Base sauce: whisk 2 tbsp cornstarch into 1 1/2 cups skim milk. Simmer 3 minutes until thick.
  6. Assemble: mix chicken with sauce in baking dish; spread stuffing on top.
  7. Bake 15–18 minutes until hot. Rest 5 minutes. Serve with green beans per Notes.

Notes

Holiday-style chicken and stuffing comfort, but portions and liquid are controlled. Portions (per person): Frank: 2 1/2 cups bake + 1 cup green beans Susan: 2 cups bake + 3/4 cup green beans Elise: 2 cups bake + 3/4 cup green beans Tips (recipe-specific): Use measured broth so stuffing stays fluffy, not soupy. Roasted green beans keep the plate feeling like a ‘full dinner’.
