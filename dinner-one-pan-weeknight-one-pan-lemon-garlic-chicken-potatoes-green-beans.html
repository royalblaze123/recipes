One-Pan Lemon-Garlic Chicken + Potatoes + Green Beans

Nutrition (approx)

Frank: 640 kcal • P 58g • C 55g • F 16g • Sat 4g • Na 680mg • Fiber 10g
Susan/Elise (each): 480 kcal • P 44g • C 41g • F 12g • Sat 3g • Na 510mg • Fiber 8g

Ingredients (purchase-clean)

  • 1.75 lb boneless skinless chicken breast
  • 1.50 lb baby potatoes
  • 1 bag frozen green beans
  • 1 each lemon
  • kosher salt
  • black pepper
  • garlic powder
  • onion powder
  • smoked paprika
  • Italian seasoning
  • ground cumin
  • chili powder
  • red pepper flakes
  • extra-virgin olive oil
  • low-sodium chicken broth

Steps

  1. Oven: heat to 425°F. Line a sheet pan with foil or parchment.
  2. Potatoes: halve baby potatoes. Toss with 2 tsp olive oil, 1 tsp garlic powder, 1 tsp smoked paprika, 1/2 tsp kosher salt, 1/2 tsp black pepper. Roast 15 minutes.
  3. Chicken: toss chicken with 2 tsp olive oil, 1 tsp garlic powder, 1 tsp onion powder, 1 tsp Italian seasoning, 1/2 tsp kosher salt, 1/2 tsp black pepper, and 2 tsp lemon juice.
  4. Add chicken to pan. Roast 10 minutes.
  5. Green beans: add frozen green beans, toss with 1 tsp olive oil, black pepper, and 1 tsp lemon juice. Roast 10–12 minutes until chicken is 165°F and beans are browned.
  6. Rest 5 minutes. Serve per Notes.

Notes

Weeknight sheet-pan dinner with real flavor from lemon + browned edges, not heavy sauces. Portions (per person): Frank: About 7 oz chicken + 1 cup potatoes + 1 cup green beans Susan: About 5 oz chicken + 3/4 cup potatoes + 3/4 cup green beans Elise: About 5 oz chicken + 3/4 cup potatoes + 3/4 cup green beans Tips (recipe-specific): Roast potatoes first so they actually brown. Finish with a squeeze of lemon right before serving to wake up flavor.
