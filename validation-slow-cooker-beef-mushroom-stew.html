Slow Cooker Beef & Mushroom Stew

Servings

3 servings (Frank 1.0×, Susan 0.75×, Elise 0.75×)

Times

  • Prep time: 20 minutes
  • Cook time: 8 hours
  • Total time: 8 hours 20 minutes

Ingredients

  • 2 lb beef chuck roast
  • 1.25 lb baby potatoes
  • 12 oz mushrooms
  • 1 lb carrots
  • 1 yellow onion
  • 2 cups low-sodium beef broth
  • 3 tsp olive oil
  • 3 tsp Worcestershire sauce
  • 3 tsp tomato paste
  • 3 tsp garlic powder
  • 2 tsp onion powder
  • 2 tsp dried thyme
  • 2 tsp black pepper
  • 2 tsp salt substitute
  • 2 tbsp cornstarch

Recipe

  1. Cube beef and optionally sear in olive oil for flavor.
  2. Add prepared vegetables to slow cooker.
  3. Whisk broth and seasonings and pour over contents.
  4. Cook on LOW for 8 hours or HIGH for 4–5 hours.
  5. Stir in cornstarch slurry during final 20 minutes to thicken.
  6. Serve according to household portions.

Notes

Portions (per person):

Frank – 1 bowl (8 oz cooked beef, 1 cup potatoes, 1 1/2 cups vegetables).

Susan – 1 bowl (6 oz cooked beef, 3/4 cup potatoes, 1 1/4 cups vegetables).

Elise – 1 bowl (6 oz cooked beef, 3/4 cup potatoes, 1 1/4 cups vegetables).

  • Elise: 480 kcal · Protein 40 g · Carbs 26 g · Fat 15 g · Fiber 5 g · Sodium 390 mg
