Chicken Spinach Feta Pizza

NOTES: Built as a full dinner using ready-made whole-grain or thin crust, lean protein, plenty of vegetables, and controlled cheese to support A1c, cholesterol, and blood pressure goals. Use low-sodium sauce and minimal added salt. PORTION SIZES: Frank: ~40% of the pizza; Susan: ~30% of the pizza; Elise: ~30% of the pizza (Frank = 1.0×, Susan/Elise = 0.75× portions).

Ingredients

  • 1 ready-made 12-inch thin whole-wheat pizza crust
  • 1/2 cup plain 2% Greek yogurt
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice
  • 8 oz cooked boneless, skinless chicken breast, chopped
  • 2 cups fresh baby spinach, roughly chopped
  • 1/4 red onion, thinly sliced
  • 1/3 cup crumbled reduced-fat feta cheese
  • 1/3 cup shredded reduced-fat mozzarella cheese
  • 1 tsp dried oregano
  • Freshly ground black pepper, to taste

Step-by-Step Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 425°F and place the pizza crust on a pizza pan or parchment-lined baking sheet.
  2. In a small bowl, mix Greek yogurt, minced garlic, and lemon juice until smooth.
  3. Spread yogurt mixture evenly over the crust as a white sauce base.
  4. Scatter chopped cooked chicken evenly over the sauce.
  5. Distribute chopped spinach and sliced red onion over the top.
  6. Sprinkle crumbled feta and shredded mozzarella evenly over the pizza.
  7. Season with dried oregano and a few grinds of black pepper.
  8. Bake 12–15 minutes, until the crust is crisp and the cheese is melted and lightly golden.
  9. Cool 3–5 minutes, then slice and portion for Frank (~40%) and Susan/Elise (~30% each).

Nutrition per Person

  • Frank: ~590 kcal, ~53 g protein, ~48 g carbs, ~18 g fat.
  • Susan: ~445 kcal, ~40 g protein, ~36 g carbs, ~13 g fat.
  • Elise: ~445 kcal, ~40 g protein, ~36 g carbs, ~13 g fat.
