Chicken Farro Salad with Roasted Peppers and Cucumber

Prep: 22 min   Cook: 18 min   Total: 40 min

Ingredients

  • 14 oz chicken breast
  • 0.5 cup dry farro
  • 0.5 jar roasted red peppers
  • 1 cucumber
  • 1 bag arugula
  • 1 lemon
  • olive oil
  • black pepper
  • salt substitute
  • vinegar
  • Italian seasoning

Hard rules enforced: Lunch validations (calories, sat fat, sodium, fiber, added sugar). Quantities appear before items; fresh meat/poultry/fish use weights. Condiments/spices/seasonings have no measurements in Ingredients; all measurements are in Steps. No olives, no squash, no Brussels sprouts, no zucchini. Yogurt is not used in dinners.

Step-by-step Instructions

  1. Farro: cook 1/2 cup dry farro per package directions (water in Steps only). Cool slightly.
  2. Chicken: season with Italian seasoning, black pepper, and salt substitute. Cook with 2 tsp olive oil 6–7 minutes per side to 165°F. Slice.
  3. Roasted peppers: drain and pat dry; slice.
  4. Build salad: arugula + farro + cucumber + peppers + chicken.
  5. Dressing: whisk 2 tsp olive oil + 2 tsp lemon juice + 1 tsp vinegar + black pepper + salt substitute. Toss lightly.
  6. Portion: Frank large bowl; Susan/Elise ~3/4 bowl each.
