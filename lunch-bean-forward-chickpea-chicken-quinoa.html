Bean-Forward Lunch: Chickpea Chicken Quinoa Bowl with Cucumber

Prep: 18 min   Cook: 16 min   Total: 34 min

Ingredients

  • 1.0 lb chicken breast
  • 0.5 cup dry quinoa
  • 0.5 can no-salt-added chickpeas
  • 1 cucumber
  • 1 pint cherry tomatoes
  • 1 lemon
  • olive oil
  • Italian seasoning
  • garlic powder
  • black pepper
  • salt substitute
  • vinegar

Rules enforced: quantities before items; fresh meat/poultry/fish use weights; packaged items use fractional containers when partial. Condiments/spices/seasonings have no measurements in Ingredients; all measurements are in Steps. No olives, no squash, no Brussels sprouts, no zucchini, no tofu/tempeh. Lunch validations included in Notes.

Step-by-step Instructions

  1. Cook quinoa: rinse 1/2 cup quinoa. Simmer with 1 cup water 15 minutes; rest 5 minutes.
  2. Chicken: season 1.0 lb chicken breast with 2 tsp Italian seasoning + 1 tsp garlic powder + 1/2 tsp pepper + 1/2 tsp salt substitute. Cook with 2 tsp olive oil 6–8 minutes until 165°F; slice.
  3. Chickpeas: drain and rinse 1/2 can chickpeas; measure 3/4 cup for bowls; refrigerate remainder.
  4. Chop: dice 1 cucumber and halve 1 pint cherry tomatoes.
  5. Dressing: whisk 2 tsp vinegar + 2 tsp lemon juice + 1 tsp olive oil + pepper.
  6. Build bowls: quinoa + chicken + chickpeas + cucumber/tomatoes; toss lightly with dressing.
  7. Portion: Frank largest quinoa/chicken share; Susan/Elise ~75%.
