Lean Beef Taco Tacos (Stovetop)

Nutrition (approx)

Frank: 525 kcal • P 42g • C 38g • F 18g
Susan/Elise (each): 394 kcal • P 32g • C 28g • F 14g

Ingredients (purchase-clean)

  • 1.25 lb 93% lean ground beef
  • 4/5 package whole-grain small tortillas
  • 2 each bell peppers
  • 1 each yellow onion
  • 1 jar salsa
  • 1 tub 2% plain Greek yogurt
  • 2 tsp Worcestershire sauce
  • 2 tsp chili powder
  • 2 tsp cumin
  • 2 tsp garlic powder
  • 1 tsp smoked paprika
  • 1 tsp black pepper
  • 1 tsp kosher salt

Steps

  1. Veg: dice bell peppers and yellow onion into 1/2-inch pieces.
  2. Cook beef: heat a large nonstick skillet over medium-high. Add ground beef and cook 7–9 minutes until browned. Drain excess liquid if needed.
  3. Add veg: stir in peppers and onion. Cook 5–7 minutes until tender-crisp.
  4. Season: add 2 tsp chili powder, 2 tsp cumin, 2 tsp garlic powder, 1 tsp smoked paprika, 1 tsp black pepper, 1 tsp kosher salt, and 2 tsp Worcestershire. Stir 60 seconds.
  5. Salsa: stir in 1/2 cup salsa, then simmer 2 minutes to thicken slightly.
  6. Tortillas: warm tortillas 20–30 seconds per side.
  7. Top each taco with a dollop of Greek yogurt and a spoon of salsa. Portion 7 tacos per Notes.

Notes

Classic beef tacos with peppers and onions for volume and flavor. Portions (per person): Frank: 3 tacos (use 3 tortillas) + 1/3 of the filling + 1/3 of the toppings Susan: 2 tacos (use 2 tortillas) + 1/4 of the filling + 1/4 of the toppings Elise: 2 tacos (use 2 tortillas) + 1/4 of the filling + 1/4 of the toppings
