Salisbury Turkey Patties + Onion Gravy + Mash

Nutrition (approx)

Frank: 650 kcal • P 60g • C 55g • F 18g • Sat 6g • Na 700mg • Fiber 10g
Susan/Elise (each): 488 kcal • P 45g • C 41g • F 14g • Sat 4g • Na 525mg • Fiber 8g

Ingredients (purchase-clean)

  • 2.00 lb 93% lean ground turkey
  • 1 each onion
  • low-sodium beef broth
  • cornstarch
  • 1 box instant mashed potatoes
  • 1 bag frozen green beans
  • kosher salt
  • black pepper
  • garlic powder
  • onion powder
  • Italian seasoning
  • smoked paprika
  • cornstarch
  • extra-virgin olive oil
  • low-sodium chicken broth
  • Worcestershire sauce

Steps

  1. Oven: heat to 425°F. Roast frozen green beans with 2 tsp olive oil and black pepper for 14–18 minutes.
  2. Patties: form 6 patties from turkey. Season with garlic powder, onion powder, black pepper, modest kosher salt, and 1 tsp Worcestershire.
  3. Skillet: cook patties 5–6 minutes per side to 165°F. Remove to plate.
  4. Onion gravy: sauté sliced onion in pan with 1 tsp olive oil 6 minutes. Add 1 1/2 cups low-sodium beef broth.
  5. Thicken: whisk 1 tbsp cornstarch into 1/4 cup cold water; stir into broth; simmer 2–3 minutes until gravy coats spoon.
  6. Return patties to gravy for 2 minutes to coat.
  7. Mash: prepare instant mashed potatoes per box for 3 servings.
  8. Serve per Notes.

Notes

Diner comfort: salisbury patties and gravy, but built from lean turkey and measured mash. Portions (per person): Frank: 2 patties + 1 cup mash + 1 cup green beans Susan: 1 patty + 3/4 cup mash + 3/4 cup green beans Elise: 1 patty + 3/4 cup mash + 3/4 cup green beans Tips (recipe-specific): Use thick gravy (not lots of it) so a little goes a long way. Roasted green beans keep the plate from feeling too heavy.
