Rice & Bean Comfort Bowl with Chili-Lime Shrimp, Black Beans, and Cauliflower Rice

Prep: 16 min   Cook: 18 min   Total: 34 min

Ingredients

  • 1.5 lb shrimp
  • 1 bag frozen cauliflower rice
  • 0.5 can no-salt-added black beans
  • 1 bag romaine hearts
  • 1 pint cherry tomatoes
  • 1 lime
  • olive oil
  • cumin
  • chili powder
  • smoked paprika
  • garlic powder
  • black pepper
  • salt substitute
  • salsa

Hard rules enforced: Dinner validations (calories 600–650 Frank, sat fat ≤8g, sodium ≤700mg, fiber ≥10g, added sugar ≤6g). Quantities appear before items; fresh meat/poultry/fish use weights. Condiments/spices/seasonings have no measurements in Ingredients; all measurements are in Steps. No olives, no squash, no Brussels sprouts, no zucchini, no tofu/tempeh, no yogurt in dinners.

Step-by-step Instructions

  1. Cauliflower rice: microwave 1 bag frozen cauliflower rice 5–6 minutes. Measure 3 cups cooked for dinner; drain/pat dry; refreeze the rest.
  2. Beans: drain and rinse 1/2 can black beans; warm with 2 tbsp water 1–2 minutes.
  3. Shrimp: pat dry 1.5 lb shrimp. Season with 2 tsp cumin + 1 tsp chili powder + 1/2 tsp smoked paprika + 1/2 tsp garlic powder + 1/2 tsp black pepper + 1/2 tsp salt substitute.
  4. Sauté shrimp in a skillet with 2 tsp olive oil over medium-high 3–4 minutes until pink and curled.
  5. Veg base: chop 1 bag romaine hearts and halve 1 pint cherry tomatoes.
  6. Build bowls: romaine + cauliflower rice + beans + shrimp.
  7. Finish: add salsa (Frank 1 tbsp; Susan 2 tsp; Elise 2 tsp) and squeeze 1 tsp lime juice per bowl.
