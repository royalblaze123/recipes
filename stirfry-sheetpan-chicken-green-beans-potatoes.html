Sheet Pan Teriyaki-Style Chicken and Green Beans with Potatoes

Prep: 18 min   Cook: 28 min   Total: 46 min

Ingredients

  • 1.75 lb chicken breast
  • 1 lb green beans
  • 1.25 lb baby potatoes
  • olive oil
  • low-sodium teriyaki sauce
  • rice vinegar
  • garlic
  • ginger
  • black pepper
  • salt substitute

Hard rules enforced: Dinner validations (calories 600–650 Frank, sat fat ≤8g, sodium ≤700mg, fiber ≥10g, added sugar ≤6g). Quantities appear before items; fresh meat/poultry/fish use weights. Condiments/spices/seasonings have no measurements in Ingredients; all measurements are in Steps. No olives, no squash, no Brussels sprouts, no zucchini, no tofu/tempeh, no yogurt in dinners.

Step-by-step Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 425°F. Line a sheet pan.
  2. Potatoes: halve baby potatoes. Toss with 2 tsp olive oil + black pepper + salt substitute. Roast 12 minutes.
  3. Chicken: cut into chunks. Toss with a measured ‘teriyaki-style’ mix: 3 tbsp low-sodium teriyaki sauce + 1 tbsp rice vinegar + grated ginger + minced garlic + black pepper. Add chicken to pan; roast 10 minutes.
  4. Green beans: toss with 1 tsp olive oil + black pepper + salt substitute. Add to pan; roast 8–10 minutes until tender-crisp and chicken hits 165°F.
  5. Finish with a splash of rice vinegar if needed for brightness (measured: 1 tsp).
  6. Portion: Frank largest chicken/potato share; Susan/Elise ~75%.
