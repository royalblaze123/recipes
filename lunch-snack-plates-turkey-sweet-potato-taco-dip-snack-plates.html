Turkey + Sweet Potato Taco Dip Snack Plates

Nutrition (approx)

Frank: 545 kcal • P 48g • C 55g • F 16g
Susan/Elise (each): 409 kcal • P 36g • C 41g • F 12g

Ingredients (purchase-clean)

  • 0.75 lb 93% lean ground turkey
  • 1.25 lb sweet potatoes
  • 1 tub plain nonfat Greek yogurt
  • 1/2 jar salsa
  • 1 each avocado
  • 1 bag mini sweet peppers
  • 1 each cucumber
  • 1 pint cherry tomatoes
  • 1 bag baby carrots
  • 1 bag baked tortilla chips
  • 1 each lime
  • 1 tsp black pepper
  • 1 tsp kosher salt
  • 2 tsp chili powder
  • 2 tsp cumin
  • 2 tsp garlic powder
  • 1 tsp smoked paprika

Steps

  1. Sweet potatoes: roast whole or diced at 425°F for 25–35 minutes until very tender. Mash 1 cup roasted sweet potato.
  2. Turkey: cook 7–9 minutes until browned; season with spices.
  3. Dip base: mix 1 cup Greek yogurt with 1/2 cup salsa and 2 tsp lime juice.
  4. Stir mashed sweet potato into the dip until smooth, then fold in 1 1/2 cups turkey.
  5. Veg prep: slice cucumber into half-moons. Halve cherry tomatoes. Slice mini sweet peppers into strips. Add baby carrots.
  6. Chips: count out 10 chips per Frank portion (use about 30 chips total for the batch; see Notes for scaling).
  7. Avocado: halve, pit, and slice. Use about 1/2 avocado total; refrigerate remaining avocado with the pit to slow browning.
  8. Plate: divide dip, veggies, chips, and avocado onto three plates. Portion per Notes.

Notes

Thick, scoopable turkey taco dip boosted with sweet potato; measured chips keep it on target. Portions (per person): Frank: 1 large snack plate + 10 chips (measured) + 1/3 avocado Susan: 1 medium snack plate + 10 chips (measured) × 0.75 + 1/4 avocado Elise: 1 medium snack plate + 10 chips (measured) × 0.75 + 1/4 avocado
