Chicken Pesto Tomato Pizza (Light Pesto)

NOTES: Built as a full dinner using ready-made whole-grain or thin crust, lean protein, plenty of vegetables, and controlled cheese to support A1c, cholesterol, and blood pressure goals. Use low-sodium sauce and minimal added salt. PORTION SIZES: Frank: ~40% of the pizza; Susan: ~30% of the pizza; Elise: ~30% of the pizza (Frank = 1.0×, Susan/Elise = 0.75× portions).

Ingredients

  • 1 ready-made 12-inch thin whole-wheat pizza crust
  • 2 tbsp prepared basil pesto
  • 1/4 cup plain 2% Greek yogurt
  • 8 oz cooked boneless, skinless chicken breast, sliced or chopped
  • 1 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1 cup fresh baby spinach, roughly chopped
  • 1/2 cup shredded reduced-fat mozzarella cheese
  • Fresh basil leaves for garnish (optional)
  • Freshly ground black pepper, to taste

Step-by-Step Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 425°F and place the crust on a pizza pan or parchment-lined baking sheet.
  2. In a small bowl, stir together basil pesto and Greek yogurt to make a lighter pesto sauce.
  3. Spread the pesto-yogurt mixture evenly over the crust.
  4. Distribute sliced or chopped chicken evenly over the sauce.
  5. Top with halved cherry tomatoes and chopped spinach.
  6. Sprinkle shredded mozzarella over the toppings and add a few grinds of black pepper.
  7. Bake 12–15 minutes, until the crust is crisp and the cheese is melted and lightly golden.
  8. Garnish with fresh basil leaves if desired, then cool 3–5 minutes before slicing and portioning.

Nutrition per Person

  • Frank: ~610 kcal, ~53 g protein, ~49 g carbs, ~20 g fat.
  • Susan: ~455 kcal, ~40 g protein, ~37 g carbs, ~15 g fat.
  • Elise: ~455 kcal, ~40 g protein, ~37 g carbs, ~15 g fat.
