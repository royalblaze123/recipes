Turkey & Sweet Potato Tostada Plates

Nutrition (approx)

Frank: 650 kcal • P 58g • C 54g • F 20g
Susan/Elise (each): 488 kcal • P 44g • C 40g • F 15g

Ingredients (purchase-clean)

  • 1.50 lb 93% lean ground turkey
  • 1.25 lb sweet potatoes
  • 1 package baked tostada shells
  • 1 each avocado
  • 1/2 jar salsa
  • 6 tsp extra-virgin olive oil
  • 2 tsp chili powder
  • 2 tsp cumin
  • 2 tsp garlic powder
  • 1 tsp smoked paprika
  • 1 tsp black pepper
  • 1 tsp kosher salt

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 425°F. Line a sheet pan with parchment.
  2. Sweet potatoes: small dice (about 1/2-inch). Toss with 4 tsp olive oil and a pinch of kosher salt. Roast 18–22 minutes, stirring once, until browned and tender.
  3. Tostadas: warm in a 350°F oven for 3–4 minutes to crisp and heat through.
  4. Turkey: cook ground turkey in a skillet 7–9 minutes until browned. Stir in the spices for 60 seconds.
  5. Combine: fold roasted sweet potato into the turkey and warm 1 minute.
  6. Avocado: halve, pit, and dice. Use about 1/2 avocado total; refrigerate remaining avocado with the pit to slow browning.
  7. Serve: top tostadas with turkey-sweet potato mix, salsa, and avocado. Portion per Notes.

Notes

Sweet potato ‘hash’ folded into taco turkey makes tostadas feel hearty while still controlled. Portions (per person): Frank: 2 tostadas + 1/3 of the protein topping + 1/3 of the avocado + salsa Susan: 1 tostada + 1/4 of the protein topping + 1/4 of the avocado + salsa Elise: 1 tostada + 1/4 of the protein topping + 1/4 of the avocado + salsa
