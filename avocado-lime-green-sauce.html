Avocado-Lime Green Sauce

Category: Sauce • Yield: About 1 1/4 cups (19 tbsp) • AnyList-ready

Ingredients

  • 1 avocado
  • 2 tbsp 2% plain Greek yogurt
  • 2 tsp lime juice
  • 1 tsp extra-virgin olive oil
  • 2 tbsp fresh cilantro
  • 1/2 tsp ground cumin
  • 1/4 tsp black pepper

Steps

  1. Blend or mash avocado, Greek yogurt, lime juice, olive oil, cilantro, cumin, and black pepper until smooth.
  2. Thin with 1–4 tbsp water, adding 1 tbsp at a time, until drizzle-ready.
