Skillet Lean Beef and Pepper Stir-Fry with Brown Rice

Prep: 20 min   Cook: 20 min   Total: 40 min

Ingredients

  • 1.5 lb lean sirloin steak
  • 2 bell peppers
  • 1 onion
  • 1 bag frozen broccoli florets
  • 0.5 cup dry brown rice
  • olive oil
  • low-sodium soy sauce
  • rice vinegar
  • sesame oil
  • garlic
  • ginger
  • black pepper
  • salt substitute
  • cornstarch

Hard rules enforced: Dinner validations (calories 600–650 Frank, sat fat ≤8g, sodium ≤700mg, fiber ≥10g, added sugar ≤6g). Quantities appear before items; fresh meat/poultry/fish use weights. Condiments/spices/seasonings have no measurements in Ingredients; all measurements are in Steps. No olives, no squash, no Brussels sprouts, no zucchini, no tofu/tempeh, no yogurt in dinners.

Step-by-step Instructions

  1. Cook brown rice: cook 1/2 cup dry brown rice per package directions (water in Steps only).
  2. Broccoli: microwave frozen broccoli 3–4 minutes to thaw; pat dry. Measure 3 cups; refreeze remainder.
  3. Sauce: whisk 2 tbsp low-sodium soy sauce + 1 tbsp rice vinegar + 1 tsp sesame oil + 1 tsp cornstarch + 2 tbsp water + grated ginger + minced garlic.
  4. Beef: slice sirloin thin across the grain. Sear in a hot skillet with 2 tsp olive oil 2–3 minutes; remove to a plate.
  5. Veg: stir-fry peppers and onion 4 minutes; add broccoli 2 minutes.
  6. Finish: return beef; pour sauce; toss 1–2 minutes until thickened.
  7. Serve over brown rice. Portion: Frank largest beef/rice share; Susan/Elise ~75%.
