Mustard-Vinaigrette Chicken Salad + Roasted Carrots

Nutrition (approx)

Frank: 640 kcal • P 58g • C 45g • F 20g • Sat 3g • Na 630mg • Fiber 12g
Susan/Elise (each): 480 kcal • P 44g • C 34g • F 15g • Sat 2g • Na 472mg • Fiber 9g

Ingredients (purchase-clean)

  • 1.75 lb boneless skinless chicken breast
  • 1 lb carrots
  • 1 head romaine lettuce
  • 1 each lemon
  • kosher salt
  • black pepper
  • garlic powder
  • onion powder
  • smoked paprika
  • Italian seasoning
  • ground cumin
  • chili powder
  • red pepper flakes
  • extra-virgin olive oil
  • balsamic vinegar
  • apple cider vinegar
  • Dijon mustard

Steps

  1. Oven: heat to 425°F.
  2. Carrots: slice carrots on a bias. Toss with 2 tsp olive oil, smoked paprika, black pepper, and a modest pinch of kosher salt. Roast 20–24 minutes until browned.
  3. Chicken: roast chicken with garlic powder and black pepper 12–15 minutes to 165°F; rest; slice.
  4. Dressing: whisk 2 tbsp Dijon mustard, 2 tbsp lemon juice, 2 tbsp apple cider vinegar, 2 tbsp olive oil, black pepper, and a small pinch of kosher salt.
  5. Salad: chop romaine; toss with dressing; top with chicken and roasted carrots.
  6. Serve per Notes.

Notes

‘Honey mustard’ vibe without sugar—Dijon + lemon + vinegar does it. Portions (per person): Frank: 4 cups salad + 7 oz chicken + 1 cup roasted carrots Susan: 3 cups salad + 5 oz chicken + 3/4 cup carrots Elise: 3 cups salad + 5 oz chicken + 3/4 cup carrots Tips (recipe-specific): Roast carrots until browned—caramelization mimics sweetness. Add dressing gradually so the salad stays crisp.
