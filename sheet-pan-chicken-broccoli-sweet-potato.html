Sheet Pan Chicken, Broccoli, and Sweet Potatoes

Prep: 18 min   Cook: 28 min   Total: 46 min

Ingredients

  • 1.75 lb chicken breast
  • 2 sweet potatoes
  • 1 bag frozen broccoli florets
  • olive oil
  • garlic powder
  • smoked paprika
  • black pepper
  • salt substitute
  • lemon

Hard rules enforced: Dinner validations (calories, sat fat, sodium, fiber, added sugar). Quantities appear before items; fresh meat/poultry/fish use weights. Condiments/spices/seasonings have no measurements in Ingredients; all measurements are in Steps. No olives, no squash, no Brussels sprouts, no zucchini. Yogurt is not used in dinners.

Step-by-step Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 425°F. Line a sheet pan with foil/parchment.
  2. Sweet potatoes: peel and cube. Toss with 2 tsp olive oil + garlic powder + black pepper + salt substitute. Spread on pan. Roast 12 minutes.
  3. Chicken: cut into 1-inch chunks. Season with smoked paprika, garlic powder, black pepper, and salt substitute. Toss with 2 tsp olive oil. Add to pan and roast 10 minutes.
  4. Broccoli: microwave frozen broccoli 3–4 minutes to thaw. Pat very dry. Measure 3 cups for dinner; refreeze the rest. Toss broccoli with 1 tsp olive oil + black pepper + salt substitute. Add to pan and roast 8–10 minutes until browned.
  5. Finish with lemon squeeze.
  6. Portion: Frank gets the largest portion of chicken and sweet potatoes; Susan/Elise each get ~75%.
