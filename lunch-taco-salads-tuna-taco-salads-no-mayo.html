Tuna Taco Salads (No Mayo)

Nutrition (approx)

Frank: 520 kcal • P 52g • C 42g • F 15g
Susan/Elise (each): 390 kcal • P 39g • C 32g • F 11g

Ingredients (purchase-clean)

  • 3 cans tuna
  • 1 bag shredded lettuce
  • 1 each avocado
  • 1/2 jar salsa
  • 1 each cucumber
  • 1 pint cherry tomatoes
  • 1 each lime
  • 4 tsp extra-virgin olive oil
  • 1 tub plain nonfat Greek yogurt
  • 2 tsp garlic powder
  • 1 tsp black pepper
  • 1 tsp kosher salt

Steps

  1. Tuna: Can size note—use a standard 5 oz can tuna.. Use three 5 oz cans tuna. Drain very well.
  2. Season tuna with garlic powder, black pepper, and kosher salt.
  3. Dressing (lunch-only yogurt): mix 1/2 cup Greek yogurt with 2 tsp lime juice and 1 tsp chili powder.
  4. Base: divide shredded lettuce into three bowls.
  5. Protein: add tuna to each bowl.
  6. Add-ins: add diced cucumber and halved tomatoes.
  7. Drizzle dressing, then top with salsa and measured diced avocado. Portion per Notes.
  8. Avocado: halve, pit, and dice. Use about 1/2 avocado total; refrigerate remaining avocado with the pit to slow browning.

Notes

Pantry taco salads using tuna (no mayo), with salsa + avocado and a light lunch-only yogurt dressing. Portions (per person): Frank: 1 large taco salad bowl + 1/3 of the avocado + salsa Susan: 1 medium taco salad bowl + 1/4 of the avocado + salsa Elise: 1 medium taco salad bowl + 1/4 of the avocado + salsa
