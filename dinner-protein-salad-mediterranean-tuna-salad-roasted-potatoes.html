Mediterranean Tuna Salad + Roasted Potatoes

Nutrition (approx)

Frank: 645 kcal • P 58g • C 55g • F 16g • Sat 3g • Na 650mg • Fiber 10g
Susan/Elise (each): 484 kcal • P 44g • C 41g • F 12g • Sat 2g • Na 488mg • Fiber 8g

Ingredients (purchase-clean)

  • 4 cans tuna
  • 1 head romaine lettuce
  • 1.50 lb baby potatoes
  • 1 each lemon
  • 1 each cucumber
  • kosher salt
  • black pepper
  • garlic powder
  • onion powder
  • smoked paprika
  • Italian seasoning
  • ground cumin
  • chili powder
  • red pepper flakes
  • extra-virgin olive oil
  • balsamic vinegar
  • apple cider vinegar
  • Dijon mustard

Steps

  1. Oven: heat to 425°F.
  2. Potatoes: roast halved potatoes with 2 tsp olive oil, black pepper, garlic powder, modest kosher salt 22–26 minutes.
  3. Tuna: drain tuna (use 4 standard cans; note size in Steps: 5 oz each).
  4. Dressing: whisk 2 tbsp lemon juice, 2 tbsp balsamic vinegar, 2 tbsp olive oil, Dijon mustard, black pepper, and a small pinch of kosher salt.
  5. Salad: chop romaine and cucumber; toss with dressing.
  6. Top salad with tuna and serve with roasted potatoes per Notes.

Notes

Simple tuna dinner salad with potatoes so it feels like a real meal. Portions (per person): Frank: 4 cups salad + 1 can tuna + 1 cup potatoes Susan: 3 cups salad + 1 can tuna + 3/4 cup potatoes Elise: 3 cups salad + 1 can tuna + 3/4 cup potatoes Tips (recipe-specific): Use 5 oz tuna cans; drain well so the salad stays crisp. Roasted potatoes replace bread as the comfort carb.
