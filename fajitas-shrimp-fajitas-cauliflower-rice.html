Shrimp Fajitas + Cauliflower Rice

Nutrition (approx)

Frank: 610 kcal • P 56g • C 36g • F 21g
Susan/Elise (each): 458 kcal • P 42g • C 27g • F 16g

Ingredients (purchase-clean)

  • 1.50 lb raw shrimp
  • 2 each bell peppers
  • 1 each yellow onion
  • 4/5 package whole-grain small tortillas
  • 1 bag frozen cauliflower rice
  • 6 tsp extra-virgin olive oil
  • 1 each lime
  • 1 tub 2% plain Greek yogurt
  • 2 tsp chili powder
  • 2 tsp cumin
  • 2 tsp garlic powder
  • 1 tsp smoked paprika
  • 1 tsp black pepper
  • 1 tsp kosher salt

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 425°F. Line a sheet pan with parchment.
  2. Veg: slice peppers and onion into thin strips. Toss with 4 tsp olive oil and spread on the sheet pan. Roast 12 minutes.
  3. Shrimp: pat dry. Season with the fajita spices. Toss shrimp with remaining 2 tsp olive oil.
  4. Add shrimp to the sheet pan and roast 6–8 minutes more until shrimp is opaque (avoid overcooking).
  5. Cauliflower rice: microwave per bag directions. Drain any excess moisture; season with a squeeze of lime and a pinch of black pepper.
  6. Tortillas: warm tortillas 20–30 seconds per side.
  7. Yogurt-lime crema: mix 6 tbsp Greek yogurt with 2 tsp lime juice and 1 tsp lime zest.
  8. Serve fajita mix with tortillas, crema, and cauliflower rice. Portion per Notes.

Notes

Fast sheet-pan shrimp fajitas with cauliflower rice and yogurt-lime crema. Portions (per person): Frank: Build a plate: 3 tortillas + 1/3 of the fajita mix + 1/3 of the side Susan: Build a plate: 2 tortillas + 1/4 of the fajita mix + 1/4 of the side Elise: Build a plate: 2 tortillas + 1/4 of the fajita mix + 1/4 of the side
