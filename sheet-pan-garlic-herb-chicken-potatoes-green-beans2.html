Sheet-Pan Garlic Herb Chicken, Baby Potatoes & Green Beans

Notes

Weeknight sheet-pan dinner with oregano-garlic chicken, crisp baby potatoes, and tender green beans.

Portions (per person):
Frank – Chicken 6 oz cooked; Baby potatoes 7.5 oz raw; Green beans 1.5 cups.

Susan – Chicken 4.5 oz cooked; Baby potatoes 5.6 oz raw; Green beans 1.125 cups.

Elise – Chicken 4.5 oz cooked; Baby potatoes 5.6 oz raw; Green beans 1.125 cups.

Tips:
• For crispier potatoes, spread them cut-side down and keep space between pieces.
• If your chicken breasts are very thick, pound to an even thickness so they finish with the potatoes.

Ingredients

  • 20 oz boneless skinless chicken breast
  • 19 oz baby potatoes
  • 16 oz green beans
  • 2 tbsp extra-virgin olive oil
  • 2 tsp Italian seasoning
  • 2 tsp garlic powder
  • 1 tsp smoked paprika
  • 0.5 tsp black pepper
  • 0.25 tsp kosher salt

Steps

  1. Heat oven to 425°F. Line a large rimmed sheet pan with parchment paper.
  2. Wash and dry the baby potatoes. Cut the baby potatoes into halves. Add the baby potatoes to the sheet pan, drizzle with 1 tbsp extra-virgin olive oil, and sprinkle with 1 tsp Italian seasoning, 1 tsp garlic powder, 0.5 tsp smoked paprika, 0.25 tsp black pepper, and 0.25 tsp kosher salt. Toss on the pan, then spread the baby potatoes in a single layer cut-side down. Roast 15 minutes.
  3. While the potatoes roast, pat the boneless skinless chicken breast dry with paper towels. Cut the boneless skinless chicken breast into 3–4 equal-thickness cutlets (or pound to an even thickness). In a bowl, toss the chicken with 1 tbsp extra-virgin olive oil, 1 tsp Italian seasoning, 1 tsp garlic powder, and 0.5 tsp smoked paprika.
  4. Trim the stem ends from the green beans. After the potatoes have roasted 15 minutes, remove the pan from the oven. Push the baby potatoes to the center of the pan. Add the seasoned chicken cutlets to one side of the pan. Add the green beans to the other side of the pan and spread into a single layer.
  5. Return the pan to the oven and roast 14–18 minutes, until the chicken reaches 165°F in the thickest part and the baby potatoes are tender when pierced with a fork. If the green beans brown too quickly, stir them once at the 10-minute mark.
  6. Rest the chicken 5 minutes. Slice the chicken. Serve with the roasted baby potatoes and roasted green beans. If needed, add a pinch of salt at the table after tasting (do not add additional salt during cooking).
