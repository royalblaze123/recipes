Lean Beef Taco Salads

Nutrition (approx)

Frank: 545 kcal • P 48g • C 44g • F 20g
Susan/Elise (each): 409 kcal • P 36g • C 33g • F 15g

Ingredients (purchase-clean)

  • 1.25 lb 93% lean ground beef
  • 1 bag shredded lettuce
  • 1 each avocado
  • 1/2 jar salsa
  • 1 each cucumber
  • 1 pint cherry tomatoes
  • 1 each lime
  • 4 tsp extra-virgin olive oil
  • 2 tsp chili powder
  • 2 tsp cumin
  • 2 tsp garlic powder
  • 1 tsp smoked paprika
  • 1 tsp black pepper
  • 1 tsp kosher salt

Steps

  1. Beef: cook ground beef in a skillet with 2 tsp olive oil 7–9 minutes until browned. Stir in spices for 60 seconds. Cool 5 minutes.
  2. Dressing: whisk 1/2 cup salsa with 2 tsp lime juice and 2 tsp olive oil.
  3. Base: divide shredded lettuce into three bowls.
  4. Protein: add seasoned beef.
  5. Add-ins: add diced cucumber and halved tomatoes.
  6. Top: add salsa and measured diced avocado. Portion per Notes.
  7. Avocado: halve, pit, and dice. Use about 1/2 avocado total; refrigerate remaining avocado with the pit to slow browning.

Notes

Lean beef taco salads (portion-controlled) with salsa + avocado finish. Portions (per person): Frank: 1 large taco salad bowl + 1/3 of the avocado + salsa Susan: 1 medium taco salad bowl + 1/4 of the avocado + salsa Elise: 1 medium taco salad bowl + 1/4 of the avocado + salsa
