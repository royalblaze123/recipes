Red Enchilada Turkey-Bean Enchiladas + Salad

Nutrition (approx)

Frank: 630 kcal • P 58g • C 38g • F 22g
Susan/Elise (each): 472 kcal • P 44g • C 28g • F 16g

Ingredients (purchase-clean)

  • 1.50 lb 93% lean ground turkey
  • 1 package whole-grain small tortillas
  • 1 can black beans
  • 1 can red enchilada sauce
  • 1/2 bag reduced-fat shredded Mexican cheese blend
  • 1 bag spring mix
  • 1 each cucumber
  • 8 tsp light salad dressing
  • 1 each lime
  • 2 tsp chili powder
  • 2 tsp cumin
  • 2 tsp garlic powder
  • 1 tsp smoked paprika
  • 1 tsp black pepper
  • 1 tsp kosher salt

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 400°F. Lightly coat a 9x13-inch baking dish with nonstick spray.
  2. Turkey filling: cook ground turkey in a nonstick skillet over medium-high 7–9 minutes, breaking it up, until browned and cooked through.
  3. Beans: can size note—use a standard 15 oz can black beans. Drain and rinse. Measure out 1 1/2 cups for this recipe; refrigerate any remainder.
  4. Season filling: add 2 tsp chili powder, 2 tsp cumin, 2 tsp garlic powder, 1 tsp smoked paprika, 1 tsp black pepper, and 1 tsp kosher salt to the turkey. Stir 60 seconds to toast spices.
  5. Add beans and 1/3 cup water from the tap (do not add water to Ingredients). Simmer 2–3 minutes until saucy and thick.
  6. Warm enchilada sauce: heat the can of red enchilada sauce in a saucepan over medium-low 4–5 minutes until steaming.
  7. Tortillas: warm tortillas 15–20 seconds per side in a dry skillet.
  8. Assembly: spread 1/2 cup sauce in the baking dish. Fill each tortilla with turkey-bean mixture and a pinch of cheese; roll and place seam-side down.
  9. Top: pour remaining sauce over enchiladas and sprinkle remaining cheese over the center line. Bake 14–18 minutes until bubbly.
  10. Salad: slice cucumber. Toss spring mix with cucumber, 8 tsp dressing, and 2 tsp lime juice right before serving.
  11. Portion: Frank 4 enchiladas; Susan 3; Elise 3. Serve with salad.

Notes

Classic red enchiladas with turkey and black beans, plus a crisp salad. Portions (per person): Frank: 4 enchiladas (4 tortillas) + 1/3 of the side/veg Susan: 3 enchiladas (3 tortillas) + 1/4 of the side/veg Elise: 3 enchiladas (3 tortillas) + 1/4 of the side/veg
