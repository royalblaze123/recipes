Salsa Verde Chicken Stew (Thick)

Nutrition (approx)

Frank: 610 kcal • P 58g • C 45g • F 16g • Sat 4g • Na 680mg • Fiber 12g
Susan/Elise (each): 458 kcal • P 44g • C 34g • F 12g • Sat 3g • Na 510mg • Fiber 9g

Ingredients (purchase-clean)

  • 1.75 lb boneless skinless chicken breast
  • 1 can cannellini beans
  • 1 bag frozen mixed vegetables
  • 1 jar salsa verde
  • 1 carton low-sodium chicken broth
  • extra-virgin olive oil
  • kosher salt
  • black pepper
  • chili powder
  • ground cumin
  • garlic powder
  • onion powder
  • smoked paprika
  • Italian seasoning
  • crushed red pepper
  • bay leaves
  • oregano

Steps

  1. Pot: heat 2 tsp olive oil. Add 3 cups frozen mixed vegetables; cook 6–8 minutes until browned.
  2. Chicken: add diced chicken; cook 8–10 minutes until 165°F.
  3. Season with cumin, garlic powder, onion powder, oregano, kosher salt, black pepper.
  4. Beans: Can size note—use a standard 15 oz can cannellini beans.. Drain/rinse. Add beans.
  5. Sauce: stir in 1 cup salsa verde + 1 cup low-sodium broth.
  6. Simmer uncovered 12–15 minutes until thick and stew-like.
  7. Thicken: mash 1/2 cup beans in the pot and stir back in.
  8. Portion per Notes.

Notes

Green, cozy stew—thickened by beans, no dairy needed. Portions (per person): Frank: About 2 1/2 cups stew Susan: About 2 cups stew Elise: About 2 cups stew Tips (recipe-specific): Salsa verde is your ‘sauce’—don’t over-salt. Mash beans to thicken instead of adding flour.
