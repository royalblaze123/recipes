Cinnamon Orange Iced Tea Highball (Pitcher Size)

Yield: 8 drinks • Prep: 10 min • Total: 10 min

Ingredients (AnyList-ready)

  • 48 oz brewed black tea
  • 4 oranges
  • 6 oz simple syrup
  • 2 tsp ground cinnamon
  • 16 oz club soda
  • 8 cups ice

Steps

  1. Brew black tea and chill until cold.
  2. In a pitcher, combine 48 oz chilled black tea, juice from 4 oranges (about 8–10 oz), 6 oz simple syrup, and 2 tsp ground cinnamon. Stir 20 seconds.
  3. To serve: fill each glass with ice, pour in 7 oz base, then top with 2 oz club soda. Stir once, gently.

Notes

Stir the pitcher briefly before each pour to keep cinnamon evenly distributed.

Batch rule: keep carbonation separate until serving for the best fizz.
