Air Fryer Garlic-Herb Steak Bites with Crispy Potatoes and Asparagus

Prep: 15 min   Cook: 22 min   Total: 37 min

Ingredients

  • 1.5 lb sirloin steak
  • 1 bag baby potatoes
  • 1 bunch asparagus
  • 1 lemon
  • 1 bulb garlic
  • olive oil
  • butter
  • Dijon mustard
  • garlic powder
  • onion powder
  • black pepper
  • salt substitute
  • dried parsley

RBM rules: quantities appear before items; fresh proteins use weight. Condiments/spices/seasonings are listed name-only; all measurements are in Steps. Water is referenced in Steps only.

Step-by-step Instructions

  1. Prep potatoes: halve baby potatoes. Toss with 10 tsp olive oil + 2 tsp salt substitute + 1 tsp garlic powder + 1 tsp onion powder + 1 tsp black pepper.
  2. Air fry potatoes (Cosori): preheat 400°F for 3 minutes. Cook 16–20 minutes, shaking at reminders, until browned and tender. Keep warm.
  3. Prep beef: cut sirloin into 1-inch cubes. In a bowl, toss with 4 tsp olive oil + 2 tsp garlic powder + 1 tsp dried parsley + 1 tsp black pepper + 2 tsp salt substitute.
  4. Air fry steak bites: set air fryer to 400°F. Cook 7–9 minutes total (for medium), shaking at reminder once, until browned. Rest 3 minutes.
  5. Prep asparagus: trim woody ends. Toss with 4 tsp olive oil + 1 tsp salt substitute + 1 tsp black pepper + zest of 1 lemon.
  6. Air fry asparagus: 390°F for 5–7 minutes until crisp-tender (add after steak is out).
  7. Make quick pan sauce (optional but chefy): melt 2 tsp butter and whisk in 4 tsp Dijon + 4 tsp lemon juice + 1 tsp black pepper. Drizzle on plates.
  8. Portion: Frank gets 6 oz cooked beef + ~1 1/4 cups potatoes + ~1 1/2 cups asparagus. Susan and Elise each get ~4.5 oz beef + ~1 cup potatoes + ~1 1/4 cups asparagus.
