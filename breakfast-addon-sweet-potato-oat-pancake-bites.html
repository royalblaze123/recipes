Sweet Potato Oat Pancake Bites (Freezer Batch)

Mini pancake bites made with oats and sweet potato for slow carbs that pair cleanly with your shake.

Servings

24 bites.

Times

  • Prep time: 15 minutes
  • Cook time: 15 minutes
  • Total time: 30 minutes

Ingredients

Main Dish

  • 2 each sweet potatoes
  • 1 container old-fashioned oats
  • 1 carton egg whites
  • 1 container baking powder
  • 1 jar ground cinnamon
  • 1 jar black pepper
    • 

    Preparation Steps

    1. Pierce 2 sweet potatoes and microwave until very tender. Cool, then scoop the flesh into a bowl.
    2. Mix in 1 1/2 cups oats, 1 1/2 cups egg whites, 2 tsp baking powder, and 2 tsp cinnamon until a thick batter forms.
    3. Heat a nonstick skillet. Cook tablespoon-size pancakes 1–2 minutes per side until set.
    4. Cool completely. Freeze on a tray 1 hour, then store in a freezer bag for up to 1 month.
    5. Breakfast use: grab 6 bites and eat with your morning shake (they thaw quickly).

      If you want faster mornings, move a 2–3 day supply to the fridge.

      For a tighter carb load, use 5 bites instead of 6.
