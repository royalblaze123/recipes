Air Fryer Taco Spiced Pork with Black Beans and Sweet Potato Bowls

Taco-spiced pork with sweet potato and black beans for slow carbs and fiber—fast, filling, and calibrated to your dinner band.

Servings

3 servings.

Times

  • Prep time: 20 minutes
  • Cook time: 16 minutes
  • Total time: 36 minutes

Ingredients

Main Dish

  • 1 lb pork tenderloin
  • 2 each sweet potatoes
  • 1 can black beans
  • 1 each lime
  • 1 each yellow onion
  • 1 bottle extra virgin olive oil
  • 1 container nonfat Greek yogurt
  • 1 jar chili powder
  • 1 jar ground cumin
  • 1 jar garlic powder
  • 1 jar black pepper
    • 

    Preparation Steps

    1. Prep sweet potato: peel and cut into 3/4-inch cubes. Measure out about 1 1/4 cups cubes for this recipe; refrigerate remaining sweet potato. Toss with 1 tbsp olive oil, chili powder, cumin, garlic powder, and black pepper.
    2. Prep pork: cut into 1-inch cubes. Toss with 2 tsp olive oil, chili powder, cumin, garlic powder, and black pepper.
    3. Prep onion: slice 1/2 onion (store remaining). Toss with 1 tsp olive oil and black pepper.
    4. Prep beans: drain and rinse black beans. Measure out 1 cup beans for this recipe; refrigerate remaining beans.
    5. Cosori setup: preheat both baskets to 400°F for 3 minutes. Put sweet potato cubes in Basket A.
    6. Basket A: cook sweet potato cubes 400°F for 14–16 minutes with shake reminder ON; shake at each reminder.
    7. Basket B: cook onion 400°F for 6 minutes, shaking once. Add pork cubes and cook 400°F for 8–10 minutes, shaking once, until pork reaches 145°F. Rest 3 minutes.
    8. Warm beans: microwave the measured beans 60–90 seconds, or add to Basket B for the final 2 minutes.
    9. Crema: stir 1/4 cup Greek yogurt with 1 tsp lime juice and 1 tsp water.
    10. Serve (40/30/30): build bowls with sweet potato, onion + pork, and beans. Add a light crema drizzle.

      Notes

      Portions (per person):


      Frank – Pork + onion: largest share. Sweet potato: about 3/4 cup. Beans: about 1/2 cup. Crema drizzle.


      Susan – Pork + onion: medium share. Sweet potato: about 1/2–5/8 cup. Beans: about 3/8 cup. Crema drizzle.


      Elise – Pork + onion: medium share. Sweet potato: about 1/2–5/8 cup. Beans: about 3/8 cup. Crema drizzle.


      Tips (recipe-specific):


      Keep pork cubes in a single layer for browning—avoid crowding Basket B.


      Use shake reminders for sweet potato so edges crisp evenly.


      If you want fewer carbs, reduce sweet potato in bowls and add extra onion.
