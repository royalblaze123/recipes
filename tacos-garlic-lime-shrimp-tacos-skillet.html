Garlic-Lime Shrimp Tacos (Skillet)

Nutrition (approx)

Frank: 525 kcal • P 42g • C 38g • F 18g
Susan/Elise (each): 394 kcal • P 32g • C 28g • F 14g

Ingredients (purchase-clean)

  • 1.25 lb raw shrimp
  • 4/5 package whole-grain small tortillas
  • 1 bag spring mix
  • 1 each cucumber
  • 1 each lime
  • 1 tub 2% plain Greek yogurt
  • 4 tsp extra-virgin olive oil
  • 2 tsp paprika
  • 2 tsp garlic powder
  • 1 tsp black pepper
  • 1 tsp kosher salt

Steps

  1. Prep shrimp: pat shrimp dry. Season with 2 tsp paprika, 2 tsp garlic powder, 1 tsp black pepper, and 1 tsp kosher salt.
  2. Cook shrimp: heat a nonstick skillet over medium-high. Add 4 tsp olive oil. Sauté shrimp 2–3 minutes total, flipping once, just until opaque.
  3. Slaw base: thinly slice cucumber. Toss cucumber with spring mix, 2 tsp lime juice, and 1 tsp lime zest to make a crisp slaw-style topping.
  4. Tortillas: warm tortillas 20–30 seconds per side.
  5. Yogurt-lime drizzle: stir 6 tbsp Greek yogurt with 2 tsp lime juice and a pinch of black pepper.
  6. Assemble tacos with shrimp + slaw topping. Portion into 7 tacos per Notes.

Notes

Fast shrimp tacos with a crunchy slaw-style topping and yogurt-lime drizzle. Portions (per person): Frank: 3 tacos (use 3 tortillas) + 1/3 of the filling + 1/3 of the toppings Susan: 2 tacos (use 2 tortillas) + 1/4 of the filling + 1/4 of the toppings Elise: 2 tacos (use 2 tortillas) + 1/4 of the filling + 1/4 of the toppings
