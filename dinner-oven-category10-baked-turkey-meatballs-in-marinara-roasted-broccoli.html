Baked Turkey Meatballs in Marinara + Roasted Broccoli

Nutrition (approx)

Frank: 630 kcal • P 58g • C 40g • F 18g
Susan/Elise (each): 472 kcal • P 44g • C 30g • F 14g

Ingredients (purchase-clean)

  • 1.75 lb 93% lean ground turkey
  • 1 jar marinara sauce
  • 1 bag frozen broccoli florets
  • 6 oz grated Parmesan cheese
  • kosher salt
  • black pepper
  • garlic powder
  • onion powder
  • smoked paprika
  • Italian seasoning
  • ground cumin
  • chili powder
  • red pepper flakes
  • extra-virgin olive oil
  • low-sodium chicken broth
  • low-sodium beef broth
  • low-sodium soy sauce
  • hot sauce
  • Dijon mustard
  • balsamic vinegar
  • apple cider vinegar
  • cornstarch

Steps (oven)

  1. Oven: heat to 425°F.
  2. Meatballs: mix turkey with Italian seasoning, garlic powder, onion powder, black pepper, and a modest pinch of kosher salt. Form 18 meatballs.
  3. Bake meatballs: place on lined pan; bake 14–16 minutes to 165°F.
  4. Sauce: pour marinara (2 cups measured) into baking dish; add meatballs; sprinkle Parmesan. Bake 8 minutes to meld.
  5. Broccoli: microwave 4–5 minutes; drain/pat dry; toss with olive oil, garlic powder, black pepper, modest salt. Roast 425°F 12–15 minutes.
  6. Serve per Notes.

Notes

Saucy meatballs plus crispy-edged broccoli for a full plate without extra starch. Portions (per person): Frank: 6 meatballs + 1 1/2 cups broccoli + 1/2 cup sauce Susan: 5 meatballs + 1 cup broccoli + 1/3 cup sauce Elise: 5 meatballs + 1 cup broccoli + 1/3 cup sauce Tips (recipe-specific): Roast broccoli hot after draining so it doesn’t steam. Bake meatballs first so they hold shape in sauce.
