Teriyaki-Style Chicken Meatballs with Snap Peas over Cauliflower Rice

Prep: 22 min   Cook: 20 min   Total: 42 min

Ingredients

  • 1.6 lb ground chicken
  • 1 egg
  • 0.25 bag whole wheat panko crumbs
  • 1 lb snap peas
  • 1 bag frozen cauliflower rice
  • olive oil
  • low-sodium teriyaki sauce
  • rice vinegar
  • sesame oil
  • garlic
  • ginger
  • black pepper
  • salt substitute
  • cornstarch

Hard rules enforced: Dinner validations (calories 600–650 Frank, sat fat ≤8g, sodium ≤700mg, fiber ≥10g, added sugar ≤6g). Quantities appear before items; fresh meat/poultry/fish use weights. Condiments/spices/seasonings have no measurements in Ingredients; all measurements are in Steps. No olives, no squash, no Brussels sprouts, no zucchini, no tofu/tempeh, no yogurt in dinners.

Step-by-step Instructions

  1. Meatballs: mix ground chicken + 1 egg + 1/3 cup panko + 1/2 tsp black pepper + 1/2 tsp salt substitute.
  2. Form 18 meatballs. Air fry at 400°F for 10–12 minutes until 165°F.
  3. Cauliflower rice: microwave 1 bag cauliflower rice 5–6 minutes. Measure 3 cups cooked; drain/pat dry; refreeze remainder.
  4. Sauce: whisk 3 tbsp low-sodium teriyaki sauce + 1 tbsp rice vinegar + 1 tsp sesame oil + 1 tsp cornstarch + 2 tbsp water + 1 tsp minced garlic + 1 tsp grated ginger.
  5. Snap peas: sauté 1 lb snap peas with 1 tsp olive oil 3 minutes.
  6. Add meatballs to pan; pour sauce; toss 2 minutes until thickened and glazed.
  7. Serve over cauliflower rice. Portion: Frank 7 meatballs; Susan 5; Elise 5.
