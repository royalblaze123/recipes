Pork Tenderloin Air-Fryer Taquitos

Nutrition (approx)

Frank: 645 kcal • P 58g • C 50g • F 20g
Susan/Elise (each): 484 kcal • P 44g • C 38g • F 15g

Ingredients (purchase-clean)

  • 1.50 lb pork tenderloin
  • 1 package corn tortillas
  • 1 each avocado
  • 1/2 jar salsa
  • 1 bag spring mix
  • 1 each lime
  • 6 tsp extra-virgin olive oil
  • 1 bag reduced-fat shredded Mexican cheese blend
  • 2 tsp chili powder
  • 2 tsp cumin
  • 2 tsp garlic powder
  • 1 tsp smoked paprika
  • 1 tsp black pepper
  • 1 tsp kosher salt

Steps

  1. Tortillas: warm corn tortillas by microwaving under a damp paper towel for 25–35 seconds so they roll without cracking.
  2. Pork: slice into 1/2-inch medallions. Cook in a skillet with 2 tsp olive oil 3–4 minutes per side until 145°F. Rest 3 minutes, then chop small.
  3. Season chopped pork with spices and a squeeze of lime (2 tsp).
  4. Filling: mix pork with 3/4 cup shredded cheese. Cool 5 minutes.
  5. Assemble taquitos: 2 tbsp filling per tortilla, roll tight, seam-side down.
  6. Preheat air fryer to 400°F.
  7. Brush or spray taquitos lightly with oil.
  8. Air fry in a single layer 8–10 minutes, turning at 5 minutes, until crisp and browned.
  9. Side salad: add spring mix to a bowl. Drizzle with 2 tsp lime juice and 2 tsp olive oil; toss.
  10. Avocado: halve, pit, and dice. Use about 1/2 avocado total; refrigerate remaining avocado with the pit to slow browning.
  11. Serve with salsa and portion per Notes.

Notes

Juicy pork taquitos with measured cheese; crisp in the air fryer and serve with avocado + salsa. Portions (per person): Frank: 6 taquitos + 1/3 of the side + 1/3 of the avocado + salsa Susan: 4 taquitos + 1/4 of the side + 1/4 of the avocado + salsa Elise: 4 taquitos + 1/4 of the side + 1/4 of the avocado + salsa
