Moroccan-Style Chicken + Sweet Potato Tagine Bowls (Thick)

Nutrition (approx)

Frank: 650 kcal • P 58g • C 70g • F 14g • Sat 3g • Na 700mg • Fiber 12g
Susan/Elise (each): 488 kcal • P 44g • C 52g • F 10g • Sat 2g • Na 525mg • Fiber 9g

Ingredients (purchase-clean)

  • 1.75 lb boneless skinless chicken breast
  • 2.00 lb sweet potatoes
  • 1 can no-salt-added diced tomatoes
  • 1 can chickpeas
  • low-sodium chicken broth
  • kosher salt
  • black pepper
  • garlic powder
  • onion powder
  • smoked paprika
  • Italian seasoning
  • ground cumin
  • chili powder
  • red pepper flakes
  • extra-virgin olive oil
  • low-sodium chicken broth

Steps

  1. Chicken: sauté diced chicken with 2 tsp olive oil, black pepper, garlic powder, cumin, and smoked paprika 6–8 minutes.
  2. Base: add 1 can diced tomatoes (14.5 oz) + 2 cups low-sodium broth. Simmer 8 minutes.
  3. Sweet potatoes: add cubed sweet potatoes; simmer 12–14 minutes until just tender.
  4. Chickpeas: add 1 1/2 cups drained/rinsed chickpeas (from a 15 oz can). Simmer 3 minutes.
  5. Thicken: simmer uncovered 3–5 minutes so it turns stew-thick, not soupy.
  6. Serve per Notes.

Notes

Warm Moroccan-ish comfort bowls with sweet potato + chickpeas (thick, not mushy). Portions (per person): Frank: About 2 1/2 cups (heaping bowl) Susan: About 2 cups (bowl) Elise: About 2 cups (bowl) Tips (recipe-specific): Simmer uncovered at the end so it thickens. Keep sweet potatoes in larger cubes so they hold structure.
