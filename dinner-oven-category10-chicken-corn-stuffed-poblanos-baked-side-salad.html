Chicken + Corn Stuffed Poblanos (Baked) + Side Salad

Nutrition (approx)

Frank: 650 kcal • P 60g • C 45g • F 24g
Susan/Elise (each): 488 kcal • P 45g • C 34g • F 18g

Ingredients (purchase-clean)

  • 6 each poblano peppers
  • 1.75 lb boneless skinless chicken breast
  • 1 bag frozen corn
  • 1 jar salsa verde
  • 6 oz reduced-fat cheddar cheese
  • 1 bag spring mix
  • apple cider vinegar
  • extra-virgin olive oil
  • kosher salt
  • black pepper
  • garlic powder
  • onion powder
  • smoked paprika
  • Italian seasoning
  • ground cumin
  • chili powder
  • red pepper flakes
  • extra-virgin olive oil
  • low-sodium chicken broth
  • low-sodium beef broth
  • low-sodium soy sauce
  • hot sauce
  • Dijon mustard
  • balsamic vinegar
  • apple cider vinegar
  • cornstarch

Steps (oven)

  1. Oven: heat to 400°F.
  2. Roast poblanos: slit lengthwise; remove seeds. Roast 400°F 10 minutes to soften slightly.
  3. Chicken: bake chicken 400°F 18–22 minutes to 165°F; shred.
  4. Filling: mix shredded chicken + corn (2 cups) + salsa verde (1 cup measured). Simmer 2 minutes to thicken if needed.
  5. Stuff: fill poblanos; top with measured cheddar; bake 400°F 12–15 minutes until melted.
  6. Salad: spring mix with vinegar (2 tsp) + olive oil (2 tsp) + black pepper.
  7. Serve per Notes.

Notes

Stuffed poblanos with thick filling and measured cheese; salad keeps it light and fresh. Portions (per person): Frank: 2 stuffed poblanos + 2 cups salad Susan: 2 stuffed poblanos + 1 1/2 cups salad Elise: 2 stuffed poblanos + 1 1/2 cups salad Tips (recipe-specific): Pre-roast peppers so they soften without collapsing. Keep filling thick so peppers don’t weep.
