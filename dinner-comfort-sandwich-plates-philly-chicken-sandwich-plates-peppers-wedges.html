Philly Chicken Sandwich Plates + Peppers & Wedges

Nutrition (approx)

Frank: 650 kcal • P 55g • C 65g • F 16g • Sat 6g • Na 700mg • Fiber 10g
Susan/Elise (each): 488 kcal • P 41g • C 49g • F 12g • Sat 4g • Na 525mg • Fiber 8g

Ingredients (purchase-clean)

  • 12 slices whole-wheat sandwich bread
  • 1.75 lb chicken breast
  • 2 each bell peppers
  • 1 each onion
  • 6 slices reduced-fat cheese slices
  • 1.50 lb baby potatoes
  • kosher salt
  • black pepper
  • garlic powder
  • onion powder
  • Italian seasoning
  • smoked paprika
  • extra-virgin olive oil
  • Dijon mustard
  • hot sauce
  • rice vinegar

Steps

  1. Oven: heat to 425°F. Cut baby potatoes into wedges; roast with 2 tsp olive oil, smoked paprika, modest kosher salt, black pepper for 22–26 minutes, flip once.
  2. Peppers/onions: sauté sliced peppers + onion with 2 tsp olive oil 10–12 minutes until browned.
  3. Chicken: slice thin. Sauté with garlic powder, black pepper, and a small pinch of kosher salt 6–8 minutes to 165°F.
  4. Build sandwiches: 3 sandwiches (1 per person) with peppers/onions + chicken + cheese (total 6 slices; 2 per sandwich). Toast in skillet 2–3 minutes per side to melt.
  5. Serve per Notes.

Notes

Cheesesteak vibes with chicken + peppers and oven wedges. Portions (per person): Frank: 1 sandwich + 3/4 cup wedges side Susan: 1 sandwich + 1/2 cup wedges side Elise: 1 sandwich + 1/2 cup wedges side Tips (recipe-specific): Brown peppers/onions until sweet—this replaces heavy sauces. Slice chicken thin so it cooks fast and stays juicy.
