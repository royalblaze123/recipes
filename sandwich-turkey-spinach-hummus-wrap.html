Turkey Spinach Hummus Wrap & Grape Tomatoes

Whole-wheat tortilla wrap with lean turkey, hummus, and spinach, served with sweet grape tomatoes on the side.

Servings

3 servings (Frank, Susan, Elise).

Times

  • Prep time: 10 minutes
  • Cook time: 0 minutes
  • Total time: 10 minutes

Ingredients

Sandwich

  • 3 medium whole-wheat tortillas (8–9 inch)
  • 9 oz low-sodium sliced turkey breast (deli-style, nitrite-free)
  • 6 tbsp plain hummus
  • 2 cups fresh baby spinach, loosely packed
  • 1/2 cup thinly sliced red bell pepper
  • 1/4 cup thinly sliced red onion

Side

  • 1 1/2 cups grape tomatoes, rinsed and patted dry

Preparation Steps

  1. Place the whole-wheat tortillas on a clean cutting board or work surface.
  2. Spread 2 tablespoons of hummus evenly over each tortilla, covering most of the surface but leaving a small border at the edges.
  3. Lay sliced turkey breast down the center of each tortilla, giving Frank a slightly thicker layer than Susan and Elise as described in the portions section.
  4. Top the turkey on each tortilla with baby spinach, sliced red bell pepper, and sliced red onion.
  5. Starting from one side of each tortilla, tightly roll it into a wrap, keeping the filling tucked inside as you roll.
  6. Slice each wrap in half on a slight diagonal to make it easier to eat and to see the filling.
  7. Rinse the grape tomatoes under cool water, pat them dry, and divide them onto three plates according to the portions listed.
  8. Serve each person one turkey hummus wrap and their portion of grape tomatoes.

Notes

Portions (per person):

Frank – 1 full turkey hummus wrap made with about 3.5 oz turkey, 1 tortilla, and a generous layer of hummus and vegetables, plus about 2/3 cup grape tomatoes.

Susan – 1 full turkey hummus wrap made with about 3 oz turkey and a moderate layer of hummus and vegetables, plus about 1/2 cup grape tomatoes.

Elise – 1 full turkey hummus wrap made with about 2.5 oz turkey and a moderate layer of hummus and vegetables, plus about 1/2 cup grape tomatoes.

Nutrition (per person)

  • Frank: 420 kcal; 39 g protein; 36 g carbs; 12 g fat; 8 g fiber; ~560 mg sodium.
  • Susan: 370 kcal; 33 g protein; 32 g carbs; 10 g fat; 7 g fiber; ~520 mg sodium.
  • Elise: 370 kcal; 33 g protein; 32 g carbs; 10 g fat; 7 g fiber; ~520 mg sodium.
