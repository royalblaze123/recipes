Mediterranean Burger Plates + Lemon Broccoli

Nutrition (approx)

Frank: 635 kcal • P 60g • C 50g • F 18g • Sat 5g • Na 670mg • Fiber 10g
Susan/Elise (each): 476 kcal • P 45g • C 38g • F 14g • Sat 4g • Na 502mg • Fiber 8g

Ingredients (purchase-clean)

  • 1.75 lb 93% lean ground turkey
  • 4 each whole-wheat burger buns
  • 1 bag frozen broccoli florets
  • 1 each lemon
  • 1 each cucumber
  • 1 pint cherry tomatoes
  • kosher salt
  • black pepper
  • garlic powder
  • onion powder
  • smoked paprika
  • dried oregano
  • extra-virgin olive oil
  • mustard
  • ketchup
  • pickle relish
  • hot sauce
  • Worcestershire sauce
  • dried oregano

Steps

  1. Oven: heat to 425°F. Roast frozen broccoli florets with 2 tsp olive oil, 1/2 tsp kosher salt, black pepper for 16–20 minutes.
  2. Patties: season turkey with 2 tsp dried oregano, 1 tsp garlic powder, 1 tsp onion powder, 1/2 tsp kosher salt, 1/2 tsp black pepper. Form 6 patties.
  3. Skillet: cook patties 5–6 minutes per side to 165°F.
  4. Finish broccoli with 2 tsp lemon juice.
  5. Fresh side: slice cucumber and halve tomatoes; season with black pepper and a squeeze of lemon.
  6. Assemble: buns or open-face with fresh veg. Portion per Notes.

Notes

Burger comfort with bright Mediterranean flavors (no olives). Portions (per person): Frank: 2 patties + 1 cup broccoli + fresh veg side + toppings Susan: 1 patty + 3/4 cup broccoli + fresh veg side + toppings Elise: 1 patty + 3/4 cup broccoli + fresh veg side + toppings Tips (recipe-specific): Lemon finish makes it taste ‘fresh’ even if you meal-prep it. If you want more punch, use hot sauce instead of extra salt.
