Chicken Hummus Crunch Wrap with Microgreens and Carrot Sticks

Prep: 18 min   Cook: 10 min   Total: 28 min

Ingredients

  • 3 whole wheat tortillas
  • 14 oz chicken breast
  • 0.25 tub hummus
  • 1 cucumber
  • 2 carrots
  • 1 bag shredded cabbage
  • 1 bag microgreens
  • 1 lemon
  • olive oil
  • Dijon mustard
  • black pepper
  • salt substitute
  • garlic powder

Hard rules enforced: Lunch validations (calories, sat fat, sodium, fiber, added sugar). Quantities appear before items; fresh meat/poultry/fish use weights. Condiments/spices/seasonings have no measurements in Ingredients; all measurements are in Steps. No olives, no squash, no Brussels sprouts, no zucchini. Yogurt is not used in dinners.

Step-by-step Instructions

  1. Chicken: season chicken with garlic powder, black pepper, and salt substitute. Cook in a skillet with 2 tsp olive oil 6–7 minutes per side to 165°F. Rest 5 minutes, then slice thin.
  2. Veg prep: slice cucumber into thin sticks. Shred or thin-slice extra cabbage if needed. Rinse and dry microgreens.
  3. Mix quick lemon-Dijon drizzle: in a small bowl combine 2 tsp lemon juice + Dijon mustard + black pepper (no yogurt).
  4. Assemble wraps: spread hummus thinly on each tortilla. Layer chicken, cabbage, cucumber, and microgreens. Drizzle lightly with lemon-Dijon mix. Roll tightly.
  5. Side: slice carrots into sticks. Portion as the side.
  6. Portion: each person gets 1 wrap. Frank gets the largest chicken share; Susan/Elise get ~75% of Frank’s filling volume.
