Citrus Calm Lite-Salt Electrolyte (3 Bottles)

NOTES: Designed for the 8:30–9:00 AM and 4:00 PM hydration windows. Stays under ~300 mg sodium per bottle while providing potassium and magnesium. Kidney-stone–friendly (includes citrate from lemon) and PPPD-safe (no intense flavors).

PORTION SIZES (each recipe makes 3 bottles): Frank: 1 full 20 oz bottle; Susan: 1 full 20 oz bottle; Elise: 1 full 20 oz bottle.

Ingredients

  • 60 fl oz cold filtered water (3 x 20 fl oz bottles)
  • 3/8 tsp lite salt (Morton Lite Salt or similar, divided across bottles)
  • 3 scoops magnesium glycinate powder (about 150–200 mg elemental Mg per scoop, divided)
  • 3 tbsp fresh lemon juice (1 tbsp per bottle)
  • 9–12 drops liquid stevia or monk fruit sweetener, to taste (3–4 drops per bottle)
  • Ice cubes, optional, for serving

Step-by-Step Instructions

  1. Line up three clean 20 fl oz bottles. Add 1/8 teaspoon lite salt to each bottle.
  2. Divide the magnesium glycinate powder evenly across the three bottles.
  3. Add 1 tablespoon of fresh lemon juice to each bottle.
  4. Add 3–4 drops of liquid stevia or monk fruit to each bottle.
  5. Fill each bottle with cold filtered water up to about 20 fl oz total volume.
  6. Cap each bottle tightly, then shake for 20–30 seconds until all powders fully dissolve.
  7. Refrigerate for at least 30 minutes before drinking, or add ice cubes just before serving.
  8. Use these bottles for your planned electrolyte windows (morning and afternoon). Shake gently before each sip.

Nutrition per Person (1 bottle)

  • Frank: ~5 kcal, 0 g protein, 0 g fat, 1 g carbs, ~250–300 mg sodium, ~300–350 mg potassium.
  • Susan: ~5 kcal, 0 g protein, 0 g fat, 1 g carbs, ~250–300 mg sodium, ~300–350 mg potassium.
  • Elise: ~5 kcal, 0 g protein, 0 g fat, 1 g carbs, ~250–300 mg sodium, ~300–350 mg potassium.
