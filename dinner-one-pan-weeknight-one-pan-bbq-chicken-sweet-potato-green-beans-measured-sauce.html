One-Pan BBQ Chicken + Sweet Potato + Green Beans (Measured Sauce)

Nutrition (approx)

Frank: 645 kcal • P 58g • C 55g • F 16g • Sat 4g • Na 700mg • Fiber 10g
Susan/Elise (each): 484 kcal • P 44g • C 41g • F 12g • Sat 3g • Na 525mg • Fiber 8g

Ingredients (purchase-clean)

  • 1.75 lb boneless skinless chicken breast
  • 2.00 lb sweet potatoes
  • 1 bag frozen green beans
  • 1 bottle low-sugar BBQ sauce
  • kosher salt
  • black pepper
  • garlic powder
  • onion powder
  • smoked paprika
  • Italian seasoning
  • ground cumin
  • chili powder
  • red pepper flakes
  • extra-virgin olive oil
  • low-sodium chicken broth

Steps

  1. Oven: heat to 425°F.
  2. Sweet potatoes: cube; toss with 2 tsp olive oil, smoked paprika, black pepper, modest kosher salt. Roast 18 minutes.
  3. Chicken: toss chicken with 2 tsp olive oil, garlic powder, black pepper, modest kosher salt. Add to pan; roast 10 minutes.
  4. Green beans: add green beans tossed with 1 tsp olive oil and black pepper; roast 10–12 minutes until chicken is 165°F.
  5. BBQ glaze: brush chicken with 2 tbsp low-sugar BBQ sauce total (measured) during the last 3 minutes of roasting.
  6. Rest 5 minutes. Serve per Notes.

Notes

BBQ flavor without sugar overload—just a measured glaze at the end. Portions (per person): Frank: About 7 oz chicken + 1 cup sweet potato + 1 cup green beans Susan: About 5 oz chicken + 3/4 cup sweet potato + 3/4 cup green beans Elise: About 5 oz chicken + 3/4 cup sweet potato + 3/4 cup green beans Tips (recipe-specific): Glaze at the end so it stays sticky and you use less. Roast sweet potatoes first so they caramelize.
