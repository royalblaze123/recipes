Strawberry Basil Spritz (Pitcher Size)

Yield: 8 drinks • Prep: 10 min • Total: 10 min

Ingredients (AnyList-ready)

  • 32 strawberries
  • 32 basil leaves
  • 8 oz lemon juice
  • 8 oz simple syrup
  • 40 oz club soda
  • 8 cups ice

Steps

  1. In a pitcher, add strawberries, basil, and simple syrup; muddle 60–80 presses.
  2. Stir in lemon juice and chill the base.
  3. To serve: fill each glass with ice, pour in 4 oz base, then top with 5 oz club soda. Stir once, gently.

Notes

Strain the base if you want less pulp.

Batch rule: keep carbonation separate until serving for the best fizz.
