Lean Beef & Corn Burrito Bowls

Nutrition (approx)

Frank: 545 kcal • P 50g • C 54g • F 17g
Susan/Elise (each): 409 kcal • P 38g • C 40g • F 13g

Ingredients (purchase-clean)

  • 1.25 lb 93% lean ground beef
  • 1 can corn
  • 1 can black beans
  • 1 cup dry brown rice
  • 1 bag shredded lettuce
  • 1 each avocado
  • 1/2 jar salsa
  • 1 each lime
  • 4 tsp extra-virgin olive oil
  • 2 tsp chili powder
  • 2 tsp cumin
  • 2 tsp garlic powder
  • 1 tsp smoked paprika
  • 1 tsp black pepper
  • 1 tsp kosher salt

Steps

  1. Rice: rinse 1 cup dry brown rice. Cook with 2 cups water (or low-sodium broth): bring to a boil, cover, reduce to low, and simmer 35–40 minutes. Rest 5 minutes, then fluff.
  2. Beef: cook ground beef in a skillet with 2 tsp olive oil 7–9 minutes until browned. Stir in spices for 60 seconds.
  3. Corn: Can size note—use a standard 15 oz can corn.. Drain. Use 1 1/2 cups corn; refrigerate remainder.
  4. Beans: Can size note—use a standard 15 oz can black beans.. Drain/rinse. Use 1 1/2 cups beans; refrigerate remainder.
  5. Combine: stir corn and beans into beef and warm 2 minutes. Finish with 2 tsp lime juice.
  6. Assembly: divide lettuce (or cauliflower rice if listed) into three bowls.
  7. Top each bowl with the protein mix and measured rice (or other carb component if used).
  8. Add salsa and measured diced avocado on top.
  9. Portion per Notes.
  10. Avocado: halve, pit, and dice. Use about 1/2 avocado total; refrigerate remaining avocado with the pit to slow browning.

Notes

Lean beef burrito bowls with controlled corn + beans; salsa and avocado finish. Portions (per person): Frank: 1 large burrito bowl (about 1/3 of the batch) + 1/3 avocado + salsa Susan: 1 medium burrito bowl (about 1/4 of the batch) + 1/4 avocado + salsa Elise: 1 medium burrito bowl (about 1/4 of the batch) + 1/4 avocado + salsa
