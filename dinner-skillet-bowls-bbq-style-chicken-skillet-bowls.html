BBQ-Style Chicken Skillet Bowls

Nutrition (approx)

Frank: 635 kcal • P 58g • C 58g • F 17g • Sat 5g • Na 690mg • Fiber 12g
Susan/Elise (each): 476 kcal • P 44g • C 44g • F 13g • Sat 4g • Na 518mg • Fiber 9g

Ingredients (purchase-clean)

  • 1.50 lb boneless skinless chicken breast
  • 1 bag frozen mixed vegetables
  • 1 can corn
  • 1 cup dry brown rice
  • 1 each avocado
  • 1/2 jar salsa
  • 1 bottle low-sugar BBQ sauce
  • kosher salt
  • black pepper
  • chili powder
  • ground cumin
  • garlic powder
  • smoked paprika
  • extra-virgin olive oil
  • apple cider vinegar

Steps

  1. Rice: cook 1 cup dry brown rice with 2 cups water (35–40 minutes), rest 5 minutes, fluff.
  2. Chicken: heat 2 tsp olive oil. Cook 1.50 lb diced chicken 8–10 minutes to 165°F.
  3. Veg: add 3 cups frozen mixed vegetables; cook 6–8 minutes until hot and lightly browned.
  4. Corn: use one 15 oz can corn. Drain. Stir 1 1/2 cups corn into skillet; cook 2 minutes. (Refrigerate remaining corn.)
  5. Sauce: stir in 1/4 cup low-sugar BBQ sauce + 2 tsp apple cider vinegar; simmer 2 minutes so it glazes the chicken.
  6. Bowls: divide rice + skillet mix. Top with salsa and avocado. Portion per Notes.

Notes

BBQ comfort bowls without the sugar-bomb sauce. Portions (per person): Frank: 1/3 rice + 1/3 skillet mix + 1/3 avocado + salsa Susan: 1/4 rice + 1/4 skillet mix + 1/4 avocado + salsa Elise: 1/4 rice + 1/4 skillet mix + 1/4 avocado + salsa Tips (recipe-specific): Use a measured 1/4 cup BBQ sauce for the whole pan; salsa adds the rest of the flavor. Add vinegar to brighten the BBQ so you don’t need more sauce.
