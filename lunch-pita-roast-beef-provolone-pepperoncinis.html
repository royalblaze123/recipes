High-Protein Pitas: Roast Beef Provolone with Pepperoncini and Microgreens

Prep: 16 min   Cook: 4 min   Total: 20 min

Ingredients

  • 3 whole wheat pitas
  • 14 slices roast beef
  • 6 slices provolone
  • 0.25 jar pepperoncini
  • 1 cucumber
  • 1 pint cherry tomatoes
  • 1 clamshell microgreens
  • Dijon mustard
  • black pepper
  • salt substitute

Rules enforced: quantities before items; fresh meat/poultry/fish use weights; packaged items use fractional containers when partial. Condiments/spices/seasonings have no measurements in Ingredients; all measurements are in Steps. No olives, no squash, no Brussels sprouts, no zucchini, no tofu/tempeh. Lunch validations included in Notes.

Step-by-step Instructions

  1. Warm pitas: toast 3 pitas 2 minutes.
  2. Veg: slice 1 cucumber; halve 1 pint cherry tomatoes.
  3. Spread: smear Dijon inside each pita (measured in Steps: Frank 2 tsp; Susan 1.5 tsp; Elise 1.5 tsp).
  4. Cheese: place provolone slices in pitas (Frank 2 slices; Susan 2 slices; Elise 2 slices total = 6).
  5. Meat: add roast beef (Frank 6 slices; Susan 4; Elise 4).
  6. Pepperoncini: chop and add (Frank 1 tbsp; Susan 2 tsp; Elise 2 tsp).
  7. Finish: add cucumber, tomatoes, and microgreens; season with pepper/salt substitute.
