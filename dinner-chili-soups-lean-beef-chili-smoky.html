Lean Beef Chili (Smoky)

Nutrition (approx)

Frank: 640 kcal • P 58g • C 48g • F 20g • Sat 6g • Na 690mg • Fiber 13g
Susan/Elise (each): 480 kcal • P 44g • C 36g • F 15g • Sat 4g • Na 518mg • Fiber 10g

Ingredients (purchase-clean)

  • 1.75 lb 93% lean ground beef
  • 1 can pinto beans
  • 1 can black beans
  • 1 can no-salt-added diced tomatoes
  • 1 carton low-sodium beef broth
  • 1 each onion
  • extra-virgin olive oil
  • kosher salt
  • black pepper
  • chili powder
  • ground cumin
  • garlic powder
  • onion powder
  • smoked paprika
  • Italian seasoning
  • crushed red pepper
  • bay leaves
  • cocoa powder

Steps

  1. Pot: heat 2 tsp olive oil. Cook 1 diced onion 6–8 minutes until browned.
  2. Add 1.75 lb lean ground beef; cook 7–9 minutes until browned. Drain if needed.
  3. Season with chili powder, cumin, garlic powder, onion powder, smoked paprika, kosher salt, and black pepper.
  4. Tomatoes: Can size note—use a standard 14.5 oz can no-salt-added diced tomatoes.. Add 1 can.
  5. Beans: Can size note—use a standard 15 oz can pinto beans. and Can size note—use a standard 15 oz can black beans.. Drain/rinse and add.
  6. Add 1 cup low-sodium beef broth. Simmer uncovered 18–22 minutes until thick.
  7. Optional depth: add 1/2 tsp cocoa powder and simmer 2 minutes (gives a ‘restaurant’ chili note).
  8. Portion per Notes.

Notes

Smoky beef chili—comforty and thick without loading fat. Portions (per person): Frank: About 2 1/2 cups chili Susan: About 2 cups chili Elise: About 2 cups chili Tips (recipe-specific): Cocoa is optional but adds depth without sugar. Keep it thick: uncovered simmer is the key.
