Chopped Salad: Tuna-Chickpea Cucumber-Tomato Salad

Prep: 16 min   Cook: 0 min   Total: 16 min

Ingredients

  • 2 cans tuna in water
  • 0.5 can no-salt-added chickpeas
  • 1 cucumber
  • 1 pint cherry tomatoes
  • 0.5 bag shredded cabbage
  • 1 lemon
  • olive oil
  • Dijon mustard
  • black pepper
  • salt substitute
  • vinegar
  • garlic powder

Rules enforced: Clean AnyList Ingredients (quantities before items; fresh meat/poultry/fish use weights; packaged items use fractional containers when partial). Condiments/spices/seasonings have no measurements in Ingredients; all measurements are in Steps. No olives, no squash, no Brussels sprouts, no zucchini, no tofu/tempeh. Lunch validations: Frank calories 500–550, sat fat ≤6g, sodium ≤650mg, fiber ≥8g, added sugar ≤6g.

Step-by-step Instructions

  1. Tuna: drain 2 cans tuna very well.
  2. Chickpeas: drain and rinse 1/2 can chickpeas; measure 3/4 cup for salad; refrigerate remainder.
  3. Chop veg: dice 1 cucumber and halve 1 pint cherry tomatoes. Combine with 1/2 bag shredded cabbage.
  4. Dressing: whisk 2 tsp olive oil + 2 tsp vinegar + 2 tsp lemon juice + 2 tsp Dijon + 1/4 tsp black pepper + 1/4 tsp salt substitute + 1/2 tsp garlic powder.
  5. Toss salad with tuna and chickpeas.
  6. Portion: Frank largest bowl; Susan/Elise ~75%.
  7. Meal-prep: keep dressing separate until serving.
