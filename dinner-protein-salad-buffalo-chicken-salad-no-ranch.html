Buffalo Chicken Salad (No Ranch)

Nutrition (approx)

Frank: 640 kcal • P 60g • C 45g • F 18g • Sat 3g • Na 650mg • Fiber 12g
Susan/Elise (each): 480 kcal • P 45g • C 34g • F 14g • Sat 2g • Na 488mg • Fiber 9g

Ingredients (purchase-clean)

  • 1.75 lb boneless skinless chicken breast
  • 1 head romaine lettuce
  • 1 each celery bunch
  • hot sauce
  • 1 can white beans
  • kosher salt
  • black pepper
  • garlic powder
  • onion powder
  • smoked paprika
  • Italian seasoning
  • ground cumin
  • chili powder
  • red pepper flakes
  • extra-virgin olive oil
  • balsamic vinegar
  • apple cider vinegar
  • Dijon mustard

Steps

  1. Chicken: roast chicken at 425°F 12–15 minutes to 165°F; rest; slice.
  2. Buffalo toss: toss chicken with 3 tbsp hot sauce (measured) and black pepper.
  3. Beans: drain/rinse white beans (15 oz can). Warm 1 1/2 cups beans with a splash of vinegar and black pepper 2 minutes.
  4. Dressing: whisk 2 tbsp apple cider vinegar, 2 tbsp olive oil, black pepper, and a small pinch of kosher salt.
  5. Salad: chop romaine and slice celery; toss with dressing; top with buffalo chicken and beans.
  6. Serve per Notes.

Notes

Buffalo salad without ranch—hot sauce + vinegar gives the punch. Portions (per person): Frank: 4 cups salad + 7 oz chicken + 1/2 cup beans Susan: 3 cups salad + 5 oz chicken + 1/3 cup beans Elise: 3 cups salad + 5 oz chicken + 1/3 cup beans Tips (recipe-specific): Toss chicken while warm so hot sauce clings. Beans add that ‘comfort fullness’ without chips.
