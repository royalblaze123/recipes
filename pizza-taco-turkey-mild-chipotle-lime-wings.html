Taco Turkey Pizza + Mild Chipotle-Lime Wings

Mild taco-seasoned turkey pizza with a bright chipotle-lime wing finish (flavor-forward, not overly spicy).

Servings

3 servings (Frank, Susan, Elise).

Times

  • Prep time: 25 minutes
  • Cook time: 40 minutes
  • Total time: 65 minutes

Ingredients

Pizza

  • 1 dough ball pizza dough
  • 1/2 jar no-salt-added salsa
  • 2 oz shredded part-skim mozzarella
  • 8 oz lean ground turkey
  • 1/2 each red onion
  • 1/2 each green bell pepper
  • 2 tsp chili powder
  • 1 1/2 tsp cumin
  • 2 tsp garlic powder
  • 32 oz chicken wings
  • 1/2 tsp ground black pepper
  • 3 tsp extra-virgin olive oil
  • 1 each lime
  • 1 tsp chipotle powder

Wings

  • 2 lb chicken wings
  • 1 tsp chili powder
  • 1 tsp garlic powder
  • 1/2 tsp cumin
  • 1/2 tsp ground black pepper

Wing Finish Sauce

  • 1 tbsp extra-virgin olive oil
  • 1 each lime
  • 1 tsp chipotle powder

Preparation Steps

  1. Remove 1 dough ball from the refrigerator (or thawed vacuum-sealed dough) and keep covered at room temperature for 60–90 minutes.
  2. Preheat oven with a pizza steel or stone to 500°F for 45 minutes (or your oven’s maximum).
  3. Cook turkey topping: In a nonstick skillet over medium heat, cook ground turkey 6–8 minutes, breaking it up, until cooked through. Sprinkle in chili powder, cumin, and garlic powder and stir 30 seconds, then remove from heat.
  4. Wings: Pat wings very dry. Toss with chili powder, garlic powder, cumin, and black pepper.
  5. Cosori dual-basket setup: Split wings between both baskets in a single layer. Use Sync Cook at 380°F for 18 minutes, shaking halfway.
  6. Crisp finish: Increase to 400°F and cook 6–8 minutes more until browned and 165°F internally.
  7. Chipotle-lime finish: In a large bowl, whisk olive oil, juice from 1 lime, and chipotle powder. Toss hot wings lightly so they are coated but not soggy.
  8. Pizza topping prep: Thin-slice red onion. Slice green bell pepper.
  9. Stretch dough into a 12-inch round. Spread salsa in a thin layer. Sprinkle mozzarella. Add seasoned turkey, red onion, and bell pepper.
  10. Bake 7–9 minutes until browned and bubbling. Rest 2 minutes before slicing.
  11. Portion and serve using the Portions split below.

Notes

Mild taco-seasoned turkey pizza with a bright chipotle-lime wing finish (flavor-forward, not overly spicy).

Portions (per person):

Frank: 4 slices (about 1/3 of the 12-inch pizza) + 8 wings (about 4 oz cooked wings)

Susan: 3 slices (about 1/4 of the 12-inch pizza) + 6 wings (about 3 oz cooked wings)

Elise: 3 slices (about 1/4 of the 12-inch pizza) + 6 wings (about 3 oz cooked wings)

Tips (recipe-specific):

Keep salsa as a thin layer; too much liquid can soften the crust.

Use chipotle powder as a measured finish so the heat stays mild and predictable.

If you want extra crunch, add shredded lettuce after baking instead of baking it on the pizza.
