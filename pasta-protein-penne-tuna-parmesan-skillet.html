Protein+ Penne Tuna Parmesan Skillet

Nutrition (approx)

Frank: 574 kcal • P 59g • C 50g • F 15g
Susan/Elise (each): 430 kcal • P 44g • C 38g • F 11g

Ingredients (purchase-clean)

  • 1/3 box Barilla Protein+ penne
  • 2 cans tuna
  • 2 tbsp grated parmesan
  • 4 tsp extra-virgin olive oil
  • 1 each yellow onion
  • 2 tsp garlic powder
  • 2 tsp Italian seasoning
  • 1 tsp black pepper
  • 1 tsp kosher salt

Steps

  1. Bring a large pot of water to a boil for the Barilla Protein+ penne. (Use enough water to fully cover pasta; do not add water to Ingredients.)
  2. Can size note: use two 5 oz cans tuna. Drain well. Set aside.
  3. Dice the yellow onion. Add to the skillet and cook 4–6 minutes until softened.
  4. Cook Barilla Protein+ penne to al dente per box time. Reserve 1 cup pasta water, then drain.
  5. Toss pasta into the sauce/skillet. Add reserved pasta water 1–2 tbsp at a time until the sauce clings well.
  6. Portion into bowls using the Portions guidance in Notes.

Notes

Tuna skillet pasta with parmesan and peppery bite. Built on Barilla Protein+ for a higher-protein pasta plate with close-approx nutrition. Portions (per person): Frank: 2 oz dry Barilla Protein+ penne + 1/3 of the skillet/sauce Susan: 1.5 oz dry Barilla Protein+ penne + 1/4 of the skillet/sauce Elise: 1.5 oz dry Barilla Protein+ penne + 1/4 of the skillet/sauce
