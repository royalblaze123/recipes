Chicken Fajita Burrito Bowls

Nutrition (approx)

Frank: 535 kcal • P 50g • C 52g • F 16g
Susan/Elise (each): 401 kcal • P 38g • C 39g • F 12g

Ingredients (purchase-clean)

  • 1.25 lb boneless skinless chicken breast
  • 1 bag frozen fajita-style pepper blend
  • 1 can black beans
  • 1 cup dry brown rice
  • 1 bag shredded lettuce
  • 1 each avocado
  • 1/2 jar salsa
  • 1 each lime
  • 4 tsp extra-virgin olive oil
  • 2 tsp chili powder
  • 2 tsp cumin
  • 2 tsp garlic powder
  • 1 tsp smoked paprika
  • 1 tsp black pepper
  • 1 tsp kosher salt

Steps

  1. Rice: rinse 1 cup dry brown rice. Cook with 2 cups water (or low-sodium broth): bring to a boil, cover, reduce to low, and simmer 35–40 minutes. Rest 5 minutes, then fluff.
  2. Chicken: cut into 1/2-inch pieces. Cook in a skillet with 2 tsp olive oil 8–10 minutes until 165°F. Season with spices.
  3. Veg: cook frozen fajita pepper blend in the same skillet 6–8 minutes until browned.
  4. Beans: Can size note—use a standard 15 oz can black beans.. Drain/rinse. Use 1 1/2 cups beans; refrigerate remainder.
  5. Combine: stir beans into chicken and peppers and warm 2 minutes. Finish with 2 tsp lime juice.
  6. Assembly: divide lettuce (or cauliflower rice if listed) into three bowls.
  7. Top each bowl with the protein mix and measured rice (or other carb component if used).
  8. Add salsa and measured diced avocado on top.
  9. Portion per Notes.
  10. Avocado: halve, pit, and dice. Use about 1/2 avocado total; refrigerate remaining avocado with the pit to slow browning.

Notes

Fajita chicken burrito bowls with controlled rice/beans and a salsa + avocado finish. Portions (per person): Frank: 1 large burrito bowl (about 1/3 of the batch) + 1/3 avocado + salsa Susan: 1 medium burrito bowl (about 1/4 of the batch) + 1/4 avocado + salsa Elise: 1 medium burrito bowl (about 1/4 of the batch) + 1/4 avocado + salsa
