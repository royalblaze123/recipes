Air Fryer Teriyaki Beef Lettuce Bowls with Cauliflower Rice and Mushrooms

Prep: 18 min   Cook: 18 min   Total: 36 min

Ingredients

  • 1.5 lb sirloin steak
  • 1 head butter lettuce
  • 1 bag frozen cauliflower rice
  • 1 package baby bella mushrooms
  • 1 bag microgreens
  • 1 lime
  • 1 knob fresh ginger
  • 1 bulb garlic
  • olive oil
  • low-sodium teriyaki sauce
  • rice vinegar
  • sesame oil
  • black pepper
  • salt substitute
  • sesame seeds

RBM rules: quantities appear before items; fresh proteins use weight. Condiments/spices/seasonings are listed name-only; all measurements are in Steps. Water is referenced in Steps only.

Step-by-step Instructions

  1. Slice mushrooms. Heat a skillet over medium-high with 4 tsp olive oil. Cook mushrooms 6–8 minutes until browned. Season with 1 tsp salt substitute + 1 tsp black pepper; set aside.
  2. Cook cauliflower rice: in same skillet add 2 tsp olive oil. Add cauliflower rice; cook 6–8 minutes until hot and steam-dried. Season with 1 tsp salt substitute + 1 tsp black pepper + 4 tsp lime juice.
  3. Make quick teriyaki-ginger glaze: whisk 10 tsp low-sodium teriyaki sauce + 6 tsp rice vinegar + 2 tsp sesame oil + 2 tsp grated ginger + 1 minced garlic clove + 1 tsp black pepper.
  4. Cut sirloin into thin strips (against the grain). Toss with half the glaze; reserve the rest for finishing.
  5. Air fry beef strips (Cosori): preheat 400°F for 3 minutes. Cook 6–8 minutes, shaking once, until browned and just cooked. Toss with remaining glaze; sprinkle 2 tsp sesame seeds.
  6. Assemble lettuce bowls: fill lettuce cups with cauliflower rice + mushrooms + beef. Top with microgreens.
  7. Portion: Frank gets ~6 oz cooked beef + ~1 1/2 cups cauliflower rice + ~1 1/2 cups mushrooms + 4–5 lettuce cups. Susan and Elise each get ~4.5 oz beef + ~1 1/4 cups cauliflower rice + ~1 1/4 cups mushrooms + 3–4 lettuce cups.
