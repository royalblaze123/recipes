Chicken Sweet Potato Green Bean Bake

NOTES: Built as a complete dinner using lean protein, high-fiber vegetables, and controlled starch portions. Use low-sodium broth and minimal added salt to support blood pressure and kidney health. PORTION SIZES: Frank: largest portion (~1/3 of pan); Susan: medium portion (~1/4 of pan); Elise: medium portion (~1/4 of pan).

Ingredients

  • 1 lb boneless, skinless chicken breast, cut into 1-inch cubes
  • 2 medium sweet potatoes, peeled and cut into 1/2-inch cubes
  • 3 cups trimmed fresh green beans, cut into 2-inch pieces
  • 1 medium red onion, sliced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 tbsp extra-virgin olive oil
  • 1 tsp smoked paprika
  • 1 tsp dried thyme
  • 1/2 tsp dried rosemary, crushed
  • 1/2 tsp kosher salt (or to taste)
  • Freshly ground black pepper, to taste
  • Olive oil spray for baking dish

Step-by-Step Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 400°F. Lightly spray a 9x13-inch baking dish with olive oil spray.
  2. In a large mixing bowl, combine sweet potato cubes, green beans, and sliced red onion.
  3. Add minced garlic, olive oil, smoked paprika, dried thyme, dried rosemary, salt, and black pepper. Toss until vegetables are evenly coated.
  4. Add chicken cubes to the bowl and toss again so the chicken is coated in the oil and spices.
  5. Spread the chicken and vegetable mixture in an even layer in the prepared baking dish.
  6. Cover loosely with foil and bake 20 minutes.
  7. Remove foil, stir gently, and continue baking 15–20 minutes until sweet potatoes are tender and chicken is cooked through (internal temperature 165°F).
  8. Let casserole rest 5–10 minutes, then divide into portions for Frank, Susan, and Elise.

Nutrition per Person

  • Frank: ~570 kcal, ~52 g protein, ~52 g carbs, ~17 g fat.
  • Susan: ~430 kcal, ~39 g protein, ~39 g carbs, ~13 g fat.
  • Elise: ~430 kcal, ~39 g protein, ~39 g carbs, ~13 g fat.
