Air Fryer Salmon with Sweet Potato Fries and Green Beans (Cosori)

NOTES: Complete dinner with lean seafood, smart carbs, and 1–2 cups of non-starchy vegetables per person. Use low-sodium ingredients, avoid added sugars, and keep portions aligned with goals. PORTION SIZES: Frank: largest portion (~1/3 of the total volume); Susan: medium portion (~1/4 of the total volume); Elise: medium portion (~1/4 of the total volume) (Frank = 1.0×, Susan/Elise ≈0.75× portions).

Ingredients

  • 3 salmon fillets (4–5 oz each)
  • 2 medium sweet potatoes, cut into fry-shaped sticks
  • 3 cups fresh or frozen green beans, trimmed
  • 1 1/2 tbsp extra-virgin olive oil
  • 1 tsp garlic powder
  • 1 tsp smoked paprika
  • 1/2 tsp dried Italian seasoning
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice
  • Freshly ground black pepper, to taste

Step-by-Step Instructions

  1. Preheat the Cosori dual-basket air fryer to 380°F (193°C).
  2. In a bowl, toss sweet potato sticks with 1 tbsp olive oil, smoked paprika, half of the garlic powder, and black pepper until evenly coated.
  3. In a separate bowl, toss green beans with remaining 1/2 tbsp olive oil, Italian seasoning, remaining garlic powder, and black pepper.
  4. Pat salmon fillets dry and brush lightly with lemon juice and a touch of any remaining oil. Season with a little black pepper.
  5. Load Basket 1 with the sweet potato fries in an even layer (use the shake reminder). Load Basket 2 with the green beans.
  6. Set Basket 1 (sweet potato fries) to 20 minutes at 380°F with shake reminder at the halfway point. Set Basket 2 (green beans) to 12 minutes at 380°F. Use Sync Finish so both baskets end together.
  7. At the 8-minute mark remaining, pause Basket 2, move green beans to one side of that basket, and add salmon fillets to the open side. Resume cooking so salmon and green beans finish together, checking salmon for doneness (flakes easily with a fork).
  8. Divide salmon, sweet potato fries, and green beans among three plates, giving Frank the largest portion of salmon and fries while keeping overall fat moderate.

Nutrition per Person

  • Frank: ~620 kcal, ~47 g protein, ~60 g carbs, ~18 g fat.
  • Susan: ~465 kcal, ~35 g protein, ~45 g carbs, ~13 g fat.
  • Elise: ~465 kcal, ~35 g protein, ~45 g carbs, ~13 g fat.
