Pork Tenderloin Dijon Provolone Sandwich + Slaw

Roasted pork tenderloin with Dijon yogurt and provolone, paired with a crunchy broccoli slaw side.

Servings

3 servings (Frank, Susan, Elise).

Times

  • Prep time: 15 minutes
  • Cook time: 22 minutes
  • Total time: 37 minutes

Ingredients

Sandwich

  • 6 slices whole wheat bread
  • 1 lb pork tenderloin
  • 3 slices reduced-fat provolone cheese
  • 1 cup nonfat Greek yogurt
  • 1 each lemon
  • 1 tsp Dijon mustard
  • 2 hearts romaine hearts
  • 1 tsp garlic powder
  • 1 tsp ground black pepper

Side

  • 1 bag broccoli slaw
  • 1 each apple

Step-by-step Instructions

  1. Cook the pork: Preheat oven to 425°F. Season pork with garlic powder and black pepper. Roast until 145°F, about 18–22 minutes. Rest 5 minutes, then thin-slice.
  2. Make Dijon yogurt: Mix 1/2 cup Greek yogurt with 1 tsp Dijon and 1 tsp lemon juice.
  3. Assemble sandwiches: Spread Dijon yogurt on bread (measure per person). Layer pork, provolone (cut slices to match 0.75 portions), and romaine. Close into 3 full sandwiches.
  4. Prep slaw side: Toss broccoli slaw with 2 tbsp of the Dijon yogurt (or plain yogurt if you prefer). Slice apple and portion per person.

Portion Sizes

Portions (per person):
Frank: 2 slices bread, 3.0 oz cooked pork, 1 slice provolone, 2 tbsp Dijon yogurt, 1 cup romaine, 1 cup broccoli slaw, 1/2 apple

Susan: 2 slices bread, 2.25 oz cooked pork, 3/4 slice provolone, 1 1/2 tbsp Dijon yogurt, 3/4 cup romaine, 3/4 cup broccoli slaw, 1/4 apple

Elise: 2 slices bread, 2.25 oz cooked pork, 3/4 slice provolone, 1 1/2 tbsp Dijon yogurt, 3/4 cup romaine, 3/4 cup broccoli slaw, 1/4 apple
