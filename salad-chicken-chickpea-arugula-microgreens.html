Chicken Chickpea Arugula Salad with Lemon Vinaigrette

Prep: 18 min   Cook: 12 min   Total: 30 min

Ingredients

  • 14 oz chicken breast
  • 0.5 can no-salt-added chickpeas
  • 1 bag arugula
  • 1 bag microgreens
  • 1 pint cherry tomatoes
  • 1 cucumber
  • 1 lemon
  • olive oil
  • black pepper
  • salt substitute
  • vinegar
  • Dijon mustard

Hard rules enforced: Lunch validations (calories, sat fat, sodium, fiber, added sugar). Quantities appear before items; fresh meat/poultry/fish use weights. Condiments/spices/seasonings have no measurements in Ingredients; all measurements are in Steps. No olives, no squash, no Brussels sprouts, no zucchini. Yogurt is not used in dinners.

Step-by-step Instructions

  1. Chicken: season with black pepper and salt substitute. Cook in a skillet with 2 tsp olive oil 6–7 minutes per side to 165°F. Rest 5 minutes and slice.
  2. Beans: drain and rinse 1/2 can chickpeas.
  3. Build salad: arugula + chickpeas + tomatoes + cucumber. Add sliced chicken and microgreens on top.
  4. Dressing: whisk 2 tsp lemon juice + 2 tsp olive oil + 1 tsp vinegar + Dijon mustard + black pepper + salt substitute. Toss lightly.
  5. Portion: Frank large bowl; Susan/Elise ~3/4 bowl each.
