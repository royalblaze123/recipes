Turkey Shepherd’s Pie with Sweet Potato Top

Nutrition (approx)

Frank: 645 kcal • P 60g • C 55g • F 16g • Sat 6g • Na 690mg • Fiber 10g
Susan/Elise (each): 484 kcal • P 45g • C 41g • F 12g • Sat 4g • Na 518mg • Fiber 8g

Ingredients (purchase-clean)

  • 1.75 lb 93% lean ground turkey
  • 2.00 lb sweet potatoes
  • 1 bag frozen mixed vegetables
  • low-sodium chicken broth
  • cornstarch
  • kosher salt
  • black pepper
  • garlic powder
  • onion powder
  • Italian seasoning
  • smoked paprika
  • cornstarch
  • extra-virgin olive oil
  • low-sodium chicken broth
  • Worcestershire sauce

Steps

  1. Oven: heat to 425°F.
  2. Sweet potato mash: peel and cube sweet potatoes. Boil 12–14 minutes until tender; drain. Mash with 1/2 cup skim milk, 1/2 tsp kosher salt, black pepper.
  3. Turkey base: brown ground turkey 7–9 minutes. Season with garlic powder, onion powder, smoked paprika, black pepper, and 1 tsp Worcestershire.
  4. Veg: add 3 cups frozen mixed vegetables; cook 6 minutes until hot and water reduces.
  5. Gravy: whisk 1 tbsp cornstarch into 1 cup low-sodium broth. Stir into turkey mixture; simmer 2–3 minutes until thick.
  6. Assemble: spread turkey base in dish, top with sweet potato mash.
  7. Bake 18–20 minutes until edges brown. Rest 5 minutes. Serve per Notes.

Notes

Shepherd’s pie comfort with sweet potato topping and a thick, savory base. Portions (per person): Frank: About 2 1/2 cups (1/3 of pan) + veg side Susan: About 2 cups (1/4 of pan) + veg side Elise: About 2 cups (1/4 of pan) + veg side Tips (recipe-specific): Brown the turkey first for deep flavor before adding broth. Let steam escape before serving so the mash stays set.
