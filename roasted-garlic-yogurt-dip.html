Roasted Garlic Yogurt Dip

Category: Dip • Yield: About 3/4 cup (14 tbsp) • AnyList-ready

Ingredients

  • 3/4 cup 2% plain Greek yogurt
  • 2 tsp extra-virgin olive oil
  • 2 tsp lemon juice
  • 1 tsp garlic powder
  • 1/2 tsp smoked paprika
  • 1/4 tsp black pepper

Steps

  1. Whisk Greek yogurt, olive oil, lemon juice, garlic powder, smoked paprika, and black pepper until smooth.
  2. Chill 10 minutes so the garlic flavor blooms.
