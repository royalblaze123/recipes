Rice & Bean Comfort Bowl with Turkey, Pinto Beans, Quinoa, and Lime Cabbage

Prep: 18 min   Cook: 22 min   Total: 40 min

Ingredients

  • 1.5 lb lean ground turkey 93%
  • 0.5 cup dry quinoa
  • 0.5 can no-salt-added pinto beans
  • 0.5 bag shredded cabbage
  • 1 bag romaine hearts
  • 1 lime
  • 1 onion
  • olive oil
  • cumin
  • chili powder
  • garlic powder
  • black pepper
  • salt substitute
  • vinegar
  • salsa

Hard rules enforced: Dinner validations (calories 600–650 Frank, sat fat ≤8g, sodium ≤700mg, fiber ≥10g, added sugar ≤6g). Quantities appear before items; fresh meat/poultry/fish use weights. Condiments/spices/seasonings have no measurements in Ingredients; all measurements are in Steps. No olives, no squash, no Brussels sprouts, no zucchini, no tofu/tempeh, no yogurt in dinners.

Step-by-step Instructions

  1. Cook quinoa: rinse 1/2 cup dry quinoa. Simmer with 1 cup water, covered, 15 minutes; rest 5 minutes.
  2. Turkey: brown 1.5 lb lean ground turkey with 2 tsp olive oil 7–9 minutes. Season with 2 tsp cumin + 1 tsp chili powder + 1/2 tsp garlic powder + 1/2 tsp black pepper + 1/2 tsp salt substitute.
  3. Beans: drain and rinse 1/2 can pinto beans; stir into turkey with 2 tbsp water; warm 2 minutes.
  4. Cabbage: toss 1/2 bag shredded cabbage with 2 tsp lime juice + 1 tsp vinegar + 1 tsp olive oil + 1/4 tsp black pepper + 1/4 tsp salt substitute.
  5. Romaine: chop 1 bag romaine hearts.
  6. Build bowls: romaine + quinoa + turkey/bean mix + cabbage.
  7. Finish: add salsa in measured amounts (Frank 1 tbsp; Susan 2 tsp; Elise 2 tsp).
