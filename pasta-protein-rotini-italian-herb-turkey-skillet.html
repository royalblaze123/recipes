Protein+ Rotini Italian Herb Turkey Skillet

Nutrition (approx)

Frank: 674 kcal • P 55g • C 50g • F 29g
Susan/Elise (each): 506 kcal • P 41g • C 38g • F 22g

Ingredients (purchase-clean)

  • 1/3 box Barilla Protein+ rotini
  • 1.25 lb 93% lean ground turkey
  • 4 tsp extra-virgin olive oil
  • 1 each yellow onion
  • 3 tbsp grated parmesan
  • 2 tsp garlic powder
  • 2 tsp Italian seasoning
  • 1 tsp black pepper
  • 1 tsp kosher salt

Steps

  1. Bring a large pot of water to a boil for the Barilla Protein+ rotini. (Use enough water to fully cover pasta; do not add water to Ingredients.)
  2. Heat a large nonstick skillet over medium-high. Add 4 tsp extra-virgin olive oil if listed. Add ground turkey and cook 7–9 minutes, breaking it up, until browned and cooked through.
  3. Dice the yellow onion. Add to the skillet and cook 4–6 minutes until softened.
  4. Cook Barilla Protein+ rotini to al dente per box time. Reserve 1 cup pasta water, then drain.
  5. Toss pasta into the sauce/skillet. Add reserved pasta water 1–2 tbsp at a time until the sauce clings well.
  6. Portion into bowls using the Portions guidance in Notes.

Notes

Italian herb turkey skillet with onions and parmesan. Built on Barilla Protein+ for a higher-protein pasta plate with close-approx nutrition. Portions (per person): Frank: 2 oz dry Barilla Protein+ rotini + 1/3 of the skillet/sauce Susan: 1.5 oz dry Barilla Protein+ rotini + 1/4 of the skillet/sauce Elise: 1.5 oz dry Barilla Protein+ rotini + 1/4 of the skillet/sauce
