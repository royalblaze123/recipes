Parmesan-Crusted Cod with Roasted Carrots and Big Salad

Prep: 18 min   Cook: 22 min   Total: 40 min

Ingredients

  • 1.5 lb cod fillets
  • 1 lb carrots
  • 1 bag spring mix
  • 1 cucumber
  • 1 pint cherry tomatoes
  • 2 tbsp grated parmesan cheese
  • olive oil
  • whole wheat panko crumbs
  • garlic powder
  • black pepper
  • salt substitute
  • lemon
  • vinegar

Hard rules enforced: Dinner validations (calories 600–650 Frank, sat fat ≤8g, sodium ≤700mg, fiber ≥10g, added sugar ≤6g). Quantities appear before items; fresh meat/poultry/fish use weights. Condiments/spices/seasonings have no measurements in Ingredients; all measurements are in Steps. No olives, no squash, no Brussels sprouts, no zucchini, no tofu/tempeh, no yogurt in dinners.

Step-by-step Instructions

  1. Roast carrots: peel/slice 1 lb carrots. Toss with 2 tsp olive oil + 1/2 tsp garlic powder + 1/4 tsp black pepper + 1/2 tsp salt substitute. Roast 425°F for 18 minutes.
  2. Cod: pat dry 1.5 lb cod. Mix topping: 2 tbsp parmesan + 1/4 cup panko + 1/2 tsp garlic powder + 1/4 tsp black pepper. Press onto cod.
  3. Bake cod at 425°F for 10–12 minutes until flaky.
  4. Salad: combine 1 bag spring mix + sliced 1 cucumber + 1 pint cherry tomatoes.
  5. Dressing: whisk 2 tsp olive oil + 2 tsp vinegar + 2 tsp lemon juice + 1/4 tsp black pepper + 1/4 tsp salt substitute; toss salad.
  6. Serve cod with carrots and salad. Portion: Frank largest cod share; Susan/Elise ~75%.
