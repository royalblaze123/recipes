Slow Cooker Tuscan Turkey Breast with White Beans and Kale

Prep: 18 min   Cook: 390 min   Total: 408 min

Ingredients

  • 2.0 lb turkey breast
  • 0.75 carton low-sodium chicken broth
  • 0.5 can cannellini beans
  • 1 onion
  • 1 lemon
  • 1 bunch kale
  • 1 package baby bella mushrooms
  • olive oil
  • tomato paste
  • Italian seasoning
  • garlic powder
  • black pepper
  • salt substitute

RBM rules: quantities appear before items; fresh meat/poultry/fish use weights. Condiments/spices/seasonings are name-only (no tsp/tbsp in Ingredients); all measurements are in Steps. Water is referenced in Steps only.

Step-by-step Instructions

  1. Slow cooker: add turkey breast and thick-sliced mushrooms. Add sliced onion.
  2. Add 0.75 carton broth (24 fl oz) and 2 tbsp tomato paste.
  3. Season: add 2 tsp Italian seasoning + 1 tsp garlic powder + 1 tsp black pepper + 2 tsp salt substitute. Add zest of 1 lemon.
  4. Cook on LOW 6–7 hours or HIGH 3.5–4.5 hours until turkey reaches 165°F.
  5. Remove turkey; slice thick. Return slices to slow cooker.
  6. Add 0.5 can cannellini beans (drained/rinsed). Cook 20 minutes on HIGH.
  7. Kale last: strip kale leaves and chop. Add kale for the last 10–12 minutes on HIGH until just wilted (not overcooked).
  8. Finish with 4 tsp lemon juice and black pepper.
  9. Portion: Frank gets 6 oz turkey + ~2 cups beans/mushrooms/kale broth. Susan and Elise each get ~4.5 oz turkey + ~1 1/2 cups bowl base.

Notes

Garlic-herb turkey with creamy beans (no cream) and kale added at the end for a hearty, structured bowl. Portions (per person): Frank: - Turkey breast: 6 oz cooked - Beans/mushrooms/kale base: ~2 cups Susan: - Turkey breast: ~4.5 oz cooked - Beans/mushrooms/kale base: ~1 1/2 cups Elise: - Turkey breast: ~4.5 oz cooked - Beans/mushrooms/kale base: ~1 1/2 cups Tips (recipe-specific): Kale goes in at the end—long cooks turn it dull and soft. Slice turkey thick; thin slices dry out faster on reheat.
