Creamy Smoky Ranch Dip

Category: Dip • Yield: About 3/4 cup (13–14 tbsp) • AnyList-ready

Ingredients

  • 3/4 cup 2% plain Greek yogurt
  • 2 tsp extra-virgin olive oil
  • 2 tsp apple cider vinegar
  • 1 tsp garlic powder
  • 1 tsp onion powder
  • 1 tsp smoked paprika
  • 1/4 tsp black pepper

Steps

  1. Whisk Greek yogurt, olive oil, apple cider vinegar, garlic powder, onion powder, smoked paprika, and black pepper until smooth.
  2. Chill 10 minutes before serving.
