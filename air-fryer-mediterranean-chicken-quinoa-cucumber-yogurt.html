Air Fryer Mediterranean Chicken with Quinoa and Cucumber Yogurt

Mediterranean herb chicken over quinoa with a cool cucumber-yogurt sauce—filling, mild, and weeknight-friendly.

Servings

3 servings.

Times

  • Prep time: 25 minutes
  • Cook time: 14 minutes
  • Total time: 39 minutes

Ingredients

Main Dish

  • 1 lb boneless skinless chicken breast
  • 1 bag quinoa
  • 1 each cucumber
  • 1 each lemon
  • 1 bunch dill
  • 1 bottle extra virgin olive oil
  • 1 container nonfat Greek yogurt
  • 1 jar oregano
  • 1 jar garlic powder
  • 1 jar black pepper
    • 

    Preparation Steps

    1. Quinoa: cook 3/4 cup dry quinoa. Measure out 1 1/2 cups cooked quinoa for this dinner; refrigerate remaining quinoa.
    2. Prep chicken: slice chicken into bite-size pieces. Toss with 2 tsp olive oil, oregano, garlic powder, black pepper, and 1 tsp lemon juice. Rest 10 minutes.
    3. Cosori setup: preheat Basket B to 400°F for 3 minutes.
    4. Cook chicken: add chicken pieces to Basket B in a single layer and cook 400°F for 8–10 minutes, shaking once, until 165°F internal.
    5. Cucumber yogurt: grate 1/2 cucumber and squeeze out moisture. Stir into 1/4 cup Greek yogurt with chopped dill and 1 tsp lemon juice.
    6. Serve (40/30/30): build bowls with quinoa, chicken, and cucumber yogurt on the side.

      Notes

      Portions (per person):


      Frank – Quinoa: about 1/2 cup cooked. Chicken: largest share. Cucumber yogurt on the side.


      Susan – Quinoa: about 3/8 cup cooked. Chicken: medium share. Cucumber yogurt on the side.


      Elise – Quinoa: about 3/8 cup cooked. Chicken: medium share. Cucumber yogurt on the side.


      Tips (recipe-specific):


      Squeeze moisture from cucumber so the sauce stays thick, not watery.


      Cook chicken in a single layer for browning; do a second batch if needed.


      If you want a lighter carb night, reduce quinoa and add a side salad.
