Turkey Chili-Mac Bake (Protein Pasta)

Nutrition (approx)

Frank: 650 kcal • P 62g • C 65g • F 16g • Sat 6g • Na 700mg • Fiber 12g
Susan/Elise (each): 488 kcal • P 46g • C 49g • F 12g • Sat 4g • Na 525mg • Fiber 9g

Ingredients (purchase-clean)

  • 1 box Barilla Protein+ pasta
  • 1.75 lb 93% lean ground turkey
  • 1 can kidney beans
  • 1 can no-salt-added diced tomatoes
  • 1 carton skim milk
  • 1 container reduced-fat shredded cheddar cheese
  • kosher salt
  • black pepper
  • garlic powder
  • onion powder
  • smoked paprika
  • Italian seasoning
  • chili powder
  • ground cumin
  • crushed red pepper
  • cornstarch
  • extra-virgin olive oil
  • Dijon mustard
  • hot sauce
  • Worcestershire sauce
  • low-sodium soy sauce
  • rice vinegar
  • chili powder
  • ground cumin

Steps

  1. Pasta: cook Protein+ pasta al dente. Drain.
  2. Turkey: brown turkey 7–9 minutes. Season with chili powder, cumin, garlic powder, onion powder, kosher salt, black pepper.
  3. Tomatoes: Can size note—use a standard 14.5 oz can no-salt-added diced tomatoes.. Add 1 can with juices.
  4. Beans: Can size note—use a standard 15 oz can kidney beans.. Drain/rinse. Add 1 1/2 cups beans; refrigerate remainder.
  5. Sauce base: whisk 2 tbsp cornstarch into 2 cups skim milk. Add and simmer 3 minutes until thick.
  6. Cheese: stir in 6 oz reduced-fat cheddar until melted.
  7. Combine: fold in pasta. Transfer to baking dish.
  8. Bake: 425°F for 10 minutes. Rest 5 minutes before portioning.
  9. Serve per Notes.

Notes

Chili mac bake that eats like comfort casserole but stays structured. Portions (per person): Frank: About 2 1/2 cups mac (1/3 pan) + extra veg Susan: About 2 cups mac (1/4 pan) + extra veg Elise: About 2 cups mac (1/4 pan) + extra veg Tips (recipe-specific): Resting after bake is part of the texture—don’t skip it. No-salt tomatoes keep sodium down; season with spices instead.
