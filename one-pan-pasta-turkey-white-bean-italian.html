One-Pan Whole Wheat Pasta with Turkey, White Beans, and Spinach

Prep: 18 min   Cook: 26 min   Total: 44 min

Ingredients

  • 1.5 lb lean ground turkey 93%
  • 8 oz whole wheat pasta
  • 0.5 can no-salt-added cannellini beans
  • 1 onion
  • 1 bag spinach
  • 0.5 jar no-salt-added marinara sauce
  • 0.5 carton low-sodium chicken broth
  • 2 tbsp grated parmesan cheese
  • olive oil
  • garlic powder
  • Italian seasoning
  • black pepper
  • salt substitute

Hard rules enforced: Dinner validations (calories 600–650 Frank, sat fat ≤8g, sodium ≤700mg, fiber ≥10g, added sugar ≤6g). Quantities appear before items; fresh meat/poultry/fish use weights. Condiments/spices/seasonings have no measurements in Ingredients; all measurements are in Steps. No olives, no squash, no Brussels sprouts, no zucchini, no tofu/tempeh, no yogurt in dinners.

Step-by-step Instructions

  1. Brown turkey in a deep skillet with 2 tsp olive oil 7–9 minutes. Season with Italian seasoning, garlic powder, black pepper, and salt substitute.
  2. Add diced onion; cook 3 minutes.
  3. Beans: drain/rinse 1/2 can cannellini beans.
  4. Add pasta + beans + 1 cup broth (from carton) + 1/2 jar marinara + 1 1/2 cups water. Cover and simmer 10–12 minutes, stirring twice.
  5. Stir in spinach to wilt. Sprinkle 2 tbsp parmesan.
  6. Portion: Frank largest share; Susan/Elise ~75%.
