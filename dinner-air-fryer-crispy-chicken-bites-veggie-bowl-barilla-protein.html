Crispy Chicken Bites + Veggie Bowl + Barilla Protein+

Steps (Cosori dual-basket)

  1. Preheat Cosori to 400°F. Basket A: chicken bites. Basket B: vegetables (optional crisp finish).
  2. Chicken: cut chicken into 1-inch bites. Toss with 2 tsp olive oil, 1 tsp garlic powder, 1 tsp smoked paprika, 1/2 tsp kosher salt, and black pepper.
  3. Air fry chicken bites 10–12 minutes at 400°F; shake at reminder; cook to 165°F.
  4. Veg: sauté frozen mixed vegetables on stovetop 8–10 minutes until water evaporates and edges brown.
  5. Sauce: whisk 5 tbsp low-sodium soy sauce, 1 cup water, and 1 tbsp cornstarch; simmer 2 minutes until glossy. Use 6 tbsp sauce total, measured.
  6. Pasta: cook Barilla Protein+ to al dente; drain.
  7. Assemble: toss pasta with browned veg and measured sauce; top with chicken bites.
  8. Serve per Notes.

Notes

Crispy chicken + saucy noodles bowl built on Protein+ pasta and a measured glossy sauce. Portions (per person): Frank: 7 oz chicken bites + 2 cups pasta bowl + 1 cup veg mixed in Susan: 5 oz protein + 2 cups pasta bowl + 3/4 cup veg Elise: 5 oz protein + 2 cups pasta bowl + 3/4 cup veg Tips (recipe-specific): Brown veggies on the stove first so the bowl tastes like takeout. Measured thickened sauce keeps sodium and calories controlled.
