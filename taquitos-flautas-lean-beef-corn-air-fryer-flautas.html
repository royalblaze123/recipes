Lean Beef & Corn Air-Fryer Flautas

Nutrition (approx)

Frank: 650 kcal • P 58g • C 52g • F 20g
Susan/Elise (each): 488 kcal • P 44g • C 39g • F 15g

Ingredients (purchase-clean)

  • 1.50 lb 93% lean ground beef
  • 1 can corn
  • 1 package flour tortillas
  • 1 each avocado
  • 1/2 jar salsa
  • 1 bag spring mix
  • 1 each lime
  • 6 tsp extra-virgin olive oil
  • 1 bag reduced-fat shredded Mexican cheese blend
  • 2 tsp chili powder
  • 2 tsp cumin
  • 2 tsp garlic powder
  • 1 tsp smoked paprika
  • 1 tsp black pepper
  • 1 tsp kosher salt

Steps

  1. Tortillas: warm flour tortillas by microwaving under a damp paper towel for 25–35 seconds so they roll without cracking.
  2. Beef: cook ground beef 7–9 minutes until browned. Season with spices.
  3. Corn: Can size note—use a standard 15 oz can corn.. Drain. Add 1 1/2 cups corn to the beef; refrigerate remainder.
  4. Filling: mix beef-corn with 3/4 cup shredded cheese. Cool 5 minutes.
  5. Assemble flautas: add 3 tbsp filling to each tortilla, roll tightly, seam-side down.
  6. Preheat air fryer to 400°F.
  7. Brush or spray taquitos lightly with oil.
  8. Air fry in a single layer 8–10 minutes, turning at 5 minutes, until crisp and browned.
  9. Side salad: add spring mix to a bowl. Drizzle with 2 tsp lime juice and 2 tsp olive oil; toss.
  10. Avocado: halve, pit, and dice. Use about 1/2 avocado total; refrigerate remaining avocado with the pit to slow browning.
  11. Serve with salsa and portion per Notes.

Notes

Air-fryer flautas with lean beef + corn—crispy and satisfying without deep frying. Portions (per person): Frank: 5 taquitos + 1/3 of the side + 1/3 of the avocado + salsa Susan: 3 taquitos + 1/4 of the side + 1/4 of the avocado + salsa Elise: 3 taquitos + 1/4 of the side + 1/4 of the avocado + salsa
