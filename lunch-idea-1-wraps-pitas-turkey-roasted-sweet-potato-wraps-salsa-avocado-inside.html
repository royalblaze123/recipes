Turkey + Roasted Sweet Potato Wraps (Salsa + Avocado Inside)

Nutrition (approx)

Frank: 545 kcal • P 48g • C 56g • F 15g
Susan/Elise (each): 409 kcal • P 36g • C 42g • F 11g

Ingredients (purchase-clean)

  • 1.25 lb 93% lean ground turkey
  • 1.25 lb sweet potatoes
  • 1 package large whole-wheat tortillas
  • 1 each avocado
  • 1/2 jar salsa
  • 1 bag shredded lettuce
  • 1 each cucumber
  • 1 bag mini sweet peppers
  • 4 tsp extra-virgin olive oil
  • 2 tsp chili powder
  • 2 tsp cumin
  • 2 tsp garlic powder
  • 1 tsp smoked paprika
  • 1 tsp black pepper
  • 1 tsp kosher salt

Steps

  1. Sweet potatoes: small dice. Toss with 2 tsp olive oil and roast at 425°F for 18–22 minutes until browned and tender.
  2. Turkey: cook ground turkey 7–9 minutes until browned. Season with spices.
  3. Combine: fold roasted sweet potato into turkey and warm 1 minute.
  4. Wrap build: add shredded lettuce, then turkey-sweet potato mix.
  5. Avocado: halve, pit, and dice. Use about 1/2 avocado total.
  6. Salsa: spoon salsa inside each wrap/pita (not just on the side) so every bite has flavor.
  7. Roll and slice.
  8. Veggie sticks: slice cucumber and mini sweet peppers into sticks for a crunchy side.
  9. Portion per Notes.

Notes

Turkey wraps with roasted sweet potato for extra staying power; salsa + avocado inside. Portions (per person): Frank: 1 large wrap (with salsa + avocado inside) + veggie sticks Susan: 3/4 wrap (with salsa + avocado inside) + veggie sticks Elise: 3/4 wrap (with salsa + avocado inside) + veggie sticks
