Garlic-Herb Chicken Pizza + Lemon Pepper Wings (Full-Flavor / Occasional)

12-inch pizza dinner served with full-flavor wings. Designed to keep the dough sodium low so toppings and wings can carry flavor.

Servings

3 servings.

Times

  • Prep time: 25 minutes
  • Cook time: 25 minutes
  • Total time: 50 minutes

Ingredients

  • 1 pizza dough ball
  • 1 can no-salt-added tomato sauce
  • 3 oz part-skim mozzarella cheese
  • 10 oz boneless skinless chicken breast
  • 12 tsp extra-virgin olive oil
  • 1 8 oz mushrooms
  • 1 5 oz baby spinach
  • 1 lemon
  • 6 tsp garlic powder
  • 3 tsp onion powder
  • 3 tsp ground black pepper
  • 2 tsp smoked paprika
  • 3 tsp Italian seasoning
  • 4 tsp lemon pepper seasoning
  • 10 chicken wings

Preparation Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 500°F. Place a pizza stone or inverted sheet pan on the middle rack to preheat for at least 20 minutes.
  2. Wing count note: 1 wing = 1 whole wing (flat + drumette). If buying separated party wings, count 2 pieces = 1 wing.
  3. Pizza sauce: Measure 1/2 cup tomato sauce from the can. Stir in 1 tsp Italian seasoning, 1 tsp garlic powder, 1 tsp onion powder, and 1/2 tsp ground black pepper. Refrigerate the remaining tomato sauce in a sealed container.
  4. Pizza chicken: Dice 10 oz chicken breast into 1/2-inch pieces. Heat 2 tsp olive oil in a nonstick skillet over medium-high for 1 minute. Add the chicken plus 1 tsp garlic powder, 1 tsp onion powder, 1 tsp smoked paprika, and 1/2 tsp ground black pepper. Cook 6–8 minutes, stirring, until the chicken is fully cooked (165°F).
  5. Veg prep: Slice 1/2 of the 8 oz mushrooms package (about 4 oz). Refrigerate the remaining mushrooms in the package. Measure 2 packed cups baby spinach from the bag. Reseal and refrigerate the remaining spinach.
  6. Wings: Pat 10 wings very dry with paper towels. Toss in a bowl with 4 tsp olive oil, 2 tsp garlic powder, 1 tsp onion powder, 2 tsp smoked paprika, and 1 tsp ground black pepper.
  7. Air fryer (Cosori): Preheat to 380°F for 3 minutes. Arrange wings in a single layer on the basket/tray (cook in batches if needed). Cook 24 minutes at 380°F, flipping/shaking at 12 minutes. Increase to 400°F and cook 6 more minutes to crisp.
  8. Wing finish: Zest the lemon and squeeze 1 tbsp lemon juice. In a bowl, mix 4 tsp lemon pepper seasoning, 2 tsp olive oil, lemon zest, and the lemon juice. Toss wings to coat.
  9. Garlic-herb brush: Mix 3 tsp olive oil with 2 tsp garlic powder and 1 tsp Italian seasoning.
  10. Assemble pizza: Stretch 1 dough ball into a 12-inch round. Place on parchment. Spread 1/2 cup prepared sauce evenly, leaving a 1/2-inch rim.
  11. Top pizza: Sprinkle 3 oz mozzarella evenly.
  12. Add toppings: Scatter all cooked chicken over the pizza. Add the prepped vegetables in an even layer.
  13. Finish pizza: Brush the garlic-herb oil (all of it) over the crust rim and lightly over the top.
  14. Bake pizza: Slide the pizza (on parchment) onto the hot stone/pan. Bake 8–11 minutes until the crust is browned and the cheese is bubbling. Rest 3 minutes before slicing.
  15. Portions: Cut pizza into 10 slices. Frank: 4 slices + 4 wings. Susan: 3 slices + 3 wings. Elise: 3 slices + 3 wings.

Portions

  • Frank: 4 pizza slices + 4 wings
  • Susan: 3 pizza slices + 3 wings
  • Elise: 3 pizza slices + 3 wings

Wing count note: 1 wing = 1 whole wing (flat + drumette). If buying separated party wings, count 2 pieces = 1 wing.
