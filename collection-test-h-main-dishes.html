AnyList Collection Test H — Main Dishes

Goal: See if recipeCategory=Main Dishes auto-adds this recipe to AnyList’s built-in Main Dishes collection.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 bag frozen broccoli florets
  • 1 jar garlic powder
  • 1 jar black pepper
  • 1 bottle olive oil
  • 1 package chicken breast tenderloins
  • 1 bag frozen cauliflower rice

Preparation Steps

  1. Preheat air fryer to 390°F for 3 minutes.
  2. Measure out 1/2 bag frozen broccoli florets; reseal and return remaining broccoli to freezer. Toss broccoli with 1 tsp olive oil, 1/2 tsp garlic powder, and black pepper.
  3. Air-fry broccoli 8–10 minutes at 390°F, shaking halfway.
  4. Measure out 12 oz chicken tenderloins from the package (use remaining for another recipe). Toss with 2 tsp olive oil, 1 tsp garlic powder, and black pepper.
  5. Air-fry chicken 10–12 minutes at 390°F, flipping at 6 minutes, until internal temp reaches 165°F.
  6. Microwave 1/2 bag frozen cauliflower rice per bag directions; fluff and serve with chicken and broccoli.
How to test: Import this file into AnyList, then check whether it automatically appears inside the Main Dishes collection (without manually assigning it).
