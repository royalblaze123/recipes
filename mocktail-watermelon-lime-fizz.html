Watermelon Lime Fizz

Yield: 1 drink • Prep: 5 min • Total: 5 min

Ingredients (AnyList-ready)

  • 4 oz watermelon juice
  • 1 oz lime juice
  • 3/4 oz simple syrup
  • 4 oz club soda
  • 1 cup ice

Steps

  1. Fill a glass with 1 cup ice.
  2. Add watermelon juice, lime juice, and simple syrup.
  3. Top with club soda.
  4. Stir once, gently.

Tip: Use plenty of ice and a quick, gentle stir to keep carbonation lively.
