Chicken Tikka-Style Tomato Sauce (No Yogurt) + Rice

Nutrition (approx)

Frank: 650 kcal • P 60g • C 70g • F 14g
Susan/Elise (each): 488 kcal • P 45g • C 52g • F 10g

Ingredients (purchase-clean)

  • 1.75 lb boneless skinless chicken breast
  • 2 cans no-salt-added diced tomatoes
  • 1 each onion
  • low-sodium chicken broth
  • cornstarch
  • 1 cup dry brown rice
  • kosher salt
  • black pepper
  • garlic powder
  • onion powder
  • smoked paprika
  • Italian seasoning
  • ground cumin
  • chili powder
  • red pepper flakes
  • extra-virgin olive oil
  • low-sodium chicken broth
  • low-sodium beef broth
  • low-sodium soy sauce
  • hot sauce
  • Dijon mustard
  • balsamic vinegar
  • apple cider vinegar
  • cornstarch

Steps (slow cooker)

  1. Base: onion + tomatoes (2x 14.5 oz) + broth (1 cup).
  2. Add chicken; season smoked paprika, chili powder, cumin, garlic powder, pepper; modest salt.
  3. LOW 4–5 hr or HIGH 2–3 hr; shred chicken.
  4. Reduce HIGH lid ajar 15 min.
  5. Thicken with cornstarch slurry 10 min.
  6. Cook rice (1 cup dry). Serve per Notes.

Notes

Tikka directionally, built thick and cozy without yogurt. Portions (per person): Frank: 7 oz chicken + 1 cup cooked rice + 1 cup sauce/veg Susan: 5 oz chicken + 3/4 cup rice + 3/4 cup sauce/veg Elise: 5 oz chicken + 3/4 cup rice + 3/4 cup sauce/veg Tips (recipe-specific): Reduce then thicken so leftovers stay saucy. Spice tastes even better on day 2.
