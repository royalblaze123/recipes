Chophouse Lite: Chicken Cutlets with Green Beans + Mushrooms + Brown Rice (Smoky Tomato Dijon Glaze)

Servings

3 plates (Frank 1.0×, Susan 0.75×, Elise 0.75×).

Times

  • Prep time: 18 minutes
  • Cook time: 22 minutes
  • Total time: 40 minutes

Ingredients

  • 2.0 lb chicken breast cutlets
  • 1.0 cup dry brown rice
  • 1 bag frozen green beans
  • 24 oz mushrooms
  • 9 tsp olive oil
  • 2 tsp black pepper
  • 2 tsp salt substitute
  • 2 tsp dried parsley
  • 5 tsp tomato paste
  • 3 tsp vinegar
  • 3 tsp Dijon mustard

Steps

  1. Base: Cook 1 cup dry brown rice to yield ~3 cups cooked. Keep warm.
  2. Vegetables: Preheat oven to 425°F. Roast vegetables until browned-tender. Add parsley at the end.
  3. Chicken: Pound cutlets to even thickness. Season with black pepper + salt substitute.
  4. Sear: Hot skillet + 2 tsp olive oil. Sear 3–4 minutes per side to 165°F; rest 3 minutes; slice.
  5. Finish: Thin with 2–3 tbsp water. Brush late for a lacquer.
  6. Plate: Base → vegetables → sliced protein → finish drizzle. Portions per Notes.

Notes

Portions (per person):

Frank – 1 plate (8 oz cooked chicken, 1 cup cooked brown rice, 2 cups vegetables, 2–3 tbsp finish baste).

Susan – 1 plate (6 oz cooked chicken, 3/4 of Frank’s base portion, 1 1/2 cups vegetables, 2 tbsp finish baste).

Elise – 1 plate (6 oz cooked chicken, 3/4 of Frank’s base portion, 1 1/2 cups vegetables, 2 tbsp finish baste).

Tips (recipe-specific):

Pat the steak dry. Steam is not a flavor profile.

Slice across the grain every time. Chewing should be optional.

Storage + Reheat

  • Cool promptly; store steak + vegetables + base in airtight containers in the fridge up to 3–4 days (typical safety guidance).
  • Reheat steak gently (microwave 45–60 sec or skillet 1–2 min) to avoid overcooking; reheat vegetables 4–6 minutes at 350°F in the air fryer for texture; add finishing drizzle after reheating.
