Garlic-Lime Shrimp Skillet + Cauliflower Rice

Nutrition (approx)

Frank: 620 kcal • P 55g • C 45g • F 18g
Susan/Elise (each): 465 kcal • P 41g • C 34g • F 14g

Ingredients (purchase-clean)

  • 1.50 lb raw shrimp
  • 1 bag frozen cauliflower rice
  • 1 can black beans
  • 1 each lime
  • low-sodium chicken broth
  • kosher salt
  • black pepper
  • garlic powder
  • onion powder
  • smoked paprika
  • Italian seasoning
  • ground cumin
  • chili powder
  • red pepper flakes
  • extra-virgin olive oil
  • low-sodium chicken broth
  • low-sodium beef broth
  • low-sodium soy sauce
  • hot sauce
  • Dijon mustard
  • balsamic vinegar
  • apple cider vinegar
  • cornstarch

Steps (stovetop)

  1. Cauli rice: microwave cauliflower rice 6–7 minutes; drain and pat dry.
  2. Shrimp: pat shrimp dry. Sear in hot skillet with olive oil 1–2 minutes per side.
  3. Season: add garlic powder, smoked paprika, black pepper, modest salt, and 2 tbsp lime juice (measured).
  4. Beans: warm drained/rinsed black beans (15 oz; use 1 1/2 cups) in skillet with 1/4 cup broth 2–3 minutes.
  5. Finish: fold in cauliflower rice; cook 2 minutes to heat through. Serve per Notes.

Notes

Fast shrimp dinner with bright lime and high volume from cauliflower rice. Portions (per person): Frank: 7 oz shrimp/beans + 1 1/2 cups cauliflower rice + (beans included) Susan: 5 oz protein + 1 cup veg + 3/4 cup Elise: 5 oz protein + 1 cup veg + 3/4 cup Tips (recipe-specific): Pat shrimp dry so it sears instead of steams. Drain cauli rice well to avoid watery leftovers.
