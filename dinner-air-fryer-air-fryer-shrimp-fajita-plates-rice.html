Air Fryer Shrimp Fajita Plates + Rice

Nutrition (approx)

Frank: 650 kcal • P 55g • C 70g • F 16g • Sat 3g • Na 700mg • Fiber 10g
Susan/Elise (each): 488 kcal • P 41g • C 52g • F 12g • Sat 2g • Na 525mg • Fiber 8g

Ingredients (purchase-clean)

  • 1.50 lb shrimp
  • 1 bag frozen fajita-style pepper blend
  • 1 cup dry brown rice
  • 3 each whole-wheat tortillas
  • 1 each lime
  • 1 jar salsa
  • kosher salt
  • black pepper
  • garlic powder
  • onion powder
  • smoked paprika
  • Italian seasoning
  • ground cumin
  • chili powder
  • red pepper flakes
  • extra-virgin olive oil
  • low-sodium soy sauce
  • hot sauce
  • Dijon mustard
  • balsamic vinegar
  • apple cider vinegar
  • cornstarch

Steps (Cosori dual-basket)

  1. Rice: cook 1 cup dry brown rice with 2 cups water 35–40 minutes; rest 5 minutes.
  2. Preheat Cosori to 400°F. Basket A: pepper blend. Basket B: shrimp.
  3. Peppers (Basket A): toss frozen fajita pepper blend with 2 tsp olive oil, 1 tsp chili powder, 1 tsp cumin, 1 tsp garlic powder, 1/2 tsp kosher salt, and black pepper. Air fry 12–14 minutes at 400°F; shake at reminder to drive off moisture.
  4. Shrimp (Basket B): toss shrimp with 2 tsp olive oil, 1 tsp chili powder, 1 tsp cumin, 1 tsp garlic powder, 1/2 tsp kosher salt, and black pepper. Air fry 6–8 minutes at 400°F; shake once; shrimp should be pink.
  5. Tortillas: warm on skillet or air fry 2 minutes at 350°F.
  6. Assemble: tortillas + peppers + shrimp + 2 tbsp salsa per taco (measured). Serve with rice per Notes; squeeze lime over top.
  7. Serve per Notes.

Notes

Fajita night with air-fryer char and salsa + lime for moisture and flavor. Portions (per person): Frank: 3 tacos + 1 cup cooked rice Susan: 3 tacos + 3/4 cup cooked rice Elise: 3 tacos + 3/4 cup cooked rice Tips (recipe-specific): Air-fry peppers first so they actually char instead of steam. Salsa is the built-in sauce—measure it, but use it.
