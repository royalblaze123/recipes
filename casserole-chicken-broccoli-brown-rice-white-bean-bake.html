Chicken Broccoli Brown Rice & White Bean Bake

Prep: 20 min   Cook: 45 min   Total: 65 min

Ingredients

  • 14 oz chicken breast
  • 1 bag frozen broccoli florets
  • 0.5 can no-salt-added cannellini beans
  • 0.5 cup dry brown rice
  • 0.5 carton low-sodium chicken broth
  • 4 oz reduced-fat cheddar cheese
  • 1 bag spinach
  • olive oil
  • garlic powder
  • onion powder
  • black pepper
  • salt substitute
  • Italian seasoning

Hard rules enforced: Dinner calories (Frank 600–650), sat fat ≤8g, sodium ≤700mg, fiber ≥10g, added sugar ≤6g. Quantities appear before items; fresh meat/poultry/fish use weights. Condiments/spices/seasonings have no measurements in Ingredients; all measurements are in Steps. No olives, no squash, no Brussels sprouts, no zucchini, no yogurt in dinners.

Step-by-step Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 375°F. Lightly coat a 9×13 baking dish with 1 tsp olive oil.
  2. Brown rice: cook 1/2 cup dry brown rice with water per package directions (water referenced in Steps only). (Yields ~1 1/2 cups cooked.)
  3. Chicken: dice chicken breast into 1-inch pieces. Season with garlic powder, onion powder, black pepper, Italian seasoning, and salt substitute. Sauté in a skillet with 2 tsp olive oil over medium-high 6–8 minutes until cooked through (165°F).
  4. Broccoli: microwave 1 bag frozen broccoli florets 4–5 minutes until hot. Measure 3 cups for the bake; reseal and freeze the rest.
  5. Beans: drain and rinse 1/2 can cannellini beans.
  6. In the baking dish, mix cooked rice + chicken + broccoli + beans + 1 cup broth (measure from the carton) + spinach. Stir well.
  7. Sprinkle 4 oz reduced-fat cheddar evenly over the top.
  8. Bake 25 minutes covered (foil), then 10–12 minutes uncovered until bubbling and lightly browned. Rest 5 minutes before serving.
  9. Portion: scoop Frank’s serving as ~1/2 the pan; Susan and Elise each get ~1/4.
