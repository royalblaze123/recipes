Warm Lunch Skillet: Turkey, Mushrooms, and Cauliflower Rice

Prep: 16 min   Cook: 12 min   Total: 28 min

Ingredients

  • 1.0 lb lean ground turkey 93%
  • 1 bag frozen cauliflower rice
  • 1 8 oz package mushrooms
  • 1 bag spinach
  • 1 lemon
  • olive oil
  • Italian seasoning
  • garlic powder
  • black pepper
  • salt substitute
  • vinegar

Rules enforced: Clean AnyList Ingredients (quantities before items; fresh meat/poultry/fish use weights; packaged items use fractional containers when partial). Condiments/spices/seasonings have no measurements in Ingredients; all measurements are in Steps. No olives, no squash, no Brussels sprouts, no zucchini, no tofu/tempeh. Lunch validations: Frank calories 500–550, sat fat ≤6g, sodium ≤650mg, fiber ≥8g, added sugar ≤6g.

Step-by-step Instructions

  1. Cauliflower rice: microwave 1 bag cauliflower rice 5–6 minutes. Measure 3 cups cooked; drain/pat dry; refreeze remainder.
  2. Turkey: brown 1 lb lean ground turkey with 2 tsp olive oil 7–9 minutes. Season with 2 tsp Italian seasoning + 1 tsp garlic powder + 1/2 tsp black pepper + 1/2 tsp salt substitute.
  3. Mushrooms: slice 1 package mushrooms; add and cook 5 minutes until browned.
  4. Spinach: stir in 1 bag spinach; cook 1–2 minutes until wilted.
  5. Stir in cauliflower rice; heat 2 minutes.
  6. Finish: add 2 tsp lemon juice per plate and 1 tsp vinegar total (optional) for brightness.
  7. Portion: Frank largest skillet portion; Susan/Elise ~75%.

An Italian-style warm lunch skillet: turkey + mushrooms + spinach over cauliflower rice—high protein, low sodium, reheats clean.
