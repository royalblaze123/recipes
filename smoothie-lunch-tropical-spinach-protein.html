Tropical Spinach Protein Lunch Smoothie

NOTES: Designed as a full lunch replacement smoothie for high protein, controlled smart carbs, and plenty of fiber, using unsweetened almond milk, Greek yogurt, and whey protein. Built to support A1c, cholesterol, weight, and BP goals while staying portable for work.

PORTION SIZES (serves 3): Blend one full batch, then divide into three portions. Frank receives the largest portion (≈1/3 of the total volume); Susan and Elise receive slightly smaller portions (≈1/4 of the total volume each). Frank = 1.0×; Susan = 0.75×; Elise = 0.75× portions.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups unsweetened almond milk
  • 1 cup plain 2% Greek yogurt
  • 2 scoops vanilla whey protein powder
  • 1 1/2 cups frozen mixed fruit
  • 1/3 cup dry old-fashioned oats
  • 1 tbsp ground flaxseed
  • Ice cubes as needed

Step-by-Step Instructions

  1. To a high-powered blender, add 2 cups unsweetened almond milk, 1 cup plain 2% Greek yogurt, 2 scoops vanilla whey protein powder, 1 1/2 cups frozen mixed fruit, 1/3 cup dry old-fashioned oats, 1 tbsp ground flaxseed, and Ice cubes as needed. Use the unsweetened almond milk and Greek yogurt on the bottom so the blades catch easily.
  2. Blend on low speed at first, then increase to high and blend for about 45–60 seconds until everything is completely smooth and no chunks remain.
  3. Taste and adjust the texture with a small splash of extra almond milk for a thinner smoothie or a few more ice cubes for a thicker texture. Keep the smoothie unsweetened by default; only add a small amount of non-nutritive sweetener if truly needed.
  4. Pour the smoothie into three bottles or tumblers. Give Frank the largest portion for his higher calorie and protein target, and divide the remaining smoothie between Susan and Elise. Keep refrigerated and shake well before drinking.

Nutrition (approximate per person)

Frank (largest portion): ~420–500 kcal, 40–50 g protein, 40–55 g carbs, 10–16 g fat
Susan (medium portion): ~320–380 kcal, 30–38 g protein, 30–42 g carbs, 8–12 g fat
Elise (medium portion): ~320–380 kcal, 30–38 g protein, 30–42 g carbs, 8–12 g fat
