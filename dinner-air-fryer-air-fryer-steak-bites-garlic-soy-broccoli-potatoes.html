Air Fryer Steak Bites + Garlic-Soy Broccoli + Potatoes

Nutrition (approx)

Frank: 650 kcal • P 55g • C 55g • F 22g • Sat 6g • Na 700mg • Fiber 10g
Susan/Elise (each): 488 kcal • P 41g • C 41g • F 16g • Sat 4g • Na 525mg • Fiber 8g

Ingredients (purchase-clean)

  • 1.25 lb lean sirloin steak
  • 1.50 lb baby potatoes
  • 1 bag frozen broccoli florets
  • kosher salt
  • black pepper
  • garlic powder
  • onion powder
  • smoked paprika
  • Italian seasoning
  • ground cumin
  • chili powder
  • red pepper flakes
  • extra-virgin olive oil
  • low-sodium soy sauce
  • hot sauce
  • Dijon mustard
  • balsamic vinegar
  • apple cider vinegar
  • cornstarch

Steps (Cosori dual-basket)

  1. Preheat Cosori to 400°F. Basket A: potatoes. Basket B: steak bites later. Use Sync Finish.
  2. Potatoes (Basket A): halve potatoes; toss with 2 tsp olive oil, 1 tsp garlic powder, 1 tsp smoked paprika, 1/2 tsp kosher salt, and 1/2 tsp black pepper. Air fry 18–22 minutes at 400°F with Shake Reminder.
  3. Steak: cut into 1-inch bites. Toss with 2 tsp low-sodium soy sauce, 2 tsp olive oil, 1 tsp garlic powder, and 1/2 tsp black pepper.
  4. Steak bites (Basket B): add when potatoes have ~8 minutes left. Air fry 6–8 minutes at 400°F, shaking once; target medium.
  5. Broccoli: microwave broccoli 4–5 minutes; drain well. Toss with 2 tsp low-sodium soy sauce, 2 tsp olive oil, 1 tsp garlic powder, and black pepper. Crisp in Basket A for last 5–6 minutes if space; otherwise roast 425°F for 10–12 minutes.
  6. Serve per Notes.

Notes

Steak-and-potatoes comfort with a garlic-soy broccoli kicker (still controlled). Portions (per person): Frank: 6 oz steak bites + 1 cup potatoes + 1 cup broccoli Susan: 5 oz protein + 1 cup potatoes + 3/4 cup veg Elise: 5 oz protein + 1 cup potatoes + 3/4 cup veg Tips (recipe-specific): Add steak late so it stays juicy. Drain broccoli well so it crisps and takes the soy glaze.
