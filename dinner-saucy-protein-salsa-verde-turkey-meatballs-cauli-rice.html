Salsa Verde Turkey Meatballs + Cauli Rice

Nutrition (approx)

Frank: 625 kcal • P 60g • C 40g • F 18g • Sat 6g • Na 680mg • Fiber 10g
Susan/Elise (each): 469 kcal • P 45g • C 30g • F 14g • Sat 4g • Na 510mg • Fiber 8g

Ingredients (purchase-clean)

  • 1.75 lb 93% lean ground turkey
  • 1 package whole-wheat breadcrumbs
  • 2 each eggs
  • 1 jar salsa verde
  • 2 bags frozen cauliflower rice
  • 1 bag frozen fajita-style pepper blend
  • 1 each lime
  • kosher salt
  • black pepper
  • garlic powder
  • onion powder
  • Italian seasoning
  • smoked paprika
  • chili powder
  • ground cumin
  • crushed red pepper
  • extra-virgin olive oil
  • oregano

Steps

  1. Oven: heat to 425°F. Line a sheet pan and lightly oil.
  2. Meatballs: mix turkey with 1/2 cup breadcrumbs, 2 eggs, 2 tsp cumin, 2 tsp garlic powder, 1 tsp oregano, 1/2 tsp kosher salt, 1/2 tsp black pepper.
  3. Form 18–20 meatballs; bake 12–14 minutes to 165°F.
  4. Peppers: sauté frozen fajita pepper blend in a skillet with 2 tsp olive oil 6–8 minutes until browned.
  5. Cauli rice: cook 2 bags cauliflower rice per package directions. Dry in a skillet 3 minutes.
  6. Sauce: warm 1 1/2 cups salsa verde. Add meatballs and simmer 2–3 minutes.
  7. Finish cauli rice with 2 tsp lime juice and black pepper.
  8. Serve: portion meatballs + cauli rice + peppers per Notes.

Notes

Green-chile comfort meatballs with a big-volume, low-carb base. Portions (per person): Frank: 8–9 meatballs + 1 1/2 cups cauli rice + 1/2 cup peppers Susan: 6–7 meatballs + 1 1/4 cups cauli rice + 1/3 cup peppers Elise: 6–7 meatballs + 1 1/4 cups cauli rice + 1/3 cup peppers Tips (recipe-specific): Dry cauliflower rice so it doesn’t dilute salsa verde. Warm salsa verde with meatballs for 2–3 minutes so it tastes cooked-in.
