Protein+ Rotini Shrimp Marinara

Nutrition (approx)

Frank: 689 kcal • P 66g • C 71g • F 18g
Susan/Elise (each): 517 kcal • P 50g • C 53g • F 14g

Ingredients (purchase-clean)

  • 1/3 box Barilla Protein+ rotini
  • 1.25 lb raw shrimp
  • 0.62 jar no-sugar-added marinara sauce
  • 4 tsp extra-virgin olive oil
  • 2 tbsp grated parmesan
  • 8 oz fresh mushrooms
  • 2 tsp garlic powder
  • 2 tsp Italian seasoning
  • 1 tsp black pepper
  • 1 tsp kosher salt

Steps

  1. Bring a large pot of water to a boil for the Barilla Protein+ rotini. (Use enough water to fully cover pasta; do not add water to Ingredients.)
  2. Pat shrimp dry. Season with 1 tsp kosher salt and 1 tsp black pepper (from your listed spices). Sauté in a hot nonstick skillet 2–3 minutes, flipping once, just until opaque. Transfer to a plate.
  3. Chop the mushrooms. Cook 6–8 minutes until moisture cooks off and mushrooms brown.
  4. Jar size note: assume a standard 24 oz jar marinara. Measure out what you need for this recipe; refrigerate the remainder.
  5. Add marinara to the skillet and simmer 5–7 minutes until hot and slightly thickened.
  6. Cook Barilla Protein+ rotini to al dente per box time. Reserve 1 cup pasta water, then drain.
  7. Toss pasta into the sauce/skillet. Add reserved pasta water 1–2 tbsp at a time until the sauce clings well.
  8. Fold shrimp back in at the end for 30–60 seconds to re-warm (avoid overcooking).
  9. Portion into bowls using the Portions guidance in Notes.

Notes

Shrimp marinara rotini with parmesan finish. Built on Barilla Protein+ for a higher-protein pasta plate with close-approx nutrition. Portions (per person): Frank: 2 oz dry Barilla Protein+ rotini + 1/3 of the skillet/sauce Susan: 1.5 oz dry Barilla Protein+ rotini + 1/4 of the skillet/sauce Elise: 1.5 oz dry Barilla Protein+ rotini + 1/4 of the skillet/sauce
