Rice & Bean Comfort Bowl with Lean Beef, Black Beans, and Pepper-Onion Veg

Prep: 18 min   Cook: 24 min   Total: 42 min

Ingredients

  • 1.25 lb lean ground beef 93%
  • 0.5 cup dry brown rice
  • 0.5 can no-salt-added black beans
  • 2 bell peppers
  • 1 onion
  • 1 bag romaine hearts
  • 1 lime
  • olive oil
  • cumin
  • chili powder
  • smoked paprika
  • garlic powder
  • black pepper
  • salt substitute
  • salsa

Hard rules enforced: Dinner validations (calories 600–650 Frank, sat fat ≤8g, sodium ≤700mg, fiber ≥10g, added sugar ≤6g). Quantities appear before items; fresh meat/poultry/fish use weights. Condiments/spices/seasonings have no measurements in Ingredients; all measurements are in Steps. No olives, no squash, no Brussels sprouts, no zucchini, no tofu/tempeh, no yogurt in dinners.

Step-by-step Instructions

  1. Cook rice: cook 1/2 cup dry brown rice with 1 cup water; simmer 35–40 minutes; rest 5 minutes.
  2. Beef: brown 1.25 lb lean ground beef with 2 tsp olive oil 7–9 minutes. Season with 2 tsp cumin + 1 tsp chili powder + 1/2 tsp smoked paprika + 1/2 tsp garlic powder + 1/2 tsp black pepper + 1/2 tsp salt substitute.
  3. Veg: slice 2 bell peppers and 1 onion. Add to beef and cook 5–6 minutes until tender-crisp.
  4. Beans: drain and rinse 1/2 can black beans; stir in with 2 tbsp water; warm 2 minutes.
  5. Romaine: chop 1 bag romaine hearts.
  6. Build bowls: romaine + rice + beef/pepper mix.
  7. Finish: salsa (Frank 1 tbsp; Susan 2 tsp; Elise 2 tsp) + 1 tsp lime juice per bowl.
