Air Fryer Beef Spinach-Provolone Pinwheels with Sweet Potato Coins and Broccoli

Prep: 22 min   Cook: 20 min   Total: 42 min

Ingredients

  • 1.5 lb top sirloin steak
  • 6 slices reduced-fat provolone
  • 1 bag spinach
  • 1 bag sweet potatoes
  • 1 bag frozen broccoli florets
  • 1 lemon
  • olive oil
  • Dijon mustard
  • garlic powder
  • onion powder
  • black pepper
  • salt substitute

RBM rules: quantities appear before items; fresh proteins use weight. Condiments/spices/seasonings are listed name-only; all measurements are in Steps. Water is referenced in Steps only.

Step-by-step Instructions

  1. Prep sweet potatoes: slice into 1/2-inch coins. Toss with 10 tsp olive oil + 2 tsp salt substitute + 1 tsp garlic powder + 1 tsp black pepper.
  2. Air fry sweet potato coins (Cosori): preheat 400°F for 3 minutes. Cook 14–18 minutes, shaking at reminders, until browned and tender. Keep warm.
  3. Prep broccoli: toss frozen broccoli with 6 tsp olive oil + 2 tsp salt substitute + 1 tsp black pepper. Air fry 390°F for 10–12 minutes, shaking once, until browned at edges.
  4. Prep steak: butterfly sirloin into a thin rectangle or pound gently to ~1/4-inch. Spread 6 tsp Dijon over the surface. Season with 1 tsp onion powder + 1 tsp garlic powder + 1 tsp black pepper + 2 tsp salt substitute.
  5. Layer spinach evenly. Lay provolone slices across. Roll tightly into a log and slice into 6–8 pinwheels. Secure each with toothpicks.
  6. Air fry pinwheels: 390°F for 10–12 minutes, flipping once, until beef reaches 130–135°F for medium and cheese is melted. Rest 5 minutes; remove toothpicks.
  7. Finish plates with lemon wedges.
  8. Portion: Frank gets 2 large pinwheels (~6 oz cooked beef) + ~1 1/4 cups sweet potatoes + ~1 3/4 cups broccoli. Susan and Elise each get ~1–2 pinwheels (~4.5 oz beef) + ~1 cup sweet potatoes + ~1 1/2 cups broccoli.

Notes
