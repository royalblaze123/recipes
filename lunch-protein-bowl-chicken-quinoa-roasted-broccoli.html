Protein Bowl: Lemon-Herb Chicken with Quinoa and Roasted Broccoli

Prep: 16 min   Cook: 18 min   Total: 34 min

Ingredients

  • 1.25 lb chicken breast
  • 0.5 cup dry quinoa
  • 1 bag frozen broccoli florets
  • 1 lemon
  • 1 pint cherry tomatoes
  • olive oil
  • Italian seasoning
  • garlic powder
  • black pepper
  • salt substitute
  • vinegar

Rules enforced: Clean AnyList Ingredients (quantities before items; fresh meat/poultry/fish use weights; packaged items use fractional containers when partial). Condiments/spices/seasonings have no measurements in Ingredients; all measurements are in Steps. No olives, no squash, no Brussels sprouts, no zucchini, no tofu/tempeh. Lunch validations: Frank calories 500–550, sat fat ≤6g, sodium ≤650mg, fiber ≥8g, added sugar ≤6g.

Step-by-step Instructions

  1. Cook quinoa: rinse 1/2 cup dry quinoa. Simmer with 1 cup water 15 minutes; rest 5 minutes.
  2. Broccoli: microwave frozen broccoli 3–4 minutes to thaw; pat dry. Measure 4 cups; refreeze remainder.
  3. Roast broccoli: toss measured broccoli with 1 tsp olive oil + 1/4 tsp black pepper + 1/4 tsp salt substitute; roast 425°F for 12 minutes.
  4. Chicken: slice 1.25 lb chicken breast into thin cutlets. Season with 2 tsp Italian seasoning + 1 tsp garlic powder + 1/2 tsp black pepper + 1/2 tsp salt substitute.
  5. Cook chicken in a skillet with 2 tsp olive oil 6–8 minutes total until 165°F; rest 3 minutes, then slice.
  6. Quick tomato topping: halve 1 pint cherry tomatoes and toss with 2 tsp vinegar + 2 tsp lemon juice + 1/4 tsp black pepper.
  7. Build bowls: quinoa + broccoli + chicken + tomato topping. Portion: Frank gets the largest quinoa/chicken share; Susan/Elise ~75%.
