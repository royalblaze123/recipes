Turkey Meatball Marinara Bake

Nutrition (approx)

Frank: 650 kcal • P 60g • C 58g • F 18g • Sat 7g • Na 690mg • Fiber 12g
Susan/Elise (each): 488 kcal • P 45g • C 44g • F 14g • Sat 5g • Na 518mg • Fiber 9g

Ingredients (purchase-clean)

  • 1.75 lb 93% lean ground turkey
  • 1 package whole-wheat breadcrumbs
  • 2 each eggs
  • 1 jar marinara sauce
  • 1 box Barilla Protein+ pasta
  • 6 oz reduced-fat shredded mozzarella cheese
  • kosher salt
  • black pepper
  • garlic powder
  • onion powder
  • smoked paprika
  • chili powder
  • ground cumin
  • Italian seasoning
  • extra-virgin olive oil

Steps

  1. Oven: heat to 425°F. Line a sheet pan with foil and lightly oil.
  2. Meatballs: in a bowl, combine turkey with 1/2 cup breadcrumbs, 2 eggs, 2 tsp garlic powder, 1 tsp onion powder, 1 tsp Italian seasoning, 1/2 tsp kosher salt, 1/2 tsp black pepper. Mix gently.
  3. Form 18–20 meatballs. Place on sheet pan. Bake 12–14 minutes until 165°F.
  4. Pasta: cook Barilla Protein+ pasta al dente; drain.
  5. Sauce: warm 2 cups marinara in a pot. Add baked meatballs and simmer 3–4 minutes.
  6. Assemble: toss pasta with meatballs + sauce. Pour into a baking dish. Top with 6 oz reduced-fat mozzarella.
  7. Bake: 10–12 minutes until cheese melts and edges bubble. Rest 5 minutes.
  8. Portion per Notes.

Notes

Meatball comfort baked fast on a sheet pan, finished as a cheesy bake. Portions (per person): Frank: 1/3 of bake Susan: 1/4 of bake Elise: 1/4 of bake Tips (recipe-specific): Bake meatballs first so they stay firm (not mushy) in the casserole. Reserve a little marinara for serving if it seems dry after reheating.
