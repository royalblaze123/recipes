Mediterranean Tilapia Bowl with White Beans and Brown Rice

Prep: 18 min   Cook: 22 min   Total: 40 min

Ingredients

  • 1.5 lb tilapia fillets
  • 0.5 cup dry brown rice
  • 0.5 can no-salt-added cannellini beans
  • 1 pint cherry tomatoes
  • 1 cucumber
  • 1 lemon
  • olive oil
  • Italian seasoning
  • garlic powder
  • black pepper
  • salt substitute
  • vinegar

Hard rules enforced: Dinner validations (calories 600–650 Frank, sat fat ≤8g, sodium ≤700mg, fiber ≥10g, added sugar ≤6g). Quantities appear before items; fresh meat/poultry/fish use weights. Condiments/spices/seasonings have no measurements in Ingredients; all measurements are in Steps. No olives, no squash, no Brussels sprouts, no zucchini, no tofu/tempeh, no yogurt in dinners.

Step-by-step Instructions

  1. Cook rice: cook 1/2 cup dry brown rice with 1 cup water; simmer 35–40 minutes; rest 5 minutes.
  2. Beans: drain and rinse 1/2 can cannellini beans; toss with 1 tsp olive oil + 1 tsp lemon juice + 1/4 tsp Italian seasoning + 1/4 tsp black pepper + 1/4 tsp salt substitute.
  3. Tilapia: pat dry 1.5 lb tilapia. Season with 2 tsp Italian seasoning + 1 tsp garlic powder + 1/4 tsp black pepper + 1/2 tsp salt substitute.
  4. Bake tilapia at 425°F for 10–12 minutes until flaky.
  5. Salad topping: dice 1 cucumber and halve 1 pint cherry tomatoes; toss with 2 tsp vinegar + 1 tsp olive oil + 1 tsp lemon juice + 1/4 tsp black pepper + 1/4 tsp salt substitute.
  6. Build bowls: rice + beans + tilapia + cucumber/tomato topping.
  7. Portion: Frank largest rice/fish share; Susan/Elise ~75%.
