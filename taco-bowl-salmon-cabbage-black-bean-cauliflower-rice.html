Salmon Taco Bowl with Cabbage, Black Beans, and Cauliflower Rice

Prep: 18 min   Cook: 18 min   Total: 36 min

Ingredients

  • 1.5 lb salmon fillets
  • 0.5 can no-salt-added black beans
  • 1 bag frozen cauliflower rice
  • 0.5 bag shredded cabbage
  • 1 lime
  • 1 pint cherry tomatoes
  • 1 bag romaine hearts
  • olive oil
  • cumin
  • smoked paprika
  • black pepper
  • salt substitute
  • salsa
  • vinegar

Hard rules enforced: Dinner validations (calories 600–650 Frank, sat fat ≤8g, sodium ≤700mg, fiber ≥10g, added sugar ≤6g). Quantities appear before items; fresh meat/poultry/fish use weights. Condiments/spices/seasonings have no measurements in Ingredients; all measurements are in Steps. No olives, no squash, no Brussels sprouts, no zucchini, no tofu/tempeh, no yogurt in dinners.

Step-by-step Instructions

  1. Cauliflower rice: microwave 1 bag cauliflower rice 5–6 minutes. Measure 3 cups cooked for dinner; refreeze remainder. Drain/pat dry if watery.
  2. Salmon: season with cumin, smoked paprika, black pepper, and salt substitute. Bake 425°F for 10–12 minutes (or air fry 400°F for 8–10) until flaky.
  3. Beans: drain/rinse 1/2 can black beans; warm 1–2 minutes.
  4. Cabbage quick slaw: toss cabbage with 2 tsp lime juice + 1 tsp vinegar + 1 tsp olive oil + black pepper + salt substitute.
  5. Build bowls: romaine + cauliflower rice + beans + tomatoes + flaked salmon + slaw.
  6. Top with measured salsa (1 tbsp Frank; 2 tsp Susan; 2 tsp Elise).
  7. Portion: Frank largest salmon share; Susan/Elise ~75%.

Omega-3 taco bowl that stays low-carb-ish using cauliflower rice and a bright cabbage slaw.
