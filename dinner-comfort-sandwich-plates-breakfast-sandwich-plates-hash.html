Breakfast Sandwich Plates + Hash

Nutrition (approx)

Frank: 640 kcal • P 55g • C 60g • F 16g • Sat 6g • Na 700mg • Fiber 10g
Susan/Elise (each): 480 kcal • P 41g • C 45g • F 12g • Sat 4g • Na 525mg • Fiber 8g

Ingredients (purchase-clean)

  • 12 slices whole-wheat sandwich bread
  • 6 each eggs
  • 12 slices deli turkey
  • 1.50 lb baby potatoes
  • 1 bag baby spinach
  • kosher salt
  • black pepper
  • garlic powder
  • onion powder
  • Italian seasoning
  • smoked paprika
  • extra-virgin olive oil
  • Dijon mustard
  • hot sauce
  • rice vinegar

Steps

  1. Oven: heat to 425°F. Dice baby potatoes; roast with 2 tsp olive oil, smoked paprika, modest kosher salt, black pepper 22–26 minutes, stirring once.
  2. Eggs: scramble 6 eggs in a nonstick pan with black pepper.
  3. Spinach: sauté spinach 60–90 seconds.
  4. Build 3 breakfast sandwiches: turkey + scrambled egg portion + spinach on toasted bread.
  5. Serve with hash per Notes.

Notes

Diner breakfast sandwich comfort, but it’s a controlled plate. Portions (per person): Frank: 1 sandwich + 1 cup hash Susan: 1 sandwich + 3/4 cup hash Elise: 1 sandwich + 3/4 cup hash Tips (recipe-specific): Roast hash until browned—color is the flavor. Use spinach in the sandwich so it’s still a ‘full plate’.
