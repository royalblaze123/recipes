Chicken Veggie Farro Bowl

NOTES: Designed as a portable grab-and-go lunch using lean protein, high-fiber vegetables, and controlled smart carbs. Use low-sodium ingredients and avoid added sugars where possible. PORTION SIZES: Frank: largest portion (~1/3 of the total volume); Susan: medium portion (~1/4 of the total volume); Elise: medium portion (~1/4 of the total volume) (Frank = 1.0×, Susan/Elise ≈0.75× portions).

Ingredients

  • 3/4 cup dry farro, rinsed
  • 2 cups low-sodium vegetable or chicken broth
  • 12 oz cooked boneless, skinless chicken breast, chopped
  • 1 1/2 cups roasted or steamed broccoli florets
  • 1 cup shredded carrots
  • 1 red bell pepper, sliced
  • 2 tbsp extra-virgin olive oil
  • 2 tbsp balsamic vinegar (no added sugar)
  • 1 tsp dried Italian seasoning
  • Freshly ground black pepper, to taste

Step-by-Step Instructions

  1. Cook farro: Combine rinsed farro and broth in a saucepan. Bring to a boil, then reduce heat, cover, and simmer 20–25 minutes until tender. Drain excess liquid and cool slightly.
  2. In a large bowl, combine cooked farro, chopped chicken, broccoli, shredded carrots, and sliced bell pepper.
  3. In a small bowl, whisk together olive oil, balsamic vinegar, Italian seasoning, and black pepper.
  4. Pour dressing over the bowl and toss until everything is evenly coated.
  5. Divide into three containers for grab-and-go lunches, giving Frank the largest portion.
  6. Refrigerate up to 3 days. Eat chilled or gently rewarmed at home.

Nutrition per Person

  • Frank: ~590 kcal, ~50 g protein, ~55 g carbs, ~17 g fat.
  • Susan: ~445 kcal, ~38 g protein, ~41 g carbs, ~13 g fat.
  • Elise: ~445 kcal, ~38 g protein, ~41 g carbs, ~13 g fat.
