BBQ Chicken Onion Pizza (No-Sugar-Added Sauce)

NOTES: Built as a full dinner using ready-made whole-grain or thin crust, lean protein, plenty of vegetables, and controlled cheese to support A1c, cholesterol, and blood pressure goals. Use low-sodium sauce and minimal added salt. PORTION SIZES: Frank: ~40% of the pizza; Susan: ~30% of the pizza; Elise: ~30% of the pizza (Frank = 1.0×, Susan/Elise = 0.75× portions).

Ingredients

  • 1 ready-made 12-inch thin whole-wheat pizza crust
  • 1/4 cup no-sugar-added BBQ sauce (low sodium if possible)
  • 1/4 cup no-salt-added tomato sauce
  • 8 oz cooked boneless, skinless chicken breast, shredded
  • 1/4 red onion, very thinly sliced
  • 1/2 cup shredded reduced-fat mozzarella cheese
  • 2 tbsp grated Parmesan cheese
  • 2 tbsp chopped fresh cilantro (optional)
  • Freshly ground black pepper, to taste

Step-by-Step Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 425°F. Place the ready-made crust on a pizza pan or parchment-lined baking sheet.
  2. In a small bowl, whisk together no-sugar-added BBQ sauce and tomato sauce.
  3. Spread the BBQ-tomato sauce evenly over the crust, leaving a small border.
  4. Scatter shredded chicken evenly over the sauce.
  5. Top with thinly sliced red onion.
  6. Sprinkle shredded mozzarella and grated Parmesan over the toppings, then add a few grinds of black pepper.
  7. Bake 12–15 minutes, or until the crust is crisp and the cheese is melted and lightly browned.
  8. If using cilantro, sprinkle it over the pizza right after baking.
  9. Let pizza cool 3–5 minutes, then slice and portion for Frank (~40%) and Susan/Elise (~30% each).

Nutrition per Person

  • Frank: ~600 kcal, ~55 g protein, ~53 g carbs, ~16 g fat.
  • Susan: ~450 kcal, ~41 g protein, ~40 g carbs, ~12 g fat.
  • Elise: ~450 kcal, ~41 g protein, ~40 g carbs, ~12 g fat.
