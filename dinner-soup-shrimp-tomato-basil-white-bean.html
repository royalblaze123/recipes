Dinner Soup Shrimp Tomato Basil White Bean

Chefy shrimp tomato basil soup with white beans—fast, bright, and protein-forward with no heavy cream.

Servings

3 servings.

Times

  • Prep time: 15 minutes
  • Cook time: 20 minutes
  • Total time: 35 minutes

Ingredients

Main Dish

  • 2 lb shrimp
  • 1 carton low sodium chicken broth
  • 1 can crushed tomatoes
  • 1 can cannellini beans
  • 1 each yellow onion
  • 1 bunch celery
  • 1 lb bag carrots
  • 6 cloves garlic
  • 1 bunch basil
  • 1 bottle extra virgin olive oil
  • 1 jar dried oregano
  • 1 jar black pepper
  • 1 jar parmesan cheese
    • 

    Preparation Steps

    1. Prep veg: dice 1/2 the onion, slice 2 celery stalks, slice 2 carrots, and mince 3 garlic cloves.
    2. Start base: heat a large pot over medium heat. Add 2 tsp olive oil, then add onion, celery, carrots, and garlic. Cook 6 minutes until softened.
    3. Add tomatoes: add the can of crushed tomatoes and stir. Simmer 3 minutes to deepen flavor.
    4. Add broth: add the carton of low sodium chicken broth plus 2 cups water. Bring to a boil, then reduce to a simmer.
    5. Beans: drain and rinse cannellini beans. Add beans and simmer 5 minutes.
    6. Shrimp: add shrimp and simmer 3–4 minutes until shrimp are opaque and cooked through.
    7. Basil: chop basil (use about 1/4 cup; refrigerate remaining basil) and stir into the pot for 30 seconds.
    8. Serve: sprinkle a measured 1 tbsp parmesan total across all bowls.

      Notes

      Portions (per person):


      Frank – About 3 1/2 cups soup.


      Susan – About 2 2/3 cups soup.


      Elise – About 2 2/3 cups soup.


      Tips (recipe-specific):


      Add shrimp at the very end so it stays tender, not rubbery.


      Simmer crushed tomatoes briefly before broth for a deeper, more chefy flavor.


      If you want it thicker, mash a few spoonfuls of beans and stir back in.
