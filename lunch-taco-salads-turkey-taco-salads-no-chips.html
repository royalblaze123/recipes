Turkey Taco Salads (No Chips)

Nutrition (approx)

Frank: 540 kcal • P 50g • C 44g • F 18g
Susan/Elise (each): 405 kcal • P 38g • C 33g • F 14g

Ingredients (purchase-clean)

  • 1.50 lb 93% lean ground turkey
  • 1 bag shredded lettuce
  • 1 each avocado
  • 1/2 jar salsa
  • 1 each cucumber
  • 1 pint cherry tomatoes
  • 1 each lime
  • 4 tsp extra-virgin olive oil
  • 2 tsp chili powder
  • 2 tsp cumin
  • 2 tsp garlic powder
  • 1 tsp smoked paprika
  • 1 tsp black pepper
  • 1 tsp kosher salt

Steps

  1. Turkey: cook ground turkey in a skillet with 2 tsp olive oil 7–9 minutes until browned. Stir in spices for 60 seconds. Cool 5 minutes.
  2. Dressing: whisk 1/2 cup salsa with 2 tsp lime juice and 2 tsp olive oil.
  3. Base: divide shredded lettuce (or spring mix) into three bowls.
  4. Protein: add seasoned turkey to each bowl.
  5. Add-ins: add tomatoes and cucumber if listed.
  6. Top: spoon salsa over the salad and add measured diced avocado. Portion per Notes.
  7. Avocado: halve, pit, and dice. Use about 1/2 avocado total; refrigerate remaining avocado with the pit to slow browning.

Notes

Classic turkey taco salad bowls (no chips) with salsa + avocado finish. Portions (per person): Frank: 1 large taco salad bowl + 1/3 of the avocado + salsa Susan: 1 medium taco salad bowl + 1/4 of the avocado + salsa Elise: 1 medium taco salad bowl + 1/4 of the avocado + salsa
