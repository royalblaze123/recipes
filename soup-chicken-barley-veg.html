Chicken Barley Vegetable Soup (Stovetop)

NOTES: Designed as a complete meal soup using lean protein, high-fiber vegetables, and controlled starch. Use low-sodium broth and avoid extra salt when possible to support blood pressure and kidney health. PORTION SIZES: Frank: largest portion (~2 cups); Susan: medium portion (~1.5 cups); Elise: medium portion (~1.5 cups).

Ingredients

  • 1 tbsp extra-virgin olive oil
  • 1 medium yellow onion, diced
  • 2 carrots, diced
  • 2 celery stalks, diced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 cup sliced mushrooms
  • 1/2 cup pearl barley, rinsed
  • 6 cups low-sodium chicken broth
  • 1 cup water
  • 1 lb boneless, skinless chicken breast
  • 1 tsp dried thyme
  • 1 tsp dried parsley
  • 1/2 tsp kosher salt (or to taste)
  • Freshly ground black pepper, to taste
  • 1 cup frozen peas (no added salt)

Step-by-Step Instructions

  1. Heat olive oil in a large soup pot over medium heat.
  2. Add diced onion, carrots, and celery and cook 5–6 minutes until vegetables start to soften.
  3. Stir in minced garlic and sliced mushrooms and cook 2–3 minutes until mushrooms soften.
  4. Add rinsed pearl barley and stir to coat in the oil and vegetable mixture.
  5. Pour in low-sodium chicken broth and water and stir.
  6. Place chicken breasts into the pot, submerging them in the liquid.
  7. Stir in dried thyme, dried parsley, salt, and black pepper.
  8. Bring to a gentle boil, then reduce heat to low, cover partially, and simmer 25–30 minutes until barley is tender and chicken is cooked through.
  9. Remove chicken to a cutting board and let cool slightly, then shred with two forks.
  10. Return shredded chicken to the pot and stir in frozen peas.
  11. Simmer another 5 minutes until peas are heated through.
  12. Taste and adjust seasoning with a small pinch more salt or pepper if needed.
  13. Remove from heat, let rest a few minutes, then ladle into bowls for Frank, Susan, and Elise.

Nutrition per Person

  • Frank: ~470 kcal, ~42 g protein, ~48 g carbs, ~9 g fat.
  • Susan: ~355 kcal, ~31 g protein, ~36 g carbs, ~7 g fat.
  • Elise: ~355 kcal, ~31 g protein, ~36 g carbs, ~7 g fat.
