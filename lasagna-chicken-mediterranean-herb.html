Mediterranean Herb Chicken Lasagna (Olive-Free)

Mediterranean-inspired chicken lasagna with herbs, tomatoes, and extra vegetables, without olives and with controlled cheese for heart health.

PORTION SIZES (3 servings total): Frank = 1.0× (largest square), Susan = 0.75×, Elise = 0.75×. Cut the baked lasagna into 4 equal squares, serve 2 squares to Frank and 1 square each to Susan and Elise.

Ingredients

  • 8 whole-wheat lasagna noodles (dry)
  • 12 oz cooked shredded chicken breast (skinless)
  • 1 small yellow onion, finely diced
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 red bell pepper, diced
  • 1 small carrot, grated
  • 2 cups no-salt-added crushed tomatoes
  • 1/2 cup low-sodium chicken broth
  • 1 tbsp extra-virgin olive oil
  • 1 tsp dried oregano
  • 1 tsp dried basil
  • 1/2 tsp dried thyme
  • 1/2 tsp black pepper
  • 1/4 tsp kosher salt (optional, to taste)
  • 1 cup part-skim ricotta cheese
  • 1 cup part-skim shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 2 tbsp grated reduced-fat Parmesan cheese
  • 1 tbsp lemon zest
  • 2 tbsp fresh parsley, chopped

Step-by-step Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C). Lightly coat a 9×9-inch baking dish with nonstick spray.
  2. Bring a large pot of water to a boil. Cook the whole-wheat lasagna noodles until just shy of al dente, about 7–8 minutes. Drain and lay flat on parchment to prevent sticking.
  3. Heat the olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat. Add the diced onion, red bell pepper, and grated carrot. Sauté 5–6 minutes until softened and lightly browned.
  4. Add the minced garlic and cook 30–60 seconds until fragrant.
  5. Stir in the dried oregano, dried basil, dried thyme, black pepper, and optional salt. Toast the herbs with the vegetables for 1–2 minutes.
  6. Pour in the crushed tomatoes and chicken broth. Stir well and bring to a gentle simmer. Cook 8–10 minutes until slightly thickened.
  7. Fold the shredded chicken and lemon zest into the tomato sauce and simmer 2 additional minutes. Remove from heat.
  8. Spread a thin layer of the chicken tomato sauce on the bottom of the prepared baking dish.
  9. Layer lasagna noodles over the sauce. Add dollops of ricotta, then more chicken tomato sauce.
  10. Repeat the layers (noodles, ricotta, sauce) until all components are used, finishing with sauce on top.
  11. Sprinkle the shredded mozzarella and grated Parmesan evenly over the top.
  12. Cover with foil and bake 25 minutes. Remove the foil and bake an additional 10–15 minutes until bubbling and lightly browned.
  13. Rest the lasagna for 10–15 minutes, then sprinkle with chopped fresh parsley.
  14. Cut into 4 equal squares. Serve 2 squares to Frank and 1 square each to Susan and Elise, paired with a simple cucumber and tomato salad if desired.

Nutrition (per person)

Frank (largest portion): approx 495 kcal, 44 g protein, 39 g carbs, 17 g fat.
Susan (medium portion): approx 370 kcal, 33 g protein, 29 g carbs, 13 g fat.
Elise (medium portion): approx 370 kcal, 33 g protein, 29 g carbs, 13 g fat.
