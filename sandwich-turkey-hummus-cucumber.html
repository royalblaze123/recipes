Turkey Hummus Cucumber Sandwich

Servings

3 sandwiches (Frank 1.0×, Susan 0.75×, Elise 0.75×).

Times

  • Prep time: 12 minutes
  • Cook time: 0 minutes
  • Total time: 12 minutes

Ingredients

  • 6 slices whole wheat bread
  • 24 slices deli turkey breast
  • 22 tsp hummus
  • 3 slices reduced-fat provolone cheese
  • 1 cucumber
  • 1/3 package romaine hearts

Recipe

  1. Rinse and dry 1/3 package romaine hearts; pull 6 large leaves (2 per sandwich). Store the remaining romaine hearts package sealed in the fridge.
  2. Slice 1 cucumber into thin rounds; divide evenly across the 3 sandwiches.
  3. Frank sandwich: spread 8 tsp hummus across 2 bread slices; add 10 slices deli turkey breast + 1 slice provolone + 2 romaine leaves + cucumber rounds; close and wrap.
  4. Susan sandwich: spread 7 tsp hummus across 2 bread slices; add 7 slices deli turkey breast + 1 slice provolone + 2 romaine leaves + cucumber rounds; close and wrap.
  5. Elise sandwich: spread 7 tsp hummus across 2 bread slices; add 7 slices deli turkey breast + 1 slice provolone + 2 romaine leaves + cucumber rounds; close and wrap.

Notes

Portions (per person):

Frank – 1 sandwich (10 turkey slices, 1 provolone slice, 8 tsp hummus).

Susan – 1 sandwich (7 turkey slices, 1 provolone slice, 7 tsp hummus).

Elise – 1 sandwich (7 turkey slices, 1 provolone slice, 7 tsp hummus).

Tips (recipe-specific):

For make-ahead, put hummus between turkey layers so the bread stays drier.

If you want extra crunch, add cucumber on top of the romaine instead of directly on the bread.

Nutrition (per person)

  • Frank: 542 kcal; Protein 54 g; Carbs 42 g; Fat 15 g; Fiber 8 g; Sodium 1236 mg
  • Susan: 458 kcal; Protein 42 g; Carbs 40 g; Fat 13 g; Fiber 7 g; Sodium 1014 mg
  • Elise: 458 kcal; Protein 42 g; Carbs 40 g; Fat 13 g; Fiber 7 g; Sodium 1014 mg
