Sheet Pan Cod with Carrots, Green Beans, and Roasted Potatoes

Prep: 18 min   Cook: 28 min   Total: 46 min

Ingredients

  • 1.5 lb cod fillets
  • 1.25 lb baby potatoes
  • 1 lb green beans
  • 1 lb carrots
  • olive oil
  • garlic powder
  • black pepper
  • salt substitute
  • lemon
  • smoked paprika

Hard rules enforced: Dinner validations (calories, sat fat, sodium, fiber, added sugar). Quantities appear before items; fresh meat/poultry/fish use weights. Condiments/spices/seasonings have no measurements in Ingredients; all measurements are in Steps. No olives, no squash, no Brussels sprouts, no zucchini. Yogurt is not used in dinners.

Step-by-step Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 425°F. Line a sheet pan with foil/parchment.
  2. Potatoes: halve baby potatoes. Toss with 2 tsp olive oil + garlic powder + black pepper + salt substitute. Spread on pan and roast 12 minutes.
  3. Carrots: peel and cut into 1/2-inch coins. Toss with 2 tsp olive oil + smoked paprika + black pepper + salt substitute. Add to pan (keep potatoes on one side). Roast 10 minutes.
  4. Green beans: trim ends. Toss with 1 tsp olive oil + black pepper + salt substitute. Add to pan and roast 6 minutes.
  5. Cod: pat dry. Season with garlic powder + black pepper + salt substitute. Add cod to the pan and roast 8–10 minutes until opaque and flakes easily.
  6. Finish with lemon squeeze.
  7. Portion: Frank gets the largest cod and potato share; Susan/Elise each get ~75%.
