Southwest Chicken Salad + Beans + Avocado + Tortilla Strips

Nutrition (approx)

Frank: 650 kcal • P 60g • C 55g • F 18g • Sat 4g • Na 650mg • Fiber 14g
Susan/Elise (each): 488 kcal • P 45g • C 41g • F 14g • Sat 3g • Na 488mg • Fiber 10g

Ingredients (purchase-clean)

  • 1.75 lb boneless skinless chicken breast
  • 1 head romaine lettuce
  • 1 can black beans
  • 1 each avocado
  • 3 each whole-wheat tortillas
  • 1 jar salsa
  • kosher salt
  • black pepper
  • garlic powder
  • onion powder
  • smoked paprika
  • Italian seasoning
  • ground cumin
  • chili powder
  • red pepper flakes
  • extra-virgin olive oil
  • balsamic vinegar
  • apple cider vinegar
  • Dijon mustard

Steps

  1. Oven: heat to 425°F.
  2. Chicken: season with chili powder, cumin, garlic powder, black pepper, modest kosher salt. Roast 12–15 minutes to 165°F; rest; slice.
  3. Beans: drain/rinse black beans (15 oz can). Warm 1 1/2 cups beans with 1/2 cup salsa (measured).
  4. Tortilla strips: slice tortillas into strips; bake 6–8 minutes until crisp.
  5. Dressing: whisk 2 tbsp salsa, 2 tbsp apple cider vinegar, 2 tbsp olive oil, black pepper, and a small pinch of kosher salt.
  6. Salad: chop romaine; toss with dressing; top with chicken, beans, avocado slices, and tortilla strips.
  7. Serve per Notes.

Notes

Southwest dinner salad that eats like a full meal—beans, avocado, and crunchy tortilla strips. Portions (per person): Frank: 4 cups salad + 7 oz chicken + 1/2 cup beans + 1/3 avocado + 1 tortilla strips Susan: 3 cups salad + 5 oz chicken + 1/3 cup beans + 1/4 avocado + 2/3 tortilla strips Elise: 3 cups salad + 5 oz chicken + 1/3 cup beans + 1/4 avocado + 2/3 tortilla strips Tips (recipe-specific): Bake strips until crisp so they stay crunchy in the salad. Warm beans with salsa so the bowl feels more like dinner.
