Pork Carnitas-Style + Beans + Tortillas

Nutrition (approx)

Frank: 650 kcal • P 58g • C 60g • F 18g
Susan/Elise (each): 488 kcal • P 44g • C 45g • F 14g

Ingredients (purchase-clean)

  • 1.75 lb pork tenderloin
  • 1 can black beans
  • 3 each whole-wheat tortillas
  • 1 jar salsa
  • 1 each lime
  • low-sodium chicken broth
  • kosher salt
  • black pepper
  • garlic powder
  • onion powder
  • smoked paprika
  • Italian seasoning
  • ground cumin
  • chili powder
  • red pepper flakes
  • extra-virgin olive oil
  • low-sodium chicken broth
  • low-sodium beef broth
  • low-sodium soy sauce
  • hot sauce
  • Dijon mustard
  • balsamic vinegar
  • apple cider vinegar
  • cornstarch

Steps (slow cooker)

  1. Pork + broth (1/2 cup) + salsa (1 cup measured). Season cumin, chili powder, garlic powder, pepper; modest salt.
  2. LOW 4–5 hr or HIGH 2–3 hr; shred.
  3. Optional crisp: broil shredded pork 3–4 min for edges.
  4. Warm beans: black beans (15 oz; 1 1/2 cups) + salsa (1/2 cup) 4 min.
  5. Warm tortillas; finish with lime; assemble per Notes.

Notes

Carnitas vibe using lean tenderloin plus optional broil for crisp edges. Portions (per person): Frank: 3 tacos + 1/2 cup beans Susan: 3 tacos + 1/3 cup beans Elise: 3 tacos + 1/3 cup beans Tips (recipe-specific): Broil briefly for the carnitas edge. Warm beans with salsa so it feels like a full dinner.
