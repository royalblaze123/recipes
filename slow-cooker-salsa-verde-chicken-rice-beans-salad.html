Slow Cooker Salsa Verde Chicken Bowls with Rice, Beans, and Crunchy Salad

Prep: 18 min   Cook: 360 min   Total: 378 min

Ingredients

  • 2.0 lb chicken breast
  • 0.75 jar salsa verde
  • 0.5 carton low-sodium chicken broth
  • 0.5 can black beans
  • 0.5 bag frozen corn
  • 0.5 bag dry brown rice
  • 1 lime
  • 1 bunch cilantro
  • 1 bag romaine hearts
  • 1 cucumber
  • 1 avocado
  • olive oil
  • cumin
  • garlic powder
  • black pepper
  • salt substitute

RBM rules: quantities appear before items; fresh meat/poultry/fish use weights. Condiments/spices/seasonings are name-only (no tsp/tbsp in Ingredients); all measurements are in Steps. Water is referenced in Steps only.

Step-by-step Instructions

  1. Slow cooker base: add chicken breast to the slow cooker. Pour in 0.75 jar salsa verde and 0.5 carton broth (16 fl oz). Stir in 2 tsp cumin + 1 tsp garlic powder + 1 tsp black pepper + 2 tsp salt substitute.
  2. Cook on LOW 5–6 hours or HIGH 3–4 hours until chicken reaches 165°F.
  3. Remove chicken, shred, and return to slow cooker. Add 0.5 can black beans (drained/rinsed) and 0.5 bag frozen corn (about 1 1/2 cups). Cook 15 minutes on HIGH to heat through.
  4. Rice (stovetop, cooked separately to avoid mush): rinse brown rice. Cook 3/4 cup dry rice with 1 1/2 cups water, simmer 35–40 minutes until tender. Fluff. (This yields ~2 1/4 cups cooked.)
  5. Finish chicken: add juice of 1 lime and 1/2 cup chopped cilantro to the slow cooker.
  6. Crunchy salad: chop romaine. Dice cucumber. Toss with 6 tsp olive oil + 6 tsp lime juice + 1 tsp black pepper + 1 tsp salt substitute.
  7. Portion bowls: Frank gets ~6 oz chicken mixture + 3/4 cup cooked rice + 1 1/2 cups salad + 1/2 avocado. Susan and Elise each get ~4.5 oz chicken mixture + 1/2 cup rice + 1 1/4 cups salad + 1/4 avocado.

Notes

Bright salsa verde chicken with controlled rice and a crunchy salad to keep the plate full and not mushy. Portions (per person): Frank: - Salsa verde chicken: ~6 oz cooked chicken portion (with beans/corn) - Brown rice: ~3/4 cup cooked - Salad: ~1 1/2 cups - Avocado: 1/2 Susan: - Salsa verde chicken: ~4.5 oz cooked chicken portion (with beans/corn) - Brown rice: ~1/2 cup cooked - Salad: ~1 1/4 cups - Avocado: 1/4 Elise: - Salsa verde chicken: ~4.5 oz cooked chicken portion (with beans/corn) - Brown rice: ~1/2 cup cooked - Salad: ~1 1/4 cups - Avocado: 1/4 Tips (recipe-specific): Cook rice separately—adding it to the crock turns leftovers into paste. If salsa verde is salty, cut broth to 1/4 carton and add extra lime at the end.
