Warm Lunch Skillet: Tuna, White Beans, and Whole Wheat Pasta with Spinach

Prep: 18 min   Cook: 16 min   Total: 34 min

Ingredients

  • 2 cans tuna in water
  • 0.5 can no-salt-added cannellini beans
  • 8 oz whole wheat penne
  • 1 bag spinach
  • 0.5 carton low-sodium chicken broth
  • 1 lemon
  • olive oil
  • garlic
  • black pepper
  • salt substitute
  • Italian seasoning
  • tomato paste

Rules enforced: Clean AnyList Ingredients (quantities before items; fresh meat/poultry/fish use weights; packaged items use fractional containers when partial). Condiments/spices/seasonings have no measurements in Ingredients; all measurements are in Steps. No olives, no squash, no Brussels sprouts, no zucchini, no tofu/tempeh. Lunch validations: Frank calories 500–550, sat fat ≤6g, sodium ≤650mg, fiber ≥8g, added sugar ≤6g.

Step-by-step Instructions

  1. Pasta: cook 8 oz whole wheat penne in boiling water until al dente (about 9–10 minutes); drain, reserving 1/2 cup cooking water.
  2. Beans: drain and rinse 1/2 can cannellini beans; set aside.
  3. Tuna: drain 2 cans tuna well.
  4. Sauce base: in a skillet, whisk 1/2 cup broth + 1/2 cup pasta water + 1 tbsp tomato paste + 1 tsp Italian seasoning + 1/4 tsp black pepper + 1/4 tsp salt substitute; simmer 2 minutes.
  5. Add tuna and beans; warm 2 minutes.
  6. Stir in 1 bag spinach until wilted (1–2 minutes).
  7. Toss in pasta and heat 2 minutes; finish with 2 tsp lemon juice per plate.
