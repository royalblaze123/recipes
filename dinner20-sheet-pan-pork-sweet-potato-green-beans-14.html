Sheet Pan Pork Sweet Potato Green Beans

Servings

3 plates (Frank 1.0×, Susan 0.75×, Elise 0.75×).

Times

  • Prep time: 15 minutes
  • Cook time: 25 minutes
  • Total time: 40 minutes

Ingredients

  • 2.0 lb lean protein (see Steps)
  • 1.0 cup dry grain OR 2 bags frozen cauliflower rice (see Steps)
  • 1 bag frozen vegetables OR fresh vegetables (see Steps)
  • 7 tsp olive oil
  • 4 tsp minced garlic
  • 2 tsp black pepper
  • 2 tsp salt substitute
  • 2 tsp dried herb blend

Steps

  1. Cook the grain/starch (or cauliflower rice) to yield portions per Notes; keep warm.
  2. Brown aromatics (onion/garlic) 6–8 minutes where applicable to build flavor without extra sodium.
  3. Cook the protein using the method implied by the category (air fryer/sheet pan/skillet) to safe internal temperature.
  4. Cook vegetables to tender-crisp and finish with black pepper and herbs.
  5. Plate portions per Notes.

Notes

Portions (per person):

Frank – 1 plate (8 oz cooked protein, 1 cup cooked grain/starch, 1.5 cups vegetables).

Susan – 1 plate (6.0 oz cooked protein, 3/4 cup cooked grain/starch, 1 1/4 cups vegetables).

Elise – 1 plate (6.0 oz cooked protein, 3/4 cup cooked grain/starch, 1 1/4 cups vegetables).

Tips (recipe-specific):

Season in two phases: base spices early, then finish with pepper/herbs at the end.

Reheat protein/veg in the air fryer for edge-crisp; microwave the starch separately with a splash of water.

Storage + Reheat

  • Cool promptly; store in airtight containers in the fridge up to 3–4 days (typical safety guidance).
  • For best texture, reheat protein + veg in the air fryer at 350°F for 4–6 minutes, and reheat grains/beans in the microwave 60–90 seconds with a splash of water.
