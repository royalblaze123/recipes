Air Fryer Pork Medallions + Sweet Potato Coins + Green Beans

Steps (Cosori dual-basket)

  1. Preheat Cosori to 390°F. Basket A: sweet potatoes. Basket B: pork later. Use Sync Finish.
  2. Sweet potato coins (Basket A): slice into 1/2-inch coins. Toss with 2 tsp olive oil, 1 tsp smoked paprika, 1 tsp garlic powder, 1/2 tsp kosher salt, and black pepper. Air fry 18–22 minutes at 390°F with Shake Reminder.
  3. Pork: slice tenderloin into 1-inch medallions. Season with 1 tsp garlic powder, 1 tsp smoked paprika, 1/2 tsp kosher salt, and black pepper. Brush with 2 tsp Dijon mustard total (measured).
  4. Pork (Basket B): add when sweet potatoes have ~10 minutes left. Air fry 10–12 minutes at 390°F, flipping once, to 145°F; rest 3 minutes.
  5. Apple quick topping: dice apple; toss with 1 tsp apple cider vinegar and black pepper. Spoon over pork.
  6. Green beans: microwave, drain, toss with 2 tsp olive oil and black pepper; crisp 5–6 minutes if space or roast 425°F for 10 minutes.
  7. Serve per Notes.

Notes

Juicy pork + sweet potato coins with a Dijon-apple topper (no sugary sauce). Portions (per person): Frank: 7 oz pork + 1 cup sweet potato coins + 1 cup green beans Susan: 5 oz protein + 1 cup sweet potato coins + 3/4 cup veg Elise: 5 oz protein + 1 cup sweet potato coins + 3/4 cup veg Tips (recipe-specific): Rest pork 3 minutes so it stays juicy. Use Dijon as a thin glaze—big flavor, tiny calories.
