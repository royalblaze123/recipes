Turkey Enchilada-Style Soup (Stovetop, No Tortillas)

NOTES: Designed as a complete meal soup using lean protein, high-fiber vegetables, and controlled starch. Use low-sodium broth and avoid extra salt when possible to support blood pressure and kidney health. PORTION SIZES: Frank: largest portion (~2 cups); Susan: medium portion (~1.5 cups); Elise: medium portion (~1.5 cups).

Ingredients

  • 1 tbsp extra-virgin olive oil
  • 1 lb 93% lean ground turkey
  • 1 medium yellow onion, diced
  • 1 green bell pepper, diced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 tbsp chili powder
  • 1 tsp ground cumin
  • 1 tsp smoked paprika
  • 1/2 tsp dried oregano
  • 1 can (14.5 oz) no-salt-added diced tomatoes
  • 1 can (8 oz) no-salt-added tomato sauce
  • 4 cups low-sodium chicken broth
  • 1 can (15 oz) no-salt-added black beans, drained and rinsed
  • 1 cup frozen corn (no added salt)
  • 1/2 tsp kosher salt (or to taste)
  • Freshly ground black pepper, to taste

Step-by-Step Instructions

  1. Heat olive oil in a large soup pot over medium heat.
  2. Add ground turkey and cook 6–8 minutes, breaking into crumbles, until no pink remains. Transfer to a plate and set aside.
  3. In the same pot, add diced onion and green bell pepper and cook 5–6 minutes until softened.
  4. Stir in minced garlic and cook 30–60 seconds until fragrant.
  5. Add chili powder, ground cumin, smoked paprika, dried oregano, salt, and black pepper and stir to coat vegetables.
  6. Pour in diced tomatoes with juices, tomato sauce, and low-sodium chicken broth and stir.
  7. Add drained black beans and frozen corn and stir to combine.
  8. Return cooked turkey to the pot and stir evenly into the soup.
  9. Bring to a gentle boil, then reduce heat to low and simmer uncovered 20–25 minutes, stirring occasionally, until flavors meld and broth thickens slightly.
  10. Taste and adjust salt and pepper as needed.
  11. Remove from heat and let rest 5 minutes, then ladle into bowls for Frank, Susan, and Elise.

Nutrition per Person

  • Frank: ~500 kcal, ~44 g protein, ~50 g carbs, ~11 g fat.
  • Susan: ~375 kcal, ~33 g protein, ~38 g carbs, ~8 g fat.
  • Elise: ~375 kcal, ~33 g protein, ~38 g carbs, ~8 g fat.
