Pan-Seared Salmon + Salsa + Sweet Potato

Nutrition (approx)

Frank: 650 kcal • P 55g • C 45g • F 28g
Susan/Elise (each): 488 kcal • P 41g • C 34g • F 21g

Ingredients (purchase-clean)

  • 1.50 lb salmon fillets
  • 2.00 lb sweet potatoes
  • 1 jar salsa
  • 1 each lime
  • kosher salt
  • black pepper
  • garlic powder
  • onion powder
  • smoked paprika
  • Italian seasoning
  • ground cumin
  • chili powder
  • red pepper flakes
  • extra-virgin olive oil
  • low-sodium chicken broth
  • low-sodium beef broth
  • low-sodium soy sauce
  • hot sauce
  • Dijon mustard
  • balsamic vinegar
  • apple cider vinegar
  • cornstarch

Steps (stovetop)

  1. Sweet potatoes: roast cubes 425°F 22–26 minutes with olive oil, smoked paprika, garlic powder, modest salt, black pepper.
  2. Salmon: pat dry. Season black pepper, garlic powder, smoked paprika, modest salt. Sear in skillet 3–4 minutes skin-side (if skin-on), then 2–3 minutes other side to 125–130°F and rest.
  3. Salsa: warm 3/4 cup salsa (measured) in small pan 1–2 minutes; add 1 tbsp lime juice (measured).
  4. Serve: salmon topped with salsa; sweet potatoes on side per Notes.

Notes

Big flavor without sauces: crisp-seared salmon plus warm salsa and roasted sweet potatoes. Portions (per person): Frank: 7 oz salmon + (none) + 1 cup roasted sweet potatoes Susan: 5 oz protein + 1 cup veg + 3/4 cup Elise: 5 oz protein + 1 cup veg + 3/4 cup Tips (recipe-specific): Rest salmon 3 minutes so it stays juicy. Warm salsa briefly; don’t cook it down.
