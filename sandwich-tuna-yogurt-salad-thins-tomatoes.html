Tuna-Yogurt Salad Sandwich Thins + Grape Tomatoes (RBM Lunch)

High-protein tuna salad made with Greek yogurt on whole-grain sandwich thins, with grape tomatoes on the side.

Servings

3 servings (Frank, Susan, Elise).

Ingredients

Sandwich

  • 3 whole-grain sandwich thins (about 100 kcal each, at least 3 g fiber)
  • 9 oz canned tuna in water, drained (clearly labeled canned tuna)
  • 6 tbsp nonfat plain Greek yogurt
  • 1 1/2 tbsp light mayonnaise
  • 3 tbsp fresh celery, finely diced
  • 1/8 tsp kosher or sea salt (or less, to taste)
  • 1/4 tsp ground black pepper
  • 3 leaves fresh lettuce

Side

  • 7.5 oz fresh grape tomatoes

Step-by-Step Instructions

  1. In a medium bowl, combine the drained canned tuna, Greek yogurt, light mayonnaise, diced celery, salt, and pepper. Stir with a fork until the tuna is broken up and everything is evenly mixed into a creamy tuna salad.
  2. Split each whole-grain sandwich thin so you have a top and bottom for each sandwich.
  3. Place a lettuce leaf on the bottom half of each sandwich thin.
  4. Divide the tuna salad evenly among the three sandwiches, spooning it over the lettuce and spreading it in an even layer.
  5. Top each sandwich with the remaining half of the sandwich thin and press gently.
  6. Divide the grape tomatoes into three portions as listed below and place them into small containers for packing.
  7. Serve immediately or wrap each sandwich in parchment or foil and refrigerate. Pack the grape tomatoes separately to keep the sandwich from getting soggy.

Portion Sizes (Per Person)

Frank: 1 whole-grain sandwich thin, 3.5 oz canned tuna in water (drained), 2 tbsp Greek-yogurt dressing, 1 tbsp diced celery, 1 leaf lettuce, 3 oz grape tomatoes.

 

Susan: 1 whole-grain sandwich thin, 2.75 oz canned tuna in water, 1 1/2 tbsp Greek-yogurt dressing, 2 tsp diced celery, 2.5 oz grape tomatoes.

 

Elise: 1 whole-grain sandwich thin, 2 oz canned tuna in water, 1 1/2 tbsp Greek-yogurt dressing, 2 tsp diced celery, 2 oz grape tomatoes.

Nutrition (Per Person)

  • Frank: 420 kcal, 39 g protein, 32 g carbs, 13 g fat, 5 g fiber, 610 mg sodium
  • Susan: 385 kcal, 36 g protein, 29 g carbs, 12 g fat, 4.5 g fiber, 575 mg sodium
  • Elise: 360 kcal, 33 g protein, 27 g carbs, 11 g fat, 4 g fiber, 540 mg sodium
