Air Fryer Lemon Herb Salmon

Ingredients

  • 1.5 lb salmon fillets
  • 3 tsp olive oil
  • 2 tsp lemon juice
  • 1 tsp dried dill
  • 1 tsp garlic powder
  • 1 tsp salt substitute
  • 1 bag frozen green beans

Steps

  1. Preheat air fryer to 390°F for 5 minutes.
  2. Brush salmon with 3 tsp olive oil and 2 tsp lemon juice; season with 1 tsp dill, 1 tsp garlic powder, and 1 tsp salt substitute.
  3. Air fry salmon at 390°F for 9–11 minutes until flakes easily.
  4. Microwave 1 bag frozen green beans; toss with 1 tsp olive oil from total and serve.
