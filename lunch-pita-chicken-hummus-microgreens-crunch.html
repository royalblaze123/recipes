High-Protein Pitas: Chicken Hummus Crunch with Microgreens

Prep: 16 min   Cook: 10 min   Total: 26 min

Ingredients

  • 3 whole wheat pitas
  • 1.0 lb chicken breast
  • 12 slices deli turkey
  • 0.5 tub hummus
  • 1 cucumber
  • 1 pint cherry tomatoes
  • 1 clamshell microgreens
  • olive oil
  • black pepper
  • salt substitute
  • garlic powder
  • lemon juice

Rules enforced: quantities before items; fresh meat/poultry/fish use weights; packaged items use fractional containers when partial. Condiments/spices/seasonings have no measurements in Ingredients; all measurements are in Steps. No olives, no squash, no Brussels sprouts, no zucchini, no tofu/tempeh. Lunch validations included in Notes.

Step-by-step Instructions

  1. Chicken: season 1.0 lb chicken breast with 1 tsp garlic powder + 1/2 tsp black pepper + 1/2 tsp salt substitute. Sear with 2 tsp olive oil 6–8 minutes until 165°F; rest 3 minutes; slice thin.
  2. Warm pitas: toast 3 whole wheat pitas in toaster/oven 2 minutes so they stay sturdy.
  3. Veg: dice 1 cucumber and halve 1 pint cherry tomatoes.
  4. Spread: smear hummus inside each pita (Frank uses the most; Susan/Elise ~75% of Frank).
  5. Protein stack: load sliced chicken plus deli turkey ribbons (divide total 12 slices: Frank 5, Susan 3–4, Elise 3–4).
  6. Crunch: add cucumber, tomatoes, and microgreens (divide evenly).
  7. Finish: add 1 tsp lemon juice per pita and a pinch of pepper; wrap and serve.
