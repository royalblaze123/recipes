Herbed Chicken & Veggie Crunch Sandwich Plate

Whole-grain chicken sandwich with Greek yogurt herb spread and carrot-cucumber crunch on the side, designed as a full RBM lunch.

Servings

3 servings (Frank, Susan, Elise).

Times

  • Prep time: 15 minutes
  • Cook time: 0 minutes
  • Total time: 15 minutes

Ingredients

Sandwich

  • 6 slices high-fiber whole-grain sandwich bread
  • 9 oz leftover home-cooked chicken breast, thinly sliced (no skin, no breading)
  • 6 tbsp low-fat (2%) plain Greek yogurt
  • 1 tsp dried Italian herb blend (no added salt)
  • 1/4 tsp garlic powder
  • 1/4 tsp black pepper
  • 2 cups fresh lettuce or spring mix, loosely packed
  • 1 cup fresh tomato slices

Side

  • 1 cup fresh cucumber slices
  • 1 cup fresh carrot sticks

Preparation Steps

  1. In a small bowl, stir together the Greek yogurt, Italian herb blend, garlic powder, and black pepper to make an herbed sandwich spread.
  2. Lay out the whole-grain bread slices and spread the herbed yogurt mixture evenly over one side of each slice.
  3. Divide the sliced chicken breast over half of the bread slices, giving Frank a slightly larger portion as noted in the portions.
  4. Top the chicken with lettuce or spring mix and tomato slices.
  5. Close each sandwich with a remaining bread slice, press gently, and cut in half if desired.
  6. Arrange cucumber slices and carrot sticks on the plates as a crunchy side, dividing them into three portions.
  7. Serve each plate with one sandwich and a generous pile of carrot and cucumber sticks, following the portions in the Notes.

Notes

Herbed chicken sandwich plate with plenty of veggie crunch.

Frank – 1 full sandwich (~3.5 oz chicken, 2 slices bread) plus about 1 cup carrot and cucumber sticks.

Susan – 1 full sandwich (~3 oz chicken, 2 slices bread) plus about 3/4 cup carrot and cucumber sticks.

Elise – 1 full sandwich (~2.5 oz chicken, 2 slices bread) plus about 3/4 cup carrot and cucumber sticks.

Nutrition (per person)

  • Frank: 410 kcal; 38 g protein; 34 g carbs; 11 g fat; 8 g fiber; ~560 mg sodium.
  • Susan: 350 kcal; 31 g protein; 30 g carbs; 9 g fat; 7 g fiber; ~500 mg sodium.
  • Elise: 350 kcal; 31 g protein; 30 g carbs; 9 g fat; 7 g fiber; ~500 mg sodium.
