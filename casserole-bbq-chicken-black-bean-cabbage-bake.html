BBQ Chicken Black Bean Bake with Crunchy Cabbage Slaw

Prep: 24 min   Cook: 40 min   Total: 64 min

Ingredients

  • 14 oz chicken breast
  • 0.5 can no-salt-added black beans
  • 0.5 can no-salt-added diced tomatoes
  • 0.5 cup dry brown rice
  • 0.25 jar sugar-free BBQ sauce
  • 0.5 carton low-sodium chicken broth
  • 1 onion
  • 1 bell pepper
  • 0.5 bag shredded cabbage
  • 1 lime
  • olive oil
  • black pepper
  • salt substitute
  • smoked paprika
  • vinegar

Hard rules enforced: Dinner calories (Frank 600–650), sat fat ≤8g, sodium ≤700mg, fiber ≥10g, added sugar ≤6g. Quantities appear before items; fresh meat/poultry/fish use weights. Condiments/spices/seasonings have no measurements in Ingredients; all measurements are in Steps. No olives, no squash, no Brussels sprouts, no zucchini, no yogurt in dinners.

Step-by-step Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 375°F.
  2. Brown rice: cook 1/2 cup dry brown rice per package directions (water in Steps only). (Yields ~1 1/2 cups cooked.)
  3. Chicken: dice chicken. Season with smoked paprika, black pepper, and salt substitute. Sauté with 2 tsp olive oil 6–8 minutes to 165°F.
  4. Veg: dice onion and bell pepper; sauté 4–5 minutes.
  5. Beans/tomatoes: drain/rinse 1/2 can black beans. Add beans + 1/2 can diced tomatoes.
  6. BBQ: stir in 1/4 jar sugar-free BBQ sauce (use as a light coating) + 3/4 cup broth (from carton). Simmer 2 minutes.
  7. Combine: mix skillet contents with cooked rice in baking dish. Bake 20 minutes covered, then 10 minutes uncovered. Rest 5 minutes.
  8. Slaw: toss shredded cabbage with 2 tsp lime juice + 1 tsp vinegar + 2 tsp olive oil + black pepper + salt substitute.
  9. Portion: Frank ~1/2 bake + ~1 1/2 cups slaw; Susan/Elise each ~1/4 bake + ~1 cup slaw.
