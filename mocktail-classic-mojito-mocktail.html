Classic Mojito Mocktail

Yield: 1 drink • Prep: 5 min • Total: 5 min

Ingredients (AnyList-ready)

  • 10 mint leaves
  • 1 lime
  • 1 oz simple syrup
  • 6 oz club soda
  • 1 cup ice

Steps

  1. Add 10 mint leaves and 1 oz simple syrup to a tall glass.
  2. Cut the lime in half. Squeeze in about 1.5 oz lime juice (about 1 lime). Drop in 1 lime half and gently muddle 8–10 presses (do not shred mint).
  3. Fill the glass with 1 cup ice.
  4. Top with 6 oz club soda.
  5. Stir once, gently. Garnish with a mint sprig and a lime wheel if desired.

Notes

If you prefer it less sweet, reduce simple syrup to 0.75 oz and increase club soda to 8 oz.

Tip: Use plenty of ice and a quick, gentle stir to keep carbonation lively.
