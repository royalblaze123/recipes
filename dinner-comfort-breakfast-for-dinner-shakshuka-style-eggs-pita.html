Shakshuka-Style Eggs + Pita

Nutrition (approx)

Frank: 640 kcal • P 55g • C 60g • F 16g • Sat 5g • Na 700mg • Fiber 10g
Susan/Elise (each): 480 kcal • P 41g • C 45g • F 12g • Sat 4g • Na 525mg • Fiber 8g

Ingredients (purchase-clean)

  • 6 each eggs
  • 1 jar marinara sauce
  • 1 can no-salt-added diced tomatoes
  • 3 each whole-wheat pitas
  • 1 bag baby spinach
  • kosher salt
  • black pepper
  • garlic powder
  • onion powder
  • smoked paprika
  • Italian seasoning
  • hot sauce
  • Dijon mustard
  • extra-virgin olive oil
  • salsa
  • cumin
  • paprika

Steps

  1. In a large skillet, simmer 2 cups marinara + 1 can diced tomatoes (14.5 oz) for 8 minutes with cumin, smoked paprika, black pepper.
  2. Stir in spinach until wilted.
  3. Make 6 wells and crack in eggs. Cover and cook 6–8 minutes until whites set.
  4. Warm pitas in oven or toaster.
  5. Serve per Notes.

Notes

Shakshuka comfort using marinara + tomatoes, with pita for dipping. Portions (per person): Frank: 2 eggs in sauce + 1 pita Susan: 2 eggs in sauce + 1/2 pita Elise: 2 eggs in sauce + 1/2 pita Tips (recipe-specific): Cover the pan so eggs steam-set without overcooking. Use spices to make jar sauce taste like a slow simmer.
