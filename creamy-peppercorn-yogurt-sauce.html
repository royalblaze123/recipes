Creamy Peppercorn Yogurt Sauce

Category: Sauce • Yield: About 1 3/8 cups (22 tbsp) • AnyList-ready

Ingredients

  • 3/4 cup 2% plain Greek yogurt
  • 1/2 cup 1% milk
  • 2 tsp Dijon mustard
  • 1 tsp Worcestershire sauce
  • 1 tsp cornstarch
  • 1 tsp cracked black pepper
  • 1/2 tsp onion powder

Steps

  1. In a small saucepan over medium heat, whisk milk, Dijon, Worcestershire, onion powder, and cracked pepper.
  2. Whisk cornstarch with 2 tsp water until smooth, then whisk into the pan.
  3. Simmer 60–90 seconds until slightly thickened, then remove from heat and cool 2 minutes.
  4. Whisk in Greek yogurt until smooth. Do not boil after adding yogurt.
