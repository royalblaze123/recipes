Philly Cheesesteak Mini Sweet Pepper Boats

Sheet Pan • Yield: 36 pepper boats

Ingredients (purchase-clean)

  • 18 each fresh mini sweet peppers
  • 24 oz fresh 93% lean beef sirloin
  • 3 oz provolone
  • 1 each fresh yellow onion
  • 1 tsp garlic powder
  • kosher salt
  • black pepper

Steps

  1. Heat oven to 425°F. Line a sheet pan with parchment.
  2. Slice 1 onion thin. Sauté onion with 1 tsp kosher salt, 1 tsp garlic powder, and 1/2 tsp black pepper for 6–8 min until soft.
  3. Add 24 oz thin-sliced beef; cook 3–5 min until just cooked through.
  4. Halve 18 mini sweet peppers lengthwise to make 36 boats; remove seeds.
  5. Fill boats with beef/onion mixture; top each with a small piece of provolone (total 3 oz across all boats).
  6. Bake 10–12 min until peppers are tender-crisp and cheese melts; broil 1 min if desired.

Notes

Flavor-forward holiday poppers designed for snacking or a light meal. Portions (guidance): Light-meal portion: 10–14 boats with a veggie platter. Keep cheese measured—flavor comes from onion + sear.
