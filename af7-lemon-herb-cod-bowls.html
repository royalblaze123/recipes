Air Fryer Lemon-Herb Cod Bowls

Bright lemon-herb cod with roasted broccoli over an edamame-quinoa base.

Servings

3 servings.

Times

  • Prep time: 15 minutes
  • Cook time: 25 minutes
  • Total time: 40 minutes

Ingredients

Main Dish

  • 20 oz cod fillets
  • 24 oz fresh broccoli florets
  • 1 2/3 cup frozen shelled edamame
  • 1/2 cup quinoa
  • 7 tsp olive oil
  • 6 tsp Dijon mustard
  • 6 tsp lemon juice
  • 4 tsp minced garlic
  • 3 tsp Italian seasoning
  • 2 tsp smoked paprika
  • 2 tsp black pepper
  • 1 tsp salt substitute

Preparation Steps

  1. Cook quinoa (stovetop): Rinse 1/2 cup quinoa in a fine-mesh strainer. In a small pot, bring 1 cup water to a boil, stir in the rinsed quinoa, reduce to low, cover, and simmer 15 minutes. Remove from heat and rest covered 5 minutes, then fluff. (Instant Pot option: combine 1/2 cup quinoa + 3/4 cup water; pressure cook High 1 minute; natural release 10 minutes; fluff.)
  2. Cook edamame: Add 1 2/3 cup frozen shelled edamame to a microwave-safe bowl with 2 tbsp water. Cover and microwave 3–4 minutes until hot, then drain. (Stovetop option: simmer edamame in boiling water 4 minutes; drain.)
  3. Preheat air fryer to 400°F for 3 minutes. If using a dual-basket air fryer, plan broccoli in Basket A and cod in Basket B. If using a single basket, cook broccoli first, then cod.
  4. Season broccoli: In a large bowl, toss 24 oz fresh broccoli florets with 4 tsp olive oil, 2 tsp minced garlic, 2 tsp smoked paprika, 1 tsp black pepper, and 1 tsp salt substitute until evenly coated.
  5. Air fry broccoli: Spread the seasoned broccoli in the air fryer basket in an even layer. Air fry at 400°F for 10–12 minutes, shaking at 6 minutes, until edges are browned and stems are crisp-tender. Transfer to a plate and keep warm.
  6. Season cod: Pat 20 oz cod fillets dry. In a bowl, whisk 3 tsp olive oil, 4 tsp Dijon mustard, 4 tsp lemon juice, 2 tsp minced garlic, 3 tsp Italian seasoning, and 1 tsp black pepper. Brush the mixture evenly over all cod fillets.
  7. Air fry cod: Place the seasoned cod in a single layer. Air fry at 400°F for 7–10 minutes (depending on thickness) until the thickest piece reaches 145°F and flakes easily. Rest 2 minutes.
  8. Flavor the quinoa: In the quinoa pot/bowl, stir in 2 tsp Dijon mustard and 2 tsp lemon juice to lightly dress the cooked quinoa. Mix until evenly coated.
  9. Portion bowls: Measure cooked quinoa into bowls — Frank: 0.6 cup; Susan: 0.45 cup; Elise: 0.45 cup. Add edamame — Frank: 2/3 cup; Susan: 1/2 cup; Elise: 1/2 cup.
  10. Finish portions: Add roasted broccoli — Frank: 9 oz; Susan: 7.5 oz; Elise: 7.5 oz. Top with cod — Frank: 8 oz; Susan: 6 oz; Elise: 6 oz. Serve immediately.

Notes

Portions (per person):

 

Frank – 8 oz cod; 9 oz broccoli; 2/3 cup edamame; 0.6 cup cooked quinoa.

 

Susan – 6 oz cod; 7.5 oz broccoli; 1/2 cup edamame; 0.45 cup cooked quinoa.

 

Elise – 6 oz cod; 7.5 oz broccoli; 1/2 cup edamame; 0.45 cup cooked quinoa.

Tips (recipe-specific):

For crisp-tender broccoli, keep florets in a single layer and shake halfway through.

 

If your air fryer runs hot, drop temp by 10°F and add 1–2 minutes to the fish cook.

  • Susan: 475 kcal; 49 g protein; 38 g carbs; 13 g fat; 9 g fiber; 340 mg sodium.
  • Elise: 475 kcal; 49 g protein; 38 g carbs; 13 g fat; 9 g fiber; 340 mg sodium.
