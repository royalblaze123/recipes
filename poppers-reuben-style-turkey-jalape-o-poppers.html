Reuben-Style Turkey Jalapeño Poppers

Air Fryer or Sheet Pan • Yield: 24 popper halves

Ingredients (purchase-clean)

  • 12 each fresh jalapeños
  • 12 oz deli turkey
  • 6 oz reduced-fat cream cheese
  • 1 oz swiss cheese
  • 1 cup shredded green cabbage
  • 1 tsp caraway seed
  • black pepper
  • kosher salt

Steps

  1. Preheat air fryer to 370°F (or oven to 425°F).
  2. Finely chop 12 oz deli turkey. Microwave 1 cup shredded cabbage 60 seconds; squeeze dry.
  3. Mix filling: soften 6 oz cream cheese; stir in cabbage + turkey + 1 tsp caraway seed + 1/2 tsp black pepper + 1/4 tsp kosher salt; fold in 1 oz swiss.
  4. Halve and seed 12 jalapeños; pat dry; stuff evenly.
  5. Cook: air-fry 370°F for 7–9 min (or bake 425°F for 12–15 min) until hot.

Notes

Flavor-forward holiday poppers designed for snacking or a light meal. Portions (guidance): Serve with a small bowl of light Thousand Island on the side if you want (optional). If you want more tang, add 1 tsp vinegar to the cabbage before mixing (optional).
