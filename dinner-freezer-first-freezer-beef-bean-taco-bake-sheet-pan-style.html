Freezer Beef & Bean Taco Bake (Sheet-Pan Style)

Nutrition (approx)

Frank: 650 kcal • P 55g • C 60g • F 20g • Sat 7g • Na 700mg • Fiber 12g
Susan/Elise (each): 488 kcal • P 41g • C 45g • F 15g • Sat 5g • Na 525mg • Fiber 9g

Ingredients (purchase-clean)

  • 1.50 lb 90% lean ground beef
  • 1 can black beans
  • 1 jar salsa
  • 3 each whole-wheat tortillas
  • 6 oz reduced-fat shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1 bag frozen fajita-style pepper blend
  • kosher salt
  • black pepper
  • garlic powder
  • onion powder
  • smoked paprika
  • Italian seasoning
  • ground cumin
  • chili powder
  • red pepper flakes
  • extra-virgin olive oil
  • low-sodium chicken broth
  • cornstarch

Steps

  1. Oven: heat to 400°F.
  2. Beef: brown beef with chili powder, cumin, garlic powder, black pepper, modest salt 8–10 minutes; drain any excess.
  3. Veg: sauté fajita pepper blend until water evaporates (6–8 minutes); fold into beef.
  4. Beans: drain/rinse black beans; stir in 1 1/2 cups beans and 1 cup salsa (measured). Simmer 5 minutes uncovered.
  5. Tortillas: cut tortillas into strips; fold 2 tortillas worth into the mixture to thicken.
  6. Build: spread into a baking dish; top with remaining tortilla strips and mozzarella (measured).
  7. Bake 20–24 minutes; rest 10 minutes.
  8. Freeze: cool; portion; freeze. Reheat covered 350°F 25–35 minutes.

Notes

Taco-bake comfort engineered for freezer portions (tortillas thicken; no chips needed). Portions (per person): Frank: About 2 1/2 cups (heaping scoop) Susan: About 2 cups (scoop) Elise: About 2 cups (scoop) Tips (recipe-specific): Simmer uncovered so the bake doesn’t weep liquid after freezing. Rest before portioning so it sets for clean freezer containers.
