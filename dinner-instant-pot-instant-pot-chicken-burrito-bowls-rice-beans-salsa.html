Instant Pot Chicken Burrito Bowls (Rice + Beans + Salsa)

Nutrition (approx)

Frank: 650 kcal • P 60g • C 70g • F 14g
Susan/Elise (each): 488 kcal • P 45g • C 52g • F 10g

Ingredients (purchase-clean)

  • 1.75 lb boneless skinless chicken breast
  • 1 cup dry brown rice
  • 1 can black beans
  • 1 jar salsa
  • 1 bag frozen corn
  • low-sodium chicken broth
  • kosher salt
  • black pepper
  • garlic powder
  • onion powder
  • smoked paprika
  • Italian seasoning
  • ground cumin
  • chili powder
  • red pepper flakes
  • extra-virgin olive oil
  • low-sodium chicken broth
  • low-sodium beef broth
  • low-sodium soy sauce
  • hot sauce
  • Dijon mustard
  • balsamic vinegar
  • apple cider vinegar
  • cornstarch

Steps (Instant Pot)

  1. Rice: cook brown rice on stovetop or pot-in-pot (High 22 minutes; NR 10).
  2. Chicken: add chicken + broth (1/2 cup) + salsa (1 cup measured). Season cumin, chili powder, garlic powder, black pepper, modest salt.
  3. Pressure cook: High 10 minutes; NR 10 then Quick Release. Shred chicken.
  4. Add beans (15 oz; 1 1/2 cups) + corn (2 cups). Sauté 5 minutes to heat and thicken.
  5. Serve over rice per Notes.

Notes

Classic burrito-bowl flavor with thick, reheatable chicken/bean topping. Portions (per person): Frank: 7 oz chicken/beans + 1 cup cooked rice + (corn in mix) Susan: 5 oz protein + 1 cup cooked rice (smaller) + (corn in mix) (smaller) Elise: 5 oz protein + 1 cup cooked rice (smaller) + (corn in mix) (smaller) Tips (recipe-specific): Sauté after pressure so it thickens up for leftovers. Add beans/corn after pressure so they keep structure.
