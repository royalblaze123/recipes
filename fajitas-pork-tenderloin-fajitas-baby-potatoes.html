Pork Tenderloin Fajitas + Baby Potatoes

Nutrition (approx)

Frank: 635 kcal • P 58g • C 38g • F 22g
Susan/Elise (each): 476 kcal • P 44g • C 28g • F 16g

Ingredients (purchase-clean)

  • 1.50 lb pork tenderloin
  • 2 each bell peppers
  • 1 each yellow onion
  • 4/5 package whole-grain small tortillas
  • 1.50 lb baby potatoes
  • 6 tsp extra-virgin olive oil
  • 1 each lime
  • 1 tub 2% plain Greek yogurt
  • 2 tsp chili powder
  • 2 tsp cumin
  • 2 tsp garlic powder
  • 1 tsp smoked paprika
  • 1 tsp black pepper
  • 1 tsp kosher salt

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 425°F. Line two sheet pans with parchment.
  2. Baby potatoes: quarter; toss with 2 tsp olive oil and 1/2 tsp kosher salt. Roast 25–30 minutes, flipping once.
  3. Pork: pat tenderloin dry and slice into 1/2-inch medallions. Season with fajita spices. Toss pork with 4 tsp olive oil.
  4. Veg: slice peppers and onion into strips; add to the pork on the sheet pan.
  5. Roast pork + veg 16–20 minutes, stirring once, until pork reaches 145°F and veg is browned at edges. Rest 3 minutes.
  6. Tortillas: warm tortillas 20–30 seconds per side.
  7. Yogurt-lime crema: mix 6 tbsp Greek yogurt with 2 tsp lime juice and 1 tsp lime zest.
  8. Serve fajita mix with tortillas, crema, and potatoes. Portion per Notes.

Notes

Pork tenderloin fajitas with roasted baby potatoes and yogurt-lime crema.
