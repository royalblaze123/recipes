Double-Veg Chicken Quesadilla Plates

Nutrition (approx)

Frank: 640 kcal • P 58g • C 45g • F 21g
Susan/Elise (each): 480 kcal • P 44g • C 34g • F 16g

Ingredients (purchase-clean)

  • 1.25 lb boneless skinless chicken breast
  • 12 oz fresh broccoli florets
  • 1 bag frozen fajita-style pepper blend
  • 1 package whole-grain tortillas
  • 1 bag reduced-fat shredded Mexican cheese blend
  • 1 bag spring mix
  • 1 each avocado
  • 1/2 jar salsa
  • 1 each lime
  • 6 tsp extra-virgin olive oil
  • 2 tsp chili powder
  • 2 tsp cumin
  • 2 tsp garlic powder
  • 1 tsp smoked paprika
  • 1 tsp black pepper
  • 1 tsp kosher salt

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 425°F. Line a sheet pan with parchment.
  2. Broccoli: cut into bite-size florets. Toss with 2 tsp olive oil and a pinch of kosher salt. Roast 12–15 minutes until browned at edges.
  3. Chicken: pat dry and cut into 1/2-inch pieces. Season with spices.
  4. Cook chicken in a skillet 8–10 minutes until browned and 165°F.
  5. Veg: cook frozen fajita pepper blend in the same skillet with 2 tsp olive oil 6–8 minutes until browned.
  6. Combine: fold roasted broccoli into the chicken/pepper mix.
  7. Assembly + cook: build quesadillas with measured cheese and skillet-cook 2–3 minutes per side until browned and melted.
  8. Side salad: add spring mix to a large bowl. Drizzle with 2 tsp lime juice and 2 tsp olive oil, then toss. Add diced cucumber/tomato if listed.
  9. Avocado: halve, pit, and dice. Use about 1/2 avocado total; refrigerate remaining avocado with the pit to slow browning.
  10. Serve with salsa and portion per Notes.

Notes

Extra vegetables keep these quesadilla plates high-volume; salsa and avocado bring the flavor. Portions (per person): Frank: Frank: 1 large quesadilla (made with 2 tortillas) + 1/3 of the salad/veg side + salsa + avocado Susan: Susan: 1 medium quesadilla (made with 1 tortilla folded) + 1/4 of the salad/veg side + salsa + avocado Elise: Elise: 1 medium quesadilla (made with 1 tortilla folded) + 1/4 of the salad/veg side + salsa + avocado
