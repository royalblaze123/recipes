Chicken Parm-Style Meatballs + Roasted Veg

Nutrition (approx)

Frank: 635 kcal • P 62g • C 40g • F 18g • Sat 7g • Na 690mg • Fiber 9g
Susan/Elise (each): 476 kcal • P 46g • C 30g • F 14g • Sat 5g • Na 518mg • Fiber 7g

Ingredients (purchase-clean)

  • 2.00 lb 99% lean ground chicken
  • 1 package whole-wheat breadcrumbs
  • 2 each eggs
  • 1 jar marinara sauce
  • 6 oz reduced-fat shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 2 oz grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 bag frozen green beans
  • kosher salt
  • black pepper
  • garlic powder
  • onion powder
  • Italian seasoning
  • smoked paprika
  • chili powder
  • ground cumin
  • crushed red pepper
  • extra-virgin olive oil

Steps

  1. Oven: heat to 425°F. Line a sheet pan and lightly oil.
  2. Meatballs: mix chicken with 1/2 cup breadcrumbs, 2 eggs, 2 tsp garlic powder, 1 tsp onion powder, 1 tsp Italian seasoning, 1/2 tsp kosher salt, 1/2 tsp black pepper.
  3. Form 18–20 meatballs; bake 13–15 minutes to 165°F.
  4. Sauce: spread 1 cup marinara in a baking dish. Add meatballs. Spoon 1 cup marinara over top.
  5. Cheese: sprinkle 6 oz reduced-fat mozzarella + 2 oz Parmesan.
  6. Bake 8–10 minutes until bubbly; broil 1–2 minutes for browning.
  7. Veg: roast 1 bag frozen green beans at 425°F with 2 tsp olive oil for 14–18 minutes.
  8. Serve per Notes.

Notes

Chicken parm comfort without breading and frying. Portions (per person): Frank: 8–9 meatballs + 1 cup veg side Susan: 6–7 meatballs + 3/4 cup veg side Elise: 6–7 meatballs + 3/4 cup veg side Tips (recipe-specific): Broil briefly for that ‘parm’ top—watch closely. Keep cheese measured; marinara brings most of the comfort flavor.
