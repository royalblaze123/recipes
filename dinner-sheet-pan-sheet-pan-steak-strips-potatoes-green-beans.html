Sheet-Pan Steak Strips + Potatoes + Green Beans

Nutrition (approx)

Frank: 650 kcal • P 55g • C 55g • F 22g • Sat 6g • Na 700mg • Fiber 10g
Susan/Elise (each): 488 kcal • P 41g • C 41g • F 16g • Sat 4g • Na 525mg • Fiber 8g

Ingredients (purchase-clean)

  • 1.25 lb lean sirloin steak
  • 1.50 lb baby potatoes
  • 1 bag frozen green beans
  • kosher salt
  • black pepper
  • garlic powder
  • onion powder
  • smoked paprika
  • Italian seasoning
  • ground cumin
  • chili powder
  • red pepper flakes
  • extra-virgin olive oil
  • Dijon mustard
  • balsamic vinegar
  • apple cider vinegar
  • low-sodium soy sauce
  • cornstarch
  • hot sauce

Steps

  1. Oven: heat to 450°F. Line sheet pan.
  2. Potatoes: halve; toss with 2 tsp olive oil, smoked paprika, garlic powder, 1/2 tsp kosher salt, black pepper. Roast 22 minutes.
  3. Green beans: microwave; drain. Toss with 2 tsp olive oil, garlic powder, 1/2 tsp kosher salt, black pepper. Add for last 10 minutes.
  4. Steak: slice into strips. Toss with 2 tsp low-sodium soy sauce, 2 tsp olive oil, garlic powder, black pepper.
  5. Add steak strips for the last 6–8 minutes (keep medium).
  6. Rest steak 3 minutes; serve per Notes.

Notes

Steak-and-potatoes comfort with soy-garlic punch and controlled portions. Portions (per person): Frank: 6 oz steak + 1 cup potatoes + 1 1/2 cups green beans Susan: 5 oz protein + 3/4 cup starch + 1 cup veg Elise: 5 oz protein + 3/4 cup starch + 1 cup veg Tips (recipe-specific): Add steak at the very end; that’s how you avoid dryness. Rest steak before serving so juices stay in.
