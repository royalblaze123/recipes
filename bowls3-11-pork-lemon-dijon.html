Composed Pork Bowl with Lentils, Mushrooms, and Lemon Dijon

Servings

3 bowls (Frank 1.0×, Susan 0.75×, Elise 0.75×).

Times

  • Prep time: 22 minutes
  • Cook time: 28 minutes
  • Total time: 50 minutes

Ingredients

  • 2 pork tenderloins
  • 1.0 cup dry lentils
  • 24 oz mushrooms
  • 1 pack microgreens
  • 11 tsp olive oil
  • 6 tsp minced garlic
  • 2 tsp black pepper
  • 2 tsp salt substitute
  • 2 tsp dried parsley
  • 4 tsp Dijon mustard
  • 4 tsp lemon juice
  • 2 tsp black pepper

Steps

  1. Lentils: Rinse 1 cup dry lentils. Simmer 18–22 minutes until tender; drain and keep warm.
  2. Vegetables: Roast per steps below to get browned edges (texture contrast matters).
  3. Mushrooms: Slice. Toss with 4 tsp olive oil + 2 tsp minced garlic + 1 tsp pepper + 1 tsp salt substitute. Roast at 425°F for 14–16 minutes, stirring once, until browned.
  4. Pork: Slice into 1-inch medallions. Sear in a skillet with 3 tsp olive oil 3–4 minutes per side to 145°F; rest 3 minutes.
  5. Sauce: In a small bowl, whisk the Sauce ingredients (lemon dijon). Keep it measured—this is a finish, not a flood.
  6. Build bowls (assembly order): Add warm starch first, then vegetables, then sliced protein. Spoon sauce on top. Finish with microgreens after heat (they’re the ‘gourmet’ signal).
  7. Plate portions per Notes.

Notes

Portions (per person):

Frank – 1 bowl (8 oz cooked protein, 3/4 cup cooked lentils, 1 1/2 cups mushrooms, 1/2 cup microgreens, 2–3 tbsp sauce).

Susan – 1 bowl (6 oz cooked protein, 3/4 of Frank’s starch portion, 1 1/4 cups mushrooms, 1/3 cup microgreens, 2 tbsp sauce).

Elise – 1 bowl (6 oz cooked protein, 3/4 of Frank’s starch portion, 1 1/4 cups mushrooms, 1/3 cup microgreens, 2 tbsp sauce).

Tips (recipe-specific):

Build order matters: warm base → dry veg → protein → sauce → microgreens last so nothing turns soggy.

Keep sauce measured; the bowl should taste ‘finished,’ not ‘dressed.’

Storage + Reheat

  • Cool promptly; store components in airtight containers in the fridge up to 3–4 days (typical safety guidance).
  • Reheat grain/beans + protein 60–90 seconds in the microwave; reheat roasted veg 4–6 minutes at 350°F in the air fryer for texture; add sauce and microgreens after reheating.
