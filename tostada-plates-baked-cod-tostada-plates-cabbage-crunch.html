Baked Cod Tostada Plates + Cabbage Crunch

Nutrition (approx)

Frank: 635 kcal • P 58g • C 48g • F 20g
Susan/Elise (each): 476 kcal • P 44g • C 36g • F 15g

Ingredients (purchase-clean)

  • 1.50 lb cod fillets
  • 1 bag shredded cabbage slaw mix
  • 1 package baked tostada shells
  • 1 each avocado
  • 1/2 jar salsa
  • 4 tsp extra-virgin olive oil
  • 1 each lime
  • 2 tsp chili powder
  • 2 tsp cumin
  • 2 tsp garlic powder
  • 1 tsp smoked paprika
  • 1 tsp black pepper
  • 1 tsp kosher salt

Steps

  1. Tostadas: warm in a 350°F oven for 3–4 minutes to crisp and heat through.
  2. Fish: pat cod dry. Rub with 4 tsp olive oil and season with the spices.
  3. Bake fish at 425°F for 10–12 minutes until 145°F and flaky. Rest 3 minutes, then break into flakes.
  4. Slaw: toss 2 cups cabbage mix with 2 tsp lime juice and a pinch of black pepper.
  5. Avocado: halve, pit, and dice. Use about 1/2 avocado total; refrigerate remaining avocado with the pit to slow browning.
  6. Serve: top tostadas with cod, then slaw, then salsa and avocado. Portion per Notes.

Notes

Fish tostadas with crunchy cabbage, salsa, and avocado—big flavor without heavy sauces. Portions (per person): Frank: 2 tostadas + 1/3 of the protein topping + 1/3 of the avocado + salsa Susan: 1 tostada + 1/4 of the protein topping + 1/4 of the avocado + salsa Elise: 1 tostada + 1/4 of the protein topping + 1/4 of the avocado + salsa
