Lemon-Pepper Salmon + Crispy Potatoes + Green Beans

Nutrition (approx)

Frank: 640 kcal • P 55g • C 55g • F 20g • Sat 5g • Na 650mg • Fiber 10g
Susan/Elise (each): 480 kcal • P 41g • C 41g • F 15g • Sat 4g • Na 488mg • Fiber 8g

Ingredients (purchase-clean)

  • 1.25 lb salmon fillets
  • 1.50 lb baby potatoes
  • 1 bag frozen green beans
  • 1 each lemon
  • kosher salt
  • black pepper
  • garlic powder
  • onion powder
  • smoked paprika
  • Italian seasoning
  • ground cumin
  • chili powder
  • red pepper flakes
  • extra-virgin olive oil
  • low-sodium soy sauce
  • hot sauce
  • Dijon mustard
  • balsamic vinegar
  • apple cider vinegar
  • cornstarch

Steps (Cosori dual-basket)

  1. Preheat Cosori to 390°F. Basket A: potatoes. Basket B: salmon (later). Use Sync Finish.
  2. Potatoes (Basket A): halve baby potatoes. Toss with 2 tsp olive oil, 1 tsp garlic powder, 1 tsp smoked paprika, 1/2 tsp kosher salt, and 1/2 tsp black pepper. Air fry 18–22 minutes at 390°F with Shake Reminder on.
  3. Salmon: pat salmon dry. Season with 1 tsp kosher salt, 1 tsp black pepper, 1 tsp garlic powder, and 2 tsp lemon juice. Lightly oil basket.
  4. Salmon (Basket B): add salmon when potatoes have ~10 minutes left. Air fry 8–10 minutes at 390°F until flaky.
  5. Green beans: microwave green beans 4–5 minutes; drain well. Toss with 2 tsp olive oil, 1 tsp garlic powder, black pepper, and a squeeze of lemon. Crisp in Basket A for the last 5–6 minutes if space; otherwise roast 425°F for 10 minutes.
  6. Serve per Notes (extra lemon squeeze on fish).

Notes

Classic crispy salmon plate with lemon-pepper brightness and no heavy sauce. Portions (per person): Frank: 6 oz salmon + 1 cup potatoes + 1 cup green beans Susan: 5 oz protein + 1 cup potatoes + 3/4 cup veg Elise: 5 oz protein + 1 cup potatoes + 3/4 cup veg Tips (recipe-specific): Add salmon late so it doesn’t overcook while potatoes finish. Drain green beans thoroughly so they crisp, not steam.
