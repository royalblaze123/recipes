Creamy Parmesan Herb Dip

Category: Dip • Yield: About 3/4 cup (14 tbsp) • AnyList-ready

Ingredients

  • 3/4 cup 2% plain Greek yogurt
  • 6 tsp grated Parmesan cheese
  • 2 tsp extra-virgin olive oil
  • 1 tsp dried Italian seasoning
  • 1/2 tsp garlic powder
  • 1/4 tsp black pepper

Steps

  1. Whisk Greek yogurt, Parmesan, olive oil, Italian seasoning, garlic powder, and black pepper until smooth.
  2. Chill 10 minutes before serving.
