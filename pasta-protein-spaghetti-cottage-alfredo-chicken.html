Protein+ Spaghetti Cottage Alfredo Chicken

Nutrition (approx)

Frank: 564 kcal • P 78g • C 43g • F 11g
Susan/Elise (each): 423 kcal • P 58g • C 32g • F 8g

Ingredients (purchase-clean)

  • 1/3 box Barilla Protein+ spaghetti
  • 1.25 lb boneless skinless chicken breast
  • 1 tub 1% cottage cheese
  • 3 tbsp grated parmesan
  • 2 tsp garlic powder
  • 2 tsp Italian seasoning
  • 1 tsp black pepper
  • 1 tsp kosher salt

Steps

  1. Bring a large pot of water to a boil for the Barilla Protein+ spaghetti. (Use enough water to fully cover pasta; do not add water to Ingredients.)
  2. Pat chicken breast dry. Season with 2 tsp garlic powder, 2 tsp Italian seasoning, 1 tsp black pepper, and 1 tsp kosher salt. Sear in a nonstick skillet over medium-high 4–5 minutes per side. Reduce to medium, cover, and cook 3–5 minutes more until 165°F. Rest 5 minutes, then chop.
  3. If using cottage-cheese sauce: blend cottage cheese with 2–4 tbsp hot pasta water until smooth; stir into the skillet over low heat until just warmed (do not boil).
  4. Cook Barilla Protein+ spaghetti to al dente per box time. Reserve 1 cup pasta water, then drain.
  5. Toss pasta into the sauce/skillet. Add reserved pasta water 1–2 tbsp at a time until the sauce clings well.
  6. Portion into bowls using the Portions guidance in Notes.

Notes

Alfredo-style without cream: cottage + parmesan + chicken. Built on Barilla Protein+ for a higher-protein pasta plate with close-approx nutrition. Portions (per person): Frank: 2 oz dry Barilla Protein+ spaghetti + 1/3 of the skillet/sauce Susan: 1.5 oz dry Barilla Protein+ spaghetti + 1/4 of the skillet/sauce Elise: 1.5 oz dry Barilla Protein+ spaghetti + 1/4 of the skillet/sauce
