Turkey Cabbage Roll Soup (Stovetop, Low Carb)

NOTES: Designed as a complete meal soup using lean protein, high-fiber vegetables, and controlled starch. Use low-sodium broth and avoid extra salt when possible to support blood pressure and kidney health. PORTION SIZES: Frank: largest portion (~2 cups); Susan: medium portion (~1.5 cups); Elise: medium portion (~1.5 cups).

Ingredients

  • 1 lb 93% lean ground turkey
  • 1 tbsp extra-virgin olive oil
  • 1 medium yellow onion, diced
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 4 cups green cabbage, thinly sliced
  • 1 medium carrot, diced
  • 1 red bell pepper, diced
  • 1 can (14.5 oz) no-salt-added diced tomatoes
  • 1 can (8 oz) no-salt-added tomato sauce
  • 5 cups low-sodium chicken broth
  • 1/2 cup cooked brown rice (optional, for a bit of starch)
  • 1 tsp dried oregano
  • 1 tsp dried basil
  • 1/2 tsp smoked paprika
  • 1/2 tsp kosher salt (or to taste)
  • Freshly ground black pepper, to taste

Step-by-Step Instructions

  1. Heat olive oil in a large soup pot over medium heat.
  2. Add ground turkey and cook 6–8 minutes, breaking into crumbles, until no pink remains. Transfer to a plate and set aside.
  3. In the same pot, add diced onion and cook 3–4 minutes until starting to soften.
  4. Stir in minced garlic and cook 30–60 seconds until fragrant.
  5. Add sliced cabbage, diced carrot, and diced red bell pepper. Cook 5–6 minutes, stirring occasionally, until vegetables begin to soften.
  6. Stir in diced tomatoes with juices, tomato sauce, and low-sodium chicken broth.
  7. Add dried oregano, dried basil, smoked paprika, salt, and black pepper and stir.
  8. Return cooked turkey to the pot and stir to combine.
  9. Bring to a gentle boil, then reduce heat to low and simmer 25–30 minutes, stirring occasionally, until cabbage is very tender.
  10. Stir in cooked brown rice (if using) and simmer 5 more minutes to warm through.
  11. Taste and adjust seasoning with a small pinch more salt or pepper if needed.
  12. Remove from heat, let rest a few minutes, then ladle into bowls for Frank, Susan, and Elise.

Nutrition per Person

  • Frank: ~440 kcal, ~40 g protein, ~35 g carbs, ~12 g fat.
  • Susan: ~330 kcal, ~30 g protein, ~26 g carbs, ~9 g fat.
  • Elise: ~330 kcal, ~30 g protein, ~26 g carbs, ~9 g fat.
