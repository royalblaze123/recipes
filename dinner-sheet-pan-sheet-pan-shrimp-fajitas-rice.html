Sheet-Pan Shrimp Fajitas + Rice

Nutrition (approx)

Frank: 650 kcal • P 55g • C 70g • F 16g • Sat 3g • Na 700mg • Fiber 10g
Susan/Elise (each): 488 kcal • P 41g • C 52g • F 12g • Sat 2g • Na 525mg • Fiber 8g

Ingredients (purchase-clean)

  • 1.50 lb shrimp
  • 1 bag frozen fajita-style pepper blend
  • 1 cup dry brown rice
  • 3 each whole-wheat tortillas
  • 1 each lime
  • 1 jar salsa
  • kosher salt
  • black pepper
  • garlic powder
  • onion powder
  • smoked paprika
  • Italian seasoning
  • ground cumin
  • chili powder
  • red pepper flakes
  • extra-virgin olive oil
  • Dijon mustard
  • balsamic vinegar
  • apple cider vinegar
  • low-sodium soy sauce
  • cornstarch
  • hot sauce

Steps

  1. Rice: cook 1 cup dry brown rice; rest 5 minutes.
  2. Oven: heat to 450°F. Line sheet pan.
  3. Peppers: toss frozen pepper blend with 2 tsp olive oil, chili powder, cumin, garlic powder, 1/2 tsp kosher salt, black pepper. Roast 16 minutes, stirring once.
  4. Shrimp: toss shrimp with 2 tsp olive oil, chili powder, cumin, garlic powder, 1/2 tsp kosher salt, black pepper, and 2 tsp lime juice.
  5. Add shrimp to pan; roast 6–8 minutes until pink.
  6. Tortillas: warm on skillet 20–30 seconds per side.
  7. Assemble: 2 tbsp salsa per taco (measured) and serve with rice per Notes.

Notes

Fast fajitas: roast peppers hard first, add shrimp last so it stays juicy. Portions (per person): Frank: 3 tacos + 1 cup cooked rice Susan: 3 tacos + 3/4 cup cooked rice Elise: 3 tacos + 3/4 cup cooked rice Tips (recipe-specific): Roast peppers longer than you think—moisture needs to cook off. Salsa is the moisture layer; measure it but use it.
