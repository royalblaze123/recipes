Balsamic Chicken + Potatoes + Carrots

Nutrition (approx)

Frank: 640 kcal • P 60g • C 60g • F 14g
Susan/Elise (each): 480 kcal • P 45g • C 45g • F 10g

Ingredients (purchase-clean)

  • 1.75 lb boneless skinless chicken breast
  • 1.50 lb baby potatoes
  • 1 lb carrots
  • balsamic vinegar
  • low-sodium chicken broth
  • kosher salt
  • black pepper
  • garlic powder
  • onion powder
  • smoked paprika
  • Italian seasoning
  • ground cumin
  • chili powder
  • red pepper flakes
  • extra-virgin olive oil
  • low-sodium chicken broth
  • low-sodium beef broth
  • low-sodium soy sauce
  • hot sauce
  • Dijon mustard
  • balsamic vinegar
  • apple cider vinegar
  • cornstarch

Steps (slow cooker)

  1. Add potatoes + carrots + broth (1/2 cup).
  2. Chicken on top; season garlic powder, pepper; modest salt.
  3. Add balsamic (3 tbsp measured) + Dijon (1 tsp measured) into liquid; stir around veg.
  4. LOW 4–5 hr or HIGH 2–3 hr to 165°F.
  5. Remove chicken; reduce HIGH lid ajar 15 min to glaze.
  6. Serve per Notes.

Notes

Cozy balsamic dinner—reduce at the end so it’s glaze-like, not brothy. Portions (per person): Frank: 7 oz chicken + 1 cup potatoes + 1 cup carrots/glaze Susan: 5 oz chicken + 3/4 cup potatoes + 3/4 cup carrots/glaze Elise: 5 oz chicken + 3/4 cup potatoes + 3/4 cup carrots/glaze Tips (recipe-specific): Reduce at the end to concentrate flavor. Use LOW for firmer potatoes.
