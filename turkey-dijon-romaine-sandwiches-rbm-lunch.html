Turkey Dijon Romaine Sandwiches - RBM Lunch

Extra-lean, extra-protein turkey with yogurt Dijon and crunchy romaine plus a cucumber and carrot side.

Portions (per person):

Frank – 4 sandwich halves (2 sandwiches) + veg side

 

Susan – 3 sandwich halves (1.5 sandwiches) + veg side

 

Elise – 3 sandwich halves (1.5 sandwiches) + veg side

Ingredients

  • 10 slices thin whole wheat bread
  • 20 slices deli turkey
  • 1 container plain Greek yogurt
  • 1 bottle Dijon mustard
  • 1 romaine heart
  • 1 cucumber
  • 1 lb bag baby carrots
  • 1 bottle olive oil spray

Step-by-step Instructions

  1. Deli order: ask for turkey sliced thin for sandwiches not shaved.
  2. Wash the romaine heart. Chop enough to measure about 4 cups chopped romaine for the sandwiches; refrigerate remaining romaine if any.
  3. Wash the cucumber. Slice enough to make 30 thin cucumber rounds for the sandwiches; reserve remaining cucumber for the veg sides.
  4. Make the spread: in a small bowl, mix 10 tablespoons plain Greek yogurt with 4 tablespoons Dijon mustard until smooth.
  5. Toast option: lightly mist 10 bread slices with olive oil spray and toast 2–4 minutes, flipping once, until lightly crisp.
  6. Assemble 5 sandwiches: spread the yogurt Dijon on 5 bread slices. Layer romaine, cucumber rounds, and 4 slices deli turkey per sandwich. Top with remaining bread slices.
  7. Cut each sandwich in half to make 10 halves. Portion: Frank gets 4 halves; Susan 3 halves; Elise 3 halves.
  8. Veg sides: measure 1 cup cucumber slices for Frank and 3/4 cup for Susan and Elise. Measure baby carrots: 1/2 cup for Frank and 1/3 cup for Susan and Elise. Pack separately.
