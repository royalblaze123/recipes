Roast Beef Swiss Dijon Sandwich

Servings

3 sandwiches (Frank 1.0×, Susan 0.75×, Elise 0.75×).

Times

  • Prep time: 13 minutes
  • Cook time: 0 minutes
  • Total time: 13 minutes

Ingredients

  • 6 slices whole wheat bread
  • 19 slices deli roast beef
  • 3 slices reduced-fat Swiss cheese
  • 9 tsp light mayonnaise
  • 6 tsp Dijon mustard
  • 1/3 package romaine hearts
  • 1 onion

Recipe

  1. Rinse and dry 1/3 package romaine hearts; pull 6 large leaves (2 per sandwich). Store the remaining romaine hearts package sealed in the fridge.
  2. Slice 1 onion into very thin rings; use about 6–8 rings per sandwich and store remaining onion wrapped in the fridge.
  3. Frank sandwich: spread 4 tsp light mayonnaise + 2 tsp Dijon mustard across 2 bread slices; add 9 slices deli roast beef + 1 slice Swiss + 2 romaine leaves + onion rings; close and wrap.
  4. Susan sandwich: spread 2 1/2 tsp light mayonnaise + 2 tsp Dijon mustard across 2 bread slices; add 5 slices deli roast beef + 1 slice Swiss + 2 romaine leaves + onion rings; close and wrap.
  5. Elise sandwich: spread 2 1/2 tsp light mayonnaise + 2 tsp Dijon mustard across 2 bread slices; add 5 slices deli roast beef + 1 slice Swiss + 2 romaine leaves + onion rings; close and wrap.

Notes

Portions (per person):

Frank – 1 sandwich (9 roast beef slices, 1 Swiss slice, 4 tsp mayo, 2 tsp Dijon).

Susan – 1 sandwich (5 roast beef slices, 1 Swiss slice, 2 1/2 tsp mayo, 2 tsp Dijon).

Elise – 1 sandwich (5 roast beef slices, 1 Swiss slice, 2 1/2 tsp mayo, 2 tsp Dijon).

Tips (recipe-specific):

Ask deli for medium-thin sandwich slices (not shaved) so the sandwich doesn’t get watery.

If onion is too sharp, rinse the rings under cold water for 10 seconds and pat dry.

  • Susan: 394 kcal; Protein 37 g; Carbs 30 g; Fat 14 g; Fiber 5 g; Sodium 910 mg
  • Elise: 394 kcal; Protein 37 g; Carbs 30 g; Fat 14 g; Fiber 5 g; Sodium 910 mg
