Freezer Chicken Tikka-Style Tomato Bake (No Yogurt)

Nutrition (approx)

Frank: 650 kcal • P 60g • C 70g • F 14g • Sat 3g • Na 700mg • Fiber 10g
Susan/Elise (each): 488 kcal • P 45g • C 52g • F 10g • Sat 2g • Na 525mg • Fiber 8g

Ingredients (purchase-clean)

  • 1.75 lb boneless skinless chicken breast
  • 2 cans no-salt-added diced tomatoes
  • 1 each onion
  • 1 carton low-sodium chicken broth
  • cornstarch
  • 1 cup dry brown rice
  • kosher salt
  • black pepper
  • garlic powder
  • onion powder
  • smoked paprika
  • Italian seasoning
  • ground cumin
  • chili powder
  • red pepper flakes
  • extra-virgin olive oil
  • low-sodium chicken broth
  • cornstarch

Steps

  1. Rice: cook 1 cup dry brown rice; rest 5 minutes.
  2. Oven: heat to 400°F.
  3. Chicken: sauté chicken with olive oil, garlic powder, onion powder, smoked paprika, chili powder, black pepper, and a modest pinch of kosher salt 6–8 minutes.
  4. Sauce: add 2 cans diced tomatoes (14.5 oz each) and 1 cup broth. Simmer uncovered 12 minutes to thicken.
  5. Thicken: whisk 1 tbsp cornstarch into 1/4 cup cold water; stir in; simmer 2 minutes until sauce coats a spoon.
  6. Bake: transfer to a dish; bake 18 minutes until bubbling; rest 10 minutes.
  7. Freeze: cool; portion chicken sauce and rice separately if possible; freeze. Reheat until piping hot.

Notes

Tikka-style comfort without yogurt—thick tomato sauce and spices carry it. Portions (per person): Frank: About 2 1/2 cups bowl (rice + chicken sauce) Susan: About 2 cups bowl Elise: About 2 cups bowl Tips (recipe-specific): Freeze sauce separate from rice for best texture. Simmer uncovered first so it reheats thick, not watery.
