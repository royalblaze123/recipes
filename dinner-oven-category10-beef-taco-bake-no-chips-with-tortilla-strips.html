Beef Taco Bake (No Chips) with Tortilla Strips

Nutrition (approx)

Frank: 650 kcal • P 55g • C 65g • F 20g
Susan/Elise (each): 488 kcal • P 41g • C 49g • F 15g

Ingredients (purchase-clean)

  • 1.50 lb 90% lean ground beef
  • 2 cans no-salt-added diced tomatoes
  • 1 can black beans
  • 6 each whole-wheat tortillas
  • 1 jar salsa
  • 6 oz reduced-fat cheddar cheese
  • low-sodium beef broth
  • kosher salt
  • black pepper
  • garlic powder
  • onion powder
  • smoked paprika
  • Italian seasoning
  • ground cumin
  • chili powder
  • red pepper flakes
  • extra-virgin olive oil
  • low-sodium chicken broth
  • low-sodium beef broth
  • low-sodium soy sauce
  • hot sauce
  • Dijon mustard
  • balsamic vinegar
  • apple cider vinegar
  • cornstarch

Steps (oven)

  1. Oven: heat to 400°F.
  2. Beef: brown with chili powder, cumin, garlic powder, black pepper, modest salt (8–10 minutes).
  3. Base: stir in tomatoes (2x 14.5 oz) + salsa (1 cup measured) + broth (1 cup). Simmer 3 minutes to thicken.
  4. Beans: add drained/rinsed black beans (1 1/2 cups).
  5. Layer: tear tortillas into strips; fold half into beef mix. Pour into dish. Top with remaining tortilla strips and measured cheese.
  6. Bake: 400°F 18–22 minutes. Rest 10 minutes.
  7. Serve per Notes.

Notes

Nacho-ish bake without chips: tortillas give chew and structure; cheese is measured. Portions (per person): Frank: About 2 1/2 cups bake Susan: About 2 cups bake Elise: About 2 cups bake Tips (recipe-specific): Rest so it slices and doesn’t weep. Simmer base first so bake stays thick.
