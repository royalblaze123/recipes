Lemon-Herb Air Fryer Chicken with Quinoa and Broccoli

Servings

3 plates (Frank 1.0×, Susan 0.75×, Elise 0.75×).

Times

  • Prep time: 15 minutes
  • Cook time: 25 minutes
  • Total time: 40 minutes

Ingredients

  • 2.0 lb chicken breast
  • 1.0 cup dry quinoa
  • 1 bag frozen broccoli florets
  • 12 tsp olive oil
  • 6 tsp lemon juice
  • 4 tsp Dijon mustard
  • 6 tsp minced garlic
  • 4 tsp dried oregano
  • 3 tsp dried thyme
  • 2 tsp black pepper
  • 2 tsp salt substitute

Steps

  1. Quinoa: Rinse 1 cup dry quinoa. Cook with 2 cups water (or low-sodium broth) to yield ~3 cups cooked. Fluff and keep warm.
  2. Chicken: Slice chicken breast into even cutlets. Mix 4 tsp olive oil + 6 tsp lemon juice + 4 tsp Dijon mustard + 4 tsp minced garlic + 4 tsp dried oregano + 3 tsp dried thyme + 2 tsp black pepper + 2 tsp salt substitute. Coat chicken.
  3. COSORI Dual Air Fryer: Preheat both baskets to 380°F for 3 minutes.
  4. Basket A (chicken): Arrange chicken in a single layer. Cook at 380°F for 10 minutes, flip, then 6–8 minutes more to 165°F internal. Use the shake reminder as your flip cue. Rest 3 minutes.
  5. Basket B (broccoli): Toss the full bag of frozen broccoli with 8 tsp olive oil + 2 tsp minced garlic + 1 tsp black pepper + 1 tsp salt substitute. Cook at 390°F for 10–12 minutes, shaking once, until hot with crisp edges.
  6. Plate portions: Frank gets 8 oz cooked chicken + 1 cup cooked quinoa + 1 1/2 cups broccoli. Susan and Elise each get 6 oz cooked chicken + 3/4 cup quinoa + 1 1/4 cups broccoli.

Notes

Portions (per person):

Frank – 1 plate (8 oz cooked chicken, 1 cup cooked quinoa, 1 1/2 cups broccoli).

Susan – 1 plate (6 oz cooked chicken, 3/4 cup cooked quinoa, 1 1/4 cups broccoli).

Elise – 1 plate (6 oz cooked chicken, 3/4 cup cooked quinoa, 1 1/4 cups broccoli).

Tips (recipe-specific):

Cut chicken to even thickness so it finishes juicy without overcooking.

If broccoli steams, spread it thinner and add 2 minutes at the end for edge-crisp.

Storage + Reheat

  • Cool promptly; store in airtight containers in the fridge up to 3–4 days (typical safety guidance).
  • For best texture, reheat protein + veg in the air fryer at 350°F for 4–6 minutes, and reheat grains/beans in the microwave 60–90 seconds with a splash of water.
