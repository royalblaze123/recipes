Cheeseburger Jalapeño Poppers

Sheet Pan • Yield: 24 popper halves

Ingredients (purchase-clean)

  • 12 each fresh jalapeños
  • 16 oz fresh 93% lean ground beef
  • 6 oz reduced-fat cream cheese
  • 2 oz reduced-fat shredded cheddar
  • 2 tsp mustard
  • kosher salt
  • black pepper
  • onion powder

Steps

  1. Heat oven to 425°F. Line a sheet pan with parchment.
  2. Brown beef: cook 16 oz lean ground beef with 1 tsp kosher salt, 1 tsp onion powder, and 1/2 tsp black pepper for 7–9 min; drain; cool 5 min.
  3. Halve and seed 12 jalapeños; pat dry.
  4. Mix filling: stir softened 6 oz cream cheese + 2 tsp mustard into beef; fold in 2 oz cheddar.
  5. Stuff halves; arrange cut-side up.
  6. Bake 12–15 min until peppers are tender-crisp and centers are hot; broil 1 min for browning if desired.

Notes

Flavor-forward holiday poppers designed for snacking or a light meal. Portions (guidance): Serve with sugar-free ketchup on the side if you want the classic vibe (optional). Snack portion: 4–6 halves.
