Full-Flavor Chicken Tortilla Soup with Lime and Avocado

Prep: 15 min   Cook: 30 min   Total: 45 min

Ingredients

  • 1.5 lb chicken breast
  • 0.75 carton low-sodium chicken broth
  • 0.5 can no-salt-added diced tomatoes
  • 0.5 can black beans
  • 0.5 bag frozen corn
  • 1 onion
  • 1 bell pepper
  • 1 lime
  • 1 bunch cilantro
  • 1 avocado
  • 6 tortillas whole wheat
  • olive oil
  • tomato paste
  • chipotle in adobo
  • cumin
  • chili powder
  • smoked paprika
  • garlic powder
  • black pepper
  • salt substitute

RBM rules: quantities appear before items; fresh proteins use weight. Condiments/spices/seasonings are listed name-only; all measurements are in Steps. Water is only referenced in Steps.

Step-by-step Instructions

  1. Dice onion and bell pepper. Mince 2 garlic cloves (from your garlic bulb if you have one) or use garlic powder in steps below.
  2. Heat a large pot over medium-high. Add 3 tsp olive oil. Add onion + bell pepper; cook 6–8 minutes until softened and lightly browned.
  3. Stir in 2 tbsp tomato paste + 1–2 tsp chipotle in adobo + 2 tsp cumin + 2 tsp chili powder + 1 tsp smoked paprika + 1 tsp black pepper + 2 tsp salt substitute. Cook 60 seconds to bloom spices.
  4. Add 0.75 carton broth (24 fl oz). Add 0.5 can diced tomatoes. Add chicken breast whole. Bring to a simmer, cover, and simmer 14–16 minutes until chicken reaches 165°F.
  5. Remove chicken to a plate; shred. Return shredded chicken to the pot. Add 0.5 can black beans (drained/rinsed) and 0.5 bag frozen corn. Simmer 6–8 minutes.
  6. Make tortilla strips (Cosori air fryer): stack 6 whole wheat tortillas, slice into thin strips. Toss strips with 3 tsp olive oil + 1 tsp salt substitute. Air fry at 370°F for 6–8 minutes, shaking at the reminder, until crisp.
  7. Finish soup: add juice of 1 lime. Stir in 1/2 cup chopped cilantro.
  8. Portion: Frank gets ~3 1/2 cups soup + 2 oz tortilla strips (about 2 tortillas worth) + 1/2 avocado. Susan and Elise each get ~2 3/4 cups soup + 1.5 oz strips (about 1.5 tortillas worth) + 1/4 avocado each.
