Italian Parmesan Meatloaf Plate

Nutrition (approx)

Frank: 625 kcal • P 55g • C 34g • F 24g
Susan/Elise (each): 469 kcal • P 41g • C 26g • F 18g

Ingredients (purchase-clean)

  • 1.50 lb 93% lean ground beef
  • 1 each yellow onion
  • 2 each large eggs
  • 3/4 cup whole wheat panko breadcrumbs
  • 4 tbsp grated parmesan
  • 2 tsp Italian seasoning
  • 2 tsp garlic powder
  • 6 tsp marinara sauce
  • 1 tsp black pepper
  • 1 tsp kosher salt
  • 1.50 lb baby carrots
  • 12 oz fresh asparagus
  • 4 tsp extra-virgin olive oil

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 400°F. Line a sheet pan with parchment.
  2. Dice the yellow onion very small.
  3. Meatloaf mix: combine ground beef, onion, eggs, panko, parmesan, Italian seasoning, garlic powder, black pepper, and kosher salt. Mix just until combined.
  4. Form a loaf on the sheet pan. Bake 35 minutes.
  5. Carrots: toss baby carrots with 2 tsp olive oil and spread on the pan. Roast 25–30 minutes, turning once.
  6. Asparagus: trim woody ends; toss with 2 tsp olive oil. Add for the last 10–12 minutes until tender-crisp.
  7. Marinara dip: warm 6 tsp marinara in a small bowl in the microwave 30–45 seconds for dipping.
  8. Meatloaf doneness: bake until the center reaches 160°F. Rest 10 minutes before slicing.

Notes

Italian-seasoned meatloaf with parmesan and a small marinara dip for extra flavor.
