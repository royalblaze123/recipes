Chicken Shawarma Sheet Pan + Sweet Potatoes + Broccoli

Nutrition (approx)

Frank: 650 kcal • P 60g • C 55g • F 18g • Sat 3g • Na 700mg • Fiber 10g
Susan/Elise (each): 488 kcal • P 45g • C 41g • F 14g • Sat 2g • Na 525mg • Fiber 8g

Ingredients (purchase-clean)

  • 1.75 lb boneless skinless chicken breast
  • 2.00 lb sweet potatoes
  • 1 bag frozen broccoli florets
  • 1 each lemon
  • kosher salt
  • black pepper
  • garlic powder
  • onion powder
  • smoked paprika
  • Italian seasoning
  • ground cumin
  • chili powder
  • red pepper flakes
  • extra-virgin olive oil
  • Dijon mustard
  • balsamic vinegar
  • apple cider vinegar
  • low-sodium soy sauce
  • cornstarch
  • hot sauce

Steps

  1. Oven: heat to 450°F. Line sheet pan.
  2. Sweet potatoes: cube; toss with 2 tsp olive oil, cumin, smoked paprika, garlic powder, 1/2 tsp kosher salt, black pepper. Roast 12 minutes.
  3. Chicken: cut into chunks; toss with 2 tsp olive oil, 2 tsp cumin, 2 tsp smoked paprika, garlic powder, 1/2 tsp kosher salt, black pepper, and 2 tsp lemon juice.
  4. Add chicken; roast 10 minutes.
  5. Broccoli: microwave; drain/pat dry. Toss with 2 tsp olive oil, garlic powder, 1/2 tsp kosher salt, black pepper. Add; roast 10 minutes.
  6. Serve with extra lemon squeeze per Notes.

Notes

Street-food flavor via shawarma spices + high-heat roasting. Portions (per person): Frank: 7 oz chicken + 1 cup sweet potatoes + 1 1/2 cups broccoli Susan: 5 oz protein + 3/4 cup starch + 1 cup veg Elise: 5 oz protein + 3/4 cup starch + 1 cup veg Tips (recipe-specific): Use 450°F to get browning fast without drying chicken. Pat broccoli dry so it roasts, not steams.
