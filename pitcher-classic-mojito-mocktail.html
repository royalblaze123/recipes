Classic Mojito Mocktail (Pitcher Size)

Yield: 8 drinks • Prep: 10 min • Total: 10 min

Ingredients (AnyList-ready)

  • 80 mint leaves
  • 8 limes
  • 8 oz simple syrup
  • 48 oz club soda
  • 8 cups ice

Steps

  1. In a large pitcher, add 80 mint leaves and 8 oz simple syrup.
  2. Juice limes to get 12 oz lime juice (about 8 limes). Pour the juice into the pitcher.
  3. Gently muddle the mint in the pitcher for 20–30 presses (do not shred).
  4. Chill the base 15 minutes if possible.
  5. To serve: fill each glass with ice, pour in about 6 oz of the mojito base, then top each glass with about 6 oz club soda. Stir once, gently.

Notes

If you prefer it less sweet, use 6 oz simple syrup and increase club soda per glass.

Batch rule: keep carbonation separate until serving for the best fizz.
