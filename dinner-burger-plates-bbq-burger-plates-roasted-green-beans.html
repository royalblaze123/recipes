BBQ Burger Plates + Roasted Green Beans

Nutrition (approx)

Frank: 640 kcal • P 58g • C 55g • F 18g • Sat 7g • Na 690mg • Fiber 10g
Susan/Elise (each): 480 kcal • P 44g • C 41g • F 14g • Sat 5g • Na 518mg • Fiber 8g

Ingredients (purchase-clean)

  • 1.75 lb 93% lean ground beef
  • 4 each whole-wheat burger buns
  • 1 bottle low-sugar BBQ sauce
  • 1 bag frozen green beans
  • 1 each onion
  • kosher salt
  • black pepper
  • garlic powder
  • onion powder
  • smoked paprika
  • dried oregano
  • extra-virgin olive oil
  • mustard
  • ketchup
  • pickle relish
  • hot sauce
  • Worcestershire sauce
  • apple cider vinegar

Steps

  1. Oven: heat to 425°F. Roast frozen green beans with 2 tsp olive oil, 1/4 tsp kosher salt, black pepper for 14–18 minutes.
  2. Patties: season beef with 2 tsp garlic powder, 1 tsp onion powder, 1 tsp smoked paprika, 1/2 tsp kosher salt, 1/2 tsp black pepper. Form 6 patties.
  3. Skillet: cook patties 4–5 minutes per side to 160°F.
  4. BBQ glaze: warm 2 tbsp low-sugar BBQ sauce + 2 tsp apple cider vinegar. Brush on patties (split across all patties).
  5. Assemble: buns + sliced onion + mustard/hot sauce. Serve with green beans. Portion per Notes.

Notes

BBQ burger comfort with a measured glaze and a roasted veg side. Portions (per person): Frank: 2 patties + 1 cup green beans side + toppings Susan: 1 patty + 3/4 cup green beans side + toppings Elise: 1 patty + 3/4 cup green beans side + toppings Tips (recipe-specific): Measured BBQ keeps sugar/sodium in range; vinegar makes it pop. Roast green beans hard for browning (flavor) instead of butter.
