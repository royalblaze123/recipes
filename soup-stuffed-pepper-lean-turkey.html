Lean Turkey Stuffed Pepper Soup (Stovetop)

NOTES: Designed as a complete meal soup using lean protein, high-fiber vegetables, and controlled starch. Use low-sodium broth and avoid extra salt when possible to support blood pressure and kidney health. PORTION SIZES: Frank: largest portion (~2 cups); Susan: medium portion (~1.5 cups); Elise: medium portion (~1.5 cups).

Ingredients

  • 1 lb 93% lean ground turkey
  • 1 tbsp extra-virgin olive oil
  • 1 medium yellow onion, diced
  • 1 green bell pepper, diced
  • 1 red bell pepper, diced
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 cup cooked brown rice (cooled)
  • 1 can (14.5 oz) no-salt-added diced tomatoes
  • 1 can (8 oz) no-salt-added tomato sauce
  • 4 cups low-sodium chicken broth
  • 1 tsp dried oregano
  • 1 tsp dried basil
  • 1/2 tsp smoked paprika
  • 1/4 tsp crushed red pepper flakes (optional)
  • 1/2 tsp kosher salt (or to taste)
  • Freshly ground black pepper, to taste
  • Fresh parsley, chopped, for garnish (optional)

Step-by-Step Instructions

  1. Heat olive oil in a large soup pot over medium heat.
  2. Add diced onion and cook 3–4 minutes until beginning to soften.
  3. Add diced green and red bell peppers and cook another 3–4 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  4. Stir in minced garlic and cook 30–60 seconds until fragrant, stirring constantly.
  5. Add ground turkey and cook 5–7 minutes, breaking into small crumbles, until no pink remains.
  6. Stir in dried oregano, dried basil, smoked paprika, crushed red pepper (if using), salt, and black pepper.
  7. Pour in diced tomatoes with juices, tomato sauce, and low-sodium chicken broth. Stir well.
  8. Bring to a gentle boil, then reduce heat to low and simmer uncovered 15–20 minutes, stirring occasionally, until peppers are tender.
  9. Stir in cooked brown rice and simmer 5 more minutes to warm through and slightly thicken.
  10. Taste and adjust seasoning with a small pinch more salt or pepper if needed, keeping sodium in mind.
  11. Remove from heat and let rest 3–5 minutes, then ladle into bowls according to Frank, Susan, and Elise portions.
  12. Garnish with chopped fresh parsley before serving, if desired.

Nutrition per Person

  • Frank: ~480 kcal, ~40 g protein, ~48 g carbs, ~13 g fat.
  • Susan: ~360 kcal, ~30 g protein, ~36 g carbs, ~10 g fat.
  • Elise: ~360 kcal, ~30 g protein, ~36 g carbs, ~10 g fat.
