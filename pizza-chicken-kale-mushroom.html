Chicken Kale Mushroom Pizza

NOTES: Built as a full dinner using ready-made whole-grain or thin crust, lean protein, plenty of vegetables, and controlled cheese to support A1c, cholesterol, and blood pressure goals. Use low-sodium sauce and minimal added salt. PORTION SIZES: Frank: ~40% of the pizza; Susan: ~30% of the pizza; Elise: ~30% of the pizza (Frank = 1.0×, Susan/Elise = 0.75× portions).

Ingredients

  • 1 ready-made 12-inch thin whole-wheat pizza crust
  • 1/2 cup no-salt-added tomato or pizza sauce
  • 1 tsp olive oil
  • 1 cup sliced mushrooms
  • 2 cups chopped kale (stems removed)
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • 8 oz cooked boneless, skinless chicken breast, chopped
  • 3/4 cup shredded reduced-fat mozzarella cheese
  • 1 tsp dried oregano
  • Freshly ground black pepper, to taste

Step-by-Step Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 425°F and place the crust on a pizza pan or parchment-lined baking sheet.
  2. Heat olive oil in a skillet over medium heat. Add sliced mushrooms and cook 3–4 minutes.
  3. Add chopped kale and minced garlic to the skillet and cook 2–3 minutes, stirring, until kale is wilted. Remove from heat.
  4. Spread tomato or pizza sauce evenly over the crust.
  5. Top with chopped chicken, sautéed mushrooms and kale mixture.
  6. Sprinkle shredded mozzarella, dried oregano, and black pepper over the top.
  7. Bake 12–15 minutes, until the crust is crisp and the cheese is melted and lightly browned.
  8. Cool 3–5 minutes before slicing and portioning for Frank (~40%) and Susan/Elise (~30% each).

Nutrition per Person

  • Frank: ~590 kcal, ~52 g protein, ~48 g carbs, ~17 g fat.
  • Susan: ~445 kcal, ~39 g protein, ~36 g carbs, ~13 g fat.
  • Elise: ~445 kcal, ~39 g protein, ~36 g carbs, ~13 g fat.
