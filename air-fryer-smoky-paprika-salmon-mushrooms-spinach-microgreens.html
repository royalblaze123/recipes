Air Fryer Smoky Paprika Salmon with Sautéed Mushrooms and Spinach-Microgreens Salad

Prep: 12 min   Cook: 18 min   Total: 30 min

Ingredients

  • 1.25 lb salmon fillets
  • 1 package baby bella mushrooms
  • 1 bag spinach
  • 1 bag microgreens
  • 1 pouch microwave brown rice
  • 1 lemon
  • olive oil
  • smoked paprika
  • garlic powder
  • black pepper
  • salt substitute
  • Dijon mustard
  • balsamic vinegar

RBM rules: quantities appear before items; fresh proteins use weight. Condiments/spices/seasonings are listed name-only; all measurements are in Steps.

Step-by-step Instructions

  1. Sauté mushrooms: heat a nonstick skillet over medium-high. Add 6 tsp olive oil and sliced mushrooms; cook 7–9 minutes until browned and moisture cooks off. Season with 2 tsp salt substitute + 1 tsp smoked paprika + 1 tsp garlic powder + 1 tsp black pepper.
  2. Microwave the brown rice pouch per package directions; fluff.
  3. Season salmon: pat dry. Mix 4 tsp olive oil + 4 tsp smoked paprika + 2 tsp garlic powder + 1 tsp black pepper; rub over salmon.
  4. Air fry salmon (Cosori): preheat 390°F for 3 minutes. Cook skin-side down 8–11 minutes until flaky and cooked to preference.
  5. Make dressing: whisk 4 tsp Dijon + 6 tsp balsamic vinegar + 4 tsp lemon juice + 1 tsp black pepper. Toss with spinach and microgreens.
  6. Portion: Frank gets 6 oz salmon + ~3/4 cup cooked rice + ~1 1/2 cups mushrooms + ~2 cups salad. Susan and Elise each get ~4.5 oz salmon + ~1/2 cup rice + ~1 1/4 cups mushrooms + ~1 1/2 cups salad.
