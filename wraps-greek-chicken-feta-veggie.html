Greek Chicken & Feta Veggie Wrap

Greek-inspired high-fiber wrap with chicken, light feta, and crunchy veggies with a lemon-herb Greek-yogurt sauce for a full lunch.

Servings

3 servings.

Times

  • Prep time: 15 minutes
  • Cook time: 0 minutes (uses pre-cooked chicken)
  • Total time: 15 minutes

Ingredients

  • 1 large high-fiber whole-wheat tortilla
  • 2 medium high-fiber whole-wheat tortillas
  • 10 oz cooked skinless chicken breast
  • 1 1/4 oz reduced-fat feta cheese
  • 6 tbsp 2% plain Greek yogurt
  • 1 tsp extra-virgin olive oil
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice
  • 1/2 tsp dried oregano
  • 1/2 tsp garlic powder
  • 1/4 tsp ground black pepper
  • 1/8 to 1/4 tsp kosher or sea salt
  • 1 1/2 cups chopped romaine lettuce
  • 1/2 cup halved grape tomatoes
  • 1/2 cup thinly sliced cucumber
  • 1/4 cup thinly sliced red onion

Preparation Steps

  1. Rinse and pat dry the romaine lettuce, grape tomatoes, cucumber, and onion. Chop the romaine, halve the tomatoes, slice the cucumber, and thinly slice the onion (if using).
  2. In a small bowl, whisk together the Greek yogurt, olive oil, lemon juice, dried oregano, garlic powder, black pepper, and a pinch of salt until smooth. Taste and adjust seasoning as desired.
  3. Gently warm each tortilla in a dry skillet over low heat for 15–20 seconds per side, or in the microwave for about 10 seconds, until just flexible.
  4. Place the large (10-inch) tortilla on a cutting board. Spread about 2 tbsp of the lemon-herb yogurt sauce down the center. Layer about 4 oz sliced chicken over the sauce. Add a generous handful of chopped romaine, a small handful of halved tomatoes and cucumber slices, and a light sprinkle of sliced onion if desired. Crumble about 1/2 oz feta over the top.
  5. Fold the sides inward, then roll up tightly from the bottom. Wrap in foil or parchment for a portable lunch and slice in half if desired.
  6. Place one medium (8-inch) tortilla on the board. Spread about 2 tbsp yogurt sauce down the center. Add about 3 oz sliced chicken, a moderate handful of romaine, some tomatoes, cucumber, and a few onion slices if desired. Crumble about 1/3 oz feta over the top.
  7. Fold in the sides and roll firmly from the bottom. Wrap in foil or parchment and slice in half if desired.
  8. Repeat the process with the remaining medium (8-inch) tortilla, using about 2 tbsp yogurt sauce, 3 oz chicken, romaine, tomatoes, cucumber, optional onion, and about 1/3 oz feta.
  9. Serve immediately or refrigerate the wrapped sandwiches for up to 24 hours. Keep tightly wrapped in foil or parchment in a lidded container for work lunches.
