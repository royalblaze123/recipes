Turkey & Sweet Potato Burrito Wrap Plates

Nutrition (approx)

Frank: 650 kcal • P 58g • C 58g • F 18g
Susan/Elise (each): 488 kcal • P 44g • C 44g • F 14g

Ingredients (purchase-clean)

  • 1.50 lb 93% lean ground turkey
  • 1.50 lb sweet potatoes
  • 1 can black beans
  • 1 package large flour tortillas
  • 1 bag spring mix
  • 1 each avocado
  • 1/2 jar salsa
  • 1 each lime
  • 6 tsp extra-virgin olive oil
  • 2 tsp chili powder
  • 2 tsp cumin
  • 2 tsp garlic powder
  • 1 tsp smoked paprika
  • 1 tsp black pepper
  • 1 tsp kosher salt

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 425°F. Line a sheet pan with parchment.
  2. Sweet potatoes: small dice (about 1/2-inch). Toss with 2 tsp olive oil and a pinch of kosher salt. Roast 18–22 minutes, stirring once, until browned and tender.
  3. Turkey: cook ground turkey in a skillet with 2 tsp olive oil 7–9 minutes until browned. Stir in spices for 60 seconds.
  4. Beans: Can size note—use a standard 15 oz can black beans.. Drain/rinse. Use 1 1/2 cups beans; refrigerate remainder.
  5. Combine: fold roasted sweet potatoes into turkey + beans and warm 1 minute.
  6. Tortillas: warm tortillas 25–35 seconds under a damp paper towel.
  7. Assembly: set out 4 tortillas. Build 2 burritos for Frank and 1 each for Susan and Elise using the turkey-sweet potato mix. Spoon salsa inside each burrito before rolling.
  8. Roll and optionally toast in a dry skillet 1–2 minutes per side.
  9. Side salad: toss spring mix with 2 tsp lime juice and 2 tsp olive oil. Add diced cucumber/tomatoes if listed.
  10. Avocado: halve, pit, and dice. Use about 1/2 avocado total; refrigerate remaining avocado with the pit to slow browning.
  11. Serve with extra salsa for dipping and portion per Notes.

Notes

Sweet potato replaces rice: turkey + beans + roasted sweet potato burritos with salad, salsa, and avocado. Portions (per person): Frank: 2 burritos (use 2 tortillas) + 1/3 of the side salad/veg + 1/3 of the avocado + salsa Susan: 1 burrito + 1/4 of the side salad/veg + 1/4 of the avocado + salsa Elise: 1 burrito + 1/4 of the side salad/veg + 1/4 of the avocado + salsa
