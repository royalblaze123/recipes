Flaked Cod Taco Wraps

Nutrition (approx)

Frank: 525 kcal • P 50g • C 50g • F 14g
Susan/Elise (each): 394 kcal • P 38g • C 38g • F 10g

Ingredients (purchase-clean)

  • 1.50 lb cod fillets
  • 1 package large whole-wheat tortillas
  • 1 bag shredded lettuce
  • 1 each avocado
  • 1/2 jar salsa
  • 1 each cucumber
  • 1 bag mini sweet peppers
  • 1 tub plain nonfat Greek yogurt
  • 1 each lime
  • 2 tsp chili powder
  • 2 tsp cumin
  • 2 tsp garlic powder
  • 1 tsp smoked paprika
  • 1 tsp black pepper
  • 1 tsp kosher salt

Steps

  1. Fish: pat cod dry. Rub with 2 tsp olive oil and season with spices. Bake at 425°F for 10–12 minutes until 145°F and flaky. Rest 3 minutes, then flake.
  2. Sauce: mix 1/2 cup Greek yogurt with 2 tsp lime juice and 1 tsp garlic powder.
  3. Wrap build: warm tortillas 15 seconds so they roll easily. Spread the sauce on each tortilla. Add the protein, then add shredded lettuce or slaw mix if listed.
  4. Roll: fold in the sides and roll tightly. Slice in half for serving if desired.
  5. Crunchy side: slice cucumber and sweet peppers into sticks. Sprinkle with black pepper. Serve alongside the wrap.
  6. Avocado: halve, pit, and slice. Use about 1/2 avocado total; refrigerate remaining avocado with the pit to slow browning.
  7. Serve with salsa and portion per Notes.

Notes

Fish taco wraps made easy: flaked cod + yogurt-lime sauce, with salsa/avocado and crunchy veg. Portions (per person): Frank: 1 large wrap + 1/3 of the crunchy veg side + salsa + avocado Susan: 3/4 wrap + 1/4 of the crunchy veg side + salsa + avocado Elise: 3/4 wrap + 1/4 of the crunchy veg side + salsa + avocado
