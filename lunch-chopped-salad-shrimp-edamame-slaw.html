Chopped Salad: Garlic Shrimp and Edamame Crunch Salad

Prep: 18 min   Cook: 10 min   Total: 28 min

Ingredients

  • 1.1 lb shrimp
  • 0.5 bag frozen shelled edamame
  • 0.5 bag broccoli slaw
  • 1 cucumber
  • 1 lime
  • 1 clamshell microgreens
  • olive oil
  • rice vinegar
  • sesame oil
  • garlic
  • ginger
  • black pepper
  • salt substitute
  • low-sodium soy sauce

Rules enforced: Clean AnyList Ingredients (quantities before items; fresh meat/poultry/fish use weights; packaged items use fractional containers when partial). Condiments/spices/seasonings have no measurements in Ingredients; all measurements are in Steps. No olives, no squash, no Brussels sprouts, no zucchini, no tofu/tempeh. Lunch validations: Frank calories 500–550, sat fat ≤6g, sodium ≤650mg, fiber ≥8g, added sugar ≤6g.

Step-by-step Instructions

  1. Edamame: microwave 1/2 bag edamame 3–4 minutes; cool.
  2. Shrimp: pat dry 1.1 lb shrimp. Sauté with 2 tsp olive oil 3 minutes. Season with 1/4 tsp black pepper + 1/4 tsp salt substitute; cool 5 minutes.
  3. Base: combine 1/2 bag broccoli slaw + diced 1 cucumber + 1 clamshell microgreens (divide).
  4. Dressing: whisk 2 tsp rice vinegar + 2 tsp lime juice + 1 tsp sesame oil + 1 tbsp low-sodium soy sauce + 1 tsp minced garlic + 1 tsp grated ginger.
  5. Toss salad with shrimp and edamame.
  6. Portion: Frank largest bowl; Susan/Elise ~75%.
  7. Meal-prep: keep dressing separate until serving for maximum crunch.
