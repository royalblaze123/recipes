Lunch Sandwich Chicken Pesto Mozzarella Pepper

Chicken pesto mozzarella sandwiches with roasted red peppers and spring mix for built-in veg volume.

Servings

3 servings.

Times

  • Prep time: 12 minutes
  • Cook time: 0 minutes
  • Total time: 12 minutes

Ingredients

Main Dish

  • 6 slices whole wheat bread
  • 18 slices deli chicken breast
  • 3 slices mozzarella cheese
  • 2 tbsp pesto
  • 1 jar roasted red peppers
  • 1 bag spring mix
  • 1 each cucumber
  • 1 each lemon
  • 1 jar black pepper
    • 

    Preparation Steps

    1. Set up: lay out 6 slices whole wheat bread (3 sandwiches).
    2. Spread: measure out 2 tbsp pesto for this recipe; refrigerate the rest.
    3. Peppers: drain roasted red peppers. Use about 6 pepper strips total for these sandwiches; refrigerate remaining peppers.
    4. Veg: measure out 6 packed cups spring mix for these sandwiches; refrigerate remaining spring mix.
    5. Slice cucumber; use about 1/2 cucumber for these sandwiches; refrigerate the rest.
    6. Build (Frank): 2 slices bread + 8 slices deli chicken + 1 slice mozzarella + about 1 tbsp pesto + 2 pepper strips + ~2 cups spring mix + cucumber slices + a squeeze of lemon.
    7. Build (Susan): 2 slices bread + 5 slices deli chicken + 1 slice mozzarella + about 1/2 tbsp pesto + 2 pepper strips + ~2 cups spring mix + cucumber slices + a squeeze of lemon.
    8. Build (Elise): 2 slices bread + 5 slices deli chicken + 1 slice mozzarella + about 1/2 tbsp pesto + 2 pepper strips + ~2 cups spring mix + cucumber slices + a squeeze of lemon.
    9. Close sandwiches and wrap for transport.
      10. 

      Notes

      Portions (per person):


      Frank – 1 whole sandwich: 8 slices deli chicken, 1 slice mozzarella, ~1 tbsp pesto, 2 pepper strips, ~2 cups spring mix, cucumber.


      Susan – 1 whole sandwich: 5 slices deli chicken, 1 slice mozzarella, ~1/2 tbsp pesto, 2 pepper strips, ~2 cups spring mix, cucumber.


      Elise – 1 whole sandwich: 5 slices deli chicken, 1 slice mozzarella, ~1/2 tbsp pesto, 2 pepper strips, ~2 cups spring mix, cucumber.


      Tips (recipe-specific):


      Pesto is calorie-dense—measure it so the lunch band stays on target.


      Roasted peppers add flavor without needing salty condiments.


      If the sandwich feels tall, compress spring mix by hand before stacking.
