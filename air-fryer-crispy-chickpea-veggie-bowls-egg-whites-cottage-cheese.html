Air Fryer Crispy Chickpea and Veggie Bowls with Egg Whites and Cottage Cheese

Plant-forward bowls with crispy chickpeas and veggies, boosted with egg whites and cottage cheese for dinner-level protein.

Servings

3 servings (Frank, Susan, Elise).

Times

  • Prep time: 15 minutes
  • Cook time: 15 minutes
  • Total time: 30 minutes

Ingredients (purchase-clean)

  • 1/2 can canned chickpeas
  • 1/2 bag frozen shelled edamame
  • 1/2 carton liquid egg whites
  • 1/2 tub low-fat cottage cheese
  • 1/2 bag frozen cauliflower florets
  • 6 tsp olive oil
  • 3 tsp garlic paste
  • 2 tsp Italian seasoning
  • 2 tsp smoked paprika
  • 1 tsp black pepper
  • 1/2 cucumber

Preparation Steps

  1. Air fry chickpeas: drain and rinse 1/2 can canned chickpeas; pat dry and toss with 2 tsp olive oil, 1 tsp garlic paste, 1 tsp smoked paprika, and 1/2 tsp black pepper; air fry at 400°F for 10–12 minutes, shaking at 6 minutes.
  2. Cook edamame: microwave 1/2 bag frozen shelled edamame with 2 tbsp water for 3–4 minutes; reseal and freeze any remaining edamame.
  3. Cook cauliflower: microwave 1/2 bag frozen cauliflower florets with 2 tbsp water for 4–6 minutes; toss with 2 tsp olive oil, 2 tsp Italian seasoning, 1 tsp garlic paste, and 1 tsp smoked paprika.
  4. Cook egg whites: scramble 1 1/2 cups liquid egg whites in a nonstick skillet over medium heat for 4–5 minutes until set; refrigerate the remaining egg whites in the carton.
  5. Prep cucumber: dice 1/2 cucumber.
  6. Assemble bowls: add cauliflower and cucumber to bowls, then top with chickpeas, edamame, egg whites, and cottage cheese; finish with the remaining 2 tsp olive oil divided across bowls.

Notes

Portions (per person):

Frank — 3/4 cup cooked egg whites; 3/4 cup low-fat cottage cheese; 1/2 cup edamame; 1/3 cup chickpeas; 1 1/2 cups cauliflower; 1/2 cup cucumber.

 

Susan — 1/2 cup cooked egg whites; 1/2 cup low-fat cottage cheese; 1/3 cup edamame; 1/4 cup chickpeas; 1 1/4 cups cauliflower; 1/3 cup cucumber.

 

Elise — 1/2 cup cooked egg whites; 1/2 cup low-fat cottage cheese; 1/3 cup edamame; 1/4 cup chickpeas; 1 1/4 cups cauliflower; 1/3 cup cucumber.

 

Tips (recipe-specific):

 

Crispier chickpeas: dry them thoroughly and don’t overcrowd the basket.

 

Keep sodium low by choosing lower-sodium cottage cheese when available.
