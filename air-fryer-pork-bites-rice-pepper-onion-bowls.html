Air Fryer Pork Bites with Brown Rice Pepper-Onion Bowls

Air-fried pork bites with peppers and onions over brown rice, finished with a light yogurt-lime crema.

Servings

3 servings.

Times

  • Prep time: 25 minutes
  • Cook time: 16 minutes
  • Total time: 41 minutes

Ingredients

Main Dish

  • 1 lb pork tenderloin
  • 1 bag brown rice
  • 1 each yellow onion
  • 2 each bell peppers
  • 1 each lime
  • 1 bottle extra virgin olive oil
  • 1 container nonfat Greek yogurt
  • 1 jar chili powder
  • 1 jar ground cumin
  • 1 jar garlic powder
  • 1 jar black pepper
    • 

    Preparation Steps

    1. Rice: cook 1 cup dry brown rice. Measure out 2 cups cooked rice for this recipe; refrigerate remaining rice.
    2. Prep veg: slice 1 bell pepper and 1/2 onion (store remaining). Toss with 1 tsp olive oil and black pepper.
    3. Prep pork: cut pork into 1-inch cubes. Toss with 2 tsp olive oil, chili powder, cumin, garlic powder, and black pepper.
    4. Cosori setup: preheat both baskets to 400°F for 3 minutes.
    5. Basket A (veg): cook peppers/onion 400°F for 8 minutes, shaking once.
    6. Basket B (pork): cook pork bites 400°F for 8–10 minutes, shaking once, until pork reaches 145°F. Rest 3 minutes.
    7. Crema: stir 1/4 cup Greek yogurt with 1 tsp lime juice, a pinch of cumin, and 1 tsp water.
    8. Serve (40/30/30): build bowls with brown rice, peppers/onion, pork bites, and a light crema drizzle.

      Notes

      Portions (per person):


      Frank – Rice: about 1/2 cup cooked. Pork + veg: largest share. Crema drizzle.


      Susan – Rice: about 3/8 cup cooked. Pork + veg: medium share. Crema drizzle.


      Elise – Rice: about 3/8 cup cooked. Pork + veg: medium share. Crema drizzle.


      Tips (recipe-specific):


      Rest pork bites 3 minutes so they stay juicy.


      Cook peppers first if you like them softer; cook pork first if you want extra char.


      If you need more volume, add extra peppers/onion without changing rice.
