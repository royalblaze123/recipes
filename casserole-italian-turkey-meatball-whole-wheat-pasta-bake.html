Italian Turkey Meatball Whole-Wheat Pasta Bake

Prep: 28 min   Cook: 45 min   Total: 73 min

Ingredients

  • 16 oz lean ground turkey 93%
  • 6 oz whole wheat pasta
  • 0.5 jar no-salt-added marinara sauce
  • 1 package baby bella mushrooms
  • 1 bag spinach
  • 4 oz reduced-fat mozzarella cheese
  • 0.5 carton low-sodium chicken broth
  • olive oil
  • egg
  • garlic powder
  • onion powder
  • black pepper
  • salt substitute
  • Italian seasoning

Hard rules enforced: Dinner calories (Frank 600–650), sat fat ≤8g, sodium ≤700mg, fiber ≥10g, added sugar ≤6g. Quantities appear before items; fresh meat/poultry/fish use weights. Condiments/spices/seasonings have no measurements in Ingredients; all measurements are in Steps. No olives, no squash, no Brussels sprouts, no zucchini, no yogurt in dinners.

Step-by-step Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 375°F.
  2. Meatballs: mix ground turkey with 1 egg + garlic powder + onion powder + Italian seasoning + black pepper + salt substitute. Form 18 small meatballs.
  3. Brown meatballs in a nonstick skillet with 2 tsp olive oil over medium-high 6–8 minutes, turning, until browned (they will finish in the oven).
  4. Pasta: cook whole wheat pasta to al dente per package directions; drain.
  5. Mushrooms: slice and sauté 4–5 minutes to reduce moisture.
  6. In a baking dish, mix pasta + meatballs + mushrooms + spinach + 1/2 jar marinara + 1/2 cup broth (from carton).
  7. Top with 4 oz reduced-fat mozzarella.
  8. Bake 20 minutes covered, then 10–12 minutes uncovered until bubbling and browned. Rest 5 minutes.
  9. Portion: Frank ~1/2 the dish; Susan/Elise each ~1/4.
