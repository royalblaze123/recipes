Chicken-Black Bean Air-Fryer Taquitos

Nutrition (approx)

Frank: 645 kcal • P 58g • C 50g • F 20g
Susan/Elise (each): 484 kcal • P 44g • C 38g • F 15g

Ingredients (purchase-clean)

  • 1.25 lb boneless skinless chicken breast
  • 1 can black beans
  • 1 package corn tortillas
  • 1 each avocado
  • 1/2 jar salsa
  • 1 bag spring mix
  • 1 each lime
  • 6 tsp extra-virgin olive oil
  • 1 bag reduced-fat shredded Mexican cheese blend
  • 2 tsp chili powder
  • 2 tsp cumin
  • 2 tsp garlic powder
  • 1 tsp smoked paprika
  • 1 tsp black pepper
  • 1 tsp kosher salt

Steps

  1. Tortillas: warm corn tortillas by microwaving under a damp paper towel for 25–35 seconds so they roll without cracking.
  2. Chicken: cut into small pieces. Cook in a skillet over medium-high 8–10 minutes until 165°F. Season with spices.
  3. Beans: Can size note—use a standard 15 oz can black beans.. Drain/rinse. Mash 1 cup beans with a fork; reserve 1/2 cup whole beans for texture; refrigerate remainder.
  4. Filling: stir mashed beans + whole beans into the chicken. Add 3/4 cup shredded cheese and mix until combined.
  5. Assemble taquitos: place 2 tbsp filling on each tortilla, roll tightly, and place seam-side down.
  6. Preheat air fryer to 400°F.
  7. Brush or spray taquitos lightly with oil.
  8. Air fry in a single layer 8–10 minutes, turning at 5 minutes, until crisp and browned.
  9. Side salad: add spring mix to a bowl. Drizzle with 2 tsp lime juice and 2 tsp olive oil; toss.
  10. Add diced cucumber and cherry tomatoes to the salad if listed; toss again.
  11. Avocado: halve, pit, and dice. Use about 1/2 avocado total; refrigerate remaining avocado with the pit to slow browning.
  12. Serve: plate taquitos with salad, salsa, and avocado. Portion per Notes.

Notes

Crispy air-fryer taquitos with chicken + beans; salsa and avocado make it a full dinner plate. Portions (per person): Frank: 6 taquitos + 1/3 of the side + 1/3 of the avocado + salsa Susan: 4 taquitos + 1/4 of the side + 1/4 of the avocado + salsa Elise: 4 taquitos + 1/4 of the side + 1/4 of the avocado + salsa
