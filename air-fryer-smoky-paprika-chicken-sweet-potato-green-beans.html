Air Fryer Smoky Paprika Chicken with Sweet Potato and Green Beans

Smoky paprika chicken with sweet potato wedges and green beans—balanced macros, minimal cleanup, and dual-basket timed.

Servings

3 servings.

Times

  • Prep time: 20 minutes
  • Cook time: 18 minutes
  • Total time: 38 minutes

Ingredients

Main Dish

  • 1 lb boneless skinless chicken breast
  • 2 each sweet potatoes
  • 1 bag fresh green beans
  • 1 bottle extra virgin olive oil
  • 1 container nonfat Greek yogurt
  • 1 jar smoked paprika
  • 1 jar garlic powder
  • 1 jar black pepper
  • 1 jar Italian seasoning
    • 

    Preparation Steps

    1. Prep sweet potatoes: cut into wedges. Measure out about 2 1/4 cups wedges for this recipe; refrigerate remaining sweet potato. Toss with 1 tbsp olive oil, smoked paprika, garlic powder, and black pepper.
    2. Prep chicken: pat dry and slice into 3 cutlets. Coat with 2 tsp olive oil, smoked paprika, Italian seasoning, garlic powder, and black pepper.
    3. Prep green beans: trim. Toss 1/2 bag green beans with 1 tsp olive oil and black pepper. Refrigerate remaining green beans.
    4. Cosori setup: preheat both baskets to 390°F for 3 minutes. Put sweet potato wedges in Basket A. Put chicken cutlets in Basket B.
    5. Sync Finish: set Basket A to 390°F for 18 minutes (shake reminder ON). Set Basket B to 390°F for 10 minutes. Press Sync Finish.
    6. Shake reminder (Basket A): shake/flip wedges when prompted.
    7. Flip chicken: at the 5-minute mark, flip cutlets. Cook to 165°F internal.
    8. Green beans finish: when chicken is done, cook green beans in Basket B at 390°F for 6–7 minutes, shaking once.
    9. Yogurt dip: stir 1/4 cup Greek yogurt with a pinch of smoked paprika and 1 tsp water.
    10. Serve (40/30/30): plate chicken with sweet potato wedges and green beans and serve dip on the side.

      Notes

      Portions (per person):


      Frank – Chicken: largest cutlet. Sweet potato wedges: about 1 1/4 cups. Green beans: about 1 1/2 cups. Yogurt dip.


      Susan – Chicken: medium cutlet. Sweet potato wedges: about 1 cup. Green beans: about 1 1/4 cups. Yogurt dip.


      Elise – Chicken: medium cutlet. Sweet potato wedges: about 1 cup. Green beans: about 1 1/4 cups. Yogurt dip.


      Tips (recipe-specific):


      Pull chicken as soon as it hits 165°F so it stays juicy.


      Cook green beans at the end so they keep a crisp bite.


      If wedges brown too fast, drop Basket A to 375°F for the last 5 minutes.
