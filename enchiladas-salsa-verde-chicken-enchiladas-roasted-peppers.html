Salsa Verde Chicken Enchiladas + Roasted Peppers

Nutrition (approx)

Frank: 630 kcal • P 58g • C 38g • F 22g
Susan/Elise (each): 472 kcal • P 44g • C 28g • F 16g

Ingredients (purchase-clean)

  • 1.50 lb boneless skinless chicken breast
  • 1 package whole-grain small tortillas
  • 1 jar salsa verde
  • 1/2 bag reduced-fat shredded Mexican cheese blend
  • 1 each yellow onion
  • 2 each bell peppers
  • 4 tsp extra-virgin olive oil
  • 2 tsp chili powder
  • 2 tsp cumin
  • 2 tsp garlic powder
  • 1 tsp smoked paprika
  • 1 tsp black pepper
  • 1 tsp kosher salt

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 400°F. Lightly coat a 9x13-inch baking dish with nonstick spray.
  2. Cook chicken: pat chicken breast dry. Season with 2 tsp chili powder, 2 tsp cumin, 2 tsp garlic powder, 1 tsp smoked paprika, 1 tsp black pepper, and 1 tsp kosher salt. Sear in a nonstick skillet over medium-high 4–5 minutes per side; reduce to medium, cover, and cook 3–5 minutes more until 165°F. Rest 5 minutes, then shred.
  3. Warm salsa verde: pour the jar into a saucepan and heat over medium-low 4–5 minutes until steaming.
  4. Veg side: slice yellow onion and bell peppers into strips. Toss with 4 tsp olive oil and spread on a sheet pan. Roast 15–18 minutes, stirring once, until browned at edges.
  5. Tortillas: warm tortillas in a dry skillet 15–20 seconds per side.
  6. Assembly: spread 1/2 cup warmed salsa verde in the baking dish. For each tortilla, add a line of shredded chicken plus a small pinch of shredded cheese, roll tightly, and place seam-side down.
  7. Top: pour remaining salsa verde over the enchiladas. Sprinkle remaining shredded cheese over the center line.
  8. Bake 14–18 minutes until bubbly and lightly browned on top.
  9. Rest 5 minutes, then portion: Frank 4 enchiladas; Susan 3; Elise 3. Serve with roasted peppers/onions.

Notes

Mild salsa verde chicken enchiladas with a roasted pepper side for volume. Portions (per person): Frank: 4 enchiladas (4 tortillas) + 1/3 of the side/veg Susan: 3 enchiladas (3 tortillas) + 1/4 of the side/veg Elise: 3 enchiladas (3 tortillas) + 1/4 of the side/veg
