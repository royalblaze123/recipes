Chicken Tomato Basil White Bean Pizza

NOTES: Built as a full dinner using ready-made whole-grain or thin crust, lean protein, plenty of vegetables, and controlled cheese to support A1c, cholesterol, and blood pressure goals. Use low-sodium sauce and minimal added salt. PORTION SIZES: Frank: ~40% of the pizza; Susan: ~30% of the pizza; Elise: ~30% of the pizza (Frank = 1.0×, Susan/Elise = 0.75× portions).

Ingredients

  • 1 ready-made 12-inch thin whole-wheat pizza crust
  • 1 can (15 oz) no-salt-added cannellini or great northern beans, drained and rinsed
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • 1–2 tbsp water to thin bean spread
  • 8 oz cooked boneless, skinless chicken breast, thinly sliced
  • 1 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 3/4 cup shredded part-skim mozzarella cheese
  • 2 tbsp grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1/4 cup fresh basil leaves, torn
  • 1 tsp dried oregano
  • Freshly ground black pepper, to taste

Step-by-Step Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 425°F and place the crust on a pizza pan or parchment-lined baking sheet.
  2. Mash or blend beans with garlic and enough water to create a smooth, spreadable mixture.
  3. Spread the seasoned bean mixture evenly over the crust as a base.
  4. Arrange sliced chicken and halved cherry tomatoes evenly over the bean layer.
  5. Sprinkle shredded mozzarella, grated Parmesan, dried oregano, and black pepper over the toppings.
  6. Bake 12–15 minutes, until the crust is crisp and the cheese is melted and lightly browned.
  7. Immediately after baking, scatter torn fresh basil leaves over the top.
  8. Cool 3–5 minutes before slicing and portioning for Frank (~40%) and Susan/Elise (~30% each).

Nutrition per Person

  • Frank: ~620 kcal, ~54 g protein, ~55 g carbs, ~18 g fat.
  • Susan: ~465 kcal, ~41 g protein, ~41 g carbs, ~13 g fat.
  • Elise: ~465 kcal, ~41 g protein, ~41 g carbs, ~13 g fat.
