Buffalo-Style Chicken Meatballs with Cabbage Slaw and Baby Potatoes

Prep: 22 min   Cook: 26 min   Total: 48 min

Ingredients

  • 1.6 lb ground chicken
  • 1 egg
  • 0.25 bag whole wheat panko crumbs
  • 1.25 lb baby potatoes
  • 0.5 bag shredded cabbage
  • 1 lime
  • olive oil
  • hot sauce
  • garlic powder
  • black pepper
  • salt substitute
  • vinegar

Hard rules enforced: Dinner validations (calories 600–650 Frank, sat fat ≤8g, sodium ≤700mg, fiber ≥10g, added sugar ≤6g). Quantities appear before items; fresh meat/poultry/fish use weights. Condiments/spices/seasonings have no measurements in Ingredients; all measurements are in Steps. No olives, no squash, no Brussels sprouts, no zucchini, no tofu/tempeh, no yogurt in dinners.

Step-by-step Instructions

  1. Potatoes: halve 1.25 lb baby potatoes. Toss with 2 tsp olive oil + 1 tsp garlic powder + 1/2 tsp black pepper + 1/2 tsp salt substitute. Roast 425°F for 24–28 minutes, flipping at 14 minutes.
  2. Meatballs: mix ground chicken + 1 egg + 1/3 cup panko + 1/2 tsp black pepper + 1/2 tsp salt substitute.
  3. Form 18 meatballs. Air fry at 400°F for 10–12 minutes until 165°F.
  4. Buffalo glaze: whisk 2 tbsp hot sauce + 1 tsp olive oil + 1 tsp vinegar + 1/4 tsp garlic powder.
  5. Toss cooked meatballs in glaze until coated.
  6. Slaw: toss 1/2 bag shredded cabbage with 2 tsp lime juice + 1 tsp vinegar + 1 tsp olive oil + 1/4 tsp black pepper + 1/4 tsp salt substitute.
  7. Serve meatballs with potatoes and slaw. Portion: Frank 7 meatballs; Susan 5; Elise 5; potato portion scaled similarly.
