Bean-Forward Lunch: Turkey and Lentil Warm Skillet

Prep: 18 min   Cook: 14 min   Total: 32 min

Ingredients

  • 1.0 lb lean ground turkey 93%
  • 0.5 bag cooked lentils
  • 1 onion
  • 1 8 oz package mushrooms
  • 1 bag spinach
  • 1 lemon
  • olive oil
  • Italian seasoning
  • garlic powder
  • black pepper
  • salt substitute
  • tomato paste

Rules enforced: quantities before items; fresh meat/poultry/fish use weights; packaged items use fractional containers when partial. Condiments/spices/seasonings have no measurements in Ingredients; all measurements are in Steps. No olives, no squash, no Brussels sprouts, no zucchini, no tofu/tempeh. Lunch validations included in Notes.

Step-by-step Instructions

  1. Turkey: brown 1 lb lean ground turkey with 2 tsp olive oil 7–9 minutes. Season with 2 tsp Italian seasoning + 1 tsp garlic powder + 1/2 tsp pepper + 1/2 tsp salt substitute.
  2. Veg: add sliced 1 onion + 1 package mushrooms; cook 6 minutes until browned.
  3. Lentils: add 1/2 bag cooked lentils plus 2 tbsp water; stir 2 minutes.
  4. Sauce: whisk 1 tbsp tomato paste with 4 tbsp water; stir in and simmer 2 minutes.
  5. Spinach: stir in 1 bag spinach until wilted.
  6. Finish: add 2 tsp lemon juice per plate.
  7. Portion: Frank largest; Susan/Elise ~75%.
