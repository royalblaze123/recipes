Chicken Fajita Skillet + Sweet Potatoes

Nutrition (approx)

Frank: 650 kcal • P 60g • C 60g • F 16g
Susan/Elise (each): 488 kcal • P 45g • C 45g • F 12g

Ingredients (purchase-clean)

  • 1.75 lb boneless skinless chicken breast
  • 2 each bell pepper
  • 1 each onion
  • 2.00 lb sweet potatoes
  • 1 each lime
  • low-sodium chicken broth
  • kosher salt
  • black pepper
  • garlic powder
  • onion powder
  • smoked paprika
  • Italian seasoning
  • ground cumin
  • chili powder
  • red pepper flakes
  • extra-virgin olive oil
  • low-sodium chicken broth
  • low-sodium beef broth
  • low-sodium soy sauce
  • hot sauce
  • Dijon mustard
  • balsamic vinegar
  • apple cider vinegar
  • cornstarch

Steps (stovetop)

  1. Roast sweet potatoes: cube; toss with olive oil, smoked paprika, garlic powder, modest kosher salt, black pepper. Roast 425°F 22–26 minutes, stirring once.
  2. Skillet chicken: slice chicken thin. Sear in a hot skillet with olive oil 3–4 minutes; add onions/peppers; cook 6–8 minutes until veg is tender-crisp and chicken is 165°F.
  3. Season: add chili powder, cumin, garlic powder, black pepper, and a modest pinch of kosher salt. Add 1/4 cup low-sodium broth to deglaze; simmer 2 minutes.
  4. Finish: squeeze lime over skillet; serve with roasted sweet potatoes per Notes.

Notes

Fajita flavor without tortillas; sweet potatoes keep it a full dinner plate. Portions (per person): Frank: 7 oz chicken + 1 1/2 cups fajita veg + 1 cup roasted sweet potatoes Susan: 5 oz protein + 1 cup veg + 3/4 cup Elise: 5 oz protein + 1 cup veg + 3/4 cup Tips (recipe-specific): Deglaze with broth to keep chicken juicy. Roast sweet potatoes at high heat for edges, not mush.
