Freezer Chicken + White Bean Soup (Thick)

Nutrition (approx)

Frank: 640 kcal • P 58g • C 50g • F 16g • Sat 3g • Na 700mg • Fiber 12g
Susan/Elise (each): 480 kcal • P 44g • C 38g • F 12g • Sat 2g • Na 525mg • Fiber 9g

Ingredients (purchase-clean)

  • 1.75 lb boneless skinless chicken breast
  • 1 can white beans
  • 2 cans no-salt-added diced tomatoes
  • 1 bag frozen mixed vegetables
  • low-sodium chicken broth
  • kosher salt
  • black pepper
  • garlic powder
  • onion powder
  • smoked paprika
  • Italian seasoning
  • ground cumin
  • chili powder
  • red pepper flakes
  • extra-virgin olive oil
  • low-sodium chicken broth
  • cornstarch

Steps

  1. Soup base: add 6 cups low-sodium broth and 2 cans diced tomatoes (14.5 oz each) to a pot; bring to a simmer.
  2. Chicken: add chicken breast plus garlic powder, onion powder, black pepper, modest salt. Simmer 14–16 minutes to 165°F.
  3. Shred chicken in the pot.
  4. Veg: add 3 cups frozen mixed vegetables; simmer 6 minutes.
  5. Beans: drain/rinse white beans; add 1 1/2 cups beans; simmer 3 minutes.
  6. Thicken: simmer uncovered 5–8 minutes until thick enough to coat a spoon.
  7. Freeze: cool; portion; freeze. Reheat to a steady simmer until hot.

Notes

Freezer soup that stays thick and hearty after thawing—beans add body. Portions (per person): Frank: About 2 1/2 cups (heaping bowl) Susan: About 2 cups (bowl) Elise: About 2 cups (bowl) Tips (recipe-specific): Simmer uncovered at the end so it freezes thick. Freeze in 2-cup portions for easy work-night dinners.
