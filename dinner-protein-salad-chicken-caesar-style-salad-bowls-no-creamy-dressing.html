Chicken Caesar-Style Salad Bowls (No Creamy Dressing)

Nutrition (approx)

Frank: 645 kcal • P 58g • C 35g • F 28g • Sat 7g • Na 650mg • Fiber 12g
Susan/Elise (each): 484 kcal • P 44g • C 26g • F 21g • Sat 5g • Na 488mg • Fiber 9g

Ingredients (purchase-clean)

  • 1.75 lb boneless skinless chicken breast
  • 1 head romaine lettuce
  • 1 each lemon
  • 6 oz grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 bag frozen green beans
  • kosher salt
  • black pepper
  • garlic powder
  • onion powder
  • smoked paprika
  • Italian seasoning
  • ground cumin
  • chili powder
  • red pepper flakes
  • extra-virgin olive oil
  • balsamic vinegar
  • apple cider vinegar
  • Dijon mustard

Steps

  1. Oven: heat to 425°F.
  2. Chicken: season chicken with garlic powder, black pepper, smoked paprika, and a modest pinch of kosher salt. Roast 12–15 minutes to 165°F; rest 5 minutes; slice.
  3. Green beans: roast frozen green beans tossed with 2 tsp olive oil and black pepper 12 minutes until browned.
  4. Dressing: whisk 2 tbsp Dijon mustard, 2 tbsp lemon juice, 2 tbsp balsamic vinegar, 2 tbsp olive oil, black pepper, and a small pinch of kosher salt.
  5. Salad: chop romaine. Toss romaine with dressing (start with half, add as needed).
  6. Finish: sprinkle Parmesan (measured) over bowls; add chicken and green beans.
  7. Serve per Notes.

Notes

Caesar-style flavor without a creamy dressing—lemon + Dijon + Parmesan carry it. Portions (per person): Frank: 4 cups salad + 7 oz protein + 1 cup roasted green beans Susan: 3 cups salad + 5 oz protein + 3/4 cup carb Elise: 3 cups salad + 5 oz protein + 3/4 cup carb Tips (recipe-specific): Add dressing gradually—salad should be coated, not drenched. Roast green beans until browned for ‘crouton’ vibes without bread.
