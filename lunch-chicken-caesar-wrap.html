Chicken Caesar Lunch Wrap

High-protein chicken Caesar wraps using light dressing.

Ingredients

  • 3 whole-grain tortillas
  • 1 lb cooked chicken breast, diced
  • 1 cup chopped romaine
  • 3 tbsp light Caesar dressing
  • 1 tbsp grated Parmesan
  • 1/4 tsp black pepper

Instructions

  1. Toss chicken with Caesar dressing and pepper.
  2. Lay tortillas flat.
  3. Add chicken mixture, romaine, and Parmesan.
  4. Roll tightly and wrap.

Serves: 3 servings
