Creamy French Onion Yogurt Dip

Category: Dip • Yield: About 1 1/8 cups (18 tbsp) • AnyList-ready

Ingredients

  • 1 onion
  • 3/4 cup 2% plain Greek yogurt
  • 2 tsp extra-virgin olive oil
  • 1 tsp Worcestershire sauce
  • 1/2 tsp dried thyme
  • 1/4 tsp black pepper

Steps

  1. Finely dice 1 onion.
  2. In a skillet over medium-low heat, warm olive oil. Add onion and cook 12–15 minutes, stirring often, until deeply golden.
  3. Remove from heat and cool 5 minutes.
  4. Stir onions into Greek yogurt with Worcestershire, thyme, and black pepper. Chill 15 minutes before serving.
