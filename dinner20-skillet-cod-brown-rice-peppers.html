Garlic-Pepper Skillet Cod with Brown Rice and Sautéed Peppers

Servings

3 plates (Frank 1.0×, Susan 0.75×, Elise 0.75×).

Times

  • Prep time: 15 minutes
  • Cook time: 25 minutes
  • Total time: 40 minutes

Ingredients

  • 2.0 lb cod fillets
  • 1.0 cup dry brown rice
  • 3 bell peppers
  • 2 onions
  • 9 tsp olive oil
  • 6 tsp minced garlic
  • 4 tsp lemon juice
  • 2 tsp black pepper
  • 2 tsp dried basil
  • 2 tsp salt substitute

Steps

  1. Brown rice: Cook 1 cup dry brown rice with water to yield ~3 cups cooked. Keep warm.
  2. Peppers/onions: Slice peppers and onions. In a large skillet, heat 5 tsp olive oil. Add onions first and cook 6–8 minutes to lightly brown. Add peppers + 3 tsp minced garlic + 1 tsp black pepper + 1 tsp salt substitute; cook 6–8 minutes until tender-crisp.
  3. Cod: Pat cod dry. Season with 1 tsp black pepper + 1 tsp dried basil + 1 tsp salt substitute. In a nonstick skillet, heat 4 tsp olive oil. Cook cod 3–4 minutes per side (thickness-dependent) until opaque and flakes easily.
  4. Finish: Off heat, drizzle cod with 4 tsp lemon juice for a bright finish without heavy acidity.
  5. Plate portions: Frank gets 8 oz cooked cod + 1 cup cooked brown rice + 1 1/2 cups peppers/onions. Susan and Elise each get 6 oz cod + 3/4 cup rice + 1 1/4 cups peppers/onions.

Notes

Portions (per person):

Frank – 1 plate (8 oz cooked cod, 1 cup cooked brown rice, 1 1/2 cups peppers/onions).

Susan – 1 plate (6 oz cooked cod, 3/4 cup cooked brown rice, 1 1/4 cups peppers/onions).

Elise – 1 plate (6 oz cooked cod, 3/4 cup cooked brown rice, 1 1/4 cups peppers/onions).

Tips (recipe-specific):

Brown the onions first—this is free flavor without sodium.

If cod sticks, it’s not ready to flip; give it 30–60 seconds more.

Storage + Reheat

  • Cool promptly; store in airtight containers in the fridge up to 3–4 days (typical safety guidance).
  • For best texture, reheat protein + veg in the air fryer at 350°F for 4–6 minutes, and reheat grains/beans in the microwave 60–90 seconds with a splash of water.
