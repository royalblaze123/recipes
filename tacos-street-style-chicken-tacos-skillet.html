Street-Style Chicken Tacos (Skillet)

Nutrition (approx)

Frank: 525 kcal • P 42g • C 38g • F 18g
Susan/Elise (each): 394 kcal • P 32g • C 28g • F 14g

Ingredients (purchase-clean)

  • 1.25 lb boneless skinless chicken breast
  • 4/5 package whole-grain small tortillas
  • 1 each yellow onion
  • 1 pack microgreens
  • 1 each lime
  • 4 tsp extra-virgin olive oil
  • 2 tsp cumin
  • 2 tsp garlic powder
  • 1 tsp black pepper
  • 1 tsp kosher salt

Steps

  1. Chicken: pat dry and cut into 1/2-inch pieces.
  2. Season chicken with 2 tsp cumin, 2 tsp garlic powder, 1 tsp black pepper, and 1 tsp kosher salt.
  3. Cook: heat a nonstick skillet over medium-high. Add 4 tsp olive oil. Cook chicken 8–10 minutes, stirring often, until browned and 165°F.
  4. Onion: finely dice the yellow onion. Toss with 2 tsp lime juice and a pinch of black pepper.
  5. Tortillas: warm tortillas 20–30 seconds per side.
  6. Assemble: add chicken, a spoon of lime-onion, and a small pinch of microgreens on each taco. Portion 7 tacos per Notes.

Notes

Street-style chicken tacos with lime-onion and microgreens for a fresh finish. Portions (per person): Frank: 3 tacos (use 3 tortillas) + 1/3 of the filling + 1/3 of the toppings Susan: 2 tacos (use 2 tortillas) + 1/4 of the filling + 1/4 of the toppings Elise: 2 tacos (use 2 tortillas) + 1/4 of the filling + 1/4 of the toppings
