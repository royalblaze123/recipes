Chicken Parmesan Skillet + Barilla Protein+

Nutrition (approx)

Frank: 650 kcal • P 60g • C 75g • F 14g
Susan/Elise (each): 488 kcal • P 45g • C 56g • F 10g

Ingredients (purchase-clean)

  • 1.75 lb boneless skinless chicken breast
  • 1 jar marinara sauce
  • 1 box Barilla Protein+ pasta
  • 6 oz grated Parmesan cheese
  • 2 cups panko breadcrumbs
  • kosher salt
  • black pepper
  • garlic powder
  • onion powder
  • smoked paprika
  • Italian seasoning
  • ground cumin
  • chili powder
  • red pepper flakes
  • extra-virgin olive oil
  • low-sodium chicken broth
  • low-sodium beef broth
  • low-sodium soy sauce
  • hot sauce
  • Dijon mustard
  • balsamic vinegar
  • apple cider vinegar
  • cornstarch

Steps (stovetop)

  1. Pasta: cook Barilla Protein+ al dente; drain.
  2. Chicken: slice into cutlets. Season garlic powder, Italian seasoning, black pepper, modest salt.
  3. Pan-crisp: in nonstick skillet with olive oil, toast panko 3–4 minutes. Remove.
  4. Sear chicken: cook cutlets 3–4 minutes per side to 165°F.
  5. Sauce: add marinara (2 cups measured) to skillet; simmer 3 minutes.
  6. Serve: plate pasta, top with chicken + marinara, sprinkle Parmesan and toasted panko per Notes.

Notes

Chicken-parm feel without frying: toasted panko gives crunch; Protein+ pasta keeps it balanced. Portions (per person): Frank: 7 oz chicken + 2 cups pasta bowl + 2 tbsp panko Susan: 5 oz chicken + 1 1/2 cups pasta bowl + 1 1/2 tbsp panko Elise: 5 oz chicken + 1 1/2 cups pasta bowl + 1 1/2 tbsp panko Tips (recipe-specific): Toast panko separately so it stays crunchy on leftovers (add at serving). Keep marinara measured; flavor comes from seasoning + Parmesan.
