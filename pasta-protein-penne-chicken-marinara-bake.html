Protein+ Penne Chicken Marinara Bake

Nutrition (approx)

Frank: 675 kcal • P 73g • C 72g • F 15g
Susan/Elise (each): 506 kcal • P 55g • C 54g • F 11g

Ingredients (purchase-clean)

  • 1/3 box Barilla Protein+ penne
  • 1.25 lb boneless skinless chicken breast
  • 0.62 jar no-sugar-added marinara sauce
  • 1 bag part-skim shredded mozzarella
  • 2 each bell peppers
  • 1 each yellow onion
  • 2 tsp garlic powder
  • 2 tsp Italian seasoning
  • 1 tsp black pepper
  • 1 tsp kosher salt

Steps

  1. Bring a large pot of water to a boil for the Barilla Protein+ penne. (Use enough water to fully cover pasta; do not add water to Ingredients.)
  2. Preheat oven to 425°F. Lightly coat a baking dish with nonstick spray.
  3. Pat chicken breast dry. Season with 2 tsp garlic powder, 2 tsp Italian seasoning, 1 tsp black pepper, and 1 tsp kosher salt. Sear in a nonstick skillet over medium-high 4–5 minutes per side. Reduce to medium, cover, and cook 3–5 minutes more until 165°F. Rest 5 minutes, then chop.
  4. Dice the yellow onion. Add to the skillet and cook 4–6 minutes until softened.
  5. Dice the bell peppers. Cook 3–5 minutes until tender-crisp.
  6. Jar size note: assume a standard 24 oz jar marinara. Measure out what you need for this recipe; refrigerate the remainder.
  7. Add marinara to the skillet and simmer 5–7 minutes until hot and slightly thickened.
  8. Cook Barilla Protein+ penne to al dente per box time. Reserve 1 cup pasta water, then drain.
  9. Toss pasta into the sauce/skillet. Add reserved pasta water 1–2 tbsp at a time until the sauce clings well.
  10. Transfer pasta mixture to the baking dish. Top with the listed shredded cheese. Bake 10–12 minutes until bubbly and lightly browned on top.
  11. Portion into bowls using the Portions guidance in Notes.

Notes

Baked chicken penne marinara with measured mozzarella. Built on Barilla Protein+ for a higher-protein pasta plate with close-approx nutrition. Portions (per person): Frank: 2 oz dry Barilla Protein+ penne + 1/3 of the skillet/sauce Susan: 1.5 oz dry Barilla Protein+ penne + 1/4 of the skillet/sauce Elise: 1.5 oz dry Barilla Protein+ penne + 1/4 of the skillet/sauce
