Steak Fajita Taco Salad Bowls

Nutrition (approx)

Frank: 645 kcal • P 58g • C 40g • F 22g
Susan/Elise (each): 484 kcal • P 44g • C 30g • F 16g

Ingredients (purchase-clean)

  • 1.25 lb sirloin steak
  • 1 bag spring mix
  • 1 each avocado
  • 1/2 jar salsa
  • 1/2 tub 2% plain Greek yogurt
  • 1/2 bag bean-based tortilla chips
  • 2 each bell peppers
  • 1 each yellow onion
  • 1 pint cherry tomatoes
  • 6 tsp extra-virgin olive oil
  • 2 tsp chili powder
  • 2 tsp cumin
  • 2 tsp garlic powder
  • 1 tsp smoked paprika
  • 1 tsp black pepper
  • 1 tsp kosher salt

Steps

  1. Greens: add the spring mix to a large serving bowl.
  2. Veg: slice bell peppers and onion into thin strips.
  3. Steak: pat dry, slice thin against the grain, and season with chili powder, cumin, garlic powder, smoked paprika, black pepper, and kosher salt.
  4. Cook steak: heat a skillet over medium-high. Add 2 tsp olive oil and cook steak 2–3 minutes, stirring often, until just cooked. Transfer steak to a plate.
  5. Cook veg: add remaining 4 tsp olive oil to the skillet and cook peppers/onion 5–7 minutes until browned at edges.
  6. Finish: return steak to the skillet and toss 30 seconds.
  7. Tomatoes: halve cherry tomatoes.
  8. Avocado: halve, pit, and dice. Use about 1/2 avocado total; refrigerate the remaining avocado with the pit to slow browning.
  9. Salsa-yogurt: in a small bowl, stir 6 tbsp Greek yogurt with 6 tbsp salsa until smooth.
  10. Crunch: portion chips by weight. Frank 1.0 oz; Susan 0.75 oz; Elise 0.75 oz.
  11. Build bowls: divide greens, then add hot protein mix, then cold toppings. Drizzle salsa-yogurt and finish with avocado and measured chips. Portion per Notes.

Notes

Steak fajita salad bowls: hot steak/pepper mix over cold greens with salsa + avocado. Portions (per person): Frank: Salad base: 1/3 of the greens + 1/3 of the hot protein mix + toppings (salsa + avocado + yogurt). Crunch: chips 1.0 oz Susan: Salad base: 1/4 of the greens + 1/4 of the hot protein mix + toppings (salsa + avocado + yogurt). Crunch: chips 0.75 oz Elise: Salad base: 1/4 of the greens + 1/4 of the hot protein mix + toppings (salsa + avocado + yogurt). Crunch: chips 0.75 oz
