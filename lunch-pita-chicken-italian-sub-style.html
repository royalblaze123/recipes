High-Protein Pitas: Italian-Style Chicken Sub Pitas

Prep: 18 min   Cook: 10 min   Total: 28 min

Ingredients

  • 3 whole wheat pitas
  • 1.0 lb chicken breast
  • 6 slices provolone
  • 0.25 jar roasted red peppers
  • 0.5 bag shredded cabbage
  • 1 cucumber
  • 1 clamshell microgreens
  • olive oil
  • Italian seasoning
  • garlic powder
  • black pepper
  • salt substitute
  • red wine vinegar

Rules enforced: quantities before items; fresh meat/poultry/fish use weights; packaged items use fractional containers when partial. Condiments/spices/seasonings have no measurements in Ingredients; all measurements are in Steps. No olives, no squash, no Brussels sprouts, no zucchini, no tofu/tempeh. Lunch validations included in Notes.

Step-by-step Instructions

  1. Chicken: season 1.0 lb chicken breast with 2 tsp Italian seasoning + 1 tsp garlic powder + 1/2 tsp pepper + 1/2 tsp salt substitute. Cook with 2 tsp olive oil 6–8 minutes until 165°F; rest 3 minutes; slice.
  2. Warm pitas: toast 3 pitas 2 minutes.
  3. Base: add shredded cabbage to each pita (divide 1/2 bag evenly) and drizzle with vinegar (Frank 2 tsp; Susan 1.5 tsp; Elise 1.5 tsp).
  4. Add sliced chicken and provolone (Frank 2 slices; Susan 2; Elise 2).
  5. Roasted peppers: slice and add (Frank 2 tbsp; Susan 1.5 tbsp; Elise 1.5 tbsp).
  6. Add cucumber slices and microgreens.
  7. Finish with pepper; pack and go.
