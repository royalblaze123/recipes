Ricotta Broccoli Chicken Pizza + Garlic Parmesan Wings

Tomato-based chicken and broccoli pizza with ricotta dollops, served with garlic-parmesan wings.

Servings

3 servings (Frank, Susan, Elise).

Times

  • Prep time: 25 minutes
  • Cook time: 35 minutes
  • Total time: 60 minutes

Ingredients

Pizza

  • 1 dough ball pizza dough
  • 1/2 jar no-salt-added pizza sauce
  • 2 oz shredded part-skim mozzarella
  • 4 oz cooked chicken breast
  • 1/2 head broccoli
  • 1/4 cup part-skim ricotta
  • 1 tsp garlic powder
  • 1 tsp Italian seasoning

Wings

  • 2 lb chicken wings
  • 1 tsp garlic powder
  • 1 tsp onion powder
  • 1/2 tsp ground black pepper

Wing Finish Sauce

  • 2 tbsp grated parmesan cheese
  • 1 tbsp extra-virgin olive oil
  • 1 tsp garlic powder
  • 1 each lemon

Preparation Steps

  1. Remove 1 dough ball from the refrigerator (or thawed vacuum-sealed dough) and keep covered at room temperature for 60–90 minutes.
  2. Preheat oven with a pizza steel or stone to 500°F for 45 minutes (or your oven’s maximum).
  3. Wing count note: 1 wing = 1 whole wing (flat + drumette). If buying separated party wings, count 2 pieces = 1 wing.
  4. Wings: Pat wings very dry. Toss with garlic powder, onion powder, and black pepper.
  5. Cosori dual-basket setup: Split wings between both baskets in a single layer. Use Sync Cook at 380°F for 18 minutes, shaking halfway.
  6. Crisp finish: Increase to 400°F and cook 6–8 minutes more until browned and 165°F internally.
  7. Garlic-parmesan toss: In a large bowl, whisk olive oil, grated parmesan cheese, garlic powder, and a squeeze of lemon. Toss hot wings until coated.
  8. Pizza topping prep: Chop broccoli into small florets. Microwave in a bowl with a small splash of water for 90 seconds, then drain well so the pizza does not get wet. Shred or chop cooked chicken.
  9. Stretch dough into a 12-inch round. Spread sauce. Sprinkle mozzarella. Add chicken and broccoli. Dollop ricotta in small spoonfuls across the top. Dust with garlic powder and Italian seasoning.
  10. Bake 7–9 minutes until browned and bubbling. Rest 2 minutes before slicing.
  11. Portion and serve using the Portions split below.

Notes

Tomato-based chicken and broccoli pizza with ricotta dollops, served with garlic-parmesan wings. Portions (per person): Frank: About 1/3 of the 12-inch pizza + 8 wings (about 4 oz cooked) Susan: About 1/4 of the 12-inch pizza + 6 wings (about 3 oz cooked) Elise: About 1/4 of the 12-inch pizza + 6 wings (about 3 oz cooked) Tips (recipe-specific): Microwaving broccoli briefly keeps it tender-crisp and prevents watery pizza. Ricotta adds creaminess without needing any yogurt-based sauce. Use parmesan as a measured finishing flavor on wings so sodium stays predictable.
