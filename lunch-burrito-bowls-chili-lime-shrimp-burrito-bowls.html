Chili-Lime Shrimp Burrito Bowls

Nutrition (approx)

Frank: 530 kcal • P 50g • C 52g • F 15g
Susan/Elise (each): 398 kcal • P 38g • C 39g • F 11g

Ingredients (purchase-clean)

  • 1.50 lb raw shrimp
  • 1 bag frozen fajita-style pepper blend
  • 1 can black beans
  • 1 cup dry brown rice
  • 1 bag shredded lettuce
  • 1 each avocado
  • 1/2 jar salsa
  • 1 each lime
  • 4 tsp extra-virgin olive oil
  • 2 tsp chili powder
  • 2 tsp garlic powder
  • 1 tsp black pepper
  • 1 tsp kosher salt

Steps

  1. Rice: rinse 1 cup dry brown rice. Cook with 2 cups water (or low-sodium broth): bring to a boil, cover, reduce to low, and simmer 35–40 minutes. Rest 5 minutes, then fluff.
  2. Veg: cook frozen fajita pepper blend in a skillet with 2 tsp olive oil 6–8 minutes until browned.
  3. Shrimp: pat dry and season with chili powder, garlic powder, black pepper, and kosher salt. Cook in the same skillet 2–3 minutes until just opaque.
  4. Beans: Can size note—use a standard 15 oz can black beans.. Drain/rinse. Use 1 1/2 cups beans; refrigerate remainder.
  5. Combine: toss shrimp with peppers and beans 1 minute. Finish with 2 tsp lime juice.
  6. Assembly: divide lettuce (or cauliflower rice if listed) into three bowls.
  7. Top each bowl with the protein mix and measured rice (or other carb component if used).
  8. Add salsa and measured diced avocado on top.
  9. Portion per Notes.
  10. Avocado: halve, pit, and dice. Use about 1/2 avocado total; refrigerate remaining avocado with the pit to slow browning.

Notes

Shrimp burrito bowls with peppers + beans—bright, fast, and filling. Portions (per person): Frank: 1 large burrito bowl (about 1/3 of the batch) + 1/3 avocado + salsa Susan: 1 medium burrito bowl (about 1/4 of the batch) + 1/4 avocado + salsa Elise: 1 medium burrito bowl (about 1/4 of the batch) + 1/4 avocado + salsa
