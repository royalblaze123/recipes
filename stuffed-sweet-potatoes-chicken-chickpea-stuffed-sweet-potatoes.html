Chicken Chickpea Stuffed Sweet Potatoes

Nutrition (approx)

Frank: 645 kcal • P 58g • C 58g • F 17g
Susan/Elise (each): 484 kcal • P 44g • C 44g • F 13g

Ingredients (purchase-clean)

  • 2.25 lb sweet potatoes
  • 1.50 lb boneless skinless chicken breast
  • 1 can chickpeas
  • 1 each avocado
  • 1/2 jar salsa
  • 4 tsp extra-virgin olive oil
  • 2 tsp chili powder
  • 2 tsp cumin
  • 2 tsp garlic powder
  • 1 tsp smoked paprika
  • 1 tsp black pepper
  • 1 tsp kosher salt

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 425°F. Line a sheet pan with parchment.
  2. Sweet potatoes: scrub and pierce each potato 6–8 times with a fork. Rub with 2 tsp olive oil and a pinch of kosher salt.
  3. Roast sweet potatoes 40–55 minutes until very tender (knife slides in easily).
  4. Chicken: cut small. Cook in a skillet with 2 tsp olive oil 8–10 minutes until 165°F. Season with spices.
  5. Chickpeas: Can size note—use a standard 15 oz can chickpeas.. Drain/rinse. Add 1 1/2 cups chickpeas; refrigerate remainder.
  6. Combine: warm 2 minutes.
  7. Split: slice each potato lengthwise, fluff the insides with a fork, and create a well for the topping.
  8. Stuff: spoon chicken-chickpea mix into potatoes.
  9. Avocado: halve, pit, and dice. Use about 1/2 avocado total; refrigerate remaining avocado with the pit to slow browning.
  10. Serve with salsa and portion per Notes.

Notes

Chicken + chickpeas gives a different bite than beans; still finished with salsa and avocado. Portions (per person): Frank: 1 stuffed sweet potato (largest) + 1/3 of the topping mix + 1/3 of the avocado + salsa Susan: 1 stuffed sweet potato (medium) + 1/4 of the topping mix + 1/4 of the avocado + salsa Elise: 1 stuffed sweet potato (medium) + 1/4 of the topping mix + 1/4 of the avocado + salsa
