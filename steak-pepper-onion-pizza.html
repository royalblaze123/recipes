Steak Pepper Onion Pizza + Cucumber Salad

Thin-crust whole-wheat pizza with seared sirloin, peppers, and onion, finished with Parmesan and served with a crisp cucumber salad.

Servings

3 servings (Frank, Susan, Elise).

Times

  • Prep time: 40 minutes
  • Cook time: 12 minutes
  • Total time: 52 minutes

Ingredients

Pizza

  • 1 dough ball whole wheat pizza dough
  • 1 can no-salt-added crushed tomatoes
  • 1 package reduced-fat mozzarella
  • 1 lb sirloin steak
  • 1 each bell pepper
  • 1 each yellow onion
  • 1 jar grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 tsp garlic powder
  • 1 tsp ground black pepper

Side salad

  • 2 hearts romaine hearts
  • 1 each cucumber
  • 2 tbsp extra-virgin olive oil
  • 2 tbsp red wine vinegar

Step-by-step Instructions

  1. Thaw and warm dough: Thaw 1 dough ball overnight in the refrigerator. Bring to room temperature 60–90 minutes.
  2. Preheat: Place a pizza stone or steel or inverted sheet pan in the oven and preheat to 500°F for 30 minutes.
  3. Cook steak: Pat steak dry and season with garlic powder and black pepper. Sear in a hot skillet 2–3 minutes per side, then rest 5 minutes. Slice thin. Measure 10 oz cooked steak for the pizza and refrigerate any extra.
  4. Make sauce: Measure 1 1/4 cups crushed tomatoes from the can and spread as a thin base. Refrigerate remaining tomatoes.
  5. Prep toppings: Slice 1/2 bell pepper and 1/4 onion for the pizza. Bag remaining produce.
  6. Stretch and top: Stretch dough into a thin 10–12 inch round on parchment. Add sauce, sprinkle 5 1/2 oz mozzarella, add steak and vegetables, then finish with 2 tbsp Parmesan.
  7. Bake: Slide pizza on parchment onto the hot surface. Bake 8–12 minutes until browned and bubbling.
  8. Side salad: Chop romaine and slice cucumber. Dress with olive oil and vinegar. Portion per person.
  9. Portion dinner: Cut pizza into 10 slices and serve with salad.

Portion Sizes

Portions (per person):
Pizza (1 dough ball): cut into 10 slices.
Frank: 4 slices pizza + 1 generous bowl cucumber salad

Susan: 3 slices pizza + 3/4 bowl cucumber salad

Elise: 3 slices pizza + 3/4 bowl cucumber salad
