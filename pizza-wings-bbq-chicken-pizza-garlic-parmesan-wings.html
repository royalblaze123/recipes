BBQ Chicken Pizza with Garlic-Parmesan Wings

Prep: 18 min   Cook: 30 min   Total: 48 min

Ingredients

  • 1 pizza dough ball
  • 0.5 jar low-sodium pizza sauce
  • 6 oz reduced-fat mozzarella cheese
  • 12 oz cooked rotisserie chicken
  • 0.25 jar sugar-free BBQ sauce
  • 1 red onion
  • 1 bunch cilantro
  • 2.25 lb chicken wings
  • 2 tbsp grated parmesan cheese
  • olive oil
  • garlic powder
  • black pepper
  • salt substitute

RBM rules: quantities appear before items; fresh meat/poultry/fish use weights. Condiments/spices/seasonings are listed name-only (no tsp/tbsp in Ingredients); all measurements are in Steps. Water is referenced in Steps only. No olives, no squash, no Brussels sprouts, no yogurt in dinners.

Step-by-step Instructions

  1. Pizza: preheat oven to 475°F. Stretch dough into a 12-inch round on parchment.
  2. Make BBQ chicken topping: toss chopped chicken with 1/4 jar sugar-free BBQ sauce. (Keep sauce off the crust edge.)
  3. Top pizza: spread ~1/2 jar pizza sauce thinly, add mozzarella, then BBQ chicken and thin-sliced red onion. Bake 10–12 minutes until browned.
  4. Finish pizza: top with chopped cilantro. Cut into 10 slices.
  5. Wings: pat wings dry. Toss with 2 tsp olive oil + 2 tsp salt substitute + 1 tsp black pepper + 2 tsp garlic powder.
  6. Air fry wings (Cosori): 380°F for 24–26 minutes shaking at reminders, then 400°F for 4–6 minutes to crisp.
  7. Garlic-parm finish: toss hot wings with 2 tbsp grated parmesan + 1 tsp olive oil + 1 tsp garlic powder + black pepper.
  8. Portion: Frank 4 slices + 8 wings; Susan/Elise 3 slices + 6 wings.
