Beef, Barley, and Mushroom Soup with Rosemary

Prep: 20 min   Cook: 55 min   Total: 75 min

Ingredients

  • 1.25 lb sirloin steak
  • 1 carton low-sodium beef broth
  • 0.5 carton low-sodium beef broth
  • 1 onion
  • 2 carrots
  • 2 celery stalks
  • 1 package baby bella mushrooms
  • 0.33 bag frozen green beans
  • 0.25 bag pearl barley
  • olive oil
  • tomato paste
  • Worcestershire sauce
  • rosemary
  • thyme
  • black pepper
  • salt substitute
  • bay leaves

RBM rules: quantities appear before items; fresh proteins use weight. Condiments/spices/seasonings are listed name-only; all measurements are in Steps. Water is only referenced in Steps.

Step-by-step Instructions

  1. Prep: cut sirloin into 3/4-inch cubes. Slice mushrooms. Dice onion, carrots, and celery.
  2. Sear beef: heat a large pot over medium-high. Add 4 tsp olive oil. Add beef in a single layer (work in batches). Sear 2–3 minutes per side until browned; transfer to a plate.
  3. Build base: add onion, carrots, and celery to the pot. Cook 6–8 minutes until softened; add mushrooms and cook 4–5 minutes until they release moisture and start browning.
  4. Bloom: stir in 2 tbsp tomato paste + 2 tsp Worcestershire sauce + 1 tsp black pepper + 2 tsp salt substitute + 1 tsp rosemary + 1 tsp thyme. Cook 60 seconds.
  5. Add liquids: pour in 1.5 cartons beef broth total (48 fl oz). Add 2 bay leaves. Return beef (and any juices) to the pot.
  6. Simmer: bring to a gentle simmer, cover, and cook 35 minutes.
  7. Add barley + green beans: add pearl barley (use ~3/4 cup dry from the 0.25 bag) and frozen green beans (use ~1 1/2 cups). Simmer 18–22 minutes uncovered until barley is tender and soup thickens. Remove bay leaves.
  8. Portion: Frank gets ~3 1/2 cups. Susan and Elise each get ~2 3/4 cups.
