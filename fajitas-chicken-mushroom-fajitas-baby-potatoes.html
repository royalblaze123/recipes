Chicken & Mushroom Fajitas + Baby Potatoes

Nutrition (approx)

Frank: 635 kcal • P 58g • C 38g • F 22g
Susan/Elise (each): 476 kcal • P 44g • C 28g • F 16g

Ingredients (purchase-clean)

  • 1.50 lb boneless skinless chicken breast
  • 8 oz fresh mushrooms
  • 2 each bell peppers
  • 1 each yellow onion
  • 4/5 package whole-grain small tortillas
  • 1.50 lb baby potatoes
  • 6 tsp extra-virgin olive oil
  • 1 jar salsa
  • 2 tsp chili powder
  • 2 tsp cumin
  • 2 tsp garlic powder
  • 1 tsp smoked paprika
  • 1 tsp black pepper
  • 1 tsp kosher salt

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 425°F. Line two sheet pans with parchment.
  2. Baby potatoes: quarter; toss with 2 tsp olive oil and 1/2 tsp kosher salt. Roast 25–30 minutes, flipping once.
  3. Veg: slice peppers and onion into strips. Slice mushrooms.
  4. Chicken: pat dry and slice into strips. Season with fajita spices. Toss chicken and veg with remaining 4 tsp olive oil.
  5. Roast chicken + veg 16–22 minutes, stirring once, until chicken reaches 165°F and veg is browned at edges.
  6. Tortillas: warm tortillas 20–30 seconds per side.
  7. Serve with salsa and potatoes. Portion per Notes.

Notes

Mushroom-boosted chicken fajitas for extra savory depth, plus roasted potatoes. Portions (per person): Frank: Build a plate: 3 tortillas + 1/3 of the fajita mix + 1/3 of the side Susan: Build a plate: 2 tortillas + 1/4 of the fajita mix + 1/4 of the side Elise: Build a plate: 2 tortillas + 1/4 of the fajita mix + 1/4 of the side
