Tuna–Greek Yogurt Power Sandwich & Cucumber Slices

High-protein tuna and Greek yogurt sandwich on whole-grain bread with cucumber slices on the side.

Servings

3 servings (Frank, Susan, Elise).

Times

  • Prep time: 10 minutes
  • Cook time: 0 minutes
  • Total time: 10 minutes

Ingredients

Sandwich

  • 2 cans tuna in water, drained (10 oz drained weight total)
  • 6 tbsp low-fat (2%) plain Greek yogurt
  • 2 tsp Dijon mustard
  • 1/4 tsp garlic powder
  • 1/4 tsp black pepper
  • 6 slices high-fiber whole-grain sandwich bread
  • 1 cup fresh lettuce or spring mix, loosely packed
  • 1/2 cup fresh tomato slices

Side

  • 1 1/2 cups fresh cucumber slices

Preparation Steps

  1. In a medium bowl, combine the drained tuna, Greek yogurt, Dijon mustard, garlic powder, and black pepper. Stir until well mixed into a creamy tuna salad.
  2. Lay out the whole-grain bread slices. Divide the tuna salad among three sandwiches, giving Frank a slightly larger portion and Susan and Elise slightly smaller, as noted in the portions.
  3. Top each sandwich with lettuce or spring mix and a few tomato slices.
  4. Close each sandwich with a second slice of bread and press gently. Cut in half if desired.
  5. Arrange cucumber slices on the plates, dividing them into three portions.
  6. Serve each person one sandwich with their portion of cucumber slices.

Notes

Frank – 1 full sandwich with ~4 oz tuna filling plus about 1 cup cucumber slices.

Susan – 1 full sandwich with ~3 oz tuna filling plus about 3/4 cup cucumber slices.

Elise – 1 full sandwich with ~3 oz tuna filling plus about 3/4 cup cucumber slices.

Nutrition (per person)

  • Frank: ~400 kcal; 38 g protein; 32 g carbs; 11 g fat; 7 g fiber; ~540 mg sodium.
  • Susan: ~360 kcal; 34 g protein; 30 g carbs; 9 g fat; 6 g fiber; ~500 mg sodium.
  • Elise: ~360 kcal; 34 g protein; 30 g carbs; 9 g fat; 6 g fiber; ~500 mg sodium.
