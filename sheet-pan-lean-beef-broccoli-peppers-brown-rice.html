Sheet Pan Lean Beef and Broccoli with Peppers and Brown Rice

Prep: 22 min   Cook: 22 min   Total: 44 min

Ingredients

  • 1.5 lb lean sirloin steak
  • 1 bag frozen broccoli florets
  • 2 bell peppers
  • 1 onion
  • 0.5 cup dry brown rice
  • olive oil
  • low-sodium soy sauce
  • garlic powder
  • black pepper
  • salt substitute
  • sesame oil
  • ginger

Hard rules enforced: Dinner validations (calories, sat fat, sodium, fiber, added sugar). Quantities appear before items; fresh meat/poultry/fish use weights. Condiments/spices/seasonings have no measurements in Ingredients; all measurements are in Steps. No olives, no squash, no Brussels sprouts, no zucchini. Yogurt is not used in dinners.

Step-by-step Instructions

  1. Cook brown rice: cook 1/2 cup dry brown rice per package directions (water in Steps only).
  2. Preheat oven to 450°F. Line a sheet pan.
  3. Broccoli: microwave frozen broccoli 3–4 minutes to thaw. Pat dry. Measure 4 cups; refreeze remainder. Toss with 2 tsp olive oil + black pepper + salt substitute. Spread on pan and roast 10 minutes.
  4. Beef: slice sirloin thin across the grain. Toss with a light marinade: 2 tbsp low-sodium soy sauce + grated ginger + garlic powder + 1 tsp sesame oil + black pepper. (Keep soy measured for sodium.)
  5. Peppers/onion: slice and toss with 1 tsp olive oil + black pepper + salt substitute.
  6. Add beef and peppers/onion to pan. Roast 6–8 minutes until beef is just cooked (don’t overcook).
  7. Serve over brown rice.
  8. Portion: Frank largest beef and rice share; Susan/Elise ~75%.
