Air Fryer Parmesan-Herb Cod + Potatoes + Green Beans

Nutrition (approx)

Frank: 645 kcal • P 55g • C 55g • F 20g • Sat 6g • Na 700mg • Fiber 10g
Susan/Elise (each): 484 kcal • P 41g • C 41g • F 15g • Sat 4g • Na 525mg • Fiber 8g

Ingredients (purchase-clean)

  • 1.50 lb cod fillets
  • 1.50 lb baby potatoes
  • 1 bag frozen green beans
  • 6 oz grated Parmesan cheese
  • kosher salt
  • black pepper
  • garlic powder
  • onion powder
  • smoked paprika
  • Italian seasoning
  • ground cumin
  • chili powder
  • red pepper flakes
  • extra-virgin olive oil
  • low-sodium soy sauce
  • hot sauce
  • Dijon mustard
  • balsamic vinegar
  • apple cider vinegar
  • cornstarch

Steps (Cosori dual-basket)

  1. Preheat Cosori to 400°F. Basket A: potatoes. Basket B: cod later. Use Sync Finish.
  2. Potatoes (Basket A): halve potatoes; toss with 2 tsp olive oil, 2 tsp Italian seasoning, 1 tsp garlic powder, 1/2 tsp kosher salt, black pepper. Air fry 18–22 minutes at 400°F with Shake Reminder.
  3. Cod: pat dry; season with 1/2 tsp kosher salt, black pepper, 1 tsp garlic powder, 2 tsp Italian seasoning. Sprinkle 3 oz Parmesan total over cod (measured).
  4. Cod (Basket B): add when potatoes have ~9 minutes left. Air fry 8–10 minutes at 400°F until flaky.
  5. Green beans: microwave, drain; toss with 2 tsp olive oil, black pepper, and 1 tsp garlic powder; crisp 5–6 minutes if space or roast 425°F 10 minutes.
  6. Serve per Notes (sprinkle remaining 3 oz Parmesan across plates, measured).

Notes

Fish-and-chips comfort directionally—crispy potatoes and parmesan-herb cod (not fried). Portions (per person): Frank: 6 oz cod + 1 cup potatoes + 1 cup green beans Susan: 5 oz protein + 1 cup potatoes + 3/4 cup veg Elise: 5 oz protein + 1 cup potatoes + 3/4 cup veg Tips (recipe-specific): Add cod late so it doesn’t dry out while potatoes finish. Use Parmesan as a topper, not a heavy sauce.
