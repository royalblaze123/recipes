Creamy Smoky Yogurt Sauce

Category: Sauce • Yield: About 3/4 cup (14 tbsp) • AnyList-ready

Ingredients

  • 3/4 cup 2% plain Greek yogurt
  • 2 tsp lime juice
  • 2 tsp extra-virgin olive oil
  • 1 tsp smoked paprika
  • 1/2 tsp ground cumin
  • 1/4 tsp black pepper

Steps

  1. Whisk Greek yogurt, lime juice, olive oil, smoked paprika, cumin, and black pepper until smooth.
  2. Chill 10 minutes before serving.
