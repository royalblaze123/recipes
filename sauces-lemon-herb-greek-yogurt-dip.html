Lemon Herb Greek Yogurt Dip

Bright, creamy yogurt dip for fish, chicken, roasted veggies, pitas, and bowls.
Servings: 8
Prep: 5 min | Cook: 0 min | Total: 5 min

Ingredients

  • 2 cup 2% Greek yogurt
  • 1 lemon
  • 2 tsp olive oil
  • 2 tsp dried dill
  • 2 tsp dried parsley
  • 1 tsp garlic powder
  • 1 tsp onion powder
  • 1 tsp black pepper

Steps

  1. Zest the lemon, then juice it. Whisk Greek yogurt with 2–3 tsp lemon juice and 1 tsp lemon zest (use more juice to taste).
  2. Whisk in olive oil, dill, parsley, garlic powder, onion powder, and black pepper until smooth.
  3. Chill 10 minutes if you have time for best flavor. Store refrigerated up to 5 days; stir before serving.

Notes

Optional one-line dish description: A bright lemon-herb yogurt dip that pairs especially well with fish and chicken. Portions (per person): Frank: 1/3 of recipe (about 2/3 cup) Susan: 1/4 of recipe (about 1/2 cup) Elise: 1/4 of recipe (about 1/2 cup)
