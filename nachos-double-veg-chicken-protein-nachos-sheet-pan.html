Double-Veg Chicken Protein Nachos (Sheet Pan)

Nutrition (approx)

Frank: 625 kcal • P 58g • C 40g • F 21g
Susan/Elise (each): 469 kcal • P 44g • C 30g • F 16g

Ingredients (purchase-clean)

  • 1.25 lb boneless skinless chicken breast
  • 2/3 bag bean-based tortilla chips
  • 3/4 bag reduced-fat shredded Mexican cheese blend
  • 1/2 tub 2% plain Greek yogurt
  • 1/2 jar salsa
  • 2 each bell peppers
  • 1 each yellow onion
  • 12 oz fresh broccoli florets
  • 6 tsp extra-virgin olive oil
  • 2 tsp chili powder
  • 2 tsp cumin
  • 2 tsp garlic powder
  • 1 tsp smoked paprika
  • 1 tsp black pepper
  • 1 tsp kosher salt

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 425°F. Line a large sheet pan with parchment.
  2. Chicken: pat chicken dry and cut into 1/2-inch pieces. Season with 2 tsp chili powder, 2 tsp cumin, 2 tsp garlic powder, 1 tsp smoked paprika, 1 tsp black pepper, and 1 tsp kosher salt.
  3. Cook chicken in a nonstick skillet over medium-high 8–10 minutes until browned and 165°F.
  4. Veg: slice bell peppers and onion into thin strips. Cut broccoli into bite-size florets.
  5. Roast veg: toss peppers, onion, and broccoli with 6 tsp olive oil on the sheet pan. Roast 12–15 minutes, stirring once, until browned at edges.
  6. Add cooked chicken over the roasted vegetables and spread evenly.
  7. Cheese: sprinkle the listed shredded cheese evenly over the topping mix.
  8. Bake 4–6 minutes until cheese is melted and bubbling.
  9. Chips: portion chips by weight: Frank 1.5 oz; Susan 1.125 oz; Elise 1.125 oz. Keep chips separate for crunch.
  10. Greek yogurt: use 6 tbsp as a drizzle across the finished nachos.
  11. Salsa: measure 1/2 jar salsa into a bowl for topping.
  12. Serve: spoon hot topping mix over each person’s chips and finish with yogurt + salsa. Portion per Notes.

Notes

Extra vegetables (peppers + broccoli) make the plate bigger, so measured chips still feel like nachos. Portions (per person): Frank: Chips: 1.5 oz + 1/3 of the hot topping mix + 1/3 of the cold toppings Susan: Chips: 1.125 oz + 1/4 of the hot topping mix + 1/4 of the cold toppings Elise: Chips: 1.125 oz + 1/4 of the hot topping mix + 1/4 of the cold toppings
