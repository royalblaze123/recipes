Lean Beef Taco Bowl with Pinto Beans and Brown Rice

Prep: 18 min   Cook: 24 min   Total: 42 min

Ingredients

  • 1.5 lb lean ground beef 93%
  • 0.5 cup dry brown rice
  • 0.5 can no-salt-added pinto beans
  • 1 bag romaine hearts
  • 1 onion
  • 1 bell pepper
  • 1 lime
  • olive oil
  • cumin
  • chili powder
  • smoked paprika
  • garlic powder
  • black pepper
  • salt substitute
  • salsa

Hard rules enforced: Dinner validations (calories 600–650 Frank, sat fat ≤8g, sodium ≤700mg, fiber ≥10g, added sugar ≤6g). Quantities appear before items; fresh meat/poultry/fish use weights. Condiments/spices/seasonings have no measurements in Ingredients; all measurements are in Steps. No olives, no squash, no Brussels sprouts, no zucchini, no tofu/tempeh, no yogurt in dinners.

Step-by-step Instructions

  1. Cook brown rice: cook 1/2 cup dry brown rice per package directions (water in Steps only).
  2. Beef: brown lean ground beef with 2 tsp olive oil 7–9 minutes. Season with cumin, chili powder, smoked paprika, garlic powder, black pepper, and salt substitute.
  3. Veg: sauté onion and bell pepper 4–5 minutes; fold into beef.
  4. Beans: drain/rinse 1/2 can pinto beans; warm 1–2 minutes.
  5. Build bowls: romaine + rice + beef/veg + beans. Top with lime and measured salsa (1 tbsp Frank; 2 tsp Susan; 2 tsp Elise).
  6. Portion: Frank largest rice/beef share; Susan/Elise ~75%.
