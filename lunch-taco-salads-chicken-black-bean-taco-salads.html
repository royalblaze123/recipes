Chicken-Black Bean Taco Salads

Nutrition (approx)

Frank: 540 kcal • P 50g • C 46g • F 16g
Susan/Elise (each): 405 kcal • P 38g • C 34g • F 12g

Ingredients (purchase-clean)

  • 1.00 lb boneless skinless chicken breast
  • 1 can black beans
  • 1 bag shredded lettuce
  • 1 each avocado
  • 1/2 jar salsa
  • 1 each cucumber
  • 1 pint cherry tomatoes
  • 1 each lime
  • 4 tsp extra-virgin olive oil
  • 2 tsp chili powder
  • 2 tsp cumin
  • 2 tsp garlic powder
  • 1 tsp smoked paprika
  • 1 tsp black pepper
  • 1 tsp kosher salt

Steps

  1. Chicken: cook diced chicken with 2 tsp olive oil 8–10 minutes until 165°F. Season with spices.
  2. Beans: Can size note—use a standard 15 oz can black beans.. Drain/rinse. Use 1 1/2 cups beans; refrigerate remainder. Warm 2 minutes with the chicken.
  3. Dressing: whisk 1/2 cup salsa with 2 tsp lime juice and 2 tsp olive oil.
  4. Base: divide shredded lettuce into three bowls.
  5. Protein: add chicken-bean mix.
  6. Add-ins: add diced cucumber and halved tomatoes.
  7. Top: add salsa and measured diced avocado. Portion per Notes.
  8. Avocado: halve, pit, and dice. Use about 1/2 avocado total; refrigerate remaining avocado with the pit to slow browning.

Notes

Chicken + black bean taco salads for extra fiber, finished with salsa + avocado. Portions (per person): Frank: 1 large taco salad bowl + 1/3 of the avocado + salsa Susan: 1 medium taco salad bowl + 1/4 of the avocado + salsa Elise: 1 medium taco salad bowl + 1/4 of the avocado + salsa
