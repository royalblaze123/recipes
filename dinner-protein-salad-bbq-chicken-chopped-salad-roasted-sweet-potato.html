BBQ Chicken Chopped Salad + Roasted Sweet Potato

Nutrition (approx)

Frank: 645 kcal • P 58g • C 55g • F 16g • Sat 3g • Na 650mg • Fiber 10g
Susan/Elise (each): 484 kcal • P 44g • C 41g • F 12g • Sat 2g • Na 488mg • Fiber 8g

Ingredients (purchase-clean)

  • 1.75 lb boneless skinless chicken breast
  • 2.00 lb sweet potatoes
  • 1 head romaine lettuce
  • 1 bottle low-sugar BBQ sauce
  • kosher salt
  • black pepper
  • garlic powder
  • onion powder
  • smoked paprika
  • Italian seasoning
  • ground cumin
  • chili powder
  • red pepper flakes
  • extra-virgin olive oil
  • balsamic vinegar
  • apple cider vinegar
  • Dijon mustard

Steps

  1. Oven: heat to 425°F.
  2. Sweet potatoes: cube; toss with 2 tsp olive oil, smoked paprika, black pepper, modest kosher salt. Roast 22–26 minutes.
  3. Chicken: roast chicken seasoned with garlic powder and black pepper 12–15 minutes to 165°F; rest; slice.
  4. BBQ: toss sliced chicken with 2 tbsp low-sugar BBQ sauce total (measured).
  5. Dressing: whisk 2 tbsp apple cider vinegar, 2 tbsp olive oil, black pepper, and a small pinch of kosher salt.
  6. Salad: chop romaine; toss with dressing; top with BBQ chicken and sweet potatoes.
  7. Serve per Notes.

Notes

BBQ chopped salad that stays healthy because the BBQ is just a measured glaze. Portions (per person): Frank: 4 cups salad + 7 oz chicken + 1 cup sweet potato Susan: 3 cups salad + 5 oz chicken + 3/4 cup sweet potato Elise: 3 cups salad + 5 oz chicken + 3/4 cup sweet potato Tips (recipe-specific): Glaze chicken with BBQ at the end so you use less. Roast sweet potatoes until browned—browning = flavor.
