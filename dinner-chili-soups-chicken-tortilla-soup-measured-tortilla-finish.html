Chicken Tortilla Soup (Measured Tortilla Finish)

Nutrition (approx)

Frank: 630 kcal • P 58g • C 55g • F 16g • Sat 4g • Na 690mg • Fiber 12g
Susan/Elise (each): 472 kcal • P 44g • C 41g • F 12g • Sat 3g • Na 518mg • Fiber 9g

Ingredients (purchase-clean)

  • 1.75 lb boneless skinless chicken breast
  • 1 can black beans
  • 1 can no-salt-added diced tomatoes
  • 1 bag frozen fajita-style pepper blend
  • 1 carton low-sodium chicken broth
  • 1 package whole-wheat tortillas
  • 1 each lime
  • extra-virgin olive oil
  • kosher salt
  • black pepper
  • chili powder
  • ground cumin
  • garlic powder
  • onion powder
  • smoked paprika
  • Italian seasoning
  • crushed red pepper
  • bay leaves

Steps

  1. Pot: heat 2 tsp olive oil. Add frozen fajita pepper blend; cook 6–8 minutes until browned.
  2. Chicken: add diced chicken; cook 8–10 minutes until 165°F.
  3. Season with chili powder, cumin, garlic powder, onion powder, smoked paprika, kosher salt, black pepper.
  4. Tomatoes: Can size note—use a standard 14.5 oz can no-salt-added diced tomatoes.. Add 1 can.
  5. Beans: Can size note—use a standard 15 oz can black beans.. Drain/rinse and add 1 1/2 cups; refrigerate remainder.
  6. Add 2 cups low-sodium broth. Simmer uncovered 12–15 minutes until slightly thick.
  7. Tortilla strips: cut 3 tortillas into thin strips. Bake at 425°F for 6–8 minutes until crisp (flip once).
  8. Finish soup with 2 tsp lime juice. Serve with measured tortilla strips on top. Portion per Notes.

Notes

Tortilla soup comfort, but the crunch is measured and baked. Portions (per person): Frank: Soup + 1/3 of tortilla strips Susan: Soup + 1/4 of tortilla strips Elise: Soup + 1/4 of tortilla strips Tips (recipe-specific): Brown pepper blend first for depth. Add tortilla strips right before eating so they stay crisp.
