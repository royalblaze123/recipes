Herb Yogurt Turkey & Cheddar Sandwich + Crunch Veg

Chefy turkey-and-cheddar sandwich with a lemon-herb Greek yogurt spread, plus a crisp veggie side for a full, portable lunch.

Servings

3 servings (Frank, Susan, Elise).

Times

  • Prep time: 15 minutes
  • Cook time: 18 minutes
  • Total time: 33 minutes

Ingredients

Sandwich

  • 6 slices whole wheat bread
  • 1 lb boneless skinless turkey breast
  • 3 slices reduced-fat cheddar cheese
  • 1 cup nonfat Greek yogurt
  • 1 each lemon
  • 1 bunch fresh dill
  • 1 each garlic clove
  • 1 each cucumber
  • 1 lb bag carrots

Side

  • 2 hearts romaine hearts
  • 1 pint cherry tomatoes

Step-by-step Instructions

  1. Cook the turkey: Preheat oven to 425°F. Pat turkey dry, season lightly with pepper and dried Italian seasoning if desired, and roast on a parchment-lined sheet pan until 165°F in the thickest part (about 16–20 minutes depending on thickness). Rest 5 minutes, then thin-slice for sandwiches. Measure out cooked turkey portions below; refrigerate any extra.
  2. Make the herb yogurt spread: In a bowl, mix 1/2 cup Greek yogurt with 1 tsp finely grated lemon zest, 1 tbsp lemon juice, 1 tbsp chopped dill, and 1 small garlic clove grated. (Keep the remaining yogurt for other uses.)
  3. Assemble sandwiches (6 bread slices = 3 sandwiches): Toast bread if you want. Spread herb yogurt on bread. Portion turkey and cheese per person (below). Add thin cucumber slices for crunch.
  4. Prep the veggie side: Chop romaine and halve cherry tomatoes. Portion per person. Serve alongside carrot sticks and extra cucumber slices. Pack wet salad components separately if taking to work.

Portion Sizes

Portions (per person):

Frank: 2 slices bread, 4 oz cooked turkey, 1 slice cheddar, 2 tbsp herb yogurt spread, 1/2 cucumber (sliced), 1 cup romaine, 1/2 cup cherry tomatoes, 2 oz carrots

Susan: 2 slices bread, 3 oz cooked turkey, 3/4 slice cheddar, 1 1/2 tbsp herb yogurt spread, 1/4 cucumber (sliced), 3/4 cup romaine, 1/3 cup cherry tomatoes, 1 1/2 oz carrots

Elise: 2 slices bread, 3 oz cooked turkey, 3/4 slice cheddar, 1 1/2 tbsp herb yogurt spread, 1/4 cucumber (sliced), 3/4 cup romaine, 1/3 cup cherry tomatoes, 1 1/2 oz carrots
