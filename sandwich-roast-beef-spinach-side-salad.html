Roast Beef & Spinach Sandwich + Simple Side Salad (RBM Lunch)

Thinly sliced roast beef on whole-grain bread with spinach and mustard, plus a small side salad.

Servings

3 servings.

Ingredients

Sandwich

  • 6 slices whole-grain sandwich bread
  • 7.5 oz home-cooked beef roast
  • 2 slices reduced-fat provolone cheese
  • 3 tbsp yellow or Dijon mustard
  • 1 cup fresh baby spinach leaves

Side

  • 2 1/4 cups fresh spring mix (or romaine), loosely packed
  • 3 tbsp light vinaigrette dressing (store-bought or homemade, no added sugar)

Step-by-Step Instructions

  1. Lay out the whole-grain bread slices on a cutting board.
  2. Spread mustard evenly over one side of each slice of bread.
  3. On three of the slices, layer the thinly sliced home-cooked beef roast, splitting the total amount into Frank, Susan, and Elise portions as listed below.
  4. Top the beef on each sandwich with reduced-fat provolone (sliced or torn to divide for different portion sizes) and a handful of fresh baby spinach.
  5. Close each sandwich with a second bread slice, mustard side down, and press gently. Cut in half if desired.
  6. In a mixing bowl, add the spring mix and drizzle with the light vinaigrette. Toss gently to coat.
  7. Portion the dressed salad into three small containers according to the amounts listed below so that each person receives their side salad portion.
  8. Serve immediately or wrap the sandwiches tightly and pack the salads in separate containers to keep the bread from getting soggy.

Portion Sizes (Per Person)

Frank: 2 slices whole-grain bread, 3 oz home-cooked beef roast (thinly sliced), 1 slice reduced-fat provolone, 1 tbsp mustard, 1/3 cup fresh spinach, side salad with 1 cup spring mix and 1 tbsp light vinaigrette.

 

Susan: 2 slices whole-grain bread, 2.25 oz home-cooked beef roast, 3/4 slice reduced-fat provolone, 2 tsp mustard, 1/4 cup spinach, side salad with 3/4 cup spring mix and 2 tsp light vinaigrette.

 

Elise: 2 slices whole-grain bread, 1.5 oz home-cooked beef roast, 1/2 slice reduced-fat provolone, 1 1/2 tsp mustard, 3 tbsp spinach, side salad with 1/2 cup spring mix and 2 tsp light vinaigrette.

Nutrition (Per Person)

  • Frank: 425 kcal, 38 g protein, 34 g carbs, 12 g fat, 6 g fiber, 620 mg sodium
  • Susan: 395 kcal, 35 g protein, 31 g carbs, 11 g fat, 5 g fiber, 580 mg sodium
  • Elise: 365 kcal, 31 g protein, 29 g carbs, 10 g fat, 4.5 g fiber, 540 mg sodium
