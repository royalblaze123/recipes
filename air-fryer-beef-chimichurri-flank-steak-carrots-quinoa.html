Air Fryer Chimichurri Flank Steak with Roasted Carrots and Quick Quinoa

Prep: 18 min   Cook: 24 min   Total: 42 min

Ingredients

  • 1.6 lb flank steak
  • 1 bag carrots
  • 1 pouch microwave quinoa
  • 1 bunch fresh parsley
  • 1 bunch fresh cilantro
  • 1 lemon
  • 1 bulb garlic
  • olive oil
  • red wine vinegar
  • crushed red pepper
  • black pepper
  • salt substitute

RBM rules: quantities appear before items; fresh proteins use weight. Condiments/spices/seasonings are listed name-only; all measurements are in Steps. Water is referenced in Steps only.

Step-by-step Instructions

  1. Make chimichurri: finely chop parsley + cilantro (about 3/4 cup total). Mince 2 garlic cloves. Whisk 12 tsp olive oil + 8 tsp red wine vinegar + 2 tsp salt substitute + 1 tsp black pepper + 1/2 tsp crushed red pepper + 4 tsp lemon juice. Set aside.
  2. Prep carrots: cut into 1/2-inch sticks. Toss with 8 tsp olive oil + 2 tsp salt substitute + 1 tsp black pepper.
  3. Air fry carrots (Cosori): preheat 400°F for 3 minutes. Cook 14–18 minutes, shaking at reminders, until browned and tender.
  4. Season steak: pat dry. Rub with 2 tsp salt substitute + 1 tsp black pepper. (Chimichurri supplies most flavor.)
  5. Air fry flank steak: 400°F for 10–12 minutes for medium (flip at 6 minutes). Rest 8 minutes before slicing across the grain.
  6. Heat quinoa pouch per package directions. Stir in 4 tsp chimichurri per Frank portion; 3 tsp per Susan/Elise.
  7. Portion: Frank gets 6 oz sliced steak + ~3/4 cup quinoa + ~1 3/4 cups carrots + 2 tbsp chimichurri. Susan and Elise each get ~4.5 oz steak + ~1/2 cup quinoa + ~1 1/2 cups carrots + 1 1/2 tbsp chimichurri.
