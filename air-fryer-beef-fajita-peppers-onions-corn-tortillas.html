Air Fryer Beef Fajita Bowls with Peppers, Onions, and Warm Corn Tortillas

Prep: 18 min   Cook: 18 min   Total: 36 min

Ingredients

  • 1.6 lb skirt steak
  • 3 bell peppers
  • 1 onion
  • 6 corn tortillas
  • 1 lime
  • 1 bunch cilantro
  • 1 bag shredded cabbage
  • olive oil
  • cumin
  • chili powder
  • smoked paprika
  • garlic powder
  • black pepper
  • salt substitute
  • hot sauce

RBM rules: quantities appear before items; fresh proteins use weight. Condiments/spices/seasonings are listed name-only; all measurements are in Steps. Water is referenced in Steps only.

Step-by-step Instructions

  1. Mix fajita seasoning: 2 tsp cumin + 2 tsp chili powder + 1 tsp smoked paprika + 1 tsp garlic powder + 1 tsp black pepper + 2 tsp salt substitute.
  2. Slice peppers and onion into strips. Toss with 8 tsp olive oil + half the seasoning mix.
  3. Air fry peppers/onions (Cosori): preheat 400°F for 3 minutes. Cook 10–12 minutes, shaking at reminder, until charred at edges. Keep warm.
  4. Season steak: pat dry. Rub skirt steak with 4 tsp olive oil + remaining seasoning mix. Rest 5 minutes.
  5. Air fry steak: 400°F for 8–10 minutes (flip at 5 minutes) for medium. Rest 8 minutes; slice thin across the grain.
  6. Quick slaw (no yogurt): toss shredded cabbage with 6 tsp lime juice + 4 tsp olive oil + 1 tsp black pepper + 1 tsp salt substitute + chopped cilantro (about 1/3 cup).
  7. Warm tortillas: air fry 340°F for 2 minutes (stacked loosely), flipping once.
  8. Portion: Frank gets 6 oz steak + ~2 cups peppers/onions + 2 tortillas + ~1 1/2 cups slaw. Susan and Elise each get ~4.5 oz steak + ~1 1/2 cups peppers/onions + 1 tortilla + ~1 1/4 cups slaw. Add hot sauce at the table.
