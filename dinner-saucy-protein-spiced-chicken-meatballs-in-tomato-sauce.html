Spiced Chicken Meatballs in Tomato Sauce

Nutrition (approx)

Frank: 630 kcal • P 60g • C 48g • F 18g • Sat 5g • Na 680mg • Fiber 12g
Susan/Elise (each): 472 kcal • P 45g • C 36g • F 14g • Sat 4g • Na 510mg • Fiber 9g

Ingredients (purchase-clean)

  • 2.00 lb 99% lean ground chicken
  • 1 package whole-wheat breadcrumbs
  • 2 each eggs
  • 1 can no-salt-added crushed tomatoes
  • 1 can chickpeas
  • 1 bag frozen cauliflower rice
  • extra-virgin olive oil
  • kosher salt
  • black pepper
  • garlic powder
  • onion powder
  • Italian seasoning
  • smoked paprika
  • chili powder
  • ground cumin
  • crushed red pepper
  • extra-virgin olive oil
  • garam masala
  • turmeric

Steps

  1. Oven: heat to 425°F. Line a sheet pan and lightly oil.
  2. Meatballs: mix chicken with 1/2 cup breadcrumbs, 2 eggs, 2 tsp garlic powder, 1 tsp onion powder, 2 tsp garam masala, 1 tsp turmeric, 1/2 tsp kosher salt, 1/2 tsp black pepper.
  3. Form 18–20 meatballs; bake 13–15 minutes to 165°F.
  4. Sauce: in a pot, heat 2 tsp olive oil. Add 1 can no-salt-added crushed tomatoes and simmer 8 minutes.
  5. Chickpeas: Can size note—use a standard 15 oz can chickpeas.. Drain/rinse. Add 1 1/2 cups to sauce; simmer 2 minutes.
  6. Add meatballs to sauce and simmer 3 minutes to coat.
  7. Cauli rice: cook and dry 1 bag cauliflower rice 3 minutes.
  8. Serve: portion cauli rice + meatballs + sauce per Notes.

Notes

Cozy spiced tomato sauce meatballs with a big-volume base. Portions (per person): Frank: 8–9 meatballs + 1 1/2 cups cauli rice + sauce/chickpeas Susan: 6–7 meatballs + 1 1/4 cups cauli rice + sauce/chickpeas Elise: 6–7 meatballs + 1 1/4 cups cauli rice + sauce/chickpeas Tips (recipe-specific): Simmer sauce before adding meatballs so it tastes developed. Garam masala + turmeric makes it feel ‘rich’ without cream.
