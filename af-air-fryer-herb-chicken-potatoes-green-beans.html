Air Fryer Herb Chicken with Potatoes and Green Beans

Servings

3 plates (Frank 1.0×, Susan 0.75×, Elise 0.75×).

Times

  • Prep time: 15 minutes
  • Cook time: 25 minutes
  • Total time: 40 minutes

Ingredients

  • 1.5 lb boneless skinless chicken breasts
  • 1.5 lb baby potatoes
  • 1 bag frozen green beans
  • 8 tsp olive oil
  • 2 tsp bottled lemon juice
  • 2 tsp minced garlic
  • 2 tsp smoked paprika
  • 2 tsp Italian seasoning
  • 1 tsp onion powder
  • 2 tsp garlic powder
  • 2 tsp salt substitute
  • 1 tsp black pepper

Recipe

  1. Preheat the air fryer to 390°F for 3–5 minutes with the empty basket in place so it is hot when you add the food.
  2. Trim 1.5 lb boneless skinless chicken breasts of excess fat. Pat dry very well with paper towels, then cut into 3 similar portions so each person gets one piece.
  3. Scrub 1.5 lb baby potatoes and cut them into 3/4-inch chunks. Place the potatoes in a large bowl and toss with 3 tsp olive oil, 1 tsp garlic powder, and 1 tsp salt substitute until the pieces are evenly coated.
  4. Spread the seasoned potatoes in a single layer in the air fryer basket. Air fry at 390°F for 12–14 minutes, shaking the basket halfway through, until the edges are browned and the potatoes are just tender. Transfer potatoes to a warm bowl and cover loosely with foil to keep warm.
  5. While the potatoes cook, microwave 1 bag frozen green beans according to the package directions until just tender-crisp. Drain any excess liquid and set aside for serving.
  6. In a small bowl, whisk together 5 tsp olive oil, 2 tsp bottled lemon juice, 2 tsp minced garlic, 2 tsp smoked paprika, 2 tsp Italian seasoning, 1 tsp onion powder, 1 tsp garlic powder, 1 tsp salt substitute, and 1 tsp black pepper to make an herb rub for the chicken.
  7. Place the dried chicken portions on a plate or tray. Spoon or brush the herb rub over all sides of the chicken so each piece is well coated. Let the chicken rest in the seasoning for about 5 minutes while you prepare the basket.
  8. Return the warm potatoes to one side of the air fryer basket in a loose single layer. Arrange the seasoned chicken pieces on the other side of the basket, leaving a little space between pieces for air circulation.
  9. Air fry the chicken and potatoes together at 390°F for 10–12 minutes, flipping the chicken once halfway through, until the thickest part of the chicken reaches 165°F on an instant-read thermometer. If your basket is small, cook the chicken in two batches and keep finished pieces warm under foil.
  10. Right before serving, toss the cooked green beans with the remaining 0–1 tsp olive oil if desired for shine and flavor. Plate portions: Frank gets about 8 oz cooked chicken, 1 1/4 cups potatoes, and 1 1/4 cups green beans; Susan and Elise each get about 5.5 oz cooked chicken, 1 cup potatoes, and 1 cup green beans.

Notes

Portions (per person):

Frank – 1 plate (about 8 oz cooked chicken, 1 1/4 cups roasted baby potatoes, 1 1/4 cups green beans).

Susan – 1 plate (about 5.5 oz cooked chicken, 1 cup roasted baby potatoes, 1 cup green beans).

Elise – 1 plate (about 5.5 oz cooked chicken, 1 cup roasted baby potatoes, 1 cup green beans).

Tips (recipe-specific):

Cut the potatoes into even 3/4-inch chunks so they crisp evenly in the air fryer.

Use an instant-read thermometer and pull the chicken right at 165°F so it stays juicy and tender.
