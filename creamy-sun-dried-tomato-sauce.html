Creamy Sun-Dried Tomato Sauce

Category: Sauce • Yield: About 1 cup (16 tbsp) • AnyList-ready

Ingredients

  • 3/4 cup 2% plain Greek yogurt
  • 2 tbsp sun-dried tomatoes
  • 2 tsp extra-virgin olive oil
  • 2 tsp lemon juice
  • 1/2 tsp garlic powder
  • 1/4 tsp black pepper

Steps

  1. Measure 2 tbsp sun-dried tomatoes from the jar; pat dry if packed in oil and refrigerate remaining tomatoes.
  2. Finely chop the measured tomatoes.
  3. Whisk Greek yogurt, tomatoes, olive oil, lemon juice, garlic powder, and black pepper until smooth.
