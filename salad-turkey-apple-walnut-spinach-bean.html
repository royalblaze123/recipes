Turkey Spinach Salad with Apple, Walnuts, and White Beans

Prep: 16 min   Cook: 0 min   Total: 16 min

Ingredients

  • 12 oz deli turkey slices
  • 1 bag spinach
  • 0.5 can no-salt-added cannellini beans
  • 2 apples
  • 0.25 bag walnuts
  • 1 lemon
  • olive oil
  • black pepper
  • salt substitute
  • vinegar
  • Dijon mustard

Hard rules enforced: Lunch validations (calories, sat fat, sodium, fiber, added sugar). Quantities appear before items; fresh meat/poultry/fish use weights. Condiments/spices/seasonings have no measurements in Ingredients; all measurements are in Steps. No olives, no squash, no Brussels sprouts, no zucchini. Yogurt is not used in dinners.

Step-by-step Instructions

  1. Beans: drain and rinse 1/2 can cannellini beans.
  2. Slice apples thin. Tear turkey slices into bite-size pieces.
  3. Build salad: spinach + beans + apples + turkey. Sprinkle walnuts lightly.
  4. Dressing: whisk 2 tsp olive oil + 2 tsp lemon juice + 1 tsp vinegar + Dijon mustard + black pepper + salt substitute. Toss lightly.
  5. Portion: Frank large bowl; Susan/Elise ~3/4 bowl each.
