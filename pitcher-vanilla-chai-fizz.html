Vanilla Chai Fizz (Pitcher Size)

Yield: 8 drinks • Prep: 10 min • Total: 10 min

Ingredients (AnyList-ready)

  • 32 oz brewed chai tea
  • 8 oz simple syrup
  • 16 oz milk
  • 24 oz club soda
  • 8 cups ice

Steps

  1. Chill the brewed chai tea.
  2. In a pitcher, combine chai tea, simple syrup, and milk. Stir 20 seconds and chill.
  3. To serve: fill each glass with ice, pour in 6 oz chai base, then top with 3 oz club soda. Stir once, gently.

Notes

Stir gently—milk + soda can foam if poured too aggressively.

Batch rule: keep carbonation separate until serving for the best fizz.
