Shawarma-Style Chicken Bowls (No Yogurt Sauce)

Nutrition (approx)

Frank: 640 kcal • P 58g • C 65g • F 14g • Sat 3g • Na 700mg • Fiber 10g
Susan/Elise (each): 480 kcal • P 44g • C 49g • F 10g • Sat 2g • Na 525mg • Fiber 8g

Ingredients (purchase-clean)

  • 1.75 lb boneless skinless chicken breast
  • 1 bag baby spinach
  • 1 each cucumber
  • 2 each tomatoes
  • 1 each lemon
  • 1 cup dry brown rice
  • 1 jar salsa
  • kosher salt
  • black pepper
  • garlic powder
  • onion powder
  • smoked paprika
  • Italian seasoning
  • ground cumin
  • chili powder
  • red pepper flakes
  • extra-virgin olive oil
  • low-sodium chicken broth

Steps

  1. Rice: cook 1 cup dry brown rice with 2 cups water 35–40 minutes; rest 5 minutes.
  2. Chicken: toss chicken strips with 2 tsp olive oil, 2 tsp ground cumin, 1 tsp smoked paprika, garlic powder, black pepper, and 2 tsp lemon juice. Roast at 450°F on a sheet pan 10–12 minutes to 165°F.
  3. Salad: chop cucumber and tomatoes; toss with lemon juice and black pepper.
  4. Assemble bowls: rice + spinach + chicken + salad + 2 tbsp salsa per bowl (measured) as the ‘sauce.’
  5. Serve per Notes.

Notes

Shawarma comfort bowls with salsa + lemon as the sauce (no yogurt). Portions (per person): Frank: About 2 1/2 cups bowl Susan: About 2 cups bowl Elise: About 2 cups bowl Tips (recipe-specific): High heat gives shawarma-style browning fast. Use salsa as a moisture layer so the bowls don’t feel dry.
