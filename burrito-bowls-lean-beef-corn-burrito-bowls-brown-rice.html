Lean Beef & Corn Burrito Bowls (Brown Rice)

Nutrition (approx)

Frank: 650 kcal • P 58g • C 50g • F 21g
Susan/Elise (each): 488 kcal • P 44g • C 38g • F 16g

Ingredients (purchase-clean)

  • 1.50 lb 93% lean ground beef
  • 1 can corn
  • 1 bag frozen fajita-style pepper blend
  • 1 cup dry brown rice
  • 1 each avocado
  • 1/2 jar salsa
  • 1/2 tub 2% plain Greek yogurt
  • 1 each lime
  • 4 tsp extra-virgin olive oil
  • 2 tsp chili powder
  • 2 tsp cumin
  • 2 tsp garlic powder
  • 1 tsp smoked paprika
  • 1 tsp black pepper
  • 1 tsp kosher salt

Steps

  1. Rice: cook brown rice using your preferred method. For this recipe, plan to cook 1 cup dry brown rice (yields about 3 cups cooked). Use about 2 1/2 cups cooked for the bowls; refrigerate any remainder.
  2. Beef: cook ground beef in a nonstick skillet over medium-high 7–9 minutes until browned.
  3. Veg: cook frozen fajita pepper blend 5–7 minutes until hot and lightly browned.
  4. Corn: can size note—use a standard 15 oz can corn. Drain well. Measure out 1 1/2 cups corn; refrigerate any remainder.
  5. Season: add spices to the beef and stir 60 seconds. Add peppers and measured corn; warm 2 minutes.
  6. Yogurt-salsa drizzle: stir 6 tbsp Greek yogurt with 6 tbsp salsa until smooth.
  7. Avocado: halve, pit, and dice. Use about 1/2 avocado total; refrigerate remaining avocado with the pit to slow browning.
  8. Build bowls and portion per Notes.

Notes

Lean beef burrito bowls with a controlled corn add-in for sweetness and variety. Portions (per person): Frank: 1/3 of the brown rice + 1/3 of the protein mix + 1/3 of the toppings (salsa + avocado + yogurt-salsa drizzle) Susan: 1/4 of the brown rice + 1/4 of the protein mix + 1/4 of the toppings (salsa + avocado + yogurt-salsa drizzle) Elise: 1/4 of the brown rice + 1/4 of the protein mix + 1/4 of the toppings (salsa + avocado + yogurt-salsa drizzle)
