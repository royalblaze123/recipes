Air Fryer Garlic Shrimp with Cauliflower Rice and Asparagus

Servings

3 plates (Frank 1.0×, Susan 0.75×, Elise 0.75×).

Times

  • Prep time: 15 minutes
  • Cook time: 20 minutes
  • Total time: 35 minutes

Ingredients

  • 2.0 lb raw shrimp (peeled, deveined)
  • 2 bags frozen cauliflower rice
  • 2 bunches asparagus
  • 10 tsp olive oil
  • 8 tsp minced garlic
  • 4 tsp low-sodium soy sauce
  • 2 tsp black pepper
  • 2 tsp smoked paprika
  • 2 tsp salt substitute

Steps

  1. Asparagus: Trim woody ends. Toss with 4 tsp olive oil + 1 tsp black pepper + 1 tsp salt substitute.
  2. Shrimp: Pat shrimp dry. Toss with 4 tsp olive oil + 8 tsp minced garlic + 4 tsp low-sodium soy sauce + 2 tsp smoked paprika + 1 tsp black pepper + 1 tsp salt substitute.
  3. COSORI Dual Air Fryer: Preheat both baskets to 390°F for 3 minutes.
  4. Basket A (shrimp): Spread shrimp in a single layer. Cook at 390°F for 6–8 minutes, shaking at the reminder, until pink and firm.
  5. Basket B (asparagus): Cook at 390°F for 8–10 minutes, shaking once, until crisp-tender with browned tips.
  6. Cauliflower rice: In a skillet, sauté 2 cups cauliflower rice per person using the remaining 2 tsp olive oil (or water sauté). Season lightly with pepper and salt substitute; cook 5–6 minutes until hot and steam-dry.
  7. Plate portions: Frank gets 8 oz cooked shrimp + 2 cups cauliflower rice + 1 1/2 cups asparagus. Susan and Elise each get 6 oz shrimp + 1 1/2 cups cauliflower rice + 1 1/4 cups asparagus.

Notes

Portions (per person):

Frank – 1 plate (8 oz cooked shrimp, 2 cups cauliflower rice, 1 1/2 cups asparagus).

Susan – 1 plate (6 oz cooked shrimp, 1 1/2 cups cauliflower rice, 1 1/4 cups asparagus).

Elise – 1 plate (6 oz cooked shrimp, 1 1/2 cups cauliflower rice, 1 1/4 cups asparagus).

Tips (recipe-specific):

Dry shrimp before seasoning—wet shrimp steam and lose bite.

Steam-dry cauliflower rice in the skillet so it doesn’t taste like freezer water.

Storage + Reheat

  • Cool promptly; store in airtight containers in the fridge up to 3–4 days (typical safety guidance).
  • For best texture, reheat protein + veg in the air fryer at 350°F for 4–6 minutes, and reheat grains/beans in the microwave 60–90 seconds with a splash of water.
