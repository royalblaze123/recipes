One-Pot Turkey Broccoli ‘Alfredo-ish’ Protein Pasta

Nutrition (approx)

Frank: 650 kcal • P 60g • C 70g • F 14g • Sat 6g • Na 700mg • Fiber 10g
Susan/Elise (each): 488 kcal • P 45g • C 52g • F 10g • Sat 4g • Na 525mg • Fiber 8g

Ingredients (purchase-clean)

  • 1 box Barilla Protein+ pasta
  • 1.50 lb 93% lean ground turkey
  • 1 bag frozen broccoli florets
  • 1 carton skim milk
  • low-sodium chicken broth
  • cornstarch
  • 6 oz reduced-fat shredded mozzarella cheese
  • kosher salt
  • black pepper
  • garlic powder
  • onion powder
  • smoked paprika
  • Italian seasoning
  • ground cumin
  • chili powder
  • red pepper flakes
  • extra-virgin olive oil
  • low-sodium chicken broth

Steps

  1. Turkey: brown turkey with 2 tsp olive oil, garlic powder, black pepper, modest kosher salt 7–9 minutes.
  2. Liquids: add 2 cups low-sodium broth + 1 1/2 cups skim milk.
  3. Thicken: whisk 1 tbsp cornstarch into 1/4 cup cold water; stir in and bring to a gentle simmer.
  4. Pasta: add Protein+ pasta; simmer 10–12 minutes, stirring often, until al dente.
  5. Broccoli: microwave broccoli; drain well. Stir into pasta for the last 2 minutes.
  6. Cheese: stir in 6 oz reduced-fat mozzarella until melted.
  7. Rest 3 minutes. Serve per Notes.

Notes

Alfredo-ish comfort without cream—thick milk base, measured cheese, lots of broccoli. Portions (per person): Frank: About 2 1/2 cups (heaping bowl) Susan: About 2 cups (bowl) Elise: About 2 cups (bowl) Tips (recipe-specific): Drain broccoli well so the sauce stays thick. Rest 3 minutes so the sauce clings before serving.
