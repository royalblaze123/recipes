Salmon Cucumber Dill Wrap with Bean Salad Side

Prep: 20 min   Cook: 10 min   Total: 30 min

Ingredients

  • 3 whole wheat tortillas
  • 14 oz salmon fillets
  • 0.5 can no-salt-added cannellini beans
  • 1 cucumber
  • 1 lemon
  • 1 bunch dill
  • 1 bag microgreens
  • 1 pint cherry tomatoes
  • olive oil
  • Dijon mustard
  • black pepper
  • salt substitute
  • vinegar

Hard rules enforced: Lunch validations (calories, sat fat, sodium, fiber, added sugar). Quantities appear before items; fresh meat/poultry/fish use weights. Condiments/spices/seasonings have no measurements in Ingredients; all measurements are in Steps. No olives, no squash, no Brussels sprouts, no zucchini. Yogurt is not used in dinners.

Step-by-step Instructions

  1. Salmon: season with black pepper and salt substitute. Bake at 425°F for 10–12 minutes until flaky (or air fry 400°F for 8–10). Cool 5 minutes and flake.
  2. Bean salad side: drain/rinse 1/2 can cannellini beans. Mix with halved cherry tomatoes and diced cucumber. Dress with 2 tsp olive oil + 2 tsp lemon juice + 1 tsp vinegar + chopped dill + black pepper + salt substitute.
  3. Assemble wraps: add flaked salmon, microgreens, and extra cucumber sticks to tortillas. Add a light Dijon-vinegar spread. Roll tightly.
  4. Portion: 1 wrap each + bean salad side (Frank larger).
