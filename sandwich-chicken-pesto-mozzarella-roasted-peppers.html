Chicken Pesto-Mozzarella Sandwiches with Roasted Peppers and Baby Carrots

Prep: 12 min   Cook: 5 min   Total: 17 min

Ingredients

  • 6 slices whole wheat bread
  • 16 oz cooked rotisserie chicken
  • 6 slices reduced-fat mozzarella
  • 1 jar roasted red peppers
  • 1 bag microgreens
  • 1 bag baby carrots
  • pesto
  • black pepper
  • salt substitute

RBM Sandwich rules: 3 full sandwiches total (6 slices bread). Deli meats/cheeses listed as slice counts; slicing guidance lives in Steps. Condiments/spices are name-only in Ingredients; all measurements are in Steps. Produce is listed in shoppable units (fractional OK). Microgreens preferred.

Step-by-step Instructions

  1. Chicken: pull 16 oz cooked rotisserie chicken from the bird (about 1/2 rotisserie chicken). Chop into bite-size pieces.
  2. Optional toast: toast bread lightly so pesto doesn’t soak in.
  3. Roasted peppers: drain and pat dry. Slice into strips.
  4. Assemble 3 sandwiches: spread pesto on one side of each sandwich (about 2 tsp per sandwich). Layer chicken, mozzarella, roasted peppers, and microgreens. Close and press.
  5. Portioning: Frank gets ~7 oz chicken + 2 mozzarella slices. Susan and Elise each get ~4.5 oz chicken + 2 mozzarella slices (or 1 slice each to lower fat).
  6. Veg side: Frank gets ~1 1/2 cups baby carrots. Susan and Elise each get ~1 cup.
