Turkey Pepperoni Veggie Pizza with Classic Buffalo Wings

Prep: 18 min   Cook: 30 min   Total: 48 min

Ingredients

  • 1 pizza dough ball
  • 0.5 jar low-sodium pizza sauce
  • 8 oz reduced-fat mozzarella cheese
  • 3 oz turkey pepperoni
  • 1 bell pepper
  • 1 onion
  • 1 package baby bella mushrooms
  • 2.25 lb chicken wings
  • olive oil
  • hot sauce
  • light mayonnaise
  • garlic powder
  • black pepper
  • salt substitute
  • oregano

RBM rules: quantities appear before items; fresh meat/poultry/fish use weights. Condiments/spices/seasonings are listed name-only (no tsp/tbsp in Ingredients); all measurements are in Steps. Water is referenced in Steps only. No olives, no squash, no Brussels sprouts, no yogurt in dinners.

Step-by-step Instructions

  1. Pizza: preheat oven to 475°F. Stretch dough into 12-inch round on parchment.
  2. Slice peppers, onion, and mushrooms. Sauté mushrooms 4–5 minutes in a dry skillet to drive off moisture (optional but helps crisp pizza).
  3. Top pizza: spread ~1/2 jar pizza sauce, add mozzarella, then turkey pepperoni and veggies. Sprinkle oregano. Bake 10–12 minutes. Cut into 10 slices.
  4. Wings: pat dry. Toss with 2 tsp olive oil + 2 tsp salt substitute + 1 tsp black pepper + 1 tsp garlic powder.
  5. Air fry wings: 380°F for 24–26 minutes; then 400°F for 4–6 minutes.
  6. Buffalo toss: mix 3 tbsp hot sauce + 1 tbsp light mayo. Toss wings lightly.
  7. Portion: Frank 4 slices + 8 wings; Susan/Elise 3 slices + 6 wings.
