Mexican Stuffed Sweet Potatoes + Turkey Taco Filling

Nutrition (approx)

Frank: 650 kcal • P 55g • C 65g • F 18g
Susan/Elise (each): 488 kcal • P 41g • C 49g • F 14g

Ingredients (purchase-clean)

  • 2.00 lb sweet potatoes
  • 1.50 lb 93% lean ground turkey
  • 1 can black beans
  • 1 jar salsa
  • 6 oz reduced-fat cheddar cheese
  • kosher salt
  • black pepper
  • garlic powder
  • onion powder
  • smoked paprika
  • Italian seasoning
  • ground cumin
  • chili powder
  • red pepper flakes
  • extra-virgin olive oil
  • low-sodium chicken broth
  • low-sodium beef broth
  • low-sodium soy sauce
  • hot sauce
  • Dijon mustard
  • balsamic vinegar
  • apple cider vinegar
  • cornstarch

Steps (oven)

  1. Oven: heat to 425°F.
  2. Sweet potatoes: poke holes; bake 425°F 45–55 minutes until very tender.
  3. Turkey: brown turkey with chili powder, cumin, garlic powder, black pepper, modest salt (8–10 minutes).
  4. Beans/salsa: add drained/rinsed black beans (1 1/2 cups) + salsa (1 cup measured). Simmer 2–3 minutes to thicken.
  5. Stuff: split sweet potatoes; mash interior lightly with fork; top with turkey mix; sprinkle measured cheddar; broil 1–2 minutes to melt.
  6. Serve per Notes.

Notes

Big comfort with controlled portions: stuffed sweet potatoes + thick turkey-bean topping. Portions (per person): Frank: 1 large stuffed sweet potato + 7 oz turkey mix + 1 oz cheese Susan: 1 medium stuffed sweet potato + 5 oz turkey mix + 3/4 oz cheese Elise: 1 medium stuffed sweet potato + 5 oz turkey mix + 3/4 oz cheese Tips (recipe-specific): Keep topping thick so it doesn’t soak the potato. Broil briefly so cheese melts without drying filling.
