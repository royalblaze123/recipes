Dinner Soup Turkey Quinoa Taco

Mild, chefy turkey taco soup with quinoa, black beans, and corn—high protein with slow carbs built in.

Servings

3 servings.

Times

  • Prep time: 15 minutes
  • Cook time: 35 minutes
  • Total time: 50 minutes

Ingredients

Main Dish

  • 2 lb turkey breast
  • 1 carton low sodium chicken broth
  • 1 each yellow onion
  • 1 bunch celery
  • 1 bag quinoa
  • 1 can black beans
  • 1 can diced tomatoes
  • 1 can tomato sauce
  • 1 bag frozen corn
  • 1 bottle extra virgin olive oil
  • 1 jar chili powder
  • 1 jar ground cumin
  • 1 jar smoked paprika
  • 1 jar garlic powder
  • 1 jar black pepper
  • 1 each lime
  • 1 container nonfat Greek yogurt
  • 1 bunch cilantro
    • 

    Preparation Steps

    1. Prep veg: dice 1/2 the onion and slice 2 celery stalks. Chop cilantro (use about 1/4 cup; refrigerate remaining cilantro).
    2. Start base: heat a large pot over medium heat. Add 2 tsp olive oil, then add onion and celery. Cook 5 minutes until softened.
    3. Season: stir in 2 tsp chili powder, 1 tsp cumin, 1/2 tsp smoked paprika, 1/2 tsp garlic powder, and black pepper.
    4. Add liquid + tomatoes: add the carton of low sodium chicken broth plus 2 cups water, then add the can of diced tomatoes and the can of tomato sauce. Bring to a boil.
    5. Add turkey: cut turkey breast into 1-inch chunks and add to the pot. Reduce to a gentle simmer and cook 12 minutes.
    6. Quinoa: rinse 1/2 cup dry quinoa. Add rinsed quinoa to the pot and simmer 15 minutes, stirring once or twice.
    7. Beans + corn: drain and rinse black beans. Add beans and 2 cups frozen corn. Simmer 5 minutes until hot.
    8. Finish: squeeze in 1 tsp lime juice and stir in chopped cilantro.
    9. Serve: top each bowl with a measured 1 tbsp Greek yogurt.

      Notes

      Portions (per person):


      Frank – About 3 1/2 cups soup, topped with 1 tbsp Greek yogurt.


      Susan – About 2 2/3 cups soup, topped with 1 tbsp Greek yogurt.


      Elise – About 2 2/3 cups soup, topped with 1 tbsp Greek yogurt.


      Tips (recipe-specific):


      Keep the heat mild by using measured chili powder and no extra hot sauces.


      Rinsing quinoa reduces bitterness and improves texture.


      Greek yogurt adds creaminess without heavy dairy or extra saturated fat.
