Turkey Hummus Veggie Wrap Box

NOTES: Designed as a portable grab-and-go lunch using lean protein, high-fiber vegetables, and controlled smart carbs. Use low-sodium ingredients and avoid added sugars where possible. PORTION SIZES: Frank: largest portion (~1/3 of the total volume); Susan: medium portion (~1/4 of the total volume); Elise: medium portion (~1/4 of the total volume) (Frank = 1.0×, Susan/Elise ≈0.75× portions).

Ingredients

  • 3 large whole-wheat tortillas (8–9 inch)
  • 9 oz sliced low-sodium turkey breast (deli or leftover roasted)
  • 3/4 cup hummus (no added sugar, low sodium)
  • 1 cup shredded romaine or spring mix
  • 1 cup matchstick carrots
  • 1 red bell pepper, cut into thin strips
  • 1 small cucumber, cut into thin sticks (seeds removed)

Step-by-Step Instructions

  1. Lay out the whole-wheat tortillas on a clean surface.
  2. Spread 1/4 cup hummus evenly over each tortilla, leaving a small border at the edges.
  3. Divide sliced turkey evenly across the tortillas.
  4. Top each tortilla with shredded greens, carrots, bell pepper strips, and cucumber sticks.
  5. Roll each tortilla tightly into a wrap, tucking in the sides as you go.
  6. Slice each wrap in half on a slight diagonal.
  7. Place wrap halves into three grab-and-go containers so Frank has the largest share and Susan/Elise slightly smaller portions. Refrigerate until ready to eat.

Nutrition per Person

  • Frank: ~550 kcal, ~45 g protein, ~53 g carbs, ~17 g fat.
  • Susan: ~410 kcal, ~34 g protein, ~40 g carbs, ~13 g fat.
  • Elise: ~410 kcal, ~34 g protein, ~40 g carbs, ~13 g fat.
