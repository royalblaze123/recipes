Protein+ Spaghetti Mushroom-Parmesan Chicken

Nutrition (approx)

Frank: 614 kcal • P 67g • C 50g • F 18g
Susan/Elise (each): 460 kcal • P 50g • C 38g • F 14g

Ingredients (purchase-clean)

  • 1/3 box Barilla Protein+ spaghetti
  • 1.25 lb boneless skinless chicken breast
  • 4 tsp extra-virgin olive oil
  • 8 oz fresh mushrooms
  • 3 tbsp grated parmesan
  • 1 each yellow onion
  • 2 tsp garlic powder
  • 2 tsp Italian seasoning
  • 1 tsp black pepper
  • 1 tsp kosher salt

Steps

  1. Bring a large pot of water to a boil for the Barilla Protein+ spaghetti. (Use enough water to fully cover pasta; do not add water to Ingredients.)
  2. Pat chicken breast dry. Season with 2 tsp garlic powder, 2 tsp Italian seasoning, 1 tsp black pepper, and 1 tsp kosher salt. Sear in a nonstick skillet over medium-high 4–5 minutes per side. Reduce to medium, cover, and cook 3–5 minutes more until 165°F. Rest 5 minutes, then chop.
  3. Dice the yellow onion. Add to the skillet and cook 4–6 minutes until softened.
  4. Chop the mushrooms. Cook 6–8 minutes until moisture cooks off and mushrooms brown.
  5. Cook Barilla Protein+ spaghetti to al dente per box time. Reserve 1 cup pasta water, then drain.
  6. Toss pasta into the sauce/skillet. Add reserved pasta water 1–2 tbsp at a time until the sauce clings well.
  7. Portion into bowls using the Portions guidance in Notes.

Notes

Browned mushroom pan sauce with parmesan and chicken. Built on Barilla Protein+ for a higher-protein pasta plate with close-approx nutrition. Portions (per person): Frank: 2 oz dry Barilla Protein+ spaghetti + 1/3 of the skillet/sauce Susan: 1.5 oz dry Barilla Protein+ spaghetti + 1/4 of the skillet/sauce Elise: 1.5 oz dry Barilla Protein+ spaghetti + 1/4 of the skillet/sauce
