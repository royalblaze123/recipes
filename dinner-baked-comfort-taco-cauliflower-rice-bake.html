Taco Cauliflower Rice Bake

Nutrition (approx)

Frank: 625 kcal • P 60g • C 40g • F 20g • Sat 7g • Na 690mg • Fiber 14g
Susan/Elise (each): 469 kcal • P 45g • C 30g • F 15g • Sat 5g • Na 518mg • Fiber 10g

Ingredients (purchase-clean)

  • 1.75 lb 93% lean ground turkey
  • 2 bags frozen cauliflower rice
  • 1 can black beans
  • 1 can no-salt-added diced tomatoes
  • 6 oz reduced-fat shredded Mexican cheese blend
  • 1 each avocado
  • 1/2 jar salsa
  • kosher salt
  • black pepper
  • garlic powder
  • onion powder
  • smoked paprika
  • chili powder
  • ground cumin
  • Italian seasoning
  • extra-virgin olive oil

Steps

  1. Oven: heat to 425°F. Lightly oil a baking dish.
  2. Cauli rice: cook 2 bags cauliflower rice per package directions. Drain/steam off moisture in a dry skillet 3 minutes.
  3. Turkey: brown 1.75 lb lean ground turkey 7–9 minutes. Season with 2 tsp chili powder, 2 tsp cumin, 2 tsp garlic powder, 1 tsp smoked paprika, 1/2 tsp kosher salt, 1/2 tsp black pepper.
  4. Beans: Can size note—use a standard 15 oz can black beans.. Drain/rinse. Use 1 1/2 cups beans; refrigerate remainder.
  5. Tomatoes: Can size note—use a standard 14.5 oz can no-salt-added diced tomatoes.. Drain lightly and add.
  6. Mix: combine turkey mix with cauliflower rice. Spread into dish.
  7. Top: sprinkle 6 oz reduced-fat Mexican blend.
  8. Bake: 12–14 minutes until hot and bubbly. Rest 5 minutes.
  9. Serve: add salsa and avocado on top. Portion per Notes.

Notes

Big-volume taco bake that feels indulgent without a huge carb load. Portions (per person): Frank: 1/3 of pan + 1/3 avocado + salsa Susan: 1/4 of pan + 1/4 avocado + salsa Elise: 1/4 of pan + 1/4 avocado + salsa Tips (recipe-specific): Dry the cauliflower rice before assembling so it doesn’t water out. Salsa at serving time keeps leftovers tasting fresh.
