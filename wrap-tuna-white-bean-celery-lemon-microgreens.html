Tuna White Bean Salad Wrap with Celery and Microgreens

Prep: 16 min   Cook: 0 min   Total: 16 min

Ingredients

  • 3 whole wheat tortillas
  • 10 oz canned tuna in water
  • 0.5 can no-salt-added cannellini beans
  • 1 celery bunch
  • 1 lemon
  • 1 bag microgreens
  • 1 cucumber
  • olive oil
  • light mayonnaise
  • Dijon mustard
  • black pepper
  • salt substitute
  • garlic powder

Hard rules enforced: Lunch validations (calories, sat fat, sodium, fiber, added sugar). Quantities appear before items; fresh meat/poultry/fish use weights. Condiments/spices/seasonings have no measurements in Ingredients; all measurements are in Steps. No olives, no squash, no Brussels sprouts, no zucchini. Yogurt is not used in dinners.

Step-by-step Instructions

  1. Beans: drain and rinse 1/2 can cannellini beans. Pat dry.
  2. Tuna mix: drain tuna well. In a bowl mash beans lightly, then mix in tuna. Add 2 tsp light mayo + Dijon mustard + 2 tsp lemon juice + black pepper + garlic powder + salt substitute.
  3. Crunch: finely dice 2 celery stalks and fold into tuna-bean mix. Slice cucumber into sticks.
  4. Assemble wraps: divide tuna-bean filling across tortillas, top with microgreens, roll tightly.
  5. Side: cucumber sticks on the side.
  6. Portion: each person gets 1 wrap; Frank gets the heaviest fill.
