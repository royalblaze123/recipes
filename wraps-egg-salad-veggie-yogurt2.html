Egg Salad & Veggie Greek Yogurt Wrap

Lightened egg salad wrap made with Greek yogurt, whole eggs plus extra egg whites, and plenty of veggies in a high-fiber tortilla for a full lunch.

Servings

3 servings.

Times

  • Prep time: 15 minutes
  • Cook time: 10 minutes
  • Total time: 25 minutes

Ingredients

  • 1 large high-fiber whole-wheat tortilla
  • 2 medium high-fiber whole-wheat tortillas
  • 5 large eggs
  • 6 tbsp 2% plain Greek yogurt
  • 1 tsp olive-oil mayonnaise
  • 1 tsp Dijon mustard
  • 1 tsp lemon juice
  • 1/4 tsp garlic powder
  • 1/4 tsp onion powder
  • 1/4 tsp ground black pepper
  • 1/8 to 1/4 tsp kosher
  • 1 romaine heart
  • 1/3 cup celery
  • 1 red bell pepper

Preparation Steps

  1. Chop the romaine and measure out 1 1/2 cup for the recipe. Refrigerate remaining romaine. Chop the red bell pepper and measure out 1/4 cup for the recipe. Refrigerate any remaining pepper.
  2. Place the eggs in a saucepan, cover with cold water, and bring just to a gentle boil. Turn off the heat, cover, and let sit for 10–12 minutes. Transfer eggs to an ice bath until cool, then peel. Chop 3 of the whole eggs. For the remaining 2 eggs, separate and discard the yolks and chop just the whites.
  3. In a medium bowl, combine the chopped eggs and chopped egg whites. Add the finely diced celery and red bell pepper.
  4. In a small bowl, whisk together the Greek yogurt, olive-oil mayonnaise (if using), Dijon mustard, lemon juice, garlic powder, onion powder, black pepper, and a pinch of salt until smooth.
  5. Add the yogurt dressing to the egg and vegetable mixture. Fold gently until everything is evenly coated. Taste and adjust seasoning with a little more salt, pepper, or lemon juice as desired.
  6. Rinse and pat dry the romaine, then shred. Warm each tortilla in a dry skillet for 15–20 seconds per side or microwave briefly until flexible.
  7. Place the large (10-inch) tortilla on a cutting board. Spoon about one-third of the egg salad mixture down the center. Top with a generous handful of shredded romaine.
  8. Fold the sides inward, then roll up tightly from the bottom into a burrito-style wrap. Wrap in foil or parchment and slice in half if desired.
  9. Place one medium (8-inch) tortilla on the board. Spoon about half of the remaining egg salad down the center. Top with a moderate handful of romaine.
  10. Fold in the sides and roll firmly from the bottom. Wrap in foil or parchment and slice in half if desired.
  11. Repeat the process with the remaining medium (8-inch) tortilla, using the last portion of egg salad and romaine.
  12. Serve immediately or refrigerate the wrapped sandwiches for up to 24 hours. Keep tightly wrapped in foil or parchment for work or school lunches.
