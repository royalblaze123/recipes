Garlic-Soy Chicken & Veggie Udon-Style Bowls (Protein Pasta)

Nutrition (approx)

Frank: 650 kcal • P 60g • C 70g • F 14g • Sat 3g • Na 700mg • Fiber 10g
Susan/Elise (each): 488 kcal • P 45g • C 52g • F 10g • Sat 2g • Na 525mg • Fiber 8g

Ingredients (purchase-clean)

  • 1 box Barilla Protein+ pasta
  • 1.75 lb boneless skinless chicken breast
  • 1 bag frozen mixed vegetables
  • low-sodium soy sauce
  • cornstarch
  • kosher salt
  • black pepper
  • garlic powder
  • onion powder
  • smoked paprika
  • Italian seasoning
  • ground cumin
  • chili powder
  • red pepper flakes
  • extra-virgin olive oil
  • low-sodium chicken broth

Steps

  1. Chicken: sauté chicken strips with 2 tsp olive oil, black pepper, garlic powder 6–8 minutes.
  2. Veg: add mixed vegetables and cook 6–8 minutes until water evaporates and edges brown.
  3. Sauce: whisk 5 tbsp low-sodium soy sauce + 1 cup water + 1 tbsp cornstarch. Pour in; simmer 2 minutes until glossy.
  4. Pasta: cook Barilla Protein+ pasta per box directions; drain; toss into skillet to coat.
  5. Serve per Notes.

Notes

Udon-style comfort using Protein+ pasta and a glossy garlic-soy sauce. Portions (per person): Frank: About 2 1/2 cups (heaping bowl) Susan: About 2 cups (bowl) Elise: About 2 cups (bowl) Tips (recipe-specific): Brown the vegetables so it tastes like a stir-fry, not steamed veg. Thickened sauce lets you use less soy overall.
