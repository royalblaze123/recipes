Greek-Style Turkey Meatballs with Farro and Cucumber-Tomato Salad

Prep: 22 min   Cook: 22 min   Total: 44 min

Ingredients

  • 1.5 lb lean ground turkey 93%
  • 1 egg
  • 0.25 bag whole wheat panko crumbs
  • 0.5 cup dry farro
  • 1 cucumber
  • 1 pint cherry tomatoes
  • 1 lemon
  • olive oil
  • oregano
  • garlic powder
  • black pepper
  • salt substitute
  • vinegar

Hard rules enforced: Dinner validations (calories 600–650 Frank, sat fat ≤8g, sodium ≤700mg, fiber ≥10g, added sugar ≤6g). Quantities appear before items; fresh meat/poultry/fish use weights. Condiments/spices/seasonings have no measurements in Ingredients; all measurements are in Steps. No olives, no squash, no Brussels sprouts, no zucchini, no tofu/tempeh, no yogurt in dinners.

Step-by-step Instructions

  1. Cook farro: cook 1/2 cup dry farro with 1 1/2 cups water per package directions (about 20 minutes).
  2. Meatballs: mix turkey + 1 egg + 1/3 cup panko + 2 tsp oregano + 1 tsp garlic powder + 1/2 tsp black pepper + 1/2 tsp salt substitute + 1 tsp lemon zest (optional).
  3. Form 18 meatballs. Air fry at 400°F for 10–12 minutes until 165°F.
  4. Salad: dice 1 cucumber and halve 1 pint cherry tomatoes. Toss with 2 tsp vinegar + 2 tsp lemon juice + 1 tsp olive oil + 1/4 tsp black pepper + 1/4 tsp salt substitute.
  5. Serve meatballs over farro with salad on the side or on top.
  6. Portion: Frank 7 meatballs; Susan 5; Elise 5. Farro portion scaled similarly.
