Cheeseburger-Style Skillet Bowls

Nutrition (approx)

Frank: 630 kcal • P 60g • C 45g • F 20g • Sat 7g • Na 690mg • Fiber 13g
Susan/Elise (each): 472 kcal • P 45g • C 34g • F 15g • Sat 5g • Na 518mg • Fiber 10g

Ingredients (purchase-clean)

  • 1.50 lb 93% lean ground beef
  • 1 bag frozen cauliflower rice
  • 1 can pinto beans
  • 1 bag shredded lettuce
  • 1 pint cherry tomatoes
  • 1 each avocado
  • 1/2 jar salsa
  • 3 oz reduced-fat shredded cheddar cheese
  • kosher salt
  • black pepper
  • chili powder
  • ground cumin
  • garlic powder
  • smoked paprika
  • extra-virgin olive oil
  • onion powder
  • mustard

Steps

  1. Cauli rice: microwave or skillet-cook 1 bag cauliflower rice per package directions. Drain/steam off moisture in a dry skillet 2 minutes so it’s not watery.
  2. Beef: heat 2 tsp olive oil in a skillet. Add 1.50 lb lean ground beef; cook 7–9 minutes until browned.
  3. Season with 1 tsp onion powder, 1/2 tsp kosher salt, and 1/2 tsp black pepper.
  4. Beans: use one 15 oz can pinto beans. Drain/rinse. Stir 1 1/2 cups beans into beef; cook 2 minutes. (Refrigerate remaining beans.)
  5. Bowls: base each bowl with cauliflower rice and a handful of shredded lettuce.
  6. Top with beef-bean mix, halved cherry tomatoes, 1 oz reduced-fat cheddar total across the bowls (about 1 tbsp each), plus salsa and diced avocado.
  7. Optional: add 1 tsp mustard to Frank’s bowl for a cheeseburger vibe without extra calories.

Notes

Cheeseburger comfort bowl without the bun—still hits the craving. Portions (per person): Frank: 1/3 beef/beans + 1/3 cauli rice + toppings (1 tbsp cheese) + 1/3 avocado + salsa Susan: 1/4 beef/beans + 1/4 cauli rice + toppings (1 tbsp cheese) + 1/4 avocado + salsa Elise: 1/4 beef/beans + 1/4 cauli rice + toppings (1 tbsp cheese) + 1/4 avocado + salsa Tips (recipe-specific): Drain cauliflower rice well so the bowl stays “skillet-y,” not soupy. Keep cheese measured—salsa carries the flavor.
