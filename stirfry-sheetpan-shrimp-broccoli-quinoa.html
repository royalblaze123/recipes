Sheet Pan Shrimp and Broccoli with Garlic-Ginger Sauce over Quinoa

Prep: 18 min   Cook: 18 min   Total: 36 min

Ingredients

  • 1.5 lb shrimp
  • 1 bag frozen broccoli florets
  • 0.5 cup dry quinoa
  • olive oil
  • low-sodium soy sauce
  • rice vinegar
  • sesame oil
  • garlic
  • ginger
  • black pepper
  • salt substitute
  • cornstarch

Hard rules enforced: Dinner validations (calories 600–650 Frank, sat fat ≤8g, sodium ≤700mg, fiber ≥10g, added sugar ≤6g). Quantities appear before items; fresh meat/poultry/fish use weights. Condiments/spices/seasonings have no measurements in Ingredients; all measurements are in Steps. No olives, no squash, no Brussels sprouts, no zucchini, no tofu/tempeh, no yogurt in dinners.

Step-by-step Instructions

  1. Cook quinoa: rinse 1/2 cup dry quinoa. Simmer with 1 cup water (Steps only) 15 minutes; rest 5 minutes.
  2. Preheat oven to 425°F. Line a sheet pan.
  3. Broccoli: microwave frozen broccoli 3–4 minutes to thaw; pat dry. Measure 4 cups; refreeze remainder. Toss with 2 tsp olive oil + black pepper + salt substitute; spread on pan and roast 8 minutes.
  4. Sauce: whisk 2 tbsp low-sodium soy sauce + 1 tbsp rice vinegar + 1 tsp sesame oil + 1 tsp cornstarch + 2 tbsp water + grated ginger + minced garlic.
  5. Shrimp: pat dry; toss shrimp with half the sauce. Add shrimp to pan with broccoli and roast 6–8 minutes until shrimp are pink and curled.
  6. Finish: warm remaining sauce in a small skillet 1 minute until thick; drizzle over shrimp/broccoli.
  7. Serve over quinoa. Portion: Frank largest shrimp/quinoa share; Susan/Elise ~75%.
