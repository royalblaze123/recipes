Slow Cooker Barbacoa-Style Beef with Peppers and Cauliflower Rice

Prep: 20 min   Cook: 420 min   Total: 440 min

Ingredients

  • 2.0 lb beef sirloin
  • 0.5 carton low-sodium beef broth
  • 0.5 can no-salt-added diced tomatoes
  • 2 bell peppers
  • 1 onion
  • 2 limes
  • 1 bunch cilantro
  • 1 bag frozen cauliflower rice
  • olive oil
  • chipotle in adobo
  • cumin
  • oregano
  • black pepper
  • salt substitute

RBM rules: quantities appear before items; fresh meat/poultry/fish use weights. Condiments/spices/seasonings are name-only (no tsp/tbsp in Ingredients); all measurements are in Steps. Water is referenced in Steps only.

Step-by-step Instructions

  1. Slow cooker base: cut sirloin into 3–4 large chunks (don’t cube—prevents over-shredded mush). Add to slow cooker with 0.5 carton beef broth (16 fl oz) and 0.5 can diced tomatoes (include juices).
  2. Season: stir in 2 tsp cumin + 2 tsp oregano + 1–2 tsp chipotle in adobo + 2 tsp salt substitute + 1 tsp black pepper. Add juice of 1 lime.
  3. Cook on LOW 7–8 hours or HIGH 4–5 hours until very tender.
  4. Shred beef into large strands (not fine). Keep warm on LOW.
  5. Add peppers late for structure: slice peppers and onion into thick strips. Toss with 6 tsp olive oil + 1 tsp salt substitute + 1 tsp black pepper. Add to slow cooker for the last 35–45 minutes on HIGH so they soften but stay structured.
  6. Cauliflower rice (stovetop): heat a skillet over medium-high with 2 tsp olive oil. Add cauliflower rice; cook 6–8 minutes until hot and steam-dried. Season with 1 tsp salt substitute + 1 tsp black pepper + juice of 1 lime.
  7. Finish: stir in 1/2 cup chopped cilantro. Taste and adjust with lime.
  8. Portion: Frank gets ~6 oz beef + 1 1/2 cups peppers/onions + 1 1/2 cups cauliflower rice. Susan and Elise each get ~4.5 oz beef + 1 1/4 cups peppers/onions + 1 1/4 cups cauliflower rice.
