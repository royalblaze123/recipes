Lean Beef Fajita Bowls (Cauliflower Rice)

Nutrition (approx)

Frank: 650 kcal • P 58g • C 40g • F 23g
Susan/Elise (each): 488 kcal • P 44g • C 30g • F 17g

Ingredients (purchase-clean)

  • 1.50 lb 93% lean ground beef
  • 2 each bell peppers
  • 1 each yellow onion
  • 1 bag frozen cauliflower rice
  • 1 each avocado
  • 1/2 jar salsa
  • 1/2 tub 2% plain Greek yogurt
  • 4 tsp extra-virgin olive oil
  • 1 each lime
  • 2 tsp chili powder
  • 2 tsp cumin
  • 2 tsp garlic powder
  • 1 tsp smoked paprika
  • 1 tsp black pepper
  • 1 tsp kosher salt

Steps

  1. Heat a large nonstick skillet over medium-high.
  2. Beef: cook ground beef 7–9 minutes until browned. Drain excess liquid if needed.
  3. Veg: slice bell peppers and onion into strips. Add to the beef with 4 tsp olive oil and cook 6–8 minutes until browned at edges.
  4. Season: add chili powder, cumin, garlic powder, smoked paprika, black pepper, and kosher salt; stir 60 seconds to coat evenly.
  5. Cauliflower rice: microwave the frozen cauliflower rice per bag directions. Drain any excess moisture. Season with a pinch of black pepper and a squeeze of lime if using.
  6. Yogurt crema: stir 6 tbsp Greek yogurt with 2 tsp lime juice and 1 tsp lime zest until smooth.
  7. Avocado: halve, pit, and dice. For this recipe, plan on using about 1/2 avocado total; refrigerate the remaining avocado with the pit to slow browning.
  8. Build bowls with cauliflower rice + beef fajita mix + salsa + avocado + yogurt crema. Portion per Notes.

Notes

Classic fajita flavor with lean beef, kept on-plan by cauliflower rice and measured toppings. Portions (per person): Frank: 1/3 of the cauliflower rice + 1/3 of the fajita protein/veg + 1/3 of salsa + avocado + yogurt Susan: 1/4 of the cauliflower rice + 1/4 of the fajita protein/veg + 1/4 of salsa + avocado + yogurt Elise: 1/4 of the cauliflower rice + 1/4 of the fajita protein/veg + 1/4 of salsa + avocado + yogurt
