Breakfast Fried Rice Plates (Turkey + Eggs)

Nutrition (approx)

Frank: 650 kcal • P 55g • C 65g • F 16g • Sat 4g • Na 700mg • Fiber 10g
Susan/Elise (each): 488 kcal • P 41g • C 49g • F 12g • Sat 3g • Na 525mg • Fiber 8g

Ingredients (purchase-clean)

  • 6 each eggs
  • 12 slices deli turkey
  • 1 cup dry brown rice
  • 1 bag frozen mixed vegetables
  • low-sodium soy sauce
  • kosher salt
  • black pepper
  • garlic powder
  • onion powder
  • smoked paprika
  • Italian seasoning
  • hot sauce
  • Dijon mustard
  • extra-virgin olive oil
  • salsa
  • ginger

Steps

  1. Rice: cook 1 cup dry brown rice (2 cups water) 35–40 minutes; cool 10 minutes so it fries better.
  2. Veg: sauté 3 cups frozen mixed vegetables 6–8 minutes until browned and water evaporates.
  3. Turkey: chop and sauté turkey 2 minutes.
  4. Eggs: scramble 6 eggs and push aside.
  5. Combine: add rice; season with low-sodium soy sauce (measured) plus ginger and black pepper. Toss 2 minutes.
  6. Serve per Notes.

Notes

Breakfast fried-rice comfort with turkey and eggs, using brown rice. Portions (per person): Frank: 2 eggs mixed in + 1 1/2 cups rice mix Susan: 2 eggs mixed in + 1 1/4 cups rice mix Elise: 2 eggs mixed in + 1 1/4 cups rice mix Tips (recipe-specific): Dry the veg first so the rice fries instead of steams. Let rice cool a bit so it stays chewy.
