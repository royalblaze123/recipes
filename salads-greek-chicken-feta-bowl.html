Greek Chicken & Feta Salad Bowl

Greek-style chicken salad with light feta, cucumber, tomato, and lemon-herb yogurt dressing over greens for a full lunch.

Servings

3 servings.

Times

  • Prep time: 15 minutes
  • Cook time: 10 minutes (for chicken if needed)
  • Total time: 25 minutes

Ingredients

  • 10 oz cooked skinless chicken breast
  • 1 1/4 oz reduced-fat feta cheese
  • 6 cups chopped romaine lettuce
  • 1 cup chopped cucumber
  • 3/4 cup halved grape tomatoes
  • 1/4 cup thinly sliced red onion
  • 6 tbsp 2% plain Greek yogurt
  • 1 tbsp extra-virgin olive oil
  • 1 1/2 tbsp lemon juice
  • 1/2 tsp dried oregano
  • 1/2 tsp garlic powder
  • 1/4 tsp ground black pepper
  • 1/4 tsp kosher or sea salt

Preparation Steps

  1. Rinse and dry the romaine, cucumber, tomatoes, and onion. Chop the romaine, chop the cucumber, halve the tomatoes, and thinly slice the onion if using.
  2. Use leftover grilled, baked, or rotisserie-style chicken breast. Trim any skin and visible fat. Slice into bite-size strips or cubes. Measure out about 10 oz cooked chicken.
  3. In a small bowl or jar, whisk together the Greek yogurt, olive oil, lemon juice, dried oregano, garlic powder, black pepper, and salt until smooth. Taste and adjust seasoning as needed.
  4. In a large mixing bowl, combine the chopped romaine, cucumber, grape tomatoes, and red onion.
  5. Add the sliced chicken to the bowl and sprinkle the crumbled reduced-fat feta over the top.
  6. Pour most of the lemon-herb yogurt dressing over the salad (reserve a tablespoon or so if you like a little extra for drizzling). Toss gently until everything is coated but the greens are still fluffy.
  7. Add about 2 cups of the mixture to a large bowl or container for Frank, ensuring his portion has roughly 4 oz chicken and 1/2 oz feta. Add a small spoonful of any reserved dressing if desired.
  8. Divide the remaining salad between two smaller bowls or containers for Susan and Elise, about 1 1/2 cups each, making sure each has about 3 oz chicken and 1/3 oz feta. Drizzle with any remaining dressing.
  9. Serve immediately or refrigerate tightly covered for up to 24 hours. Keep chilled for work or school lunches.

Portion Sizes (Per Person, Approximate)

  • Frank (F · 1.0×): ~2 cups salad with ~4 oz chicken, ~1/2 oz reduced-fat feta, ~1 cup veggies, and ~2 tbsp lemon-herb yogurt dressing.
  • Susan (S · 0.75×): ~1 1/2 cups salad with ~3 oz chicken, ~1/3 oz feta, ~3/4 cup veggies, and ~2 tbsp dressing.
  • Elise (E · 0.75×): ~1 1/2 cups salad with ~3 oz chicken, ~1/3 oz feta, ~3/4 cup veggies, and ~2 tbsp dressing.
