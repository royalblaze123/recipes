Creamy Au Jus-Style Dip

Category: Dip • Yield: About 1 3/4 cups (28 tbsp) • AnyList-ready

Ingredients

  • 1 carton low-sodium beef broth
  • 3/4 cup 2% plain Greek yogurt
  • 2 tsp Worcestershire sauce
  • 1 tsp cornstarch
  • 1/2 tsp garlic powder
  • 1/4 tsp black pepper

Steps

  1. In a small saucepan, bring 1 cup broth from the 32 fl oz carton to a gentle simmer (reserve remaining broth refrigerated).
  2. Whisk cornstarch with 2 tsp water until smooth. Whisk into the simmering broth and cook 60–90 seconds until slightly thickened.
  3. Remove from heat and cool 2 minutes. Whisk in Greek yogurt, Worcestershire, garlic powder, and black pepper until smooth. Do not boil after adding yogurt.
