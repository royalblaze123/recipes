Sesame-Garlic Shrimp + Veggie Rice Bowls

Nutrition (approx)

Frank: 640 kcal • P 55g • C 65g • F 14g • Sat 2g • Na 700mg • Fiber 10g
Susan/Elise (each): 480 kcal • P 41g • C 49g • F 10g • Sat 2g • Na 525mg • Fiber 8g

Ingredients (purchase-clean)

  • 1.50 lb shrimp
  • 1 bag frozen fajita-style pepper blend
  • 1 bag frozen broccoli florets
  • 1 cup dry brown rice
  • low-sodium soy sauce
  • sesame oil
  • cornstarch
  • kosher salt
  • black pepper
  • garlic powder
  • onion powder
  • smoked paprika
  • Italian seasoning
  • ground cumin
  • chili powder
  • red pepper flakes
  • extra-virgin olive oil
  • low-sodium chicken broth

Steps

  1. Rice: cook 1 cup dry brown rice with 2 cups water 35–40 minutes; rest 5 minutes.
  2. Sauce: whisk 5 tbsp low-sodium soy sauce + 3/4 cup water + 1 tbsp cornstarch. Add 1 tsp sesame oil.
  3. Veg: sauté fajita pepper blend until water evaporates (6–8 minutes). Add broccoli (microwaved and drained) and sauté 2 minutes.
  4. Shrimp: add shrimp, garlic powder, black pepper. Cook 2–3 minutes until pink.
  5. Finish: pour in sauce; simmer 2 minutes until thick.
  6. Serve over rice per Notes.

Notes

Takeout-style shrimp bowls with sesame-garlic flavor and a measured sauce. Portions (per person): Frank: About 2 1/2 cups (heaping bowl) Susan: About 2 cups (bowl) Elise: About 2 cups (bowl) Tips (recipe-specific): Drain broccoli well so the sauce stays glossy. Sesame oil is potent—1 tsp is enough for big flavor.
