Tuna–Greek Yogurt Power Sandwich & Cucumber Slices

High-protein tuna and Greek yogurt sandwich on whole-grain bread with cucumber slices on the side.

Servings

3 servings.

Times

  • Prep time: 10 minutes
  • Cook time: 0 minutes
  • Total time: 10 minutes

Ingredients

Sandwich

  • 2 cans tuna in water
  • 6 tbsp low-fat plain Greek yogurt
  • 2 tsp Dijon mustard
  • 1/4 tsp garlic powder
  • 1/4 tsp black pepper
  • 6 slices high-fiber whole-grain sandwich bread
  • 1 cup lettuce
  • 1/2 cup tomato slices

Side

  • 1 1/2 cups fresh cucumber slices

Preparation Steps

  1. Open the tuna cans, drain them well, and transfer the drained tuna to a medium mixing bowl.
  2. Add the Greek yogurt, Dijon mustard, garlic powder, and black pepper to the bowl. Stir until the tuna is evenly coated and a creamy tuna salad forms.
  3. Lay out all six slices of whole-grain bread. Spread a thin layer of the tuna mixture on three slices to establish an even base layer.
  4. Continue adding tuna salad to create three sandwiches, giving Frank a slightly larger portion and Susan and Elise slightly smaller portions as listed in the portions section.
  5. Top each tuna-covered slice with lettuce or spring mix and a few tomato slices.
  6. Close the sandwiches with the remaining bread slices, press gently, and cut in half if desired.
  7. Slice the cucumber into rounds if not already sliced, then divide the slices onto three plates in the amounts described for each person.
  8. Serve each person one sandwich with their portion of cucumber slices.

Notes

Portions (per person):

Frank – 1 full sandwich made with about 4 oz tuna filling plus about 1 cup cucumber slices.

Susan – 1 full sandwich made with about 3 oz tuna filling plus about 3/4 cup cucumber slices.

Elise – 1 full sandwich made with about 3 oz tuna filling plus about 3/4 cup cucumber slices.

Nutrition (per person)

  • Frank: 400 kcal; 38 g protein; 32 g carbs; 11 g fat; 7 g fiber; ~540 mg sodium.
  • Susan: 360 kcal; 34 g protein; 30 g carbs; 9 g fat; 6 g fiber; ~500 mg sodium.
  • Elise: 360 kcal; 34 g protein; 30 g carbs; 9 g fat; 6 g fiber; ~500 mg sodium.
