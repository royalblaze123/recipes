Pesto Chicken Stuffed Cherry Peppers

Sheet Pan • Yield: 24 stuffed peppers

Ingredients (purchase-clean)

  • 24 each fresh cherry peppers
  • 16 oz fresh chicken breast
  • 2 oz part-skim shredded mozzarella
  • 4 tsp pesto
  • kosher salt
  • black pepper
  • garlic powder

Steps

  1. Heat oven to 425°F. Line a sheet pan with parchment.
  2. Cook chicken: season 16 oz chicken with 1 tsp kosher salt, 1 tsp garlic powder, and 1/2 tsp black pepper; air-fry 370°F for 10–12 min or bake 425°F for 18–22 min until 165°F; chop fine.
  3. Core 24 cherry peppers (remove seeds).
  4. Mix filling: stir 4 tsp pesto into chopped chicken; fold in 2 oz mozzarella.
  5. Stuff peppers; stand upright.
  6. Bake 16–20 min until peppers are tender and filling is hot.

Notes

Flavor-forward holiday poppers designed for snacking or a light meal. Portions (guidance): Pesto is potent—keep it measured for flavor without calorie creep. Snack portion: 3–5 peppers.
