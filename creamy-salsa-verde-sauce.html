Creamy Salsa Verde Sauce

Category: Sauce • Yield: About 1 1/8 cups (18 tbsp) • AnyList-ready

Ingredients

  • 1 jar salsa verde
  • 3/4 cup 2% plain Greek yogurt
  • 2 tsp lime juice
  • 2 tsp extra-virgin olive oil

Steps

  1. Measure 1/2 cup salsa verde from the jar; refrigerate remaining salsa verde.
  2. Whisk salsa verde, Greek yogurt, lime juice, and olive oil until smooth.
  3. Chill 10 minutes before serving.
