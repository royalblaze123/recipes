Chicken Verde Burrito Bowls

Nutrition (approx)

Frank: 535 kcal • P 50g • C 52g • F 16g
Susan/Elise (each): 401 kcal • P 38g • C 39g • F 12g

Ingredients (purchase-clean)

  • 1.25 lb boneless skinless chicken breast
  • 1 jar salsa verde
  • 1 can cannellini beans
  • 1 cup dry brown rice
  • 1 bag shredded lettuce
  • 1 each avocado
  • 1/2 jar salsa
  • 1 each lime
  • 4 tsp extra-virgin olive oil
  • 2 tsp cumin
  • 2 tsp garlic powder
  • 1 tsp black pepper
  • 1 tsp kosher salt

Steps

  1. Rice: rinse 1 cup dry brown rice. Cook with 2 cups water (or low-sodium broth): bring to a boil, cover, reduce to low, and simmer 35–40 minutes. Rest 5 minutes, then fluff.
  2. Chicken: cook diced chicken in a skillet with 2 tsp olive oil 8–10 minutes until 165°F. Season with cumin, garlic powder, black pepper, and kosher salt.
  3. Verde: jar size note—use a 16 oz jar salsa verde. Stir 1/2 cup verde into the chicken; refrigerate remainder.
  4. Beans: Can size note—use a standard 15 oz can cannellini beans.. Drain/rinse. Use 1 1/2 cups beans; refrigerate remainder.
  5. Combine: stir beans into verde chicken and warm 2 minutes. Finish with 2 tsp lime juice.
  6. Assembly: divide shredded lettuce into three bowls.
  7. Top with the verde chicken mix and measured rice.
  8. Add salsa (red) and measured diced avocado on top. Portion per Notes.
  9. Avocado: halve, pit, and dice. Use about 1/2 avocado total; refrigerate remaining avocado with the pit to slow browning.

Notes

Chicken verde burrito bowls with beans, topped with salsa and avocado. Portions (per person): Frank: 1 large burrito bowl (about 1/3 of the batch) + 1/3 avocado + salsa Susan: 1 medium burrito bowl (about 1/4 of the batch) + 1/4 avocado + salsa Elise: 1 medium burrito bowl (about 1/4 of the batch) + 1/4 avocado + salsa
