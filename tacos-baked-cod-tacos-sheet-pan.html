Baked Cod Tacos (Sheet Pan)

Nutrition (approx)

Frank: 525 kcal • P 42g • C 38g • F 18g
Susan/Elise (each): 394 kcal • P 32g • C 28g • F 14g

Ingredients (purchase-clean)

  • 1.25 lb cod fillets
  • 4/5 package whole-grain small tortillas
  • 1 bag spring mix
  • 1 each lime
  • 1 tub 2% plain Greek yogurt
  • 4 tsp extra-virgin olive oil
  • 2 tsp chili powder
  • 2 tsp cumin
  • 2 tsp garlic powder
  • 1 tsp black pepper
  • 1 tsp kosher salt

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 425°F. Line a sheet pan with parchment.
  2. Fish: pat cod dry. Rub with 4 tsp olive oil. Season with 2 tsp chili powder, 2 tsp cumin, 2 tsp garlic powder, 1 tsp black pepper, and 1 tsp kosher salt.
  3. Bake cod 10–12 minutes until it flakes easily and reaches 145°F. Rest 3 minutes, then break into large flakes.
  4. Tortillas: warm tortillas 20–30 seconds per side.
  5. Crema: stir 6 tbsp Greek yogurt with 2 tsp lime juice and 1 tsp lime zest.
  6. Greens: use spring mix as the base topping.
  7. Assemble tacos with cod + greens + crema. Portion 7 tacos per Notes.

Notes

Sheet-pan cod tacos with a simple yogurt-lime crema (mild and clean). Portions (per person): Frank: 3 tacos (use 3 tortillas) + 1/3 of the filling + 1/3 of the toppings Susan: 2 tacos (use 2 tortillas) + 1/4 of the filling + 1/4 of the toppings Elise: 2 tacos (use 2 tortillas) + 1/4 of the filling + 1/4 of the toppings
