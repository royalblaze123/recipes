Chicken Artichoke Brown Rice Bake

NOTES: Built as a complete dinner using lean protein, high-fiber vegetables, and controlled starch portions. Use low-sodium broth and minimal added salt to support blood pressure and kidney health. PORTION SIZES: Frank: largest portion (~1/3 of pan); Susan: medium portion (~1/4 of pan); Elise: medium portion (~1/4 of pan).

Ingredients

  • 1 cup uncooked brown rice, rinsed
  • 2 cups low-sodium chicken broth
  • 1 lb boneless, skinless chicken breast, cut into bite-sized pieces
  • 1 tbsp extra-virgin olive oil
  • 1 medium yellow onion, diced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 red bell pepper, diced
  • 1 can (14 oz) artichoke hearts in water, drained and quartered
  • 1 cup halved cherry or grape tomatoes
  • 1 lemon, zested and juiced
  • 1 tsp dried oregano
  • 1 tsp dried basil
  • 1/2 tsp kosher salt (or to taste)
  • Freshly ground black pepper, to taste
  • 1/4 cup plain 2% Greek yogurt
  • Olive oil spray for baking dish

Step-by-Step Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 375°F. Lightly spray a 9x13-inch baking dish with olive oil spray.
  2. In a saucepan, combine rinsed brown rice and low-sodium chicken broth. Bring to a boil, then reduce heat, cover, and simmer 25–30 minutes until rice is just tender. Remove from heat and fluff with a fork.
  3. While rice cooks, heat olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat.
  4. Add diced onion and cook 4–5 minutes until softened.
  5. Stir in minced garlic and diced red bell pepper and cook 2–3 minutes more.
  6. Add chicken pieces, season with a small pinch of salt and pepper, and cook 6–8 minutes until just cooked through.
  7. Stir in artichoke hearts, halved tomatoes, lemon zest, lemon juice, dried oregano, dried basil, remaining salt, and black pepper. Cook 2–3 minutes to warm through.
  8. In a large mixing bowl, combine cooked brown rice with the chicken and vegetable mixture. Let cool 2–3 minutes, then fold in Greek yogurt until creamy.
  9. Transfer mixture to the prepared baking dish and spread into an even layer.
  10. Bake uncovered 20–25 minutes until hot and lightly browned at the edges.
  11. Let casserole rest 5–10 minutes before cutting into portions for Frank, Susan, and Elise.

Nutrition per Person

  • Frank: ~560 kcal, ~48 g protein, ~55 g carbs, ~15 g fat.
  • Susan: ~420 kcal, ~36 g protein, ~41 g carbs, ~11 g fat.
  • Elise: ~420 kcal, ~36 g protein, ~41 g carbs, ~11 g fat.
