Basil-Garlic Chicken + Tomatoes + Barilla Protein+

Nutrition (approx)

Frank: 650 kcal • P 60g • C 75g • F 14g
Susan/Elise (each): 488 kcal • P 45g • C 56g • F 10g

Ingredients (purchase-clean)

  • 1.75 lb boneless skinless chicken breast
  • 1 box Barilla Protein+ pasta
  • 2 cups cherry tomatoes
  • 6 oz grated Parmesan cheese
  • fresh basil
  • low-sodium chicken broth
  • kosher salt
  • black pepper
  • garlic powder
  • onion powder
  • smoked paprika
  • Italian seasoning
  • ground cumin
  • chili powder
  • red pepper flakes
  • extra-virgin olive oil
  • low-sodium chicken broth
  • low-sodium beef broth
  • low-sodium soy sauce
  • hot sauce
  • Dijon mustard
  • balsamic vinegar
  • apple cider vinegar
  • cornstarch

Steps (stovetop)

  1. Pasta: cook Barilla Protein+ al dente; drain (reserve 1/2 cup pasta water).
  2. Chicken: slice thin; season garlic powder, black pepper, modest salt. Sear 3–4 minutes; remove.
  3. Tomatoes: add cherry tomatoes; cook 3–4 minutes until blistered. Add broth (1/4 cup) to deglaze.
  4. Combine: return chicken; add pasta; add Parmesan (measured) and a splash of reserved pasta water; toss 1–2 minutes to coat.
  5. Finish: tear basil over top; serve per Notes.

Notes

Fresh, bright pasta plate with controlled cheese and lots of tomato volume. Portions (per person): Frank: 7 oz chicken + 2 cups pasta bowl Susan: 5 oz chicken + 1 1/2 cups pasta bowl Elise: 5 oz chicken + 1 1/2 cups pasta bowl Tips (recipe-specific): Use pasta water to make a glossy sauce without extra oil. Blister tomatoes for sweetness without sugar.
