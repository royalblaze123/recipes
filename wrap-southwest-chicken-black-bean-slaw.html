Southwest Chicken Wrap with Black Bean Slaw

Prep: 18 min   Cook: 12 min   Total: 30 min

Ingredients

  • 3 whole wheat tortillas
  • 14 oz chicken breast
  • 0.5 can no-salt-added black beans
  • 0.5 bag shredded cabbage
  • 1 lime
  • 1 bell pepper
  • 1 onion
  • 1 bag microgreens
  • olive oil
  • cumin
  • chili powder
  • smoked paprika
  • black pepper
  • salt substitute
  • vinegar

Hard rules enforced: Lunch validations (calories, sat fat, sodium, fiber, added sugar). Quantities appear before items; fresh meat/poultry/fish use weights. Condiments/spices/seasonings have no measurements in Ingredients; all measurements are in Steps. No olives, no squash, no Brussels sprouts, no zucchini. Yogurt is not used in dinners.

Step-by-step Instructions

  1. Chicken: slice chicken into strips. Season with cumin, chili powder, smoked paprika, black pepper, and salt substitute. Cook in a skillet with 2 tsp olive oil 6–8 minutes to 165°F.
  2. Slaw: drain/rinse black beans. Toss cabbage + beans with 2 tsp lime juice + 1 tsp vinegar + black pepper + salt substitute + 1 tsp olive oil.
  3. Veg: thin-slice bell pepper and onion. Sauté 3–4 minutes (optional) or leave raw for more crunch.
  4. Assemble wraps: layer chicken, peppers/onion, slaw, and microgreens. Roll tightly.
  5. Portion: 1 wrap each; Frank gets the largest chicken share.

Southwest wrap that stays low-sat-fat and high-fiber using a black bean slaw base.
