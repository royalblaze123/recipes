Turkey Pepperoni Mushroom Pizza

NOTES: Built as a full dinner using ready-made whole-grain or thin crust, lean protein, plenty of vegetables, and controlled cheese to support A1c, cholesterol, and blood pressure goals. Use low-sodium sauce and minimal added salt. PORTION SIZES: Frank: ~40% of the pizza; Susan: ~30% of the pizza; Elise: ~30% of the pizza (Frank = 1.0×, Susan/Elise = 0.75× portions).

Ingredients

  • 1 ready-made 12-inch thin whole-wheat pizza crust
  • 1/2 cup no-sugar-added, low-sodium pizza or tomato sauce
  • 3/4 cup shredded reduced-fat mozzarella cheese
  • 2 oz turkey pepperoni slices, quartered
  • 1 cup sliced cremini or button mushrooms
  • 1/4 red onion, thinly sliced
  • 1 tsp dried oregano
  • 1/2 tsp garlic powder
  • Freshly ground black pepper, to taste

Step-by-Step Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 425°F. Place the whole-wheat crust on a pizza pan or parchment-lined baking sheet.
  2. Spread low-sodium tomato or pizza sauce evenly over the crust, leaving a small border.
  3. Sprinkle half of the shredded mozzarella over the sauce.
  4. Distribute sliced mushrooms and red onion evenly over the pizza.
  5. Scatter turkey pepperoni pieces over the top.
  6. Sprinkle remaining mozzarella, dried oregano, garlic powder, and a few grinds of black pepper over the toppings.
  7. Bake 12–15 minutes, or until the crust is crisp and the cheese is melted and lightly browned.
  8. Cool 3–5 minutes before slicing and portioning for Frank (~40% of the pizza) and Susan/Elise (~30% each).

Nutrition per Person

  • Frank: ~630 kcal, ~52 g protein, ~54 g carbs, ~20 g fat.
  • Susan: ~470 kcal, ~39 g protein, ~41 g carbs, ~15 g fat.
  • Elise: ~470 kcal, ~39 g protein, ~41 g carbs, ~15 g fat.
