Rotisserie Chicken Taco Salads

Nutrition (approx)

Frank: 535 kcal • P 50g • C 44g • F 16g
Susan/Elise (each): 401 kcal • P 38g • C 33g • F 12g

Ingredients (purchase-clean)

  • 1/2 rotisserie chicken
  • 1 bag shredded lettuce
  • 1 each avocado
  • 1/2 jar salsa
  • 1 each cucumber
  • 1 pint cherry tomatoes
  • 1 each lime
  • 4 tsp extra-virgin olive oil
  • 1 tub plain nonfat Greek yogurt
  • 2 tsp chili powder
  • 2 tsp cumin
  • 2 tsp garlic powder
  • 1 tsp black pepper
  • 1 tsp kosher salt

Steps

  1. Chicken: pull meat from 1/2 rotisserie chicken and chop small. Warm in a skillet 2–3 minutes.
  2. Season chicken with chili powder, cumin, garlic powder, black pepper, and kosher salt.
  3. Dressing (lunch-only yogurt): mix 1/2 cup Greek yogurt with 2 tsp lime juice.
  4. Base: divide shredded lettuce into three bowls.
  5. Protein: add seasoned chicken.
  6. Add-ins: add diced cucumber and halved tomatoes.
  7. Top: add salsa and measured diced avocado. Portion per Notes.
  8. Avocado: halve, pit, and dice. Use about 1/2 avocado total; refrigerate remaining avocado with the pit to slow browning.

Notes

Fast taco salad lunches using rotisserie chicken (fractional unit), topped with salsa + avocado. Portions (per person): Frank: 1 large taco salad bowl + 1/3 of the avocado + salsa Susan: 1 medium taco salad bowl + 1/4 of the avocado + salsa Elise: 1 medium taco salad bowl + 1/4 of the avocado + salsa
