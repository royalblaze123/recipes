Chicken Parm Sub Plates + Salad

Nutrition (approx)

Frank: 640 kcal • P 55g • C 60g • F 16g • Sat 6g • Na 700mg • Fiber 10g
Susan/Elise (each): 480 kcal • P 41g • C 45g • F 12g • Sat 4g • Na 525mg • Fiber 8g

Ingredients (purchase-clean)

  • 3 each whole-wheat sub rolls
  • 1.75 lb chicken breast cutlets
  • 1 package whole-wheat panko breadcrumbs
  • 1 carton egg whites
  • 1 jar marinara sauce
  • 6 oz reduced-fat shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1 bag shredded lettuce
  • 1 each cucumber
  • kosher salt
  • black pepper
  • garlic powder
  • onion powder
  • Italian seasoning
  • smoked paprika
  • extra-virgin olive oil
  • Dijon mustard
  • hot sauce
  • rice vinegar

Steps

  1. Oven: heat to 425°F.
  2. Chicken breading: Bowl 1: 1/2 cup egg whites. Bowl 2: 1 1/2 cups panko + Italian seasoning + garlic powder + black pepper.
  3. Bake cutlets 12–14 minutes, flip at 7, to 165°F.
  4. Warm 1 cup marinara (measured).
  5. Assemble subs: each roll gets chicken + marinara + mozzarella (total 6 oz across all subs). Broil 1 minute to melt.
  6. Salad: lettuce + sliced cucumber. Dress with 1 tsp rice vinegar + hot sauce + black pepper.
  7. Serve per Notes.

Notes

Chicken parm sub comfort with baked cutlets and measured cheese. Portions (per person): Frank: 1 sandwich + big salad side Susan: 1 sandwich + big salad side Elise: 1 sandwich + big salad side Tips (recipe-specific): Broil 1 minute after assembly for restaurant-style melt. Keep marinara measured; let the seasoned crumb carry flavor.
