Instant Pot Chicken + Black Bean Chili (Thick)

Nutrition (approx)

Frank: 650 kcal • P 60g • C 55g • F 16g
Susan/Elise (each): 488 kcal • P 45g • C 41g • F 12g

Ingredients (purchase-clean)

  • 1.75 lb boneless skinless chicken breast
  • 1 can black beans
  • 2 cans no-salt-added diced tomatoes
  • 1 each onion
  • 1 bag frozen corn
  • low-sodium chicken broth
  • kosher salt
  • black pepper
  • garlic powder
  • onion powder
  • smoked paprika
  • Italian seasoning
  • ground cumin
  • chili powder
  • red pepper flakes
  • extra-virgin olive oil
  • low-sodium chicken broth
  • low-sodium beef broth
  • low-sodium soy sauce
  • hot sauce
  • Dijon mustard
  • balsamic vinegar
  • apple cider vinegar
  • cornstarch

Steps (Instant Pot)

  1. Sauté: select Sauté (High). Add olive oil, diced onion; cook 3–4 minutes. Add garlic powder, cumin, chili powder, smoked paprika; bloom 30 seconds.
  2. Load: add diced tomatoes (2x 14.5 oz), broth (2 cups), and chicken.
  3. Pressure cook: seal. Pressure Cook (High) 10 minutes; Natural Release 10 minutes, then Quick Release.
  4. Shred: shred chicken in pot.
  5. Beans/corn: drain/rinse black beans (15 oz). Add 1 1/2 cups beans + 2 cups frozen corn. Sauté 5 minutes.
  6. Thicken: whisk 1 tbsp cornstarch + cold water; stir in; simmer 2–3 minutes until thick.
  7. Serve per Notes.

Notes

Instant Pot chili texture (not soup) with big volume and freezer-friendly thickness. Portions (per person): Frank: About 2 1/2 cups (heaping bowl) Susan: About 2 cups (bowl) Elise: About 2 cups (bowl) Tips (recipe-specific): Natural release keeps chicken tender. Add beans/corn after pressure so they keep structure.
