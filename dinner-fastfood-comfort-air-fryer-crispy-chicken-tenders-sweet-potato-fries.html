Air Fryer Crispy Chicken Tenders + Sweet Potato Fries

Nutrition (approx)

Frank: 645 kcal • P 60g • C 60g • F 16g • Sat 4g • Na 700mg • Fiber 10g
Susan/Elise (each): 484 kcal • P 45g • C 45g • F 12g • Sat 3g • Na 525mg • Fiber 8g

Ingredients (purchase-clean)

  • 1.75 lb chicken tenderloins
  • 1 carton egg whites
  • 1 package whole-wheat panko breadcrumbs
  • 1.75 lb sweet potatoes
  • 1 bottle sugar-free ketchup
  • kosher salt
  • black pepper
  • garlic powder
  • onion powder
  • smoked paprika
  • chili powder
  • ground cumin
  • Italian seasoning
  • crushed red pepper
  • cornstarch
  • low-sodium soy sauce
  • rice vinegar
  • hot sauce
  • Dijon mustard
  • extra-virgin olive oil

Steps

  1. Air fryer (Cosori dual-basket): preheat to 400°F.
  2. Fries: cut sweet potatoes into fries. Toss with 2 tsp olive oil, 1 tsp smoked paprika, 1/2 tsp kosher salt, 1/2 tsp black pepper.
  3. Cook fries: Basket A, single layer. 400°F for 14–18 minutes with shake reminder ON; shake at prompt.
  4. Tenders breading: set up 2 bowls. Bowl 1: 1/2 cup egg whites + 1 tsp Dijon. Bowl 2: 1 cup whole-wheat panko + 1 tsp garlic powder + 1 tsp onion powder + 1/2 tsp smoked paprika + 1/2 tsp black pepper.
  5. Coat: dip each tender in egg whites, then press into panko. Place on a tray.
  6. Cook tenders: Basket B, single layer. 400°F for 10–12 minutes with shake reminder ON; flip at shake prompt. Target internal temp 165°F in the thickest tender.
  7. Sync Finish so fries and tenders finish together if needed.
  8. Serve: portion tenders + fries per Notes. Dip: measured ketchup/hot sauce.

Notes

Fast-food crispy tenders at home, with a real side and controlled portions. Portions (per person): Frank: 6 tenders + 1 cup fries side + dip Susan: 4 tenders + 3/4 cup fries side + dip Elise: 4 tenders + 3/4 cup fries side + dip Tips (recipe-specific): Press panko firmly so you get crunch without deep frying. Flip at the shake prompt for even browning.
