High-Protein Pitas: Shrimp and Avocado Slaw Pitas

Prep: 18 min   Cook: 8 min   Total: 26 min

Ingredients

  • 3 whole wheat pitas
  • 1.1 lb shrimp
  • 1 avocado
  • 0.5 bag broccoli slaw
  • 1 lime
  • 1 clamshell microgreens
  • olive oil
  • black pepper
  • salt substitute
  • garlic powder
  • vinegar

Rules enforced: quantities before items; fresh meat/poultry/fish use weights; packaged items use fractional containers when partial. Condiments/spices/seasonings have no measurements in Ingredients; all measurements are in Steps. No olives, no squash, no Brussels sprouts, no zucchini, no tofu/tempeh. Lunch validations included in Notes.

Step-by-step Instructions

  1. Shrimp: pat dry 1.1 lb shrimp. Season with 1 tsp garlic powder + 1/2 tsp pepper + 1/2 tsp salt substitute. Sauté with 2 tsp olive oil 3 minutes; cool 5 minutes.
  2. Slaw: toss 1/2 bag broccoli slaw with 2 tsp vinegar + 2 tsp lime juice + 1 tsp olive oil + pepper; chill 5 minutes.
  3. Avocado: dice 1 avocado; keep separate until serving for best texture.
  4. Warm pitas: toast 3 pitas 2 minutes.
  5. Fill: add slaw, then shrimp, then avocado (Frank gets the most avocado; Susan/Elise ~75%).
  6. Top: add microgreens.
  7. Finish: 1 tsp lime juice per pita and serve.
