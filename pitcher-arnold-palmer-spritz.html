Arnold Palmer Spritz (Pitcher Size)

Yield: 8 drinks • Prep: 10 min • Total: 10 min

Ingredients (AnyList-ready)

  • 32 oz brewed black tea
  • 24 oz lemonade
  • 24 oz club soda
  • 8 cups ice

Steps

  1. Chill the brewed tea.
  2. In a pitcher, combine black tea and lemonade and chill.
  3. To serve: fill each glass with ice, pour in 7 oz base, then top with 3 oz club soda. Stir once, gently.

Batch rule: keep carbonation separate until serving for the best fizz.
