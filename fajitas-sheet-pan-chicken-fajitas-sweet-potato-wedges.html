Sheet-Pan Chicken Fajitas + Sweet Potato Wedges

Nutrition (approx)

Frank: 635 kcal • P 58g • C 38g • F 22g
Susan/Elise (each): 476 kcal • P 44g • C 28g • F 16g

Ingredients (purchase-clean)

  • 1.50 lb boneless skinless chicken breast
  • 2 each bell peppers
  • 1 each yellow onion
  • 4/5 package whole-grain small tortillas
  • 1.50 lb sweet potatoes
  • 6 tsp extra-virgin olive oil
  • 1 each lime
  • 1 tub 2% plain Greek yogurt
  • 2 tsp chili powder
  • 2 tsp cumin
  • 2 tsp garlic powder
  • 1 tsp smoked paprika
  • 1 tsp black pepper
  • 1 tsp kosher salt

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 425°F. Line a sheet pan with parchment.
  2. Veg: slice bell peppers and yellow onion into thin strips.
  3. Chicken: pat dry, slice into strips if needed, and season with 2 tsp chili powder, 2 tsp cumin, 2 tsp garlic powder, 1 tsp smoked paprika, 1 tsp black pepper, and 1 tsp kosher salt.
  4. Toss protein and vegetables with the listed olive oil. Spread into an even layer.
  5. Roast 16–22 minutes, stirring once halfway, until vegetables are browned at edges and protein is fully cooked (chicken 165°F; shrimp opaque; steak 145°F for medium; pork 145°F).
  6. Tortillas: warm tortillas in a dry skillet 20–30 seconds per side or wrap in foil and warm in the oven 3–4 minutes.
  7. Sweet potatoes: cut into 1/2-inch wedges. Toss with 2 tsp olive oil and 1/2 tsp kosher salt. Roast on a second sheet pan at 425°F for 18–22 minutes, flipping once, until browned and tender.
  8. Yogurt-lime crema: stir 6 tbsp Greek yogurt with 2 tsp lime juice and 1 tsp lime zest.
  9. Serve fajita mix with tortillas, crema, and sweet potato wedges. Portion per Notes.

Notes

Classic chicken fajitas with sweet potato wedges and a yogurt-lime crema. Portions (per person): Frank: Build a plate: 3 tortillas + 1/3 of the fajita mix + 1/3 of the side Susan: Build a plate: 2 tortillas + 1/4 of the fajita mix + 1/4 of the side Elise: Build a plate: 2 tortillas + 1/4 of the fajita mix + 1/4 of the side
