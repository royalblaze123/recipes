Slow Cooker Moroccan-Style Chicken with Chickpeas and Carrots (with Couscous)

Prep: 20 min   Cook: 360 min   Total: 380 min

Ingredients

  • 2.0 lb chicken breast
  • 0.75 carton low-sodium chicken broth
  • 0.5 can chickpeas
  • 1 onion
  • 1 bag carrots
  • 1 lemon
  • 1 bunch cilantro
  • 0.33 box whole-wheat couscous
  • olive oil
  • tomato paste
  • cumin
  • cinnamon
  • smoked paprika
  • black pepper
  • salt substitute

RBM rules: quantities appear before items; fresh meat/poultry/fish use weights. Condiments/spices/seasonings are name-only (no tsp/tbsp in Ingredients); all measurements are in Steps. Water is referenced in Steps only.

Step-by-step Instructions

  1. Slow cooker: add chicken breast, sliced onion, and thick carrot coins (1/2-inch).
  2. Add 0.75 carton broth (24 fl oz) + 2 tbsp tomato paste.
  3. Season: add 2 tsp cumin + 1/2 tsp cinnamon + 1 tsp smoked paprika + 1 tsp black pepper + 2 tsp salt substitute. Add zest of 1 lemon.
  4. Cook on LOW 5–6 hours or HIGH 3–4 hours until chicken reaches 165°F.
  5. Remove chicken; shred into large strands and return.
  6. Add 0.5 can chickpeas (drained/rinsed) for the last 25–30 minutes on HIGH so they stay intact.
  7. Couscous (separate): bring 1 1/4 cups water to a boil. Stir in whole-wheat couscous (use ~1 cup dry from the 0.33 box) + 1 tsp olive oil + 1 tsp salt substitute. Cover, remove from heat 5 minutes; fluff.
  8. Finish: stir in 1/2 cup chopped cilantro and 4 tsp lemon juice.
  9. Portion: Frank gets ~6 oz chicken + ~1 1/2 cups chickpea/carrot base + ~3/4 cup couscous. Susan and Elise each get ~4.5 oz chicken + ~1 1/4 cups base + ~1/2 cup couscous.
