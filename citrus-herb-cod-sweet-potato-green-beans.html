Citrus Herb Cod + Sweet Potato + Green Beans

Sheet-pan citrus-herb cod with roasted sweet potato wedges and green beans.

Servings

3 servings (Frank, Susan, Elise).

Times

  • Prep time: 20 minutes
  • Cook time: 25 minutes
  • Total time: 45 minutes

Ingredients

Main

  • 2 lb cod fillets
  • 3 each sweet potato
  • 1 bag green beans
  • 1 each lemon
  • 3 cloves garlic
  • 2 tbsp extra-virgin olive oil
  • 1 tsp dried thyme
  • 1 tsp dried oregano
  • 1 tsp ground black pepper

Step-by-step Instructions

  1. Roast potatoes: Heat oven to 425°F. Scrub sweet potatoes. Cut 2 sweet potatoes into wedges for dinner; refrigerate the third potato for another meal. Toss wedges with 2 tsp olive oil, thyme, oregano, and black pepper. Roast 20 minutes, flipping once.
  2. Prep cod: Pat cod dry. Mince garlic and zest lemon. Mix 2 tsp olive oil with garlic, lemon zest, and black pepper. Brush over cod.
  3. Green beans: Toss green beans with remaining olive oil and black pepper.
  4. Roast: Push potatoes to one side, add cod and green beans to the sheet pan. Roast 10–12 minutes until cod flakes and beans are blistered-tender.
  5. Finish: Squeeze lemon over cod and green beans right before serving.

Portion Sizes

Portions (per person):
Frank: 7 oz cooked cod + 1 medium sweet potato + 2 cups green beans

Susan: 5.25 oz cooked cod + 3/4 medium sweet potato + 1 1/2 cups green beans

Elise: 5.25 oz cooked cod + 3/4 medium sweet potato + 1 1/2 cups green beans
