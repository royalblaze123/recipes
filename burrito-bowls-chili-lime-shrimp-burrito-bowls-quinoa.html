Chili-Lime Shrimp Burrito Bowls (Quinoa)

Nutrition (approx)

Frank: 640 kcal • P 56g • C 48g • F 20g
Susan/Elise (each): 480 kcal • P 42g • C 36g • F 15g

Ingredients (purchase-clean)

  • 1.50 lb raw shrimp
  • 1 can black beans
  • 1 bag frozen fajita-style pepper blend
  • 3/4 cup dry quinoa
  • 1 each avocado
  • 1/2 jar salsa
  • 1/2 tub 2% plain Greek yogurt
  • 1 each lime
  • 6 tsp extra-virgin olive oil
  • 2 tsp chili powder
  • 2 tsp garlic powder
  • 1 tsp black pepper
  • 1 tsp kosher salt

Steps

  1. Quinoa: cook quinoa per package directions. For this recipe, plan 3/4 cup dry quinoa (yields about 2 1/4 cups cooked). Use all cooked quinoa for the bowls.
  2. Veg: cook frozen fajita pepper blend in a skillet with 4 tsp olive oil 6–8 minutes until browned at edges.
  3. Shrimp: pat dry. Season with chili powder, garlic powder, black pepper, and kosher salt.
  4. Cook shrimp in the same skillet with remaining 2 tsp olive oil for 2–3 minutes total, flipping once, until opaque.
  5. Beans: can size note—use a standard 15 oz can black beans. Drain and rinse. Measure out 1 1/2 cups beans; refrigerate any remainder.
  6. Combine: add measured beans and cooked peppers to the shrimp and warm 1 minute. Finish with 2 tsp lime juice.
  7. Yogurt-salsa drizzle: stir 6 tbsp Greek yogurt with 6 tbsp salsa until smooth.
  8. Avocado: halve, pit, and dice. Use about 1/2 avocado total; refrigerate remaining avocado with the pit to slow browning.
  9. Build bowls and portion per Notes.

Notes

Shrimp burrito bowls with quinoa and the full salsa + avocado finish. Portions (per person): Frank: 1/3 of the quinoa + 1/3 of the protein mix + 1/3 of the toppings (salsa + avocado + yogurt-salsa drizzle) Susan: 1/4 of the quinoa + 1/4 of the protein mix + 1/4 of the toppings (salsa + avocado + yogurt-salsa drizzle) Elise: 1/4 of the quinoa + 1/4 of the protein mix + 1/4 of the toppings (salsa + avocado + yogurt-salsa drizzle)
