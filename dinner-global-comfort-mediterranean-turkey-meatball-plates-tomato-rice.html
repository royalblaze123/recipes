Mediterranean Turkey Meatball Plates + Tomato Rice

Nutrition (approx)

Frank: 650 kcal • P 58g • C 70g • F 14g • Sat 4g • Na 700mg • Fiber 10g
Susan/Elise (each): 488 kcal • P 44g • C 52g • F 10g • Sat 3g • Na 525mg • Fiber 8g

Ingredients (purchase-clean)

  • 1.75 lb 93% lean ground turkey
  • 1 each onion
  • 1 jar marinara sauce
  • 1 can no-salt-added diced tomatoes
  • 1 cup dry brown rice
  • 1 bag baby spinach
  • kosher salt
  • black pepper
  • garlic powder
  • onion powder
  • smoked paprika
  • Italian seasoning
  • ground cumin
  • chili powder
  • red pepper flakes
  • extra-virgin olive oil
  • low-sodium chicken broth

Steps

  1. Rice: cook 1 cup dry brown rice with 2 cups water 35–40 minutes; rest 5 minutes. Stir in 1/2 cup marinara (measured) for tomato rice.
  2. Meatballs: mix turkey with black pepper, garlic powder, onion powder, Italian seasoning, and a modest pinch of kosher salt. Form 18 meatballs.
  3. Bake meatballs at 425°F for 14–16 minutes to 165°F.
  4. Sauce: simmer 2 cups marinara (measured) + 1 can diced tomatoes (14.5 oz) 8 minutes to thicken.
  5. Spinach: stir spinach into sauce until wilted.
  6. Serve meatballs over tomato rice per Notes.

Notes

Meatball comfort with tomato rice and spinach (Mediterranean vibe, no cream). Portions (per person): Frank: 6 meatballs + 1 cup tomato rice + 1 cup spinach sauce Susan: 5 meatballs + 3/4 cup tomato rice + 3/4 cup sauce Elise: 5 meatballs + 3/4 cup tomato rice + 3/4 cup sauce Tips (recipe-specific): Bake meatballs hot so they brown without frying. Use spinach in the sauce for volume and fiber.
