Chicken Dijon Apple Sandwich

Servings

3 sandwiches (Frank 1.0×, Susan 0.75×, Elise 0.75×).

Times

  • Prep time: 15 minutes
  • Cook time: 0 minutes
  • Total time: 15 minutes

Ingredients

  • 6 slices whole wheat bread
  • 2 cans canned chicken breast
  • 6 tsp Dijon mustard
  • 4 tsp olive oil
  • 4 slices reduced-fat cheddar cheese
  • 1 apple
  • 1/5 bunch celery

Recipe

  1. Open 2 cans canned chicken breast and drain well. In a bowl, break up the chicken with a fork.
  2. From 1/5 bunch celery, finely chop 2 stalks (about 1 cup). Store the remaining celery bunch.
  3. Mix chicken salad: add chopped celery + 6 tsp Dijon mustard + 4 tsp olive oil to the chicken and stir until coated.
  4. Thinly slice 1 apple. Portion apple slices: Frank uses about 1/3 of the apple slices; Susan 1/3; Elise 1/3. Store any extra slices in a container.
  5. Frank sandwich: use 2 bread slices; add about 1/2 of the chicken mixture + 2 cheese slices + apple slices; close and wrap.
  6. Susan sandwich: use 2 bread slices; add about 1/4 of the chicken mixture + 1 cheese slice + apple slices; close and wrap.
  7. Elise sandwich: use 2 bread slices; add about 1/4 of the chicken mixture + 1 cheese slice + apple slices; close and wrap.

Notes

Portions (per person):

Frank – 1 sandwich (about 1/2 chicken salad, 2 cheddar slices, ~1/3 apple, 2 tsp olive oil share).

Susan – 1 sandwich (about 1/4 chicken salad, 1 cheddar slice, ~1/3 apple, 1 tsp olive oil share).

Elise – 1 sandwich (about 1/4 chicken salad, 1 cheddar slice, ~1/3 apple, 1 tsp olive oil share).

Tips (recipe-specific):

Drain chicken well and break it up finely so it spreads evenly without tearing the bread.

If you prefer it creamier, add 1–2 tsp light mayonnaise per sandwich (count those tsp in Ingredients).
