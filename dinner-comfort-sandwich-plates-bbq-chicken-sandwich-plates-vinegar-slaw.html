BBQ Chicken Sandwich Plates + Vinegar Slaw

Nutrition (approx)

Frank: 640 kcal • P 55g • C 60g • F 16g • Sat 4g • Na 700mg • Fiber 10g
Susan/Elise (each): 480 kcal • P 41g • C 45g • F 12g • Sat 3g • Na 525mg • Fiber 8g

Ingredients (purchase-clean)

  • 12 slices whole-wheat sandwich bread
  • 1.75 lb boneless skinless chicken breast
  • 1 bottle low-sugar BBQ sauce
  • 1 bag shredded cabbage
  • rice vinegar
  • kosher salt
  • black pepper
  • garlic powder
  • onion powder
  • Italian seasoning
  • smoked paprika
  • extra-virgin olive oil
  • Dijon mustard
  • hot sauce
  • rice vinegar

Steps

  1. Chicken: poach chicken in a pot with water 12–14 minutes to 165°F. Shred.
  2. BBQ: toss shredded chicken with 3 tbsp low-sugar BBQ sauce (measured) and black pepper.
  3. Slaw: toss cabbage with 2 tbsp rice vinegar, black pepper, and a pinch of kosher salt.
  4. Sandwiches: make 3 sandwiches (1 per person) with shredded BBQ chicken and slaw.
  5. Toast lightly in skillet 2 minutes per side if desired. Serve per Notes.

Notes

BBQ sandwich comfort, but vinegar slaw keeps it bright and not heavy. Portions (per person): Frank: 1 sandwich + 1 cup slaw side Susan: 1 sandwich + 3/4 cup slaw side Elise: 1 sandwich + 3/4 cup slaw side Tips (recipe-specific): Keep BBQ measured; vinegar slaw gives the ‘BBQ joint’ feel. Shredded chicken stays moist and reheats well.
