High-Protein Pitas: Turkey Swiss with Horseradish Crunch

Prep: 16 min   Cook: 4 min   Total: 20 min

Ingredients

  • 3 whole wheat pitas
  • 14 slices deli turkey
  • 6 slices Swiss cheese
  • 1 bunch celery
  • 0.5 bag shredded cabbage
  • 1 lemon
  • 1 clamshell microgreens
  • prepared horseradish
  • Dijon mustard
  • black pepper
  • salt substitute
  • vinegar

Rules enforced: quantities before items; fresh meat/poultry/fish use weights; packaged items use fractional containers when partial. Condiments/spices/seasonings have no measurements in Ingredients; all measurements are in Steps. No olives, no squash, no Brussels sprouts, no zucchini, no tofu/tempeh. Lunch validations included in Notes.

Step-by-step Instructions

  1. Warm pitas: toast 3 pitas 2 minutes.
  2. Crunch base: thin-slice 4 celery stalks; combine with 1/2 bag shredded cabbage.
  3. Spread: mix horseradish + Dijon in measured amounts (Frank 2 tsp + 2 tsp; Susan 1.5 tsp + 1.5 tsp; Elise 1.5 tsp + 1.5 tsp).
  4. Cheese: add Swiss slices (Frank 2; Susan 2; Elise 2).
  5. Turkey: add deli turkey (Frank 6 slices; Susan 4; Elise 4).
  6. Add crunch base and microgreens.
  7. Finish: add 1 tsp lemon juice per pita and pepper/salt substitute.
