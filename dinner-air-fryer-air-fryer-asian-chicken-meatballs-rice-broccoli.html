Air Fryer Asian Chicken Meatballs + Rice + Broccoli

Ingredients (purchase-clean)

Steps (Cosori dual-basket)

  1. Rice: cook 1 cup dry brown rice; rest 5 minutes.
  2. Preheat Cosori to 380°F. Basket A: meatballs. Basket B: broccoli (later).
  3. Meatballs: mix ground chicken with 1 tsp garlic powder, 1 tsp onion powder, 1/2 tsp kosher salt, and black pepper. Form 18 meatballs.
  4. Air fry meatballs 10–12 minutes at 380°F; shake/roll at reminder; cook to 165°F.
  5. Glaze: whisk 5 tbsp low-sodium soy sauce, 3/4 cup water, and 1 tbsp cornstarch. Simmer on stovetop 2–3 minutes until glossy.
  6. Broccoli: microwave broccoli, drain/pat dry. Crisp in Basket B 6 minutes at 400°F with 2 tsp olive oil, black pepper, and 1 tsp garlic powder.
  7. Finish: toss meatballs with glaze (use 6 tbsp glaze total, measured).
  8. Serve with rice and broccoli per Notes.

Notes

Sticky-sauce meatball comfort with the glaze thickened so you use less soy. Portions (per person): Frank: 6 meatballs + 1 cup rice + 1 cup broccoli Susan: 5 meatballs + 3/4 cup rice + 3/4 cup broccoli Elise: 5 meatballs + 3/4 cup rice + 3/4 cup broccoli Tips (recipe-specific): Thickened glaze = less soy needed for the same ‘sticky’ feel. Crisp broccoli after microwaving for better texture.
