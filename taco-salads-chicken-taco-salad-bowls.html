Chicken Taco Salad Bowls

Nutrition (approx)

Frank: 635 kcal • P 58g • C 38g • F 22g
Susan/Elise (each): 476 kcal • P 44g • C 28g • F 16g

Ingredients (purchase-clean)

  • 1.50 lb boneless skinless chicken breast
  • 1 bag spring mix
  • 1 each avocado
  • 1/2 jar salsa
  • 1/2 tub 2% plain Greek yogurt
  • 1/2 bag bean-based tortilla chips
  • 1 pint cherry tomatoes
  • 1 each cucumber
  • 1 each lime
  • 4 tsp extra-virgin olive oil
  • 2 tsp chili powder
  • 2 tsp cumin
  • 2 tsp garlic powder
  • 1 tsp smoked paprika
  • 1 tsp black pepper
  • 1 tsp kosher salt

Steps

  1. Greens: add the spring mix to a large serving bowl.
  2. Chicken: pat dry and cut into 1/2-inch pieces. Season with chili powder, cumin, garlic powder, smoked paprika, black pepper, and kosher salt.
  3. Cook chicken in a nonstick skillet over medium-high 8–10 minutes, stirring often, until browned and 165°F. Finish with 2 tsp lime juice.
  4. Tomatoes: halve cherry tomatoes.
  5. Cucumber: dice into 1/2-inch pieces.
  6. Avocado: halve, pit, and dice. Use about 1/2 avocado total; refrigerate the remaining avocado with the pit to slow browning.
  7. Salsa-yogurt: in a small bowl, stir 6 tbsp Greek yogurt with 6 tbsp salsa until smooth.
  8. Crunch: portion chips by weight. Frank 1.0 oz; Susan 0.75 oz; Elise 0.75 oz.
  9. Build bowls: divide greens, then add hot protein mix, then cold toppings. Drizzle salsa-yogurt and finish with avocado and measured chips. Portion per Notes.

Notes

High-protein chicken taco salad with salsa-yogurt dressing and measured chip crunch. Portions (per person): Frank: Salad base: 1/3 of the greens + 1/3 of the hot protein mix + toppings (salsa + avocado + yogurt). Crunch: chips 1.0 oz Susan: Salad base: 1/4 of the greens + 1/4 of the hot protein mix + toppings (salsa + avocado + yogurt). Crunch: chips 0.75 oz Elise: Salad base: 1/4 of the greens + 1/4 of the hot protein mix + toppings (salsa + avocado + yogurt). Crunch: chips 0.75 oz
