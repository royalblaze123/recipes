Chicken Meatballs Tikka-Style with Chickpeas over Brown Rice

Prep: 22 min   Cook: 22 min   Total: 44 min

Ingredients

  • 1.6 lb ground chicken
  • 1 egg
  • 0.25 bag whole wheat panko crumbs
  • 0.5 can no-salt-added chickpeas
  • 0.5 cup dry brown rice
  • 0.33 carton low-sodium chicken broth
  • 1 onion
  • olive oil
  • garam masala
  • cumin
  • paprika
  • garlic powder
  • black pepper
  • salt substitute
  • tomato paste

Hard rules enforced: Dinner validations (calories 600–650 Frank, sat fat ≤8g, sodium ≤700mg, fiber ≥10g, added sugar ≤6g). Quantities appear before items; fresh meat/poultry/fish use weights. Condiments/spices/seasonings have no measurements in Ingredients; all measurements are in Steps. No olives, no squash, no Brussels sprouts, no zucchini, no tofu/tempeh, no yogurt in dinners.

Step-by-step Instructions

  1. Cook rice: cook 1/2 cup dry brown rice with 1 cup water; simmer 35–40 minutes; rest 5 minutes.
  2. Meatballs: mix ground chicken + 1 egg + 1/3 cup panko + 2 tsp garam masala + 1 tsp cumin + 1 tsp paprika + 1 tsp garlic powder + 1/2 tsp black pepper + 1/2 tsp salt substitute.
  3. Form 18 meatballs and bake at 425°F for 14–16 minutes until 165°F.
  4. Sauce base: sauté 1/2 diced onion in 2 tsp olive oil 3 minutes. Stir in 1 tbsp tomato paste and cook 1 minute.
  5. Add 3/4 cup broth + 1/2 cup water; simmer 4 minutes.
  6. Chickpeas: drain/rinse 1/2 can chickpeas; add to sauce with meatballs; simmer 3 minutes.
  7. Serve over brown rice. Portion: Frank 7 meatballs; Susan 5; Elise 5; rice portion scaled similarly.
