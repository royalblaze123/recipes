Turkey Green Enchilada Bake (No-Roll)

Nutrition (approx)

Frank: 640 kcal • P 60g • C 50g • F 19g
Susan/Elise (each): 480 kcal • P 45g • C 38g • F 14g

Ingredients (purchase-clean)

  • 1.50 lb 93% lean ground turkey
  • 1 package corn tortillas
  • 1 bag reduced-fat shredded Mexican cheese blend
  • 1 each avocado
  • 1/2 jar salsa
  • 1 can salsa verde
  • 1 can black beans
  • 1 bag frozen fajita-style pepper blend
  • 4 tsp extra-virgin olive oil
  • 2 tsp chili powder
  • 2 tsp cumin
  • 2 tsp garlic powder
  • 1 tsp smoked paprika
  • 1 tsp black pepper
  • 1 tsp kosher salt

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 400°F.
  2. Prep a 9x13-inch baking dish with a light spray of cooking spray.
  3. Turkey: cook ground turkey in a skillet over medium-high 7–9 minutes until browned and cooked through.
  4. Season turkey with the spices and stir 60 seconds.
  5. Veg: cook the frozen fajita pepper blend with 4 tsp olive oil 6–8 minutes until browned at edges.
  6. Beans: can size note—use a standard 15 oz can black beans. Drain and rinse. Measure out 1 1/2 cups beans; refrigerate any remainder.
  7. Combine: add peppers and measured beans to the turkey and warm 2 minutes.
  8. Verde: jar/can size note—use a standard 15 oz salsa verde. Use 1 1/2 cups as the layering sauce; refrigerate any remainder.
  9. Assemble: spread 1/2 cup enchilada sauce in the bottom of the dish. Layer 1/2 of the tortillas, then 1/2 of the filling, then 1/3 of the cheese. Repeat tortillas + filling + 1/3 cheese. Top with remaining sauce and the last 1/3 cheese.
  10. Bake 18–22 minutes until bubbling at edges and cheese is melted. Rest 10 minutes before slicing.
  11. Toppings: dice avocado and serve salsa on the side. Top each serving with measured avocado and salsa.

Notes

Green enchilada bake with turkey—bright, filling, and finished with avocado + salsa. Portions (per person): Frank: 1/3 of the casserole + 1/3 of the avocado + salsa topping Susan: 1/4 of the casserole + 1/4 of the avocado + salsa topping Elise: 1/4 of the casserole + 1/4 of the avocado + salsa topping
