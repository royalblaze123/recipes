Turkey Provolone Crunch Sandwiches with Microgreens and Carrot-Cucumber Side

Prep: 12 min   Cook: 5 min   Total: 17 min

Ingredients

  • 6 slices whole wheat bread
  • 12 slices deli turkey breast
  • 6 slices reduced-fat provolone
  • 1 tomato
  • 1 cucumber
  • 1 bag microgreens
  • 1 bag baby carrots
  • mustard
  • light mayonnaise
  • black pepper
  • salt substitute

RBM Sandwich rules: 3 full sandwiches total (6 slices bread). Deli meats/cheeses listed as slice counts; slicing guidance lives in Steps. Condiments/spices are name-only in Ingredients; all measurements are in Steps. Produce is listed in shoppable units (fractional OK). Microgreens preferred.

Step-by-step Instructions

  1. Deli request: ask for turkey breast **thin sliced for sandwiches (not shaved)** (~1–1.5 mm).
  2. Optional toast: toast bread lightly (about 2–3 minutes) so it holds up to the fillings.
  3. Make spread (per sandwich): mix mayo + mustard at a 1:1 ratio (about 1 tsp each per sandwich). Add a pinch of black pepper.
  4. Slice tomato into thin rounds. Slice cucumber into thin rounds.
  5. Assemble 3 sandwiches (use 6 bread slices total). For each sandwich: spread the mayo-mustard mix on both bread slices. Layer turkey, then provolone, then tomato, then cucumber, then a small handful of microgreens. Close and press gently.
  6. Portion fillings (to preserve 1.0× / 0.75× / 0.75× while each gets one whole sandwich): Frank gets 6 turkey slices + 2 provolone slices. Susan and Elise each get 3 turkey slices + 2 provolone slices (or 1–2 slices if you want lower fat).
  7. Veg side: Frank gets ~1 cup baby carrots + ~1 cup cucumber slices. Susan and Elise each get ~3/4 cup carrots + ~3/4 cup cucumber.
