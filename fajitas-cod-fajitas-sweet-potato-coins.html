Cod Fajitas + Sweet Potato Coins

Nutrition (approx)

Frank: 635 kcal • P 58g • C 38g • F 22g
Susan/Elise (each): 476 kcal • P 44g • C 28g • F 16g

Ingredients (purchase-clean)

  • 1.50 lb cod fillets
  • 2 each bell peppers
  • 1 each yellow onion
  • 4/5 package whole-grain small tortillas
  • 1.50 lb sweet potatoes
  • 6 tsp extra-virgin olive oil
  • 1 each lime
  • 1 jar salsa
  • 2 tsp chili powder
  • 2 tsp cumin
  • 2 tsp garlic powder
  • 1 tsp smoked paprika
  • 1 tsp black pepper
  • 1 tsp kosher salt

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 425°F. Line two sheet pans with parchment.
  2. Sweet potatoes: slice into 1/2-inch coins. Toss with 2 tsp olive oil and 1/2 tsp kosher salt. Roast 18–22 minutes, flipping once, until browned and tender.
  3. Fish: pat cod dry. Rub with 4 tsp olive oil and season with fajita spices.
  4. Veg: slice peppers and onion into strips and spread on the fish sheet pan around the cod.
  5. Roast fish + veg 10–12 minutes until cod reaches 145°F and flakes. Rest 3 minutes, then break cod into chunks.
  6. Tortillas: warm tortillas 20–30 seconds per side.
  7. Finish with lime juice and salsa. Serve with sweet potato coins. Portion per Notes.

Notes

Fajita-style baked cod with peppers and onions, plus sweet potato coins. Portions (per person): Frank: Build a plate: 3 tortillas + 1/3 of the fajita mix + 1/3 of the side Susan: Build a plate: 2 tortillas + 1/4 of the fajita mix + 1/4 of the side Elise: Build a plate: 2 tortillas + 1/4 of the fajita mix + 1/4 of the side
