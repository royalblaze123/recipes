Chicken Pesto Veggie Panini & Apple Slices

Grilled panini-style sandwich with sliced chicken, light pesto spread, and vegetables on whole-grain bread, served with fresh apple slices.

Servings

3 servings.

Times

  • Prep time: 10 minutes
  • Cook time: 0 minutes
  • Total time: 10 minutes

Ingredients

Sandwich

  • 6 slices whole-grain sandwich bread
  • 9 oz cooked chicken breast
  • 3 tbsp prepared basil pesto
  • 3 tbsp low-fat plain Greek yogurt
  • 1/2 cup shredded part-skim mozzarella cheese
  • 1 cup fresh baby spinach
  • 1/2 cup thinly sliced red bell pepper

Side

  • 1 1/2 medium apples, cored and thinly sliced (leave peel on)

Preparation Steps

  1. In a small bowl, stir together the basil pesto and Greek yogurt to make a lighter pesto spread.
  2. Place the cooked chicken breast on a cutting board and slice it thinly if not already sliced.
  3. Lay out the whole-grain bread slices. Spread the pesto-yogurt mixture evenly over one side of each slice.
  4. Divide the sliced chicken among three slices of bread, giving Frank a slightly larger share and Susan and Elise slightly smaller shares as described in the portions section.
  5. Sprinkle shredded mozzarella cheese over the chicken on each sandwich, then top with baby spinach and sliced red bell pepper.
  6. Close each sandwich with a remaining slice of bread, pressing gently to hold the filling in place.
  7. Preheat a panini press or a nonstick skillet over medium heat. If using a skillet, press the sandwiches with a spatula or a second pan.
  8. Grill each sandwich for 3–5 minutes, turning once if using a skillet, until the bread is crisp and the cheese is melted.
  9. While the sandwiches cook, core the apples and slice them thinly, then divide the apple slices onto three plates according to the portions listed.
  10. Cut the panini sandwiches in halves if desired and serve each person one sandwich with their portion of apple slices.

Notes

Portions (per person):

Frank – 1 full panini made with about 3.5 oz chicken, pesto-yogurt spread, vegetables, and cheese on 2 slices whole-grain bread, plus about 2/3 of a medium apple in slices.

Susan – 1 full panini made with about 3 oz chicken, pesto-yogurt spread, vegetables, and cheese on 2 slices whole-grain bread, plus about 1/2 of a medium apple in slices.

Elise – 1 full panini made with about 2.5 oz chicken, pesto-yogurt spread, vegetables, and cheese on 2 slices whole-grain bread, plus about 1/2 of a medium apple in slices.

Nutrition (per person)

  • Frank: 430 kcal; 38 g protein; 42 g carbs; 12 g fat; 7 g fiber; ~560 mg sodium.
  • Susan: 380 kcal; 32 g protein; 38 g carbs; 10 g fat; 6 g fiber; ~520 mg sodium.
  • Elise: 380 kcal; 32 g protein; 38 g carbs; 10 g fat; 6 g fiber; ~520 mg sodium.
