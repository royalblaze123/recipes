Chicken & Broccoli Pizza with Chickpea Arugula Salad

Prep: 22 min   Cook: 18 min   Total: 40 min

Ingredients

  • 1 pizza dough ball
  • 0.33 jar no-salt-added pizza sauce
  • 5 oz reduced-fat mozzarella cheese
  • 14 oz chicken breast
  • 1 bag frozen broccoli florets
  • 0.5 can no-salt-added chickpeas
  • 1 bag arugula
  • 1 lemon
  • olive oil
  • garlic powder
  • black pepper
  • salt substitute
  • red pepper flakes
  • vinegar

Hard rules enforced: Dinner calories (Frank 600–650), sat fat ≤8g, sodium ≤700mg, fiber ≥10g, added sugar ≤6g. Quantities appear before items; fresh meat/poultry/fish use weights. Condiments/spices/seasonings are listed name-only (no tsp/tbsp in Ingredients); all measurements are in Steps. No olives, no squash, no Brussels sprouts, no zucchini, no yogurt in dinners.

Step-by-step Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 475°F with rack in lower third. Preheat a pizza stone/steel if you have one.
  2. Chicken: season 14 oz chicken breast with 2 tsp garlic powder + 1 tsp black pepper + 2 tsp salt substitute. Cook in a skillet with 2 tsp olive oil over medium-high 6–7 minutes per side (or until 165°F). Rest 5 minutes, then slice thin.
  3. Broccoli: microwave 1 bag frozen broccoli florets just until thawed (about 3–4 minutes). Pat very dry with paper towels. Measure out 2 cups for the pizza; reseal and freeze the rest.
  4. Shape dough into a 12-inch pizza. Spread 1/3 jar sauce in a thin layer. Add mozzarella evenly.
  5. Top with sliced chicken (use all cooked chicken) and the 2 cups dried broccoli. Sprinkle red pepper flakes if desired. Bake 10–12 minutes until browned and crisp. Cut into 10 slices.
  6. Salad: drain and rinse 1/2 can chickpeas. In a bowl, combine arugula + chickpeas. Dress with 2 tsp olive oil + 2 tsp lemon juice + 1 tsp vinegar + black pepper + salt substitute.
  7. Portion: Frank 4 slices + ~2 cups salad. Susan and Elise 3 slices + ~1 1/2 cups salad each.
