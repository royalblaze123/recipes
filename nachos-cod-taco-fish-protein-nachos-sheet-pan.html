Cod Taco-Fish Protein Nachos (Sheet Pan)

Nutrition (approx)

Frank: 620 kcal • P 58g • C 38g • F 20g
Susan/Elise (each): 465 kcal • P 44g • C 28g • F 15g

Ingredients (purchase-clean)

  • 1.50 lb cod fillets
  • 2/3 bag bean-based tortilla chips
  • 3/4 bag reduced-fat shredded Mexican cheese blend
  • 1/2 tub 2% plain Greek yogurt
  • 1/2 jar salsa
  • 2 each bell peppers
  • 1 each yellow onion
  • 1 each lime
  • 6 tsp extra-virgin olive oil
  • 2 tsp chili powder
  • 2 tsp cumin
  • 2 tsp garlic powder
  • 1 tsp smoked paprika
  • 1 tsp black pepper
  • 1 tsp kosher salt

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 425°F. Line a large sheet pan with parchment.
  2. Fish: pat cod dry. Rub with 2 tsp olive oil. Season with 2 tsp chili powder, 2 tsp cumin, 2 tsp garlic powder, 1 tsp smoked paprika, 1 tsp black pepper, and 1 tsp kosher salt.
  3. Veg: slice bell peppers and onion into thin strips. Toss vegetables with 4 tsp olive oil on the sheet pan and roast 10 minutes.
  4. Add cod fillets to the sheet pan (nestle among the vegetables). Roast 10–12 minutes until cod reaches 145°F and flakes easily.
  5. Break the cod into large flakes directly on the pan and spread across the vegetables.
  6. Cheese: sprinkle the listed shredded cheese evenly over the fish-and-veg topping mix.
  7. Bake 4–6 minutes until cheese is melted and bubbling.
  8. Chips: portion chips by weight: Frank 1.5 oz; Susan 1.125 oz; Elise 1.125 oz. Keep chips separate for crunch.
  9. Yogurt-lime drizzle: stir 6 tbsp Greek yogurt with 2 tsp lime juice and 1 tsp lime zest.
  10. Salsa: measure 1/2 jar salsa into a bowl for topping.
  11. Serve: spoon hot topping mix over each person’s chips and finish with yogurt-lime drizzle and salsa. Portion per Notes.

Notes

Taco-seasoned cod + fajita veg makes a lighter, high-protein nacho topping. Portions (per person): Frank: Chips: 1.5 oz + 1/3 of the hot topping mix + 1/3 of the cold toppings Susan: Chips: 1.125 oz + 1/4 of the hot topping mix + 1/4 of the cold toppings Elise: Chips: 1.125 oz + 1/4 of the hot topping mix + 1/4 of the cold toppings
