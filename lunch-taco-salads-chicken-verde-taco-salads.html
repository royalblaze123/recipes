Chicken Verde Taco Salads

Nutrition (approx)

Frank: 535 kcal • P 48g • C 44g • F 17g
Susan/Elise (each): 401 kcal • P 36g • C 33g • F 13g

Ingredients (purchase-clean)

  • 1.25 lb boneless skinless chicken breast
  • 1 jar salsa verde
  • 1 bag shredded lettuce
  • 1 each avocado
  • 1/2 jar salsa
  • 1 each cucumber
  • 1 pint cherry tomatoes
  • 1 each lime
  • 4 tsp extra-virgin olive oil
  • 2 tsp cumin
  • 2 tsp garlic powder
  • 1 tsp black pepper
  • 1 tsp kosher salt

Steps

  1. Chicken: cook diced chicken with 2 tsp olive oil 8–10 minutes until 165°F. Season with cumin, garlic powder, black pepper, and kosher salt.
  2. Verde: jar size note—use a 16 oz jar salsa verde. Stir 1/2 cup verde into the chicken; refrigerate remainder.
  3. Dressing: whisk 2 tsp olive oil with 2 tsp lime juice and 2 tsp salsa verde.
  4. Base: divide shredded lettuce into three bowls.
  5. Protein: add verde chicken.
  6. Add-ins: add diced cucumber and halved tomatoes.
  7. Top: add salsa (red) and measured diced avocado. Portion per Notes.
  8. Avocado: halve, pit, and dice. Use about 1/2 avocado total; refrigerate remaining avocado with the pit to slow browning.

Notes

Green-style chicken taco salads (verde in protein), still topped with salsa + avocado. Portions (per person): Frank: 1 large taco salad bowl + 1/3 of the avocado + salsa Susan: 1 medium taco salad bowl + 1/4 of the avocado + salsa Elise: 1 medium taco salad bowl + 1/4 of the avocado + salsa
