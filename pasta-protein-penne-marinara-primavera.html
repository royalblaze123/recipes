Protein+ Penne Marinara Primavera

Nutrition (approx)

Frank: 719 kcal • P 70g • C 71g • F 21g
Susan/Elise (each): 539 kcal • P 52g • C 53g • F 16g

Ingredients (purchase-clean)

  • 1/3 box Barilla Protein+ penne
  • 1.25 lb boneless skinless chicken breast
  • 0.62 jar no-sugar-added marinara sauce
  • 4 tsp extra-virgin olive oil
  • 2 each bell peppers
  • 1 each yellow onion
  • 8 oz fresh mushrooms
  • 2 tbsp grated parmesan
  • 2 tsp garlic powder
  • 2 tsp Italian seasoning
  • 1 tsp black pepper
  • 1 tsp kosher salt

Steps

  1. Bring a large pot of water to a boil for the Barilla Protein+ penne. (Use enough water to fully cover pasta; do not add water to Ingredients.)
  2. Pat chicken breast dry. Season with 2 tsp garlic powder, 2 tsp Italian seasoning, 1 tsp black pepper, and 1 tsp kosher salt. Sear in a nonstick skillet over medium-high 4–5 minutes per side. Reduce to medium, cover, and cook 3–5 minutes more until 165°F. Rest 5 minutes, then chop.
  3. Dice the yellow onion. Add to the skillet and cook 4–6 minutes until softened.
  4. Chop the mushrooms. Cook 6–8 minutes until moisture cooks off and mushrooms brown.
  5. Dice the bell peppers. Cook 3–5 minutes until tender-crisp.
  6. Jar size note: assume a standard 24 oz jar marinara. Measure out what you need for this recipe; refrigerate the remainder.
  7. Add marinara to the skillet and simmer 5–7 minutes until hot and slightly thickened.
  8. Cook Barilla Protein+ penne to al dente per box time. Reserve 1 cup pasta water, then drain.
  9. Toss pasta into the sauce/skillet. Add reserved pasta water 1–2 tbsp at a time until the sauce clings well.
  10. Portion into bowls using the Portions guidance in Notes.

Notes

Marinara primavera with peppers, onions, and mushrooms. Built on Barilla Protein+ for a higher-protein pasta plate with close-approx nutrition. Portions (per person): Frank: 2 oz dry Barilla Protein+ penne + 1/3 of the skillet/sauce Susan: 1.5 oz dry Barilla Protein+ penne + 1/4 of the skillet/sauce Elise: 1.5 oz dry Barilla Protein+ penne + 1/4 of the skillet/sauce
