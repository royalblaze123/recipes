Beef & Cauliflower Rice Skillet

NOTES: Lower-carb ground-beef skillet with cauliflower rice, bell pepper, and spinach. This is a full-plate beef dinner built around lean beef, smart carbs, and plenty of non-starchy vegetables. Uses olive oil or avocado oil only, keeps sodium moderate, and avoids added sugars to support A1c, cholesterol, blood pressure, and PPPD-friendly eating. PORTION SIZES: Frank: largest portion (~1/3 of the total cooked meal); Susan: medium portion (~1/4 of the total cooked meal); Elise: medium portion (~1/4 of the total cooked meal) (Frank = 1.0×, Susan/Elise ≈0.75× portions).

PORTION SIZES (per person): Frank: largest portion (~1/3 of the total cooked meal); Susan: medium portion (~1/4 of the total cooked meal); Elise: medium portion (~1/4 of the total cooked meal) (Frank = 1.0×, Susan/Elise ≈0.75× portions).

Ingredients

  • 1 pound 93% lean ground beef
  • 4 cups cauliflower rice (fresh or frozen)
  • 1 small bell pepper, diced
  • 1 small onion, diced
  • 2 cups baby spinach
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 1/2 tsp mild chili powder
  • 1 tsp ground cumin
  • 1/2 tsp smoked paprika
  • 1/2 tsp kosher salt (or to taste)
  • 1/2 tsp ground black pepper

Step-by-Step Instructions

  1. Heat olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat. Add onion and bell pepper and cook 3–4 minutes until softened.
  2. Add ground beef and cook 4–5 minutes, breaking it up, until no longer pink. Drain excess fat if needed.
  3. Stir in garlic, chili powder, cumin, smoked paprika, salt, and pepper. Cook 1 minute.
  4. Add cauliflower rice to the skillet and cook 5–7 minutes, stirring, until tender and any excess moisture has evaporated.
  5. Stir in baby spinach and cook 1–2 minutes until wilted.
  6. Taste and adjust seasoning with a pinch of salt or extra herbs if needed, keeping overall sodium moderate.
  7. Divide the finished meal into three plates or containers: give Frank roughly 1/3 of the total volume, and split the remaining 2/3 between Susan and Elise as slightly smaller but still generous portions.

Nutrition per Person

  • Frank: ~520 kcal, ~48 g protein, ~32 g carbs, ~18 g fat.
  • Susan: ~390 kcal, ~36 g protein, ~24 g carbs, ~14 g fat.
  • Elise: ~390 kcal, ~36 g protein, ~24 g carbs, ~14 g fat.
