Chicken & Rice Dumpling Bake (Biscuit Dumplings)

Nutrition (approx)

Frank: 650 kcal • P 60g • C 62g • F 16g • Sat 5g • Na 700mg • Fiber 10g
Susan/Elise (each): 488 kcal • P 45g • C 46g • F 12g • Sat 4g • Na 525mg • Fiber 8g

Ingredients (purchase-clean)

  • 1.75 lb boneless skinless chicken breast
  • 1 cup dry brown rice
  • 1 bag frozen mixed vegetables
  • low-sodium chicken broth
  • cornstarch
  • 1 can refrigerated biscuits
  • kosher salt
  • black pepper
  • garlic powder
  • onion powder
  • Italian seasoning
  • smoked paprika
  • cornstarch
  • extra-virgin olive oil
  • low-sodium chicken broth

Steps

  1. Oven: heat to 400°F.
  2. Rice: cook 1 cup dry brown rice with 2 cups water 35–40 minutes; rest 5 minutes.
  3. Chicken: sauté diced chicken with 2 tsp olive oil, garlic powder, onion powder, modest kosher salt, black pepper 8–10 minutes to 165°F.
  4. Veg: add 3 cups frozen mixed vegetables and cook 6 minutes until hot and water reduces.
  5. Sauce: whisk 2 tbsp cornstarch into 1 1/2 cups low-sodium broth. Pour in and simmer 3 minutes until thick.
  6. Stir in cooked rice. Transfer to baking dish.
  7. Top with 3 biscuit pieces (tear each biscuit into 4 pieces; distribute).
  8. Bake 14–16 minutes until dumplings are golden. Rest 5 minutes. Serve per Notes.

Notes

Chicken-and-dumplings vibe, but it’s a bake with measured biscuit dumplings. Portions (per person): Frank: 1 biscuit worth of dumplings + 2 cups bake Susan: 1 biscuit worth of dumplings + 1 1/2 cups bake Elise: 1 biscuit worth of dumplings + 1 1/2 cups bake Tips (recipe-specific): Tear biscuits into pieces so they cook through like dumplings. Simmer sauce thick before baking so it doesn’t turn soupy.
