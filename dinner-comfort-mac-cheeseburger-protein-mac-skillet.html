Cheeseburger Protein Mac (Skillet)

Nutrition (approx)

Frank: 650 kcal • P 58g • C 62g • F 18g • Sat 7g • Na 700mg • Fiber 10g
Susan/Elise (each): 488 kcal • P 44g • C 46g • F 14g • Sat 5g • Na 525mg • Fiber 8g

Ingredients (purchase-clean)

  • 1 box Barilla Protein+ pasta
  • 1.75 lb 93% lean ground beef
  • 1 jar dill pickle relish
  • 1 bottle sugar-free ketchup
  • 1 carton skim milk
  • 1 container reduced-fat shredded cheddar cheese
  • kosher salt
  • black pepper
  • garlic powder
  • onion powder
  • smoked paprika
  • Italian seasoning
  • chili powder
  • ground cumin
  • crushed red pepper
  • cornstarch
  • extra-virgin olive oil
  • Dijon mustard
  • hot sauce
  • Worcestershire sauce
  • low-sodium soy sauce
  • rice vinegar
  • Worcestershire sauce
  • mustard

Steps

  1. Pasta: cook Protein+ pasta al dente. Drain.
  2. Beef: brown lean beef 7–9 minutes. Season with garlic powder, onion powder, kosher salt, black pepper. Add 1 tsp Worcestershire.
  3. Sauce base: whisk 2 tbsp cornstarch into 2 cups skim milk. Add to pot and simmer 3–5 minutes until thick.
  4. Cheese: stir in 6 oz reduced-fat cheddar until melted.
  5. Burger flavor: stir in 2 tbsp sugar-free ketchup + 1 tsp mustard + 2 tsp pickle relish (measured).
  6. Combine: fold in pasta. Rest 5 minutes to thicken.
  7. Serve per Notes.

Notes

Cheeseburger mac comfort with the classic tang (pickle + ketchup) but controlled. Portions (per person): Frank: About 2 1/2 cups mac (1/3 pan) + extra veg Susan: About 2 cups mac (1/4 pan) + extra veg Elise: About 2 cups mac (1/4 pan) + extra veg Tips (recipe-specific): A little relish goes a long way—keep it measured so sodium stays in range. Resting 5 minutes makes it feel like boxed mac texture (in a good way).
