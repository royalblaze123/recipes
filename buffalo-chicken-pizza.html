Buffalo Chicken Pizza + Celery Carrot Crunch

Thin-crust whole-wheat pizza topped with buffalo chicken and light cheese, served with crunchy celery and carrots plus yogurt ranch.

Servings

3 servings (Frank, Susan, Elise).

Times

  • Prep time: 35 minutes
  • Cook time: 15 minutes
  • Total time: 50 minutes

Ingredients

Pizza

  • 1 dough ball whole wheat pizza dough
  • 1 can no-salt-added crushed tomatoes
  • 1 package reduced-fat mozzarella
  • 1 lb boneless skinless chicken breast
  • 1 bottle hot sauce
  • 1 tsp garlic powder
  • 1 tsp smoked paprika
  • 1 tsp ground black pepper

Side

  • 1 bunch celery
  • 1 lb bag carrots
  • 1 cup nonfat Greek yogurt
  • 2 tbsp extra-virgin olive oil
  • 1 tsp garlic powder
  • 1 tsp dried dill
  • 1 tsp ground black pepper

Step-by-step Instructions

  1. Thaw and warm dough: Thaw 1 dough ball overnight in the refrigerator. Bring to room temperature 60–90 minutes.
  2. Preheat: Place a pizza stone/steel or inverted sheet pan in the oven and preheat to 500°F for 30 minutes.
  3. Cook chicken: Season chicken with garlic powder, smoked paprika, and black pepper. Bake at 425°F until 165°F (about 15–18 minutes). Rest 5 minutes, then dice. Measure 9 oz cooked chicken for the pizza; refrigerate the rest.
  4. Buffalo the chicken: Toss the measured chicken with 2 tbsp hot sauce until evenly coated.
  5. Make sauce: Measure 1 cup crushed tomatoes from the can and spread as a thin base. Refrigerate remaining tomatoes.
  6. Stretch and top: Stretch dough into a thin 10–12 inch round on parchment. Add sauce, sprinkle 6 oz mozzarella (save remaining), then scatter buffalo chicken.
  7. Bake: Slide pizza (on parchment) onto the hot surface. Bake 8–12 minutes until browned and bubbling.
  8. Yogurt ranch: Stir 1/2 cup Greek yogurt with 1 tsp dill, 1/2 tsp garlic powder, black pepper, and 2 tsp olive oil. Refrigerate remaining yogurt.
  9. Crunchy side: Slice celery and carrots. Serve with yogurt ranch for dipping, portioned per person.
  10. Portion dinner: Cut pizza into 10 slices and serve with the celery-carrot side.

Portion Sizes

Portions (per person):
Pizza (1 dough ball): cut into 10 slices.
Frank: 4 slices pizza + 1 cup celery sticks + 1 cup carrot sticks + 3 tbsp yogurt ranch

Susan: 3 slices pizza + 3/4 cup celery + 3/4 cup carrots + 2 tbsp yogurt ranch

Elise: 3 slices pizza + 3/4 cup celery + 3/4 cup carrots + 2 tbsp yogurt ranch
