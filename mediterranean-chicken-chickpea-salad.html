Mediterranean Chicken + Chickpea Tomato Salad

Oregano lemon chicken served with a bright chickpea tomato salad and a small feta finish.

Servings

3 servings (Frank, Susan, Elise).

Times

  • Prep time: 20 minutes
  • Cook time: 20 minutes
  • Total time: 40 minutes

Ingredients

Main

  • 2 lb boneless skinless chicken breast
  • 2 cans chickpeas
  • 1 pint cherry tomatoes
  • 1 each cucumber
  • 1 bunch parsley
  • 1 package feta cheese
  • 1 each lemon
  • 3 cloves garlic
  • 2 tbsp extra-virgin olive oil
  • 1 tsp dried oregano
  • 1 tsp ground black pepper

Step-by-step Instructions

  1. Cook chicken: Heat oven to 425°F. Season chicken with oregano and black pepper. Bake 15–18 minutes until 165°F. Rest 5 minutes, then slice. Measure the cooked chicken for dinner and refrigerate any extra.
  2. Chickpeas: Drain and rinse chickpeas. Measure 2 cups chickpeas for the salad and refrigerate any extra chickpeas.
  3. Salad: Halve 1 cup cherry tomatoes and dice 1/2 cucumber; refrigerate remaining cucumber and tomatoes. Chop parsley.
  4. Dressing: Whisk olive oil, juice of 1 lemon, minced garlic, oregano, and black pepper.
  5. Combine: Toss chickpeas, tomatoes, cucumber, parsley, and 2 oz crumbled feta with dressing.
  6. Serve: Plate sliced chicken alongside the chickpea salad.

Portion Sizes

Portions (per person):
Frank: 6 oz cooked chicken + 1 1/4 cups chickpea salad

Susan: 4.5 oz cooked chicken + 1 cup chickpea salad

Elise: 4.5 oz cooked chicken + 1 cup chickpea salad
