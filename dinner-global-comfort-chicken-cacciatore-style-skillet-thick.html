Chicken Cacciatore-Style Skillet (Thick)

Nutrition (approx)

Frank: 650 kcal • P 58g • C 70g • F 14g • Sat 3g • Na 700mg • Fiber 10g
Susan/Elise (each): 488 kcal • P 44g • C 52g • F 10g • Sat 2g • Na 525mg • Fiber 8g

Ingredients (purchase-clean)

  • 1.75 lb boneless skinless chicken breast
  • 1 each onion
  • 1 jar marinara sauce
  • 1 can no-salt-added diced tomatoes
  • 1 bag frozen green beans
  • 1 cup dry brown rice
  • kosher salt
  • black pepper
  • garlic powder
  • onion powder
  • smoked paprika
  • Italian seasoning
  • ground cumin
  • chili powder
  • red pepper flakes
  • extra-virgin olive oil
  • low-sodium chicken broth

Steps

  1. Rice: cook 1 cup dry brown rice with 2 cups water 35–40 minutes; rest 5 minutes.
  2. Chicken: sauté diced chicken with 2 tsp olive oil, black pepper, garlic powder, Italian seasoning, and a modest pinch of kosher salt 6–8 minutes.
  3. Sauce: add 1 can diced tomatoes (14.5 oz) + 2 cups marinara (measured). Simmer uncovered 10 minutes to thicken.
  4. Green beans: microwave green beans; drain well. Stir into sauce for the last 3 minutes.
  5. Serve chicken sauce over rice per Notes.

Notes

Cozy Italian skillet dinner with a thick cacciatore-style sauce (not soupy). Portions (per person): Frank: About 2 1/2 cups bowl (chicken + sauce over rice) Susan: About 2 cups bowl Elise: About 2 cups bowl Tips (recipe-specific): Simmer uncovered so the sauce reduces and clings. Drain green beans well so the sauce stays thick.
