Creamy Balsamic Yogurt Dip

Category: Dip • Yield: About 3/4 cup (14 tbsp) • AnyList-ready

Ingredients

  • 3/4 cup 2% plain Greek yogurt
  • 2 tsp balsamic vinegar
  • 2 tsp extra-virgin olive oil
  • 1 tsp Dijon mustard
  • 1/2 tsp garlic powder
  • 1/4 tsp black pepper

Steps

  1. Whisk Greek yogurt, balsamic vinegar, olive oil, Dijon mustard, garlic powder, and black pepper until smooth.
  2. Optional: thin with 1–2 tsp water for a drizzle consistency.
