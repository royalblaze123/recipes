Chicken Brown Rice Black Bean Bowl

NOTES: Designed as a portable grab-and-go lunch using lean protein, high-fiber vegetables, and controlled smart carbs. Use low-sodium ingredients and avoid added sugars where possible. PORTION SIZES: Frank: largest portion (~1/3 of the total volume); Susan: medium portion (~1/4 of the total volume); Elise: medium portion (~1/4 of the total volume) (Frank = 1.0×, Susan/Elise ≈0.75× portions).

Ingredients

  • 1 cup uncooked brown rice, rinsed
  • 2 cups low-sodium chicken broth or water
  • 12 oz cooked boneless, skinless chicken breast, chopped
  • 1 can (15 oz) no-salt-added black beans, drained and rinsed
  • 1 cup frozen corn (no added salt), thawed and drained
  • 1 red bell pepper, diced
  • 1 cup halved cherry tomatoes
  • 1 tsp chili powder
  • 1/2 tsp ground cumin
  • 1/2 tsp smoked paprika
  • 2 tbsp lime juice
  • 1 tbsp extra-virgin olive oil
  • Freshly ground black pepper, to taste

Step-by-Step Instructions

  1. Cook brown rice: Combine rinsed rice and broth or water in a saucepan. Bring to a boil, then reduce heat, cover, and simmer 25–30 minutes until tender. Fluff with a fork and cool slightly.
  2. In a large bowl, combine cooked rice, chopped chicken, black beans, thawed corn, diced bell pepper, and cherry tomatoes.
  3. In a small bowl, whisk together chili powder, ground cumin, smoked paprika, lime juice, olive oil, and black pepper.
  4. Pour dressing over the bowl contents and toss thoroughly to combine.
  5. Divide mixture into three meal prep containers, giving Frank the largest share.
  6. Refrigerate up to 3 days. Enjoy chilled or gently rewarmed in a microwave if eating at home.

Nutrition per Person

  • Frank: ~590 kcal, ~50 g protein, ~65 g carbs, ~15 g fat.
  • Susan: ~445 kcal, ~38 g protein, ~49 g carbs, ~11 g fat.
  • Elise: ~445 kcal, ~38 g protein, ~49 g carbs, ~11 g fat.
