Tuna Salsa Bowls + Pickled Jalapeño Bite

Nutrition (approx)

Frank: 510 kcal • P 52g • C 42g • F 15g
Susan/Elise (each): 382 kcal • P 39g • C 32g • F 11g

Ingredients (purchase-clean)

  • 3 cans tuna
  • 1 bag spring mix
  • 1 each avocado
  • 1/2 jar salsa
  • 1 package whole-wheat tortillas
  • 1 each lime
  • 4 tsp extra-virgin olive oil
  • 1 tsp black pepper
  • 1 tsp kosher salt
  • 1 jar pickled jalapeños

Steps

  1. Tuna: Can size note—use a standard 5 oz can tuna.. Use three 5 oz cans tuna. Drain very well.
  2. Tuna mix: in a bowl, combine drained tuna with 1/2 cup salsa, 2 tsp lime juice, 2 tsp olive oil, black pepper, and kosher salt.
  3. Optional heat: chop 1–2 tbsp pickled jalapeños and stir into the tuna mix. Keep the rest of the jar for future bowls.
  4. Greens: divide spring mix into three bowls.
  5. Top: spoon tuna salsa mixture over the greens.
  6. Avocado: halve, pit, and dice. Use about 1/2 avocado total; refrigerate remaining avocado with the pit.
  7. Finish: add measured avocado on each bowl and add an extra spoon of salsa if desired.
  8. Tortillas: warm tortillas 15 seconds. Serve 1 tortilla on the side (measured) for scooping. Portion per Notes.

Notes

Tuna salsa bowls with a pickled-jalapeño kick (use a small amount). Portions (per person): Frank: 1 large tuna salsa bowl + 1 tortilla on the side + 1/3 avocado Susan: 1 medium tuna salsa bowl + 3/4 tortilla on the side + 1/4 avocado Elise: 1 medium tuna salsa bowl + 3/4 tortilla on the side + 1/4 avocado
