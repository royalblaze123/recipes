Turkey Sausage Pepper Onion Pizza

NOTES: Built as a full dinner using ready-made whole-grain or thin crust, lean protein, plenty of vegetables, and controlled cheese to support A1c, cholesterol, and blood pressure goals. Use low-sodium sauce and minimal added salt. PORTION SIZES: Frank: ~40% of the pizza; Susan: ~30% of the pizza; Elise: ~30% of the pizza (Frank = 1.0×, Susan/Elise = 0.75× portions).

Ingredients

  • 1 ready-made 12-inch thin whole-wheat pizza crust
  • 1/2 cup no-salt-added tomato or pizza sauce
  • 6 oz lean turkey sausage (bulk or links with casings removed)
  • 1 tsp olive oil
  • 1 small yellow onion, thinly sliced
  • 1 red bell pepper, thinly sliced
  • 1 green bell pepper, thinly sliced
  • 3/4 cup shredded reduced-fat mozzarella cheese
  • 1 tsp dried Italian seasoning
  • Freshly ground black pepper, to taste

Step-by-Step Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 425°F. Place the crust on a pizza pan or parchment-lined baking sheet.
  2. Heat olive oil in a skillet over medium heat. Add turkey sausage and cook 5–7 minutes, breaking into crumbles, until browned and cooked through. Remove from heat.
  3. In the same skillet, add sliced onion and bell peppers and cook 4–5 minutes until slightly softened. Remove from heat.
  4. Spread tomato or pizza sauce evenly over the crust.
  5. Sprinkle half of the mozzarella over the sauce.
  6. Distribute cooked turkey sausage and sautéed peppers and onions evenly over the pizza.
  7. Top with remaining mozzarella, Italian seasoning, and a few grinds of black pepper.
  8. Bake 12–15 minutes, or until the crust is crisp and the cheese is melted and lightly browned.
  9. Cool 3–5 minutes before slicing and portioning for Frank (~40%) and Susan/Elise (~30% each).

Nutrition per Person

  • Frank: ~620 kcal, ~52 g protein, ~50 g carbs, ~19 g fat.
  • Susan: ~465 kcal, ~39 g protein, ~38 g carbs, ~14 g fat.
  • Elise: ~465 kcal, ~39 g protein, ~38 g carbs, ~14 g fat.
