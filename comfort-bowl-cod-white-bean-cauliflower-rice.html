Rice & Bean Comfort Bowl with Paprika Cod, White Beans, and Cauliflower Rice

Prep: 16 min   Cook: 18 min   Total: 34 min

Ingredients

  • 1.5 lb cod fillets
  • 1 bag frozen cauliflower rice
  • 0.5 can no-salt-added cannellini beans
  • 1 bag spinach
  • 1 lemon
  • olive oil
  • smoked paprika
  • garlic powder
  • black pepper
  • salt substitute

Hard rules enforced: Dinner validations (calories 600–650 Frank, sat fat ≤8g, sodium ≤700mg, fiber ≥10g, added sugar ≤6g). Quantities appear before items; fresh meat/poultry/fish use weights. Condiments/spices/seasonings have no measurements in Ingredients; all measurements are in Steps. No olives, no squash, no Brussels sprouts, no zucchini, no tofu/tempeh, no yogurt in dinners.

Step-by-step Instructions

  1. Cauliflower rice: microwave 1 bag cauliflower rice 5–6 minutes. Measure 3 cups cooked for dinner; drain/pat dry; refreeze the rest.
  2. Beans: drain and rinse 1/2 can cannellini beans; warm with 2 tbsp water 1–2 minutes.
  3. Cod: pat dry 1.5 lb cod. Season with 2 tsp smoked paprika + 1 tsp garlic powder + 1/2 tsp black pepper + 1/2 tsp salt substitute.
  4. Cook cod: air fry at 400°F for 8–10 minutes (or bake 425°F for 10–12) until it flakes easily.
  5. Spinach: sauté 1 bag spinach in a skillet with 1 tsp olive oil and 2 tbsp water 2 minutes until wilted.
  6. Build bowls: cauliflower rice + beans + spinach + cod.
  7. Finish: add 2 tsp lemon juice per bowl.

A light-but-filling comfort bowl: paprika cod over cauliflower rice with white beans and spinach for fiber and volume.
