Korean-Style Turkey Meatballs with Broccoli over Brown Rice

Prep: 22 min   Cook: 22 min   Total: 44 min

Ingredients

  • 1.5 lb lean ground turkey 93%
  • 1 egg
  • 0.25 bag whole wheat panko crumbs
  • 1 bag frozen broccoli florets
  • 0.5 cup dry brown rice
  • olive oil
  • gochujang
  • low-sodium soy sauce
  • rice vinegar
  • sesame oil
  • garlic
  • ginger
  • black pepper
  • salt substitute
  • cornstarch

Hard rules enforced: Dinner validations (calories 600–650 Frank, sat fat ≤8g, sodium ≤700mg, fiber ≥10g, added sugar ≤6g). Quantities appear before items; fresh meat/poultry/fish use weights. Condiments/spices/seasonings have no measurements in Ingredients; all measurements are in Steps. No olives, no squash, no Brussels sprouts, no zucchini, no tofu/tempeh, no yogurt in dinners.

Step-by-step Instructions

  1. Cook rice: cook 1/2 cup dry brown rice with 1 cup water; simmer 35–40 minutes; rest 5 minutes.
  2. Meatballs: mix turkey + 1 egg + 1/3 cup panko + 1/2 tsp black pepper + 1/2 tsp salt substitute. Form 18 meatballs.
  3. Air fry meatballs at 400°F for 10–12 minutes until 165°F.
  4. Broccoli: microwave frozen broccoli 3–4 minutes to thaw; pat dry. Measure 4 cups; refreeze remainder. Sauté broccoli with 1 tsp olive oil 3 minutes.
  5. Glaze: whisk 1 tbsp gochujang + 1 tbsp low-sodium soy sauce + 1 tbsp rice vinegar + 1 tsp sesame oil + 1 tsp cornstarch + 2 tbsp water + 1 tsp minced garlic + 1 tsp grated ginger.
  6. Add meatballs to skillet with broccoli; pour glaze; toss 2 minutes until thick and coated.
  7. Serve over brown rice. Portion: Frank 7 meatballs; Susan 5; Elise 5; rice portion scaled similarly.
