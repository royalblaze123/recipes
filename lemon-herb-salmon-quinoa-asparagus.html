Lemon Herb Salmon + Quinoa + Roasted Asparagus

Roasted lemon-herb salmon with fluffy quinoa and roasted asparagus.

Servings

3 servings (Frank, Susan, Elise).

Times

  • Prep time: 20 minutes
  • Cook time: 18 minutes
  • Total time: 38 minutes

Ingredients

Main

  • 1.5 lb salmon fillets
  • 1 cup dry quinoa
  • 1 bunch asparagus
  • 1 each lemon
  • 3 cloves garlic
  • 2 tbsp extra-virgin olive oil
  • 1 tsp dried dill
  • 1 tsp dried oregano
  • 1 tsp ground black pepper

Step-by-step Instructions

  1. Cook quinoa: Rinse quinoa. Cook 3/5 cup dry quinoa with 1 1/5 cups water until fluffy (about 15 minutes). This yields about 1 3/4 cups cooked quinoa; cool and fluff.
  2. Prep salmon: Pat salmon dry. Mince garlic and zest the lemon. Mix 2 tsp olive oil with garlic, lemon zest, dill, oregano, and black pepper. Brush over salmon.
  3. Prep asparagus: Snap woody ends. Toss asparagus with remaining olive oil and black pepper.
  4. Roast: Heat oven to 425°F. Roast salmon and asparagus on separate areas of a sheet pan for 10–14 minutes (salmon flakes; asparagus browned-tender).
  5. Finish: Squeeze lemon over salmon and asparagus right before serving.

Portion Sizes

Portions (per person):
Frank: 6 oz cooked salmon + 2/3 cup cooked quinoa + 2 cups asparagus

Susan: 4.5 oz cooked salmon + 1/2 cup cooked quinoa + 1 1/2 cups asparagus

Elise: 4.5 oz cooked salmon + 1/2 cup cooked quinoa + 1 1/2 cups asparagus
