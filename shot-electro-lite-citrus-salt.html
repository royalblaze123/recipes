Electro-Lite Citrus Salt Shot

NOTES: Concentrated energy shot designed as a small, quick dose of gentle stimulation and clarity—built with green tea, herbal teas, spices, or very small amounts of juice. No added sugar, no energy drinks, and caffeine is kept modest per shot to protect anxiety, BP, and PPPD.

PORTION SIZES (makes 3 small shots): This batch makes about 3 small shots (≈2–3 oz each). Frank takes the largest shot (≈1/3 of the total volume); Susan and Elise each take slightly smaller shots (≈1/4 of the total volume each). Frank = 1.0×; Susan = 0.75×; Elise = 0.75× portions.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup cold water
  • 2 tsp lemon juice
  • 2 tsp lime juice
  • Very small pinch fine sea salt (about 1/16 tsp)

Step-by-Step Instructions

  1. In a small measuring cup or jar, combine 1/2 cup cold water, 2 tsp lemon juice, 2 tsp lime juice, and Very small pinch fine sea salt (about 1/16 tsp).
  2. Stir or whisk thoroughly until everything is evenly mixed and any powders are fully dispersed. Use a mini blender if you prefer a smoother texture.
  3. Taste the shot and add a splash of extra water if the flavor is too intense. Keep it unsweetened; only add a very small amount of non-nutritive sweetener if absolutely necessary.
  4. Pour the mixture into three small shot glasses or mini bottles. Give Frank the largest shot and split the remaining amount between Susan and Elise. Sip slowly rather than swallowing in a single gulp.

Nutrition (approximate per person)

Frank (largest shot): ~5–35 kcal, 0–3 g protein, 1–8 g carbs, 0–2 g fat
Susan (medium shot): ~5–25 kcal, 0–2 g protein, 1–6 g carbs, 0–1.5 g fat
Elise (medium shot): ~5–25 kcal, 0–2 g protein, 1–6 g carbs, 0–1.5 g fat
