Crispy Chicken Sandwich Plates + Pickle Salad

Nutrition (approx)

Frank: 645 kcal • P 60g • C 55g • F 16g • Sat 4g • Na 700mg • Fiber 10g
Susan/Elise (each): 484 kcal • P 45g • C 41g • F 12g • Sat 3g • Na 525mg • Fiber 8g

Ingredients (purchase-clean)

  • 1.75 lb chicken breast cutlets
  • 1 carton egg whites
  • 1 package whole-wheat panko breadcrumbs
  • 4 each whole-wheat sandwich buns
  • 1 jar dill pickle slices
  • 1 bag shredded lettuce
  • 1 each tomato
  • kosher salt
  • black pepper
  • garlic powder
  • onion powder
  • smoked paprika
  • chili powder
  • ground cumin
  • Italian seasoning
  • crushed red pepper
  • cornstarch
  • low-sodium soy sauce
  • rice vinegar
  • hot sauce
  • Dijon mustard
  • extra-virgin olive oil
  • cayenne pepper

Steps

  1. Oven: heat to 425°F. Line a sheet pan and lightly oil.
  2. Breading: Bowl 1: 1/2 cup egg whites + 1 tsp Dijon. Bowl 2: 1 1/2 cups panko + 1 tsp garlic powder + 1 tsp onion powder + 1/2 tsp smoked paprika + 1/4 tsp cayenne + 1/2 tsp black pepper.
  3. Coat cutlets and place on sheet pan. Bake 12–14 minutes; flip at 7 minutes; cook to 165°F.
  4. Toast buns in oven for 2 minutes.
  5. Salad: lettuce + sliced tomato + pickles. Dress with 1 tsp rice vinegar + black pepper + hot sauce.
  6. Assemble sandwiches. Portion per Notes.

Notes

Fast-food crispy chicken sandwich, but you control the breading and portions. Portions (per person): Frank: 1 sandwich + 1 extra cutlet piece + big salad Susan: 1 sandwich (smaller) + big salad Elise: 1 sandwich (smaller) + big salad Tips (recipe-specific): Use pickles + hot sauce for big flavor without mayo. Flip at 7 minutes so the crust stays crisp.
