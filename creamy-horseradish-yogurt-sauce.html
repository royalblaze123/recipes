Creamy Horseradish Yogurt Sauce

Category: Sauce • Yield: About 3/4 cup (13 tbsp) • AnyList-ready

Ingredients

  • 3/4 cup 2% plain Greek yogurt
  • 2 tsp prepared horseradish
  • 2 tsp Dijon mustard
  • 2 tsp lemon juice
  • 1/4 tsp black pepper

Steps

  1. Whisk Greek yogurt, horseradish, Dijon, lemon juice, and black pepper until smooth.
  2. Chill 10 minutes before serving.
