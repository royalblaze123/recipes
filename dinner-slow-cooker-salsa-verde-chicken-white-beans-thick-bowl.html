Salsa Verde Chicken + White Beans (Thick Bowl)

Nutrition (approx)

Frank: 640 kcal • P 58g • C 55g • F 16g
Susan/Elise (each): 480 kcal • P 44g • C 41g • F 12g

Ingredients (purchase-clean)

  • 1.75 lb boneless skinless chicken breast
  • 1 jar salsa verde
  • 1 can white beans
  • 1 bag frozen corn
  • low-sodium chicken broth
  • kosher salt
  • black pepper
  • garlic powder
  • onion powder
  • smoked paprika
  • Italian seasoning
  • ground cumin
  • chili powder
  • red pepper flakes
  • extra-virgin olive oil
  • low-sodium chicken broth
  • low-sodium beef broth
  • low-sodium soy sauce
  • hot sauce
  • Dijon mustard
  • balsamic vinegar
  • apple cider vinegar
  • cornstarch

Steps (slow cooker)

  1. Layer chicken + 1 1/2 cups salsa verde + 1 cup broth.
  2. Season with chili powder, cumin, garlic powder, black pepper; modest salt.
  3. LOW 4–5 hr or HIGH 2–3 hr to 165°F.
  4. Shred chicken.
  5. Add beans (15 oz; 1 1/2 cups) + corn (2 cups); HIGH 20–25 min.
  6. Thicken: 1 tbsp cornstarch + cold water; 10 min.
  7. Serve per Notes.

Notes

Salsa-verde chicken bowl built for freezer-style thickness (not soup). Portions (per person): Frank: About 2 1/2 cups (heaping bowl) Susan: About 2 cups (bowl) Elise: About 2 cups (bowl) Tips (recipe-specific): Add beans/corn at the end so they keep bite. Thicken before serving so leftovers reheat like chili.
