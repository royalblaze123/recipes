Air Fryer Smoky Paprika Pork Chops with Sweet Potato and Green Beans

Smoky paprika pork chops with sweet potato wedges and green beans—dual-basket timed for a fast, balanced dinner.

Servings

3 servings.

Times

  • Prep time: 20 minutes
  • Cook time: 18 minutes
  • Total time: 38 minutes

Ingredients

Main Dish

  • 1 lb boneless pork chops
  • 2 each sweet potatoes
  • 1 bag fresh green beans
  • 1 bottle extra virgin olive oil
  • 1 bag reduced fat shredded cheese
  • 1 container nonfat Greek yogurt
  • 1 jar smoked paprika
  • 1 jar garlic powder
  • 1 jar black pepper
  • 1 jar Italian seasoning
    • 

    Preparation Steps

    1. Prep sweet potatoes: cut into wedges. Measure out about 2 1/4 cups wedges for this recipe; refrigerate remaining sweet potato. Toss with 1 tbsp olive oil, smoked paprika, garlic powder, and black pepper.
    2. Prep pork chops: pat dry. Brush with 2 tsp olive oil and season with Italian seasoning, smoked paprika, garlic powder, and black pepper.
    3. Prep green beans: trim. Toss 1/2 bag green beans with 1 tsp olive oil, garlic powder, and black pepper. Refrigerate remaining green beans.
    4. Cosori setup: preheat both baskets to 390°F for 3 minutes. Put sweet potato wedges in Basket A (single layer). Put pork chops in Basket B.
    5. Sync Finish: set Basket A to 390°F for 18 minutes (shake reminder ON). Set Basket B to 390°F for 12 minutes. Press Sync Finish.
    6. Shake reminder (Basket A): shake/flip wedges when prompted.
    7. Flip pork: at 6 minutes, flip pork chops. Cook to 145°F internal, then rest 3 minutes.
    8. Green beans finish: when pork is done, cook green beans in Basket B at 390°F for 6–7 minutes, shaking once.
    9. Yogurt dip: stir 1/4 cup Greek yogurt with a pinch of smoked paprika and 1 tsp water.
    10. Serve (40/30/30): plate pork with sweet potato wedges and green beans. Sprinkle 1/2 oz reduced-fat cheese total across all plates (optional) and serve yogurt dip on the side.

      Notes

      Portions (per person):


      Frank – Pork: largest chop. Sweet potato wedges: about 1 1/4 cups. Green beans: about 1 1/2 cups. Yogurt dip + light cheese sprinkle.


      Susan – Pork: smaller chop. Sweet potato wedges: about 1 cup. Green beans: about 1 1/4 cups. Yogurt dip + light cheese sprinkle.


      Elise – Pork: smaller chop. Sweet potato wedges: about 1 cup. Green beans: about 1 1/4 cups. Yogurt dip + light cheese sprinkle.


      Tips (recipe-specific):


      Pull pork at 145°F and rest—this keeps it juicy and prevents dryness.


      Cook green beans at the end so they stay crisp-tender.


      If wedges are browning too fast, drop Basket A to 375°F for the last 5 minutes.
