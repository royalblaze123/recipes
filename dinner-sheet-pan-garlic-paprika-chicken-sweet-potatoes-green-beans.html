Garlic-Paprika Chicken + Sweet Potatoes + Green Beans

Frank: 650 kcal • P 60g • C 55g • F 18g • Sat 3g • Na 680mg • Fiber 10g
Susan/Elise (each): 488 kcal • P 45g • C 41g • F 14g • Sat 2g • Na 510mg • Fiber 8g

Steps

  1. Oven: heat to 425°F. Line a large sheet pan with foil; lightly oil.
  2. Sweet potatoes: cube. Toss with 2 tsp olive oil, 2 tsp smoked paprika, 1 tsp garlic powder, 1/2 tsp kosher salt, and 1/2 tsp black pepper. Spread on half the pan. Roast 12 minutes.
  3. Chicken: cut into 1-inch chunks. Toss with 2 tsp olive oil, 2 tsp smoked paprika, 1 tsp garlic powder, 1/2 tsp kosher salt, and 1/2 tsp black pepper.
  4. Add chicken to the pan (single layer). Roast 12 minutes.
  5. Green beans: microwave 4–5 minutes; drain well. Toss with 2 tsp olive oil, 1/2 tsp kosher salt, black pepper, and 1 tsp garlic powder. Add to pan; roast 8–10 minutes until browned.
  6. Serve per Notes.

Notes

Sheet-pan classic: high flavor from paprika + garlic and browning, not heavy sauce. Portions (per person): Frank: 7 oz chicken + 1 cup sweet potatoes + 1 1/2 cups green beans Susan: 5 oz protein + 3/4 cup starch + 1 cup veg Elise: 5 oz protein + 3/4 cup starch + 1 cup veg Tips (recipe-specific): Roast in stages so potatoes brown and chicken stays juicy. Drain green beans well so they roast, not steam.
