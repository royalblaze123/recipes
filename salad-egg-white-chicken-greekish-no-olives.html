Egg-White Chicken Salad with Tomato, Cucumber, and Microgreens

Prep: 18 min   Cook: 10 min   Total: 28 min

Ingredients

  • 10 oz cooked chicken breast
  • 1 carton egg whites
  • 1 bag romaine hearts
  • 1 pint cherry tomatoes
  • 1 cucumber
  • 1 bag microgreens
  • 1 lemon
  • olive oil
  • black pepper
  • salt substitute
  • vinegar
  • Dijon mustard

Hard rules enforced: Lunch validations (calories, sat fat, sodium, fiber, added sugar). Quantities appear before items; fresh meat/poultry/fish use weights. Condiments/spices/seasonings have no measurements in Ingredients; all measurements are in Steps. No olives, no squash, no Brussels sprouts, no zucchini. Yogurt is not used in dinners.

Step-by-step Instructions

  1. Egg whites: scramble 1 cup egg whites in a nonstick skillet 3–4 minutes until set. Cool slightly.
  2. Build salad: chopped romaine + tomatoes + cucumber. Add chicken pieces and egg whites. Top with microgreens.
  3. Dressing: whisk 2 tsp lemon juice + 2 tsp olive oil + 1 tsp vinegar + Dijon mustard + black pepper + salt substitute. Toss lightly.
  4. Portion: Frank large bowl; Susan/Elise ~3/4 bowl each.
