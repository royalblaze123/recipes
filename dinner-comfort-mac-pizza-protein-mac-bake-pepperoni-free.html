Pizza Protein Mac Bake (Pepperoni-Free)

Nutrition (approx)

Frank: 645 kcal • P 60g • C 60g • F 16g • Sat 7g • Na 700mg • Fiber 10g
Susan/Elise (each): 484 kcal • P 45g • C 45g • F 12g • Sat 5g • Na 525mg • Fiber 8g

Ingredients (purchase-clean)

  • 1 box Barilla Protein+ pasta
  • 1.75 lb boneless skinless chicken breast
  • 1 jar marinara sauce
  • 1 container reduced-fat shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 2 oz grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 bag frozen green beans
  • kosher salt
  • black pepper
  • garlic powder
  • onion powder
  • smoked paprika
  • Italian seasoning
  • chili powder
  • ground cumin
  • crushed red pepper
  • cornstarch
  • extra-virgin olive oil
  • Dijon mustard
  • hot sauce
  • Worcestershire sauce
  • low-sodium soy sauce
  • rice vinegar
  • Italian seasoning

Steps

  1. Pasta: cook Protein+ pasta al dente. Drain.
  2. Chicken: dice and sauté chicken with 2 tsp olive oil, 1 tsp garlic powder, 1 tsp Italian seasoning, 1/2 tsp kosher salt, 1/2 tsp black pepper 8–10 minutes to 165°F.
  3. Green beans: roast frozen green beans at 425°F with 2 tsp olive oil and black pepper for 14–18 minutes.
  4. Sauce: warm 1 1/2 cups marinara (measured).
  5. Combine: toss pasta + chicken with marinara. Transfer to a baking dish.
  6. Cheese: top with 6 oz reduced-fat mozzarella + 2 oz Parmesan.
  7. Bake: 425°F for 10–12 minutes until bubbly; broil 1 minute for browning.
  8. Serve with green beans per Notes.

Notes

Pizza-night comfort as a bake—saucy, cheesy, portioned. Portions (per person): Frank: About 2 1/2 cups bake + 1 cup green beans Susan: About 2 cups bake + 3/4 cup green beans Elise: About 2 cups bake + 3/4 cup green beans Tips (recipe-specific): Broil 1 minute for that ‘pizza cheese’ browning. Keep marinara measured; flavor comes from browning + cheese top.
