Philly Burger Plates + Peppers & Onions

Nutrition (approx)

Frank: 645 kcal • P 58g • C 50g • F 20g • Sat 7g • Na 690mg • Fiber 10g
Susan/Elise (each): 484 kcal • P 44g • C 38g • F 15g • Sat 5g • Na 518mg • Fiber 8g

Ingredients (purchase-clean)

  • 1.75 lb 93% lean ground beef
  • 4 each whole-wheat burger buns
  • 2 each bell peppers
  • 1 each onion
  • 4 slices reduced-fat cheese slices
  • 1 bag shredded lettuce
  • kosher salt
  • black pepper
  • garlic powder
  • onion powder
  • smoked paprika
  • dried oregano
  • extra-virgin olive oil
  • mustard
  • ketchup
  • pickle relish
  • hot sauce
  • Worcestershire sauce

Steps

  1. Veg: sauté sliced bell peppers and onion with 2 tsp olive oil 10–12 minutes until browned and sweet.
  2. Patties: form 6 patties. Season with 1 tsp garlic powder, 1/2 tsp kosher salt, 1/2 tsp black pepper.
  3. Skillet: cook patties 4–5 minutes per side to 160°F.
  4. Cheese: add 1 slice to Frank’s 2 patties; split the remaining slices across the other patties.
  5. Assemble: top patties with peppers/onions. Serve on buns or open-face over lettuce. Portion per Notes.

Notes

Cheesesteak vibes, burger format, lots of peppers/onions for volume. Portions (per person): Frank: 2 patties + 1 cup peppers/onions + lettuce side + toppings Susan: 1 patty + 3/4 cup peppers/onions + lettuce side + toppings Elise: 1 patty + 3/4 cup peppers/onions + lettuce side + toppings Tips (recipe-specific): Brown peppers/onions until sweet—this is where the ‘comfort’ comes from. Open-face (one bun half) is an easy calorie lever if needed.
