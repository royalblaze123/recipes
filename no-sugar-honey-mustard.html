No-Sugar Honey Mustard

Category: Sauce • Yield: About 1/3 cup (5–6 tbsp) • AnyList-ready

Ingredients

  • 2 tbsp Dijon mustard
  • 2 tbsp 2% plain Greek yogurt
  • 1 tsp apple cider vinegar
  • 2 tsp date paste
  • 1/4 tsp black pepper

Steps

  1. Whisk Dijon mustard, Greek yogurt, apple cider vinegar, date paste, and black pepper until smooth.
  2. Optional: thin with 1–2 tsp water until drizzle-ready.
