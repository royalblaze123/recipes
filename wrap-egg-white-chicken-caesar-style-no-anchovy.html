Chicken Egg-White Caesar-Style Wrap with Romaine Crunch

Prep: 18 min   Cook: 10 min   Total: 28 min

Ingredients

  • 3 whole wheat tortillas
  • 10 oz cooked chicken breast
  • 1 carton egg whites
  • 1 bag romaine hearts
  • 1 lemon
  • 2 tbsp grated parmesan cheese
  • olive oil
  • light mayonnaise
  • Dijon mustard
  • black pepper
  • salt substitute
  • garlic powder

Hard rules enforced: Lunch validations (calories, sat fat, sodium, fiber, added sugar). Quantities appear before items; fresh meat/poultry/fish use weights. Condiments/spices/seasonings have no measurements in Ingredients; all measurements are in Steps. No olives, no squash, no Brussels sprouts, no zucchini. Yogurt is not used in dinners.

Step-by-step Instructions

  1. Egg whites: pour 1 cup egg whites into a nonstick skillet. Cook over medium 3–4 minutes, stirring, until set. Season with black pepper and salt substitute. Cool slightly.
  2. Caesar-style quick dressing: mix 2 tsp light mayo + Dijon mustard + 2 tsp lemon juice + garlic powder + black pepper. (No yogurt; keep light.)
  3. Romaine: chop romaine. Toss lightly with the dressing and 2 tbsp parmesan.
  4. Assemble wraps: add chicken, egg whites, and dressed romaine to tortillas. Roll tightly.
  5. Extra romaine on the side if desired.
  6. Portion: 1 wrap each; Frank gets more chicken and egg whites within the wrap.
