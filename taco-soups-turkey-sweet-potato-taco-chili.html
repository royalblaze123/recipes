Turkey & Sweet Potato Taco Chili

Nutrition (approx)

Frank: 650 kcal • P 58g • C 50g • F 19g
Susan/Elise (each): 488 kcal • P 44g • C 38g • F 14 g

Ingredients (purchase-clean)

  • 1.50 lb 93% lean ground turkey
  • 1.50 lb sweet potatoes
  • 1 can black beans
  • 1 can crushed tomatoes
  • 1 carton low-sodium chicken broth
  • 1 each avocado
  • 1/2 jar salsa
  • 1 each lime
  • 2 tsp extra-virgin olive oil
  • 2 tsp chili powder
  • 2 tsp cumin
  • 2 tsp garlic powder
  • 1 tsp smoked paprika
  • 1 tsp black pepper
  • 1 tsp kosher salt

Steps

  1. Sweet potatoes: peel if desired and cube into 3/4-inch pieces.
  2. Turkey: brown ground turkey in a pot with 2 tsp olive oil 7–9 minutes until cooked through.
  3. Season: stir in spices for 60 seconds.
  4. Crushed tomatoes: Can size note—use a standard 28 oz can crushed tomatoes.. Add 2 1/2 cups crushed tomatoes; refrigerate remainder.
  5. Broth: carton size note—use a 32 oz carton low-sodium chicken broth. Add 2 cups broth; refrigerate remainder.
  6. Beans: Can size note—use a standard 15 oz can black beans.. Drain/rinse. Add 1 1/2 cups; refrigerate remainder.
  7. Simmer: add sweet potatoes and simmer 20–25 minutes until tender.
  8. Acid: add 2 tsp lime juice.
  9. Finish: taste and adjust seasoning with additional black pepper if needed.
  10. Toppings: dice avocado and serve salsa on the side. Top each bowl with measured avocado + salsa. Portion per Notes.

Notes

Turkey taco chili with sweet potatoes for comfort (no extra starch needed), topped with avocado + salsa. Portions (per person): Frank: 1/3 of the pot + 1/3 of the avocado + 1/3 of the salsa Susan: 1/4 of the pot + 1/4 of the avocado + 1/4 of the salsa Elise: 1/4 of the pot + 1/4 of the avocado + 1/4 of the salsa
