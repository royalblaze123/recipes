Teriyaki-Style Chicken Stir-Fry Bowls (Light Sauce)

Nutrition (approx)

Frank: 645 kcal • P 58g • C 70g • F 14g • Sat 3g • Na 700mg • Fiber 10g
Susan/Elise (each): 484 kcal • P 44g • C 52g • F 10g • Sat 2g • Na 525mg • Fiber 8g

Ingredients (purchase-clean)

  • 1.75 lb boneless skinless chicken breast
  • 1 bag frozen mixed vegetables
  • 1 each pineapple
  • 1 cup dry brown rice
  • low-sodium soy sauce
  • cornstarch
  • kosher salt
  • black pepper
  • garlic powder
  • onion powder
  • smoked paprika
  • Italian seasoning
  • ground cumin
  • chili powder
  • red pepper flakes
  • extra-virgin olive oil
  • low-sodium chicken broth

Steps

  1. Rice: cook 1 cup dry brown rice with 2 cups water 35–40 minutes; rest 5 minutes.
  2. Sauce: whisk 6 tbsp low-sodium soy sauce with 1 cup water and 1 tbsp cornstarch until smooth.
  3. Chicken: sauté chicken strips with 2 tsp olive oil, black pepper, garlic powder 6–8 minutes.
  4. Veg: sauté 3 cups frozen mixed vegetables until browned and water evaporates (6–8 minutes).
  5. Pineapple: dice pineapple; add 2 cups diced pineapple to the pan.
  6. Finish: pour in sauce; simmer 2–3 minutes until glossy and thick.
  7. Serve over rice per Notes.

Notes

Teriyaki comfort without sugar overload—pineapple sweetness and a light, thickened sauce. Portions (per person): Frank: About 2 1/2 cups (heaping bowl) Susan: About 2 cups (bowl) Elise: About 2 cups (bowl) Tips (recipe-specific): Brown the veg first so the stir-fry tastes like takeout. Use cornstarch thickening so you need less soy sauce overall.
