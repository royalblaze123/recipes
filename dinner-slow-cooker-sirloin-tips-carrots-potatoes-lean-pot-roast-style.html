Sirloin Tips + Carrots + Potatoes (Lean Pot-Roast Style)

Nutrition (approx)

Frank: 650 kcal • P 55g • C 60g • F 18g
Susan/Elise (each): 488 kcal • P 41g • C 45g • F 14g

Ingredients (purchase-clean)

  • 1.50 lb lean sirloin steak
  • 1.50 lb baby potatoes
  • 1 lb carrots
  • 1 each onion
  • low-sodium beef broth
  • cornstarch
  • kosher salt
  • black pepper
  • garlic powder
  • onion powder
  • smoked paprika
  • Italian seasoning
  • ground cumin
  • chili powder
  • red pepper flakes
  • extra-virgin olive oil
  • low-sodium chicken broth
  • low-sodium beef broth
  • low-sodium soy sauce
  • hot sauce
  • Dijon mustard
  • balsamic vinegar
  • apple cider vinegar
  • cornstarch

Steps (slow cooker)

  1. Cube sirloin; season pepper, garlic powder, onion powder; modest salt.
  2. Sear beef briefly for browning (recommended).
  3. Layer potatoes + carrots + onion; beef on top.
  4. Add 2 cups low-sodium beef broth.
  5. LOW 4–5 hr (best texture) or HIGH 2–3 hr (stop when tender but intact).
  6. Thicken: cornstarch slurry; HIGH 10–15 min.
  7. Serve per Notes.

Notes

Pot-roast comfort without chuck roast—still lean and thickened like gravy. Portions (per person): Frank: 7 oz beef + 1 cup potatoes + 1 cup carrots/gravy Susan: 5 oz beef + 3/4 cup potatoes + 3/4 cup carrots/gravy Elise: 5 oz beef + 3/4 cup potatoes + 3/4 cup carrots/gravy Tips (recipe-specific): LOW keeps beef from shredding. Thicken at the end so leftovers stay gravy-like.
