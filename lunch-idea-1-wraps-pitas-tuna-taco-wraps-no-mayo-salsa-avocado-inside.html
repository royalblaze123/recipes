Tuna Taco Wraps (No Mayo; Salsa + Avocado Inside)

Nutrition (approx)

Frank: 520 kcal • P 52g • C 48g • F 14g
Susan/Elise (each): 390 kcal • P 39g • C 36g • F 10g

Ingredients (purchase-clean)

  • 3 cans tuna
  • 1 package large whole-wheat tortillas
  • 1 each avocado
  • 1/2 jar salsa
  • 1 bag shredded lettuce
  • 1 each cucumber
  • 1 bag mini sweet peppers
  • 4 tsp extra-virgin olive oil
  • 2 tsp garlic powder
  • 1 tsp black pepper
  • 1 tsp kosher salt

Steps

  1. Tuna: Can size note—use a standard 5 oz can tuna.. Use three 5 oz cans tuna. Drain very well.
  2. Warm tuna in a skillet with 2 tsp olive oil for 1–2 minutes. Season with garlic powder, black pepper, and kosher salt.
  3. Wrap build: add shredded lettuce, then tuna.
  4. Avocado: halve, pit, and dice. Use about 1/2 avocado total.
  5. Salsa: spoon salsa inside each wrap/pita (not just on the side) so every bite has flavor.
  6. Roll and slice.
  7. Veggie sticks: slice cucumber and mini sweet peppers into sticks for a crunchy side.
  8. Portion per Notes.

Notes

Pantry-protein taco wraps: tuna + salsa + avocado inside for moisture and flavor. Portions (per person): Frank: 1 large wrap (with salsa + avocado inside) + veggie sticks Susan: 3/4 wrap (with salsa + avocado inside) + veggie sticks Elise: 3/4 wrap (with salsa + avocado inside) + veggie sticks
