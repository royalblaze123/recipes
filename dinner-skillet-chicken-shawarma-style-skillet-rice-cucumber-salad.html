Chicken Shawarma-Style Skillet + Rice + Cucumber Salad

Nutrition (approx)

Frank: 650 kcal • P 60g • C 70g • F 14g
Susan/Elise (each): 488 kcal • P 45g • C 52g • F 10g

Ingredients (purchase-clean)

  • 1.75 lb boneless skinless chicken breast
  • 1 cup dry brown rice
  • 1 each cucumber
  • 1 each lemon
  • 1 each onion
  • low-sodium chicken broth
  • kosher salt
  • black pepper
  • garlic powder
  • onion powder
  • smoked paprika
  • Italian seasoning
  • ground cumin
  • chili powder
  • red pepper flakes
  • extra-virgin olive oil
  • low-sodium chicken broth
  • low-sodium beef broth
  • low-sodium soy sauce
  • hot sauce
  • Dijon mustard
  • balsamic vinegar
  • apple cider vinegar
  • cornstarch

Steps (stovetop)

  1. Rice: cook 1 cup dry brown rice.
  2. Chicken: slice thin; season cumin (2 tsp), smoked paprika (2 tsp), garlic powder (2 tsp), black pepper, modest salt. Sear with sliced onion 10–12 minutes to 165°F.
  3. Deglaze: add 1/4 cup broth; simmer 2 minutes.
  4. Cucumber salad: dice cucumber; toss with lemon juice (2 tbsp), olive oil (2 tsp), black pepper, and a small pinch of salt.
  5. Serve: chicken over rice with cucumber salad on side.

Notes

Shawarma directionally without sauces; lemony cucumber salad keeps it fresh. Portions (per person): Frank: 7 oz chicken + 1 cup rice + 1 cup cucumber salad Susan: 5 oz chicken + 3/4 cup rice + 3/4 cup salad Elise: 5 oz chicken + 3/4 cup rice + 3/4 cup salad Tips (recipe-specific): Thin-slice chicken for tenderness. Add lemon salad at the end so it stays crisp.
