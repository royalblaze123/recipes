Creamy Miso-Ginger Sauce

Category: Sauce • Yield: About 3/4 cup (14 tbsp) • AnyList-ready

Ingredients

  • 3/4 cup 2% plain Greek yogurt
  • 2 tsp white miso paste
  • 2 tsp rice vinegar
  • 2 tsp sesame oil
  • 1 tsp fresh ginger

Steps

  1. Grate fresh ginger to measure 1 tsp.
  2. Whisk Greek yogurt, miso paste, rice vinegar, sesame oil, and ginger until smooth.
  3. If needed, thin with 1–2 tsp water.
