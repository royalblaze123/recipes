Chicken and Black Bean Chili

NOTES: Each chili is designed as a full meal with lean protein, smart carbs, and plenty of vegetables. Use low-sodium, no-sugar-added canned goods, avoid added sugars, and keep portions aligned with your goals. PORTION SIZES: Frank: largest portion (~1/3 of the total pot); Susan: medium portion (~1/4 of the pot); Elise: medium portion (~1/4 of the pot) (Frank = 1.0×, Susan/Elise ≈0.75× portions).

Ingredients

  • 12 oz boneless skinless chicken breast, diced small
  • 1 small yellow onion, diced
  • 1 red bell pepper, diced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced or 1 tsp garlic powder
  • 1 can (15 oz) no-salt-added black beans, drained and rinsed
  • 1 can (15 oz) no-salt-added diced tomatoes
  • 1 cup low-sodium chicken broth
  • 1 tbsp tomato paste (no sugar added)
  • 1 tbsp extra-virgin olive oil
  • 1 1/2 tsp mild chili powder
  • 1 tsp ground cumin
  • Freshly ground black pepper, to taste

Step-by-Step Instructions

  1. Dice chicken, onion, and bell pepper into small, even pieces.
  2. Heat olive oil in a soup pot over medium heat for 1–2 minutes.
  3. Add diced chicken and cook 5–6 minutes, stirring occasionally, until lightly browned and no longer pink.
  4. Add onion, bell pepper, and garlic. Cook 4–5 minutes, stirring occasionally, until vegetables soften.
  5. Stir in chili powder, cumin, and black pepper. Cook 1 minute.
  6. Add black beans, diced tomatoes with juice, tomato paste, and chicken broth. Stir well.
  7. Bring to a gentle boil, then reduce heat to low. Cover and simmer 20–25 minutes, stirring once or twice, until chili thickens.
  8. Taste and adjust seasoning. Ladle into three bowls, giving Frank the largest portion. Serve with a side of steamed green beans or a small salad.

Nutrition per Person

  • Frank: ~620 kcal, ~48 g protein, ~50 g carbs, ~18 g fat.
  • Susan: ~465 kcal, ~36 g protein, ~38 g carbs, ~13 g fat.
  • Elise: ~465 kcal, ~36 g protein, ~38 g carbs, ~13 g fat.
