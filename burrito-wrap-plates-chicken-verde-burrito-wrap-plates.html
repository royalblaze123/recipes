Chicken Verde Burrito Wrap Plates

Nutrition (approx)

Frank: 645 kcal • P 58g • C 56g • F 18g
Susan/Elise (each): 484 kcal • P 44g • C 42g • F 14g

Ingredients (purchase-clean)

  • 1.50 lb boneless skinless chicken breast
  • 1 jar salsa verde
  • 1 can cannellini beans
  • 1 package large flour tortillas
  • 1 bag spring mix
  • 1 each avocado
  • 1/2 jar salsa
  • 1 each lime
  • 6 tsp extra-virgin olive oil
  • 1 cup dry brown rice
  • 2 tsp cumin
  • 2 tsp garlic powder
  • 1 tsp black pepper
  • 1 tsp kosher salt

Steps

  1. Brown rice: rinse 1 cup dry brown rice. Cook with 2 cups water (or low-sodium broth) on stovetop: bring to a boil, cover, reduce to low, and simmer 35–40 minutes. Rest 5 minutes, then fluff.
  2. Chicken: cut into small pieces. Cook in a skillet with 2 tsp olive oil 8–10 minutes until 165°F.
  3. Season chicken with cumin, garlic powder, black pepper, and kosher salt.
  4. Verde: jar size note—use a 16 oz jar salsa verde. Stir 1/2 cup verde into the chicken; refrigerate remainder.
  5. Beans: Can size note—use a standard 15 oz can cannellini beans.. Drain/rinse. Use 1 1/2 cups beans; refrigerate remainder.
  6. Combine: warm 2 minutes.
  7. Tortillas: warm tortillas by microwaving under a damp paper towel for 25–35 seconds so they fold without cracking.
  8. Assembly: set out 4 tortillas. Build 2 burritos for Frank and 1 each for Susan and Elise. Use the cooked protein + rice + beans/veg as directed, then spoon salsa inside each burrito before rolling.
  9. Roll: fold in the sides, then roll tight. Optionally sear seam-side down in a dry nonstick skillet 1–2 minutes per side for a toasted finish.
  10. Side salad: toss spring mix with 2 tsp lime juice and 2 tsp olive oil. Add diced cucumber/tomatoes if listed.
  11. Avocado: halve, pit, and dice. Use about 1/2 avocado total; refrigerate remaining avocado with the pit to slow browning.
  12. Serve with salsa (red) on the side plus avocado. Portion per Notes.

Notes

Green chicken burrito plates (verde inside) with rice + beans; still serve salsa + avocado. Portions (per person): Frank: 2 burritos (use 2 tortillas) + 1/3 of the side salad/veg + 1/3 of the avocado + salsa Susan: 1 burrito + 1/4 of the side salad/veg + 1/4 of the avocado + salsa Elise: 1 burrito + 1/4 of the side salad/veg + 1/4 of the avocado + salsa
