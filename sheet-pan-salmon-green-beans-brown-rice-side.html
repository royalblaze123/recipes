Sheet Pan Salmon and Green Beans with Brown Rice

Prep: 18 min   Cook: 18 min   Total: 36 min

Ingredients

  • 1.5 lb salmon fillets
  • 1 lb green beans
  • 0.5 cup dry brown rice
  • olive oil
  • black pepper
  • salt substitute
  • lemon
  • garlic powder
  • Dijon mustard

Hard rules enforced: Dinner validations (calories, sat fat, sodium, fiber, added sugar). Quantities appear before items; fresh meat/poultry/fish use weights. Condiments/spices/seasonings have no measurements in Ingredients; all measurements are in Steps. No olives, no squash, no Brussels sprouts, no zucchini. Yogurt is not used in dinners.

Step-by-step Instructions

  1. Cook brown rice: cook 1/2 cup dry brown rice per package directions (water in Steps only).
  2. Preheat oven to 425°F. Line sheet pan.
  3. Green beans: trim ends. Toss with 2 tsp olive oil + garlic powder + black pepper + salt substitute. Spread on pan and roast 8 minutes.
  4. Salmon: pat dry. Brush lightly with Dijon mustard (thin). Season with black pepper + salt substitute. Add salmon to pan and roast 10–12 minutes until flaky.
  5. Finish with lemon squeeze.
  6. Portion: Frank gets the largest salmon and rice share; Susan/Elise each ~75%.
