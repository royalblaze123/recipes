Air Fryer Turkey Meatballs + Marinara + Barilla Protein+

Ingredients (purchase-clean)

  • 1.75 lb 93% lean ground turkey
  • 1 box Barilla Protein+ pasta
  • 1 jar marinara sauce
  • 6 oz grated Parmesan cheese

Steps (Cosori dual-basket)

  1. Preheat Cosori to 380°F. Basket A: meatballs. Basket B optional: broccoli (if adding).
  2. Meatballs: mix turkey with 2 tsp Italian seasoning, 2 tsp garlic powder, 1 tsp onion powder, 1/2 tsp kosher salt, and 1/2 tsp black pepper. Form 18 meatballs.
  3. Air fry meatballs (Basket A) 10–12 minutes at 380°F with Shake Reminder; shake/roll at reminder; cook to 165°F.
  4. Sauce: simmer 2 cups marinara (measured) on stovetop 8 minutes to thicken; add 1/2 tsp red pepper flakes if desired.
  5. Pasta: cook Barilla Protein+ pasta per box to al dente; drain.
  6. Combine: toss pasta with marinara and meatballs. Finish with 2 oz Parmesan per plate total (measured).
  7. Serve per Notes.

Notes

Meatball pasta comfort with air-fryer browning and controlled cheese. Portions (per person): Frank: 6 meatballs + 2 cups pasta bowl Susan: 5 meatballs + 1 1/2 cups pasta bowl Elise: 5 meatballs + 1 1/2 cups pasta bowl Tips (recipe-specific): Air-fryer meatballs brown without frying oil. Simmer sauce thicker so pasta stays saucy, not watery.
