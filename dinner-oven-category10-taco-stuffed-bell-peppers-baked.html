Taco-Stuffed Bell Peppers (Baked)

Nutrition (approx)

Frank: 650 kcal • P 55g • C 70g • F 16g
Susan/Elise (each): 488 kcal • P 41g • C 52g • F 12g

Ingredients (purchase-clean)

  • 6 each bell pepper
  • 1.50 lb 93% lean ground turkey
  • 1 can black beans
  • 1 cup dry brown rice
  • 1 jar salsa
  • 6 oz reduced-fat cheddar cheese
  • low-sodium chicken broth
  • kosher salt
  • black pepper
  • garlic powder
  • onion powder
  • smoked paprika
  • Italian seasoning
  • ground cumin
  • chili powder
  • red pepper flakes
  • extra-virgin olive oil
  • low-sodium chicken broth
  • low-sodium beef broth
  • low-sodium soy sauce
  • hot sauce
  • Dijon mustard
  • balsamic vinegar
  • apple cider vinegar
  • cornstarch

Steps (oven)

  1. Oven: heat to 400°F.
  2. Rice: cook 1 cup dry brown rice (35–40 minutes).
  3. Peppers: halve and remove seeds. Roast cut-side down 400°F 12 minutes.
  4. Turkey filling: brown turkey with chili powder, cumin, garlic powder, black pepper, modest salt (8–10 minutes).
  5. Mix: add cooked rice (3 cups cooked) + beans (15 oz; 1 1/2 cups drained/rinsed) + salsa (1 cup measured). Simmer 2 minutes to thicken.
  6. Stuff: fill peppers; top with measured cheddar; bake 400°F 12–15 minutes.
  7. Serve per Notes.

Notes

Stuffed peppers that eat like a full dinner—thick filling, measured cheese, no sides needed. Portions (per person): Frank: 2 stuffed pepper halves Susan: 1 1/2 halves (one full + half) Elise: 1 1/2 halves (one full + half) Tips (recipe-specific): Roast peppers first so they soften without turning mushy. Keep filling thick so peppers don’t get watery.
