Salsa Verde Meatloaf Plate

Nutrition (approx)

Frank: 640 kcal • P 55g • C 42g • F 23g
Susan/Elise (each): 480 kcal • P 41g • C 32g • F 17g

Ingredients (purchase-clean)

  • 1.50 lb 93% lean ground beef
  • 1 each yellow onion
  • 2 each large eggs
  • 3/4 cup whole wheat panko breadcrumbs
  • 8 tsp salsa verde
  • 2 tsp cumin
  • 2 tsp garlic powder
  • 1 tsp black pepper
  • 1 tsp kosher salt
  • 1.50 lb sweet potatoes
  • 2 each bell peppers
  • 4 tsp extra-virgin olive oil

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 400°F. Line a sheet pan with parchment.
  2. Dice yellow onion. Dice bell peppers into 3/4-inch pieces.
  3. Meatloaf mix: combine ground beef, onion, eggs, panko, 6 tsp salsa verde, cumin, garlic powder, black pepper, and kosher salt. Mix just until combined.
  4. Form loaf and bake 35–40 minutes until 160°F in the center.
  5. Top: spoon remaining 2 tsp salsa verde over slices at serving time.
  6. Sweet potatoes: cube into 3/4-inch pieces; toss with 2 tsp olive oil; roast 25–30 minutes, turning once.
  7. Peppers: toss peppers with 2 tsp olive oil; add for the last 12–15 minutes until tender-crisp and browned at edges.
  8. Rest meatloaf 10 minutes before slicing.

Notes

Salsa verde meatloaf with roasted sweet potatoes and peppers for a bright, savory plate.
