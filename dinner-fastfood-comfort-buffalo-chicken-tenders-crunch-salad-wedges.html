Buffalo Chicken Tenders + Crunch Salad + Wedges

Nutrition (approx)

Frank: 645 kcal • P 60g • C 58g • F 18g • Sat 5g • Na 700mg • Fiber 11g
Susan/Elise (each): 484 kcal • P 45g • C 44g • F 14g • Sat 4g • Na 525mg • Fiber 8g

Ingredients (purchase-clean)

  • 1.75 lb chicken tenderloins
  • 1 carton egg whites
  • 1 package whole-wheat panko breadcrumbs
  • 1 bottle buffalo sauce
  • 1.50 lb baby potatoes
  • 1 bag shredded lettuce
  • 1 each cucumber
  • 1 pint cherry tomatoes
  • kosher salt
  • black pepper
  • garlic powder
  • onion powder
  • smoked paprika
  • chili powder
  • ground cumin
  • Italian seasoning
  • crushed red pepper
  • cornstarch
  • low-sodium soy sauce
  • rice vinegar
  • hot sauce
  • Dijon mustard
  • extra-virgin olive oil

Steps

  1. Air fryer (Cosori): preheat to 400°F.
  2. Wedges: cut baby potatoes into wedges. Toss with 2 tsp olive oil, 1 tsp smoked paprika, 1/2 tsp kosher salt, 1/2 tsp black pepper.
  3. Cook wedges: Basket A, 400°F for 14–18 minutes with shake reminder ON.
  4. Breading bowls: Bowl 1: 1/2 cup egg whites. Bowl 2: 1 cup panko + 1 tsp garlic powder + 1 tsp onion powder + 1/2 tsp black pepper.
  5. Cook tenders: Basket B, 400°F for 10–12 minutes with shake reminder ON; flip at prompt; cook to 165°F.
  6. Sauce: toss cooked tenders with 2 tbsp buffalo sauce (measured).
  7. Salad: shred lettuce; slice cucumber; halve tomatoes. Dress with 1 tsp rice vinegar + hot sauce + black pepper.
  8. Serve per Notes.

Notes

Wings-night energy without wings: buffalo tenders, crunchy salad, and wedges. Portions (per person): Frank: 6 tenders + 3/4 cup wedges + big salad side + dip Susan: 4 tenders + 1/2 cup wedges + big salad side + dip Elise: 4 tenders + 1/2 cup wedges + big salad side + dip Tips (recipe-specific): Keep buffalo sauce measured; add more per bite at the table if needed. Acid (vinegar) in the salad makes the whole plate taste louder.
