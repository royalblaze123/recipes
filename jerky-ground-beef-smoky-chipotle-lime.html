Jerky Gun Ground Beef — Smoky Chipotle Lime (Health-Goal)

Servings

3 portions (Frank 1.0×, Susan 0.75×, Elise 0.75×).

Times

    Prep time: 20 minutes Cook time: 390 minutes Total time: 410 minutes

Ingredients

  • 1 lb 96% lean ground beef
  • 1 large egg
  • 6 tsp old fashioned oats
  • 6 tsp no salt added tomato paste
  • 3 tsp apple cider vinegar
  • 2 tsp smoked paprika
  • 2 tsp chili powder
  • 1 tsp chipotle powder
  • 1 tsp garlic powder
  • 1 tsp onion powder
  • 1 tsp lime zest
  • 1 tsp black pepper
  • 1/2 tsp liquid smoke

Recipe

  1. Preheat oven to 275°F. Line 2 rimmed baking sheets with foil and set wire racks on top. Lightly coat racks with cooking spray. Set dehydrator to 160°F.
  2. In a bowl, mix 1 lb 96% lean ground beef, 1 large egg (use the white; save yolk), 6 tsp old fashioned oats, 6 tsp no salt added tomato paste, 3 tsp apple cider vinegar, 2 tsp smoked paprika, 2 tsp chili powder, 1 tsp chipotle powder, 1 tsp garlic powder, 1 tsp onion powder, 1 tsp lime zest, 1 tsp black pepper, and 1/2 tsp liquid smoke until very tacky (60–90 seconds).
  3. Cover and refrigerate the mixture 30–60 minutes to hydrate spices and improve cohesion.
  4. Load mixture into jerky gun. Extrude 1 test strip onto a rack (about 1/4-inch wide strip). If the strip tears or feels sticky, chill mixture 10–15 minutes. If it crumbles, mix in 1–2 tsp water and extrude again.
  5. Extrude remaining mixture in straight, even strips on racks, leaving space so strips do not touch.
  6. Oven lethality step: bake at 275°F until the thickest strip reaches 160°F (about 10–15 minutes). Do not skip; use an instant-read probe in the thickest section.
  7. Transfer racks/trays to dehydrator. Dry at 160°F for 4–7 hours, rotating trays top-to-bottom every 2 hours, until jerky passes the bend/tear test (bends then cracks; no visible moisture can be squeezed out).
  8. Cool strips on racks 30–60 minutes. Condition in a loosely covered container for 12–24 hours; if condensation appears, return to dehydrator 30–60 minutes and re-cool.
  9. Portion and store: see Notes. Refrigerate for short-term; freeze for longer storage.

Notes

Bold smoky chipotle-lime jerky with a tomato-paste concentrate and low-sodium layering for full flavor without tipping health goals.
