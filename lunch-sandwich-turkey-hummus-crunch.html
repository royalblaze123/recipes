Lunch Sandwich Turkey Hummus Crunch

Turkey hummus sandwiches with big crunch from cucumber and carrots plus spring mix—full lunch built into the sandwich.

Servings

3 servings.

Times

  • Prep time: 12 minutes
  • Cook time: 0 minutes
  • Total time: 12 minutes

Ingredients

Main Dish

  • 6 slices whole wheat bread
  • 14 slices deli turkey
  • 3 slices provolone cheese
  • 1 container hummus
  • 1 bag spring mix
  • 1 each cucumber
  • 1 lb bag carrots
  • 1 jar black pepper
  • 1 each lemon
    • 

    Preparation Steps

    1. Set up: lay out 6 slices whole wheat bread (3 sandwiches).
    2. Spread: measure out 4 tbsp hummus for this recipe; refrigerate remaining hummus.
    3. Veg: measure out 6 packed cups spring mix for these sandwiches; refrigerate remaining spring mix.
    4. Slice: slice cucumber. Use about 1/2 cucumber for these sandwiches; refrigerate the rest.
    5. Carrot ribbons: peel 1 carrot into ribbons (about 1 cup ribbons).
    6. Build (Frank): 2 slices bread + 6 slices turkey + 1 slice provolone + 2 tbsp hummus + ~2 cups spring mix + cucumber + 1/3 of the carrot ribbons + a squeeze of lemon.
    7. Build (Susan): 2 slices bread + 4 slices turkey + 1 slice provolone + 1 tbsp hummus + ~2 cups spring mix + cucumber + 1/3 of the carrot ribbons + a squeeze of lemon.
    8. Build (Elise): 2 slices bread + 4 slices turkey + 1 slice provolone + 1 tbsp hummus + ~2 cups spring mix + cucumber + 1/3 of the carrot ribbons + a squeeze of lemon.
    9. Close sandwiches, press gently, and wrap.
      10. 

      Notes

      Portions (per person):


      Frank – 1 whole sandwich: 6 slices turkey, 1 slice provolone, 2 tbsp hummus, ~2 cups spring mix, cucumber, carrot ribbons.


      Susan – 1 whole sandwich: 4 slices turkey, 1 slice provolone, 1 tbsp hummus, ~2 cups spring mix, cucumber, carrot ribbons.


      Elise – 1 whole sandwich: 4 slices turkey, 1 slice provolone, 1 tbsp hummus, ~2 cups spring mix, cucumber, carrot ribbons.


      Tips (recipe-specific):


      Hummus is calorie-dense—measure it so Frank stays in the lunch band.


      Carrot ribbons add bulk and crunch without much sodium.


      If the sandwich feels tall, compress spring mix by hand before stacking.

      • Elise: 409 kcal; 39 g protein; 36 g carbs; 14 g fat; 8 g fiber; 480 mg sodium
