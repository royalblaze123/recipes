Protein Bowl: Turkey Farro Bowl with White Beans and Wilted Spinach

Prep: 16 min   Cook: 16 min   Total: 32 min

Ingredients

  • 1.0 lb lean ground turkey 93%
  • 0.5 cup dry farro
  • 0.5 can no-salt-added cannellini beans
  • 1 bag spinach
  • 1 lemon
  • 1 onion
  • olive oil
  • Italian seasoning
  • garlic powder
  • black pepper
  • salt substitute

Rules enforced: Clean AnyList Ingredients (quantities before items; fresh meat/poultry/fish use weights; packaged items use fractional containers when partial). Condiments/spices/seasonings have no measurements in Ingredients; all measurements are in Steps. No olives, no squash, no Brussels sprouts, no zucchini, no tofu/tempeh. Lunch validations: Frank calories 500–550, sat fat ≤6g, sodium ≤650mg, fiber ≥8g, added sugar ≤6g.

Step-by-step Instructions

  1. Cook farro: cook 1/2 cup dry farro with 1 1/2 cups water per package directions (about 20 minutes).
  2. Turkey: brown 1 lb lean ground turkey with 2 tsp olive oil 7–9 minutes. Season with 2 tsp Italian seasoning + 1 tsp garlic powder + 1/2 tsp black pepper + 1/2 tsp salt substitute.
  3. Beans: drain and rinse 1/2 can cannellini beans. Stir into turkey with 2 tbsp water; warm 2 minutes.
  4. Spinach: stir in 1 bag spinach and cook 1–2 minutes until wilted.
  5. Finish: add 2 tsp lemon juice per bowl and 1 tsp lemon zest total (optional).
  6. Build bowls: farro base + turkey/bean/spinach mix. Portion: Frank largest farro/turkey share; Susan/Elise ~75%.
