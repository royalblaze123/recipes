Chicken Taco Salads + Yogurt-Lime Dressing

Nutrition (approx)

Frank: 535 kcal • P 50g • C 42g • F 18g
Susan/Elise (each): 401 kcal • P 38g • C 32g • F 14g

Ingredients (purchase-clean)

  • 1.25 lb boneless skinless chicken breast
  • 1 bag shredded lettuce
  • 1 each avocado
  • 1/2 jar salsa
  • 1 each cucumber
  • 1 pint cherry tomatoes
  • 1 each lime
  • 4 tsp extra-virgin olive oil
  • 1 tub plain nonfat Greek yogurt
  • 2 tsp chili powder
  • 2 tsp cumin
  • 2 tsp garlic powder
  • 1 tsp smoked paprika
  • 1 tsp black pepper
  • 1 tsp kosher salt

Steps

  1. Chicken: cut into small pieces. Cook in a skillet with 2 tsp olive oil 8–10 minutes until 165°F. Season with spices. Cool 5 minutes.
  2. Dressing (lunch-only yogurt): whisk 1/2 cup plain nonfat Greek yogurt with 2 tsp lime juice and 1 tsp cumin.
  3. Base: divide shredded lettuce into three bowls.
  4. Protein: add seasoned chicken to each bowl.
  5. Add-ins: add diced cucumber and halved tomatoes.
  6. Drizzle dressing over each salad.
  7. Top: add salsa and measured diced avocado. Portion per Notes.
  8. Avocado: halve, pit, and dice. Use about 1/2 avocado total; refrigerate remaining avocado with the pit to slow browning.

Notes

Chicken taco salads with a small lunch-only yogurt-lime dressing for creaminess. Portions (per person): Frank: 1 large taco salad bowl + 1/3 of the avocado + salsa Susan: 1 medium taco salad bowl + 1/4 of the avocado + salsa Elise: 1 medium taco salad bowl + 1/4 of the avocado + salsa
