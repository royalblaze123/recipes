Mediterranean Feta Chicken Mini Sweet Pepper Boats

Sheet Pan • Yield: 36 pepper boats

Ingredients (purchase-clean)

  • 18 each fresh mini sweet peppers
  • 16 oz fresh chicken breast
  • 2 oz feta cheese
  • 1 each fresh lemon
  • dried oregano
  • garlic powder
  • kosher salt
  • black pepper

Steps

  1. Heat oven to 425°F. Line a sheet pan with parchment.
  2. Cook chicken with 1 tsp kosher salt, 1 tsp garlic powder, 2 tsp oregano, and 1/2 tsp black pepper until 165°F; chop fine.
  3. Halve 18 mini sweet peppers into 36 boats; remove seeds.
  4. Mix chicken with 1 tsp lemon zest and 2 tsp lemon juice; fold in 2 oz feta.
  5. Fill boats and bake 8–10 min until peppers are tender-crisp and filling is hot.

Notes

Flavor-forward holiday poppers designed for snacking or a light meal. Portions (guidance): Feta is high-flavor—keep it measured for balance. Snack portion: 6–8 boats.
