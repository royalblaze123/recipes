Tuna Melt Plates + Crunchy Veg Side

Nutrition (approx)

Frank: 640 kcal • P 55g • C 55g • F 18g • Sat 6g • Na 700mg • Fiber 10g
Susan/Elise (each): 480 kcal • P 41g • C 41g • F 14g • Sat 4g • Na 525mg • Fiber 8g

Ingredients (purchase-clean)

  • 12 slices whole-wheat sandwich bread
  • 3 cans tuna
  • 1 jar salsa
  • 6 slices reduced-fat cheese slices
  • 1 bunch celery
  • 1 bag baby carrots
  • kosher salt
  • black pepper
  • garlic powder
  • onion powder
  • Italian seasoning
  • smoked paprika
  • extra-virgin olive oil
  • Dijon mustard
  • hot sauce
  • rice vinegar

Steps

  1. Tuna: drain 3 cans tuna (5 oz each). Mix with 1/2 cup salsa (measured), black pepper, and a pinch of garlic powder.
  2. Build melts: spread tuna on 6 bread slices. Top each sandwich with 2 slices cheese (1 per half) and close with bread. Make 3 sandwiches.
  3. Skillet: toast 3–4 minutes per side over medium until cheese melts.
  4. Veg side: serve celery sticks + baby carrots. Add hot sauce if desired.
  5. Serve per Notes.

Notes

Tuna melts without mayo—salsa stands in for moisture and flavor. Portions (per person): Frank: 1 sandwich + big handful veg side Susan: 1 sandwich + big handful veg side Elise: 1 sandwich + big handful veg side Tips (recipe-specific): Salsa keeps it moist without mayo. Use celery/carrots for the crunchy ‘chip’ moment.
