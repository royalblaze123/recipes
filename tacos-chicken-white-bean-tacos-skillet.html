Chicken & White Bean Tacos (Skillet)

Nutrition (approx)

Frank: 525 kcal • P 42g • C 38g • F 18g
Susan/Elise (each): 394 kcal • P 32g • C 28g • F 14g

Ingredients (purchase-clean)

  • 1.00 lb boneless skinless chicken breast
  • 4/5 package whole-grain small tortillas
  • 1 can cannellini beans
  • 1 each yellow onion
  • 1 jar salsa
  • 1 tub 2% plain Greek yogurt
  • 2 tsp chili powder
  • 2 tsp cumin
  • 2 tsp garlic powder
  • 1 tsp black pepper
  • 1 tsp kosher salt

Steps

  1. Chicken: pat dry and cut into 1/2-inch pieces. Season with chili powder, cumin, garlic powder, black pepper, and kosher salt.
  2. Cook chicken in a nonstick skillet over medium-high 8–10 minutes until browned and 165°F.
  3. Beans: can size note—use a standard 15 oz can cannellini beans. Drain and rinse. Measure out 1 1/2 cups; refrigerate any remainder.
  4. Onion: dice yellow onion and cook 3–4 minutes with the chicken until softened.
  5. Stir in beans and 1/2 cup salsa. Simmer 2–3 minutes until saucy.
  6. Tortillas: warm tortillas 20–30 seconds per side.
  7. Top with Greek yogurt. Portion 7 tacos per Notes.

Notes

Bean-forward tacos using white beans as a fiber engine, with chicken to keep protein high. Portions (per person): Frank: 3 tacos (use 3 tortillas) + 1/3 of the filling + 1/3 of the toppings Susan: 2 tacos (use 2 tortillas) + 1/4 of the filling + 1/4 of the toppings Elise: 2 tacos (use 2 tortillas) + 1/4 of the filling + 1/4 of the toppings
