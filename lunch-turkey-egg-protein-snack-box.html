Turkey Egg Protein Snack Box

NOTES: Designed as a portable grab-and-go lunch using lean protein, high-fiber vegetables, and controlled smart carbs. Use low-sodium ingredients and avoid added sugars where possible. PORTION SIZES: Frank: largest portion (~1/3 of the total volume); Susan: medium portion (~1/4 of the total volume); Elise: medium portion (~1/4 of the total volume) (Frank = 1.0×, Susan/Elise ≈0.75× portions).

Ingredients

  • 6 large eggs
  • 8 oz sliced low-sodium turkey breast
  • 18 mini whole-grain crackers
  • 1 cup baby carrots
  • 1 cup cucumber slices (seeds removed)
  • 1 red bell pepper, cut into strips
  • 1/2 cup hummus (no added sugar, low sodium)

Step-by-Step Instructions

  1. Place eggs in a saucepan, cover with water, and bring to a gentle boil. Turn off heat, cover, and let sit 10–12 minutes. Drain, cool in cold water, peel, and slice each egg in half.
  2. Arrange three grab-and-go containers: In each, place a portion of sliced turkey, 2 boiled eggs (Frank may receive 3 halves, others 2 each), whole-grain crackers, baby carrots, cucumber slices, and bell pepper strips.
  3. Add a small container of hummus to each box for dipping vegetables and crackers.
  4. Cover and refrigerate up to 3 days. Eat cold straight from the box.

Nutrition per Person

  • Frank: ~540 kcal, ~48 g protein, ~34 g carbs, ~20 g fat.
  • Susan: ~400 kcal, ~35 g protein, ~25 g carbs, ~15 g fat.
  • Elise: ~400 kcal, ~35 g protein, ~25 g carbs, ~15 g fat.
