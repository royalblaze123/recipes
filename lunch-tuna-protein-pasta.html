Tuna Protein Pasta Lunch

High-protein tuna pasta made with whole-grain or lentil pasta.

Ingredients

  • 8 oz whole-grain or lentil pasta
  • 2 cans tuna, drained
  • 1/2 cup light mayo or Greek yogurt
  • 1/2 cup steamed peas
  • 1/4 tsp black pepper
  • 1/8 tsp salt

Instructions

  1. Cook pasta and drain.
  2. Steam peas.
  3. Combine tuna, mayo/Yogurt, pepper, and salt.
  4. Mix pasta and peas into tuna mixture.
  5. Chill or microwave 20â€“30 seconds.

Serves: 3 servings
