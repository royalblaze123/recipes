Pesto Chicken Tomato Pizza with Herb-Black Pepper Wings

Prep: 20 min   Cook: 30 min   Total: 50 min

Ingredients

  • 1 pizza dough ball
  • 0.5 jar low-sodium pizza sauce
  • 6 oz reduced-fat mozzarella cheese
  • 12 oz cooked rotisserie chicken
  • 0.25 jar pesto
  • 1 tomato
  • 1 bag spinach
  • 2.25 lb chicken wings
  • 1 lemon
  • olive oil
  • black pepper
  • salt substitute
  • garlic powder
  • Italian seasoning

RBM rules: quantities appear before items; fresh meat/poultry/fish use weights. Condiments/spices/seasonings are listed name-only (no tsp/tbsp in Ingredients); all measurements are in Steps. Water is referenced in Steps only. No olives, no squash, no Brussels sprouts, no yogurt in dinners.

Step-by-step Instructions

  1. Pizza: preheat oven to 475°F. Stretch dough into 12-inch round on parchment.
  2. Top pizza: spread ~1/2 jar pizza sauce thinly, add mozzarella, then chicken and thin tomato slices. Bake 10–12 minutes.
  3. Finish pizza: dot with pesto (about 1 tbsp per pizza; keep light) and add spinach to wilt from residual heat. Cut into 10 slices.
  4. Wings: pat dry. Toss with 2 tsp olive oil + 2 tsp salt substitute + 2 tsp black pepper + 1 tsp garlic powder + 1 tsp Italian seasoning + zest of 1 lemon.
  5. Air fry wings: 380°F for 24–26 minutes; then 400°F for 4–6 minutes. Finish with lemon squeeze.
  6. Portion: Frank 4 slices + 8 wings; Susan/Elise 3 slices + 6 wings.
