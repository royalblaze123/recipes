Lunch Sandwich Tuna Dill Yogurt Cucumber Tomato

Tuna-dill Greek-yogurt sandwiches with romaine, cucumber, and tomato—high protein with slow carbs and veg inside the sandwich.

Servings

3 servings.

Times

  • Prep time: 14 minutes
  • Cook time: 0 minutes
  • Total time: 14 minutes

Ingredients

Main Dish

  • 6 slices whole wheat bread
  • 2 cans tuna
  • 1 container nonfat Greek yogurt
  • 1 bunch dill
  • 1 each lemon
  • 1 each cucumber
  • 2 each tomatoes
  • 1 package romaine hearts
  • 1 jar Dijon mustard
  • 1 jar black pepper
    • 

    Preparation Steps

    1. Drain tuna: drain both cans well. Flake into a bowl.
    2. Mix: stir in 6 tbsp Greek yogurt, 1 tsp Dijon mustard, chopped dill, 1 tsp lemon juice, and black pepper.
    3. Veg: tear romaine. Measure out 6 packed cups romaine for these sandwiches; refrigerate remaining romaine.
    4. Slice cucumber and tomatoes. Use about 1/2 cucumber and 1 tomato for these sandwiches; refrigerate the rest.
    5. Build (Frank): 2 slices bread + about 1/2 the tuna mix + ~2 cups romaine + cucumber + tomato.
    6. Build (Susan): 2 slices bread + about 1/4 the tuna mix + ~2 cups romaine + cucumber + tomato.
    7. Build (Elise): 2 slices bread + about 1/4 the tuna mix + ~2 cups romaine + cucumber + tomato.
    8. Close sandwiches and wrap tightly so they hold together.
      9. 

      Notes

      Portions (per person):


      Frank – 1 whole sandwich: ~1/2 tuna mix batch, ~2 cups romaine, cucumber + tomato.


      Susan – 1 whole sandwich: ~1/4 tuna mix batch, ~2 cups romaine, cucumber + tomato.


      Elise – 1 whole sandwich: ~1/4 tuna mix batch, ~2 cups romaine, cucumber + tomato.


      Tips (recipe-specific):


      Drain tuna very well so the sandwich doesn’t get soggy.


      Romaine holds up better than delicate greens for a 12–3 lunch window.


      If you want extra crunch, add more cucumber and reduce tomato.

      Nutrition (per person)

      • Frank: 520 kcal; 55 g protein; 40 g carbs; 14 g fat; 8 g fiber; 620 mg sodium
      • Susan: 390 kcal; 41 g protein; 30 g carbs; 10 g fat; 6 g fiber; 465 mg sodium
      • Elise: 390 kcal; 41 g protein; 30 g carbs; 10 g fat; 6 g fiber; 465 mg sodium
