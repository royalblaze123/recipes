AnyList Collection Test I — Side Dishes

Goal: See if recipeCategory=Side Dishes auto-adds this recipe to AnyList’s built-in Side Dishes collection.

Ingredients

  • 1 bag frozen green beans
  • 1 bottle olive oil
  • 1 jar garlic powder
  • 1 jar black pepper

Preparation Steps

  1. Microwave frozen green beans per bag directions; drain any excess water.
  2. Toss green beans with 2 tsp olive oil, 1/2 tsp garlic powder, and black pepper.
  3. Air-fry 6–8 minutes at 390°F, shaking once, to dry/char edges slightly.
How to test: Import this file into AnyList, then check whether it automatically appears inside the Side Dishes collection (without manually assigning it).
