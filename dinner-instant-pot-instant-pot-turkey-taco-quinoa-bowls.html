Instant Pot Turkey Taco Quinoa Bowls

Nutrition (approx)

Frank: 650 kcal • P 58g • C 70g • F 14g
Susan/Elise (each): 488 kcal • P 44g • C 52g • F 10g

Ingredients (purchase-clean)

  • 1.50 lb 93% lean ground turkey
  • 1 cup dry quinoa
  • 1 jar salsa
  • 1 can black beans
  • low-sodium chicken broth
  • kosher salt
  • black pepper
  • garlic powder
  • onion powder
  • smoked paprika
  • Italian seasoning
  • ground cumin
  • chili powder
  • red pepper flakes
  • extra-virgin olive oil
  • low-sodium chicken broth
  • low-sodium beef broth
  • low-sodium soy sauce
  • hot sauce
  • Dijon mustard
  • balsamic vinegar
  • apple cider vinegar
  • cornstarch

Steps (Instant Pot)

  1. Sauté: brown turkey with chili powder, cumin, garlic powder, black pepper, modest salt (7–9 minutes).
  2. Load: add quinoa (1 cup dry), broth (1 1/2 cups), and salsa (1 cup measured). Stir well.
  3. Pressure cook: High 2 minutes; Natural Release 10 minutes, then Quick Release.
  4. Beans: stir in drained/rinsed black beans (15 oz; use 1 1/2 cups). Sauté 2 minutes to heat.
  5. Serve per Notes.

Notes

Taco bowls that cook fast and stay thick—quinoa absorbs the flavor. Portions (per person): Frank: About 2 1/2 cups bowl Susan: About 2 cups bowl Elise: About 2 cups bowl Tips (recipe-specific): Rinse quinoa before cooking for best flavor. Add beans after pressure so they don’t get soft.
