Lemon Dill Salmon Salad Sandwich + Pepper Strips

Canned salmon salad brightened with lemon and dill, finished with Swiss and paired with crunchy peppers.

Servings

3 servings (Frank, Susan, Elise).

Times

  • Prep time: 15 minutes
  • Cook time: 0 minutes
  • Total time: 15 minutes

Ingredients

Sandwich

  • 6 slices whole wheat bread
  • 2 cans salmon
  • 1 cup nonfat Greek yogurt
  • 1 bunch celery
  • 1 bunch fresh dill
  • 1 each lemon
  • 3 slices reduced-fat Swiss cheese
  • 2 hearts romaine hearts

Side

  • 1 bag mini sweet peppers
  • 1 each cucumber

Step-by-step Instructions

  1. Make salmon salad: Drain salmon well. In a bowl, mix 1/2 cup Greek yogurt with 1 tbsp lemon juice and 1 tbsp chopped dill. Finely chop celery and fold in with salmon until evenly coated.
  2. Assemble sandwiches: Layer romaine on bread, add salmon salad, then portion Swiss per person (cut slices to match 0.75 portions). Close into 3 full sandwiches.
  3. Prep side: Slice peppers lengthwise and slice cucumber into rounds. Portion per person. Pack cucumbers separately from bread if transporting.

Portion Sizes

Portions (per person):
Frank: 2 slices bread, 3.0 oz drained salmon, 2 tbsp Greek yogurt, 1/3 cup chopped celery, 1 slice Swiss, 1 cup romaine, 1 cup sliced peppers, 1/2 cucumber

Susan: 2 slices bread, 2.25 oz drained salmon, 1 1/2 tbsp Greek yogurt, 1/4 cup chopped celery, 3/4 slice Swiss, 3/4 cup romaine, 3/4 cup sliced peppers, 1/4 cucumber

Elise: 2 slices bread, 2.25 oz drained salmon, 1 1/2 tbsp Greek yogurt, 1/4 cup chopped celery, 3/4 slice Swiss, 3/4 cup romaine, 3/4 cup sliced peppers, 1/4 cucumber
