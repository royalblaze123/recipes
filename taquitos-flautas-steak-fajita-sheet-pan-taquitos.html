Steak Fajita Sheet-Pan Taquitos

Nutrition (approx)

Frank: 645 kcal • P 58g • C 50g • F 20g
Susan/Elise (each): 484 kcal • P 44g • C 38g • F 15g

Ingredients (purchase-clean)

  • 1.25 lb sirloin steak
  • 1 bag frozen fajita-style pepper blend
  • 1 package corn tortillas
  • 1 each avocado
  • 1/2 jar salsa
  • 1 bag spring mix
  • 1 each lime
  • 6 tsp extra-virgin olive oil
  • 1 bag reduced-fat shredded Mexican cheese blend
  • 2 tsp chili powder
  • 2 tsp cumin
  • 2 tsp garlic powder
  • 1 tsp smoked paprika
  • 1 tsp black pepper
  • 1 tsp kosher salt

Steps

  1. Tortillas: warm corn tortillas by microwaving under a damp paper towel for 25–35 seconds so they roll without cracking.
  2. Veg: cook frozen fajita pepper blend in a skillet with 2 tsp olive oil 6–8 minutes until browned.
  3. Steak: slice thin against grain. Cook in the same skillet with 2 tsp olive oil for 2–3 minutes until just cooked. Season with spices.
  4. Filling: chop steak-pepper mix finer if needed. Mix with 3/4 cup shredded cheese. Cool 5 minutes.
  5. Assemble taquitos: add 2 tbsp filling per tortilla; roll tight; seam-side down on sheet pan.
  6. Preheat oven to 425°F. Line a sheet pan with parchment.
  7. Brush taquitos lightly with oil and bake 14–18 minutes, turning once, until crisp and browned.
  8. Side salad: add spring mix to a bowl. Drizzle with 2 tsp lime juice and 2 tsp olive oil; toss.
  9. Avocado: halve, pit, and dice. Use about 1/2 avocado total; refrigerate remaining avocado with the pit to slow browning.
  10. Serve with salsa and portion per Notes.

Notes

Fajita taquitos: steak + peppers rolled and crisped, with salsa and avocado on the side. Portions (per person): Frank: 6 taquitos + 1/3 of the side + 1/3 of the avocado + salsa Susan: 4 taquitos + 1/4 of the side + 1/4 of the avocado + salsa Elise: 4 taquitos + 1/4 of the side + 1/4 of the avocado + salsa
