Tuna Tostada Plates (No Mayo)

Nutrition (approx)

Frank: 615 kcal • P 60g • C 46g • F 18g
Susan/Elise (each): 461 kcal • P 45g • C 34g • F 14g

Ingredients (purchase-clean)

  • 3 cans tuna
  • 1 package baked tostada shells
  • 1 each avocado
  • 1/2 jar salsa
  • 1 pint cherry tomatoes
  • 1 each cucumber
  • 2 tsp extra-virgin olive oil
  • 1 each lime
  • 1 tsp black pepper
  • 1 tsp kosher salt

Steps

  1. Tostadas: warm in a 350°F oven for 3–4 minutes to crisp and heat through.
  2. Tuna: can size note—use three 5 oz cans tuna. Drain very well.
  3. Pico-style veg: dice cherry tomatoes and cucumber. Toss with 2 tsp lime juice, 2 tsp olive oil, black pepper, and kosher salt.
  4. Avocado: halve, pit, and dice. Use about 1/2 avocado total; refrigerate remaining avocado with the pit to slow browning.
  5. Serve: top tostadas with tuna, then pico veg, then salsa and avocado. Portion per Notes.

Notes

Pantry-friendly tuna tostadas with fresh pico-style veg, plus salsa and avocado (no mayo). Portions (per person): Frank: 2 tostadas + 1/3 of the protein topping + 1/3 of the avocado + salsa Susan: 1 tostada + 1/4 of the protein topping + 1/4 of the avocado + salsa Elise: 1 tostada + 1/4 of the protein topping + 1/4 of the avocado + salsa
