Creamy Dill Mustard Sauce

Category: Sauce • Yield: About 3/4 cup (14 tbsp) • AnyList-ready

Ingredients

  • 3/4 cup 2% plain Greek yogurt
  • 2 tsp Dijon mustard
  • 2 tsp lemon juice
  • 2 tsp extra-virgin olive oil
  • 2 tsp fresh dill
  • 1/4 tsp black pepper

Steps

  1. Whisk Greek yogurt, Dijon, lemon juice, olive oil, and black pepper until smooth.
  2. Stir in dill. Chill 10 minutes.
