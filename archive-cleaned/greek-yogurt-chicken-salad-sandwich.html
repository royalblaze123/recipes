Greek Yogurt Chicken Salad Sandwich + Pepper Strips

Creamy Greek-yogurt chicken salad with celery and herbs, built into full sandwiches with a crunchy pepper side.

Servings

3 servings (Frank, Susan, Elise).

Times

  • Prep time: 12 minutes
  • Cook time: 15 minutes
  • Total time: 27 minutes

Ingredients

Sandwich

  • 6 slices whole wheat bread
  • 1 lb boneless skinless chicken breast
  • 1 cup nonfat Greek yogurt
  • 1 bunch celery
  • 1 each lemon
  • 1 bunch fresh parsley
  • 1 bunch scallions

Side

  • 1 bag mini sweet peppers
  • 1 each cucumber

Step-by-step Instructions

  1. Cook the chicken: Season chicken lightly with pepper and garlic powder if desired. Air fry at 380°F until 165°F (about 10–14 minutes depending on thickness) or bake at 425°F for 15–18 minutes. Rest 5 minutes, then chop.
  2. Make chicken salad: In a bowl, mix 1/2 cup Greek yogurt with 1 tbsp lemon juice and 1 tbsp chopped parsley. Finely chop celery and scallions; stir in. Add chopped chicken and mix until creamy. Measure out portions below; refrigerate any extra.
  3. Assemble sandwiches: Spoon chicken salad onto bread slices. Add extra chopped parsley if you like. Close into 3 full sandwiches.
  4. Prep side: Slice mini peppers lengthwise and slice cucumber into rounds. Portion per person. Pack cucumbers separately from bread if transporting.

Portion Sizes

Portions (per person):

Frank: 2 slices bread, 3.5 oz cooked chicken, 1/4 cup chicken salad dressing mix (from the Greek yogurt), 1/3 cup chopped celery/scallions mix, 1 cup sliced peppers, 1/2 cucumber

Susan: 2 slices bread, 2.75 oz cooked chicken, 3 tbsp chicken salad dressing mix, 1/4 cup chopped celery/scallions mix, 3/4 cup sliced peppers, 1/4 cucumber

Elise: 2 slices bread, 2.75 oz cooked chicken, 3 tbsp chicken salad dressing mix, 1/4 cup chopped celery/scallions mix, 3/4 cup sliced peppers, 1/4 cucumber
