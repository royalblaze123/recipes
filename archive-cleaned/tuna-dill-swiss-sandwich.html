Tuna Dill Swiss Sandwich + Snap Peas

Dill-forward tuna salad lightened with Greek yogurt, finished with Swiss for a chefy, satisfying lunch.

Servings

3 servings (Frank, Susan, Elise).

Times

  • Prep time: 12 minutes
  • Cook time: 0 minutes
  • Total time: 12 minutes

Ingredients

Sandwich

  • 6 slices whole wheat bread
  • 2 cans tuna
  • 1 cup nonfat Greek yogurt
  • 1 bunch celery
  • 1 bunch fresh dill
  • 1 each lemon
  • 3 slices reduced-fat Swiss cheese
  • 2 hearts romaine hearts

Side

  • 1 bag snap peas
  • 1 pint cherry tomatoes

Step-by-step Instructions

  1. Make tuna salad: Drain tuna well. In a bowl, mix 1/2 cup Greek yogurt with 1 tbsp lemon juice and 1 tbsp chopped dill. Finely chop celery and fold in with tuna until evenly coated.
  2. Assemble sandwiches: Layer romaine on bread, add tuna salad, then portion Swiss cheese per person (cut slices as needed for 0.75 portions). Close into 3 full sandwiches. Optional: toast in a skillet 2–3 minutes per side to melt cheese.
  3. Prep side: Rinse snap peas and tomatoes. Portion per person for a crunchy, high-volume side. Pack tomatoes separately if you want bread to stay extra dry.

Portion Sizes

Portions (per person):

Frank: 2 slices bread, 3 oz drained tuna, 2 tbsp Greek yogurt, 1/3 cup chopped celery, 1 slice Swiss, 1 cup romaine, 1 cup snap peas, 1/2 cup cherry tomatoes

Susan: 2 slices bread, 2.25 oz drained tuna, 1 1/2 tbsp Greek yogurt, 1/4 cup chopped celery, 3/4 slice Swiss, 3/4 cup romaine, 3/4 cup snap peas, 1/3 cup cherry tomatoes

Elise: 2 slices bread, 2.25 oz drained tuna, 1 1/2 tbsp Greek yogurt, 1/4 cup chopped celery, 3/4 slice Swiss, 3/4 cup romaine, 3/4 cup snap peas, 1/3 cup cherry tomatoes
