Black Pepper Sirloin + Broccoli + Brown Rice

Peppery seared sirloin and crisp broccoli in a light soy-ginger glaze over brown rice.

Servings

3 servings (Frank, Susan, Elise).

Times

  • Prep time: 20 minutes
  • Cook time: 15 minutes
  • Total time: 35 minutes

Ingredients

Main

  • 1.5 lb sirloin steak
  • 1 cup dry brown rice
  • 1 head broccoli
  • 4 cloves garlic
  • 1 each fresh ginger
  • 1 tbsp low-sodium soy sauce
  • 2 tbsp extra-virgin olive oil
  • 1 tbsp cornstarch
  • 2 tsp ground black pepper

Step-by-step Instructions

  1. Cook rice: Cook 11/20 cup dry brown rice with 1 1/10 cups water until tender (about 35–40 minutes). This yields about 1 2/3 cups cooked rice.
  2. Prep steak: Slice steak thin against the grain. Toss with 1 tbsp cornstarch and 1 tsp black pepper.
  3. Prep broccoli: Cut broccoli into florets. Measure about 6 cups florets for this dinner; refrigerate any remainder.
  4. Stir-fry: Heat 2 tsp olive oil in a hot skillet. Sear steak 2–3 minutes, then remove to a plate.
  5. Sauce: Mince garlic and grate ginger. Add 2 tsp olive oil, cook garlic/ginger 30 seconds, then add 1/2 cup water, soy sauce, and remaining black pepper. Simmer 1 minute.
  6. Finish: Add broccoli and stir 4–6 minutes until crisp-tender. Return steak and toss 1 minute until glazed.
  7. Serve: Plate over brown rice.

Portion Sizes

Portions (per person):
Frank: 7 oz cooked sirloin + 2/3 cup cooked brown rice + 2 cups broccoli

Susan: 5.25 oz cooked sirloin + 1/2 cup cooked brown rice + 1 1/2 cups broccoli

Elise: 5.25 oz cooked sirloin + 1/2 cup cooked brown rice + 1 1/2 cups broccoli
