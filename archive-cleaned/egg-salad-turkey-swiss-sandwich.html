Greek Yogurt Egg Salad Turkey Swiss Sandwich + Tomatoes

Chefy egg salad lightened with Greek yogurt, layered with turkey and Swiss, plus a high-volume tomato side.

Servings

3 servings (Frank, Susan, Elise).

Times

  • Prep time: 15 minutes
  • Cook time: 12 minutes
  • Total time: 27 minutes

Ingredients

Sandwich

  • 6 slices whole wheat bread
  • 6 each eggs
  • 1 lb boneless skinless turkey breast
  • 1 cup nonfat Greek yogurt
  • 3 slices reduced-fat Swiss cheese
  • 1 bunch scallions
  • 2 hearts romaine hearts
  • 1 tsp ground black pepper

Side

  • 1 pint cherry tomatoes
  • 1 each cucumber

Step-by-step Instructions

  1. Cook the turkey: Roast at 425°F to 165°F (about 16–20 minutes). Rest 5 minutes, then thin-slice.
  2. Hard-boil eggs: Cover eggs with water, bring to a boil, then cover and rest off-heat 10 minutes. Chill, peel, and chop.
  3. Make egg salad: Stir chopped eggs with 1/3 cup Greek yogurt, sliced scallions, and black pepper until creamy.
  4. Assemble sandwiches: Spread egg salad on bread. Layer turkey, Swiss (cut slices to match 0.75 portions), and romaine. Close into 3 full sandwiches.
  5. Prep side: Portion cherry tomatoes and cucumber slices per person.

Portion Sizes

Portions (per person):
Frank: 2 slices bread, 2.5 oz cooked turkey, 1 slice Swiss, 1/2 cup egg salad, 1 cup romaine, 1/2 cup cherry tomatoes, 1/2 cucumber

Susan: 2 slices bread, 1.9 oz cooked turkey, 3/4 slice Swiss, 3/8 cup egg salad, 3/4 cup romaine, 1/3 cup cherry tomatoes, 1/4 cucumber

Elise: 2 slices bread, 1.9 oz cooked turkey, 3/4 slice Swiss, 3/8 cup egg salad, 3/4 cup romaine, 1/3 cup cherry tomatoes, 1/4 cucumber
