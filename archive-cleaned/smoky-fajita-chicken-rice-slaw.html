Smoky Fajita Chicken + Brown Rice + Lime Slaw

Sheet-pan smoky fajita chicken with brown rice and a bright lime-yogurt slaw.

Servings

3 servings (Frank, Susan, Elise).

Times

  • Prep time: 25 minutes
  • Cook time: 22 minutes
  • Total time: 47 minutes

Ingredients

Main

  • 2 lb boneless skinless chicken breast
  • 1 cup dry brown rice
  • 3 each bell pepper
  • 1 each yellow onion
  • 1 bag broccoli slaw mix
  • 2 each lime
  • 1 cup nonfat Greek yogurt
  • 2 tbsp extra-virgin olive oil
  • 1 tsp chili powder
  • 1 tsp ground cumin
  • 1 tsp smoked paprika
  • 1 tsp garlic powder
  • 1 tsp ground black pepper

Step-by-step Instructions

  1. Cook rice: Cook 7/10 cup dry brown rice with 1 2/5 cups water until tender (about 35–40 minutes). This yields about 2 cups cooked rice.
  2. Sheet-pan fajita chicken: Heat oven to 425°F. Slice 2 peppers and 1 onion; bag the remaining pepper for another meal. Toss sliced veg with 2 tsp olive oil and spices.
  3. Cook chicken: Slice chicken into strips. Toss with 1 tsp olive oil and the same spice mix. Spread chicken and veg on a sheet pan and roast 16–20 minutes, stirring once, until chicken is 165°F.
  4. Lime slaw: Toss slaw mix with juice of 1 lime, 1/2 cup Greek yogurt, black pepper, and 1 tsp olive oil. Refrigerate remaining yogurt.
  5. Serve: Bowl rice, add fajita chicken and veg, top with slaw. Add extra lime wedges if desired.

Portion Sizes

Portions (per person):
Frank: 6 oz cooked chicken + 3/4 cup cooked brown rice + 2 cups fajita veg + 1 cup lime slaw

Susan: 4.5 oz cooked chicken + 1/2 cup cooked brown rice + 1 1/2 cups veg + 3/4 cup slaw

Elise: 4.5 oz cooked chicken + 1/2 cup cooked brown rice + 1 1/2 cups veg + 3/4 cup slaw
