Avocado Turkey Mozzarella Sandwich + Pepper Salad

Avocado-lime Greek yogurt spread with roasted turkey and mozzarella, plus a quick pepper-and-romaine salad side.

Servings

3 servings (Frank, Susan, Elise).

Times

  • Prep time: 15 minutes
  • Cook time: 18 minutes
  • Total time: 33 minutes

Ingredients

Sandwich

  • 6 slices whole wheat bread
  • 1 lb boneless skinless turkey breast
  • 3 slices part-skim mozzarella cheese
  • 1 each avocado
  • 1 cup nonfat Greek yogurt
  • 1 each lime
  • 2 hearts romaine hearts

Side

  • 1 bag mini sweet peppers
  • 1 pint cherry tomatoes

Step-by-step Instructions

  1. Cook the turkey: Roast at 425°F to 165°F (about 16–20 minutes depending on thickness). Rest 5 minutes, then thin-slice.
  2. Make avocado spread: Mash 3 oz avocado with 1/4 cup Greek yogurt and 1 tbsp lime juice until smooth.
  3. Assemble sandwiches: Spread avocado mix on bread (measure per person). Layer turkey and mozzarella (cut slices to match 0.75 portions). Add romaine. Close into 3 full sandwiches.
  4. Prep side salad: Slice peppers and chop romaine. Toss with halved tomatoes. Portion per person. Keep salad separate from bread for transport.

Portion Sizes

Portions (per person):
Frank: 2 slices bread, 3.0 oz cooked turkey, 1 slice mozzarella, 1 1/2 tbsp avocado spread, 1 cup romaine, 1 cup sliced peppers, 1/2 cup cherry tomatoes

Susan: 2 slices bread, 2.25 oz cooked turkey, 3/4 slice mozzarella, 1 tbsp avocado spread, 3/4 cup romaine, 3/4 cup sliced peppers, 1/3 cup cherry tomatoes

Elise: 2 slices bread, 2.25 oz cooked turkey, 3/4 slice mozzarella, 1 tbsp avocado spread, 3/4 cup romaine, 3/4 cup sliced peppers, 1/3 cup cherry tomatoes
