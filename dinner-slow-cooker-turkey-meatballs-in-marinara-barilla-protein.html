Turkey Meatballs in Marinara + Barilla Protein+

Nutrition (approx)

Frank: 650 kcal • P 60g • C 70g • F 14g
Susan/Elise (each): 488 kcal • P 45g • C 52g • F 10g

Ingredients (purchase-clean)

  • 1.75 lb 93% lean ground turkey
  • 1 jar marinara sauce
  • 1 can no-salt-added diced tomatoes
  • 1 box Barilla Protein+ pasta
  • 6 oz grated Parmesan cheese
  • kosher salt
  • black pepper
  • garlic powder
  • onion powder
  • smoked paprika
  • Italian seasoning
  • ground cumin
  • chili powder
  • red pepper flakes
  • extra-virgin olive oil
  • low-sodium chicken broth
  • low-sodium beef broth
  • low-sodium soy sauce
  • hot sauce
  • Dijon mustard
  • balsamic vinegar
  • apple cider vinegar
  • cornstarch

Steps (slow cooker)

  1. Mix turkey with Italian seasoning, garlic powder, onion powder, pepper; modest salt. Form 18 meatballs.
  2. Add marinara (2 cups) + tomatoes (14.5 oz) to cooker.
  3. Add meatballs; LOW 4 hr or HIGH 2 hr to 165°F (don’t over-stir early).
  4. Reduce HIGH lid ajar 15 min if thin.
  5. Cook Barilla Protein+ pasta al dente; drain.
  6. Toss pasta with sauce/meatballs; measure Parmesan across plates.

Notes

Hands-off meatballs with Protein+ pasta cooked fresh (no mush). Portions (per person): Frank: 6 meatballs + 2 cups pasta bowl Susan: 5 meatballs + 1 1/2 cups pasta bowl Elise: 5 meatballs + 1 1/2 cups pasta bowl Tips (recipe-specific): Don’t over-stir early or meatballs break. Cook pasta fresh; never slow-cook pasta.
