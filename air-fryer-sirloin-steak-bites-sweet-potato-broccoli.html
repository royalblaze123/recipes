Air Fryer Sirloin Steak Bites with Sweet Potato and Broccoli

Sirloin steak bites with roasted sweet potato and broccoli—high protein, easy cleanup, and fully dual-basket timed.

Servings

3 servings.

Times

  • Prep time: 20 minutes
  • Cook time: 16 minutes
  • Total time: 36 minutes

Ingredients

Main Dish

  • 1 lb sirloin steak
  • 2 each sweet potatoes
  • 1 head broccoli
  • 1 bottle extra virgin olive oil
  • 1 bag reduced fat shredded cheese
  • 1 container nonfat Greek yogurt
  • 1 jar garlic powder
  • 1 jar paprika
  • 1 jar black pepper
  • 1 jar Italian seasoning
  • 1 each lemon
    • 

    Preparation Steps

    1. Prep (sweet potato): peel and cut into 3/4-inch cubes. Measure out about 2 1/4 cups cubes for this recipe; refrigerate remaining sweet potato. Toss with 1 tbsp olive oil, paprika, and black pepper.
    2. Prep (broccoli): cut florets. Measure out 1/2 head broccoli for this recipe; refrigerate remaining. Toss with 1 tsp olive oil, garlic powder, and black pepper.
    3. Prep (steak): cut sirloin into 1-inch cubes. Toss with 2 tsp olive oil, garlic powder, Italian seasoning, paprika, and black pepper.
    4. Cosori setup: preheat both baskets to 400°F for 3 minutes. Put sweet potato cubes in Basket A.
    5. Basket A: cook sweet potato cubes 400°F for 14–16 minutes with shake reminder ON; shake at each reminder.
    6. Basket B (broccoli): cook broccoli 400°F for 6–7 minutes, shaking once. Remove broccoli to a plate.
    7. Basket B (steak bites): add steak bites and cook 400°F for 6–8 minutes, shaking once, until desired doneness (130–135°F medium-rare, 140°F medium). Rest 3 minutes.
    8. Yogurt drizzle: stir 1/4 cup Greek yogurt with 1 tsp lemon juice and a pinch of garlic powder.
    9. Serve (40/30/30): plate steak bites with sweet potato and broccoli. Sprinkle 1/2 oz reduced-fat cheese total across all plates and add a small yogurt drizzle.

      Notes

      Portions (per person):


      Frank – Steak bites: largest share. Sweet potato: about 1 1/4 cups. Broccoli: about 1 1/2 cups. Yogurt drizzle + light cheese sprinkle.


      Susan – Steak bites: medium share. Sweet potato: about 1 cup. Broccoli: about 1 1/4 cups. Yogurt drizzle + light cheese sprinkle.


      Elise – Steak bites: medium share. Sweet potato: about 1 cup. Broccoli: about 1 1/4 cups. Yogurt drizzle + light cheese sprinkle.


      Tips (recipe-specific):


      Cook broccoli first in Basket B, then steak bites—this prevents the steak from sitting while vegetables finish.


      Rest steak bites 3 minutes so juices redistribute and texture stays tender.


      If you want more char, cook steak bites in a single layer and avoid crowding.
