Open-Face Melt: Tuna Mozzarella Tomato Melt

Prep: 16 min   Cook: 8 min   Total: 24 min

Ingredients

  • 6 slices whole wheat bread
  • 3 cans tuna in water
  • 6 slices part-skim mozzarella
  • 1 pint cherry tomatoes
  • 1 bag spinach
  • Dijon mustard
  • black pepper
  • salt substitute
  • vinegar
  • lemon juice

Rules enforced: quantities before items; fresh meat/poultry/fish use weights; packaged items use fractional containers when partial. Condiments/spices/seasonings have no measurements in Ingredients; all measurements are in Steps. No olives, no squash, no Brussels sprouts, no zucchini, no tofu/tempeh. Lunch validations included in Notes.

Step-by-step Instructions

  1. Tuna: drain 3 cans tuna very well. Mix with Dijon (measured: Frank 2 tsp; Susan 1.5 tsp; Elise 1.5 tsp) + 1 tsp vinegar + 1 tsp lemon juice total; pepper/salt substitute.
  2. Broiler: set to high. Line sheet pan.
  3. Toast bread: broil 1 minute.
  4. Spread tuna mix evenly across 6 slices.
  5. Add spinach across slices (divide 1 bag).
  6. Top with mozzarella slices.
  7. Add halved cherry tomatoes (divide 1 pint). Broil 2–3 minutes; rest 2 minutes.
