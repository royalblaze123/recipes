Chicken Enchilada Bake (Sheet-Pan Layer)

Nutrition (approx)

Frank: 645 kcal • P 58g • C 58g • F 18g • Sat 7g • Na 690mg • Fiber 13g
Susan/Elise (each): 484 kcal • P 44g • C 44g • F 14g • Sat 5g • Na 518mg • Fiber 10g

Ingredients (purchase-clean)

  • 1.75 lb boneless skinless chicken breast
  • 1 can black beans
  • 1 can no-salt-added diced tomatoes
  • 1 package whole-wheat tortillas
  • 1 can enchilada sauce
  • 8 oz reduced-fat shredded Mexican cheese blend
  • 1 each avocado
  • kosher salt
  • black pepper
  • garlic powder
  • onion powder
  • smoked paprika
  • chili powder
  • ground cumin
  • Italian seasoning
  • extra-virgin olive oil

Steps

  1. Oven: heat to 425°F. Lightly oil a 9x13 baking dish.
  2. Chicken: cook diced chicken in a skillet with 2 tsp olive oil 8–10 minutes to 165°F.
  3. Season with 2 tsp chili powder, 2 tsp cumin, 2 tsp garlic powder, 1 tsp smoked paprika, 1/2 tsp kosher salt, and 1/2 tsp black pepper.
  4. Beans: Can size note—use a standard 15 oz can black beans.. Drain/rinse. Use 1 1/2 cups beans; refrigerate remainder.
  5. Tomatoes: Can size note—use a standard 14.5 oz can no-salt-added diced tomatoes.. Drain lightly and add to chicken.
  6. Sauce: Can size note—use a standard 10 oz can enchilada sauce.. Use 1 cup sauce for the bake; refrigerate the remainder.
  7. Layer: spread 1/3 cup sauce in the dish. Add 2 tortillas (torn to fit). Add half the chicken-bean mix. Add 1/3 cup sauce. Repeat tortillas + remaining filling + final 1/3 cup sauce.
  8. Cheese: sprinkle 8 oz reduced-fat Mexican blend evenly over top.
  9. Bake: 14–16 minutes until hot and bubbly. Rest 5 minutes.
  10. Serve: top plates with salsa and diced avocado.
  11. Portion per Notes.

Notes

Enchilada comfort without rolling—layered like lasagna. Portions (per person): Frank: 1/3 of bake + 1/3 avocado + salsa Susan: 1/4 of bake + 1/4 avocado + salsa Elise: 1/4 of bake + 1/4 avocado + salsa Tips (recipe-specific): Tear tortillas so layers sit flat—better texture when reheating. Use salsa at serving time for big flavor without adding extra cheese.
