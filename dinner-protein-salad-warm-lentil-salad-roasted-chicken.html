Warm Lentil Salad + Roasted Chicken

Nutrition (approx)

Frank: 650 kcal • P 60g • C 60g • F 16g • Sat 3g • Na 620mg • Fiber 14g
Susan/Elise (each): 488 kcal • P 45g • C 45g • F 12g • Sat 2g • Na 465mg • Fiber 10g

Ingredients (purchase-clean)

  • 1.75 lb boneless skinless chicken breast
  • 1 cup dry lentils
  • 1 bag baby spinach
  • 1 each onion
  • kosher salt
  • black pepper
  • garlic powder
  • onion powder
  • smoked paprika
  • Italian seasoning
  • ground cumin
  • chili powder
  • red pepper flakes
  • extra-virgin olive oil
  • balsamic vinegar
  • apple cider vinegar
  • Dijon mustard

Steps

  1. Lentils: simmer 1 cup dry lentils in 3 cups water 18–22 minutes until tender; drain.
  2. Chicken: roast chicken with garlic powder, black pepper, and a modest pinch of kosher salt at 425°F for 12–15 minutes to 165°F; rest; slice.
  3. Onion: sauté diced onion with 2 tsp olive oil until browned (6–8 minutes).
  4. Dressing: whisk 2 tbsp balsamic vinegar, 2 tbsp olive oil, Dijon mustard, black pepper, and a small pinch of kosher salt.
  5. Salad: toss warm lentils with onion and dressing; fold in spinach to wilt slightly.
  6. Top with chicken. Serve per Notes.

Notes

Warm lentil salad dinner—cozy, high fiber, and still protein-forward. Portions (per person): Frank: 2 cups lentil salad + 7 oz chicken Susan: 1 1/2 cups lentil salad + 5 oz chicken Elise: 1 1/2 cups lentil salad + 5 oz chicken Tips (recipe-specific): Dress lentils while warm so they absorb flavor. Browned onion makes it taste like comfort food.
