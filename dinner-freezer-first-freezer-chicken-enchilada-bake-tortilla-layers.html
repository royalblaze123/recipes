Freezer Chicken Enchilada Bake (Tortilla Layers)

Nutrition (approx)

Frank: 650 kcal • P 60g • C 60g • F 18g • Sat 6g • Na 700mg • Fiber 12g
Susan/Elise (each): 488 kcal • P 45g • C 45g • F 14g • Sat 4g • Na 525mg • Fiber 9g

Ingredients (purchase-clean)

  • 1.75 lb boneless skinless chicken breast
  • 1 jar salsa
  • 2 cans no-salt-added diced tomatoes
  • 3 each whole-wheat tortillas
  • 6 oz reduced-fat shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1 can black beans
  • low-sodium chicken broth
  • kosher salt
  • black pepper
  • garlic powder
  • onion powder
  • smoked paprika
  • Italian seasoning
  • ground cumin
  • chili powder
  • red pepper flakes
  • extra-virgin olive oil
  • low-sodium chicken broth
  • cornstarch

Steps

  1. Oven: heat to 400°F. Lightly oil a 9x13 dish.
  2. Chicken: sauté diced chicken with chili powder, cumin, garlic powder, black pepper, modest salt 6–8 minutes.
  3. Sauce: add salsa (1 1/2 cups measured), diced tomatoes (2 cans, 14.5 oz each), and 1/2 cup broth. Simmer 8 minutes uncovered to thicken.
  4. Beans: drain/rinse black beans; stir in 1 1/2 cups beans.
  5. Layer: add a thin sauce layer, then tortillas, then chicken mix; repeat; top with mozzarella (measured).
  6. Bake 22–26 minutes; rest 10 minutes.
  7. Freeze: cool fully; portion; freeze. Reheat covered 350°F 25–35 minutes.

Notes

Enchilada-bake comfort with thick sauce so it freezes and reheats without turning watery. Portions (per person): Frank: About 2 1/2 cups (heaping scoop) Susan: About 2 cups (scoop) Elise: About 2 cups (scoop) Tips (recipe-specific): Simmer sauce uncovered first—thickness matters for freezer reheats. Rest before portioning so the layers hold together.
