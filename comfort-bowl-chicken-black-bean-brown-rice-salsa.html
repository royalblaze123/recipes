Rice & Bean Comfort Bowl with Spiced Chicken, Black Beans, and Romaine

Prep: 18 min   Cook: 22 min   Total: 40 min

Ingredients

  • 1.6 lb chicken breast
  • 0.5 cup dry brown rice
  • 0.5 can no-salt-added black beans
  • 1 bag romaine hearts
  • 1 pint cherry tomatoes
  • 1 onion
  • 1 lime
  • olive oil
  • salsa
  • cumin
  • chili powder
  • smoked paprika
  • garlic powder
  • black pepper
  • salt substitute

Hard rules enforced: Dinner validations (calories 600–650 Frank, sat fat ≤8g, sodium ≤700mg, fiber ≥10g, added sugar ≤6g). Quantities appear before items; fresh meat/poultry/fish use weights. Condiments/spices/seasonings have no measurements in Ingredients; all measurements are in Steps. No olives, no squash, no Brussels sprouts, no zucchini, no tofu/tempeh, no yogurt in dinners.

Step-by-step Instructions

  1. Cook rice: cook 1/2 cup dry brown rice with 1 cup water; simmer 35–40 minutes; rest 5 minutes.
  2. Chicken: cut 1.6 lb chicken breast into bite-size pieces. Season with 2 tsp cumin + 1 tsp chili powder + 1/2 tsp smoked paprika + 1/2 tsp garlic powder + 1/2 tsp black pepper + 1/2 tsp salt substitute.
  3. Sear chicken in a skillet with 2 tsp olive oil over medium-high 6–8 minutes until 165°F.
  4. Beans: drain and rinse 1/2 can black beans. Warm with 2 tbsp water in the skillet 1–2 minutes.
  5. Veg base: chop 1 bag romaine hearts and halve 1 pint cherry tomatoes. Slice 1/2 onion thin.
  6. Build bowls: divide romaine and tomatoes among 3 bowls. Add rice, chicken, and beans.
  7. Finish: add salsa in measured amounts (Frank 1 tbsp; Susan 2 tsp; Elise 2 tsp) and squeeze 1 tsp lime juice per bowl.
