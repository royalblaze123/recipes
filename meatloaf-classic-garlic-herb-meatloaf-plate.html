Classic Garlic-Herb Meatloaf Plate

Nutrition (approx)

Frank: 630 kcal • P 55g • C 35g • F 25g
Susan/Elise (each): 472 kcal • P 41g • C 26g • F 19g

Ingredients (purchase-clean)

  • 1.50 lb 93% lean ground beef
  • 1 each yellow onion
  • 2 each large eggs
  • 3/4 cup whole wheat panko breadcrumbs
  • 6 tsp ketchup
  • 2 tsp Dijon mustard
  • 2 tsp Worcestershire sauce
  • 2 tsp garlic powder
  • 2 tsp Italian seasoning
  • 1 tsp black pepper
  • 1 tsp kosher salt
  • 1.50 lb baby potatoes
  • 12 oz fresh green beans
  • 4 tsp extra-virgin olive oil

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 400°F. Line a sheet pan with parchment. Place an instant-read thermometer nearby.
  2. Meatloaf veg prep: finely dice the yellow onion.
  3. Meatloaf mix: in a large bowl, combine 1.50 lb 93% lean ground beef, diced onion, 2 eggs, 3/4 cup panko, 6 tsp ketchup, 2 tsp Dijon, 2 tsp Worcestershire, 2 tsp garlic powder, 2 tsp Italian seasoning, 1 tsp black pepper, and 1 tsp kosher salt. Mix with your hands just until combined (do not overmix).
  4. Form and bake meatloaf: shape into a loaf on one side of the sheet pan. Bake 35 minutes.
  5. Potatoes: halve baby potatoes if large. Toss potatoes with 2 tsp olive oil and spread on the other side of the sheet pan. Bake 25–30 minutes total, flipping once, until browned and tender.
  6. Green beans: toss green beans with 2 tsp olive oil. Add to the pan for the last 10–12 minutes of cooking; roast until tender-crisp.
  7. Doneness + rest: meatloaf is done when the center reads 160°F. Rest 10 minutes before slicing for clean slices.

Notes

Classic meatloaf flavor with a sheet-pan potato + green bean plate.
