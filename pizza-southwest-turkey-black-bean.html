Southwest Turkey Black Bean Pizza

NOTES: Built as a full dinner using ready-made whole-grain or thin crust, lean protein, plenty of vegetables, and controlled cheese to support A1c, cholesterol, and blood pressure goals. Use low-sodium sauce and minimal added salt. PORTION SIZES: Frank: ~40% of the pizza; Susan: ~30% of the pizza; Elise: ~30% of the pizza (Frank = 1.0×, Susan/Elise = 0.75× portions).

Ingredients

  • 1 ready-made 12-inch thin whole-wheat pizza crust
  • 1/2 cup no-salt-added tomato or enchilada-style sauce (low sodium)
  • 6 oz 93% lean ground turkey, cooked and crumbled
  • 1/2 cup no-salt-added black beans, drained and rinsed
  • 1/2 red bell pepper, diced
  • 1/2 yellow bell pepper, diced
  • 1/4 red onion, thinly sliced
  • 1/2 cup shredded reduced-fat Mexican blend cheese
  • 1/4 cup plain 2% Greek yogurt (for drizzle, optional)
  • 1 tsp chili powder
  • 1/2 tsp ground cumin
  • Freshly ground black pepper, to taste

Step-by-Step Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 425°F. Place the crust on a pizza pan or parchment-lined baking sheet.
  2. Spread tomato or enchilada-style sauce evenly over the crust.
  3. Scatter cooked lean turkey crumbles and black beans evenly over the sauce.
  4. Top with diced bell peppers and sliced red onion.
  5. Sprinkle shredded Mexican blend cheese over the toppings.
  6. Season with chili powder, ground cumin, and a few grinds of black pepper.
  7. Bake 12–15 minutes, until the crust is crisp and the cheese is melted and lightly browned.
  8. If desired, drizzle with Greek yogurt just before serving.
  9. Cool 3–5 minutes, then slice and portion for Frank (~40%) and Susan/Elise (~30% each).

Nutrition per Person

  • Frank: ~610 kcal, ~50 g protein, ~55 g carbs, ~18 g fat.
  • Susan: ~455 kcal, ~38 g protein, ~41 g carbs, ~13 g fat.
  • Elise: ~455 kcal, ~38 g protein, ~41 g carbs, ~13 g fat.
