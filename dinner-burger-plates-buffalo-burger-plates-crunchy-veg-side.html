Buffalo Burger Plates + Crunchy Veg Side

Nutrition (approx)

Frank: 630 kcal • P 58g • C 45g • F 18g • Sat 5g • Na 690mg • Fiber 10g
Susan/Elise (each): 472 kcal • P 44g • C 34g • F 14g • Sat 4g • Na 518mg • Fiber 8g

Ingredients (purchase-clean)

  • 1.75 lb 93% lean ground turkey
  • 4 each whole-wheat burger buns
  • 1 bottle buffalo sauce
  • 1 bunch celery
  • 1 bag baby carrots
  • 1 jar dill pickle slices
  • kosher salt
  • black pepper
  • garlic powder
  • onion powder
  • smoked paprika
  • dried oregano
  • extra-virgin olive oil
  • mustard
  • ketchup
  • pickle relish
  • hot sauce
  • Worcestershire sauce

Steps

  1. Patties: season turkey with 1 tsp garlic powder, 1 tsp onion powder, 1/2 tsp kosher salt, 1/2 tsp black pepper. Form 6 patties.
  2. Skillet: cook 5–6 minutes per side to 165°F.
  3. Sauce: brush 2 tbsp buffalo sauce across the patties (add more at the table if desired).
  4. Veg side: slice celery into sticks. Serve with baby carrots and pickles.
  5. Assemble: buns + mustard/hot sauce if desired. Portion per Notes.

Notes

Buffalo comfort with a crunchy, high-volume side (like wings night). Portions (per person): Frank: 2 patties + big handful celery + carrots side + toppings Susan: 1 patty + big handful celery + carrots side + toppings Elise: 1 patty + big handful celery + carrots side + toppings Tips (recipe-specific): Keep buffalo sauce measured on the patties; add more per bite if needed. Crunchy veg gives the ‘bar food’ satisfaction without chips.
