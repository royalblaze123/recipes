Chicken + Lentil Stew (Structured)

Nutrition (approx)

Frank: 640 kcal • P 58g • C 55g • F 14g
Susan/Elise (each): 480 kcal • P 44g • C 41g • F 10g

Ingredients (purchase-clean)

  • 1.75 lb boneless skinless chicken breast
  • 1 cup dry lentils
  • 1 lb carrots
  • 2 stalks celery
  • 1 each onion
  • low-sodium chicken broth
  • kosher salt
  • black pepper
  • garlic powder
  • onion powder
  • smoked paprika
  • Italian seasoning
  • ground cumin
  • chili powder
  • red pepper flakes
  • extra-virgin olive oil
  • low-sodium chicken broth
  • low-sodium beef broth
  • low-sodium soy sauce
  • hot sauce
  • Dijon mustard
  • balsamic vinegar
  • apple cider vinegar
  • cornstarch

Steps (slow cooker)

  1. Add onion + carrots + celery + lentils (1 cup dry) + broth (6 cups).
  2. Add chicken; season garlic powder, smoked paprika, pepper; modest salt.
  3. LOW 5–6 hr or HIGH 3–4 hr until lentils are tender but hold shape.
  4. Shred chicken lightly into larger pieces.
  5. Reduce HIGH lid ajar 10–15 min if desired.
  6. Serve per Notes.

Notes

Stew that keeps structure: lentils hold up and chicken stays in bigger pieces. Portions (per person): Frank: About 2 1/2 cups (heaping bowl) Susan: About 2 cups (bowl) Elise: About 2 cups (bowl) Tips (recipe-specific): Shred lightly so it stays stew-like. If lentils finish early, switch to Warm to prevent mush.
