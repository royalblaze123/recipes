Ginger-Lime Highball Mocktail

Yield: 1 drink • Prep: 5 min • Total: 5 min

Ingredients (AnyList-ready)

  • 2 oz lime juice
  • 1 oz simple syrup
  • 6 oz ginger beer
  • 2 dashes non-alcoholic aromatic bitters
  • 1 oz soda water
  • 1 sprig mint
  • 1 lime wheel
  • 1 cup ice

Steps

  1. Fill a tall glass with 1 cup ice.
  2. Pour in 2 oz lime juice and 1 oz simple syrup.
  3. Add 2 dashes non-alcoholic aromatic bitters.
  4. Top with 6 oz ginger beer, then add 1 oz soda water.
  5. Stir once, gently, to combine without knocking out carbonation.
  6. Garnish with 1 sprig mint and 1 lime wheel. Serve immediately.

Notes

Bitters note: This version uses non-alcoholic aromatic bitters to keep it zero-proof.
Dial-in: If it’s too sharp, reduce lime juice to 1.5 oz. If it’s too sweet, reduce simple syrup to 0.75 oz or increase soda water to 2 oz.
Glassware: Best in a tall highball with plenty of ice.
