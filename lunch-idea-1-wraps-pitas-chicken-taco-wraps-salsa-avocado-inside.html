Chicken Taco Wraps (Salsa + Avocado Inside)

Nutrition (approx)

Frank: 530 kcal • P 50g • C 50g • F 16g
Susan/Elise (each): 398 kcal • P 38g • C 38g • F 12g

Ingredients (purchase-clean)

  • 1.25 lb boneless skinless chicken breast
  • 1 package large whole-wheat tortillas
  • 1 each avocado
  • 1/2 jar salsa
  • 1 bag shredded lettuce
  • 1 each cucumber
  • 1 bag mini sweet peppers
  • 4 tsp extra-virgin olive oil
  • 2 tsp chili powder
  • 2 tsp cumin
  • 2 tsp garlic powder
  • 1 tsp smoked paprika
  • 1 tsp black pepper
  • 1 tsp kosher salt

Steps

  1. Chicken: cut into small pieces. Cook in a skillet with 2 tsp olive oil 8–10 minutes until 165°F. Season with spices.
  2. Wrap build: warm tortillas 15 seconds so they roll easily. Add shredded lettuce, then add the seasoned chicken.
  3. Avocado: halve, pit, and dice. Use about 1/2 avocado total.
  4. Salsa: spoon salsa inside each wrap/pita (not just on the side) so every bite has flavor.
  5. Roll: fold in sides and roll tightly. Slice in half if desired.
  6. Veggie sticks: slice cucumber and mini sweet peppers into sticks for a crunchy side.
  7. Portion per Notes.

Notes

High-protein taco wraps with salsa + avocado inside (not on the side). Portions (per person): Frank: 1 large wrap (with salsa + avocado inside) + veggie sticks Susan: 3/4 wrap (with salsa + avocado inside) + veggie sticks Elise: 3/4 wrap (with salsa + avocado inside) + veggie sticks
