Instant Pot Beef, Carrot & Pea Stew

NOTES: Pressure-cooked beef stew with carrots, peas, and potatoes in a light gravy. This is a full-plate beef dinner built around lean beef, smart carbs, and plenty of non-starchy vegetables. Uses olive oil or avocado oil only, keeps sodium moderate, and avoids added sugars to support A1c, cholesterol, blood pressure, and PPPD-friendly eating. PORTION SIZES: Frank: largest portion (~1/3 of the total cooked meal); Susan: medium portion (~1/4 of the total cooked meal); Elise: medium portion (~1/4 of the total cooked meal) (Frank = 1.0×, Susan/Elise ≈0.75× portions).
