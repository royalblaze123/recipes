Turkey, Mushroom & Broccoli Lasagna

A veg-forward lasagna with lean turkey, mushrooms, and broccoli for extra fiber and volume, using whole-wheat noodles and light cheese.

PORTION SIZES (3 servings total): Frank = 1.0× (largest square), Susan = 0.75×, Elise = 0.75×. Cut the baked lasagna into 4 equal squares, serve 2 squares to Frank and 1 square each to Susan and Elise.

Ingredients

  • 8 whole-wheat lasagna noodles (dry)
  • 1 lb lean ground turkey (93% lean or better)
  • 1 small yellow onion, finely diced
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 cups sliced mushrooms
  • 2 cups small broccoli florets
  • 2 cups no-salt-added crushed tomatoes
  • 1/2 cup low-sodium vegetable broth
  • 1 tbsp extra-virgin olive oil
  • 1 tsp dried oregano
  • 1 tsp dried basil
  • 1/2 tsp dried thyme
  • 1/2 tsp black pepper
  • 1/4 tsp kosher salt (optional, to taste)
  • 1 cup part-skim ricotta cheese
  • 1 cup part-skim shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 2 tbsp grated reduced-fat Parmesan cheese

Step-by-step Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C). Lightly coat a 9×9-inch baking dish with nonstick spray.
  2. Bring a large pot of water to a boil. Cook the whole-wheat lasagna noodles until just shy of al dente, about 7–8 minutes. Drain and lay flat on parchment to prevent sticking.
  3. Heat the olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat. Add the diced onion and sliced mushrooms and sauté 5–6 minutes until the mushrooms release their moisture and begin to brown.
  4. Add the minced garlic and cook 30–60 seconds until fragrant.
  5. Add the lean ground turkey to the skillet and cook 6–8 minutes, breaking it into small crumbles, until browned and cooked through.
  6. Stir in the dried oregano, dried basil, dried thyme, black pepper, and optional salt. Toast the herbs with the turkey and mushrooms for 1–2 minutes.
  7. Pour in the crushed tomatoes and vegetable broth. Stir well, reduce heat to low, and simmer 8–10 minutes.
  8. Meanwhile, lightly steam the broccoli florets for 2–3 minutes until bright green and just tender. Drain well.
  9. Fold the steamed broccoli into the turkey and mushroom tomato sauce.
  10. Spread a thin layer of the sauce in the bottom of the prepared baking dish.
  11. Layer lasagna noodles over the sauce, followed by dollops of ricotta, then more sauce.
  12. Repeat layers (noodles, ricotta, sauce) until all components are used, finishing with sauce on top.
  13. Sprinkle the shredded mozzarella and grated Parmesan evenly over the top.
  14. Cover with foil and bake for 25 minutes. Remove the foil and bake an additional 10–15 minutes until bubbling and lightly golden.
  15. Rest the lasagna for 10–15 minutes before cutting into 4 equal squares. Serve 2 squares to Frank and 1 square each to Susan and Elise.

Nutrition (per person)

Frank (largest portion): approx 510 kcal, 45 g protein, 40 g carbs, 18 g fat.
Susan (medium portion): approx 385 kcal, 34 g protein, 30 g carbs, 14 g fat.
Elise (medium portion): approx 385 kcal, 34 g protein, 30 g carbs, 14 g fat.
