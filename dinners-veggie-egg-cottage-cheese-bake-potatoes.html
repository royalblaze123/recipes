Veggie Egg & Cottage Cheese Bake with Potatoes (RBM Dinner)

High-protein vegetarian dinner bake with eggs, low-fat cottage cheese, potatoes, and colorful vegetables.

Servings

3 servings.

Times

  • Prep time: 20m
  • Cook time: 35m
  • Total time: 55m

Ingredients

  • 6 large eggs
  • 6 large egg whites
  • 2 cups low-fat cottage cheese
  • 12 oz baby potatoes
  • 8 oz fresh bell peppers
  • 4 oz yellow onion
  • 3 oz fresh baby spinach
  • 1 tbsp extra-virgin olive oil
  • 1 tsp garlic powder
  • 1 tsp dried Italian seasoning
  • 1/4 tsp kosher or sea salt
  • 1/2 tsp ground black pepper
  • Nonstick cooking spray

Step-by-Step Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C). Lightly coat a 9x13-inch baking dish with nonstick cooking spray.
  2. Heat a large nonstick skillet over medium heat and add the olive oil.
  3. Add the diced onion and bell peppers to the skillet. Sauté for 4–5 minutes until they begin to soften.
  4. Add the chopped baby spinach and cook for 1–2 more minutes, stirring, until just wilted. Turn off the heat and let the vegetables cool slightly.
  5. In a large mixing bowl, whisk together the whole eggs and egg whites until well combined. Stir in the low-fat cottage cheese, garlic powder, dried Italian seasoning, salt, and pepper.
  6. Fold the cooked vegetables into the egg and cottage cheese mixture until evenly distributed.
  7. Spread the thinly sliced baby potatoes in an even layer over the bottom of the prepared baking dish.
  8. Pour the egg, cottage cheese, and vegetable mixture over the sliced potatoes, gently spreading it into an even layer.
  9. Bake for 30–35 minutes, until the center is set and the top is lightly golden. A knife inserted near the center should come out mostly clean.
  10. Remove from the oven and let the bake rest for 5–10 minutes before slicing. Cut into three portions sized as listed below and serve.

Portion Sizes (Cooked Approx.)

  • Frank (F · 1.0×): slice of bake equal to ~3 whole eggs + 3 egg whites and ~3/4 cup cottage cheese, plus ~6 oz potatoes
  • Susan (S · 0.75×): slice of bake equal to ~2 whole eggs + 2 egg whites and ~1/2 cup cottage cheese, plus ~4.5 oz potatoes
  • Elise (E · 0.5×): slice of bake equal to ~1 1/2 whole eggs + 1 1/2 egg whites and ~1/3 cup cottage cheese, plus ~3 oz potatoes

  • Susan: 460 kcal, 43 g protein, 26 g carbs, 14 g fat, 4 g fiber, 405 mg sodium
