Turkey Taco Sweet Potato Skillet Bowls

Nutrition (approx)

Frank: 640 kcal • P 58g • C 58g • F 18g • Sat 6g • Na 680mg • Fiber 14g
Susan/Elise (each): 480 kcal • P 44g • C 44g • F 14g • Sat 4g • Na 510mg • Fiber 10g

Ingredients (purchase-clean)

  • 1.50 lb 93% lean ground turkey
  • 1.50 lb sweet potatoes
  • 1 can black beans
  • 1 each avocado
  • 1/2 jar salsa
  • kosher salt
  • black pepper
  • chili powder
  • ground cumin
  • garlic powder
  • smoked paprika
  • extra-virgin olive oil

Steps

  1. Sweet potatoes: small dice 1.50 lb. Heat 2 tsp olive oil in a large skillet over medium-high. Add sweet potato; cook 14–18 minutes, stirring, until browned and tender. (Splash 2 tbsp water and cover 2 minutes if needed.)
  2. Turkey: push sweet potato to the side. Add 1.50 lb lean ground turkey; cook 7–9 minutes until browned.
  3. Season turkey with 2 tsp chili powder, 2 tsp cumin, 2 tsp garlic powder, 1 tsp smoked paprika, 1/2 tsp kosher salt, and 1/2 tsp black pepper.
  4. Beans: use one 15 oz can black beans. Drain/rinse. Stir 1 1/2 cups beans into the skillet; cook 2 minutes. (Refrigerate remaining beans.)
  5. Bowls: divide skillet mixture into 3 bowls. Top with salsa and diced avocado. Portion per Notes.

Notes

Comforty taco-skillet bowls with sweet potato instead of rice. Portions (per person): Frank: 1/3 of skillet mix + 1/3 avocado + salsa Susan: 1/4 of skillet mix + 1/4 avocado + salsa Elise: 1/4 of skillet mix + 1/4 avocado + salsa Tips (recipe-specific): Dice sweet potatoes small so they brown fast (more flavor, less mush). If it tastes flat, add an extra spoon of salsa instead of extra salt.
