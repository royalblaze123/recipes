Air Fryer Garlic Parmesan Chicken with Potatoes and Asparagus

Garlic-parmesan chicken cutlets with crispy potatoes and asparagus, timed with Sync Finish for a clean weeknight plate.

Servings

3 servings.

Times

  • Prep time: 22 minutes
  • Cook time: 20 minutes
  • Total time: 42 minutes

Ingredients

Main Dish

  • 1 lb boneless skinless chicken breast
  • 1 bag baby potatoes
  • 1 bunch asparagus
  • 1 bottle extra virgin olive oil
  • 1 jar parmesan cheese
  • 1 container nonfat Greek yogurt
  • 6 cloves garlic
  • 1 jar Italian seasoning
  • 1 jar black pepper
  • 1 each lemon
    • 

    Preparation Steps

    1. Prep chicken: pat chicken dry and slice into 3 even cutlets. Mix 2 tsp olive oil with minced garlic, Italian seasoning, black pepper, and 1 tbsp parmesan. Coat chicken and rest 10 minutes.
    2. Prep potatoes: halve baby potatoes. Measure out about 3 cups potatoes for this recipe; refrigerate remaining potatoes. Toss with 1 tbsp olive oil, Italian seasoning, and black pepper.
    3. Cosori setup: preheat both baskets to 390°F for 3 minutes. Put potatoes in Basket A. Put chicken cutlets in Basket B.
    4. Sync Finish: set Basket A to 390°F for 20 minutes (shake reminder ON). Set Basket B to 390°F for 10 minutes. Press Sync Finish.
    5. Shake reminder (Basket A): shake/flip potatoes when prompted.
    6. Flip chicken: at the 5-minute mark, flip cutlets. Chicken is done at 165°F internal.
    7. Asparagus: trim asparagus. Toss with 1 tsp olive oil and black pepper. When chicken finishes, cook asparagus in Basket B at 390°F for 6–7 minutes, shaking once.
    8. Yogurt lemon drizzle: stir 1/4 cup Greek yogurt with 1 tsp lemon juice and 1 tbsp parmesan (thin with 1 tsp water if needed).
    9. Serve (40/30/30): plate chicken with potatoes and asparagus and add a light drizzle.

      Notes

      Portions (per person):


      Frank – Chicken: largest cutlet. Potatoes: about 1 1/4 cups. Asparagus: about 1 1/2 cups. Yogurt-parmesan drizzle.


      Susan – Chicken: medium cutlet. Potatoes: about 1 cup. Asparagus: about 1 1/4 cups. Yogurt-parmesan drizzle.


      Elise – Chicken: medium cutlet. Potatoes: about 1 cup. Asparagus: about 1 1/4 cups. Yogurt-parmesan drizzle.


      Tips (recipe-specific):


      Slice chicken into even cutlets so it cooks fast without drying out.


      Cook asparagus after chicken so it stays crisp-tender (not soft).


      If potatoes need extra crisp, add 2 minutes at 400°F after shaking.
