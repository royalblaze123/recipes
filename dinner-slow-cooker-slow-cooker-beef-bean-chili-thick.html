Slow Cooker Beef + Bean Chili (Thick)

Nutrition (approx)

Frank: 650 kcal • P 55g • C 55g • F 20g
Susan/Elise (each): 488 kcal • P 41g • C 41g • F 15g

Ingredients (purchase-clean)

  • 1.50 lb 90% lean ground beef
  • 1 can kidney beans
  • 1 can black beans
  • 2 cans no-salt-added diced tomatoes
  • 1 each onion
  • low-sodium beef broth
  • kosher salt
  • black pepper
  • garlic powder
  • onion powder
  • smoked paprika
  • Italian seasoning
  • ground cumin
  • chili powder
  • red pepper flakes
  • extra-virgin olive oil
  • low-sodium chicken broth
  • low-sodium beef broth
  • low-sodium soy sauce
  • hot sauce
  • Dijon mustard
  • balsamic vinegar
  • apple cider vinegar
  • cornstarch

Steps (slow cooker)

  1. Brown beef + onion with chili powder, cumin, garlic powder, smoked paprika, pepper; modest salt (8–10 min).
  2. Add to cooker with tomatoes (2x 14.5 oz) + 1 cup low-sodium broth.
  3. Add beans (drain/rinse; add 1 1/2 cups each).
  4. LOW 4–5 hr or HIGH 2–3 hr.
  5. Thicken: HIGH lid ajar 20 min OR cornstarch slurry 10 min.
  6. Serve per Notes.

Notes

Real chili texture (not stew) with controlled sodium and big flavor. Portions (per person): Frank: About 2 1/2 cups (heaping bowl) Susan: About 2 cups (bowl) Elise: About 2 cups (bowl) Tips (recipe-specific): Brown beef first for flavor. Lid ajar at the end prevents watery leftovers.
