Protein Bowl: Teriyaki-Style Chicken with Edamame and Microgreens over Brown Rice

Prep: 18 min   Cook: 18 min   Total: 36 min

Ingredients

  • 1.25 lb chicken breast
  • 0.5 cup dry brown rice
  • 0.5 bag frozen shelled edamame
  • 1 clamshell microgreens
  • 1 cucumber
  • olive oil
  • low-sodium teriyaki sauce
  • rice vinegar
  • sesame oil
  • garlic
  • ginger
  • black pepper
  • salt substitute
  • cornstarch

Rules enforced: Clean AnyList Ingredients (quantities before items; fresh meat/poultry/fish use weights; packaged items use fractional containers when partial). Condiments/spices/seasonings have no measurements in Ingredients; all measurements are in Steps. No olives, no squash, no Brussels sprouts, no zucchini, no tofu/tempeh. Lunch validations: Frank calories 500–550, sat fat ≤6g, sodium ≤650mg, fiber ≥8g, added sugar ≤6g.

Step-by-step Instructions

  1. Cook rice: cook 1/2 cup dry brown rice with 1 cup water; simmer 35–40 minutes; rest 5 minutes.
  2. Edamame: microwave 1/2 bag edamame 3–4 minutes; measure 1 cup cooked; refreeze remainder.
  3. Chicken: slice 1.25 lb chicken breast thin. Season with 1/4 tsp black pepper + 1/4 tsp salt substitute.
  4. Sauté chicken with 2 tsp olive oil 6–8 minutes until 165°F.
  5. Sauce: whisk 3 tbsp low-sodium teriyaki + 1 tbsp rice vinegar + 1 tsp sesame oil + 1 tsp cornstarch + 2 tbsp water + 1 tsp minced garlic + 1 tsp grated ginger.
  6. Pour sauce over chicken; toss 1–2 minutes until thickened. Stir in edamame.
  7. Build bowls: rice + chicken/edamame + sliced 1 cucumber + 1 clamshell microgreens (divide evenly).
