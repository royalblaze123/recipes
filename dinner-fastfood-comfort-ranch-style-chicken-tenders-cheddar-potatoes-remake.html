Ranch-Style Chicken Tenders + Cheddar Potatoes (Remake)

Nutrition (approx)

Frank: 650 kcal • P 60g • C 55g • F 18g • Sat 7g • Na 700mg • Fiber 11g
Susan/Elise (each): 488 kcal • P 45g • C 41g • F 14g • Sat 5g • Na 525mg • Fiber 8g

Ingredients (purchase-clean)

  • 1.75 lb chicken tenderloins
  • 1 carton egg whites
  • 1 package whole-wheat panko breadcrumbs
  • 6 oz reduced-fat shredded cheddar cheese
  • 1.50 lb baby potatoes
  • 1 bag frozen broccoli florets
  • kosher salt
  • black pepper
  • garlic powder
  • onion powder
  • smoked paprika
  • chili powder
  • ground cumin
  • Italian seasoning
  • crushed red pepper
  • cornstarch
  • low-sodium soy sauce
  • rice vinegar
  • hot sauce
  • Dijon mustard
  • extra-virgin olive oil
  • ranch seasoning

Steps

  1. Oven: heat to 425°F. Line two sheet pans and lightly oil.
  2. Potatoes: halve baby potatoes. Toss with 2 tsp olive oil, 1 tsp ranch seasoning, 1/2 tsp black pepper. Roast 22–26 minutes, stirring once.
  3. At the end, sprinkle 6 oz reduced-fat cheddar over potatoes and return to oven 2 minutes to melt.
  4. Broccoli: roast frozen broccoli with 2 tsp olive oil, black pepper, and a pinch of kosher salt for 16–20 minutes, stirring once.
  5. Breading: Bowl 1: 1/2 cup egg whites. Bowl 2: 1 1/2 cups panko + 2 tsp ranch seasoning + 1 tsp garlic powder + 1/2 tsp black pepper.
  6. Coat tenders and bake 12–14 minutes (flip at 7) to 165°F, or air fry 400°F for 10–12 minutes with shake reminder ON.
  7. Serve per Notes.

Notes

Ranch comfort plate (no bacon, no creamy sauce) but still hits the craving. Portions (per person): Frank: 6 tenders + 3/4 cup cheddar potatoes + 1 cup broccoli side + dip Susan: 4 tenders + 1/2 cup cheddar potatoes + 3/4 cup broccoli side + dip Elise: 4 tenders + 1/2 cup cheddar potatoes + 3/4 cup broccoli side + dip Tips (recipe-specific): Use ranch seasoning in the crumb instead of a creamy ranch dip. Melt cheddar only at the end so you don’t need much.
