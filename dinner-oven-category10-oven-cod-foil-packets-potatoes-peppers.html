Oven Cod Foil Packets + Potatoes + Peppers

Nutrition (approx)

Frank: 640 kcal • P 55g • C 60g • F 14g
Susan/Elise (each): 480 kcal • P 41g • C 45g • F 10g

Ingredients (purchase-clean)

  • 1.75 lb cod fillets
  • 1.50 lb baby potatoes
  • 2 each bell pepper
  • 1 each onion
  • 1 each lemon
  • low-sodium chicken broth
  • kosher salt
  • black pepper
  • garlic powder
  • onion powder
  • smoked paprika
  • Italian seasoning
  • ground cumin
  • chili powder
  • red pepper flakes
  • extra-virgin olive oil
  • low-sodium chicken broth
  • low-sodium beef broth
  • low-sodium soy sauce
  • hot sauce
  • Dijon mustard
  • balsamic vinegar
  • apple cider vinegar
  • cornstarch

Steps (oven)

  1. Oven: heat to 425°F.
  2. Potatoes: halve baby potatoes; toss with olive oil, garlic powder, black pepper, modest salt. Roast 425°F 22–26 minutes.
  3. Veg: slice peppers/onion; toss with olive oil, smoked paprika, black pepper, modest salt.
  4. Packets: on foil, add veg + cod. Add 2 tbsp broth per packet. Season cod with garlic powder and black pepper; top with lemon slices.
  5. Bake packets: 425°F 10–13 minutes until cod flakes and hits 140°F.
  6. Serve: cod + veg over potatoes per Notes.

Notes

Foil packets keep cod moist; roasted potatoes bring the plate together. Portions (per person): Frank: 7 oz cod + 1 cup potatoes + 1 1/2 cups peppers/onion Susan: 5 oz protein + 3/4 cup starch + 1 cup veg Elise: 5 oz protein + 3/4 cup starch + 1 cup veg Tips (recipe-specific): Add a splash of broth to each packet for steam-moisture. Pull cod at 140°F and let carryover finish.
