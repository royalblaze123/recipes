Ham Cheddar Pickle Mustard Sandwich

Servings

3 sandwiches (Frank 1.0×, Susan 0.75×, Elise 0.75×).

Times

  • Prep time: 12 minutes
  • Cook time: 0 minutes
  • Total time: 12 minutes

Ingredients

  • 6 slices whole wheat bread
  • 28 slices deli ham
  • 3 slices reduced-fat cheddar cheese
  • 6 tsp light mayonnaise
  • 6 tsp yellow mustard
  • 1/6 jar dill pickle chips
  • 1/3 package romaine hearts

Recipe

  1. Rinse and dry 1/3 package romaine hearts; pull 6 large leaves (2 per sandwich). Store the remaining romaine hearts package sealed in the fridge.
  2. From 1/6 jar dill pickle chips, measure 18 pickle chips (6 per sandwich). Return remaining pickle chips to the jar and refrigerate.
  3. Frank sandwich: spread 3 tsp light mayonnaise + 2 tsp yellow mustard across 2 bread slices; add 12 slices deli ham + 1 slice cheddar + 2 romaine leaves + 6 pickle chips; close and wrap.
  4. Susan sandwich: spread 1 1/2 tsp light mayonnaise + 2 tsp yellow mustard across 2 bread slices; add 8 slices deli ham + 1 slice cheddar + 2 romaine leaves + 6 pickle chips; close and wrap.
  5. Elise sandwich: spread 1 1/2 tsp light mayonnaise + 2 tsp yellow mustard across 2 bread slices; add 8 slices deli ham + 1 slice cheddar + 2 romaine leaves + 6 pickle chips; close and wrap.

Notes

Portions (per person):

Frank – 1 sandwich (12 ham slices, 1 cheddar slice, 3 tsp mayo, 2 tsp yellow mustard, 6 pickle chips).

Susan – 1 sandwich (8 ham slices, 1 cheddar slice, 1 1/2 tsp mayo, 2 tsp yellow mustard, 6 pickle chips).

Elise – 1 sandwich (8 ham slices, 1 cheddar slice, 1 1/2 tsp mayo, 2 tsp yellow mustard, 6 pickle chips).

Tips (recipe-specific):

Pickles go in the middle (between ham layers) to keep the bread edges from getting soggy.

If sodium is running high that day, reduce pickles to 3 chips per sandwich and keep the rest for another lunch.

  • Susan: 414 kcal; Protein 40 g; Carbs 33 g; Fat 14 g; Fiber 5 g; Sodium 1345 mg
  • Elise: 414 kcal; Protein 40 g; Carbs 33 g; Fat 14 g; Fiber 5 g; Sodium 1345 mg
