Chicken Sweet Potato Power Bowl

Chicken, roasted sweet potatoes, and steamed spinach.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb cooked chicken breast, diced
  • 2 medium sweet potatoes, cubed
  • 1 cup steamed spinach
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1/2 tsp garlic powder
  • 1/2 tsp smoked paprika
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1/4 tsp black pepper

Instructions

  1. Roast sweet potatoes at 400°F for 20–25 min.
  2. Steam spinach.
  3. Dice cooked chicken.
  4. Assemble bowls and reheat 60–90 seconds.

Serves: 3 servings
