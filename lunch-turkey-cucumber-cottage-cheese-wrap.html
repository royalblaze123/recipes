Turkey Cucumber Cottage Cheese Wrap Box

NOTES: Designed as a portable grab-and-go lunch using lean protein, high-fiber vegetables, and controlled smart carbs. Use low-sodium ingredients and avoid added sugars where possible. PORTION SIZES: Frank: largest portion (~1/3 of the total volume); Susan: medium portion (~1/4 of the total volume); Elise: medium portion (~1/4 of the total volume) (Frank = 1.0×, Susan/Elise ≈0.75× portions).

Ingredients

  • 3 large whole-wheat tortillas (8–9 inch)
  • 9 oz sliced low-sodium turkey breast
  • 1 1/4 cups low-fat cottage cheese (1–2%)
  • 1 small cucumber, cut into thin sticks (seeds removed)
  • 1 red bell pepper, cut into thin strips
  • 1 cup shredded romaine or spring mix
  • Freshly ground black pepper, to taste

Step-by-Step Instructions

  1. Lay out tortillas on a clean surface.
  2. Spread about 1/3–1/2 cup cottage cheese down the center of each tortilla.
  3. Layer sliced turkey, cucumber sticks, bell pepper strips, and shredded greens on top of the cottage cheese.
  4. Add a few grinds of black pepper over each wrap.
  5. Roll tortillas tightly into wraps, tucking in the sides, and slice each in half.
  6. Place wrap halves into three containers, giving Frank the largest share, for grab-and-go lunches.
  7. Refrigerate up to 2–3 days. Eat chilled.

Nutrition per Person

  • Frank: ~540 kcal, ~48 g protein, ~50 g carbs, ~14 g fat.
  • Susan: ~400 kcal, ~35 g protein, ~37 g carbs, ~10 g fat.
  • Elise: ~400 kcal, ~35 g protein, ~37 g carbs, ~10 g fat.
