Air Fryer Garlic-Parmesan Salmon with Roasted Carrots and Balsamic-Lemon Salad

Prep: 12 min   Cook: 22 min   Total: 34 min

Ingredients

  • 1 package salmon fillets (~1–1.25 lb total)
  • 1 bag carrots
  • 1 bag spring mix
  • 1 lemon
  • olive oil (tsp) — pantry
  • grated Parmesan (tsp) — pantry
  • garlic powder (tsp) — pantry
  • black pepper (tsp) — pantry
  • salt substitute (tsp) — pantry
  • balsamic vinegar (tsp) — pantry
  • light mayonnaise (tsp) — pantry

Ingredient list is purchase-clean (AnyList aggregation). All measuring/used amounts and remainder handling are in Steps.

Step-by-step Instructions

  1. Heat oven to 425°F and line a sheet pan with parchment. Peel carrots if needed; cut into 1/2-inch sticks.
  2. Measure olive oil: use 12 tsp total for this recipe. Toss carrots with 8 tsp olive oil + 2 tsp salt substitute + 1 tsp black pepper. Roast 18–22 minutes, tossing once, until caramelized.
  3. Season salmon: pat dry. In a bowl mix 4 tsp olive oil + 6 tsp grated Parmesan + 2 tsp garlic powder + 1 tsp black pepper. Spread over salmon in an even layer.
  4. Air fry salmon (Cosori): preheat 390°F for 3 minutes. Cook salmon skin-side down 8–11 minutes until flaky. Parmesan topping should brown slightly.
  5. Make simple balsamic-lemon dressing (no yogurt): whisk 6 tsp balsamic vinegar + 4 tsp lemon juice + 6 tsp light mayonnaise + 1 tsp black pepper. Toss with spring mix.
  6. Portion: Frank gets 6 oz salmon + ~1 3/4 cups roasted carrots + ~2 cups salad. Susan and Elise each get ~4.5 oz salmon + ~1 1/2 cups carrots + ~1 1/2 cups salad.
