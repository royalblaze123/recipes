Salmon Yogurt-Dill Sandwich + Snap Peas (RBM Lunch)

Whole-grain salmon salad sandwich made with Greek yogurt and dill, plus fresh snap peas on the side.

Servings

3 servings.

Ingredients

Sandwich

  • 10.25 oz canned salmon in water
  • 6 slices whole-grain sandwich bread
  • 6 tbsp nonfat plain Greek yogurt
  • 3 tsp light mayonnaise
  • 3 tbsp fresh celery
  • 3 tsp red onion
  • 1 tbsp fresh dill
  • 1/8 tsp kosher or sea salt
  • 1/4 tsp ground black pepper
  • 1 small fresh tomato
  • 6 leaves fresh romaine or leaf lettuce

Side

  • 7.5 oz fresh sugar snap peas, strings removed if needed

Step-by-Step Instructions

  1. In a medium bowl, combine the drained canned salmon, Greek yogurt, light mayonnaise, diced celery, minced red onion, chopped dill, salt, and pepper. Use a fork to break up the salmon and stir until everything is evenly combined into a creamy salmon salad.
  2. Lay out the whole-grain bread slices on a cutting board.
  3. Place lettuce leaves and tomato slices on three of the bread slices, dividing them so that Frank, Susan, and Elise receive their portions as listed below.
  4. Spoon the salmon salad onto the lettuce-topped slices, dividing it into three portions for Frank, Susan, and Elise. Spread each portion in an even layer over the lettuce and tomato.
  5. Top each sandwich with a second slice of bread and press gently. Cut each sandwich in half if desired for easier packing.
  6. Rinse the snap peas and trim any stems or strings. Pat dry with a clean towel.
  7. Divide the snap peas into three small containers or bags according to the side portion sizes listed below so each person has a crunchy vegetable side.
  8. Serve immediately, or wrap each sandwich tightly in parchment or foil and refrigerate. Pack the snap peas separately to keep the sandwich from getting soggy.

Portion Sizes (Per Person)

Frank: 2 slices whole-grain bread, salmon salad made from 3.5 oz canned salmon, 2 tbsp Greek yogurt, 1 tsp light mayo, 1 tbsp diced celery, 1 tsp minced red onion, 2 slices tomato, 3 lettuce leaves, 3 oz snap peas.

 

Susan: 2 slices whole-grain bread, salmon salad made from 2.75 oz canned salmon, 1 1/2 tbsp Greek yogurt, 1 tsp light mayo, 2 tsp diced celery, 1 tsp minced red onion, 2 slices tomato, 2 lettuce leaves, 2.5 oz snap peas.

 

Elise: 2 slices whole-grain bread, salmon salad made from 2 oz canned salmon, 1 1/2 tbsp Greek yogurt, 1 tsp light mayo, 2 tsp diced celery, 1 tsp minced red onion, 1–2 slices tomato, 2 lettuce leaves, 2 oz snap peas.

Nutrition (Per Person)

  • Frank: 405 kcal, 37 g protein, 34 g carbs, 13 g fat, 6 g fiber, 560 mg sodium
  • Susan: 380 kcal, 34 g protein, 31 g carbs, 12 g fat, 5.5 g fiber, 520 mg sodium
  • Elise: 355 kcal, 31 g protein, 29 g carbs, 11 g fat, 5 g fiber, 480 mg sodium
