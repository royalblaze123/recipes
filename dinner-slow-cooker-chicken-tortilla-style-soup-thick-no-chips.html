Chicken Tortilla-Style Soup (Thick, No Chips)

Nutrition (approx)

Frank: 650 kcal • P 60g • C 60g • F 16g
Susan/Elise (each): 488 kcal • P 45g • C 45g • F 12g

Ingredients (purchase-clean)

  • 1.75 lb boneless skinless chicken breast
  • 1 jar salsa
  • 2 cans no-salt-added diced tomatoes
  • 1 can black beans
  • 3 each whole-wheat tortillas
  • low-sodium chicken broth
  • kosher salt
  • black pepper
  • garlic powder
  • onion powder
  • smoked paprika
  • Italian seasoning
  • ground cumin
  • chili powder
  • red pepper flakes
  • extra-virgin olive oil
  • low-sodium chicken broth
  • low-sodium beef broth
  • low-sodium soy sauce
  • hot sauce
  • Dijon mustard
  • balsamic vinegar
  • apple cider vinegar
  • cornstarch

Steps (slow cooker)

  1. Chicken + salsa (1 1/2 cups) + tomatoes (2x 14.5 oz) + broth (2 cups).
  2. Season chili powder, cumin, garlic powder, pepper; modest salt.
  3. LOW 4–5 hr or HIGH 2–3 hr to 165°F; shred chicken.
  4. Add black beans (15 oz; 1 1/2 cups) for last 25 min on HIGH.
  5. Thicken: slice 2 tortillas into strips; stir in; cook 15 min.
  6. Warm remaining tortilla; slice for serving if desired.

Notes

Tortilla-soup flavor but thick enough to freeze/reheat well. Portions (per person): Frank: About 2 1/2 cups (heaping bowl) Susan: About 2 cups (bowl) Elise: About 2 cups (bowl) Tips (recipe-specific): Use tortillas inside the pot for body. Add beans at the end for better texture.
