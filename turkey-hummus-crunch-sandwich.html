Turkey Hummus Crunch Sandwich + Carrots

Roasted turkey with a measured hummus spread and crunchy veg, plus carrot sticks and romaine for a complete lunch.

Servings

3 servings (Frank, Susan, Elise).

Times

  • Prep time: 12 minutes
  • Cook time: 18 minutes
  • Total time: 30 minutes

Ingredients

Sandwich

  • 6 slices whole wheat bread
  • 1 lb boneless skinless turkey breast
  • 1 container hummus
  • 1 each cucumber
  • 1 bunch scallions
  • 2 hearts romaine hearts
  • 1 tsp garlic powder
  • 1 tsp ground black pepper

Side

  • 1 lb bag carrots
  • 1 pint cherry tomatoes

Step-by-step Instructions

  1. Cook the turkey: Preheat oven to 425°F. Season turkey with garlic powder and black pepper. Roast on a parchment-lined sheet pan until 165°F (about 16–20 minutes depending on thickness). Rest 5 minutes, then thin-slice.
  2. Assemble sandwiches: Spread hummus on bread (measure per person). Layer sliced turkey, romaine, cucumber slices, and sliced scallions. Close into 3 full sandwiches.
  3. Prep side: Portion carrot sticks and cherry tomatoes per person. Pack tomatoes separately if transporting.

Portion Sizes

Portions (per person):
Frank: 2 slices bread, 3.0 oz cooked turkey, 2 tbsp hummus, 1 cup romaine, 1/2 cucumber, 2 tbsp scallions, 2 oz carrots, 1/2 cup cherry tomatoes

Susan: 2 slices bread, 2.25 oz cooked turkey, 1 1/2 tbsp hummus, 3/4 cup romaine, 1/4 cucumber, 1 1/2 tbsp scallions, 1 1/2 oz carrots, 1/3 cup cherry tomatoes

Elise: 2 slices bread, 2.25 oz cooked turkey, 1 1/2 tbsp hummus, 3/4 cup romaine, 1/4 cucumber, 1 1/2 tbsp scallions, 1 1/2 oz carrots, 1/3 cup cherry tomatoes
