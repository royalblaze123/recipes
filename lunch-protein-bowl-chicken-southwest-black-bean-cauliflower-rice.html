Protein Bowl: Southwest Chicken with Black Beans over Cauliflower Rice

Prep: 16 min   Cook: 14 min   Total: 30 min

Ingredients

  • 1.25 lb chicken breast
  • 1 bag frozen cauliflower rice
  • 0.5 can no-salt-added black beans
  • 1 bell pepper
  • 1 onion
  • 1 lime
  • olive oil
  • cumin
  • chili powder
  • smoked paprika
  • garlic powder
  • black pepper
  • salt substitute
  • salsa

Rules enforced: Clean AnyList Ingredients (quantities before items; fresh meat/poultry/fish use weights; packaged items use fractional containers when partial). Condiments/spices/seasonings have no measurements in Ingredients; all measurements are in Steps. No olives, no squash, no Brussels sprouts, no zucchini, no tofu/tempeh. Lunch validations: Frank calories 500–550, sat fat ≤6g, sodium ≤650mg, fiber ≥8g, added sugar ≤6g.

Step-by-step Instructions

  1. Cauliflower rice: microwave 1 bag cauliflower rice 5–6 minutes. Measure 3 cups cooked; drain/pat dry; refreeze remainder.
  2. Chicken: cut 1.25 lb chicken breast into bite-size pieces. Season with 2 tsp cumin + 1 tsp chili powder + 1/2 tsp smoked paprika + 1/2 tsp garlic powder + 1/2 tsp black pepper + 1/2 tsp salt substitute.
  3. Sauté chicken with 2 tsp olive oil 6–8 minutes until 165°F.
  4. Veg: slice 1 bell pepper and 1 onion; sauté 4 minutes until tender-crisp.
  5. Beans: drain and rinse 1/2 can black beans; stir into skillet with 2 tbsp water; warm 2 minutes.
  6. Build bowls: cauliflower rice + chicken/veg/beans.
  7. Finish: add salsa in measured amounts (Frank 1 tbsp; Susan 2 tsp; Elise 2 tsp) + 1 tsp lime juice per bowl.
