Chicken Enchilada Bake (Tortilla-Layer) + Side Salad

Nutrition (approx)

Frank: 650 kcal • P 58g • C 60g • F 18g
Susan/Elise (each): 488 kcal • P 44g • C 45g • F 14g

Ingredients (purchase-clean)

  • 1.75 lb boneless skinless chicken breast
  • 1 can enchilada sauce
  • 6 each whole-wheat tortillas
  • 6 oz reduced-fat cheddar cheese
  • 1 bag spring mix
  • apple cider vinegar
  • extra-virgin olive oil
  • kosher salt
  • black pepper
  • garlic powder
  • onion powder
  • smoked paprika
  • Italian seasoning
  • ground cumin
  • chili powder
  • red pepper flakes
  • extra-virgin olive oil
  • low-sodium chicken broth
  • low-sodium beef broth
  • low-sodium soy sauce
  • hot sauce
  • Dijon mustard
  • balsamic vinegar
  • apple cider vinegar
  • cornstarch

Steps (oven)

  1. Oven: heat to 400°F.
  2. Chicken: season garlic powder, cumin, black pepper, modest salt. Bake 400°F 18–22 minutes to 165°F; shred.
  3. Sauce: warm enchilada sauce (10 oz can) with 1/2 cup broth.
  4. Layer: in baking dish, spread a thin layer of sauce. Add tortillas (tear to fit), chicken, and a sprinkle of cheese; repeat 2–3 layers, ending with sauce + cheese.
  5. Bake: 400°F 18–22 minutes until hot and melted. Rest 10 minutes.
  6. Salad: toss spring mix with vinegar (2 tsp) + olive oil (2 tsp) + black pepper.
  7. Serve per Notes.

Notes

Enchilada comfort with measured cheese and a bright salad to balance. Portions (per person): Frank: 1/3 pan enchilada bake + 2 cups salad Susan: 1/4 pan + 1 1/2 cups salad Elise: 1/4 pan + 1 1/2 cups salad Tips (recipe-specific): Rest 10 minutes so slices hold together. Warm sauce with broth so it spreads without adding more cheese.
