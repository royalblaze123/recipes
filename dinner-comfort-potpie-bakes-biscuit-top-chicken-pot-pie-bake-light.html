Biscuit-Top Chicken Pot Pie Bake (Light)

Nutrition (approx)

Frank: 650 kcal • P 60g • C 60g • F 16g • Sat 5g • Na 700mg • Fiber 10g
Susan/Elise (each): 488 kcal • P 45g • C 45g • F 12g • Sat 4g • Na 525mg • Fiber 8g

Ingredients (purchase-clean)

  • 1.75 lb boneless skinless chicken breast
  • 1 bag frozen mixed vegetables
  • 1 carton skim milk
  • low-sodium chicken broth
  • cornstarch
  • 1 can refrigerated biscuits
  • kosher salt
  • black pepper
  • garlic powder
  • onion powder
  • Italian seasoning
  • smoked paprika
  • cornstarch
  • extra-virgin olive oil
  • low-sodium chicken broth

Steps

  1. Oven: heat to 400°F.
  2. Chicken: dice chicken. Sauté in a large skillet with 2 tsp olive oil, 1 tsp garlic powder, 1 tsp onion powder, 1/2 tsp kosher salt, 1/2 tsp black pepper 8–10 minutes to 165°F.
  3. Veg: add 3 cups frozen mixed vegetables. Cook 6–8 minutes until hot and most water evaporates.
  4. Sauce: whisk 2 tbsp cornstarch into 1 cup skim milk + 1 cup low-sodium chicken broth. Pour into skillet and simmer 3–5 minutes until thick like pot-pie filling.
  5. Transfer filling to a baking dish. Top with 3 biscuits (1 per person).
  6. Bake 14–16 minutes until biscuits are golden and filling bubbles.
  7. Rest 5 minutes before portioning. Serve per Notes.

Notes

Pot-pie comfort without a heavy cream base—thickened broth + a measured biscuit top. Portions (per person): Frank: 1 biscuit + 2 cups filling Susan: 1 biscuit + 1 1/2 cups filling Elise: 1 biscuit + 1 1/2 cups filling Tips (recipe-specific): Evaporate veg water before adding sauce so it stays thick. Rest 5 minutes so the filling sets and portions cleanly.
