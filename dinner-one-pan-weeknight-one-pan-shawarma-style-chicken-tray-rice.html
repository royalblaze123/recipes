One-Pan Shawarma-Style Chicken Tray + Rice

Nutrition (approx)

Frank: 640 kcal • P 58g • C 60g • F 16g • Sat 4g • Na 690mg • Fiber 10g
Susan/Elise (each): 480 kcal • P 44g • C 45g • F 12g • Sat 3g • Na 518mg • Fiber 8g

Ingredients (purchase-clean)

  • 1.75 lb boneless skinless chicken breast
  • 2 each bell peppers
  • 1 each onion
  • 1 cup dry brown rice
  • 1 each lemon
  • kosher salt
  • black pepper
  • garlic powder
  • onion powder
  • smoked paprika
  • Italian seasoning
  • ground cumin
  • chili powder
  • red pepper flakes
  • extra-virgin olive oil
  • low-sodium chicken broth

Steps

  1. Rice: cook 1 cup dry brown rice with 2 cups water 35–40 minutes; rest 5 minutes.
  2. Oven: heat to 450°F.
  3. Veg: slice peppers/onion. Toss with 2 tsp olive oil, 1 tsp cumin, 1 tsp smoked paprika, 1 tsp garlic powder, black pepper, modest kosher salt. Roast 10 minutes.
  4. Chicken: toss chicken strips with 2 tsp olive oil, 2 tsp cumin, 1 tsp smoked paprika, 1 tsp garlic powder, black pepper, and 2 tsp lemon juice.
  5. Add chicken; roast 10–12 minutes to 165°F.
  6. Serve over rice with lemon per Notes.

Notes

Shawarma-style flavor from spices + high heat (no heavy sauce needed). Portions (per person): Frank: About 7 oz chicken + 1 cup veg over 1 cup cooked rice Susan: About 5 oz chicken + 3/4 cup veg over 3/4 cup cooked rice Elise: About 5 oz chicken + 3/4 cup veg over 3/4 cup cooked rice Tips (recipe-specific): High heat gives that ‘street-food’ edge. Add lemon at the end to pop the spices.
