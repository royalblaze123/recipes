Creamy Mushroom Gravy Dip

Category: Sauce • Yield: About 2 cups (32 tbsp) • AnyList-ready

Ingredients

  • 8 oz package mushrooms
  • 1 carton low-sodium chicken broth
  • 3/4 cup 2% plain Greek yogurt
  • 2 tsp extra-virgin olive oil
  • 1 tsp Worcestershire sauce
  • 1 tsp cornstarch
  • 1 tsp garlic powder
  • 1/2 tsp onion powder
  • 1/4 tsp black pepper

Steps

  1. Slice mushrooms from the 8 oz package.
  2. In a small saucepan over medium heat, warm 2 tsp olive oil. Add mushrooms and cook 6–8 minutes, stirring, until browned and most moisture cooks off.
  3. Add garlic powder and onion powder; stir 15 seconds.
  4. Pour in 1 cup broth from the 32 fl oz carton (reserve remaining broth refrigerated). Bring to a gentle simmer.
  5. Whisk cornstarch with 2 tsp water until smooth. Whisk slurry into the pan; simmer 60–90 seconds until lightly thickened.
  6. Remove from heat and cool 2 minutes. Whisk in Greek yogurt and Worcestershire until smooth. Do not boil after adding yogurt.
