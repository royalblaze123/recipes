Air Fryer Pork Tenderloin with Sweet Potatoes and Vegetables

Ingredients

  • 1 pork tenderloin
  • 1 large sweet potato
  • 1 bag frozen broccoli florets
  • 6 tsp olive oil
  • 4 tsp low-sodium soy sauce
  • 2 tsp Dijon mustard
  • 2 tsp smoked paprika
  • 2 tsp garlic powder
  • 1 tsp onion powder
  • 1 tsp black pepper
  • 1 tsp dried thyme

Preparation Steps

Pork Tenderloin

  1. Pat dry the pork tenderloin.
  2. In a small bowl, mix 3 tsp olive oil, 4 tsp low-sodium soy sauce, 2 tsp Dijon mustard, 1 tsp smoked paprika, 1 tsp garlic powder, 1 tsp black pepper, and 1 tsp dried thyme.
  3. Rub the mixture evenly over the pork tenderloin.
  4. Preheat air fryer to 380°F.
  5. Air fry pork for 18–20 minutes, turning once, until internal temperature reaches 145°F.
  6. Remove and rest 5 minutes before slicing.

Sweet Potatoes

  1. Peel and dice the sweet potato into ¾-inch cubes.
  2. Toss with 2 tsp olive oil, 1 tsp smoked paprika, and 1 tsp garlic powder.
  3. Air fry at 380°F for 14–16 minutes, shaking halfway, until tender and lightly browned.

Vegetables

  1. Measure out 4 cups frozen broccoli florets from the bag.
  2. Toss with 1 tsp olive oil and 1 tsp onion powder.
  3. Air fry at 380°F for 10–12 minutes, shaking once, until heated through and lightly crisped.
  4. Reseal and return remaining broccoli to freezer.

Notes

Balanced air fryer dinner designed for weeknights.
