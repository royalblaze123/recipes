Salmon Quinoa-Edamame Salad with Microgreens

Prep: 22 min   Cook: 10 min   Total: 32 min

Ingredients

  • 14 oz salmon fillets
  • 0.5 cup dry quinoa
  • 0.5 bag frozen shelled edamame
  • 1 bag spinach
  • 1 bag microgreens
  • 1 cucumber
  • 1 lemon
  • olive oil
  • black pepper
  • salt substitute
  • vinegar
  • Dijon mustard

Hard rules enforced: Lunch validations (calories, sat fat, sodium, fiber, added sugar). Quantities appear before items; fresh meat/poultry/fish use weights. Condiments/spices/seasonings have no measurements in Ingredients; all measurements are in Steps. No olives, no squash, no Brussels sprouts, no zucchini. Yogurt is not used in dinners.

Step-by-step Instructions

  1. Quinoa: rinse 1/2 cup dry quinoa. Simmer with 1 cup water (Steps only) 15 minutes; rest 5 minutes. Cool slightly.
  2. Edamame: microwave 1/2 bag shelled edamame 4–5 minutes. Measure 1 cup for salad; refreeze the rest.
  3. Salmon: season with black pepper and salt substitute. Bake 425°F for 10–12 minutes until flaky. Cool 5 minutes and flake.
  4. Build salad: spinach + quinoa + edamame + diced cucumber. Top with salmon and microgreens.
  5. Dressing: whisk 2 tsp olive oil + 2 tsp lemon juice + 1 tsp vinegar + Dijon mustard + black pepper + salt substitute. Toss lightly.
  6. Portion: Frank large bowl; Susan/Elise ~3/4 bowl each.
