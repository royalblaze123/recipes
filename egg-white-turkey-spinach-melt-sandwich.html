Egg White Turkey Spinach Melt Sandwich + Tomatoes

Protein-forward egg-white patty with turkey and spinach, finished with melty cheese and a tomato side.

Servings

3 servings (Frank, Susan, Elise).

Times

  • Prep time: 12 minutes
  • Cook time: 10 minutes
  • Total time: 22 minutes

Ingredients

Sandwich

  • 6 slices whole wheat bread
  • 1 cup egg whites
  • 4 each eggs
  • 1 lb boneless skinless turkey breast
  • 3 slices reduced-fat cheddar cheese
  • 1 bag baby spinach

Side

  • 1 pint cherry tomatoes
  • 1 each cucumber

Step-by-step Instructions

  1. Cook the turkey: Roast or air fry turkey until 165°F, rest, then thin-slice. Measure cooked portions for sandwiches.
  2. Cook egg patties: Whisk egg whites with 1 egg. Cook in a nonstick skillet over medium heat to form 3 thin patties (about 2–3 minutes per side).
  3. Assemble sandwiches: On each sandwich, layer turkey, a slice of cheddar (cut slices to match portions below), and a handful of spinach. Add the egg patty. Close sandwich. Optional: toast in skillet 2 minutes per side to warm through and wilt spinach.
  4. Prep side: Portion cherry tomatoes and cucumber slices per person.

Portion Sizes

Portions (per person):

Frank: 2 slices bread, 3.5 oz cooked turkey, 1 slice cheddar, 1 egg patty made from 1/2 cup egg whites + 1/3 egg, 1 cup spinach, 1/2 cup cherry tomatoes, 1/2 cucumber

Susan: 2 slices bread, 2.75 oz cooked turkey, 3/4 slice cheddar, 1 egg patty made from 3/8 cup egg whites + 1/3 egg, 3/4 cup spinach, 1/3 cup cherry tomatoes, 1/4 cucumber

Elise: 2 slices bread, 2.75 oz cooked turkey, 3/4 slice cheddar, 1 egg patty made from 3/8 cup egg whites + 1/3 egg, 3/4 cup spinach, 1/3 cup cherry tomatoes, 1/4 cucumber
