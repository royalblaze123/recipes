Protein+ Spaghetti Pesto Chicken

Nutrition (approx)

Frank: 694 kcal • P 69g • C 52g • F 25g
Susan/Elise (each): 520 kcal • P 52g • C 39g • F 19g

Ingredients (purchase-clean)

  • 1/3 box Barilla Protein+ spaghetti
  • 1.25 lb boneless skinless chicken breast
  • 1 jar pesto
  • 2 each bell peppers
  • 2 tbsp grated parmesan
  • 2 tsp garlic powder
  • 2 tsp Italian seasoning
  • 1 tsp black pepper
  • 1 tsp kosher salt

Steps

  1. Bring a large pot of water to a boil for the Barilla Protein+ spaghetti. (Use enough water to fully cover pasta; do not add water to Ingredients.)
  2. Pat chicken breast dry. Season with 2 tsp garlic powder, 2 tsp Italian seasoning, 1 tsp black pepper, and 1 tsp kosher salt. Sear in a nonstick skillet over medium-high 4–5 minutes per side. Reduce to medium, cover, and cook 3–5 minutes more until 165°F. Rest 5 minutes, then chop.
  3. Dice the bell peppers. Cook 3–5 minutes until tender-crisp.
  4. Jar size note: measure pesto by tablespoons; refrigerate the remainder.
  5. Cook Barilla Protein+ spaghetti to al dente per box time. Reserve 1 cup pasta water, then drain.
  6. Toss pasta into the sauce/skillet. Add reserved pasta water 1–2 tbsp at a time until the sauce clings well.
  7. Portion into bowls using the Portions guidance in Notes.

Notes

Pesto chicken spaghetti with measured pesto and peppers. Built on Barilla Protein+ for a higher-protein pasta plate with close-approx nutrition. Portions (per person): Frank: 2 oz dry Barilla Protein+ spaghetti + 1/3 of the skillet/sauce Susan: 1.5 oz dry Barilla Protein+ spaghetti + 1/4 of the skillet/sauce Elise: 1.5 oz dry Barilla Protein+ spaghetti + 1/4 of the skillet/sauce
