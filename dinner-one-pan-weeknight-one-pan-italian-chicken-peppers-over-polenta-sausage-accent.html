One-Pan Italian Chicken + Peppers over Polenta (Sausage Accent)

Nutrition (approx)

Frank: 645 kcal • P 58g • C 55g • F 16g • Sat 6g • Na 700mg • Fiber 10g
Susan/Elise (each): 484 kcal • P 44g • C 41g • F 12g • Sat 4g • Na 525mg • Fiber 8g

Ingredients (purchase-clean)

  • 1.50 lb boneless skinless chicken breast
  • 6 oz chicken sausage
  • 2 each bell peppers
  • 1 each onion
  • 1 tube prepared polenta
  • 1 jar marinara sauce
  • kosher salt
  • black pepper
  • garlic powder
  • onion powder
  • smoked paprika
  • Italian seasoning
  • ground cumin
  • chili powder
  • red pepper flakes
  • extra-virgin olive oil
  • low-sodium chicken broth

Steps

  1. Oven: heat to 425°F.
  2. Peppers/onion: slice peppers and onion. Roast on a sheet pan with 2 tsp olive oil, black pepper, and 1 tsp Italian seasoning for 18–20 minutes, stirring once.
  3. Chicken: slice chicken strips; season with garlic powder, onion powder, Italian seasoning, black pepper, and a modest pinch of kosher salt. Add to pan at minute 10; roast until 165°F (about 10–12 minutes).
  4. Sausage accent: slice 6 oz chicken sausage; add to pan for the last 8 minutes to brown.
  5. Polenta: slice polenta into 1/2-inch rounds and warm in a dry skillet 2 minutes per side.
  6. Sauce: warm 1 cup marinara (measured).
  7. Serve: polenta + chicken/pepper mix + marinara drizzle per Notes.

Notes

Italian comfort with sausage used as a flavor accent, not the main protein. Portions (per person): Frank: About 2 cups chicken/pepper mix + 6 polenta rounds Susan: About 1 1/2 cups mix + 4 polenta rounds Elise: About 1 1/2 cups mix + 4 polenta rounds Tips (recipe-specific): Roast peppers/onions hard to get sweetness without sugar. Keep sausage to 6 oz total—flavor without blowing sat fat.
