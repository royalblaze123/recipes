Chicken Hummus Wrap + Bell Pepper Strips (RBM Lunch)

Whole-wheat wrap stuffed with cooked chicken, hummus, and veggies, with bell pepper strips on the side.

Servings

3 servings (Frank, Susan, Elise).

Ingredients

Sandwich

  • 3 large whole-wheat tortillas (8-inch, at least 3 g fiber per tortilla)
  • 8.25 oz cooked chicken breast, chopped (home-cooked or rotisserie, skin removed; clearly labeled home-cooked meat)
  • 7 1/2 tbsp hummus (about 1/4 cup + 1 1/2 tsp total)
  • 1 1/4 cups shredded fresh romaine or baby spinach
  • 1/2 cup fresh tomato, diced
  • 1/2 cup fresh cucumber, sliced into half-moons

Side

  • 7.5 oz fresh bell pepper strips (any color), sliced

Step-by-Step Instructions

  1. If cooking chicken just for these wraps, season boneless skinless chicken breast lightly with pepper and a pinch of salt, then bake or sauté until it reaches 165°F (74°C), cool slightly, and chop. You can also use rotisserie chicken breast with the skin removed.
  2. Lay the whole-wheat tortillas flat on a clean work surface or cutting board.
  3. Spread hummus evenly over each tortilla, leaving about 1 inch around the edges to prevent overflow when rolling.
  4. Divide the chopped cooked chicken breast among the tortillas, placing the chicken in a line across the center of each tortilla.
  5. Top the chicken on each tortilla with shredded lettuce or spinach, diced tomato, and sliced cucumber.
  6. Fold in the sides of each tortilla, then roll up tightly from the bottom to form a wrap. Slice each wrap in half on a slight diagonal if desired for easier packing.
  7. Divide the fresh bell pepper strips into three portions as listed below and place in small containers or snack bags.
  8. Serve the wraps with bell pepper strips immediately, or wrap the sandwiches tightly in foil or parchment and refrigerate until ready to take to work or school.

Portion Sizes (Per Person)

Frank: 1 large whole-wheat tortilla (8-inch), 3.5 oz cooked chicken breast (home-cooked or rotisserie, chopped), 3 tbsp hummus, 1/2 cup shredded lettuce or spinach, 1/4 cup diced tomato, 1/4 cup sliced cucumber, 3 oz bell pepper strips.

 

Susan: 1 large whole-wheat tortilla (8-inch), 2.75 oz cooked chicken breast, 2 1/2 tbsp hummus, 1/3 cup shredded lettuce or spinach, 3 tbsp diced tomato, 3 tbsp sliced cucumber, 2.5 oz bell pepper strips.

 

Elise: 1 large whole-wheat tortilla (8-inch), 2 oz cooked chicken breast, 2 tbsp hummus, 1/4 cup shredded lettuce or spinach, 2 tbsp diced tomato, 2 tbsp sliced cucumber, 2 oz bell pepper strips.

Nutrition (Per Person)

  • Frank: 430 kcal, 40 g protein, 40 g carbs, 12 g fat, 8 g fiber, 620 mg sodium
  • Susan: 395 kcal, 36 g protein, 35 g carbs, 11 g fat, 7 g fiber, 575 mg sodium
  • Elise: 360 kcal, 32 g protein, 30 g carbs, 10 g fat, 6 g fiber, 530 mg sodium
