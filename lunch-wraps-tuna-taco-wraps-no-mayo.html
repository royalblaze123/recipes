Tuna Taco Wraps (No Mayo)

Nutrition (approx)

Frank: 520 kcal • P 52g • C 48g • F 14g
Susan/Elise (each): 390 kcal • P 39g • C 36g • F 10g

Ingredients (purchase-clean)

  • 3 cans tuna
  • 1 package large whole-wheat tortillas
  • 1 bag shredded lettuce
  • 1 each avocado
  • 1/2 jar salsa
  • 1 each cucumber
  • 1 bag mini sweet peppers
  • 1 tub plain nonfat Greek yogurt
  • 1 each lime
  • 2 tsp garlic powder
  • 1 tsp black pepper
  • 1 tsp kosher salt

Steps

  1. Tuna: Can size note—use a standard 5 oz can tuna.. Use three 5 oz cans tuna. Drain very well.
  2. Season tuna with garlic powder, black pepper, and kosher salt.
  3. Sauce: mix 1/2 cup Greek yogurt with 2 tsp lime juice and 1 tsp chili powder.
  4. Wrap build: warm tortillas 15 seconds so they roll easily. Spread the sauce on each tortilla. Add the protein, then add shredded lettuce or slaw mix if listed.
  5. Roll: fold in the sides and roll tightly. Slice in half for serving if desired.
  6. Crunchy side: slice cucumber and sweet peppers into sticks. Sprinkle with black pepper. Serve alongside the wrap.
  7. Avocado: halve, pit, and slice. Use about 1/2 avocado total; refrigerate remaining avocado with the pit to slow browning.
  8. Serve with salsa and portion per Notes.

Notes

Pantry taco wraps: tuna + yogurt-lime sauce, served with crunchy veg and salsa/avocado. Portions (per person): Frank: 1 large wrap + 1/3 of the crunchy veg side + salsa + avocado Susan: 3/4 wrap + 1/4 of the crunchy veg side + salsa + avocado Elise: 3/4 wrap + 1/4 of the crunchy veg side + salsa + avocado
