Beef Bulgogi-Style Skillet Bowls (No Sugar) + Rice

Nutrition (approx)

Frank: 650 kcal • P 55g • C 70g • F 18g
Susan/Elise (each): 488 kcal • P 41g • C 52g • F 14g

Ingredients (purchase-clean)

  • 1.50 lb lean sirloin steak
  • low-sodium soy sauce
  • apple cider vinegar
  • cornstarch
  • 1 cup dry brown rice
  • 1 bag frozen broccoli florets
  • low-sodium beef broth
  • kosher salt
  • black pepper
  • garlic powder
  • onion powder
  • smoked paprika
  • Italian seasoning
  • ground cumin
  • chili powder
  • red pepper flakes
  • extra-virgin olive oil
  • low-sodium chicken broth
  • low-sodium beef broth
  • low-sodium soy sauce
  • hot sauce
  • Dijon mustard
  • balsamic vinegar
  • apple cider vinegar
  • cornstarch

Steps (stovetop)

  1. Cook rice: 1 cup dry brown rice.
  2. Broccoli: microwave 4–5 minutes; drain/pat dry.
  3. Beef: slice sirloin very thin across grain; sear in hot skillet with olive oil 2–3 minutes.
  4. Sauce: whisk soy sauce (6 tbsp) + beef broth (3/4 cup) + apple cider vinegar (1 tbsp) + cornstarch (1 tbsp) + hot sauce (2 tsp).
  5. Finish: add broccoli; pour sauce; simmer 2–3 minutes until glossy; beef medium.
  6. Serve per Notes.

Notes

Bulgogi directionally without sugar—glossy savory sauce and thin-sliced sirloin. Portions (per person): Frank: 7 oz beef + 1 cup rice + 1 cup broccoli Susan: 5 oz beef + 3/4 cup rice + 3/4 cup broccoli Elise: 5 oz beef + 3/4 cup rice + 3/4 cup broccoli Tips (recipe-specific): Slice beef thin for tenderness. Cornstarch makes it ‘takeout glossy’ without extra calories.
