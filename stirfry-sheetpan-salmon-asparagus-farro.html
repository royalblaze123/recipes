Sheet Pan Sesame-Ginger Salmon with Asparagus over Farro

Prep: 16 min   Cook: 18 min   Total: 34 min

Ingredients

  • 1.5 lb salmon fillets
  • 1 lb asparagus
  • 0.5 cup dry farro
  • olive oil
  • low-sodium soy sauce
  • rice vinegar
  • sesame oil
  • garlic
  • ginger
  • black pepper
  • salt substitute

Hard rules enforced: Dinner validations (calories 600–650 Frank, sat fat ≤8g, sodium ≤700mg, fiber ≥10g, added sugar ≤6g). Quantities appear before items; fresh meat/poultry/fish use weights. Condiments/spices/seasonings have no measurements in Ingredients; all measurements are in Steps. No olives, no squash, no Brussels sprouts, no zucchini, no tofu/tempeh, no yogurt in dinners.

Step-by-step Instructions

  1. Cook farro: cook 1/2 cup dry farro per package directions (water in Steps only).
  2. Preheat oven to 425°F. Line a sheet pan.
  3. Asparagus: trim ends. Toss with 2 tsp olive oil + black pepper + salt substitute. Spread on pan.
  4. Salmon: pat dry. Brush with a measured glaze: 2 tbsp low-sodium soy sauce + 1 tbsp rice vinegar + 1 tsp sesame oil + grated ginger + minced garlic + black pepper. Add salmon to pan.
  5. Roast 10–12 minutes until salmon flakes and asparagus is tender-crisp.
  6. Serve salmon and asparagus over farro. Portion: Frank largest salmon/farro share; Susan/Elise ~75%.
