Chicken Lentil Veggie Bowl

NOTES: Designed as a portable grab-and-go lunch using lean protein, high-fiber vegetables, and controlled smart carbs. Use low-sodium ingredients and avoid added sugars where possible. PORTION SIZES: Frank: largest portion (~1/3 of the total volume); Susan: medium portion (~1/4 of the total volume); Elise: medium portion (~1/4 of the total volume) (Frank = 1.0×, Susan/Elise ≈0.75× portions).

Ingredients

  • 3/4 cup dry brown or green lentils, rinsed
  • 2 cups low-sodium vegetable or chicken broth
  • 12 oz cooked boneless, skinless chicken breast, chopped
  • 1 1/2 cups steamed broccoli florets
  • 1 cup shredded carrots
  • 1/2 red onion, thinly sliced
  • 2 tbsp extra-virgin olive oil
  • 2 tbsp red wine vinegar or apple cider vinegar (no sugar added)
  • 1 tsp dried Italian seasoning
  • Freshly ground black pepper, to taste

Step-by-Step Instructions

  1. Cook lentils: Combine rinsed lentils and broth in a saucepan. Bring to a boil, then reduce heat, cover, and simmer 20–25 minutes until tender but not mushy. Drain excess liquid if needed and cool slightly.
  2. In a large bowl, combine warm lentils, chopped chicken, steamed broccoli, shredded carrots, and sliced red onion.
  3. In a small bowl, whisk together olive oil, vinegar, Italian seasoning, and black pepper.
  4. Pour dressing over the bowl and toss until evenly coated.
  5. Divide into three containers with lids, making Frank’s portion largest. These can be eaten warm, room temperature, or chilled.

Nutrition per Person

  • Frank: ~580 kcal, ~50 g protein, ~50 g carbs, ~17 g fat.
  • Susan: ~435 kcal, ~38 g protein, ~38 g carbs, ~13 g fat.
  • Elise: ~435 kcal, ~38 g protein, ~38 g carbs, ~13 g fat.
