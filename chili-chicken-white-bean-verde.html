Chicken White Bean Chili Verde

NOTES: Each chili is designed as a full meal with lean protein, smart carbs, and plenty of vegetables. Use low-sodium, no-sugar-added canned goods, avoid added sugars, and keep portions aligned with your goals. PORTION SIZES: Frank: largest portion (~1/3 of the total pot); Susan: medium portion (~1/4 of the pot); Elise: medium portion (~1/4 of the pot) (Frank = 1.0×, Susan/Elise ≈0.75× portions).

Ingredients

  • 12 oz boneless skinless chicken breast, diced into 1/2-inch cubes
  • 1 small yellow onion, diced
  • 1 green bell pepper, diced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced or 1 tsp garlic powder
  • 1 can (15 oz) no-salt-added cannellini or great northern beans, drained and rinsed
  • 1 can (10 oz) mild diced green chiles (no added sugar)
  • 2 cups low-sodium chicken broth
  • 1 tbsp extra-virgin olive oil
  • 1 tsp ground cumin
  • 1/2 tsp dried oregano
  • Juice of 1/2 lime (about 1 tbsp)
  • Freshly ground black pepper, to taste
  • 2 tbsp chopped fresh cilantro (optional garnish)

Step-by-Step Instructions

  1. Dice chicken, onion, and bell pepper into small, even pieces.
  2. Heat olive oil in a soup pot over medium heat for 1–2 minutes.
  3. Add diced chicken and cook 5–6 minutes, stirring occasionally, until lightly browned and no longer pink on the outside.
  4. Add onion, bell pepper, and garlic. Cook 4–5 minutes, stirring occasionally, until vegetables are softened.
  5. Stir in cumin, oregano, and black pepper. Cook 1 minute to bloom spices.
  6. Add white beans, mild green chiles with their liquid, and chicken broth. Stir to combine.
  7. Bring to a gentle boil, then reduce heat to low. Cover and simmer 20 minutes, stirring once or twice, until flavors develop and chicken is cooked through.
  8. Stir in lime juice. Taste and adjust seasoning. Ladle into three bowls, giving Frank the largest portion. Garnish with cilantro if desired and serve with a side of steamed broccoli or a small salad.

Nutrition per Person

  • Frank: ~610 kcal, ~48 g protein, ~45 g carbs, ~17 g fat.
  • Susan: ~460 kcal, ~36 g protein, ~34 g carbs, ~12 g fat.
  • Elise: ~460 kcal, ~36 g protein, ~34 g carbs, ~12 g fat.
