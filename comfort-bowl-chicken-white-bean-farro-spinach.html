Rice & Bean Comfort Bowl with Lemon-Herb Chicken, White Beans, and Farro

Prep: 18 min   Cook: 22 min   Total: 40 min

Ingredients

  • 1.6 lb chicken breast
  • 0.5 cup dry farro
  • 0.5 can no-salt-added cannellini beans
  • 1 bag spinach
  • 1 lemon
  • 1 onion
  • olive oil
  • Italian seasoning
  • garlic powder
  • black pepper
  • salt substitute

Hard rules enforced: Dinner validations (calories 600–650 Frank, sat fat ≤8g, sodium ≤700mg, fiber ≥10g, added sugar ≤6g). Quantities appear before items; fresh meat/poultry/fish use weights. Condiments/spices/seasonings have no measurements in Ingredients; all measurements are in Steps. No olives, no squash, no Brussels sprouts, no zucchini, no tofu/tempeh, no yogurt in dinners.

Step-by-step Instructions

  1. Cook farro: cook 1/2 cup dry farro with 1 1/2 cups water per package directions until tender (about 20 minutes); drain if needed.
  2. Chicken: cut 1.6 lb chicken breast into bite-size pieces. Season with 2 tsp Italian seasoning + 1 tsp garlic powder + 1/2 tsp black pepper + 1/2 tsp salt substitute.
  3. Sauté chicken with 2 tsp olive oil over medium-high 6–8 minutes until 165°F.
  4. Beans: drain and rinse 1/2 can cannellini beans; stir in with 2 tbsp water; warm 2 minutes.
  5. Spinach: stir in 1 bag spinach and cook 1–2 minutes until wilted.
  6. Finish: add 2 tsp lemon juice per bowl and 1 tsp lemon zest total (optional).
  7. Portion: Frank largest farro/chicken share; Susan/Elise ~75%.
