Instant Pot Tomato-Spice Chicken + Chickpeas

Nutrition (approx)

Frank: 650 kcal • P 60g • C 55g • F 16g
Susan/Elise (each): 488 kcal • P 45g • C 41g • F 12g

Ingredients (purchase-clean)

  • 1.75 lb boneless skinless chicken breast
  • 1 can chickpeas
  • 2 cans no-salt-added diced tomatoes
  • 1 each onion
  • low-sodium chicken broth
  • cornstarch
  • kosher salt
  • black pepper
  • garlic powder
  • onion powder
  • smoked paprika
  • Italian seasoning
  • ground cumin
  • chili powder
  • red pepper flakes
  • extra-virgin olive oil
  • low-sodium chicken broth
  • low-sodium beef broth
  • low-sodium soy sauce
  • hot sauce
  • Dijon mustard
  • balsamic vinegar
  • apple cider vinegar
  • cornstarch

Steps (Instant Pot)

  1. Sauté: olive oil + onion 3–4 minutes. Bloom cumin, smoked paprika, chili powder, garlic powder 30 seconds.
  2. Load: tomatoes (2x 14.5 oz) + broth (1 cup) + chicken.
  3. Pressure cook: High 10 minutes; NR 10 then Quick Release. Shred chicken.
  4. Chickpeas: drain/rinse chickpeas (15 oz; use 1 1/2 cups). Stir in; Sauté 5 minutes to thicken.
  5. Optional thicken: cornstarch slurry 2 minutes.
  6. Serve per Notes.

Notes

Tomato-spice comfort with chickpeas for fiber and ‘stew’ body (no coconut). Portions (per person): Frank: About 2 1/2 cups (heaping bowl) Susan: About 2 cups (bowl) Elise: About 2 cups (bowl) Tips (recipe-specific): Sauté after pressure to build thickness for leftovers. Add chickpeas after pressure so they keep bite.
