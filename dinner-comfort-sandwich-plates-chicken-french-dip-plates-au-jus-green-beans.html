Chicken French Dip Plates + Au Jus + Green Beans

Nutrition (approx)

Frank: 640 kcal • P 55g • C 60g • F 16g • Sat 6g • Na 700mg • Fiber 10g
Susan/Elise (each): 480 kcal • P 41g • C 45g • F 12g • Sat 4g • Na 525mg • Fiber 8g

Ingredients (purchase-clean)

  • 3 each whole-wheat sub rolls
  • 1.75 lb chicken breast
  • low-sodium beef broth
  • 1 bag frozen green beans
  • kosher salt
  • black pepper
  • garlic powder
  • onion powder
  • Italian seasoning
  • smoked paprika
  • extra-virgin olive oil
  • Dijon mustard
  • hot sauce
  • rice vinegar
  • Worcestershire sauce

Steps

  1. Oven: heat to 425°F. Roast green beans with 2 tsp olive oil and black pepper 14–18 minutes.
  2. Chicken: slice thin; sauté with garlic powder, black pepper, modest kosher salt 6–8 minutes to 165°F.
  3. Au jus: warm 2 cups low-sodium beef broth with 1 tsp Worcestershire.
  4. Assemble: pile chicken into sub rolls.
  5. Serve: each person gets a small cup of au jus for dipping plus green beans. Portion per Notes.

Notes

French-dip comfort with chicken and a controlled au jus dip. Portions (per person): Frank: 1 sub + 1 cup green beans + 1/3 cup au jus Susan: 1 sub + 3/4 cup green beans + 1/4 cup au jus Elise: 1 sub + 3/4 cup green beans + 1/4 cup au jus Tips (recipe-specific): Keep au jus portioned so sodium stays controlled. Slice chicken thin so it stays tender and juicy.
