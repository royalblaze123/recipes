Split Pea & Smoked Turkey Soup (Stovetop)

NOTES: Designed as a complete meal soup using lean protein, high-fiber vegetables, and controlled starch. Use low-sodium broth and avoid extra salt when possible to support blood pressure and kidney health. PORTION SIZES: Frank: largest portion (~2 cups); Susan: medium portion (~1.5 cups); Elise: medium portion (~1.5 cups).

Ingredients

  • 1 tbsp extra-virgin olive oil
  • 1 medium yellow onion, diced
  • 2 carrots, diced
  • 2 celery stalks, diced
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1.5 cups dry green split peas, rinsed and picked over
  • 5 cups low-sodium chicken or vegetable broth
  • 1 cup water
  • 8 oz smoked turkey breast or turkey ham, diced
  • 1 tsp dried thyme
  • 1 tsp smoked paprika
  • 1 bay leaf
  • 1/2 tsp kosher salt (or to taste)
  • Freshly ground black pepper, to taste
  • Chopped fresh parsley, for garnish (optional)

Step-by-Step Instructions

  1. Heat olive oil in a large soup pot over medium heat.
  2. Add diced onion, carrots, and celery. Cook 5–6 minutes until vegetables begin to soften.
  3. Stir in minced garlic and cook 30–60 seconds until fragrant.
  4. Add rinsed split peas, low-sodium broth, and water to the pot. Stir to combine.
  5. Stir in diced smoked turkey, dried thyme, smoked paprika, bay leaf, salt, and black pepper.
  6. Bring to a gentle boil, then reduce heat to low, cover partially, and simmer 45–55 minutes, stirring occasionally, until split peas are very tender and breaking down.
  7. Add a little extra water or broth 1/4 cup at a time if soup becomes too thick.
  8. Remove bay leaf and discard.
  9. Lightly mash some peas against the side of the pot with a spoon to create a creamier texture while leaving some peas whole.
  10. Taste and adjust salt and pepper as needed.
  11. Let rest 5 minutes off heat, then divide into Frank, Susan, and Elise portions in bowls.
  12. Garnish with chopped fresh parsley if desired.

Nutrition per Person

  • Frank: ~460 kcal, ~38 g protein, ~55 g carbs, ~8 g fat.
  • Susan: ~345 kcal, ~29 g protein, ~41 g carbs, ~6 g fat.
  • Elise: ~345 kcal, ~29 g protein, ~41 g carbs, ~6 g fat.
