Chicken Pita Plates + Cucumber-Tomato (No Yogurt)

Nutrition (approx)

Frank: 640 kcal • P 55g • C 60g • F 16g • Sat 4g • Na 690mg • Fiber 10g
Susan/Elise (each): 480 kcal • P 41g • C 45g • F 12g • Sat 3g • Na 518mg • Fiber 8g

Ingredients (purchase-clean)

  • 6 each whole-wheat pitas
  • 1.75 lb chicken breast
  • 1 each cucumber
  • 1 pint cherry tomatoes
  • 1 each lemon
  • kosher salt
  • black pepper
  • garlic powder
  • onion powder
  • Italian seasoning
  • smoked paprika
  • extra-virgin olive oil
  • Dijon mustard
  • hot sauce
  • rice vinegar
  • dried oregano

Steps

  1. Chicken: slice into strips. Sauté with 2 tsp olive oil, dried oregano, garlic powder, black pepper, modest kosher salt 8–10 minutes to 165°F.
  2. Salad: slice cucumber; halve tomatoes; add 2 tsp lemon juice and black pepper.
  3. Vinaigrette drizzle: whisk 1 tsp rice vinegar + 1 tsp lemon juice + hot sauce + black pepper.
  4. Assemble pitas: each person gets 2 pita halves stuffed with chicken and cucumber-tomato.
  5. Serve per Notes.

Notes

Gyro-style pita comfort without tzatziki—bright vinegar/lemon keeps it bold. Portions (per person): Frank: 2 pita halves + 1 cup cucumber-tomato Susan: 2 pita halves + 3/4 cup cucumber-tomato Elise: 2 pita halves + 3/4 cup cucumber-tomato Tips (recipe-specific): Use acid + oregano for that gyro taste without creamy sauce. Warm pitas briefly so they fold without tearing.
