High-Protein Pitas: Tuna Dijon Crunch with Celery and Cabbage

Prep: 16 min   Cook: 0 min   Total: 16 min

Ingredients

  • 3 whole wheat pitas
  • 3 cans tuna in water
  • 0.5 tub plain nonfat Greek yogurt
  • 1 bunch celery
  • 0.5 bag shredded cabbage
  • 1 lemon
  • 1 clamshell microgreens
  • Dijon mustard
  • black pepper
  • salt substitute
  • vinegar

Rules enforced: quantities before items; fresh meat/poultry/fish use weights; packaged items use fractional containers when partial. Condiments/spices/seasonings have no measurements in Ingredients; all measurements are in Steps. No olives, no squash, no Brussels sprouts, no zucchini, no tofu/tempeh. Lunch validations included in Notes.

Step-by-step Instructions

  1. Tuna: drain 3 cans tuna very well.
  2. Mix: in a bowl, combine tuna with yogurt and Dijon. Measure: Frank uses the largest scoop; Susan/Elise ~75%. Add 1 tsp vinegar and 1 tsp lemon juice total; pepper/salt substitute to taste.
  3. Crunch: thin-slice celery (use 4 stalks; refrigerate the rest).
  4. Base: put a handful of shredded cabbage inside each pita (divide 1/2 bag evenly).
  5. Fill: add tuna mix, then celery, then microgreens.
  6. Finish: add 1 tsp lemon juice per pita.
  7. Meal-prep: pack tuna mix separately and assemble at work for maximum crunch.
