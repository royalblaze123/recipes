Ingredients

Step-by-step Instructions

  1. Cook chicken: Pat the chicken breast dry. Season with 1 tsp kosher salt, 1/2 tsp black pepper, and 1 tsp garlic powder. Cook in a nonstick skillet over medium heat for 5–7 minutes per side until 165°F internal. Rest 5 minutes, then slice.
  2. Prep salad: Rinse and dry the spring mix. Slice the cucumber. Slice the red bell pepper.
  3. Make dressing: In a bowl, whisk 1/2 tub 2% plain Greek yogurt with 1/2 jar salsa and 2 tbsp olive oil until smooth.
  4. Assemble plates (per person): Divide spring mix evenly into 3 bowls. Top with sliced chicken, cucumber, and bell pepper. Drizzle dressing over each bowl and serve.
