Roasted Pepper & Tomato Turkey Skillet with Protein Pasta

Nutrition (approx)

Frank: 650 kcal • P 60g • C 70g • F 14g • Sat 3g • Na 700mg • Fiber 10g
Susan/Elise (each): 488 kcal • P 45g • C 52g • F 10g • Sat 2g • Na 525mg • Fiber 8g

Ingredients (purchase-clean)

  • 1 box Barilla Protein+ pasta
  • 1.50 lb 93% lean ground turkey
  • 1 jar roasted red peppers
  • 1 jar marinara sauce
  • 1 can no-salt-added diced tomatoes
  • kosher salt
  • black pepper
  • garlic powder
  • onion powder
  • smoked paprika
  • Italian seasoning
  • ground cumin
  • chili powder
  • red pepper flakes
  • extra-virgin olive oil
  • low-sodium chicken broth

Steps

  1. Turkey: brown turkey with black pepper, garlic powder, Italian seasoning, and a modest pinch of kosher salt 7–9 minutes.
  2. Sauce: add 2 cups marinara (measured) + 1 can diced tomatoes (14.5 oz). Simmer uncovered 8 minutes to thicken.
  3. Peppers: slice roasted red peppers; stir in and simmer 2 minutes.
  4. Pasta: cook Barilla Protein+ pasta per box; drain; toss into sauce.
  5. Serve per Notes.

Notes

Big Italian comfort with roasted peppers for sweetness (no added sugar). Portions (per person): Frank: About 2 1/2 cups (heaping bowl) Susan: About 2 cups (bowl) Elise: About 2 cups (bowl) Tips (recipe-specific): Simmer sauce uncovered so it clings to pasta. Roasted peppers add sweetness without sugar.
