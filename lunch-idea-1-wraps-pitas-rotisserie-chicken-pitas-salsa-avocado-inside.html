Rotisserie Chicken Pitas (Salsa + Avocado Inside)

Nutrition (approx)

Frank: 525 kcal • P 50g • C 50g • F 15g
Susan/Elise (each): 394 kcal • P 38g • C 38g • F 11g

Ingredients (purchase-clean)

  • 1/2 rotisserie chicken
  • 1 package whole-wheat pitas
  • 1 each avocado
  • 1/2 jar salsa
  • 1 bag shredded lettuce
  • 1 each cucumber
  • 1 bag mini sweet peppers
  • 4 tsp extra-virgin olive oil
  • 2 tsp chili powder
  • 2 tsp cumin
  • 2 tsp garlic powder
  • 1 tsp black pepper
  • 1 tsp kosher salt

Steps

  1. Chicken: pull meat from 1/2 rotisserie chicken and chop small. Warm in a skillet 2–3 minutes with 2 tsp olive oil.
  2. Season chicken with chili powder, cumin, garlic powder, black pepper, and kosher salt.
  3. Pitas: warm and split.
  4. Stuff: add shredded lettuce, then seasoned chicken.
  5. Avocado: halve, pit, and dice. Use about 1/2 avocado total.
  6. Salsa: spoon salsa inside each wrap/pita (not just on the side) so every bite has flavor.
  7. Veggie sticks: slice cucumber and mini sweet peppers into sticks for a crunchy side.
  8. Portion per Notes.

Notes

Fast stuffed pitas using rotisserie chicken; salsa + avocado inside. Portions (per person): Frank: 2 stuffed pitas (with salsa + avocado inside) + veggie sticks Susan: 1 stuffed pita + veggie sticks Elise: 1 stuffed pita + veggie sticks
