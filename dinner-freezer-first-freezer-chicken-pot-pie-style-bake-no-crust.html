Freezer Chicken Pot-Pie-Style Bake (No Crust)

Nutrition (approx)

Frank: 650 kcal • P 58g • C 65g • F 14g • Sat 3g • Na 700mg • Fiber 10g
Susan/Elise (each): 488 kcal • P 44g • C 49g • F 10g • Sat 2g • Na 525mg • Fiber 8g

Ingredients (purchase-clean)

  • 1.75 lb boneless skinless chicken breast
  • 1.50 lb baby potatoes
  • 1 bag frozen mixed vegetables
  • 1 carton skim milk
  • low-sodium chicken broth
  • cornstarch
  • kosher salt
  • black pepper
  • garlic powder
  • onion powder
  • smoked paprika
  • Italian seasoning
  • ground cumin
  • chili powder
  • red pepper flakes
  • extra-virgin olive oil
  • low-sodium chicken broth
  • cornstarch

Steps

  1. Oven: heat to 400°F.
  2. Potatoes: dice potatoes; microwave 6 minutes, then roast on a sheet pan with olive oil, black pepper, garlic powder, modest salt 18 minutes to brown.
  3. Chicken: dice chicken; sauté with olive oil, black pepper, garlic powder, onion powder 6–8 minutes.
  4. Sauce: add 2 cups low-sodium broth + 1 1/2 cups skim milk. Whisk 2 tbsp cornstarch into 1/2 cup cold water; stir in. Simmer 3–4 minutes until thick gravy texture.
  5. Veg: stir in 3 cups frozen mixed vegetables; simmer 3 minutes.
  6. Assemble: fold roasted potatoes into chicken gravy. Transfer to 9x13 dish.
  7. Bake 18–22 minutes until bubbling; rest 10 minutes.
  8. Freeze: cool fully; portion and freeze. Reheat covered 350°F 25–35 minutes or microwave.

Notes

Pot-pie comfort without crust—thick gravy base and browned potatoes hold up in the freezer. Portions (per person): Frank: About 2 1/2 cups (heaping scoop) Susan: About 2 cups (scoop) Elise: About 2 cups (scoop) Tips (recipe-specific): Brown potatoes first so the bake doesn’t taste ‘boiled.’ Thicken to gravy before baking so it reheats creamy without cream.
