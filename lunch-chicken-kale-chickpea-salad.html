Chicken Kale Chickpea Salad Box

NOTES: Designed as a portable grab-and-go lunch using lean protein, high-fiber vegetables, and controlled smart carbs. Use low-sodium ingredients and avoid added sugars where possible. PORTION SIZES: Frank: largest portion (~1/3 of the total volume); Susan: medium portion (~1/4 of the total volume); Elise: medium portion (~1/4 of the total volume) (Frank = 1.0×, Susan/Elise ≈0.75× portions).

Ingredients

  • 12 oz cooked boneless, skinless chicken breast, chopped
  • 1 can (15 oz) no-salt-added chickpeas, drained and rinsed
  • 4 cups finely shredded kale (stems removed)
  • 1 red bell pepper, diced
  • 1/2 small red onion, thinly sliced
  • 1/3 cup plain 2% Greek yogurt
  • 2 tbsp extra-virgin olive oil
  • 2 tbsp lemon juice
  • 1 tsp Dijon mustard (no added sugar)
  • 1/2 tsp garlic powder
  • Freshly ground black pepper, to taste

Step-by-Step Instructions

  1. Place shredded kale in a large bowl. Massage with clean hands for 1–2 minutes until slightly softened.
  2. Add chopped chicken, chickpeas, diced bell pepper, and sliced red onion to the bowl.
  3. In a small bowl, whisk together Greek yogurt, olive oil, lemon juice, Dijon mustard, garlic powder, and black pepper to form a creamy dressing.
  4. Pour dressing over the salad and toss thoroughly until everything is well coated.
  5. Divide the salad into three containers, giving Frank the largest portion for his higher calorie and protein needs.
  6. Refrigerate up to 3 days. Stir before eating.

Nutrition per Person

  • Frank: ~570 kcal, ~48 g protein, ~45 g carbs, ~18 g fat.
  • Susan: ~430 kcal, ~36 g protein, ~34 g carbs, ~13 g fat.
  • Elise: ~430 kcal, ~36 g protein, ~34 g carbs, ~13 g fat.
