Air Fryer Crispy Chicken Bites with Potatoes and Carrots

Crispy chicken bites with roasted potatoes and carrots—simple seasoning, big volume, and fully dual-basket timed.

Servings

3 servings.

Times

  • Prep time: 25 minutes
  • Cook time: 20 minutes
  • Total time: 45 minutes

Ingredients

Main Dish

  • 1 lb boneless skinless chicken breast
  • 2 each russet potatoes
  • 1 lb bag carrots
  • 1 bottle extra virgin olive oil
  • 1 container nonfat Greek yogurt
  • 1 jar garlic powder
  • 1 jar paprika
  • 1 jar black pepper
  • 1 jar cornstarch
  • 1 jar Italian seasoning
    • 

    Preparation Steps

    1. Prep potatoes: cut into wedges. Measure out about 3 cups wedges for this dinner; refrigerate remaining potato. Toss with 1 tbsp olive oil, paprika, and black pepper.
    2. Prep carrots: cut into 3-inch sticks. Measure out about 2 cups carrots for this dinner; refrigerate remaining carrots. Toss with 1 tsp olive oil and black pepper.
    3. Prep chicken: cut into 1-inch bites. Toss with 2 tsp olive oil, Italian seasoning, garlic powder, paprika, black pepper, and 1 tsp cornstarch (helps crisp).
    4. Cosori setup: preheat both baskets to 390°F for 3 minutes. Put potato wedges in Basket A. Put carrots in Basket B.
    5. Sync Finish: set Basket A to 390°F for 20 minutes (shake reminder ON). Set Basket B to 390°F for 14 minutes (shake reminder ON). Press Sync Finish.
    6. Shake reminders: shake/flip potatoes and carrots when prompted.
    7. Add chicken: when Basket B has 8 minutes left, push carrots to the side and add chicken bites. Cook until chicken reaches 165°F internal.
    8. Yogurt dip: stir 1/4 cup Greek yogurt with a pinch of garlic powder and 1 tsp water.
    9. Serve (40/30/30): plate chicken bites with potatoes and carrots and serve dip on the side.

      Notes

      Portions (per person):


      Frank – Chicken bites: largest share. Potato wedges: about 1 1/4 cups. Carrots: about 1 cup. Yogurt dip.


      Susan – Chicken bites: medium share. Potato wedges: about 1 cup. Carrots: about 3/4 cup. Yogurt dip.


      Elise – Chicken bites: medium share. Potato wedges: about 1 cup. Carrots: about 3/4 cup. Yogurt dip.


      Tips (recipe-specific):


      Cornstarch on chicken adds crisp without extra oil.


      Add chicken near the end so it stays juicy while carrots finish.


      If chicken is crowded, cook in two quick batches for better browning.
