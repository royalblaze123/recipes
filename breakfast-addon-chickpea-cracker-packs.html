Roasted Chickpea + Whole-Grain Cracker Packs (No-Cook Bulk)

Savory slow carbs with fiber: portioned roasted chickpeas and whole-grain crackers for zero-prep mornings.

Servings

14 packs.

Times

  • Prep time: 10 minutes
  • Cook time: 0 minutes
  • Total time: 10 minutes

Ingredients

Main Dish

  • 1 box whole-grain crackers
  • 1 bag roasted chickpeas
    • 

    Preparation Steps

    1. Build 14 packs: add a small handful of roasted chickpeas and a serving of whole-grain crackers to each bag/container.
    2. Keep packs at home and work as a reliable slow-carb add-on to your shake.
    3. Breakfast use: grab 1 pack and eat with your morning shake.
      4. 

      Notes

      Portions (per person):


      Frank – 1 pack with your morning shake (crackers + chickpeas).


      Susan – Optional: 1 pack with breakfast if desired.


      Elise – Optional: 1 pack with breakfast if desired.


      Tips (recipe-specific):


      Pick lower-sodium chickpeas when possible—sodium adds up quickly across the day.


      If lunch is early, reduce the chickpeas slightly and keep the crackers the same.


      If you get bloating, switch this pack to an afternoon snack instead of breakfast.

      Nutrition (per person)

      • Frank: 220 kcal; 8 g protein; 32 g carbs; 7 g fat; 7 g fiber; 260 mg sodium
      • Susan: 220 kcal; 8 g protein; 32 g carbs; 7 g fat; 7 g fiber; 260 mg sodium
      • Elise: 220 kcal; 8 g protein; 32 g carbs; 7 g fat; 7 g fiber; 260 mg sodium
