Taco Turkey Pizza + Romaine Tomato Salad

Thin-crust whole-wheat taco-style pizza with seasoned turkey, peppers, and a light yogurt drizzle, served with a romaine tomato salad.

Servings

3 servings (Frank, Susan, Elise).

Times

  • Prep time: 40 minutes
  • Cook time: 12 minutes
  • Total time: 52 minutes

Ingredients

Pizza

  • 1 dough ball whole wheat pizza dough
  • 1 can no-salt-added crushed tomatoes
  • 1 package reduced-fat mozzarella
  • 1 lb lean ground turkey
  • 1 each bell pepper
  • 1 each yellow onion
  • 1 cup nonfat Greek yogurt
  • 1 tsp chili powder
  • 1 tsp ground cumin
  • 1 tsp garlic powder
  • 1 tsp smoked paprika
  • 1 tsp ground black pepper

Side salad

  • 2 hearts romaine hearts
  • 1 pint cherry tomatoes
  • 2 tbsp extra-virgin olive oil
  • 2 tbsp red wine vinegar

Step-by-step Instructions

  1. Thaw and warm dough: Thaw 1 dough ball overnight in the refrigerator. Bring to room temperature 60–90 minutes.
  2. Preheat: Place a pizza stone or steel or inverted sheet pan in the oven and preheat to 500°F for 30 minutes.
  3. Cook turkey: Brown ground turkey in a skillet over medium heat. Season with chili powder, cumin, garlic powder, smoked paprika, and black pepper. Cook until no pink remains. Measure 14 oz cooked turkey for the pizza and refrigerate any extra.
  4. Make sauce: Measure 1 1/4 cups crushed tomatoes from the can into a bowl and stir in 1/2 tsp cumin and 1/2 tsp garlic powder. Refrigerate remaining tomatoes.
  5. Prep toppings: Slice 1/2 bell pepper and 1/4 onion for the pizza and set aside. Bag remaining produce for another meal.
  6. Stretch and top: Stretch dough into a thin 10–12 inch round on parchment. Spread sauce. Sprinkle 5 1/2 oz mozzarella. Add seasoned turkey, pepper, and onion.
  7. Bake: Slide pizza on parchment onto the hot surface. Bake 8–12 minutes until browned and bubbling.
  8. Yogurt drizzle: Stir 1/4 cup Greek yogurt with 1 tbsp water until drizzleable. Spoon lightly over the finished pizza.
  9. Side salad: Chop romaine and halve tomatoes. Dress with olive oil and vinegar. Portion per person.
  10. Portion dinner: Cut pizza into 10 slices and serve with salad.

Portion Sizes

Portions (per person):
Pizza (1 dough ball): cut into 10 slices.
Frank: 4 slices pizza + 1 generous bowl side salad

Susan: 3 slices pizza + 3/4 bowl side salad

Elise: 3 slices pizza + 3/4 bowl side salad
