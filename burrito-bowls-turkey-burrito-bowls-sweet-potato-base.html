Turkey Burrito Bowls (Sweet Potato Base)

Nutrition (approx)

Frank: 650 kcal • P 58g • C 52g • F 18g
Susan/Elise (each): 488 kcal • P 44g • C 39g • F 14g

Ingredients (purchase-clean)

  • 1.50 lb 93% lean ground turkey
  • 1 can black beans
  • 1.75 lb sweet potatoes
  • 1 bag frozen fajita-style pepper blend
  • 1 each avocado
  • 1/2 jar salsa
  • 1/2 tub 2% plain Greek yogurt
  • 1 each lime
  • 6 tsp extra-virgin olive oil
  • 2 tsp chili powder
  • 2 tsp cumin
  • 2 tsp garlic powder
  • 1 tsp smoked paprika
  • 1 tsp black pepper
  • 1 tsp kosher salt

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 425°F. Line a sheet pan with parchment.
  2. Sweet potatoes: cube into 3/4-inch pieces. Toss with 2 tsp olive oil and 1/2 tsp kosher salt. Roast 22–26 minutes, turning once, until browned and tender.
  3. Turkey: cook ground turkey in a nonstick skillet over medium-high 7–9 minutes until browned.
  4. Veg: cook frozen fajita pepper blend with 4 tsp olive oil 6–8 minutes until browned.
  5. Beans: can size note—use a standard 15 oz can black beans. Drain and rinse. Measure out 1 1/2 cups beans; refrigerate any remainder.
  6. Season: add spices to the turkey; stir 60 seconds. Add peppers and beans and warm 2 minutes. Finish with 2 tsp lime juice.
  7. Yogurt-salsa drizzle: stir 6 tbsp Greek yogurt with 6 tbsp salsa until smooth.
  8. Avocado: halve, pit, and dice. Use about 1/2 avocado total; refrigerate remaining avocado with the pit to slow browning.
  9. Build bowls: sweet potato base + turkey/pepper/bean mix + salsa-yogurt + avocado. Portion per Notes.

Notes

Sweet potato burrito bowls: hearty, potassium-rich base with turkey, beans, salsa, and avocado. Portions (per person): Frank: 1/3 of the sweet potatoes + 1/3 of the protein mix + 1/3 of the toppings (salsa + avocado + yogurt-salsa drizzle) Susan: 1/4 of the sweet potatoes + 1/4 of the protein mix + 1/4 of the toppings (salsa + avocado + yogurt-salsa drizzle) Elise: 1/4 of the sweet potatoes + 1/4 of the protein mix + 1/4 of the toppings (salsa + avocado + yogurt-salsa drizzle)
