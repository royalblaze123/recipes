Creamy Garlic-Parmesan Gravy Dip

Category: Sauce • Yield: About 1 3/4 cups (28 tbsp) • AnyList-ready

Ingredients

  • 1 carton low-sodium chicken broth
  • 3/4 cup 2% plain Greek yogurt
  • 1/2 cup 1% milk
  • 2 tsp extra-virgin olive oil
  • 6 tsp grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 tsp garlic powder
  • 1 tsp cornstarch
  • 1/4 tsp black pepper

Steps

  1. In a small saucepan over medium heat, whisk milk and olive oil. Warm 60 seconds.
  2. Whisk in 1 cup broth from the 32 fl oz carton (reserve remaining broth refrigerated) and garlic powder; bring to a gentle simmer.
  3. Whisk cornstarch with 2 tsp water until smooth; whisk into the pan and simmer 60–90 seconds until lightly thickened.
  4. Remove from heat and cool 2 minutes. Whisk in Greek yogurt, Parmesan, and black pepper until smooth. Do not boil after adding yogurt.
