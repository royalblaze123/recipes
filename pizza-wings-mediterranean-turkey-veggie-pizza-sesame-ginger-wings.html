Mediterranean Turkey Veggie Pizza with Sesame-Ginger Wings

Prep: 22 min   Cook: 32 min   Total: 54 min

Ingredients

  • 1 pizza dough ball
  • 0.5 jar low-sodium pizza sauce
  • 6 oz reduced-fat mozzarella cheese
  • 10 oz lean ground turkey 93%
  • 1 bell pepper
  • 1 onion
  • 1 jar roasted red peppers
  • 1 bag spinach
  • 2.25 lb chicken wings
  • 1 knob fresh ginger
  • 1 lime
  • olive oil
  • low-sodium soy sauce
  • sesame oil
  • black pepper
  • salt substitute
  • oregano

RBM rules: quantities appear before items; fresh meat/poultry/fish use weights. Condiments/spices/seasonings are listed name-only (no tsp/tbsp in Ingredients); all measurements are in Steps. Water is referenced in Steps only. No olives, no squash, no Brussels sprouts, no yogurt in dinners.

Step-by-step Instructions

  1. Cook turkey topping: brown ground turkey 7–9 minutes. Season with oregano, black pepper, and salt substitute. Set aside.
  2. Pizza: preheat oven to 475°F. Stretch dough into 12-inch round on parchment.
  3. Top pizza: spread ~1/2 jar pizza sauce, add mozzarella, then turkey, sliced bell pepper/onion, and a few drained roasted red pepper strips. Bake 10–12 minutes. Add spinach after baking to wilt. Cut into 10 slices.
  4. Wings: pat dry. Make glaze: mix 2 tbsp low-sodium soy sauce + 1 tsp sesame oil + 2 tsp grated ginger + 2 tsp lime juice + black pepper.
  5. Toss wings with 2 tsp olive oil + 2 tsp salt substitute + 1 tsp black pepper. Air fry 380°F for 24–26 minutes, then 400°F for 4–6 minutes to crisp.
  6. Toss hot wings with glaze lightly (coat, don’t drown).
  7. Portion: Frank 4 slices + 8 wings; Susan/Elise 3 slices + 6 wings.
