Chicken Pizza-Style Pasta Bake

NOTES: Built as a complete dinner using lean protein, high-fiber vegetables, and controlled starch portions. Use low-sodium broth and minimal added salt to support blood pressure and kidney health. PORTION SIZES: Frank: largest portion (~1/3 of pan); Susan: medium portion (~1/4 of pan); Elise: medium portion (~1/4 of pan).

Ingredients

  • 8 oz dry whole-wheat fusilli or rotini pasta
  • 1 lb boneless, skinless chicken breast, cut into bite-sized pieces
  • 1 tbsp extra-virgin olive oil
  • 1 medium yellow onion, diced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 red bell pepper, diced
  • 2 cups baby spinach, chopped
  • 1 can (14.5 oz) no-salt-added crushed tomatoes
  • 1/2 cup no-sugar-added tomato sauce (low sodium)
  • 1 tsp dried oregano
  • 1 tsp dried basil
  • 1/2 tsp garlic powder
  • 1/2 tsp kosher salt (or to taste)
  • Freshly ground black pepper, to taste
  • 1 oz turkey pepperoni, sliced or quartered
  • 1/2 cup shredded reduced-fat mozzarella cheese
  • Olive oil spray for baking dish

Step-by-Step Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 375°F. Lightly spray a 9x13-inch baking dish with olive oil spray.
  2. Cook whole-wheat pasta in a large pot of lightly salted water according to package directions until just al dente. Drain well and set aside.
  3. While pasta cooks, heat olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat.
  4. Add diced onion and cook 4–5 minutes until softened.
  5. Stir in minced garlic and cook 30–60 seconds until fragrant.
  6. Add chicken pieces, season with a small pinch of salt and pepper, and cook 6–8 minutes until just cooked through.
  7. Stir in diced red bell pepper and cook 2–3 minutes until slightly softened.
  8. Add crushed tomatoes, tomato sauce, oregano, basil, garlic powder, remaining salt, and black pepper. Simmer 5 minutes to thicken slightly.
  9. Fold in chopped spinach and cook 2–3 minutes until wilted.
  10. In a large mixing bowl, combine cooked pasta, chicken-vegetable sauce, and turkey pepperoni pieces. Toss until everything is evenly distributed.
  11. Transfer mixture to the prepared baking dish and spread into an even layer.
  12. Sprinkle reduced-fat mozzarella over the top.
  13. Bake uncovered 20–25 minutes until bubbly and cheese is melted and lightly browned.
  14. Let casserole rest 5–10 minutes before slicing into portions for Frank, Susan, and Elise.

Nutrition per Person

  • Frank: ~600 kcal, ~52 g protein, ~58 g carbs, ~17 g fat.
  • Susan: ~450 kcal, ~39 g protein, ~44 g carbs, ~13 g fat.
  • Elise: ~450 kcal, ~39 g protein, ~44 g carbs, ~13 g fat.
