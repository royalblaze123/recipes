Chicken Artichoke Spinach Pizza

NOTES: Built as a full dinner using ready-made whole-grain or thin crust, lean protein, plenty of vegetables, and controlled cheese to support A1c, cholesterol, and blood pressure goals. Use low-sodium sauce and minimal added salt. PORTION SIZES: Frank: ~40% of the pizza; Susan: ~30% of the pizza; Elise: ~30% of the pizza (Frank = 1.0×, Susan/Elise = 0.75× portions).

Ingredients

  • 1 ready-made 12-inch thin whole-wheat pizza crust
  • 1/2 cup plain 2% Greek yogurt
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • 8 oz cooked boneless, skinless chicken breast, chopped
  • 1 can (14 oz) artichoke hearts in water, drained and chopped
  • 1 1/2 cups fresh baby spinach, roughly chopped
  • 1/2 cup shredded reduced-fat mozzarella cheese
  • 2 tbsp grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 tsp dried oregano
  • Freshly ground black pepper, to taste

Step-by-Step Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 425°F and place the crust on a pizza pan or parchment-lined baking sheet.
  2. In a small bowl, mix Greek yogurt and minced garlic to form a simple white sauce.
  3. Spread yogurt sauce evenly over the crust.
  4. Distribute chopped chicken, chopped artichoke hearts, and chopped spinach evenly over the sauce.
  5. Sprinkle shredded mozzarella and grated Parmesan over the toppings.
  6. Season with dried oregano and a few grinds of black pepper.
  7. Bake 12–15 minutes, until the crust is crisp and the cheese is melted and lightly browned.
  8. Cool 3–5 minutes before slicing and portioning for Frank (~40%) and Susan/Elise (~30% each).

Nutrition per Person

  • Frank: ~600 kcal, ~53 g protein, ~48 g carbs, ~18 g fat.
  • Susan: ~450 kcal, ~40 g protein, ~36 g carbs, ~13 g fat.
  • Elise: ~450 kcal, ~40 g protein, ~36 g carbs, ~13 g fat.
