Bean-Forward Lunch: Chickpea ‘Tuna’ Smash Bowl

Prep: 16 min   Cook: 0 min   Total: 16 min

Ingredients

  • 0.5 can no-salt-added chickpeas
  • 2 cans tuna in water
  • 0.5 bag shredded cabbage
  • 1 cucumber
  • 1 bunch celery
  • 1 lemon
  • 1 clamshell microgreens
  • Dijon mustard
  • black pepper
  • salt substitute
  • vinegar

Rules enforced: quantities before items; fresh meat/poultry/fish use weights; packaged items use fractional containers when partial. Condiments/spices/seasonings have no measurements in Ingredients; all measurements are in Steps. No olives, no squash, no Brussels sprouts, no zucchini, no tofu/tempeh. Lunch validations included in Notes.

Step-by-step Instructions

  1. Chickpeas: drain and rinse 1/2 can chickpeas. Mash with a fork until chunky.
  2. Tuna: drain 2 cans tuna very well; fold into mashed chickpeas.
  3. Season: mix in Dijon (Frank 2 tsp; Susan 1.5 tsp; Elise 1.5 tsp) + 1 tsp vinegar + 1 tsp lemon juice total; pepper/salt substitute.
  4. Crunch base: shred 1/2 bag cabbage into bowls.
  5. Add diced cucumber and thin-sliced 4 celery stalks.
  6. Top with chickpea-tuna smash and microgreens.
  7. Meal-prep: keep smash separate until serving to preserve crunch.
