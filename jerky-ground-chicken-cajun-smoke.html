Jerky Gun Ground Chicken — Cajun Smoke (Health-Goal)

Servings

3 portions (Frank 1.0×, Susan 0.75×, Elise 0.75×).

Times

    Prep time: 20 minutes Cook time: 390 minutes Total time: 410 minutes

Ingredients

  • 1 lb 93% lean ground chicken
  • 1 large egg
  • 6 tsp old fashioned oats
  • 6 tsp no salt added tomato paste
  • 2 tsp apple cider vinegar
  • 1 tsp garlic powder
  • 1 tsp onion powder
  • 1 tsp black pepper
  • 2 tsp paprika
  • 1 tsp cayenne pepper
  • 2 tsp dried oregano
  • 1 tsp dried thyme
  • 1/2 tsp liquid smoke

Recipe

  1. Preheat oven to 300°F. Line 2 rimmed baking sheets with foil and set wire racks on top. Lightly coat racks with cooking spray. Set dehydrator to 165°F.
  2. In a bowl, mix 1 lb 93% lean ground chicken, 1 large egg (use the white; save yolk), 6 tsp old fashioned oats, 6 tsp no salt added tomato paste, 2 tsp apple cider vinegar, 1 tsp garlic powder, 1 tsp onion powder, 1 tsp black pepper, and 2 tsp paprika, 1 tsp cayenne pepper, 2 tsp dried oregano, and 1 tsp thyme until tacky (60–90 seconds).
  3. Cover and refrigerate 30–60 minutes.
  4. Extrude a test strip; adjust texture (chill 10–15 minutes if sticky/tearing; add 1–2 tsp water if crumbly).
  5. Extrude remaining mixture on racks in even strips, leaving space so strips do not touch.
  6. Oven lethality step: bake at 300°F until thickest strip reaches 165°F (about 12–18 minutes).
  7. Dehydrate at 165°F for 4–7 hours, rotating trays every 2 hours, until bend/tear test passes and strips cool without tackiness.
  8. Cool 30–60 minutes. Condition 12–24 hours; re-dry if condensation appears.
  9. Portion and store per Notes.

Notes

Lean chicken jerky with bold spice layering and a tomato concentrate to keep flavor high without heavy sodium.
