Turkey Cabbage Roll Bake

NOTES: Built as a complete dinner using lean protein, high-fiber vegetables, and controlled starch portions. Use low-sodium broth and minimal added salt to support blood pressure and kidney health. PORTION SIZES: Frank: largest portion (~1/3 of pan); Susan: medium portion (~1/4 of pan); Elise: medium portion (~1/4 of pan).

Ingredients

  • 1 lb 93% lean ground turkey
  • 1 tbsp extra-virgin olive oil
  • 1 medium yellow onion, diced
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 4 cups green cabbage, thinly sliced
  • 1 cup cooked brown rice
  • 1 can (14.5 oz) no-salt-added diced tomatoes
  • 1 can (8 oz) no-salt-added tomato sauce
  • 1 tsp dried oregano
  • 1 tsp dried basil
  • 1/2 tsp smoked paprika
  • 1/2 tsp kosher salt (or to taste)
  • Freshly ground black pepper, to taste
  • Olive oil spray for baking dish

Step-by-Step Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 375°F. Lightly spray a 9x13-inch baking dish with olive oil spray.
  2. Heat olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat.
  3. Add diced onion and cook 4–5 minutes until softened.
  4. Stir in minced garlic and cook 30–60 seconds until fragrant.
  5. Add ground turkey, breaking it into crumbles, and cook 6–8 minutes until no pink remains.
  6. Stir in sliced cabbage and cook 5–7 minutes, stirring frequently, until cabbage begins to soften and wilt.
  7. Add cooked brown rice, diced tomatoes with juices, tomato sauce, dried oregano, dried basil, smoked paprika, salt, and black pepper. Stir well and simmer 5 minutes to thicken slightly.
  8. Transfer mixture to the prepared baking dish and spread into an even layer.
  9. Cover tightly with foil and bake 25–30 minutes until cabbage is very tender and casserole is bubbling around the edges.
  10. Remove foil and bake an additional 5–10 minutes to lightly brown the top, if desired.
  11. Let casserole rest 10 minutes before cutting into portions for Frank, Susan, and Elise.

Nutrition per Person

  • Frank: ~540 kcal, ~46 g protein, ~50 g carbs, ~15 g fat.
  • Susan: ~405 kcal, ~35 g protein, ~38 g carbs, ~11 g fat.
  • Elise: ~405 kcal, ~35 g protein, ~38 g carbs, ~11 g fat.
