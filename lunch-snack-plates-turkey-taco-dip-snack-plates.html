Turkey Taco Dip Snack Plates

Nutrition (approx)

Frank: 535 kcal • P 52g • C 45g • F 18g
Susan/Elise (each): 401 kcal • P 39g • C 34g • F 14g

Ingredients (purchase-clean)

  • 1.00 lb 93% lean ground turkey
  • 1 tub plain nonfat Greek yogurt
  • 1/2 jar salsa
  • 1 each avocado
  • 1 bag mini sweet peppers
  • 1 each cucumber
  • 1 pint cherry tomatoes
  • 1 bag baby carrots
  • 1 bag baked tortilla chips
  • 1 each lime
  • 1 tsp black pepper
  • 1 tsp kosher salt
  • 2 tsp chili powder
  • 2 tsp cumin
  • 2 tsp garlic powder
  • 1 tsp smoked paprika

Steps

  1. Turkey: cook ground turkey 7–9 minutes until browned. Season with 2 tsp chili powder, 2 tsp cumin, 2 tsp garlic powder, and 1 tsp smoked paprika.
  2. Dip base: in a bowl, mix 1 cup plain nonfat Greek yogurt with 1/2 cup salsa.
  3. Add 2 tsp lime juice and a pinch of black pepper.
  4. Fold 1 1/2 cups warm turkey into the yogurt-salsa dip for a protein-forward taco dip.
  6. Stir until smooth. Chill 10 minutes if time allows.
  7. Veg prep: slice cucumber into half-moons. Halve cherry tomatoes. Slice mini sweet peppers into strips. Add baby carrots.
  8. Chips: count out 10 chips per Frank portion (use about 30 chips total for the batch; see Notes for scaling).
  9. Avocado: halve, pit, and slice. Use about 1/2 avocado total; refrigerate remaining avocado with the pit to slow browning.
  10. Plate: divide dip, veggies, chips, and avocado onto three plates. Portion per Notes.

Notes

High-protein snack plates with turkey taco dip, avocado, veggies, and measured chips. Portions (per person): Frank: 1 large snack plate + 10 chips (measured) + 1/3 avocado Susan: 1 medium snack plate + 10 chips (measured) × 0.75 + 1/4 avocado Elise: 1 medium snack plate + 10 chips (measured) × 0.75 + 1/4 avocado
