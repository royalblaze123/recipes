Tuna Verde Enchiladas + Roasted Peppers

Nutrition (approx)

Frank: 610 kcal • P 55g • C 34g • F 21g
Susan/Elise (each): 458 kcal • P 41g • C 26g • F 16g

Ingredients (purchase-clean)

  • 2 cans tuna
  • 1 package whole-grain small tortillas
  • 1 jar salsa verde
  • 1 tub 2% plain Greek yogurt
  • 1/2 bag reduced-fat shredded Mexican cheese blend
  • 2 each bell peppers
  • 1 each yellow onion
  • 4 tsp extra-virgin olive oil
  • 2 tsp garlic powder
  • 1 tsp black pepper
  • 1 tsp kosher salt

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 400°F. Lightly coat a 9x13-inch baking dish with nonstick spray.
  2. Veg side: slice bell peppers and yellow onion into strips. Toss with 4 tsp olive oil. Roast 15–18 minutes, stirring once, until browned at edges.
  3. Tuna: can size note—use two 5 oz cans tuna. Drain very well.
  4. Verde yogurt sauce: warm salsa verde over low heat 3–4 minutes. Remove from heat and whisk in 1/2 cup Greek yogurt until smooth.
  5. Tortillas: warm tortillas 15–20 seconds per side.
  6. Filling: mix drained tuna with 1 tsp garlic powder and 1/2 tsp kosher salt.
  7. Assembly: spread 1/2 cup verde yogurt sauce in the dish. Fill tortillas with tuna plus a pinch of cheese; roll and place seam-side down.
  8. Top: pour remaining verde yogurt sauce over enchiladas; sprinkle remaining cheese. Bake 12–14 minutes until bubbly.
  9. Portion: Frank 4 enchiladas; Susan 3; Elise 3. Serve with roasted peppers/onions.

Notes

Tuna verde enchiladas with yogurt to keep it creamy without heavy sauces. Portions (per person): Frank: 4 enchiladas (4 tortillas) + 1/3 of the side/veg Susan: 3 enchiladas (3 tortillas) + 1/4 of the side/veg Elise: 3 enchiladas (3 tortillas) + 1/4 of the side/veg
