Garlic Herb Chicken Spinach Pizza + Cucumber Salad

Thin-crust whole-wheat pizza with garlic-herb chicken, spinach, and roasted pepper, served with a crisp cucumber salad.

Servings

3 servings (Frank, Susan, Elise).

Times

  • Prep time: 30 minutes
  • Cook time: 12 minutes
  • Total time: 42 minutes

Ingredients

Pizza

  • 1 dough ball whole wheat pizza dough
  • 1 can no-salt-added crushed tomatoes
  • 1 package reduced-fat mozzarella
  • 1 lb boneless skinless chicken breast
  • 1 each bell pepper
  • 1 each yellow onion
  • 1 bag spinach
  • 1 tsp garlic powder
  • 1 tsp dried oregano
  • 1 tsp dried basil
  • 1 tsp ground black pepper

Side salad

  • 2 hearts romaine hearts
  • 1 each cucumber
  • 2 tbsp extra-virgin olive oil
  • 2 tbsp red wine vinegar

Step-by-step Instructions

  1. Thaw and warm dough: Thaw 1 dough ball overnight in the refrigerator. Bring to room temperature 60–90 minutes.
  2. Preheat: Place a pizza stone/steel or inverted sheet pan in the oven and preheat to 500°F for 30 minutes.
  3. Cook chicken: Season chicken with garlic powder, oregano, basil, and black pepper. Bake at 425°F until 165°F (about 15–18 minutes). Rest 5 minutes, then dice. Measure 9 oz cooked chicken for the pizza; refrigerate the rest for another meal.
  4. Make sauce: From the can of crushed tomatoes, measure 1 1/2 cups into a bowl and stir in 1/2 tsp garlic powder, 1/2 tsp oregano, and 1/2 tsp basil. Refrigerate remaining tomatoes.
  5. Prep veg: Slice 1/2 bell pepper and 1/4 onion for the pizza; bag and refrigerate the remainder. Measure 2 packed cups spinach for the pizza; reseal remaining spinach.
  6. Stretch and top: Stretch dough into a thin 10–12 inch round on parchment. Spread sauce thinly. Sprinkle 6 oz mozzarella (save remaining cheese). Add chicken, peppers, onion, and spinach.
  7. Bake: Slide pizza (on parchment) onto the hot surface. Bake 8–12 minutes until browned and bubbling.
  8. Side salad: Chop romaine and slice cucumber. Dress with olive oil and vinegar. Portion per person.
  9. Portion dinner: Cut pizza into 10 slices and serve with salad.

Portion Sizes

Portions (per person):
Pizza (1 dough ball): cut into 10 slices.
Frank: 4 slices pizza + 1 generous bowl side salad

Susan: 3 slices pizza + 3/4 bowl side salad

Elise: 3 slices pizza + 3/4 bowl side salad
