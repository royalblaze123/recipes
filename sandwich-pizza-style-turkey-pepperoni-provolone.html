Pizza-Style Turkey Pepperoni Sandwiches with Marinara Dip and Cucumber Side

Prep: 10 min   Cook: 6 min   Total: 16 min

Ingredients

  • 6 slices whole wheat bread
  • 12 slices deli turkey breast
  • 24 slices turkey pepperoni
  • 6 slices reduced-fat provolone
  • 1 jar marinara sauce
  • 1 cucumber
  • 1 bag microgreens
  • olive oil
  • garlic powder
  • black pepper
  • salt substitute
  • Italian seasoning

RBM Sandwich rules: 3 full sandwiches total (6 slices bread). Deli meats/cheeses listed as slice counts; slicing guidance lives in Steps. Condiments/spices are name-only in Ingredients; all measurements are in Steps. Produce is listed in shoppable units (fractional OK). Microgreens preferred.

Step-by-step Instructions

  1. Optional toast: brush outer bread lightly with olive oil (about 1 tsp total across all sandwiches). Toast in a skillet 2–3 minutes per side or air fry 360°F for 4 minutes, flipping once.
  2. Season the marinara dip: warm 1/2 cup marinara and stir in 1 tsp Italian seasoning + 1 tsp garlic powder + black pepper.
  3. Assemble 3 sandwiches: turkey, provolone, turkey pepperoni, microgreens. Close.
  4. Portioning: Frank gets 6 turkey slices + 10–12 pepperoni slices + 2 provolone slices. Susan and Elise each get 3 turkey slices + 6–7 pepperoni slices + 2 provolone slices (or 1 slice to lower fat).
  5. Cucumber side: slice cucumber into rounds. Frank gets ~1 1/2 cups cucumber with a pinch of salt substitute + pepper. Susan and Elise each get ~1 cup.
  6. Serve with marinara dip: Frank ~1/4 cup; Susan and Elise ~3 tbsp each.
