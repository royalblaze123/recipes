Buffalo Chicken Veggie Pizza (Mild)

NOTES: Built as a full dinner using ready-made whole-grain or thin crust, lean protein, plenty of vegetables, and controlled cheese to support A1c, cholesterol, and blood pressure goals. Use low-sodium sauce and minimal added salt. PORTION SIZES: Frank: ~40% of the pizza; Susan: ~30% of the pizza; Elise: ~30% of the pizza (Frank = 1.0×, Susan/Elise = 0.75× portions).

Ingredients

  • 1 ready-made 12-inch thin whole-wheat pizza crust
  • 2 tbsp mild hot sauce (no added sugar)
  • 1/4 cup no-salt-added tomato sauce
  • 1/4 cup plain 2% Greek yogurt
  • 8 oz cooked boneless, skinless chicken breast, chopped
  • 1/4 cup finely chopped celery
  • 1/2 red bell pepper, thinly sliced
  • 1/4 red onion, thinly sliced
  • 1/2 cup shredded reduced-fat mozzarella cheese
  • 2 tbsp crumbled reduced-fat blue cheese (optional)
  • Freshly ground black pepper, to taste

Step-by-Step Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 425°F and place the crust on a pizza pan or parchment-lined baking sheet.
  2. In a small bowl, whisk together mild hot sauce, tomato sauce, and Greek yogurt to form a creamy buffalo-style sauce.
  3. Spread sauce evenly over the crust.
  4. Scatter chopped chicken evenly over the sauce.
  5. Top with chopped celery, sliced red bell pepper, and red onion.
  6. Sprinkle shredded mozzarella over the top, then add crumbled blue cheese if using.
  7. Grind a little black pepper over the pizza.
  8. Bake 12–15 minutes, or until the crust is crisp and the cheese has melted and begun to brown.
  9. Cool 3–5 minutes before slicing and portioning for Frank (~40%) and Susan/Elise (~30% each).

Nutrition per Person

  • Frank: ~600 kcal, ~54 g protein, ~49 g carbs, ~18 g fat.
  • Susan: ~450 kcal, ~40 g protein, ~37 g carbs, ~13 g fat.
  • Elise: ~450 kcal, ~40 g protein, ~37 g carbs, ~13 g fat.
