Air Fryer Turkey Meatballs with Garlic Green Beans and Potato Bites

Crispy turkey meatballs, roasted potato bites, and garlicky green beans using the Cosori dual-basket Sync Cook workflow.

Servings

3 servings.

Times

  • Prep time: 25 minutes
  • Cook time: 20 minutes
  • Total time: 45 minutes

Ingredients

Main Dish

  • 1 lb ground turkey
  • 1 bag baby potatoes
  • 1 bag fresh green beans
  • 1 each yellow onion
  • 6 cloves garlic
  • 1 bottle extra virgin olive oil
  • 1 bag reduced fat shredded cheese
  • 1 container nonfat Greek yogurt
  • 1 jar Italian seasoning
  • 1 jar garlic powder
  • 1 jar black pepper
  • 1 jar paprika
  • 1 jar breadcrumbs
    • 

    Preparation Steps

    1. Prep (potatoes): halve baby potatoes, then cut into 3/4-inch bites. Measure out about 3 cups potato bites for this recipe; refrigerate remaining potatoes. Toss with 1 tbsp olive oil, garlic powder, paprika, and black pepper.
    2. Prep (meatballs): in a bowl, mix ground turkey with 1/3 cup breadcrumbs, 1 tbsp minced onion, 1 minced garlic clove, Italian seasoning, and black pepper. Form 15 meatballs (about golf-ball size).
    3. Cosori setup: preheat both baskets to 390°F for 3 minutes.
    4. Basket A (potatoes): add potato bites in a single layer. Cook 390°F for 18–20 minutes with shake reminder ON; shake at each reminder.
    5. Basket B (meatballs): add meatballs with space between. Cook 390°F for 10 minutes, then flip/roll meatballs and cook 6–8 minutes more until 165°F internal.
    6. Green beans quick finish: trim green beans. Toss 1/2 bag green beans with 1 tsp olive oil and 1 minced garlic clove. When meatballs finish, cook green beans in Basket B at 390°F for 6–7 minutes, shaking once.
    7. Yogurt dip: stir 1/4 cup nonfat Greek yogurt with 1 tsp water, a pinch of garlic powder, and black pepper.
    8. Serve (40/30/30): plate meatballs with potato bites and green beans. Sprinkle 3/4 oz reduced-fat cheese total across all plates (optional) and serve yogurt dip on the side.

      Notes

      Portions (per person):


      Frank – Meatballs: 6 pieces. Potatoes: about 1 1/4 cups. Green beans: about 1 1/2 cups. Yogurt dip + light cheese sprinkle.


      Susan – Meatballs: 4–5 pieces. Potatoes: about 1 cup. Green beans: about 1 1/4 cups. Yogurt dip + light cheese sprinkle.


      Elise – Meatballs: 4–5 pieces. Potatoes: about 1 cup. Green beans: about 1 1/4 cups. Yogurt dip + light cheese sprinkle.


      Tips (recipe-specific):


      Keep meatballs evenly sized so they finish together and stay juicy.


      Run potatoes in Basket A the full time and use Basket B as a quick swap for green beans at the end.


      If potatoes brown too fast, drop Basket A to 375°F for the last 5 minutes.
