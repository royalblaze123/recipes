Oven BBQ Chicken Drumsticks + Sweet Potatoes + Slaw

Nutrition (approx)

Frank: 650 kcal • P 55g • C 55g • F 22g
Susan/Elise (each): 488 kcal • P 41g • C 41g • F 16g

Ingredients (purchase-clean)

  • 2.50 lb chicken drumsticks
  • low-sugar BBQ sauce
  • 2.00 lb sweet potatoes
  • 1 bag coleslaw mix
  • apple cider vinegar
  • kosher salt
  • black pepper
  • garlic powder
  • onion powder
  • smoked paprika
  • Italian seasoning
  • ground cumin
  • chili powder
  • red pepper flakes
  • extra-virgin olive oil
  • low-sodium chicken broth
  • low-sodium beef broth
  • low-sodium soy sauce
  • hot sauce
  • Dijon mustard
  • balsamic vinegar
  • apple cider vinegar
  • cornstarch

Steps (oven)

  1. Oven: heat to 425°F. Line pan with rack.
  2. Drumsticks: pat dry; season garlic powder, smoked paprika, black pepper, modest salt. Bake 425°F 25 minutes.
  3. Glaze: brush with BBQ sauce (5 tbsp measured total). Bake 10–12 minutes more to 175°F.
  4. Sweet potatoes: roast cubes 425°F 22–26 minutes with olive oil, garlic powder, smoked paprika, modest salt, black pepper.
  5. Slaw: coleslaw mix + vinegar (2 tbsp) + Dijon (2 tsp) + olive oil (2 tsp) + black pepper + small pinch salt.
  6. Serve per Notes.

Notes

Oven drumsticks with measured BBQ and crisp vinegar slaw—holiday party-friendly as a dinner plate. Portions (per person): Frank: 2 drumsticks + 1 cup sweet potatoes + 1 cup slaw Susan: 2 drumsticks + 3/4 cup sweet potatoes + 3/4 cup slaw Elise: 2 drumsticks + 3/4 cup sweet potatoes + 3/4 cup slaw Tips (recipe-specific): Bake on a rack for better skin texture. Measure BBQ; brush late so it doesn’t burn.
