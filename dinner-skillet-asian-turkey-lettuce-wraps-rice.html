Asian Turkey Lettuce Wraps + Rice

Nutrition (approx)

Frank: 640 kcal • P 58g • C 70g • F 14g
Susan/Elise (each): 480 kcal • P 44g • C 52g • F 10g

Ingredients (purchase-clean)

  • 1.50 lb 93% lean ground turkey
  • 1 head butter lettuce
  • 1 each carrot
  • 1 each bell pepper
  • low-sodium soy sauce
  • 1 cup dry brown rice
  • low-sodium chicken broth
  • kosher salt
  • black pepper
  • garlic powder
  • onion powder
  • smoked paprika
  • Italian seasoning
  • ground cumin
  • chili powder
  • red pepper flakes
  • extra-virgin olive oil
  • low-sodium chicken broth
  • low-sodium beef broth
  • low-sodium soy sauce
  • hot sauce
  • Dijon mustard
  • balsamic vinegar
  • apple cider vinegar
  • cornstarch

Steps (stovetop)

  1. Cook rice: 1 cup dry brown rice, 35–40 minutes.
  2. Turkey: brown turkey 8–10 minutes; add garlic powder, onion powder, black pepper, modest salt.
  3. Sauce: whisk soy sauce (5 tbsp) + apple cider vinegar (1 tbsp) + hot sauce (2 tsp) + broth (1/4 cup). Stir in 2 minutes to glaze.
  4. Veg: add shredded carrot + diced bell pepper; cook 2–3 minutes (tender-crisp).
  5. Serve: spoon turkey mix into lettuce cups; serve rice on the side per Notes.

Notes

Lettuce wraps that actually feel like dinner because the plate includes rice. Portions (per person): Frank: 7 oz turkey mix + 1 cup cooked rice + 4 lettuce cups Susan: 5 oz turkey mix + 3/4 cup rice + 4 lettuce cups Elise: 5 oz turkey mix + 3/4 cup rice + 4 lettuce cups Tips (recipe-specific): Keep veg tender-crisp for texture. Glaze sauce with broth so you need less soy overall.
