Creamy Dill Pickle Dip (Low-Sodium Style)

Category: Dip • Yield: About 1 cup (15 tbsp) • AnyList-ready

Ingredients

  • 3/4 cup 2% plain Greek yogurt
  • 1/4 cup chopped dill pickles
  • 2 tsp pickle brine
  • 2 tsp extra-virgin olive oil
  • 1/2 tsp garlic powder
  • 1/4 tsp black pepper

Steps

  1. Finely chop pickles to measure 1/4 cup.
  2. Whisk Greek yogurt, pickle brine, olive oil, garlic powder, and black pepper until smooth.
  3. Fold in chopped pickles. Chill 10 minutes before serving.
