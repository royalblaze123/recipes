Roasted Pork Tenderloin + Potatoes + Green Beans

Nutrition (approx)

Frank: 640 kcal • P 58g • C 60g • F 16g
Susan/Elise (each): 480 kcal • P 44g • C 45g • F 12g

Ingredients (purchase-clean)

  • 1.75 lb pork tenderloin
  • 1.50 lb baby potatoes
  • 1 bag frozen green beans
  • low-sodium chicken broth
  • kosher salt
  • black pepper
  • garlic powder
  • onion powder
  • smoked paprika
  • Italian seasoning
  • ground cumin
  • chili powder
  • red pepper flakes
  • extra-virgin olive oil
  • low-sodium chicken broth
  • low-sodium beef broth
  • low-sodium soy sauce
  • hot sauce
  • Dijon mustard
  • balsamic vinegar
  • apple cider vinegar
  • cornstarch

Steps (oven)

  1. Oven: heat to 425°F.
  2. Potatoes: halve; toss with olive oil, garlic powder, black pepper, modest salt. Roast 425°F 22–26 minutes.
  3. Pork: pat dry; season garlic powder, onion powder, smoked paprika, black pepper, modest salt. Roast 425°F 18–22 minutes to 145°F. Rest 5–7 minutes then slice.
  4. Green beans: microwave 4–5 minutes; drain/pat dry; toss with olive oil, black pepper, modest salt; roast 425°F 8–10 minutes.
  5. Pan drizzle: warm 1/4 cup broth in a small pan with 1 tsp Dijon; spoon over pork if desired.
  6. Serve per Notes.

Notes

Classic roast plate with minimal fuss; broth + Dijon makes a quick ‘pan sauce’ without butter. Portions (per person): Frank: 7 oz pork + 1 cup potatoes + 1 1/2 cups green beans Susan: 5 oz protein + 3/4 cup starch + 1 cup veg Elise: 5 oz protein + 3/4 cup starch + 1 cup veg Tips (recipe-specific): Rest pork before slicing so it stays juicy. Drain green beans well so they roast, not steam.
