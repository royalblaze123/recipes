Turkey-Black Bean Taco Soup

Nutrition (approx)

Frank: 640 kcal • P 60g • C 42g • F 21g
Susan/Elise (each): 480 kcal • P 45g • C 32g • F 16 g

Ingredients (purchase-clean)

  • 1.50 lb 93% lean ground turkey
  • 1 can black beans
  • 1 can fire-roasted diced tomatoes
  • 1 carton low-sodium chicken broth
  • 1 each avocado
  • 1/2 jar salsa
  • 1 each lime
  • 2 tsp extra-virgin olive oil
  • 2 tsp chili powder
  • 2 tsp cumin
  • 2 tsp garlic powder
  • 1 tsp smoked paprika
  • 1 tsp black pepper
  • 1 tsp kosher salt

Steps

  1. Turkey: brown ground turkey in a large pot over medium-high 7–9 minutes until cooked through.
  2. Season: stir in chili powder, cumin, garlic powder, smoked paprika, black pepper, and kosher salt for 60 seconds.
  3. Tomatoes: Can size note—use a standard 14.5 oz can fire-roasted diced tomatoes.. Add the full can (with juices).
  4. Beans: Can size note—use a standard 15 oz can black beans.. Drain and rinse. Add 1 1/2 cups beans; refrigerate any remainder.
  5. Broth: carton size note—use a 32 oz carton low-sodium chicken broth. Add 3 cups broth; refrigerate remainder.
  6. Simmer: bring to a gentle simmer and cook 12–15 minutes.
  7. Acid: add 2 tsp lime juice.
  8. Finish: taste and adjust seasoning with additional black pepper if needed.
  9. Toppings: dice avocado and serve salsa on the side. Top each bowl with measured avocado + salsa. Portion per Notes.

Notes

Classic taco soup: turkey + black beans in a tomato broth, finished with avocado and salsa. Portions (per person): Frank: 1/3 of the pot + 1/3 of the avocado + 1/3 of the salsa Susan: 1/4 of the pot + 1/4 of the avocado + 1/4 of the salsa Elise: 1/4 of the pot + 1/4 of the avocado + 1/4 of the salsa
