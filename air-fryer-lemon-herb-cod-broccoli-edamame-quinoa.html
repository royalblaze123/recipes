Air Fryer Lemon-Herb Cod with Broccoli, Edamame, and Quinoa

Crisp-edged cod with a gentle lemon-herb glaze, served over quinoa with broccoli and edamame.

Servings

3 servings (Frank, Susan, Elise).

Times

  • Prep time: 15 minutes
  • Cook time: 20 minutes
  • Total time: 35 minutes

Ingredients (purchase-clean)

  • 1 1/2 lb fresh cod fillets
  • 6 tsp olive oil
  • 4 tsp lemon juice
  • 3 tsp Dijon mustard
  • 3 tsp garlic paste
  • 3 tsp Italian seasoning
  • 2 tsp paprika
  • 1 tsp onion powder
  • 1 tsp black pepper
  • 2/3 cup dry quinoa
  • 1/2 bag frozen broccoli florets
  • 1/2 bag frozen shelled edamame

Preparation Steps

  1. Cook quinoa: rinse 2/3 cup dry quinoa, then simmer it in 1 1/3 cups water (in a covered pot) for 15 minutes; remove from heat and steam 5 minutes, then fluff.
  2. Prep air fryer: preheat air fryer to 400°F for 3 minutes; cut 1 1/2 lb cod into 3 fillet portions (8 oz for Frank, 6 oz for Susan, 6 oz for Elise) and pat dry.
  3. Make glaze: whisk 6 tsp olive oil, 4 tsp lemon juice, 3 tsp Dijon mustard, 3 tsp garlic paste, 3 tsp Italian seasoning, 2 tsp paprika, 1 tsp onion powder, and 1 tsp black pepper.
  4. Air fry cod: brush cod with half of the glaze (use about 8 tsp of glaze), then air fry at 400°F for 8–10 minutes until flakes easily and the thickest part reaches 145°F.
  5. Cook broccoli: microwave 1/2 bag frozen broccoli florets with 2 tbsp water in a covered bowl for 4–6 minutes, then toss with 1 tsp of the glaze; reseal and freeze the remaining broccoli if any.
  6. Cook edamame: microwave 1/2 bag frozen shelled edamame with 2 tbsp water in a covered bowl for 3–4 minutes, then toss with 1 tsp of the glaze; reseal and freeze any remaining edamame.
  7. Assemble bowls: portion cooked quinoa, broccoli, and edamame into 3 bowls, top with cod, and drizzle the remaining glaze evenly (about 4 tsp total).

Notes

Portions (per person):

Frank — 8 oz cod; 3/4 cup cooked quinoa; 1 1/2 cups broccoli; 3/4 cup edamame.

 

Susan — 6 oz cod; 2/3 cup cooked quinoa; 1 1/4 cups broccoli; 1/2 cup edamame.

 

Elise — 6 oz cod; 2/3 cup cooked quinoa; 1 1/4 cups broccoli; 1/2 cup edamame.

 

Tips (recipe-specific):

 

Don’t overcook cod: pull it as soon as it flakes and hits 145°F.

 

Glaze control: if you prefer less tang, use only 3 tsp lemon juice and add 1 tsp water in the glaze (water goes in Steps only).
