Chicken Veggie Alfredo Pizza

NOTES: Built as a full dinner using ready-made whole-grain or thin crust, lean protein, plenty of vegetables, and controlled cheese to support A1c, cholesterol, and blood pressure goals. Use low-sodium sauce and minimal added salt. PORTION SIZES: Frank: ~40% of the pizza; Susan: ~30% of the pizza; Elise: ~30% of the pizza (Frank = 1.0×, Susan/Elise = 0.75× portions).

Ingredients

  • 1 ready-made 12-inch thin whole-wheat pizza crust
  • 3/4 cup plain 2% Greek yogurt
  • 2 tbsp grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • 1–2 tbsp water or unsweetened almond milk, as needed to thin sauce
  • 8 oz cooked boneless, skinless chicken breast, chopped
  • 1 cup small broccoli florets, lightly steamed
  • 1/2 red bell pepper, thinly sliced
  • 1/4 red onion, thinly sliced
  • 1/2 cup shredded reduced-fat mozzarella cheese
  • 1 tsp Italian seasoning
  • Olive oil spray
  • Freshly ground black pepper, to taste

Step-by-Step Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 425°F. Place the ready-made whole-wheat crust on a pizza pan or parchment-lined baking sheet.
  2. In a small bowl, stir together Greek yogurt, grated Parmesan, minced garlic, and a splash of water or almond milk until it forms a spreadable Alfredo-style sauce.
  3. Lightly spray the crust with olive oil, then spread the yogurt Alfredo sauce evenly over the surface, leaving a small border for the crust.
  4. Scatter chopped cooked chicken evenly over the sauce.
  5. Distribute lightly steamed broccoli florets, sliced red bell pepper, and red onion evenly over the pizza.
  6. Sprinkle shredded reduced-fat mozzarella and Italian seasoning over the top, then add a few grinds of black pepper.
  7. Bake 12–15 minutes, or until the crust is crisp at the edges and the cheese is melted and lightly golden.
  8. Let pizza cool 3–5 minutes, then slice into 6–8 slices and portion for Frank (~40%), Susan, and Elise (each ~30%).

Nutrition per Person

  • Frank: ~610 kcal, ~55 g protein, ~52 g carbs, ~18 g fat.
  • Susan: ~455 kcal, ~41 g protein, ~39 g carbs, ~13 g fat.
  • Elise: ~455 kcal, ~41 g protein, ~39 g carbs, ~13 g fat.
