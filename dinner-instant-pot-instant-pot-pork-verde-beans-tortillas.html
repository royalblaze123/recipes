Instant Pot Pork Verde + Beans + Tortillas

Nutrition (approx)

Frank: 650 kcal • P 58g • C 60g • F 18g
Susan/Elise (each): 488 kcal • P 44g • C 45g • F 14g

Ingredients (purchase-clean)

  • 1.75 lb pork tenderloin
  • 1 jar salsa verde
  • 1 can black beans
  • 3 each whole-wheat tortillas
  • 1 each lime
  • low-sodium chicken broth
  • kosher salt
  • black pepper
  • garlic powder
  • onion powder
  • smoked paprika
  • Italian seasoning
  • ground cumin
  • chili powder
  • red pepper flakes
  • extra-virgin olive oil
  • low-sodium chicken broth
  • low-sodium beef broth
  • low-sodium soy sauce
  • hot sauce
  • Dijon mustard
  • balsamic vinegar
  • apple cider vinegar
  • cornstarch

Steps (Instant Pot)

  1. Load: add pork + broth (1/2 cup) + salsa verde (1 cup measured). Season cumin, garlic powder, black pepper, modest salt.
  2. Pressure cook: High 12 minutes; NR 10 then Quick Release.
  3. Shred pork; Sauté 3 minutes to reduce.
  4. Beans: warm drained/rinsed black beans (15 oz; use 1 1/2 cups) with salsa verde (1/2 cup measured) 2 minutes.
  5. Tortillas: warm on skillet.
  6. Finish with lime; serve per Notes.

Notes

Verde pork that stays juicy, plus beans and tortillas for a full plate. Portions (per person): Frank: 3 tacos + 1/2 cup beans Susan: 3 tacos + 1/3 cup beans Elise: 3 tacos + 1/3 cup beans Tips (recipe-specific): Reduce on Sauté for a thick, clingy verde finish. Warm beans separately so they keep bite.
