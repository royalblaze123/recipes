Egg & Greek Yogurt Salad Sandwich + Cucumber Slices (RBM Lunch)

Creamy egg salad made with Greek yogurt on whole-grain bread, plus fresh cucumber slices on the side.

Servings

3 servings.

Ingredients

Sandwich

  • 5 whole eggs
  • 5 large egg whites
  • 6 slices whole-grain sandwich bread
  • 6 tbsp nonfat plain Greek yogurt
  • 3 tsp light mayonnaise
  • 3 tbsp fresh celery
  • 1/8 tsp kosher or sea salt
  • 1/4 tsp ground black pepper

Side

  • 7.5 oz fresh cucumber, sliced

Step-by-Step Instructions

  1. Place the whole eggs in a small saucepan and cover with cold water. Bring to a gentle boil over medium-high heat, then turn off the heat, cover, and let sit for 10–12 minutes.
  2. Drain the hot water and run cold water over the eggs until cool enough to handle. Peel all the eggs.
  3. Chop the peeled whole eggs into small pieces and transfer them to a mixing bowl.
  4. While the eggs cool, heat a small nonstick skillet over medium heat. Add the egg whites (from a carton or separated), stirring frequently, and cook until the whites are fully set and no liquid remains. Let the cooked egg whites cool slightly, then chop them into small pieces and add to the mixing bowl with the chopped eggs.
  5. Add the Greek yogurt, light mayonnaise, diced celery, salt, and pepper to the bowl. Stir until everything is well combined into an egg salad.
  6. Lay out the whole-grain bread slices. Spoon the egg salad over three slices, dividing the total amount into Frank, Susan, and Elise portions as listed below.
  7. Top each with a second slice of bread and press gently. Cut in half if desired.
  8. Slice the cucumber and divide into three small containers or bags based on the side portion sizes listed below.
  9. Serve immediately, or chill the egg salad sandwiches and cucumber slices in the refrigerator until ready to pack for lunch.

Portion Sizes (Per Person)

Frank: 2 slices whole-grain bread, salad made from 2 whole eggs + 2 egg whites, 2 tbsp Greek yogurt, 1 tsp light mayo, 1 tbsp diced celery, 3 oz cucumber slices.

 

Susan: 2 slices whole-grain bread, salad made from 1 1/2 whole eggs + 1 1/2 egg whites, 1 1/2 tbsp Greek yogurt, 1 tsp light mayo, 2 tsp diced celery, 2.5 oz cucumber slices.

 

Elise: 2 slices whole-grain bread, salad made from 1 whole egg + 1 egg white, 1 1/2 tbsp Greek yogurt, 1 tsp light mayo, 2 tsp diced celery, 2 oz cucumber slices.

Nutrition (Per Person)

  • Frank: 410 kcal, 36 g protein, 33 g carbs, 13 g fat, 5 g fiber, 600 mg sodium
  • Susan: 380 kcal, 33 g protein, 30 g carbs, 12 g fat, 4.5 g fiber, 560 mg sodium
  • Elise: 350 kcal, 30 g protein, 28 g carbs, 11 g fat, 4 g fiber, 520 mg sodium
