Chicken Broccoli Protein Mac (Classic)

Nutrition (approx)

Frank: 645 kcal • P 60g • C 60g • F 16g • Sat 6g • Na 690mg • Fiber 10g
Susan/Elise (each): 484 kcal • P 45g • C 45g • F 12g • Sat 4g • Na 518mg • Fiber 8g

Ingredients (purchase-clean)

  • 1 box Barilla Protein+ pasta
  • 1.75 lb boneless skinless chicken breast
  • 1 bag frozen broccoli florets
  • 1 carton skim milk
  • 1 container reduced-fat shredded cheddar cheese
  • 2 oz grated Parmesan cheese
  • kosher salt
  • black pepper
  • garlic powder
  • onion powder
  • smoked paprika
  • Italian seasoning
  • chili powder
  • ground cumin
  • crushed red pepper
  • cornstarch
  • extra-virgin olive oil
  • Dijon mustard
  • hot sauce
  • Worcestershire sauce
  • low-sodium soy sauce
  • rice vinegar

Steps

  1. Pasta: cook Barilla Protein+ pasta al dente (about 8–10 minutes). Drain and set aside.
  2. Chicken: dice 1.75 lb chicken breast. Sauté in a large pot with 2 tsp olive oil, 1 tsp garlic powder, 1 tsp onion powder, 1/2 tsp kosher salt, 1/2 tsp black pepper for 8–10 minutes to 165°F.
  3. Broccoli: add 3 cups frozen broccoli to the pot with 1/4 cup water. Cover 4 minutes, then uncover and cook 2 minutes so water evaporates.
  4. Cheese sauce base: whisk 2 tbsp cornstarch into 2 cups skim milk in a bowl until smooth.
  5. Sauce: pour milk slurry into pot. Simmer 3–5 minutes, stirring, until thick.
  6. Cheese: turn heat low. Stir in 6 oz reduced-fat cheddar + 2 oz Parmesan until melted.
  7. Combine: fold in cooked pasta. If too thick, add 2–4 tbsp milk.
  8. Optional bake: transfer to a dish and bake at 425°F for 8 minutes to set top.
  9. Serve per Notes.

Notes

Mac & cheese comfort with chicken + broccoli to keep it a full plate. Portions (per person): Frank: About 2 1/2 cups mac (1/3 pan) + extra veg Susan: About 2 cups mac (1/4 pan) + extra veg Elise: About 2 cups mac (1/4 pan) + extra veg Tips (recipe-specific): Thicken the milk first, then melt cheese—prevents grainy sauce. Dry off excess broccoli water so the sauce stays thick.
