Bean-Forward Lunch: White Bean Turkey Chop Bowl with Microgreens

Prep: 16 min   Cook: 0 min   Total: 16 min

Ingredients

  • 12 slices deli turkey
  • 0.5 can no-salt-added cannellini beans
  • 0.5 bag shredded cabbage
  • 1 cucumber
  • 1 pint cherry tomatoes
  • 1 lemon
  • 1 clamshell microgreens
  • Dijon mustard
  • black pepper
  • salt substitute
  • vinegar
  • olive oil

Rules enforced: quantities before items; fresh meat/poultry/fish use weights; packaged items use fractional containers when partial. Condiments/spices/seasonings have no measurements in Ingredients; all measurements are in Steps. No olives, no squash, no Brussels sprouts, no zucchini, no tofu/tempeh. Lunch validations included in Notes.

Step-by-step Instructions

  1. Beans: drain and rinse 1/2 can cannellini beans; measure 3/4 cup for bowls; refrigerate remainder.
  2. Base: combine 1/2 bag shredded cabbage + diced 1 cucumber + halved 1 pint cherry tomatoes.
  3. Turkey: cut 12 slices deli turkey into ribbons and add to base.
  4. Dressing: whisk 2 tsp olive oil + 2 tsp vinegar + 2 tsp lemon juice + Dijon measured (Frank 2 tsp; Susan 1.5 tsp; Elise 1.5 tsp) + pepper/salt substitute.
  5. Toss base with beans and dressing.
  6. Top with microgreens (divide evenly).
  7. Portion: Frank largest bowl; Susan/Elise ~75%.
