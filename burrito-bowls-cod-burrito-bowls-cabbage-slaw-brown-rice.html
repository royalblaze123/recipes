Cod Burrito Bowls + Cabbage Slaw (Brown Rice)

Nutrition (approx)

Frank: 650 kcal • P 58g • C 50g • F 20g
Susan/Elise (each): 488 kcal • P 44g • C 38g • F 15g

Ingredients (purchase-clean)

  • 1.50 lb cod fillets
  • 1 can black beans
  • 1 bag shredded cabbage slaw mix
  • 1 bag frozen fajita-style pepper blend
  • 1 cup dry brown rice
  • 1 each avocado
  • 1/2 jar salsa
  • 1/2 tub 2% plain Greek yogurt
  • 1 each lime
  • 6 tsp extra-virgin olive oil
  • 2 tsp chili powder
  • 2 tsp cumin
  • 2 tsp garlic powder
  • 1 tsp smoked paprika
  • 1 tsp black pepper
  • 1 tsp kosher salt

Steps

  1. Rice: cook brown rice using your preferred method. For this recipe, plan to cook 1 cup dry brown rice (yields about 3 cups cooked). Use about 2 1/2 cups cooked for the bowls; refrigerate any remainder.
  2. Fish: pat cod dry. Rub with 2 tsp olive oil and season with the spices.
  3. Bake fish: place on a parchment-lined sheet pan and bake at 425°F for 10–12 minutes until 145°F and flaky. Rest 3 minutes, then break into flakes.
  4. Veg: cook frozen fajita pepper blend in a skillet with 4 tsp olive oil 6–8 minutes until browned at edges.
  5. Beans: can size note—use a standard 15 oz can black beans. Drain and rinse. Measure out 1 1/2 cups beans; refrigerate any remainder.
  6. Slaw: toss 2 cups cabbage mix with 2 tsp lime juice and a pinch of black pepper.
  7. Yogurt-salsa drizzle: stir 6 tbsp Greek yogurt with 6 tbsp salsa until smooth.
  8. Avocado: halve, pit, and dice. Use about 1/2 avocado total; refrigerate remaining avocado with the pit to slow browning.
  9. Build bowls: rice + peppers/beans + cod + slaw + salsa-yogurt + avocado. Portion per Notes.

Notes

Fish burrito bowls with a crunchy cabbage slaw and the full salsa + avocado finish. Portions (per person): Frank: 1/3 of the brown rice + 1/3 of the protein mix + 1/3 of the toppings (salsa + avocado + yogurt-salsa drizzle) Susan: 1/4 of the brown rice + 1/4 of the protein mix + 1/4 of the toppings (salsa + avocado + yogurt-salsa drizzle) Elise: 1/4 of the brown rice + 1/4 of the protein mix + 1/4 of the toppings (salsa + avocado + yogurt-salsa drizzle)
