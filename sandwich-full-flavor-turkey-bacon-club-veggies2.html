Full-Flavor Turkey Bacon Club + Veggie Sticks (RBM Lunch, Occasional)

Full-flavor but portion-controlled turkey bacon club sandwich on whole-grain bread with veggie sticks on the side (tagged as Full-Flavor / Occasional).

Servings

3 servings.

Ingredients

Sandwich

  • 6 slices whole-grain sandwich bread
  • 16 slices deli turkey
  • 5
  • 3 tbsp light mayonnaise
  • 3 leaves lettuce
  • 1 small tomato

Side

  • 4 oz fresh baby carrots, cut into sticks if needed
  • 3.5 oz fresh celery sticks

Step-by-Step Instructions

  1. Cook the turkey bacon in a nonstick skillet over medium heat until crisp, then transfer to a plate lined with paper towels to drain excess fat.
  2. Lay out the whole-grain bread slices on a cutting board.
  3. Spread light mayonnaise on one side of each slice of bread.
  4. On three of the slices, layer the lower-sodium deli turkey breast (portioning according to the amounts listed below), cooked turkey bacon strips, a lettuce leaf, and tomato slices.
  5. Top each sandwich with a second slice of bread, mayo side down, and press gently. Cut in half if desired.
  6. Arrange the baby carrots and celery sticks and divide into three small containers or bags based on the side portion sizes listed below so everyone has a crunchy veggie side.
  7. Serve right away or wrap sandwiches tightly and refrigerate. This sandwich is labeled Full-Flavor / Occasional due to bacon and slightly higher sodium, but it remains portion-controlled and within the RBM lunch band for Frank.

Portion Sizes (Per Person)

Frank: 2 slices whole-grain bread, 3 oz lower-sodium deli turkey breast, 2 strips cooked turkey bacon, 1 tbsp light mayo, 1 leaf lettuce, 2 tomato slices, 3 oz mixed veggie sticks (carrot and celery).

 

Susan: 2 slices whole-grain bread, 2.5 oz lower-sodium deli turkey breast, 1 1/2 strips cooked turkey bacon, 2 tsp light mayo, 1 leaf lettuce, 2 tomato slices, 2.5 oz mixed veggie sticks.

 

Elise: 2 slices whole-grain bread, 2 oz lower-sodium deli turkey breast, 1 strip cooked turkey bacon, 2 tsp light mayo, 1 leaf lettuce, 1–2 tomato slices, 2 oz mixed veggie sticks.

Nutrition (Per Person)

  • Frank: 425 kcal, 38 g protein, 34 g carbs, 13 g fat, 6 g fiber, 750 mg sodium
  • Susan: 395 kcal, 35 g protein, 31 g carbs, 12 g fat, 5.5 g fiber, 700 mg sodium
  • Elise: 365 kcal, 32 g protein, 29 g carbs, 11 g fat, 5 g fiber, 650 mg sodium
