Greek-Style Turkey Meatballs + Lemon Veg

Nutrition (approx)

Frank: 620 kcal • P 60g • C 40g • F 18g • Sat 6g • Na 670mg • Fiber 10g
Susan/Elise (each): 465 kcal • P 45g • C 30g • F 14g • Sat 4g • Na 502mg • Fiber 8g

Ingredients (purchase-clean)

  • 1.75 lb 93% lean ground turkey
  • 1 package whole-wheat breadcrumbs
  • 2 each eggs
  • 1 each lemon
  • 1 bag frozen broccoli florets
  • 1 bag frozen cauliflower rice
  • kosher salt
  • black pepper
  • garlic powder
  • onion powder
  • Italian seasoning
  • smoked paprika
  • chili powder
  • ground cumin
  • crushed red pepper
  • extra-virgin olive oil
  • dried oregano

Steps

  1. Oven: heat to 425°F. Line a sheet pan and lightly oil.
  2. Meatballs: mix turkey with 1/2 cup breadcrumbs, 2 eggs, 2 tsp garlic powder, 1 tsp onion powder, 2 tsp dried oregano, 1/2 tsp kosher salt, 1/2 tsp black pepper.
  3. Form 18–20 meatballs; bake 12–14 minutes to 165°F.
  4. Broccoli: roast frozen broccoli florets at 425°F with 2 tsp olive oil for 16–20 minutes, stirring once.
  5. Cauli rice: cook 1 bag cauliflower rice, then dry in skillet 3 minutes.
  6. Lemon finish: add 2 tsp lemon juice to broccoli and 2 tsp lemon juice to cauliflower rice. Add black pepper.
  7. Serve per Notes.

Notes

Bright, savory meatball comfort that doesn’t feel heavy. Portions (per person): Frank: 8–9 meatballs + 1 cup broccoli + 1 1/2 cups cauli rice Susan: 6–7 meatballs + 3/4 cup broccoli + 1 1/4 cups cauli rice Elise: 6–7 meatballs + 3/4 cup broccoli + 1 1/4 cups cauli rice Tips (recipe-specific): Lemon at the end keeps it bright and cuts richness. Dry cauliflower rice so it stays fluffy, not wet.
