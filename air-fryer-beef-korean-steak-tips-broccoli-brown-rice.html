Air Fryer Korean-Style Steak Tips with Roasted Broccoli and Quick Brown Rice

Prep: 15 min   Cook: 20 min   Total: 35 min

Ingredients

  • 1.5 lb sirloin steak
  • 1 bag frozen broccoli florets
  • 1 pouch microwave brown rice
  • 1 lime
  • 1 knob fresh ginger
  • 1 bulb garlic
  • olive oil
  • low-sodium soy sauce
  • gochujang
  • rice vinegar
  • sesame oil
  • sesame seeds
  • black pepper
  • salt substitute

RBM rules: quantities appear before items; fresh proteins use weight. Condiments/spices/seasonings are listed name-only; all measurements are in Steps. Water is referenced in Steps only.

Step-by-step Instructions

  1. Make marinade/glaze: whisk 10 tsp low-sodium soy sauce + 6 tsp rice vinegar + 4 tsp sesame oil + 2–3 tsp gochujang + 2 tsp grated ginger + 2 minced garlic cloves + 1 tsp black pepper.
  2. Cut sirloin into 1-inch tips. Toss with marinade; rest 10 minutes. Reserve 2 tbsp of the marinade to brush at the end.
  3. Roast broccoli: toss frozen broccoli with 8 tsp olive oil + 2 tsp salt substitute + 1 tsp black pepper. Oven roast at 425°F for 16–20 minutes, tossing once, until browned (or air fry 390°F for 10–12 minutes, shaking once).
  4. Air fry steak tips (Cosori): preheat 400°F for 3 minutes. Cook 7–9 minutes, shaking once, until browned and medium. Brush with reserved marinade and sprinkle with 2 tsp sesame seeds; rest 3 minutes.
  5. Heat brown rice pouch per package directions. Finish rice with 4 tsp lime juice + 1 tsp black pepper.
  6. Portion: Frank gets 6 oz cooked beef + ~3/4 cup cooked rice + ~1 3/4 cups broccoli. Susan and Elise each get ~4.5 oz beef + ~1/2 cup rice + ~1 1/2 cups broccoli.
