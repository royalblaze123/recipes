Air Fryer Italian Turkey Meatballs with Whole-Wheat Couscous and Green Beans

Juicy air-fried turkey meatballs with a gentle Italian profile, served with whole-wheat couscous and green beans.

Servings

3 servings (Frank, Susan, Elise).

Times

  • Prep time: 20 minutes
  • Cook time: 15 minutes
  • Total time: 35 minutes

Ingredients (purchase-clean)

  • 1 1/4 lb 93% lean ground turkey
  • 1/3 carton liquid egg whites
  • 6 tsp grated Parmesan
  • 4 tsp garlic paste
  • 3 tsp Italian seasoning
  • 2 tsp onion powder
  • 1 tsp paprika
  • 2 tsp black pepper
  • 6 tsp olive oil
  • 3/4 cup dry whole-wheat couscous
  • 1/2 bag frozen green beans

Preparation Steps

  1. Mix meatballs: in a bowl combine 1 1/4 lb 93% lean ground turkey, 1/3 carton liquid egg whites (use 3/4 cup), 6 tsp grated Parmesan, 4 tsp garlic paste, 3 tsp Italian seasoning, 2 tsp onion powder, 1 tsp paprika, and 1 1/2 tsp black pepper.
  2. Form meatballs: shape into 18 meatballs (about 1 1/2 tbsp each).
  3. Air fry meatballs: preheat air fryer to 390°F for 3 minutes; lightly brush basket with 1 tsp olive oil (from the 6 tsp total); air fry meatballs at 390°F for 10–12 minutes, shaking at 6 minutes, until 165°F inside.
  4. Cook couscous: bring 1 cup water to a boil, stir in 3/4 cup dry whole-wheat couscous and 2 tsp olive oil, cover, remove from heat for 5 minutes, then fluff.
  5. Cook green beans: microwave 1/2 bag frozen green beans with 2 tbsp water for 4–5 minutes; toss with 3 tsp olive oil and 1/2 tsp black pepper (from the 2 tsp total).
  6. Portion: divide meatballs, couscous, and green beans per the Portions notes; refrigerate leftover egg whites in the carton.

Notes

Portions (per person):

Frank — 8 meatballs; 2/3 cup cooked couscous; 1 1/2 cups green beans.

 

Susan — 5 meatballs; 1/2 cup cooked couscous; 1 1/4 cups green beans.

 

Elise — 5 meatballs; 1/2 cup cooked couscous; 1 1/4 cups green beans.

 

Tips (recipe-specific):

 

Meatball texture: egg whites keep them tender; don’t pack the meatballs tightly.

 

Batching: cook in two air-fryer rounds if needed so meatballs brown instead of steaming.

  • Susan: 495 kcal; 13 g fat; 460 mg sodium; 27 g carbs; 5 g fiber; 45 g protein.
  • Elise: 495 kcal; 13 g fat; 460 mg sodium; 27 g carbs; 5 g fiber; 45 g protein.
