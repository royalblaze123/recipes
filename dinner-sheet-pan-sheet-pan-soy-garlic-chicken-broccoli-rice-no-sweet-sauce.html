Sheet-Pan Soy-Garlic Chicken + Broccoli + Rice (No Sweet Sauce)

Nutrition (approx)

Frank: 650 kcal • P 60g • C 70g • F 14g • Sat 3g • Na 700mg • Fiber 10g
Susan/Elise (each): 488 kcal • P 45g • C 52g • F 10g • Sat 2g • Na 525mg • Fiber 8g

Ingredients (purchase-clean)

  • 1.75 lb boneless skinless chicken breast
  • 1 bag frozen broccoli florets
  • 1 cup dry brown rice
  • kosher salt
  • black pepper
  • garlic powder
  • onion powder
  • smoked paprika
  • Italian seasoning
  • ground cumin
  • chili powder
  • red pepper flakes
  • extra-virgin olive oil
  • Dijon mustard
  • balsamic vinegar
  • apple cider vinegar
  • low-sodium soy sauce
  • cornstarch
  • hot sauce

Steps

  1. Rice: cook 1 cup dry brown rice; rest 5 minutes.
  2. Oven: heat to 450°F. Line sheet pan.
  3. Chicken: cut into chunks. Toss with 2 tsp olive oil, 2 tsp low-sodium soy sauce, garlic powder, black pepper, 1/2 tsp kosher salt. Roast 12 minutes.
  4. Broccoli: microwave; drain/pat dry. Toss with 2 tsp olive oil, garlic powder, 1/2 tsp kosher salt, black pepper. Add to pan; roast 10 minutes.
  5. Sauce (gloss): whisk 5 tbsp low-sodium soy sauce, 3/4 cup water, and 1 tbsp cornstarch; simmer 2–3 minutes until glossy. Use 6 tbsp sauce total, measured, tossed over chicken and broccoli.
  6. Serve with rice per Notes.

Notes

Takeout feel without sugary teriyaki: a measured glossy soy sauce does it. Portions (per person): Frank: 7 oz chicken + 1 cup cooked rice + 1 1/2 cups broccoli Susan: 5 oz protein + 3/4 cup starch + 1 cup veg Elise: 5 oz protein + 3/4 cup starch + 1 cup veg Tips (recipe-specific): Thicken sauce so you need less soy for the same effect. Pat broccoli dry before roasting for real browning.
