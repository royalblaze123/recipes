Taco Soup (Thick, Not Watery)

Nutrition (approx)

Frank: 625 kcal • P 60g • C 50g • F 16g • Sat 4g • Na 690mg • Fiber 13g
Susan/Elise (each): 469 kcal • P 45g • C 38g • F 12g • Sat 3g • Na 518mg • Fiber 10g

Ingredients (purchase-clean)

  • 1.75 lb 93% lean ground turkey
  • 1 can black beans
  • 1 can corn
  • 1 can no-salt-added diced tomatoes
  • 1 can enchilada sauce
  • 1 each onion
  • extra-virgin olive oil
  • kosher salt
  • black pepper
  • chili powder
  • ground cumin
  • garlic powder
  • onion powder
  • smoked paprika
  • Italian seasoning
  • crushed red pepper
  • bay leaves

Steps

  1. Pot: heat 2 tsp olive oil. Cook 1 diced onion 6–8 minutes.
  2. Add turkey; cook 7–9 minutes until browned.
  3. Season with chili powder, cumin, garlic powder, onion powder, smoked paprika, kosher salt, black pepper.
  4. Tomatoes: Can size note—use a standard 14.5 oz can no-salt-added diced tomatoes.. Add 1 can.
  5. Beans: Can size note—use a standard 15 oz can black beans.. Drain/rinse and add.
  6. Corn: Can size note—use a standard 15 oz can corn.. Drain and add 1 1/2 cups; refrigerate remainder.
  7. Enchilada sauce: Can size note—use a standard 10 oz can enchilada sauce.. Add 1 cup; refrigerate remainder.
  8. Simmer uncovered 15–18 minutes until thick and scoopable.
  9. Portion per Notes.

Notes

Taco soup that eats like chili. Portions (per person): Frank: About 2 1/2 cups soup Susan: About 2 cups soup Elise: About 2 cups soup Tips (recipe-specific): Go light on enchilada sauce (measured) and let spices carry flavor. Uncovered simmer concentrates it into a chili texture.
