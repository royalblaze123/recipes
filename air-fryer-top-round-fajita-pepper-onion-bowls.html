Air Fryer Top Round Fajita Bowls with Peppers and Onions

Lean top round fajita strips with peppers and onions over brown rice, finished with a yogurt-lime crema.

Servings

3 servings.

Times

  • Prep time: 25 minutes
  • Cook time: 16 minutes
  • Total time: 41 minutes

Ingredients

Main Dish

  • 1 lb top round steak
  • 1 bag brown rice
  • 2 each bell peppers
  • 1 each yellow onion
  • 1 bottle extra virgin olive oil
  • 1 container nonfat Greek yogurt
  • 1 each lime
  • 1 jar chili powder
  • 1 jar ground cumin
  • 1 jar garlic powder
  • 1 jar black pepper
    • 

    Preparation Steps

    1. Rice: cook 1 cup dry brown rice. Measure out 2 cups cooked rice for this dinner; refrigerate remaining rice.
    2. Prep veg: slice 1 bell pepper and 1/2 onion (store remaining). Toss with 1 tsp olive oil and black pepper.
    3. Prep beef: slice top round steak into thin strips across the grain. Toss with 2 tsp olive oil, chili powder, cumin, garlic powder, and black pepper.
    4. Cosori setup: preheat both baskets to 400°F for 3 minutes.
    5. Basket A (veg): cook peppers/onion 400°F for 8 minutes, shaking once.
    6. Basket B (beef): cook beef strips 400°F for 6–8 minutes, shaking once, until browned and cooked through. Rest 3 minutes.
    7. Crema: stir 1/4 cup Greek yogurt with 1 tsp lime juice, a pinch of cumin, and 1 tsp water.
    8. Serve (40/30/30): build bowls with brown rice, peppers/onion, beef strips, and a light crema drizzle.

      Notes

      Portions (per person):


      Frank – Rice: about 1/2 cup cooked. Beef + veg: largest share. Crema drizzle.


      Susan – Rice: about 3/8 cup cooked. Beef + veg: medium share. Crema drizzle.


      Elise – Rice: about 3/8 cup cooked. Beef + veg: medium share. Crema drizzle.


      Tips (recipe-specific):


      Slice beef thin across the grain so it stays tender.


      If beef is crowding Basket B, cook in two quick batches for better browning.


      Keep the crema light—just enough to add creaminess without pushing calories.
