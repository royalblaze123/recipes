Chicken Broccoli Rice Bake

Nutrition (approx)

Frank: 630 kcal • P 58g • C 55g • F 18g • Sat 6g • Na 680mg • Fiber 12g
Susan/Elise (each): 472 kcal • P 44g • C 41g • F 14g • Sat 4g • Na 510mg • Fiber 9g

Ingredients (purchase-clean)

  • 1.75 lb boneless skinless chicken breast
  • 1 bag frozen broccoli florets
  • 3/4 cup dry brown rice
  • 1 carton low-sodium chicken broth
  • 1 tub low-fat cottage cheese
  • 6 oz reduced-fat shredded mozzarella cheese
  • kosher salt
  • black pepper
  • garlic powder
  • onion powder
  • smoked paprika
  • chili powder
  • ground cumin
  • Italian seasoning
  • extra-virgin olive oil
  • Dijon mustard

Steps

  1. Oven: heat to 400°F. Lightly oil a 9x13 (or similar) baking dish.
  2. Rice: cook 3/4 cup dry brown rice with 1 1/2 cups broth (or water) until tender (about 35–40 minutes). Rest 5 minutes, fluff.
  3. Chicken: cut into bite-size pieces. Sauté in a skillet with 2 tsp olive oil 8–10 minutes until 165°F.
  4. Season chicken with 2 tsp garlic powder, 1 tsp onion powder, 1 tsp smoked paprika, 1/2 tsp kosher salt, and 1/2 tsp black pepper.
  5. Broccoli: microwave 1 bag frozen broccoli florets until just hot (about 4–6 minutes). Drain well and pat dry.
  6. Mix: in a bowl, stir 1 cup low-fat cottage cheese with 1 tsp Dijon and 1/4 cup broth until creamy.
  7. Assemble: add cooked rice to baking dish. Top with chicken, then broccoli. Dollop cottage mixture over the top and spread lightly. Sprinkle 6 oz reduced-fat mozzarella evenly.
  8. Bake: 15–18 minutes until bubbly. Broil 1–2 minutes for light browning (watch closely). Rest 5 minutes.
  9. Portion per Notes.

Notes

Creamy, cozy chicken-and-broccoli casserole without heavy cream. Portions (per person): Frank: About 1/3 of pan (a heaping plate) Susan: About 1/4 of pan Elise: About 1/4 of pan Tips (recipe-specific): Pat broccoli dry so the casserole stays thick, not watery. Cottage cheese gives creaminess with less saturated fat than cream sauces.
