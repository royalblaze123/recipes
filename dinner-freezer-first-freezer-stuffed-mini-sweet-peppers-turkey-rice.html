Freezer Stuffed Mini Sweet Peppers (Turkey + Rice)

Nutrition (approx)

Frank: 640 kcal • P 58g • C 60g • F 16g • Sat 5g • Na 700mg • Fiber 12g
Susan/Elise (each): 480 kcal • P 44g • C 45g • F 12g • Sat 4g • Na 525mg • Fiber 9g

Ingredients (purchase-clean)

  • 1.75 lb 93% lean ground turkey
  • 1 bag mini sweet peppers
  • 1 cup dry brown rice
  • 1 jar salsa
  • 6 oz reduced-fat shredded mozzarella cheese
  • kosher salt
  • black pepper
  • garlic powder
  • onion powder
  • smoked paprika
  • Italian seasoning
  • ground cumin
  • chili powder
  • red pepper flakes
  • extra-virgin olive oil
  • low-sodium chicken broth
  • cornstarch

Steps

  1. Rice: cook 1 cup dry brown rice with 2 cups water 35–40 minutes; cool 10 minutes.
  2. Oven: heat to 400°F.
  3. Peppers: halve mini sweet peppers lengthwise; remove seeds; place in a baking dish.
  4. Filling: brown turkey with seasonings (chili powder, cumin, garlic powder, black pepper, modest salt) 7–9 minutes.
  5. Mix: stir turkey with 2 cups cooked rice and 1 cup salsa (measured).
  6. Stuff: fill pepper halves; top with mozzarella (measured).
  7. Bake 18–22 minutes until peppers are tender and cheese melts.
  8. Freeze: cool fully; freeze stuffed peppers in a single layer, then bag. Reheat at 350°F 18–25 minutes or microwave.

Notes

Stuffed peppers that freeze well and reheat without drying out (salsa keeps them moist). Portions (per person): Frank: 6 pepper halves Susan: 5 pepper halves Elise: 5 pepper halves Tips (recipe-specific): Freeze on a sheet first so peppers don’t stick together. Salsa in the filling prevents freezer ‘dryness’.
