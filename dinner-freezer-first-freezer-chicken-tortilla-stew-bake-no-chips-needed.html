Freezer Chicken Tortilla Stew Bake (No Chips Needed)

Nutrition (approx)

Frank: 650 kcal • P 60g • C 60g • F 18g • Sat 6g • Na 700mg • Fiber 12g
Susan/Elise (each): 488 kcal • P 45g • C 45g • F 14g • Sat 4g • Na 525mg • Fiber 9g

Ingredients (purchase-clean)

  • 1.75 lb boneless skinless chicken breast
  • 1 can black beans
  • 1 jar salsa
  • 2 cans no-salt-added diced tomatoes
  • 3 each whole-wheat tortillas
  • 6 oz reduced-fat shredded mozzarella cheese
  • low-sodium chicken broth
  • kosher salt
  • black pepper
  • garlic powder
  • onion powder
  • smoked paprika
  • Italian seasoning
  • ground cumin
  • chili powder
  • red pepper flakes
  • extra-virgin olive oil
  • low-sodium chicken broth
  • cornstarch

Steps

  1. Oven: heat to 400°F. Lightly oil a 9x13 baking dish.
  2. Chicken: dice chicken; sauté with olive oil and seasonings (chili powder, cumin, garlic powder, black pepper, modest salt) 6–8 minutes.
  3. Sauce: stir in salsa (1 1/2 cups measured), diced tomatoes (2 cans, 14.5 oz each), and 1 cup low-sodium broth. Simmer 8 minutes to thicken.
  4. Beans: drain/rinse black beans (15 oz can); stir in 1 1/2 cups beans.
  5. Tortillas: cut tortillas into strips. Fold 2 tortillas worth of strips into the mixture to soak and thicken.
  6. Build: pour into dish; top with remaining tortilla strips and 6 oz mozzarella (measured).
  7. Bake 22–26 minutes until bubbling; rest 10 minutes to set.
  8. Freeze option: cool fully, portion into containers, and freeze. Reheat covered at 350°F for 25–35 minutes or microwave until hot.

Notes

Casserole-style tortilla comfort built to freeze and reheat without chips getting soggy. Portions (per person): Frank: About 2 1/2 cups (heaping scoop) Susan: About 2 cups (scoop) Elise: About 2 cups (scoop) Tips (recipe-specific): Rest 10 minutes so portions cut cleanly for freezer prep. Use tortillas inside the bake for body instead of crunchy chips.
