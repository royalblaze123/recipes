Protein Bowl: Garlic Shrimp with Brown Rice, Edamame, and Broccoli Slaw

Prep: 18 min   Cook: 18 min   Total: 36 min

Ingredients

  • 1.25 lb shrimp
  • 0.5 cup dry brown rice
  • 0.5 bag frozen shelled edamame
  • 0.5 bag broccoli slaw
  • 1 lime
  • olive oil
  • low-sodium soy sauce
  • rice vinegar
  • sesame oil
  • garlic
  • ginger
  • black pepper
  • salt substitute
  • cornstarch

Rules enforced: Clean AnyList Ingredients (quantities before items; fresh meat/poultry/fish use weights; packaged items use fractional containers when partial). Condiments/spices/seasonings have no measurements in Ingredients; all measurements are in Steps. No olives, no squash, no Brussels sprouts, no zucchini, no tofu/tempeh. Lunch validations: Frank calories 500–550, sat fat ≤6g, sodium ≤650mg, fiber ≥8g, added sugar ≤6g.

Step-by-step Instructions

  1. Cook rice: cook 1/2 cup dry brown rice with 1 cup water; simmer 35–40 minutes; rest 5 minutes.
  2. Edamame: microwave 1/2 bag edamame 3–4 minutes; measure 1 cup cooked for bowls; refreeze remainder.
  3. Slaw: toss 1/2 bag broccoli slaw with 2 tsp lime juice + 1 tsp rice vinegar + 1 tsp olive oil + 1/4 tsp black pepper + 1/4 tsp salt substitute.
  4. Sauce: whisk 2 tbsp low-sodium soy sauce + 1 tbsp rice vinegar + 1 tsp sesame oil + 1 tsp cornstarch + 2 tbsp water + 1 tsp minced garlic + 1 tsp grated ginger.
  5. Shrimp: pat dry 1.25 lb shrimp. Sauté with 2 tsp olive oil over medium-high 3 minutes.
  6. Pour sauce over shrimp; toss 1–2 minutes until thickened and glossy.
  7. Build bowls: rice + shrimp + edamame + slaw. Portion: Frank largest rice/shrimp share; Susan/Elise ~75%.
