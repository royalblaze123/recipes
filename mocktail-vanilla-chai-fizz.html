Vanilla Chai Fizz

Yield: 1 drink • Prep: 5 min • Total: 5 min

Ingredients (AnyList-ready)

  • 4 oz brewed chai tea
  • 1 oz simple syrup
  • 2 oz milk
  • 3 oz club soda
  • 1 cup ice

Steps

  1. Chill the brewed chai tea.
  2. Fill a glass with 1 cup ice.
  3. Add chai tea, simple syrup, and milk; stir 10 seconds.
  4. Top with 3 oz club soda.
  5. Stir once, gently.

Notes

If using a frothy milk, stir gently to avoid overflow when adding soda.

Tip: Use plenty of ice and a quick, gentle stir to keep carbonation lively.
