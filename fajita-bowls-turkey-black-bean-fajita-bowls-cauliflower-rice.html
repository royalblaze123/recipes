Turkey-Black Bean Fajita Bowls (Cauliflower Rice)

Nutrition (approx)

Frank: 645 kcal • P 60g • C 44g • F 21g
Susan/Elise (each): 484 kcal • P 45g • C 33g • F 16g

Ingredients (purchase-clean)

  • 1.50 lb 93% lean ground turkey
  • 2 each bell peppers
  • 1 each yellow onion
  • 1 can black beans
  • 1 bag frozen cauliflower rice
  • 1 each avocado
  • 1/2 jar salsa
  • 1/2 tub 2% plain Greek yogurt
  • 4 tsp extra-virgin olive oil
  • 1 each lime
  • 2 tsp chili powder
  • 2 tsp cumin
  • 2 tsp garlic powder
  • 1 tsp smoked paprika
  • 1 tsp black pepper
  • 1 tsp kosher salt

Steps

  1. Heat a large nonstick skillet over medium-high.
  2. Turkey: add ground turkey and cook 7–9 minutes, breaking up, until browned and cooked through.
  3. Beans: can size note—use a standard 15 oz can black beans. Drain and rinse. Measure out 1 1/2 cups beans; refrigerate any remainder.
  4. Veg: slice bell peppers and onion into thin strips. Cook in the same skillet with 4 tsp olive oil 6–8 minutes until tender-crisp and browned at edges.
  5. Season: add chili powder, cumin, garlic powder, smoked paprika, black pepper, and kosher salt; stir 60 seconds. Add turkey and beans back to the skillet and simmer 2 minutes.
  6. Cauliflower rice: microwave the frozen cauliflower rice per bag directions. Drain any excess moisture. Season with a pinch of black pepper and a squeeze of lime if using.
  7. Yogurt crema: stir 6 tbsp Greek yogurt with 2 tsp lime juice and 1 tsp lime zest until smooth.
  8. Avocado: halve, pit, and dice. For this recipe, plan on using about 1/2 avocado total; refrigerate the remaining avocado with the pit to slow browning.
  9. Build bowls and portion per Notes.

Notes

Turkey + black beans for extra fiber and satiety, finished with salsa and avocado. Portions (per person): Frank: 1/3 of the cauliflower rice + 1/3 of the fajita protein/veg + 1/3 of salsa + avocado + yogurt Susan: 1/4 of the cauliflower rice + 1/4 of the fajita protein/veg + 1/4 of salsa + avocado + yogurt Elise: 1/4 of the cauliflower rice + 1/4 of the fajita protein/veg + 1/4 of salsa + avocado + yogurt
