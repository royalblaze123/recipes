Chicken Fajita Rice Bake (Casserole-Style)

Nutrition (approx)

Frank: 650 kcal • P 60g • C 70g • F 14g
Susan/Elise (each): 488 kcal • P 45g • C 52g • F 10g

Ingredients (purchase-clean)

  • 1.75 lb boneless skinless chicken breast
  • 1 cup dry brown rice
  • 2 each bell pepper
  • 1 each onion
  • 1 jar salsa
  • low-sodium chicken broth
  • kosher salt
  • black pepper
  • garlic powder
  • onion powder
  • smoked paprika
  • Italian seasoning
  • ground cumin
  • chili powder
  • red pepper flakes
  • extra-virgin olive oil
  • low-sodium chicken broth
  • low-sodium beef broth
  • low-sodium soy sauce
  • hot sauce
  • Dijon mustard
  • balsamic vinegar
  • apple cider vinegar
  • cornstarch

Steps (oven)

  1. Oven: heat to 375°F.
  2. Rice base: in a 9x13 dish, stir dry brown rice (1 cup) + broth (2 1/2 cups) + salsa (1 cup measured).
  3. Veg: add sliced peppers/onion; season with cumin, chili powder, garlic powder, black pepper, modest salt.
  4. Chicken: nestle chicken on top; cover tightly with foil.
  5. Bake: 375°F 55–65 minutes until rice is tender and chicken is 165°F.
  6. Rest 10 minutes covered; shred chicken into the bake; stir to combine. Return uncovered 5 minutes if you want drier top.
  7. Serve per Notes.

Notes

Hands-off casserole: rice cooks in broth + salsa; chicken stays juicy under foil. Portions (per person): Frank: About 2 1/2 cups casserole bowl Susan: About 2 cups bowl Elise: About 2 cups bowl Tips (recipe-specific): Tight foil seal is key so rice cooks evenly. Rest before stirring so it sets up (less watery).
