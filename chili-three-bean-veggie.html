Three-Bean Veggie Chili

NOTES: Each chili is designed as a full meal with lean protein, smart carbs, and plenty of vegetables. Use low-sodium, no-sugar-added canned goods, avoid added sugars, and keep portions aligned with your goals. PORTION SIZES: Frank: largest portion (~1/3 of the total pot); Susan: medium portion (~1/4 of the pot); Elise: medium portion (~1/4 of the pot) (Frank = 1.0×, Susan/Elise ≈0.75× portions).

Ingredients

  • 1 can (15 oz) no-salt-added black beans, drained and rinsed
  • 1 can (15 oz) no-salt-added kidney beans, drained and rinsed
  • 1 can (15 oz) no-salt-added pinto beans, drained and rinsed
  • 1 small yellow onion, diced
  • 1 red bell pepper, diced
  • 1 green bell pepper, diced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced or 1 tsp garlic powder
  • 1 can (15 oz) no-salt-added diced tomatoes
  • 1 cup low-sodium vegetable broth
  • 1 tbsp tomato paste (no sugar added)
  • 1 tbsp extra-virgin olive oil
  • 1 1/2 tsp mild chili powder
  • 1 tsp ground cumin
  • 1/2 tsp smoked paprika
  • Freshly ground black pepper, to taste

Step-by-Step Instructions

  1. Dice onion and both bell peppers into small, even pieces.
  2. Heat olive oil in a soup pot over medium heat for 1–2 minutes.
  3. Add onion and bell peppers. Cook 5–6 minutes, stirring occasionally, until softened.
  4. Add garlic and cook 1 minute, stirring, until fragrant.
  5. Stir in chili powder, cumin, smoked paprika, and black pepper. Cook 1 minute to bloom spices.
  6. Add black beans, kidney beans, pinto beans, diced tomatoes with their juice, tomato paste, and vegetable broth. Stir well.
  7. Bring to a gentle boil, then reduce heat to low. Cover and simmer 25–30 minutes, stirring once or twice, until chili is thick and flavors are well developed.
  8. Taste and adjust seasoning. Ladle into three bowls, giving Frank the largest portion. Serve with a side of steamed green beans or a small salad if desired.

Nutrition per Person

  • Frank: ~630 kcal, ~32 g protein, ~95 g carbs, ~14 g fat.
  • Susan: ~475 kcal, ~24 g protein, ~71 g carbs, ~10 g fat.
  • Elise: ~475 kcal, ~24 g protein, ~71 g carbs, ~10 g fat.
