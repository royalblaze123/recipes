Peanut-Lite Satay Sauce

Category: Sauce • Yield: About 1/3 cup (5 tbsp) • AnyList-ready

Ingredients

  • 2 tbsp natural peanut butter
  • 2 tbsp 2% plain Greek yogurt
  • 2 tsp low-sodium tamari
  • 1 tsp rice vinegar
  • 1/2 tsp fresh ginger
  • 1/4 tsp black pepper

Steps

  1. Whisk peanut butter, Greek yogurt, tamari, vinegar, ginger, and black pepper until smooth.
  2. Thin with 1–6 tsp water, adding 1 tsp at a time, until drizzle-ready.
