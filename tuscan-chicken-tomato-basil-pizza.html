Tuscan Chicken Tomato Basil Pizza + Roasted Broccoli

Thin-crust whole-wheat pizza with oregano chicken, tomatoes, and basil, finished with Parmesan and served with roasted broccoli.

Servings

3 servings (Frank, Susan, Elise).

Times

  • Prep time: 35 minutes
  • Cook time: 15 minutes
  • Total time: 50 minutes

Ingredients

Pizza

  • 1 dough ball whole wheat pizza dough
  • 1 can no-salt-added crushed tomatoes
  • 1 package reduced-fat mozzarella
  • 1 lb boneless skinless chicken breast
  • 1 pint cherry tomatoes
  • 1 bunch fresh basil
  • 1 jar grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 tsp garlic powder
  • 1 tsp dried oregano
  • 1 tsp ground black pepper

Side

  • 1 head broccoli
  • 2 tbsp extra-virgin olive oil
  • 1 tsp ground black pepper

Step-by-step Instructions

  1. Thaw and warm dough: Thaw 1 dough ball overnight in the refrigerator. Bring to room temperature 60–90 minutes.
  2. Preheat: Place a pizza stone or steel or inverted sheet pan in the oven and preheat to 500°F for 30 minutes.
  3. Cook chicken: Season chicken with garlic powder, oregano, and black pepper. Bake at 425°F until 165°F (about 15–18 minutes). Rest 5 minutes, then slice. Measure 10 oz cooked chicken for the pizza and refrigerate any extra.
  4. Make sauce: Measure 1 1/4 cups crushed tomatoes from the can and spread as a thin base. Refrigerate remaining tomatoes.
  5. Prep toppings: Halve 1 cup cherry tomatoes. Tear basil leaves.
  6. Stretch and top: Stretch dough into a thin 10–12 inch round on parchment. Add sauce, sprinkle 5 1/2 oz mozzarella, add chicken and tomatoes, then finish with 2 tbsp Parmesan.
  7. Bake: Slide pizza on parchment onto the hot surface. Bake 8–12 minutes until browned and bubbling. Scatter basil on top after baking.
  8. Roast broccoli: Cut broccoli into florets. Toss with olive oil and black pepper. Roast at 425°F for 12–15 minutes until browned-tender.
  9. Portion dinner: Cut pizza into 10 slices and serve with broccoli.

Portion Sizes

Portions (per person):
Pizza (1 dough ball): cut into 10 slices.
Frank: 4 slices pizza + 2 cups roasted broccoli

Susan: 3 slices pizza + 1 1/2 cups roasted broccoli

Elise: 3 slices pizza + 1 1/2 cups roasted broccoli
