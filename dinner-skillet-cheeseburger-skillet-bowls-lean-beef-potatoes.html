Cheeseburger Skillet Bowls (Lean Beef) + Potatoes

Nutrition (approx)

Frank: 650 kcal • P 55g • C 55g • F 20g
Susan/Elise (each): 488 kcal • P 41g • C 41g • F 15g

Ingredients (purchase-clean)

  • 1.50 lb 90% lean ground beef
  • 1.50 lb baby potatoes
  • 1 each onion
  • 1 bag shredded lettuce
  • 6 oz reduced-fat cheddar cheese
  • kosher salt
  • black pepper
  • garlic powder
  • onion powder
  • smoked paprika
  • Italian seasoning
  • ground cumin
  • chili powder
  • red pepper flakes
  • extra-virgin olive oil
  • low-sodium chicken broth
  • low-sodium beef broth
  • low-sodium soy sauce
  • hot sauce
  • Dijon mustard
  • balsamic vinegar
  • apple cider vinegar
  • cornstarch

Steps (stovetop)

  1. Potatoes: boil baby potatoes 12–14 minutes until tender; drain and smash lightly. Sear in skillet with olive oil 6–8 minutes until crisp edges; season modest salt/pepper/garlic powder.
  2. Beef: brown beef with diced onion 8–10 minutes; season with black pepper, garlic powder, onion powder, smoked paprika, and modest salt.
  3. Sauce (skillet): whisk Dijon mustard (2 tsp) + apple cider vinegar (2 tsp) + hot sauce (2 tsp) + 1/4 cup broth; stir into beef for 1–2 minutes until glossy.
  4. Assemble bowls: lettuce in bowls, beef on top, potatoes on the side, sprinkle measured cheddar.

Notes

Burger comfort without buns; crisp potatoes and a tangy mustard sauce keep it flavorful. Portions (per person): Frank: 7 oz beef + 1 cup potatoes + 1 cup lettuce bowl Susan: 5 oz beef + 3/4 cup potatoes + 1 cup lettuce bowl Elise: 5 oz beef + 3/4 cup potatoes + 1 cup lettuce bowl Tips (recipe-specific): Smash-then-sear potatoes for crisp edges without deep frying. Use broth + vinegar + Dijon to get ‘sauce’ without mayo.
