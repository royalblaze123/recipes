Freezer Beef Meatballs + Pepper Gravy Bowls

Nutrition (approx)

Frank: 650 kcal • P 55g • C 70g • F 18g • Sat 7g • Na 700mg • Fiber 10g
Susan/Elise (each): 488 kcal • P 41g • C 52g • F 14g • Sat 5g • Na 525mg • Fiber 8g

Ingredients (purchase-clean)

  • 1.50 lb 90% lean ground beef
  • 1 bag frozen fajita-style pepper blend
  • 1 carton low-sodium beef broth
  • cornstarch
  • 1 cup dry brown rice
  • kosher salt
  • black pepper
  • garlic powder
  • onion powder
  • smoked paprika
  • Italian seasoning
  • ground cumin
  • chili powder
  • red pepper flakes
  • extra-virgin olive oil
  • low-sodium chicken broth
  • cornstarch

Steps

  1. Rice: cook 1 cup dry brown rice; rest 5 minutes.
  2. Oven: heat to 425°F.
  3. Meatballs: mix beef with garlic powder, onion powder, black pepper, and a modest pinch of kosher salt. Form 18 meatballs.
  4. Bake 14–16 minutes to 160–165°F.
  5. Gravy: sauté pepper blend until water evaporates and edges brown. Add 2 cups low-sodium beef broth. Whisk 2 tbsp cornstarch into 1/2 cup cold water; stir in; simmer 3 minutes until thick.
  6. Combine: add meatballs to gravy; simmer 2 minutes.
  7. Freeze: cool meatballs in gravy; portion with rice; freeze. Reheat covered until hot.

Notes

Freezer-friendly meatballs in a pepper gravy—reheats like comfort food, not meal prep. Portions (per person): Frank: 6 meatballs + 1 cup rice + gravy Susan: 5 meatballs + 3/4 cup rice + gravy Elise: 5 meatballs + 3/4 cup rice + gravy Tips (recipe-specific): Freeze meatballs in gravy so they stay juicy. Brown peppers first so the gravy tastes roasted, not boiled.
