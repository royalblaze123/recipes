Veggie Supreme Pizza (No Olives)

NOTES: Built as a full dinner using ready-made whole-grain or thin crust, lean protein, plenty of vegetables, and controlled cheese to support A1c, cholesterol, and blood pressure goals. Use low-sodium sauce and minimal added salt. PORTION SIZES: Frank: ~40% of the pizza; Susan: ~30% of the pizza; Elise: ~30% of the pizza (Frank = 1.0×, Susan/Elise = 0.75× portions).

Ingredients

  • 1 ready-made 12-inch thin whole-wheat pizza crust
  • 1/2 cup no-salt-added tomato or pizza sauce
  • 1 cup sliced mushrooms
  • 1/2 red bell pepper, thinly sliced
  • 1/2 yellow bell pepper, thinly sliced
  • 1/4 red onion, thinly sliced
  • 1 cup fresh baby spinach, roughly chopped
  • 1/2 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 3/4 cup shredded reduced-fat mozzarella cheese
  • 1 tsp dried Italian seasoning
  • Freshly ground black pepper, to taste

Step-by-Step Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 425°F. Place the crust on a pizza pan or parchment-lined baking sheet.
  2. Spread tomato or pizza sauce evenly over the crust.
  3. Distribute sliced mushrooms, bell peppers, red onion, chopped spinach, and halved cherry tomatoes evenly over the sauce.
  4. Sprinkle shredded mozzarella evenly over the vegetables.
  5. Season with Italian seasoning and a few grinds of black pepper.
  6. Bake 12–15 minutes, until the crust is crisp and the cheese is melted and lightly golden.
  7. Cool 3–5 minutes, then slice and portion for Frank (~40%) and Susan/Elise (~30% each).

Nutrition per Person

  • Frank: ~560 kcal, ~40 g protein, ~60 g carbs, ~16 g fat.
  • Susan: ~420 kcal, ~30 g protein, ~45 g carbs, ~12 g fat.
  • Elise: ~420 kcal, ~30 g protein, ~45 g carbs, ~12 g fat.
