Chicken Broccoli White Bean Pizza

NOTES: Built as a full dinner using ready-made whole-grain or thin crust, lean protein, plenty of vegetables, and controlled cheese to support A1c, cholesterol, and blood pressure goals. Use low-sodium sauce and minimal added salt. PORTION SIZES: Frank: ~40% of the pizza; Susan: ~30% of the pizza; Elise: ~30% of the pizza (Frank = 1.0×, Susan/Elise = 0.75× portions).

Ingredients

  • 1 ready-made 12-inch thin whole-wheat pizza crust
  • 1 can (15 oz) no-salt-added cannellini beans, drained and rinsed
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice
  • 1–2 tbsp water to thin bean spread
  • 8 oz cooked boneless, skinless chicken breast, chopped
  • 1 cup small broccoli florets, lightly steamed
  • 1/4 red onion, thinly sliced
  • 1/2 cup shredded reduced-fat mozzarella cheese
  • 2 tbsp grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 tsp dried Italian seasoning
  • Freshly ground black pepper, to taste

Step-by-Step Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 425°F. Place the crust on a pizza pan or parchment-lined baking sheet.
  2. In a food processor or bowl, mash cannellini beans with garlic, lemon juice, and enough water to form a spreadable mixture.
  3. Spread the seasoned bean mixture evenly over the crust as a high-fiber base.
  4. Top with chopped chicken, steamed broccoli florets, and sliced red onion.
  5. Sprinkle shredded mozzarella, grated Parmesan, Italian seasoning, and black pepper over the toppings.
  6. Bake 12–15 minutes, until the crust is crisp and the cheese is melted and lightly browned.
  7. Cool 3–5 minutes before slicing and portioning for Frank (~40%) and Susan/Elise (~30% each).

Nutrition per Person

  • Frank: ~620 kcal, ~54 g protein, ~55 g carbs, ~18 g fat.
  • Susan: ~465 kcal, ~41 g protein, ~41 g carbs, ~13 g fat.
  • Elise: ~465 kcal, ~41 g protein, ~41 g carbs, ~13 g fat.
