Buffalo Chicken Tenders + Sweet Potato Wedges + Broccoli

Ingredients (purchase-clean)

  • 2.00 lb sweet potatoes

Steps (Cosori dual-basket)

  1. Preheat Cosori to 400°F. Use Basket A for chicken; Basket B for sweet potatoes. Plan to use Sync Finish so both baskets end together.
  2. Sweet potatoes (Basket B): cut sweet potatoes into wedges. Toss with 2 tsp olive oil, 2 tsp smoked paprika, 1 tsp garlic powder, 1/2 tsp kosher salt, and 1/2 tsp black pepper. Load single layer. Air fry 18–22 minutes at 400°F with Shake Reminder on.
  3. Chicken breading: whisk 1/2 cup egg whites with 2 tbsp hot sauce. In a shallow plate, mix 1 cup panko with 2 tsp garlic powder, 2 tsp smoked paprika, 1/2 tsp kosher salt, and 1/2 tsp black pepper.
  4. Chicken (Basket A): dip tenderloins in egg-white mix, then panko. Spray basket lightly with oil. Air fry 10–12 minutes at 400°F; flip at the shake reminder; cook to 165°F.
  5. Broccoli: microwave broccoli 4–5 minutes; drain and pat dry. Toss with 2 tsp olive oil, 1 tsp garlic powder, 1/2 tsp kosher salt, and black pepper. If Basket B finishes early, add broccoli for the last 6 minutes at 400°F to blister; otherwise roast in oven 425°F for 10–12 minutes.
  6. Buffalo finish: toss cooked tenders with 3 tbsp hot sauce (measured).
  7. Serve per Notes.

Notes

Holiday-level flavor with buffalo heat, but still a clean plate: tenders + sweet potatoes + broccoli.
