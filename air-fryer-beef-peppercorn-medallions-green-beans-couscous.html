Air Fryer Peppercorn Beef Medallions with Green Beans and Lemon Couscous

Prep: 15 min   Cook: 20 min   Total: 35 min

Ingredients

  • 1.5 lb beef tenderloin
  • 1 bag fresh green beans
  • 0.33 box whole-wheat couscous
  • 1 lemon
  • 1 bulb garlic
  • olive oil
  • butter
  • black pepper
  • salt substitute
  • garlic powder
  • Dijon mustard

RBM rules: quantities appear before items; fresh proteins use weight. Condiments/spices/seasonings are listed name-only; all measurements are in Steps. Water is referenced in Steps only.

Step-by-step Instructions

  1. Prep couscous: bring 1 1/4 cups water to a boil in a small pot. Stir in whole-wheat couscous (use ~1 cup dry from the 0.33 box) + 1 tsp olive oil + 1 tsp salt substitute. Cover, remove from heat 5 minutes; fluff.
  2. Finish couscous with zest of 1 lemon + 4 tsp lemon juice + 2 tsp butter + 1 tsp black pepper.
  3. Prep beans: trim. Toss green beans with 6 tsp olive oil + 1 tsp salt substitute + 1 tsp garlic powder + 1 tsp black pepper.
  4. Air fry green beans (Cosori): preheat 390°F for 3 minutes. Cook 7–9 minutes, shaking once, until blistered and crisp-tender.
  5. Prep beef: slice tenderloin into 1 1/2-inch medallions. Pat dry. Brush with 4 tsp Dijon; press on 2 tbsp cracked black pepper + 2 tsp salt substitute.
  6. Air fry beef medallions: 400°F for 8–11 minutes depending on thickness (flip at halfway) until 130–135°F for medium. Rest 5 minutes.
  7. Portion: Frank gets 6 oz cooked beef + ~3/4 cup couscous + ~1 1/2 cups green beans. Susan and Elise each get ~4.5 oz beef + ~1/2 cup couscous + ~1 1/4 cups green beans.
