Turkey Black Bean Sweet Potato Bake

NOTES: Built as a complete dinner using lean protein, high-fiber vegetables, and controlled starch portions. Use low-sodium broth and minimal added salt to support blood pressure and kidney health. PORTION SIZES: Frank: largest portion (~1/3 of pan); Susan: medium portion (~1/4 of pan); Elise: medium portion (~1/4 of pan).

Ingredients

  • 1 lb 93% lean ground turkey
  • 1 tbsp extra-virgin olive oil
  • 1 medium yellow onion, diced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 medium sweet potatoes, peeled and cut into 1/2-inch cubes
  • 1 red bell pepper, diced
  • 1 can (15 oz) no-salt-added black beans, drained and rinsed
  • 1 cup low-sodium chicken broth
  • 2 tsp chili powder
  • 1 tsp ground cumin
  • 1/2 tsp smoked paprika
  • 1/2 tsp dried oregano
  • 1/2 tsp kosher salt (or to taste)
  • Freshly ground black pepper, to taste
  • Olive oil spray for baking dish

Step-by-Step Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 400°F. Lightly spray a 9x13-inch baking dish with olive oil spray.
  2. Heat olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat.
  3. Add diced onion and cook 3–4 minutes until softened.
  4. Stir in minced garlic and cook 30–60 seconds until fragrant.
  5. Add ground turkey, breaking it into crumbles, and cook 6–8 minutes until no pink remains.
  6. Stir in sweet potato cubes and diced red bell pepper and cook 4–5 minutes to start softening the vegetables.
  7. Add drained black beans, low-sodium chicken broth, chili powder, ground cumin, smoked paprika, dried oregano, salt, and black pepper. Stir to combine.
  8. Bring to a gentle simmer and cook 5 minutes to slightly reduce the liquid.
  9. Transfer mixture to the prepared baking dish and spread into an even layer.
  10. Cover with foil and bake 20 minutes.
  11. Remove foil and bake an additional 10–15 minutes until sweet potatoes are tender and the top is lightly browned.
  12. Let casserole rest 5–10 minutes before portioning for Frank, Susan, and Elise.

Nutrition per Person

  • Frank: ~590 kcal, ~48 g protein, ~58 g carbs, ~17 g fat.
  • Susan: ~445 kcal, ~36 g protein, ~44 g carbs, ~13 g fat.
  • Elise: ~445 kcal, ~36 g protein, ~44 g carbs, ~13 g fat.
