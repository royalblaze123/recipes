Chopped Salad: Lean Beef and Pepper Chop Salad with White Beans

Prep: 18 min   Cook: 12 min   Total: 30 min

Ingredients

  • 1.0 lb lean ground beef 93%
  • 0.5 can no-salt-added cannellini beans
  • 0.5 bag shredded cabbage
  • 2 bell peppers
  • 1 onion
  • 1 lemon
  • olive oil
  • Italian seasoning
  • black pepper
  • salt substitute
  • vinegar
  • Dijon mustard

Rules enforced: Clean AnyList Ingredients (quantities before items; fresh meat/poultry/fish use weights; packaged items use fractional containers when partial). Condiments/spices/seasonings have no measurements in Ingredients; all measurements are in Steps. No olives, no squash, no Brussels sprouts, no zucchini, no tofu/tempeh. Lunch validations: Frank calories 500–550, sat fat ≤6g, sodium ≤650mg, fiber ≥8g, added sugar ≤6g.

Step-by-step Instructions

  1. Beef: brown 1 lb 93% lean ground beef with 2 tsp olive oil 7–9 minutes. Season with 2 tsp Italian seasoning + 1/2 tsp black pepper + 1/2 tsp salt substitute. Cool 5 minutes.
  2. Beans: drain and rinse 1/2 can cannellini beans; measure 3/4 cup for salad; refrigerate remainder.
  3. Veg: dice 2 bell peppers and 1 onion; sauté 4 minutes; cool 5 minutes.
  4. Base: combine 1/2 bag shredded cabbage with cooled peppers/onion.
  5. Dressing: whisk 2 tsp olive oil + 2 tsp vinegar + 2 tsp lemon juice + 2 tsp Dijon + 1/4 tsp black pepper + 1/4 tsp salt substitute.
  6. Toss base with beef and beans.
  7. Portion: Frank largest bowl; Susan/Elise ~75%.
