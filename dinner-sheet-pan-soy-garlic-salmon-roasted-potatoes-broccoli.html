Soy-Garlic Salmon + Roasted Potatoes + Broccoli

Nutrition (approx)

Frank: 645 kcal • P 55g • C 55g • F 22g • Sat 5g • Na 700mg • Fiber 10g
Susan/Elise (each): 484 kcal • P 41g • C 41g • F 16g • Sat 4g • Na 525mg • Fiber 8g

Ingredients (purchase-clean)

  • 1.25 lb salmon fillets
  • 1.50 lb baby potatoes
  • 1 bag frozen broccoli florets
  • kosher salt
  • black pepper
  • garlic powder
  • onion powder
  • smoked paprika
  • Italian seasoning
  • ground cumin
  • chili powder
  • red pepper flakes
  • extra-virgin olive oil
  • Dijon mustard
  • balsamic vinegar
  • apple cider vinegar
  • low-sodium soy sauce
  • cornstarch
  • hot sauce

Steps

  1. Oven: heat to 425°F. Line a sheet pan.
  2. Potatoes: halve. Toss with 2 tsp olive oil, garlic powder, smoked paprika, 1/2 tsp kosher salt, black pepper. Roast 18 minutes.
  3. Broccoli: microwave 4–5 minutes; drain/pat dry. Toss with 2 tsp olive oil, garlic powder, 1/2 tsp kosher salt, black pepper. Add to pan; roast 10 minutes.
  4. Salmon: whisk 2 tsp low-sodium soy sauce + 2 tsp olive oil + black pepper. Brush on salmon.
  5. Add salmon to pan for the last 8–10 minutes until flaky.
  6. Serve per Notes.

Notes

Roasted salmon plate with soy-garlic shine (tiny soy, big flavor). Portions (per person): Frank: 6 oz salmon + 1 cup potatoes + 1 1/2 cups broccoli Susan: 5 oz protein + 3/4 cup starch + 1 cup veg Elise: 5 oz protein + 3/4 cup starch + 1 cup veg Tips (recipe-specific): Add salmon late so it stays juicy. Roast broccoli hard for browning = flavor.
