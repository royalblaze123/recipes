Open-Face Melt: Turkey Provolone Tomato Melt (Whole Wheat)

Prep: 14 min   Cook: 10 min   Total: 24 min

Ingredients

  • 6 slices whole wheat bread
  • 14 slices deli turkey
  • 6 slices provolone
  • 1 pint cherry tomatoes
  • 1 bag spinach
  • Dijon mustard
  • black pepper
  • salt substitute

Rules enforced: quantities before items; fresh meat/poultry/fish use weights; packaged items use fractional containers when partial. Condiments/spices/seasonings have no measurements in Ingredients; all measurements are in Steps. No olives, no squash, no Brussels sprouts, no zucchini, no tofu/tempeh. Lunch validations included in Notes.

Step-by-step Instructions

  1. Oven: set broiler to high. Line a sheet pan.
  2. Toast bread: place 6 slices whole wheat bread on pan; broil 1 minute to dry the surface (watch closely).
  3. Spread: apply Dijon in measured amounts (Frank 2 tsp total across 2 slices; Susan 1.5 tsp; Elise 1.5 tsp).
  4. Layer: add turkey (Frank 6 slices; Susan 4; Elise 4).
  5. Add spinach (divide 1 bag evenly across 6 slices).
  6. Cheese: top with provolone (1 slice per bread slice = 6).
  7. Top: halve cherry tomatoes and add a thin layer (divide 1 pint). Broil 2–3 minutes until melted; rest 2 minutes.

Notes

Two open-face melts per person: turkey + spinach + measured provolone, finished under the broiler with tomatoes. Portions (per person): Frank: - Plate: 1 serving (largest portion) Susan: - Plate: ~3/4 of Frank’s portion Elise: - Plate: ~3/4 of Frank’s portion Tips (recipe-specific): Broil in short bursts—bread burns fast. Spinach under cheese prevents dryness and boosts fiber.
