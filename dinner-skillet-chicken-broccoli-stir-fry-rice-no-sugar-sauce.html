Chicken Broccoli Stir-Fry + Rice (No Sugar Sauce)

Nutrition (approx)

Frank: 650 kcal • P 60g • C 70g • F 14g
Susan/Elise (each): 488 kcal • P 45g • C 52g • F 10g

Ingredients (purchase-clean)

  • 1.75 lb boneless skinless chicken breast
  • 1 bag frozen broccoli florets
  • low-sodium soy sauce
  • cornstarch
  • 1 cup dry brown rice
  • low-sodium chicken broth
  • kosher salt
  • black pepper
  • garlic powder
  • onion powder
  • smoked paprika
  • Italian seasoning
  • ground cumin
  • chili powder
  • red pepper flakes
  • extra-virgin olive oil
  • low-sodium chicken broth
  • low-sodium beef broth
  • low-sodium soy sauce
  • hot sauce
  • Dijon mustard
  • balsamic vinegar
  • apple cider vinegar
  • cornstarch

Steps (stovetop)

  1. Cook rice: 1 cup dry brown rice (35–40 minutes).
  2. Broccoli: microwave broccoli 4–5 minutes; drain and pat dry.
  3. Chicken: slice thin; sear in hot skillet with olive oil 3–4 minutes.
  4. Sauce: whisk soy sauce (6 tbsp) + broth (3/4 cup) + cornstarch (1 tbsp) + hot sauce (2 tsp).
  5. Finish: add broccoli; pour sauce; simmer 2–3 minutes until glossy and thick; chicken 165°F.
  6. Serve per Notes.

Notes

Stir-fry texture without bottled sugar sauce—cornstarch + broth gives the glossy coat. Portions (per person): Frank: 7 oz chicken + 1 1/2 cups broccoli + 1 cup cooked rice Susan: 5 oz protein + 1 cup veg + 3/4 cup Elise: 5 oz protein + 1 cup veg + 3/4 cup Tips (recipe-specific): Pat broccoli dry so sauce stays thick, not watery. Slice chicken thin for tenderness.
