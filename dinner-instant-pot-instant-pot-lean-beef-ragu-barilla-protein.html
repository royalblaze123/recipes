Instant Pot Lean Beef Ragu + Barilla Protein+

Nutrition (approx)

Frank: 650 kcal • P 55g • C 75g • F 14g
Susan/Elise (each): 488 kcal • P 41g • C 56g • F 10g

Ingredients (purchase-clean)

  • 1.25 lb lean sirloin steak
  • 1 jar marinara sauce
  • 1 can no-salt-added diced tomatoes
  • 1 box Barilla Protein+ pasta
  • 1 each onion
  • low-sodium beef broth
  • kosher salt
  • black pepper
  • garlic powder
  • onion powder
  • smoked paprika
  • Italian seasoning
  • ground cumin
  • chili powder
  • red pepper flakes
  • extra-virgin olive oil
  • low-sodium chicken broth
  • low-sodium beef broth
  • low-sodium soy sauce
  • hot sauce
  • Dijon mustard
  • balsamic vinegar
  • apple cider vinegar
  • cornstarch

Steps (Instant Pot)

  1. Sauté: olive oil + diced onion 3 minutes. Add beef cubes; brown 2–3 minutes.
  2. Load: add marinara (2 cups measured) + diced tomatoes (14.5 oz) + broth (1/2 cup). Season Italian seasoning, garlic powder, black pepper, modest salt.
  3. Pressure cook: High 18 minutes; Natural Release 10 then Quick Release.
  4. Shred/Break: use tongs to break beef into ragu strands; Sauté 6–8 minutes to reduce.
  5. Pasta: cook Barilla Protein+ al dente; drain.
  6. Serve: ragu over pasta per Notes.

Notes

Ragu texture without fatty chuck roast—sirloin plus reduction makes it rich. Portions (per person): Frank: 2 cups pasta bowl + 1 cup ragu Susan: 1 1/2 cups pasta bowl + 3/4 cup ragu Elise: 1 1/2 cups pasta bowl + 3/4 cup ragu Tips (recipe-specific): Reduce after pressure to deepen flavor without extra cheese/oil. Cook pasta fresh; never pressure-cook pasta.
