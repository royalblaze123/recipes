Steak Fajita Tostada Plates

Nutrition (approx)

Frank: 645 kcal • P 58g • C 48g • F 22g
Susan/Elise (each): 484 kcal • P 44g • C 36g • F 16g

Ingredients (purchase-clean)

  • 1.25 lb sirloin steak
  • 1 bag frozen fajita-style pepper blend
  • 1 package baked tostada shells
  • 1 each avocado
  • 1/2 jar salsa
  • 6 tsp extra-virgin olive oil
  • 2 tsp chili powder
  • 2 tsp cumin
  • 2 tsp garlic powder
  • 1 tsp smoked paprika
  • 1 tsp black pepper
  • 1 tsp kosher salt

Steps

  1. Tostadas: warm in a 350°F oven for 3–4 minutes to crisp and heat through.
  2. Veg: cook frozen fajita pepper blend in a skillet with 4 tsp olive oil 6–8 minutes until browned at edges.
  3. Steak: pat dry, slice thin against the grain, and season with the spices.
  4. Cook steak in the same hot skillet with remaining 2 tsp olive oil for 2–3 minutes, stirring often, until just cooked.
  5. Combine: toss steak with peppers for 30 seconds.
  6. Avocado: halve, pit, and dice. Use about 1/2 avocado total; refrigerate remaining avocado with the pit to slow browning.
  7. Serve: top tostadas with steak/pepper mix, salsa, and avocado. Portion per Notes.

Notes

Steak fajita tostadas with peppers for volume, finished with salsa and avocado. Portions (per person): Frank: 2 tostadas + 1/3 of the protein topping + 1/3 of the avocado + salsa Susan: 1 tostada + 1/4 of the protein topping + 1/4 of the avocado + salsa Elise: 1 tostada + 1/4 of the protein topping + 1/4 of the avocado + salsa
