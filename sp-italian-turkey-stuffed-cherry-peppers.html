Italian Turkey Stuffed Cherry Peppers with Green Beans & Quinoa (Sheet Pan)

Dinner • Sheet Pan • Cook time: 35 min

Ingredients (purchase-clean)

  • 24 each fresh cherry peppers
  • 24 oz fresh 93% lean ground turkey
  • 13 oz fresh green beans
  • 8 oz cooked quinoa
  • 5.5 oz 2% plain Greek yogurt
  • 1.5 oz grated parmesan
  • 1 each fresh yellow onion
  • Italian seasoning
  • garlic powder
  • black pepper
  • kosher salt
  • extra-virgin olive oil

Steps

  1. Heat oven to 425°F. Line a sheet pan with parchment.
  2. Cherry peppers: Cut tops off 24 cherry peppers and remove seeds. Set peppers upright on the sheet pan (a muffin tin also works).
  3. Filling base: Dice 1 onion (use about 5.5 oz). In a skillet, heat 2 tsp olive oil and cook onion 4–5 minutes until soft.
  4. Turkey: Add 24 oz ground turkey with 2 tsp kosher salt, 2 tsp garlic powder, 2 tbsp Italian seasoning, and 1/2 tsp black pepper. Cook 7–9 minutes until fully cooked.
  5. Moisture + bind: Off heat, stir in 5.5 oz Greek yogurt and 1 oz parmesan.
  6. Stuff & bake: Spoon filling into the peppers. Sprinkle remaining 0.5 oz parmesan on top. Bake 16–20 minutes until peppers are tender and filling is hot.
  7. Green beans: Toss 13 oz green beans with 2 tsp olive oil, 1 tsp kosher salt, 1 tsp garlic powder, and black pepper. Roast on a second sheet pan (or alongside) for 12–15 minutes, shaking once.
  8. Quinoa: Warm 8 oz cooked quinoa. Season with 1/2 tsp kosher salt and black pepper.
  9. Serve: Plate stuffed cherry peppers with green beans and quinoa.

Notes

Sheet Pan dinner built to be high-protein and satisfying. Portions (per person): Frank: 8 stuffed cherry peppers + 4 oz green beans + 3 oz quinoa Susan: 6 stuffed cherry peppers + 3 oz green beans + 2 oz quinoa Elise: 6 stuffed cherry peppers + 3 oz green beans + 2 oz quinoa Tips (recipe-specific): If the peppers wobble, wedge them into a mini muffin pan to keep them upright while baking. For a ‘sausage’ vibe without extra fat, add 1 tsp fennel seed to the turkey while browning.

Nutrition (per person)

  • Frank (1.0×): 625 kcal • 2 oz protein • 1.5 oz carbs • 1 oz fat
  • Susan (0.75×): 469 kcal • 1.5 oz protein • 1 oz carbs • 1 oz fat
  • Elise (0.75×): 469 kcal • 1.5 oz protein • 1 oz carbs • 1 oz fat
