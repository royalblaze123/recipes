Thai-Style Coconut Curry Shrimp Soup with Quick Brown Rice

Prep: 15 min   Cook: 25 min   Total: 40 min

Ingredients

  • 1.25 lb shrimp (peeled/deveined)
  • 1 carton low-sodium chicken broth
  • 0.5 carton low-sodium chicken broth
  • 0.5 can light coconut milk
  • 1 onion
  • 1 bell pepper
  • 1 bag frozen broccoli florets
  • 1 lime
  • 1 bunch cilantro
  • 1 pouch microwave brown rice
  • olive oil
  • red curry paste
  • fish sauce
  • ginger
  • garlic
  • black pepper
  • salt substitute

RBM rules: quantities appear before items; fresh proteins use weight. Condiments/spices/seasonings are listed name-only; all measurements are in Steps. Water is only referenced in Steps.

Step-by-step Instructions

  1. Prep: dice onion and bell pepper. Grate ginger (about 2 tsp). Mince 2 garlic cloves.
  2. Heat a pot over medium-high. Add 3 tsp olive oil. Add onion + bell pepper; cook 5–6 minutes.
  3. Stir in 2–3 tsp red curry paste + ginger + garlic; cook 45 seconds until fragrant.
  4. Add liquids: pour in 1.5 cartons broth total (48 fl oz). Add 0.5 can light coconut milk (about 3/4 cup). Bring to a gentle simmer.
  5. Add frozen broccoli (use ~3 cups). Simmer 6–8 minutes until broccoli is crisp-tender.
  6. Add shrimp and cook 3–4 minutes until pink and cooked through.
  7. Season: add 2 tsp fish sauce + juice of 1 lime + 1 tsp black pepper + 1 tsp salt substitute (optional). Stir in 1/2 cup chopped cilantro.
  8. Heat brown rice pouch per package directions.
  9. Portion: Frank gets ~3 cups soup + ~3/4 cup cooked rice mixed into the bowl. Susan and Elise each get ~2 1/2 cups soup + ~1/2 cup rice.
