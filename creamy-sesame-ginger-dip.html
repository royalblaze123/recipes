Creamy Sesame-Ginger Dip

Category: Dip • Yield: About 3/4 cup (14 tbsp) • AnyList-ready

Ingredients

  • 3/4 cup 2% plain Greek yogurt
  • 2 tsp sesame oil
  • 2 tsp rice vinegar
  • 1 tsp fresh ginger
  • 2 tsp low-sodium soy sauce
  • 1/4 tsp black pepper

Steps

  1. Grate fresh ginger to measure 1 tsp.
  2. Whisk Greek yogurt, sesame oil, rice vinegar, ginger, soy sauce, and black pepper until smooth.
  3. Chill 10 minutes before serving.
