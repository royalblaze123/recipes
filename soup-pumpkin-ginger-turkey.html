Savory Pumpkin Turkey Soup (Stovetop)

NOTES: Designed as a complete meal soup using lean protein, high-fiber vegetables, and controlled starch. Use low-sodium broth and avoid extra salt when possible to support blood pressure and kidney health. PORTION SIZES: Frank: largest portion (~2 cups); Susan: medium portion (~1.5 cups); Elise: medium portion (~1.5 cups).

Ingredients

  • 1 lb 93% lean ground turkey
  • 1 tbsp extra-virgin olive oil
  • 1 medium yellow onion, diced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tbsp fresh ginger, grated
  • 1 can (15 oz) pure pumpkin puree (not pumpkin pie filling)
  • 4 cups low-sodium chicken broth
  • 1 cup water, as needed
  • 1 tsp ground cumin
  • 1/2 tsp ground coriander
  • 1/2 tsp smoked paprika
  • 1/4 tsp ground cinnamon
  • 1/4 tsp ground nutmeg
  • 1/2 tsp kosher salt (or to taste)
  • Freshly ground black pepper, to taste
  • 1/2 cup plain 2% Greek yogurt
  • Chopped fresh parsley or chives, for garnish (optional)

Step-by-Step Instructions

  1. Heat olive oil in a large soup pot over medium heat.
  2. Add diced onion and cook 4–5 minutes until softened.
  3. Stir in minced garlic and grated ginger and cook 30–60 seconds until fragrant.
  4. Add ground turkey and cook 5–7 minutes, breaking into small crumbles, until no pink remains.
  5. Stir in ground cumin, coriander, smoked paprika, cinnamon, nutmeg, salt, and black pepper.
  6. Add pumpkin puree and stir until the turkey and spices are fully coated.
  7. Pour in low-sodium chicken broth and stir. Add up to 1 cup water if the mixture is very thick.
  8. Bring to a gentle boil, then reduce heat to low and simmer uncovered 15–20 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  9. Remove from heat and let cool 2–3 minutes, then stir in Greek yogurt until fully incorporated and creamy.
  10. Taste and adjust salt and pepper as needed.
  11. Let rest another 3–5 minutes, then ladle into bowls per Frank, Susan, and Elise portion sizes.
  12. Garnish with chopped parsley or chives before serving, if desired.

Nutrition per Person

  • Frank: ~430 kcal, ~38 g protein, ~30 g carbs, ~15 g fat.
  • Susan: ~320 kcal, ~28 g protein, ~22 g carbs, ~11 g fat.
  • Elise: ~320 kcal, ~28 g protein, ~22 g carbs, ~11 g fat.
