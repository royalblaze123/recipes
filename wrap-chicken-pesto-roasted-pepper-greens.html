Chicken Pesto Roasted Pepper Wrap with Crunch Salad Cup

Prep: 18 min   Cook: 12 min   Total: 30 min

Ingredients

  • 3 whole wheat tortillas
  • 14 oz chicken breast
  • 0.2 jar pesto
  • 0.33 jar roasted red peppers
  • 1 bag arugula
  • 1 bag microgreens
  • 1 cucumber
  • 1 bag shredded cabbage
  • 1 lemon
  • olive oil
  • black pepper
  • salt substitute
  • vinegar
  • garlic powder

Hard rules enforced: Lunch validations (calories, sat fat, sodium, fiber, added sugar). Quantities appear before items; fresh meat/poultry/fish use weights. Condiments/spices/seasonings have no measurements in Ingredients; all measurements are in Steps. No olives, no squash, no Brussels sprouts, no zucchini. Yogurt is not used in dinners.

Step-by-step Instructions

  1. Chicken: season with garlic powder, black pepper, and salt substitute. Cook with 2 tsp olive oil 6–7 minutes per side to 165°F. Slice thin.
  2. Roasted peppers: drain and pat dry; slice into strips.
  3. Crunch cup: toss cabbage + diced cucumber with 2 tsp lemon juice + 1 tsp vinegar + black pepper + salt substitute + 1 tsp olive oil.
  4. Assemble wraps: spread a thin smear of pesto on each tortilla (keep light). Add chicken, roasted peppers, arugula, and microgreens. Roll tightly.
  5. Portion: 1 wrap each + crunch cup on the side.
