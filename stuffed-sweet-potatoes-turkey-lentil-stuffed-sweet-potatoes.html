Turkey Lentil Stuffed Sweet Potatoes

Nutrition (approx)

Frank: 650 kcal • P 60g • C 58g • F 16g
Susan/Elise (each): 488 kcal • P 45g • C 44g • F 12g

Ingredients (purchase-clean)

  • 2.25 lb sweet potatoes
  • 1.50 lb 93% lean ground turkey
  • 1 bag dry lentils
  • 1 can crushed tomatoes
  • 1 each avocado
  • 1/2 jar salsa
  • 4 tsp extra-virgin olive oil
  • 2 tsp chili powder
  • 2 tsp cumin
  • 2 tsp garlic powder
  • 1 tsp smoked paprika
  • 1 tsp black pepper
  • 1 tsp kosher salt

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 425°F. Line a sheet pan with parchment.
  2. Sweet potatoes: scrub and pierce each potato 6–8 times with a fork. Rub with 2 tsp olive oil and a pinch of kosher salt.
  3. Roast sweet potatoes 40–55 minutes until very tender (knife slides in easily).
  4. Turkey: brown ground turkey in a pot with 2 tsp olive oil 7–9 minutes until cooked through. Stir in spices for 60 seconds.
  5. Lentils: rinse 3/4 cup dry lentils and add to the pot.
  6. Crushed tomatoes: Can size note—use a standard 28 oz can crushed tomatoes.. Add 2 cups crushed tomatoes; refrigerate remainder.
  7. Water: add 2 cups water and simmer 25–30 minutes until lentils are tender and mixture is thick. Stir occasionally.
  8. Split: slice each potato lengthwise, fluff the insides with a fork, and create a well for the topping.
  9. Stuff: spoon thick turkey-lentil taco topping into potatoes.
  10. Avocado: halve, pit, and dice. Use about 1/2 avocado total; refrigerate remaining avocado with the pit to slow browning.
  11. Serve with salsa and portion per Notes.

Notes

Thick turkey-lentil taco topping over sweet potatoes—high fiber and very filling with salsa + avocado. Portions (per person): Frank: 1 stuffed sweet potato (largest) + 1/3 of the topping mix + 1/3 of the avocado + salsa Susan: 1 stuffed sweet potato (medium) + 1/4 of the topping mix + 1/4 of the avocado + salsa Elise: 1 stuffed sweet potato (medium) + 1/4 of the topping mix + 1/4 of the avocado + salsa
