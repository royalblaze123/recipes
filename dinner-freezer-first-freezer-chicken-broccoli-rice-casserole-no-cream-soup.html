Freezer Chicken + Broccoli Rice Casserole (No Cream Soup)

Nutrition (approx)

Frank: 650 kcal • P 58g • C 70g • F 14g • Sat 6g • Na 700mg • Fiber 10g
Susan/Elise (each): 488 kcal • P 44g • C 52g • F 10g • Sat 4g • Na 525mg • Fiber 8g

Ingredients (purchase-clean)

  • 1.75 lb boneless skinless chicken breast
  • 1 bag frozen broccoli florets
  • 1 cup dry brown rice
  • 1 carton skim milk
  • 6 oz reduced-fat shredded mozzarella cheese
  • cornstarch
  • low-sodium chicken broth
  • kosher salt
  • black pepper
  • garlic powder
  • onion powder
  • smoked paprika
  • Italian seasoning
  • ground cumin
  • chili powder
  • red pepper flakes
  • extra-virgin olive oil
  • low-sodium chicken broth
  • cornstarch

Steps

  1. Rice: cook 1 cup dry brown rice; cool 10 minutes.
  2. Oven: heat to 375°F.
  3. Chicken: sauté diced chicken with olive oil, garlic powder, black pepper, modest salt 6–8 minutes.
  4. Broccoli: microwave broccoli; drain well and chop.
  5. Sauce: simmer 1 1/2 cups skim milk + 1 cup broth. Whisk 2 tbsp cornstarch into 1/2 cup cold water; stir in; simmer 2–3 minutes until thick.
  6. Assemble: mix rice + chicken + broccoli + sauce; pour into dish; top with mozzarella (measured).
  7. Bake 25–30 minutes; rest 10 minutes.
  8. Freeze: cool fully; portion; freeze. Reheat covered 350°F 25–35 minutes.

Notes

Classic comfort casserole rebuilt without cream soup—freezer-stable and reheats creamy. Portions (per person): Frank: About 2 1/2 cups (heaping scoop) Susan: About 2 cups (scoop) Elise: About 2 cups (scoop) Tips (recipe-specific): Drain broccoli well so the casserole doesn’t get watery after thawing. Thicken sauce first so it bakes into a creamy texture.
