Garlic-Herb Chicken, Potatoes & Green Beans (RBM Dinner)

High-protein, lower-sodium sheet pan dinner with chicken breast, baby potatoes, and green beans.

Servings

3 servings (Frank, Susan, Elise).

Times

  • Prep time: 15m
  • Cook time: 25m
  • Total time: 40m

Ingredients

  • 18 oz boneless skinless chicken breast, cut into 1-inch pieces
  • 18 oz baby potatoes, halved (leave skin on)
  • 12 oz fresh green beans, trimmed
  • 1 1/2 tbsp extra-virgin olive oil
  • 1 tsp garlic powder
  • 1 tsp smoked paprika (or sweet paprika)
  • 1 tsp Italian seasoning (or dried oregano + basil)
  • 1/4 tsp kosher or sea salt (or less, to taste)
  • 1/2 tsp ground black pepper
  • Optional: lemon wedges for serving

Step-by-Step Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 425°F (220°C). Line a large rimmed sheet pan with parchment paper.
  2. Add the halved baby potatoes to a large bowl. Drizzle with about 2 teaspoons of the olive oil and sprinkle with half of the garlic powder, smoked paprika, Italian seasoning, salt, and pepper. Toss to coat evenly.
  3. Spread the seasoned potatoes over one-third of the sheet pan in a single layer, cut sides facing down so they brown well.
  4. Add the trimmed fresh green beans to the same bowl. Drizzle with about 2 teaspoons olive oil and sprinkle with a little of the remaining seasoning. Toss to coat.
  5. Spread the green beans over another third of the sheet pan in a single layer.
  6. Add the chicken breast pieces to the same bowl. Drizzle with the remaining olive oil and sprinkle with the remaining seasoning. Toss until the chicken is evenly coated.
  7. Spread the seasoned chicken pieces over the final third of the sheet pan in a single layer, leaving a little space between pieces so they roast instead of steam.
  8. Roast for 20–25 minutes, stirring the potatoes and green beans once halfway through, until the chicken reaches 165°F (74°C) in the thickest pieces and the potatoes are fork-tender.
  9. If you want extra browning, broil on high for 1–2 minutes at the end, watching closely so nothing burns.
  10. Turn off the oven. Transfer the cooked chicken, potatoes, and green beans to plates in portions as listed below, then serve with optional lemon wedges.

Portion Sizes (Cooked Approx.)

  • Frank (F · 1.0×): ~8 oz cooked chicken, ~8 oz potatoes, ~5 oz green beans
  • Susan (S · 0.75×): ~6 oz cooked chicken, ~6 oz potatoes, ~4 oz green beans
  • Elise (E · 0.5×): ~4 oz cooked chicken, ~4 oz potatoes, ~3 oz green beans

Nutrition (Per Person)

  • Frank: 600 kcal, 58 g protein, 50 g carbs, 17 g fat, 5 g fiber, 575 mg sodium
  • Susan: 450 kcal, 44 g protein, 38 g carbs, 13 g fat, 4 g fiber, 430 mg sodium
  • Elise: 300 kcal, 29 g protein, 25 g carbs, 8 g fat, 2.5 g fiber, 290 mg sodium
