Breakfast Burrito Plates + Sweet Potato Hash

Nutrition (approx)

Frank: 640 kcal • P 55g • C 60g • F 16g • Sat 6g • Na 700mg • Fiber 10g
Susan/Elise (each): 480 kcal • P 41g • C 45g • F 12g • Sat 4g • Na 525mg • Fiber 8g

Ingredients (purchase-clean)

  • 6 each whole-wheat tortillas
  • 6 each eggs
  • 12 slices deli turkey
  • 1.75 lb sweet potatoes
  • 1 bag baby spinach
  • kosher salt
  • black pepper
  • garlic powder
  • onion powder
  • smoked paprika
  • Italian seasoning
  • hot sauce
  • Dijon mustard
  • extra-virgin olive oil
  • salsa

Steps

  1. Oven: heat to 425°F.
  2. Hash: dice sweet potatoes. Toss with 2 tsp olive oil, smoked paprika, black pepper, modest kosher salt. Roast 22–26 minutes, stirring once.
  3. Eggs: scramble 6 eggs in a nonstick skillet with black pepper.
  4. Spinach: sauté spinach 60–90 seconds.
  5. Build burritos: each person gets 2 tortillas. Fill with scrambled egg portion + turkey + spinach. Add 2 tbsp salsa per burrito (measured).
  6. Warm burritos in dry skillet 1–2 minutes per side.
  7. Serve with hash per Notes.

Notes

Weekend breakfast burrito comfort, but the hash and fillings are portioned and high-protein. Portions (per person): Frank: 2 burritos + 1 cup hash Susan: 2 burritos + 3/4 cup hash Elise: 2 burritos + 3/4 cup hash Tips (recipe-specific): Roast hash until browned—color is the flavor. Warm burritos in a dry skillet so you don’t add extra oil.
