Covered-Bake Shawarma Chicken + Onions + Peppers

Nutrition (approx)

Frank: 650 kcal • P 60g • C 70g • F 14g
Susan/Elise (each): 488 kcal • P 45g • C 52g • F 10g

Ingredients (purchase-clean)

  • 1.75 lb boneless skinless chicken breast
  • 1 each onion
  • 2 each bell pepper
  • low-sodium chicken broth
  • 1 cup dry brown rice
  • 1 each lemon
  • kosher salt
  • black pepper
  • garlic powder
  • onion powder
  • smoked paprika
  • Italian seasoning
  • ground cumin
  • chili powder
  • red pepper flakes
  • extra-virgin olive oil
  • low-sodium chicken broth
  • low-sodium beef broth
  • low-sodium soy sauce
  • hot sauce
  • Dijon mustard
  • balsamic vinegar
  • apple cider vinegar
  • cornstarch

Steps (oven)

  1. Rice: cook 1 cup dry brown rice.
  2. Oven: heat to 400°F.
  3. Season: mix cumin (2 tsp), smoked paprika (2 tsp), garlic powder (2 tsp), black pepper, and a modest pinch of kosher salt. Toss chicken strips + sliced onion/peppers with seasoning and 2 tsp olive oil.
  4. Bake covered: add chicken/veg to a covered casserole (or foil tightly). Add broth (1/2 cup). Bake 400°F 20–25 minutes to 165°F.
  5. Uncover: broil 2–3 minutes for edges if desired.
  6. Finish: squeeze lemon juice (2 tbsp) over top. Serve over rice per Notes.

Notes

Shawarma directionally with a covered bake to keep it juicy; lemon finish makes it pop. Portions (per person): Frank: 7 oz chicken/veg + 1 cup rice Susan: 5 oz chicken/veg + 3/4 cup rice Elise: 5 oz chicken/veg + 3/4 cup rice Tips (recipe-specific): Covering prevents dryness; broil at the end adds edge. Add lemon after baking so it stays bright.
