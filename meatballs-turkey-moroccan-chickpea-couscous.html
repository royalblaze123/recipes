Moroccan-Spiced Turkey Meatballs with Chickpeas over Whole Wheat Couscous

Prep: 22 min   Cook: 18 min   Total: 40 min

Ingredients

  • 1.5 lb lean ground turkey 93%
  • 1 egg
  • 0.25 bag whole wheat panko crumbs
  • 0.5 can no-salt-added chickpeas
  • 0.75 cup dry whole wheat couscous
  • 0.33 carton low-sodium chicken broth
  • olive oil
  • cumin
  • coriander
  • cinnamon
  • smoked paprika
  • garlic powder
  • black pepper
  • salt substitute
  • tomato paste
  • lemon

Hard rules enforced: Dinner validations (calories 600–650 Frank, sat fat ≤8g, sodium ≤700mg, fiber ≥10g, added sugar ≤6g). Quantities appear before items; fresh meat/poultry/fish use weights. Condiments/spices/seasonings have no measurements in Ingredients; all measurements are in Steps. No olives, no squash, no Brussels sprouts, no zucchini, no tofu/tempeh, no yogurt in dinners.

Step-by-step Instructions

  1. Couscous: bring 3/4 cup water to a boil. Stir in 3/4 cup whole wheat couscous, cover 5 minutes, fluff.
  2. Meatballs: mix turkey + 1 egg + 1/3 cup panko + 2 tsp cumin + 1 tsp coriander + 1/4 tsp cinnamon + 1 tsp smoked paprika + 1 tsp garlic powder + 1/2 tsp black pepper + 1/2 tsp salt substitute.
  3. Form 18 meatballs. Bake at 425°F for 14–16 minutes until 165°F.
  4. Sauce: simmer 3/4 cup broth + 1/2 cup water + 1 tbsp tomato paste + 1 tsp lemon juice for 5 minutes.
  5. Chickpeas: drain/rinse 1/2 can chickpeas; add to sauce with meatballs; simmer 3 minutes.
  6. Serve meatballs and chickpeas over couscous. Portion: Frank 7 meatballs; Susan 5; Elise 5; couscous portion scaled similarly.
  7. Finish with 1 tsp lemon juice per plate.
