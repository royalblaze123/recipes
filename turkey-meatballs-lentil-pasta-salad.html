Turkey Meatballs Marinara + Lentil Pasta + Salad

Oven-baked turkey meatballs in marinara over lentil pasta, served with a simple salad.

Servings

3 servings (Frank, Susan, Elise).

Times

  • Prep time: 20 minutes
  • Cook time: 20 minutes
  • Total time: 40 minutes

Ingredients

Main

  • 2 lb lean ground turkey
  • 8 oz lentil pasta
  • 1 jar marinara sauce
  • 2 hearts romaine hearts
  • 1 each cucumber
  • 2 tbsp extra-virgin olive oil
  • 2 tbsp red wine vinegar
  • 1 tsp garlic powder
  • 1 tsp dried oregano
  • 1 tsp ground black pepper

Step-by-step Instructions

  1. Meatballs: Heat oven to 425°F. Mix turkey with garlic powder, oregano, and black pepper. Form small 1-inch meatballs and bake 12–15 minutes until 165°F. Measure cooked meatballs for dinner and refrigerate any extra.
  2. Pasta: Cook lentil pasta until al dente. Drain and toss with a splash of water to keep loose.
  3. Warm sauce: Warm 1 cup marinara in a saucepan and add baked meatballs to coat.
  4. Salad: Chop romaine and slice cucumber. Dress with olive oil and vinegar and black pepper. Portion per person.
  5. Serve: Plate lentil pasta topped with meatballs and marinara, with salad on the side.

Portion Sizes

Portions (per person):
Frank: 8 oz cooked meatballs + 2 oz dry lentil pasta cooked + 1 cup salad

Susan: 6 oz cooked meatballs + 1.5 oz dry lentil pasta cooked + 3/4 cup salad

Elise: 6 oz cooked meatballs + 1.5 oz dry lentil pasta cooked + 3/4 cup salad
