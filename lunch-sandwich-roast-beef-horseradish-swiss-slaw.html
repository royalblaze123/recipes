Lunch Sandwich Roast Beef Horseradish Swiss Slaw

Lean roast beef sandwiches with horseradish-yogurt spread, Swiss, and crunchy slaw built into the sandwich (no side).

Servings

3 servings.

Times

  • Prep time: 12 minutes
  • Cook time: 0 minutes
  • Total time: 12 minutes

Ingredients

Main Dish

  • 6 slices whole wheat bread
  • 14 slices deli roast beef
  • 3 slices Swiss cheese
  • 1 container nonfat Greek yogurt
  • 2 tsp prepared horseradish
  • 1 bag coleslaw mix
  • 1 each apple
  • 1 jar Dijon mustard
  • 1 jar black pepper
    • 

    Preparation Steps

    1. Set up: lay out 6 slices whole wheat bread (3 sandwiches).
    2. Spread: mix 3 tbsp Greek yogurt with 2 tsp prepared horseradish and 1 tsp Dijon mustard. Measure out 3 tbsp spread for this recipe; refrigerate the rest.
    3. Slaw: measure out 6 packed cups coleslaw mix for these sandwiches; refrigerate remaining slaw.
    4. Apple: slice 1 apple into thin slices (use about 12 slices total).
    5. Build (Frank): 2 slices bread + 6 slices roast beef + 1 slice Swiss + ~2 cups coleslaw mix + 4 apple slices + ~1 tbsp horseradish-yogurt spread.
    6. Build (Susan): 2 slices bread + 4 slices roast beef + 1 slice Swiss + ~2 cups coleslaw mix + 4 apple slices + ~1 tbsp horseradish-yogurt spread.
    7. Build (Elise): 2 slices bread + 4 slices roast beef + 1 slice Swiss + ~2 cups coleslaw mix + 4 apple slices + ~1 tbsp horseradish-yogurt spread.
    8. Close sandwiches and press gently to hold the slaw.
      9. 

      Notes

      Portions (per person):


      Frank – 1 whole sandwich: 6 slices roast beef, 1 slice Swiss, ~2 cups slaw, 4 apple slices, ~1 tbsp horseradish-yogurt.


      Susan – 1 whole sandwich: 4 slices roast beef, 1 slice Swiss, ~2 cups slaw, 4 apple slices, ~1 tbsp horseradish-yogurt.


      Elise – 1 whole sandwich: 4 slices roast beef, 1 slice Swiss, ~2 cups slaw, 4 apple slices, ~1 tbsp horseradish-yogurt.


      Tips (recipe-specific):


      Coleslaw mix is the veg volume anchor—pack it in, then press the sandwich closed.


      Ask for medium-thin roast beef slices (sandwich slice, not shaved).


      If you need less sweetness, use fewer apple slices and add more slaw.
