Turkey Meatball Sub Plates + Marinara Dip

Nutrition (approx)

Frank: 650 kcal • P 58g • C 62g • F 16g • Sat 6g • Na 700mg • Fiber 10g
Susan/Elise (each): 488 kcal • P 44g • C 46g • F 12g • Sat 4g • Na 525mg • Fiber 8g

Ingredients (purchase-clean)

  • 3 each whole-wheat sub rolls
  • 1.75 lb 93% lean ground turkey
  • 1 carton egg whites
  • 1 package whole-wheat panko breadcrumbs
  • 1 jar marinara sauce
  • 6 oz reduced-fat shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1 bag shredded lettuce
  • kosher salt
  • black pepper
  • garlic powder
  • onion powder
  • Italian seasoning
  • smoked paprika
  • extra-virgin olive oil
  • Dijon mustard
  • hot sauce
  • rice vinegar

Steps

  1. Oven: heat to 425°F.
  2. Meatballs: mix turkey with 1/2 cup egg whites, 3/4 cup panko, garlic powder, onion powder, Italian seasoning, black pepper, modest kosher salt. Form 18 meatballs (6 per person).
  3. Bake meatballs 14–16 minutes to 165°F.
  4. Warm 1 cup marinara (measured) for sauce + dip.
  5. Assemble subs: each roll gets 6 meatballs + a spoon of marinara + mozzarella (total 6 oz across all subs). Broil 1 minute to melt.
  6. Salad: lettuce with vinegar/hot sauce/black pepper. Serve per Notes.

Notes

Meatball sub comfort with baked turkey meatballs and measured marinara. Portions (per person): Frank: 1 sub (6 meatballs) + big salad Susan: 1 sub (6 meatballs) + big salad Elise: 1 sub (6 meatballs) + big salad Tips (recipe-specific): Bake meatballs on a rack or parchment so they brown, not steam. Keep marinara measured; the broiled cheese top sells it.
