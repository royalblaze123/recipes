Oven Shrimp Scampi-Style + Barilla Protein+

Frank: 650 kcal • P 55g • C 75g • F 16g
Susan/Elise (each): 488 kcal • P 41g • C 56g • F 12g

Steps (oven)

  1. Oven: heat to 425°F.
  2. Pasta: cook Barilla Protein+ al dente; reserve 1/2 cup pasta water; drain.
  3. Shrimp: pat dry. Toss with olive oil (2 tsp), garlic powder (2 tsp), black pepper, modest salt, and red pepper flakes (1/2 tsp).
  4. Roast shrimp: spread on pan; roast 425°F 6–8 minutes until just opaque.
  5. Sauce: in a pan, warm broth (1/2 cup) + lemon juice (2 tbsp) + 2 tsp olive oil; simmer 1 minute.
  6. Toss: combine pasta + shrimp + sauce; add splashes of pasta water to coat. Add parsley.
  7. Serve per Notes.

Notes

Scampi directionally without butter: lemon-broth + measured oil makes it bright and light. Portions (per person): Frank: 2 cups pasta bowl + 7 oz shrimp Susan: 1 1/2 cups pasta bowl + 5 oz shrimp Elise: 1 1/2 cups pasta bowl + 5 oz shrimp Tips (recipe-specific): Roast shrimp fast so it stays tender. Use pasta water to make a glossy coat without extra oil.
