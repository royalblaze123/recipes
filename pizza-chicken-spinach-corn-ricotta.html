Chicken Spinach Corn Ricotta Pizza

NOTES: Built as a full dinner using ready-made whole-grain or thin crust, lean protein, plenty of vegetables, and controlled cheese to support A1c, cholesterol, and blood pressure goals. Use low-sodium sauce and minimal added salt. PORTION SIZES: Frank: ~40% of the pizza; Susan: ~30% of the pizza; Elise: ~30% of the pizza (Frank = 1.0×, Susan/Elise = 0.75× portions).

Ingredients

  • 1 ready-made 12-inch thin whole-wheat pizza crust
  • 1/3 cup no-salt-added tomato or pizza sauce
  • 1/3 cup part-skim ricotta cheese
  • 8 oz cooked boneless, skinless chicken breast, chopped
  • 1 cup fresh baby spinach, roughly chopped
  • 1/3 cup frozen corn (no added salt), thawed and drained
  • 1/4 red onion, thinly sliced
  • 1/2 cup shredded reduced-fat mozzarella cheese
  • 1 tsp dried Italian seasoning
  • Freshly ground black pepper, to taste

Step-by-Step Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 425°F and place the crust on a pizza pan or parchment-lined baking sheet.
  2. Spread tomato or pizza sauce over the crust, then dollop ricotta in small spoonfuls evenly across the surface.
  3. Top with chopped chicken, chopped spinach, thawed corn, and sliced red onion.
  4. Sprinkle shredded mozzarella, Italian seasoning, and black pepper over the toppings.
  5. Bake 12–15 minutes, until the crust is crisp and the cheese is melted and lightly browned.
  6. Cool 3–5 minutes before slicing and portioning for Frank (~40%) and Susan/Elise (~30% each).

Nutrition per Person

  • Frank: ~600 kcal, ~53 g protein, ~50 g carbs, ~18 g fat.
  • Susan: ~450 kcal, ~40 g protein, ~38 g carbs, ~13 g fat.
  • Elise: ~450 kcal, ~40 g protein, ~38 g carbs, ~13 g fat.
