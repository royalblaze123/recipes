Balsamic Chicken Mushroom Pizza + Roasted Green Beans

Thin-crust whole-wheat pizza with thyme chicken, mushrooms, and a light balsamic finish, served with roasted green beans.

Servings

3 servings (Frank, Susan, Elise).

Times

  • Prep time: 35 minutes
  • Cook time: 15 minutes
  • Total time: 50 minutes

Ingredients

Pizza

  • 1 dough ball whole wheat pizza dough
  • 1 can no-salt-added crushed tomatoes
  • 1 package reduced-fat mozzarella
  • 1 lb boneless skinless chicken breast
  • 1 package mushrooms
  • 1 bottle balsamic vinegar
  • 1 tsp garlic powder
  • 1 tsp dried thyme
  • 1 tsp ground black pepper

Side

  • 1 bag green beans
  • 2 tbsp extra-virgin olive oil
  • 1 tsp garlic powder
  • 1 tsp ground black pepper

Step-by-step Instructions

  1. Thaw and warm dough: Thaw 1 dough ball overnight in the refrigerator. Bring to room temperature 60–90 minutes.
  2. Preheat: Place a pizza stone or steel or inverted sheet pan in the oven and preheat to 500°F for 30 minutes.
  3. Cook chicken: Season chicken with garlic powder, thyme, and black pepper. Bake at 425°F until 165°F (about 15–18 minutes). Rest 5 minutes, then slice. Measure 10 oz cooked chicken for the pizza and refrigerate any extra.
  4. Make sauce: Measure 1 1/4 cups crushed tomatoes from the can. Stir in 1 tbsp balsamic vinegar for brightness. Refrigerate remaining tomatoes.
  5. Prep mushrooms: Slice 8 oz mushrooms for the pizza.
  6. Stretch and top: Stretch dough into a thin 10–12 inch round on parchment. Spread sauce. Sprinkle 5 1/2 oz mozzarella. Add chicken and mushrooms. Drizzle 1 tbsp balsamic over the top.
  7. Bake: Slide pizza on parchment onto the hot surface. Bake 8–12 minutes until browned and bubbling.
  8. Roast green beans: Toss green beans with olive oil, garlic powder, and black pepper. Roast at 425°F for 12–15 minutes until blistered-tender.
  9. Portion dinner: Cut pizza into 10 slices and serve with green beans.

Portion Sizes

Portions (per person):
Pizza (1 dough ball): cut into 10 slices.
Frank: 4 slices pizza + 2 cups roasted green beans

Susan: 3 slices pizza + 1 1/2 cups roasted green beans

Elise: 3 slices pizza + 1 1/2 cups roasted green beans
