Lean Beef & Pepper Brown Rice Skillet (RBM Dinner)

Stovetop skillet dinner with lean ground beef, bell peppers, broccoli, and brown rice for a high-protein, one-pan style meal.

Servings

3 servings.

Times

  • Prep time: 20m
  • Cook time: 25m
  • Total time: 45m

Ingredients

  • 18 oz 93% lean ground beef
  • 3/4 cup dry brown rice
  • 1 1/2 cups water
  • 1 head broccoli
  • 2 bell peppers
  • 1 yellow onion
  • 1 tbsp extra-virgin olive oil
  • 2 tsp garlic powder
  • 1 tsp smoked paprika
  • 1 tsp dried Italian seasoning
  • 1/4 tsp kosher
  • 1/2 tsp ground black pepper

Step-by-Step Instructions

  1. Cook the brown rice: In a small saucepan, combine the rinsed brown rice with water (or follow package directions for water amount). Bring to a boil, then reduce heat to low, cover, and simmer for 25–30 minutes until the rice is tender and liquid is absorbed. Turn off heat, fluff with a fork, and keep covered.
  2. While the rice cooks, heat a large nonstick skillet over medium heat. Add the extra-virgin olive oil.
  3. Add the diced onion and sliced bell peppers to the skillet. Sauté for 3–4 minutes until they begin to soften.
  4. Add the lean ground beef to the skillet. Cook, breaking it up with a spatula, for 5–7 minutes until fully browned and cooked through. Drain any visible excess fat if needed.
  5. Sprinkle the garlic powder, smoked paprika, dried Italian seasoning, salt, and pepper over the beef and vegetables. Stir to coat evenly.
  6. Add the chopped broccoli florets to the skillet along with a splash of water if the pan seems dry. Cover and cook for 4–5 minutes, stirring once, until the broccoli is crisp-tender.
  7. Add the cooked brown rice to the skillet. Fold the rice into the beef and vegetable mixture until everything is evenly combined and heated through. Taste and adjust seasoning with a pinch more salt-free seasoning or pepper if desired.
  8. Turn off the heat. Divide the skillet mixture into portions as listed below and serve hot.

Portion Sizes (Cooked Approx.)

  • Frank (F · 1.0×): ~6 oz cooked lean ground beef, ~3/4 cup cooked brown rice, ~1 1/2 cups mixed peppers and broccoli
  • Susan (S · 0.75×): ~4.5 oz cooked lean ground beef, ~1/2 cup cooked brown rice, ~1 cup mixed peppers and broccoli
  • Elise (E · 0.5×): ~3 oz cooked lean ground beef, ~1/3 cup cooked brown rice, ~3/4 cup mixed peppers and broccoli
