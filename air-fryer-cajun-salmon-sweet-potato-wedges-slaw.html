Air Fryer Cajun Salmon with Sweet Potato Wedges and Creamy-Lime Slaw

Prep: 14 min   Cook: 26 min   Total: 40 min

Ingredients

  • 1 package salmon fillets (~1–1.25 lb total)
  • 1 bag sweet potatoes
  • 1 bag coleslaw mix
  • 1 lime
  • 1 package garlic cloves
  • olive oil (tsp) — pantry
  • light mayonnaise (tsp) — pantry
  • apple cider vinegar (tsp) — pantry
  • Cajun seasoning (tsp) — pantry
  • smoked paprika (tsp) — pantry
  • black pepper (tsp) — pantry
  • salt substitute (tsp) — pantry

Ingredient list is purchase-clean (AnyList aggregation). All measuring/used amounts and remainder handling are in Steps.

Step-by-step Instructions

  1. Heat oven to 425°F. Line a sheet pan with parchment. Scrub sweet potatoes; cut into 1/2-inch wedges (keep thickness consistent).
  2. Measure olive oil: use 14 tsp total for this recipe. Toss wedges with 10 tsp olive oil + 6 tsp Cajun seasoning + 2 tsp smoked paprika + 2 tsp salt substitute + 1 tsp black pepper. Roast 22–26 minutes, flipping once, until browned and tender.
  3. Make slaw dressing: measure light mayonnaise 8 tsp, apple cider vinegar 6 tsp, and lime juice 6 tsp (juice the lime). Whisk with 2 tsp salt substitute and 1 tsp black pepper. Toss with coleslaw mix; refrigerate while salmon cooks.
  4. Season salmon: measure Cajun seasoning 6 tsp. Pat salmon dry, then rub with 4 tsp olive oil + 6 tsp Cajun seasoning + 1 tsp black pepper.
  5. Air fry salmon (Cosori): preheat 390°F for 3 minutes. Cook salmon skin-side down 8–11 minutes until flaky and cooked through.
  6. Portion and serve: Frank gets 6 oz salmon + ~1 1/4 cups sweet potato wedges + ~1 1/2 cups slaw. Susan and Elise each get ~4.5 oz salmon + ~1 cup wedges + ~1 1/4 cups slaw.
