Creamy Herb Ranch-Style Yogurt Dip

Category: Dip • Yield: About 3/4 cup (13–14 tbsp) • AnyList-ready

Ingredients

  • 3/4 cup 2% plain Greek yogurt
  • 2 tsp extra-virgin olive oil
  • 2 tsp apple cider vinegar
  • 1 tsp garlic powder
  • 1 tsp onion powder
  • 2 tsp dried dill
  • 1/4 tsp black pepper

Steps

  1. Whisk Greek yogurt, olive oil, apple cider vinegar, garlic powder, onion powder, dill, and black pepper until smooth.
  2. Optional: thin with 1–2 tbsp water, adding 1 tsp at a time, until dip reaches desired consistency.
