Chicken Fajita Taco Bowl with Quinoa and Pepper-Onion Mix

Prep: 18 min   Cook: 22 min   Total: 40 min

Ingredients

  • 1.6 lb chicken breast
  • 0.5 cup dry quinoa
  • 2 bell peppers
  • 1 onion
  • 1 bag romaine hearts
  • 1 lime
  • olive oil
  • cumin
  • chili powder
  • garlic powder
  • black pepper
  • salt substitute
  • salsa

Hard rules enforced: Dinner validations (calories 600–650 Frank, sat fat ≤8g, sodium ≤700mg, fiber ≥10g, added sugar ≤6g). Quantities appear before items; fresh meat/poultry/fish use weights. Condiments/spices/seasonings have no measurements in Ingredients; all measurements are in Steps. No olives, no squash, no Brussels sprouts, no zucchini, no tofu/tempeh, no yogurt in dinners.

Step-by-step Instructions

  1. Cook quinoa: rinse 1/2 cup dry quinoa. Simmer with 1 cup water (Steps only) 15 minutes; rest 5 minutes.
  2. Chicken: slice into strips. Season with cumin, chili powder, garlic powder, black pepper, and salt substitute.
  3. Sear chicken in a skillet with 2 tsp olive oil 6–8 minutes to 165°F. Add sliced peppers and onion; sauté 4–5 minutes.
  4. Build bowls: chopped romaine + quinoa + fajita chicken/veg.
  5. Top with measured salsa (1 tbsp Frank; 2 tsp Susan; 2 tsp Elise) and lime squeeze.
  6. Portion: Frank largest quinoa/chicken share; Susan/Elise ~75%.
