Smoky Red Pepper Sauce

Category: Sauce • Yield: About 2/3 cup (11–12 tbsp) • AnyList-ready

Ingredients

  • 1 jar roasted red peppers
  • 1 tbsp tomato paste
  • 1 tbsp red wine vinegar
  • 2 tsp extra-virgin olive oil
  • 1 clove garlic
  • 1/2 tsp smoked paprika
  • 1/4 tsp black pepper

Steps

  1. Drain roasted red peppers. Measure out 1/2 cup peppers; refrigerate remaining peppers.
  2. Blend measured peppers with tomato paste, vinegar, olive oil, garlic, smoked paprika, and black pepper until smooth.
  3. Thin with 1–3 tbsp water, adding 1 tbsp at a time, until pourable.
