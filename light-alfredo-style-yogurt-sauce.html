Light Alfredo-Style Yogurt Sauce

Category: Sauce • Yield: About 1 3/8 cups (22 tbsp) • AnyList-ready

Ingredients

  • 3/4 cup 2% plain Greek yogurt
  • 1/2 cup 1% milk
  • 2 tsp extra-virgin olive oil
  • 4 tsp grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 tsp garlic powder
  • 1/4 tsp black pepper
  • 1 tsp cornstarch

Steps

  1. In a small saucepan over medium heat, whisk milk, olive oil, garlic powder, and black pepper.
  2. Whisk cornstarch with 2 tsp water until smooth; whisk into the pan and simmer 60–90 seconds until slightly thickened.
  3. Remove from heat and cool 2 minutes. Whisk in Greek yogurt and Parmesan until smooth. Do not boil after adding yogurt.
