Warm Lunch Skillet: Lean Beef, Pinto Beans, and Peppers over Cauliflower Rice

Prep: 18 min   Cook: 14 min   Total: 32 min

Ingredients

  • 1.0 lb lean ground beef 93%
  • 0.5 can no-salt-added pinto beans
  • 1 bag frozen cauliflower rice
  • 2 bell peppers
  • 1 onion
  • 1 lime
  • olive oil
  • cumin
  • smoked paprika
  • garlic powder
  • black pepper
  • salt substitute
  • salsa

Rules enforced: Clean AnyList Ingredients (quantities before items; fresh meat/poultry/fish use weights; packaged items use fractional containers when partial). Condiments/spices/seasonings have no measurements in Ingredients; all measurements are in Steps. No olives, no squash, no Brussels sprouts, no zucchini, no tofu/tempeh. Lunch validations: Frank calories 500–550, sat fat ≤6g, sodium ≤650mg, fiber ≥8g, added sugar ≤6g.

Step-by-step Instructions

  1. Cauliflower rice: microwave 1 bag cauliflower rice 5–6 minutes. Measure 3 cups cooked; drain/pat dry; refreeze remainder.
  2. Beef: brown 1 lb 93% lean ground beef with 2 tsp olive oil 7–9 minutes. Season with 2 tsp cumin + 1 tsp smoked paprika + 1 tsp garlic powder + 1/2 tsp black pepper + 1/2 tsp salt substitute.
  3. Veg: slice 2 bell peppers and 1 onion; add and cook 5 minutes.
  4. Beans: drain and rinse 1/2 can pinto beans; stir in with 2 tbsp water; warm 2 minutes.
  5. Stir in cauliflower rice; heat 2 minutes.
  6. Finish: salsa measured (Frank 1 tbsp; Susan 2 tsp; Elise 2 tsp) + 1 tsp lime juice per plate.
  7. Portion: Frank largest skillet portion; Susan/Elise ~75%.
