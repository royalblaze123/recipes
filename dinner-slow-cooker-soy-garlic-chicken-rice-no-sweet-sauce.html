Soy-Garlic Chicken + Rice (No Sweet Sauce)

Nutrition (approx)

Frank: 650 kcal • P 60g • C 70g • F 14g
Susan/Elise (each): 488 kcal • P 45g • C 52g • F 10g

Ingredients (purchase-clean)

  • 1.75 lb boneless skinless chicken breast
  • low-sodium soy sauce
  • cornstarch
  • 1 cup dry brown rice
  • 1 bag frozen broccoli florets
  • low-sodium chicken broth
  • kosher salt
  • black pepper
  • garlic powder
  • onion powder
  • smoked paprika
  • Italian seasoning
  • ground cumin
  • chili powder
  • red pepper flakes
  • extra-virgin olive oil
  • low-sodium chicken broth
  • low-sodium beef broth
  • low-sodium soy sauce
  • hot sauce
  • Dijon mustard
  • balsamic vinegar
  • apple cider vinegar
  • cornstarch

Steps (slow cooker)

  1. Cook rice (1 cup dry).
  2. Chicken + 1/2 cup broth; season garlic powder, pepper; modest salt.
  3. LOW 4–5 hr or HIGH 2–3 hr to 165°F.
  4. Whisk soy sauce (6 tbsp) + water (1 cup) + cornstarch (1 tbsp); stir in; HIGH 15 min to thicken.
  5. Microwave broccoli; drain/pat dry; stir in 10 min.
  6. Serve: chicken + broccoli + rice per Notes.

Notes

Takeout vibe without sugar: thickened soy-garlic coats everything. Portions (per person): Frank: 7 oz chicken + 1 cup cooked rice + 1 cup broccoli Susan: 5 oz chicken + 3/4 cup rice + 3/4 cup broccoli Elise: 5 oz chicken + 3/4 cup rice + 3/4 cup broccoli Tips (recipe-specific): Add broccoli after thickening so it stays structured. Thick sauce = less soy needed.
