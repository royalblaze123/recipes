One-Pan Cod + Sweet Potato + Green Beans

Nutrition (approx)

Frank: 640 kcal • P 58g • C 50g • F 16g • Sat 3g • Na 680mg • Fiber 10g
Susan/Elise (each): 480 kcal • P 44g • C 38g • F 12g • Sat 2g • Na 510mg • Fiber 8g

Ingredients (purchase-clean)

  • 1.50 lb cod fillets
  • 2.00 lb sweet potatoes
  • 1 bag frozen green beans
  • 1 each lemon
  • kosher salt
  • black pepper
  • garlic powder
  • onion powder
  • smoked paprika
  • Italian seasoning
  • ground cumin
  • chili powder
  • red pepper flakes
  • extra-virgin olive oil
  • low-sodium chicken broth

Steps

  1. Oven: heat to 425°F.
  2. Sweet potatoes: cube; toss with 2 tsp olive oil, smoked paprika, garlic powder, black pepper, modest kosher salt. Roast 18 minutes.
  3. Green beans: add beans tossed with 2 tsp olive oil and black pepper; roast 10 minutes.
  4. Cod: season cod with garlic powder, Italian seasoning, black pepper, and lemon juice. Add to pan and roast 8–10 minutes until flaky.
  5. Finish with lemon. Serve per Notes.

Notes

Cod dinner that doesn’t feel diet—sweet potato caramelization + lemon carry the flavor. Portions (per person): Frank: About 7 oz cod + 1 cup sweet potato + 1 cup green beans Susan: About 5 oz cod + 3/4 cup sweet potato + 3/4 cup green beans Elise: About 5 oz cod + 3/4 cup sweet potato + 3/4 cup green beans Tips (recipe-specific): Roast sweet potato first so it browns. Don’t overbake cod—pull when it flakes easily.
