Warm Lunch Skillet: Garlic Shrimp, Broccoli, and Whole Wheat Orzo

Prep: 18 min   Cook: 14 min   Total: 32 min

Ingredients

  • 1.1 lb shrimp
  • 8 oz whole wheat orzo
  • 1 bag frozen broccoli florets
  • 0.5 carton low-sodium chicken broth
  • 1 lemon
  • olive oil
  • garlic
  • black pepper
  • salt substitute
  • Italian seasoning

Rules enforced: Clean AnyList Ingredients (quantities before items; fresh meat/poultry/fish use weights; packaged items use fractional containers when partial). Condiments/spices/seasonings have no measurements in Ingredients; all measurements are in Steps. No olives, no squash, no Brussels sprouts, no zucchini, no tofu/tempeh. Lunch validations: Frank calories 500–550, sat fat ≤6g, sodium ≤650mg, fiber ≥8g, added sugar ≤6g.

Step-by-step Instructions

  1. Broccoli: microwave frozen broccoli 3–4 minutes to thaw; pat dry. Measure 4 cups; refreeze remainder.
  2. Orzo: simmer 8 oz whole wheat orzo in 2 cups broth + 1 cup water, stirring often, 9–10 minutes until al dente.
  3. Shrimp: pat dry 1.1 lb shrimp. Season with 1/4 tsp black pepper + 1/4 tsp salt substitute + 1 tsp Italian seasoning.
  4. Sauté shrimp with 2 tsp olive oil 3 minutes. Add 1 tsp minced garlic; cook 30 seconds.
  5. Add broccoli to skillet; toss 2 minutes to warm.
  6. Combine shrimp/broccoli with cooked orzo; splash in 2–4 tbsp broth if needed to loosen.
  7. Finish: add 2 tsp lemon juice per plate.
