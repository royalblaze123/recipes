Instant Pot Beef + Barley (Hearty Bowl)

Nutrition (approx)

Frank: 650 kcal • P 55g • C 65g • F 16g
Susan/Elise (each): 488 kcal • P 41g • C 49g • F 12g

Ingredients (purchase-clean)

  • 1.25 lb lean sirloin steak
  • 1 cup dry pearl barley
  • 1 lb carrots
  • 2 stalks celery
  • 1 each onion
  • low-sodium beef broth
  • kosher salt
  • black pepper
  • garlic powder
  • onion powder
  • smoked paprika
  • Italian seasoning
  • ground cumin
  • chili powder
  • red pepper flakes
  • extra-virgin olive oil
  • low-sodium chicken broth
  • low-sodium beef broth
  • low-sodium soy sauce
  • hot sauce
  • Dijon mustard
  • balsamic vinegar
  • apple cider vinegar
  • cornstarch

Steps (Instant Pot)

  1. Sauté: olive oil + onion 3 minutes; add carrots/celery 2 minutes.
  2. Brown beef: add cubed sirloin; sear 2–3 minutes.
  3. Load: add broth (7 cups) + barley (1 cup). Season with garlic powder, black pepper, and a modest pinch of salt.
  4. Pressure cook: High 18 minutes; Natural Release 12 minutes, then Quick Release.
  5. Reduce: if you want thicker, Sauté 5–8 minutes.
  6. Serve per Notes.

Notes

Hearty, structured barley bowl—tender beef and chewy barley without mush. Portions (per person): Frank: About 2 1/2 cups (heaping bowl) Susan: About 2 cups (bowl) Elise: About 2 cups (bowl) Tips (recipe-specific): Natural release helps barley finish without blowing out texture. Reduce on Sauté to concentrate flavor instead of adding more salt.
