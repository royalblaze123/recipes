Air Fryer Orange-Style Chicken Bites + Broccoli + Rice

Ingredients (purchase-clean)

  • 2.00 lb chicken breast
  • 1 carton egg whites
  • cornstarch
  • 1 each orange

Steps

  1. Rice: cook 1 cup dry brown rice with 2 cups water 35–40 minutes. Rest 5 minutes, fluff.
  2. Air fryer: preheat to 400°F.
  3. Chicken: cut 2.00 lb chicken breast into 1-inch chunks.
  4. Coat: toss chicken with 3 tbsp cornstarch + 1/2 tsp kosher salt + 1/2 tsp black pepper. Then dip quickly in 1/2 cup egg whites and re-dust with 2 tbsp cornstarch.
  5. Cook: Basket A, single layer. 400°F for 10–12 minutes with shake reminder ON; shake at prompt. Cook to 165°F.
  6. Sauce: in a saucepan, whisk 3 tbsp low-sodium soy sauce + 2 tbsp rice vinegar + 2 tsp orange juice + 1 tsp orange zest + 1 tsp ginger + 1 tsp cornstarch + 1/4 cup water. Simmer 2–3 minutes until glossy.
  7. Broccoli: roast frozen broccoli at 425°F for 16–20 minutes with 2 tsp olive oil, or air fry in Basket B 400°F for 10–12 minutes (shake once).
  8. Toss chicken in sauce. Serve with measured rice and broccoli per Notes.

Notes

Takeout-style orange chicken vibe with a measured sauce and real sides. Portions (per person): Frank: Chicken bites + 1 cup cooked rice + 1 cup broccoli Susan: Chicken bites + 3/4 cup cooked rice + 3/4 cup broccoli Elise: Chicken bites + 3/4 cup cooked rice + 3/4 cup broccoli Tips (recipe-specific): Use zest (not sugar) for big orange flavor. Keep chicken in a single layer so it crisps instead of steaming.
