Balsamic Turkey Tenderloin with Carrots & Broccoli (RBM Dinner)

Sheet pan dinner with lean turkey tenderloin, baby potatoes, carrots, and broccoli, finished with a light balsamic glaze.

Servings

3 servings.

Times

  • Prep time: 20m
  • Cook time: 30m
  • Total time: 50m

Ingredients

  • 18 oz turkey breast tenderloin
  • 15 oz baby potatoes
  • 8 oz fresh carrots
  • 12 oz fresh broccoli florets
  • 1 1/2 tbsp extra-virgin olive oil
  • 2 tbsp balsamic vinegar
  • 1 tsp garlic powder
  • 1 tsp dried thyme
  • 1/4 tsp kosher or sea salt
  • 1/2 tsp ground black pepper

Step-by-Step Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 400°F (205°C). Line a large rimmed sheet pan with parchment paper.
  2. In a small bowl, whisk together the olive oil, balsamic vinegar, garlic powder, dried thyme, salt, and pepper to make a light marinade and glaze.
  3. Add the halved baby potatoes and sliced carrots to a large bowl. Spoon about one-third of the balsamic mixture over the vegetables and toss to coat evenly.
  4. Spread the potatoes and carrots over one-half of the sheet pan in a single layer.
  5. Add the fresh broccoli florets to the same bowl and toss with another third of the balsamic mixture. Spread the broccoli over one-quarter of the sheet pan in a single layer.
  6. Place the turkey breast medallions in the bowl and pour in the remaining balsamic mixture. Toss until the turkey pieces are evenly coated.
  7. Arrange the turkey medallions on the remaining quarter of the sheet pan in a single layer, leaving space between pieces.
  8. Roast for 25–30 minutes, stirring the potatoes, carrots, and broccoli once halfway through, until the turkey reaches 165°F (74°C) in the thickest pieces and the potatoes are fork-tender.
  9. If desired, switch the oven to broil on high for 1–2 minutes at the end to lightly caramelize the balsamic on the turkey and vegetables. Watch closely so it does not burn.
  10. Turn off the oven. Transfer turkey, potatoes, carrots, and broccoli to plates in the portions listed below and serve warm.

Portion Sizes (Cooked Approx.)

  • Frank (F · 1.0×): ~7 oz cooked turkey, ~6 oz potatoes, ~1 1/2 cups mixed carrots and broccoli
  • Susan (S · 0.75×): ~5 oz cooked turkey, ~4.5 oz potatoes, ~1 cup mixed carrots and broccoli
  • Elise (E · 0.5×): ~3.5 oz cooked turkey, ~3 oz potatoes, ~3/4 cup mixed carrots and broccoli
