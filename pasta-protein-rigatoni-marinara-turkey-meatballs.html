Protein+ Rigatoni Marinara Turkey Meatballs

Nutrition (approx)

Frank: 719 kcal • P 55g • C 59g • F 32g
Susan/Elise (each): 539 kcal • P 41g • C 44g • F 24g

Ingredients (purchase-clean)

  • 1/3 box Barilla Protein+ rigatoni
  • 1.25 lb 93% lean ground turkey
  • 0.62 jar no-sugar-added marinara sauce
  • 4 tsp extra-virgin olive oil
  • 2 tbsp grated parmesan
  • 2 tsp garlic powder
  • 2 tsp Italian seasoning
  • 1 tsp black pepper
  • 1 tsp kosher salt

Steps

  1. Bring a large pot of water to a boil for the Barilla Protein+ rigatoni. (Use enough water to fully cover pasta; do not add water to Ingredients.)
  2. Heat a large nonstick skillet over medium-high. Add 4 tsp extra-virgin olive oil if listed. Add ground turkey and cook 7–9 minutes, breaking it up, until browned and cooked through.
  3. Jar size note: assume a standard 24 oz jar marinara. Measure out what you need for this recipe; refrigerate the remainder.
  4. Add marinara to the skillet and simmer 5–7 minutes until hot and slightly thickened.
  5. Cook Barilla Protein+ rigatoni to al dente per box time. Reserve 1 cup pasta water, then drain.
  6. Toss pasta into the sauce/skillet. Add reserved pasta water 1–2 tbsp at a time until the sauce clings well.
  7. Portion into bowls using the Portions guidance in Notes.

Notes

Sheet-pan style turkey meatballs in marinara over rigatoni. Built on Barilla Protein+ for a higher-protein pasta plate with close-approx nutrition. Portions (per person): Frank: 2 oz dry Barilla Protein+ rigatoni + 1/3 of the skillet/sauce Susan: 1.5 oz dry Barilla Protein+ rigatoni + 1/4 of the skillet/sauce Elise: 1.5 oz dry Barilla Protein+ rigatoni + 1/4 of the skillet/sauce
