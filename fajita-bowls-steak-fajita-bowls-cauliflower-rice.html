Steak Fajita Bowls (Cauliflower Rice)

Nutrition (approx)

Frank: 640 kcal • P 58g • C 38g • F 22g
Susan/Elise (each): 480 kcal • P 44g • C 28g • F 16g

Ingredients (purchase-clean)

  • 1.25 lb sirloin steak
  • 2 each bell peppers
  • 1 each yellow onion
  • 1 bag frozen cauliflower rice
  • 1 each avocado
  • 1/2 jar salsa
  • 1/2 tub 2% plain Greek yogurt
  • 6 tsp extra-virgin olive oil
  • 1 each lime
  • 2 tsp chili powder
  • 2 tsp cumin
  • 2 tsp garlic powder
  • 1 tsp smoked paprika
  • 1 tsp black pepper
  • 1 tsp kosher salt

Steps

  1. Steak: pat dry and slice thin against the grain. Season with chili powder, cumin, garlic powder, smoked paprika, black pepper, and kosher salt.
  2. Veg: slice bell peppers and onion into strips.
  3. Cook steak: heat a skillet over medium-high. Add 2 tsp olive oil and cook steak 2–3 minutes, stirring often, until just cooked. Transfer steak to a plate.
  4. Cook veg: add remaining 4 tsp olive oil to the skillet. Cook peppers/onion 5–7 minutes until browned at edges.
  5. Finish: return steak to the skillet with 2 tsp lime juice and toss 30 seconds.
  6. Cauliflower rice: microwave the frozen cauliflower rice per bag directions. Drain any excess moisture. Season with a pinch of black pepper and a squeeze of lime if using.
  7. Yogurt crema: stir 6 tbsp Greek yogurt with 2 tsp lime juice and 1 tsp lime zest until smooth.
  8. Avocado: halve, pit, and dice. For this recipe, plan on using about 1/2 avocado total; refrigerate the remaining avocado with the pit to slow browning.
  9. Build bowls with cauliflower rice, steak fajita mix, salsa, avocado, and crema. Portion per Notes.

Notes

Steak fajita bowls that still hit targets by using cauliflower rice and measured toppings. Portions (per person): Frank: 1/3 of the cauliflower rice + 1/3 of the fajita protein/veg + 1/3 of salsa + avocado + yogurt Susan: 1/4 of the cauliflower rice + 1/4 of the fajita protein/veg + 1/4 of salsa + avocado + yogurt Elise: 1/4 of the cauliflower rice + 1/4 of the fajita protein/veg + 1/4 of salsa + avocado + yogurt
