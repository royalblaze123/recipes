Cottage Cheese Veggie Pita + Apple Slices (RBM Lunch, Vegetarian)

High-protein cottage cheese stuffed into a whole-wheat pita with veggies, plus apple slices on the side.

Servings

3 servings.

Ingredients

Sandwich

  • 3 whole-wheat pitas
  • 1 5/8 cups low-fat cottage cheese
  • 3/4 cup fresh tomato
  • 3/4 cup fresh cucumber
  • 3/4 cup shredded fresh romaine or baby spinach

Side

  • 8 oz fresh apple, sliced (about 2 small apples or 1 large)
  • Optional: squeeze of fresh lemon juice to toss with apples to prevent browning

Step-by-Step Instructions

  1. Cut each whole-wheat pita in half to form pockets and gently open them so they are ready to be filled.
  2. In a medium bowl, stir together the low-fat cottage cheese, diced tomato, diced cucumber, and shredded lettuce or spinach until just combined.
  3. Spoon the cottage cheese mixture into each pita half, dividing the total amount into Frank, Susan, and Elise portions as listed below so each person receives their share.
  4. Slice the apples and, if desired, toss the slices with a small amount of lemon juice to help reduce browning.
  5. Divide the apple slices into three small containers or bags according to the side portion sizes listed below.
  6. Serve immediately or wrap each filled pita in parchment or foil and refrigerate. Pack the apple slices separately for a portable, high-protein vegetarian lunch.

Portion Sizes (Per Person)

Frank: 1 whole-wheat pita (halved), 3/4 cup low-fat cottage cheese, 1/4 cup diced tomato, 1/4 cup diced cucumber, 1/4 cup shredded lettuce or spinach, 1/2 small apple (about 3 oz), sliced.

 

Susan: 1 whole-wheat pita (halved), 1/2 cup low-fat cottage cheese, 3 tbsp diced tomato, 3 tbsp diced cucumber, 3 tbsp shredded lettuce or spinach, 2.5 oz apple slices.

 

Elise: 1 whole-wheat pita (halved), 1/3 cup low-fat cottage cheese, 2 tbsp diced tomato, 2 tbsp diced cucumber, 2 tbsp shredded lettuce or spinach, 2 oz apple slices.

Nutrition (Per Person)

  • Frank: 400 kcal, 36 g protein, 40 g carbs, 7 g fat, 6 g fiber, 540 mg sodium
  • Susan: 370 kcal, 32 g protein, 36 g carbs, 6 g fat, 5.5 g fiber, 500 mg sodium
  • Elise: 340 kcal, 28 g protein, 32 g carbs, 5 g fat, 5 g fiber, 460 mg sodium
