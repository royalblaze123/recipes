Lunch Sandwich Turkey Apple Cheddar Mustard

Turkey-apple cheddar sandwiches with mustard and romaine—slow carbs from bread + fruit, with high protein and big veg inside.

Servings

3 servings.

Times

  • Prep time: 12 minutes
  • Cook time: 0 minutes
  • Total time: 12 minutes

Ingredients

Main Dish

  • 6 slices whole wheat bread
  • 14 slices deli turkey
  • 3 slices cheddar cheese
  • 1 each apple
  • 1 package romaine hearts
  • 1 jar Dijon mustard
  • 1 jar black pepper
    • 

    Preparation Steps

    1. Veg: tear romaine. Measure out 6 packed cups romaine for these sandwiches; refrigerate remaining romaine.
    2. Apple: slice 1 apple into thin slices (about 12 slices total).
    3. Build (Frank): 2 slices bread + 6 slices deli turkey + 1 slice cheddar + ~2 cups romaine + 4 apple slices + 1 tsp Dijon mustard.
    4. Build (Susan): 2 slices bread + 4 slices deli turkey + 1 slice cheddar + ~2 cups romaine + 4 apple slices + 1 tsp Dijon mustard.
    5. Build (Elise): 2 slices bread + 4 slices deli turkey + 1 slice cheddar + ~2 cups romaine + 4 apple slices + 1 tsp Dijon mustard.
    6. Close sandwiches and wrap tightly.
      7. 

      Notes

      Portions (per person):


      Frank – 1 whole sandwich: 6 slices turkey, 1 slice cheddar, ~2 cups romaine, 4 apple slices, ~1 tsp Dijon.


      Susan – 1 whole sandwich: 4 slices turkey, 1 slice cheddar, ~2 cups romaine, 4 apple slices, ~1 tsp Dijon.


      Elise – 1 whole sandwich: 4 slices turkey, 1 slice cheddar, ~2 cups romaine, 4 apple slices, ~1 tsp Dijon.


      Tips (recipe-specific):


      Cheddar + turkey is plenty salty—keep mustard measured so sodium doesn’t creep.


      Romaine is the volume anchor; pack it tight so it doesn’t slide out.


      If you want fewer carbs, use fewer apple slices and add more romaine.

      Nutrition (per person)

      • Frank: 530 kcal; 50 g protein; 46 g carbs; 18 g fat; 8 g fiber; 660 mg sodium
      • Susan: 398 kcal; 38 g protein; 34 g carbs; 14 g fat; 6 g fiber; 495 mg sodium
      • Elise: 398 kcal; 38 g protein; 34 g carbs; 14 g fat; 6 g fiber; 495 mg sodium
