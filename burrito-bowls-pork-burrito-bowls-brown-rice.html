Pork Burrito Bowls (Brown Rice)

Nutrition (approx)

Frank: 645 kcal • P 58g • C 48g • F 20g
Susan/Elise (each): 484 kcal • P 44g • C 36g • F 15g

Ingredients (purchase-clean)

  • 1.50 lb pork tenderloin
  • 1 can black beans
  • 1 bag frozen fajita-style pepper blend
  • 1 cup dry brown rice
  • 1 each avocado
  • 1/2 jar salsa verde
  • 1/2 tub 2% plain Greek yogurt
  • 1 each lime
  • 6 tsp extra-virgin olive oil
  • 2 tsp chili powder
  • 2 tsp cumin
  • 2 tsp garlic powder
  • 1 tsp smoked paprika
  • 1 tsp black pepper
  • 1 tsp kosher salt

Steps

  1. Rice: cook brown rice using your preferred method. For this recipe, plan to cook 1 cup dry brown rice (yields about 3 cups cooked). Use about 2 1/2 cups cooked for the bowls; refrigerate any remainder.
  2. Pork: pat tenderloin dry and slice into 1/2-inch medallions. Season with chili powder, cumin, garlic powder, smoked paprika, black pepper, and kosher salt.
  3. Cook pork in a hot nonstick skillet with 2 tsp olive oil for 3–4 minutes per side until 145°F. Rest 3 minutes, then slice into bite-size pieces.
  4. Veg: cook frozen fajita pepper blend in the same skillet with remaining 4 tsp olive oil 6–8 minutes until browned.
  5. Beans: can size note—use a standard 15 oz can black beans. Drain and rinse. Measure out 1 1/2 cups beans; refrigerate any remainder.
  6. Combine: add pork and measured beans to the peppers and warm 2 minutes. Finish with 2 tsp lime juice.
  7. Yogurt-verde drizzle: stir 6 tbsp Greek yogurt with 6 tbsp salsa verde until smooth.
  8. Avocado: halve, pit, and dice. Use about 1/2 avocado total; refrigerate remaining avocado with the pit to slow browning.
  9. Build bowls and portion per Notes.

Notes

Verde burrito bowls with pork, peppers, beans, and a salsa-verde yogurt drizzle. Portions (per person): Frank: 1/3 of the brown rice + 1/3 of the protein mix + 1/3 of the toppings (salsa verde + avocado + yogurt-verde drizzle) Susan: 1/4 of the brown rice + 1/4 of the protein mix + 1/4 of the toppings (salsa verde + avocado + yogurt-verde drizzle) Elise: 1/4 of the brown rice + 1/4 of the protein mix + 1/4 of the toppings (salsa verde + avocado + yogurt-verde drizzle)
