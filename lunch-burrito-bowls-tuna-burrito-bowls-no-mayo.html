Tuna Burrito Bowls (No Mayo)

Nutrition (approx)

Frank: 530 kcal • P 54g • C 50g • F 14g
Susan/Elise (each): 398 kcal • P 40g • C 38g • F 10g

Ingredients (purchase-clean)

  • 3 cans tuna
  • 1 can black beans
  • 1 cup dry brown rice
  • 1 bag shredded lettuce
  • 1 each avocado
  • 1/2 jar salsa
  • 1 each lime
  • 4 tsp extra-virgin olive oil
  • 2 tsp garlic powder
  • 1 tsp black pepper
  • 1 tsp kosher salt

Steps

  1. Rice: rinse 1 cup dry brown rice. Cook with 2 cups water (or low-sodium broth): bring to a boil, cover, reduce to low, and simmer 35–40 minutes. Rest 5 minutes, then fluff.
  2. Tuna: Can size note—use a standard 5 oz can tuna.. Use three 5 oz cans tuna. Drain very well.
  3. Warm tuna in a skillet with 2 tsp olive oil for 1–2 minutes. Season with garlic powder, black pepper, and kosher salt.
  4. Beans: Can size note—use a standard 15 oz can black beans.. Drain/rinse. Use 1 1/2 cups beans; refrigerate remainder.
  5. Combine: stir beans into tuna and warm 1 minute. Finish with 2 tsp lime juice.
  6. Assembly: divide lettuce (or cauliflower rice if listed) into three bowls.
  7. Top each bowl with the protein mix and measured rice (or other carb component if used).
  8. Add salsa and measured diced avocado on top.
  9. Portion per Notes.
  10. Avocado: halve, pit, and dice. Use about 1/2 avocado total; refrigerate remaining avocado with the pit to slow browning.

Notes

Pantry burrito bowls: tuna + beans with measured rice, salsa, and avocado. Portions (per person): Frank: 1 large burrito bowl (about 1/3 of the batch) + 1/3 avocado + salsa Susan: 1 medium burrito bowl (about 1/4 of the batch) + 1/4 avocado + salsa Elise: 1 medium burrito bowl (about 1/4 of the batch) + 1/4 avocado + salsa
