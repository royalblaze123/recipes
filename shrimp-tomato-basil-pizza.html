Shrimp Tomato Basil Pizza + Roasted Broccoli

Thin-crust whole-wheat pizza topped with garlicky shrimp, tomatoes, and basil, served with roasted broccoli.

Servings

3 servings (Frank, Susan, Elise).

Times

  • Prep time: 30 minutes
  • Cook time: 15 minutes
  • Total time: 45 minutes

Ingredients

Pizza

  • 1 dough ball whole wheat pizza dough
  • 1 can no-salt-added crushed tomatoes
  • 1 package reduced-fat mozzarella
  • 1 lb shrimp
  • 1 pint cherry tomatoes
  • 1 each yellow onion
  • 1 bunch fresh basil
  • 1 tsp garlic powder
  • 1 tsp crushed red pepper flakes

Side

  • 1 head broccoli
  • 2 tbsp extra-virgin olive oil
  • 1 tsp ground black pepper

Step-by-step Instructions

  1. Thaw and warm dough: Thaw 1 dough ball overnight in the refrigerator. Bring to room temperature 60–90 minutes.
  2. Preheat: Place a pizza stone/steel or inverted sheet pan in the oven and preheat to 500°F for 30 minutes.
  3. Make sauce: Measure 1 1/4 cups crushed tomatoes from the can into a bowl. Stir in 1/2 tsp garlic powder and 1/4 tsp red pepper flakes. Refrigerate remaining tomatoes.
  4. Cook shrimp: Pat shrimp dry and season with 1/2 tsp garlic powder and black pepper. Sauté in a nonstick skillet 2–3 minutes total until just opaque. Cool 5 minutes.
  5. Prep toppings: Slice 1/4 onion. Halve 1 cup cherry tomatoes. Chop a handful of basil leaves.
  6. Stretch and top: Stretch dough into a thin 10–12 inch round on parchment. Spread sauce. Sprinkle 5 1/2 oz mozzarella (save remaining cheese). Add shrimp and tomatoes. Finish with basil after baking.
  7. Bake: Slide pizza (on parchment) onto the hot surface. Bake 8–12 minutes until browned and bubbling. Scatter basil on top.
  8. Roast broccoli: Cut broccoli into florets. Toss with olive oil and black pepper. Roast at 425°F for 12–15 minutes until browned-tender.
  9. Portion dinner: Cut pizza into 10 slices and serve with broccoli.

Portion Sizes

Portions (per person):
Pizza (1 dough ball): cut into 10 slices.
Frank: 4 slices pizza + 2 cups roasted broccoli

Susan: 3 slices pizza + 1 1/2 cups roasted broccoli

Elise: 3 slices pizza + 1 1/2 cups roasted broccoli
