Turkey Fajita Sheet Pan + Green Beans + Sweet Potatoes

Nutrition (approx)

Frank: 635 kcal • P 58g • C 38g • F 22g
Susan/Elise (each): 476 kcal • P 44g • C 28g • F 16g

Ingredients (purchase-clean)

  • 1.50 lb 93% lean ground turkey
  • 2 each bell peppers
  • 1 each yellow onion
  • 4/5 package whole-grain small tortillas
  • 1.50 lb sweet potatoes
  • 12 oz fresh green beans
  • 6 tsp extra-virgin olive oil
  • 1 each lime
  • 1 tub 2% plain Greek yogurt
  • 2 tsp chili powder
  • 2 tsp cumin
  • 2 tsp garlic powder
  • 1 tsp smoked paprika
  • 1 tsp black pepper
  • 1 tsp kosher salt

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 425°F. Line two sheet pans with parchment.
  2. Sweet potatoes: cube into 3/4-inch pieces. Toss with 2 tsp olive oil and 1/2 tsp kosher salt. Roast 22–26 minutes, turning once.
  3. Green beans: toss with 2 tsp olive oil and a pinch of kosher salt. Roast 10–12 minutes until blistered.
  4. Veg: slice peppers and onion into strips.
  5. Turkey: cook ground turkey in a large nonstick skillet over medium-high 7–9 minutes until browned.
  6. Add peppers/onion to turkey and cook 5–7 minutes until tender-crisp.
  7. Season with fajita spices and stir 60 seconds.
  8. Tortillas: warm tortillas 20–30 seconds per side.
  9. Yogurt-lime crema: stir 6 tbsp Greek yogurt with 2 tsp lime juice and 1 tsp lime zest.
  10. Serve fajita mix with tortillas, crema, sweet potatoes, and green beans. Portion per Notes.

Notes

Turkey fajita plate with two sides (sweet potatoes + green beans) and a yogurt-lime crema.
