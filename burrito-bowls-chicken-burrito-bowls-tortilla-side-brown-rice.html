Chicken Burrito Bowls + Tortilla Side (Brown Rice)

Nutrition (approx)

Frank: 645 kcal • P 58g • C 48g • F 20g
Susan/Elise (each): 484 kcal • P 44g • C 36g • F 15g

Ingredients (purchase-clean)

  • 1.25 lb boneless skinless chicken breast
  • 1 can black beans
  • 1 bag frozen fajita-style pepper blend
  • 1 cup dry brown rice
  • 1 package whole-grain small tortillas
  • 1 each avocado
  • 1/2 jar salsa
  • 1/2 tub 2% plain Greek yogurt
  • 1 each lime
  • 4 tsp extra-virgin olive oil
  • 2 tsp chili powder
  • 2 tsp cumin
  • 2 tsp garlic powder
  • 1 tsp smoked paprika
  • 1 tsp black pepper
  • 1 tsp kosher salt

Steps

  1. Rice: cook brown rice using your preferred method. For this recipe, plan to cook 1 cup dry brown rice (yields about 3 cups cooked). Use about 2 1/2 cups cooked for the bowls; refrigerate any remainder.
  2. Chicken: pat dry and cut into 1/2-inch pieces. Season with the spices.
  3. Cook chicken in a nonstick skillet over medium-high 8–10 minutes until browned and 165°F.
  4. Veg: cook frozen fajita pepper blend with 4 tsp olive oil 6–8 minutes until browned.
  5. Beans: can size note—use a standard 15 oz can black beans. Drain and rinse. Measure out 1 1/2 cups beans; refrigerate any remainder.
  6. Combine: add measured beans to the chicken/peppers and warm 2 minutes. Finish with 2 tsp lime juice.
  7. Tortillas: warm tortillas in a dry skillet 20–30 seconds per side. Portion tortillas: Frank 1 tortilla; Susan 1 tortilla; Elise 1 tortilla.
  8. Yogurt-salsa drizzle: stir 6 tbsp Greek yogurt with 6 tbsp salsa until smooth.
  9. Avocado: halve, pit, and dice. Use about 1/2 avocado total; refrigerate remaining avocado with the pit to slow browning.
  10. Build bowls and serve with the tortilla side. Portion per Notes.

Notes

Burrito bowls with a measured tortilla side to keep the ‘burrito’ feel without blowing the macros. Portions (per person): Frank: 1/3 of the brown rice + 1/3 of the protein mix + 1/3 of the toppings (salsa + avocado + yogurt-salsa drizzle) + 1 tortilla each Susan: 1/4 of the brown rice + 1/4 of the protein mix + 1/4 of the toppings (salsa + avocado + yogurt-salsa drizzle) + 1 tortilla each Elise: 1/4 of the brown rice + 1/4 of the protein mix + 1/4 of the toppings (salsa + avocado + yogurt-salsa drizzle) + 1 tortilla each
