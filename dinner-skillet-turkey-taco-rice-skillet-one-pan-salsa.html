Turkey Taco Rice Skillet (One-Pan) + Salsa

Nutrition (approx)

Frank: 640 kcal • P 58g • C 70g • F 14g
Susan/Elise (each): 480 kcal • P 44g • C 52g • F 10g

Ingredients (purchase-clean)

  • 1.50 lb 93% lean ground turkey
  • 1 cup dry brown rice
  • 1 jar salsa
  • 1 can black beans
  • 1 bag frozen corn
  • low-sodium chicken broth
  • kosher salt
  • black pepper
  • garlic powder
  • onion powder
  • smoked paprika
  • Italian seasoning
  • ground cumin
  • chili powder
  • red pepper flakes
  • extra-virgin olive oil
  • low-sodium chicken broth
  • low-sodium beef broth
  • low-sodium soy sauce
  • hot sauce
  • Dijon mustard
  • balsamic vinegar
  • apple cider vinegar
  • cornstarch

Steps (stovetop)

  1. Cook rice: simmer 1 cup dry brown rice until tender (35–40 minutes).
  2. Brown turkey: cook turkey with chili powder, cumin, garlic powder, onion powder, black pepper, modest kosher salt (8–10 minutes).
  3. Beans/corn: drain/rinse black beans (15 oz) and add 1 1/2 cups beans. Add 2 cups frozen corn; cook 4–5 minutes.
  4. Salsa: stir in 1 cup salsa (measured) and 2 tbsp hot sauce (measured). Simmer 2 minutes.
  5. Serve: spoon turkey mix over rice bowls per Notes.

Notes

Taco-night comfort in a skillet—built thick and reheats well. Portions (per person): Frank: 7 oz turkey mix + (beans/corn in mix) + 1 cup cooked rice Susan: 5 oz protein + 1 cup veg + 3/4 cup Elise: 5 oz protein + 1 cup veg + 3/4 cup Tips (recipe-specific): Add salsa at the end so it tastes bright. Keep it thick (not soupy) for better leftovers.
