Umami Pepper Lean Beef with Quinoa and Broccoli

Servings

3 plates (Frank 1.0×, Susan 0.75×, Elise 0.75×).

Times

  • Prep time: 18 minutes
  • Cook time: 45 minutes
  • Total time: 63 minutes

Ingredients

  • 2.0 lb lean beef steak or roast (top sirloin or top round), trimmed
  • 1.0 cup dry quinoa
  • 1 bag frozen broccoli
  • 9 tsp olive oil
  • 6 tsp minced garlic
  • 2 tsp garlic powder
  • 2 tsp onion powder
  • 2 tsp black pepper
  • 2 tsp salt substitute
  • 2 tsp Worcestershire sauce
  • 2 tsp dried parsley

Steps

  1. Quinoa: Rinse 1 cup dry quinoa. Cook with 2 cups water to yield ~3 cups cooked. Fluff and keep warm.
  2. Broccoli: Toss the full bag with 4 tsp olive oil + 2 tsp minced garlic + 1 tsp black pepper + 1 tsp salt substitute. Sauté in a skillet 7–9 minutes until tender-crisp.
  3. Lean beef: Cut into bite-size pieces. Instant Pot Pro (Sauté): Brown beef in 2 batches with 3 tsp olive oil (do not crowd) for 6–8 minutes total. Proceed with sauce steps, then Pressure Cook 18 minutes with 10-minute natural release.
  4. Finish: Stir in 2 tsp Worcestershire sauce and 2 tsp dried parsley off heat; reduce 2–3 minutes so the sauce clings.
  5. Plate portions per Notes.

Notes

Portions (per person):

Frank – 1 plate (8 oz cooked lean beef, 1 cup cooked quinoa, 1 1/2 cups vegetables).

Susan – 1 plate (6 oz cooked lean beef, 3/4 of Frank’s starch portion, 1 1/4 cups vegetables).

Elise – 1 plate (6 oz cooked lean beef, 3/4 of Frank’s starch portion, 1 1/4 cups vegetables).

Tips (recipe-specific):

Slice beef across the grain for tenderness without needing fatty cuts.

Brown-first rule: don’t crowd the pan; browning is flavor you don’t have to salt.

Storage + Reheat

  • Cool promptly; store in airtight containers in the fridge up to 3–4 days (typical safety guidance).
  • Reheat protein + vegetables in the air fryer at 350°F for 4–6 minutes for edge-crisp; microwave grains/beans 60–90 seconds with a splash of water.
