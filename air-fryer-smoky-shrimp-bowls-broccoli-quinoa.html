Air Fryer Smoky Shrimp Bowls with Broccoli and Quinoa

Mild smoky shrimp cooked fast in the air fryer, served with broccoli and quinoa for a clean, high-protein dinner.

Servings

3 servings (Frank, Susan, Elise).

Times

  • Prep time: 12 minutes
  • Cook time: 14 minutes
  • Total time: 26 minutes

Ingredients (purchase-clean)

  • 1 1/2 lb raw shrimp
  • 6 tsp olive oil
  • 4 tsp lemon juice
  • 4 tsp garlic paste
  • 4 tsp smoked paprika
  • 2 tsp cumin
  • 1 tsp black pepper
  • 2/3 cup dry quinoa
  • 1/2 bag frozen broccoli florets
  • 1/2 can canned chickpeas

Preparation Steps

  1. Cook quinoa: simmer 2/3 cup dry quinoa in 1 1/3 cups water (covered) for 15 minutes; rest 5 minutes and fluff.
  2. Prep shrimp: pat 1 1/2 lb raw shrimp dry; toss with 4 tsp olive oil, 4 tsp lemon juice, 4 tsp garlic paste, 3 tsp smoked paprika, 2 tsp cumin, and 1 tsp black pepper.
  3. Air fry shrimp: preheat air fryer to 400°F for 3 minutes; air fry shrimp at 400°F for 6–8 minutes, shaking once, until opaque.
  4. Cook broccoli: microwave 1/2 bag frozen broccoli florets with 2 tbsp water for 4–6 minutes; toss with 2 tsp olive oil.
  5. Prep chickpeas: drain and rinse 1/2 can canned chickpeas; microwave chickpeas for 60 seconds, then toss with 1 tsp smoked paprika (from the 4 tsp total).
  6. Assemble bowls: portion quinoa, broccoli, chickpeas, and shrimp into bowls per the Portions notes.

Notes

Portions (per person):

Frank — 7 oz shrimp; 3/4 cup cooked quinoa; 1 1/2 cups broccoli; 1/3 cup chickpeas.

 

Susan — 5 1/4 oz shrimp; 2/3 cup cooked quinoa; 1 1/4 cups broccoli; 1/4 cup chickpeas.

 

Elise — 5 1/4 oz shrimp; 2/3 cup cooked quinoa; 1 1/4 cups broccoli; 1/4 cup chickpeas.

 

Tips (recipe-specific):

 

Dry shrimp well before seasoning so it roasts instead of steaming.

 

If shrimp are small, start checking at 6 minutes to avoid rubbery texture.

 

Nutrition (per person)

  • Frank: 620 kcal; 14 g fat; 560 mg sodium; 39 g carbs; 9 g fiber; 59 g protein.
  • Susan: 500 kcal; 11 g fat; 480 mg sodium; 33 g carbs; 8 g fiber; 47 g protein.
  • Elise: 500 kcal; 11 g fat; 480 mg sodium; 33 g carbs; 8 g fiber; 47 g protein.
