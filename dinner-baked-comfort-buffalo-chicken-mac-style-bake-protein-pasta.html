Buffalo Chicken Mac-Style Bake (Protein Pasta)

Nutrition (approx)

Frank: 650 kcal • P 62g • C 58g • F 18g • Sat 7g • Na 700mg • Fiber 9g
Susan/Elise (each): 488 kcal • P 46g • C 44g • F 14g • Sat 5g • Na 525mg • Fiber 7g

Ingredients (purchase-clean)

  • 1.75 lb boneless skinless chicken breast
  • 1 box Barilla Protein+ pasta
  • 1 bottle buffalo sauce
  • 1 tub low-fat cottage cheese
  • 6 oz reduced-fat shredded cheddar cheese
  • 1 bag baby spinach
  • kosher salt
  • black pepper
  • garlic powder
  • onion powder
  • smoked paprika
  • chili powder
  • ground cumin
  • Italian seasoning
  • extra-virgin olive oil

Steps

  1. Oven: heat to 400°F. Lightly oil a baking dish.
  2. Pasta: cook Barilla Protein+ pasta al dente; drain, reserve 1/2 cup pasta water.
  3. Chicken: dice and sauté with 2 tsp olive oil 8–10 minutes to 165°F.
  4. Sauce: mix 1/3 cup buffalo sauce with 1/2 cup cottage cheese and 1/4 cup pasta water until creamy.
  5. Combine: toss chicken + pasta with sauce. Stir in 4 cups spinach until just wilted from heat.
  6. Assemble: spread into dish. Top with 6 oz reduced-fat cheddar.
  7. Bake: 12–15 minutes until bubbly. Rest 8 minutes.
  8. Portion per Notes.

Notes

Buffalo ‘mac’ comfort without a butter-heavy cheese sauce. Portions (per person): Frank: 1/3 of pan Susan: 1/4 of pan Elise: 1/4 of pan Tips (recipe-specific): Cottage + buffalo makes a creamy sauce without heavy fat. Add buffalo sauce at serving time if you want more heat instead of adding more cheese.
