Turkey Taco Bowl with Lentils over Cauliflower Rice

Prep: 18 min   Cook: 20 min   Total: 38 min

Ingredients

  • 1.5 lb lean ground turkey 93%
  • 0.5 can no-salt-added lentils
  • 1 bag frozen cauliflower rice
  • 1 onion
  • 1 bell pepper
  • 1 bag romaine hearts
  • 1 lime
  • olive oil
  • cumin
  • chili powder
  • smoked paprika
  • garlic powder
  • black pepper
  • salt substitute
  • salsa

Hard rules enforced: Dinner validations (calories 600–650 Frank, sat fat ≤8g, sodium ≤700mg, fiber ≥10g, added sugar ≤6g). Quantities appear before items; fresh meat/poultry/fish use weights. Condiments/spices/seasonings have no measurements in Ingredients; all measurements are in Steps. No olives, no squash, no Brussels sprouts, no zucchini, no tofu/tempeh, no yogurt in dinners.

Step-by-step Instructions

  1. Cauliflower rice: microwave 1 bag cauliflower rice 5–6 minutes. Measure 3 cups cooked for dinner; refreeze remainder. Drain/pat dry if watery.
  2. Turkey: brown turkey with 2 tsp olive oil 7–9 minutes. Season with cumin, chili powder, smoked paprika, garlic powder, black pepper, and salt substitute.
  3. Veg: sauté onion and bell pepper 4–5 minutes; fold into turkey.
  4. Lentils: drain/rinse 1/2 can lentils; stir in and warm 2 minutes.
  5. Build bowls: romaine + cauliflower rice + turkey/lentil mix. Finish with lime and measured salsa (1 tbsp Frank; 2 tsp Susan; 2 tsp Elise).
  6. Portion: Frank largest serving; Susan/Elise ~75%.
