Sheet Pan Chicken with Roasted Veg over Cauliflower Rice and Edamame

Prep: 20 min   Cook: 26 min   Total: 46 min

Ingredients

  • 1.75 lb chicken breast
  • 1 bag frozen cauliflower rice
  • 0.5 bag frozen shelled edamame
  • 1 package baby bella mushrooms
  • 1 onion
  • 1 lemon
  • olive oil
  • garlic powder
  • black pepper
  • salt substitute
  • Italian seasoning

Hard rules enforced: Dinner validations (calories, sat fat, sodium, fiber, added sugar). Quantities appear before items; fresh meat/poultry/fish use weights. Condiments/spices/seasonings have no measurements in Ingredients; all measurements are in Steps. No olives, no squash, no Brussels sprouts, no zucchini. Yogurt is not used in dinners.

Step-by-step Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 425°F. Line a sheet pan.
  2. Chicken: cut into chunks. Season with Italian seasoning, garlic powder, black pepper, salt substitute. Toss with 2 tsp olive oil.
  3. Veg: slice mushrooms and onion. Toss with 2 tsp olive oil + black pepper + salt substitute. Spread chicken and veg on pan.
  4. Roast 18–20 minutes until chicken is 165°F and mushrooms browned, stirring once at 10 minutes.
  5. Cauliflower rice: microwave 1 bag cauliflower rice 5–6 minutes. Measure 3 cups cooked for dinner; refreeze remainder. Drain/pat dry if watery.
  6. Edamame: microwave 1/2 bag shelled edamame 4–5 minutes. Measure 1 cup for dinner; refreeze remainder.
  7. Assemble bowls: cauliflower rice base + chicken/veg + edamame. Finish with lemon squeeze.
  8. Portion: Frank largest bowl; Susan/Elise ~75%.
