Chicken-Black Bean Pitas (Salsa + Avocado Inside)

Nutrition (approx)

Frank: 540 kcal • P 52g • C 54g • F 14g
Susan/Elise (each): 405 kcal • P 39g • C 40g • F 10g

Ingredients (purchase-clean)

  • 1.00 lb boneless skinless chicken breast
  • 1 can black beans
  • 1 package whole-wheat pitas
  • 1 each avocado
  • 1/2 jar salsa
  • 1 bag shredded lettuce
  • 1 each cucumber
  • 1 bag mini sweet peppers
  • 4 tsp extra-virgin olive oil
  • 2 tsp chili powder
  • 2 tsp cumin
  • 2 tsp garlic powder
  • 1 tsp smoked paprika
  • 1 tsp black pepper
  • 1 tsp kosher salt

Steps

  1. Chicken: cook diced chicken in a skillet with 2 tsp olive oil 8–10 minutes until 165°F. Season with spices.
  2. Beans: Can size note—use a standard 15 oz can black beans.. Drain/rinse. Use 1 1/2 cups beans; refrigerate remainder. Warm 2 minutes with chicken.
  3. Pitas: warm pitas 15–20 seconds so they open easily. Carefully split.
  4. Stuff: add shredded lettuce, then chicken-beans.
  5. Avocado: halve, pit, and dice. Use about 1/2 avocado total.
  6. Salsa: spoon salsa inside each wrap/pita (not just on the side) so every bite has flavor.
  7. Close and serve.
  8. Veggie sticks: slice cucumber and mini sweet peppers into sticks for a crunchy side.
  9. Portion per Notes.

Notes

Stuffed pitas with chicken + beans; salsa and avocado go inside for zero dryness. Portions (per person): Frank: 2 stuffed pitas (with salsa + avocado inside) + veggie sticks Susan: 1 stuffed pita + veggie sticks Elise: 1 stuffed pita + veggie sticks
