Air Fryer Smoky Chicken Fajita Bowls

Smoky, mild fajita-seasoned chicken with peppers and onions, served over brown rice with black beans.

Servings

3 servings (Frank, Susan, Elise).

Times

  • Prep time: 15 minutes
  • Cook time: 16 minutes
  • Total time: 31 minutes

Ingredients (purchase-clean)

  • 1 1/2 lb boneless skinless chicken breast
  • 1 1/2 bell peppers
  • 1 large onion
  • 6 tsp olive oil
  • 4 tsp lime juice
  • 4 tsp garlic paste
  • 4 tsp chili powder
  • 2 tsp cumin
  • 2 tsp smoked paprika
  • 1 tsp oregano
  • 1 tsp black pepper
  • 1/4 jar low-sodium salsa
  • 1/2 can canned black beans
  • 1 cup dry brown rice

Preparation Steps

  1. Cook rice: simmer 1 cup dry brown rice in 2 cups water (covered) for 35–40 minutes, then rest 5 minutes and fluff.
  2. Prep veggies: slice 1 1/2 bell peppers and 1 large onion into thin strips.
  3. Season chicken: slice 1 1/2 lb chicken breast into strips; toss with 4 tsp olive oil, 4 tsp lime juice, 4 tsp garlic paste, 4 tsp chili powder, 2 tsp cumin, 2 tsp smoked paprika, 1 tsp oregano, and 1 tsp black pepper.
  4. Air fry chicken: preheat air fryer to 400°F for 3 minutes; air fry chicken at 400°F for 10–12 minutes, shaking at 6 minutes, until the thickest piece reaches 165°F.
  5. Air fry veggies: toss peppers and onion with 2 tsp olive oil; air fry at 400°F for 8–10 minutes, shaking once, until tender-crisp.
  6. Prep beans: drain and rinse 1/2 can canned black beans; warm beans in microwave with 2 tbsp water for 60–90 seconds.
  7. Finish and portion: portion rice, chicken, veggies, and beans into bowls; top each bowl with 2 tbsp low-sodium salsa (use 6 tbsp total) and refrigerate the remaining salsa from the opened jar.

Notes

Portions (per person):

Frank — 8 oz chicken; 2/3 cup cooked brown rice; 1/2 cup black beans; 1 1/2 cups peppers/onion; 2 tbsp salsa.

 

Susan — 6 oz chicken; 1/2 cup cooked brown rice; 1/3 cup black beans; 1 1/4 cups peppers/onion; 2 tbsp salsa.

 

Elise — 6 oz chicken; 1/2 cup cooked brown rice; 1/3 cup black beans; 1 1/4 cups peppers/onion; 2 tbsp salsa.

 

Tips (recipe-specific):

 

Keep it mild: if chili powder is strong, use 2 tsp chili powder and add 2 tsp paprika instead.

 

Meal-prep friendly: store rice, chicken, and veggies separately so reheating stays crisp.

  • Susan: 520 kcal; 11 g fat; 490 mg sodium; 34 g carbs; 6 g fiber; 46 g protein.
  • Elise: 520 kcal; 11 g fat; 490 mg sodium; 34 g carbs; 6 g fiber; 46 g protein.
