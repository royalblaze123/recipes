Air Fryer Apple Onion Pork with Sweet Potato and Green Beans

Mild apple-onion pork with sweet potato and green beans, finished with a mustard-yogurt drizzle.

Servings

3 servings.

Times

  • Prep time: 25 minutes
  • Cook time: 18 minutes
  • Total time: 43 minutes

Ingredients

Main Dish

  • 1 lb pork tenderloin
  • 2 each sweet potatoes
  • 1 bag fresh green beans
  • 2 each apples
  • 1 each yellow onion
  • 1 bottle extra virgin olive oil
  • 1 container nonfat Greek yogurt
  • 1 jar Dijon mustard
  • 1 jar garlic powder
  • 1 jar black pepper
  • 1 jar dried thyme
    • 

    Preparation Steps

    1. Prep sweet potatoes: cut into 3/4-inch cubes. Measure out about 2 cups cubes for this recipe; refrigerate remaining sweet potato. Toss with 1 tbsp olive oil, garlic powder, and black pepper.
    2. Prep pork: cut into 1-inch cubes. Toss with 2 tsp olive oil, dried thyme, garlic powder, and black pepper.
    3. Prep apple/onion: slice 1 apple and 1/2 onion (store remaining). Toss with 1 tsp olive oil and black pepper.
    4. Prep green beans: trim. Toss 1/2 bag green beans with 1 tsp olive oil and black pepper.
    5. Cosori setup: preheat both baskets to 400°F for 3 minutes. Put sweet potato cubes in Basket A.
    6. Basket A: cook sweet potato cubes 400°F for 14–16 minutes with shake reminder ON; shake at each reminder.
    7. Basket B: cook pork cubes 400°F for 8–10 minutes, shaking once, until 145°F internal. Remove and rest 3 minutes.
    8. Basket B fruit/veg: cook apple/onion 400°F for 6 minutes, shaking once. Add green beans for the final 6–7 minutes and shake once.
    9. Mustard yogurt: stir 1/4 cup Greek yogurt with 1 tsp Dijon mustard and 1 tsp water.
    10. Serve (40/30/30): plate pork with sweet potato, apple/onion, and green beans. Add a light mustard-yogurt drizzle.

      Notes

      Portions (per person):


      Frank – Pork: largest share. Sweet potato: about 1 1/4 cups. Green beans: about 1 1/2 cups. Apple/onion on the side. Yogurt drizzle.


      Susan – Pork: medium share. Sweet potato: about 1 cup. Green beans: about 1 1/4 cups. Apple/onion on the side. Yogurt drizzle.


      Elise – Pork: medium share. Sweet potato: about 1 cup. Green beans: about 1 1/4 cups. Apple/onion on the side. Yogurt drizzle.


      Tips (recipe-specific):


      Cook apples/onion after pork so they soften and caramelize without turning mushy.


      Keep sweet potato cubes moving with shake reminders to crisp edges.


      If pork pieces are small, start checking at 7 minutes to avoid dryness.
