Taco Turkey Jalapeño Poppers

Air Fryer or Sheet Pan • Yield: 24 popper halves

Ingredients (purchase-clean)

  • 12 each fresh jalapeños
  • 16 oz fresh 93% lean ground turkey
  • 6 oz reduced-fat cream cheese
  • 2 oz reduced-fat shredded cheddar
  • taco seasoning
  • cumin
  • kosher salt
  • black pepper

Steps

  1. Preheat air fryer to 370°F (or oven to 425°F).
  2. Brown turkey: cook 16 oz ground turkey with 2 tsp taco seasoning + 1 tsp cumin + 1 tsp kosher salt + 1/2 tsp black pepper for 7–9 min; drain any liquid; cool 5 min.
  3. Halve 12 jalapeños; remove seeds/ribs; pat dry.
  4. Mix filling: stir softened 6 oz cream cheese into turkey; add 2 oz cheddar; mix until uniform.
  5. Stuff peppers evenly.
  6. Cook: air-fry 370°F for 7–9 min (or bake 425°F for 12–15 min) until hot and lightly browned.

Notes

Flavor-forward holiday poppers designed for snacking or a light meal. Portions (guidance): Light-meal portion: 8–10 halves with a big veggie tray. Optional topping: sprinkle 1 tsp smoked paprika over all poppers before cooking for color.
