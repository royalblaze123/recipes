Ginger Garlic Shrimp + Broccoli + Brown Rice

Glossy ginger-garlic shrimp and broccoli served over brown rice.

Servings

3 servings (Frank, Susan, Elise).

Times

  • Prep time: 20 minutes
  • Cook time: 15 minutes
  • Total time: 35 minutes

Ingredients

Main

  • 2 lb shrimp
  • 1 cup dry brown rice
  • 1 head broccoli
  • 4 cloves garlic
  • 1 each fresh ginger
  • 1 tbsp low-sodium soy sauce
  • 2 tbsp extra-virgin olive oil
  • 1 tbsp rice vinegar
  • 1 tbsp cornstarch
  • 1 tsp ground black pepper

Step-by-step Instructions

  1. Cook rice: Cook 3/5 cup dry brown rice with 1 1/5 cups water until tender (about 35–40 minutes). This yields about 1 3/4 cups cooked rice.
  2. Prep broccoli: Cut broccoli into florets. Measure about 6 cups florets for this dinner; refrigerate any remainder.
  3. Make sauce: Grate ginger and mince garlic. Whisk soy sauce, vinegar, 1/2 cup water, cornstarch, ginger, garlic, and black pepper.
  4. Cook shrimp and broccoli: Heat 2 tsp olive oil in a large nonstick skillet. Sauté broccoli 4–6 minutes until crisp-tender. Add shrimp and cook 2–3 minutes until just opaque.
  5. Glaze: Pour sauce in and stir 1–2 minutes until glossy and thickened. Remove from heat.
  6. Serve: Spoon shrimp-broccoli over rice.

Portion Sizes

Portions (per person):
Frank: 10 oz cooked shrimp + 3/4 cup cooked brown rice + 2 cups broccoli

Susan: 7.5 oz cooked shrimp + 1/2 cup cooked brown rice + 1 1/2 cups broccoli

Elise: 7.5 oz cooked shrimp + 1/2 cup cooked brown rice + 1 1/2 cups broccoli
