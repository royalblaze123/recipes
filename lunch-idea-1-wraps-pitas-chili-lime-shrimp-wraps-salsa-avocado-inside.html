Chili-Lime Shrimp Wraps (Salsa + Avocado Inside)

Nutrition (approx)

Frank: 525 kcal • P 50g • C 50g • F 14g
Susan/Elise (each): 394 kcal • P 38g • C 38g • F 10g

Ingredients (purchase-clean)

  • 1.50 lb raw shrimp
  • 1 package large whole-wheat tortillas
  • 1 each avocado
  • 1/2 jar salsa
  • 1 bag shredded lettuce
  • 1 each cucumber
  • 1 bag mini sweet peppers
  • 4 tsp extra-virgin olive oil
  • 1 each lime
  • 2 tsp chili powder
  • 2 tsp garlic powder
  • 1 tsp black pepper
  • 1 tsp kosher salt

Steps

  1. Shrimp: pat dry and season with chili powder, garlic powder, black pepper, and kosher salt.
  2. Cook shrimp in a hot skillet with 2 tsp olive oil for 2–3 minutes total until opaque. Finish with 2 tsp lime juice.
  3. Wrap build: add shredded lettuce, then shrimp.
  4. Avocado: halve, pit, and dice. Use about 1/2 avocado total.
  5. Salsa: spoon salsa inside each wrap/pita (not just on the side) so every bite has flavor.
  6. Roll and slice.
  7. Veggie sticks: slice cucumber and mini sweet peppers into sticks for a crunchy side.
  8. Portion per Notes.

Notes

Bright shrimp wraps; salsa + avocado inside replaces heavy sauces. Portions (per person): Frank: 1 large wrap (with salsa + avocado inside) + veggie sticks Susan: 3/4 wrap (with salsa + avocado inside) + veggie sticks Elise: 3/4 wrap (with salsa + avocado inside) + veggie sticks
