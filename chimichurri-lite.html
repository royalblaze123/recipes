Chimichurri-Lite

Category: Sauce • Yield: About 3/4 cup (11–12 tbsp) • AnyList-ready

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup fresh parsley
  • 1 tbsp fresh oregano
  • 1 clove garlic
  • 1 tbsp red wine vinegar
  • 2 tsp extra-virgin olive oil
  • 1/4 tsp black pepper

Steps

  1. Finely chop parsley and oregano. Mince 1 clove garlic.
  2. Stir herbs and garlic with vinegar, olive oil, and black pepper.
  3. Add 2–4 tbsp water, 1 tbsp at a time, until spoonable.
