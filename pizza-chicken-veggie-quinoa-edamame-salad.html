Garden Veg Chicken Pizza with Quinoa-Edamame Salad

Prep: 28 min   Cook: 20 min   Total: 48 min

Ingredients

  • 1 pizza dough ball
  • 0.33 jar no-salt-added pizza sauce
  • 4 oz reduced-fat mozzarella cheese
  • 14 oz chicken breast
  • 1 bell pepper
  • 1 onion
  • 1 package baby bella mushrooms
  • 0.5 bag frozen shelled edamame
  • 0.5 cup dry quinoa
  • 1 lemon
  • olive oil
  • black pepper
  • salt substitute
  • garlic powder
  • Italian seasoning
  • vinegar

Hard rules enforced: Dinner calories (Frank 600–650), sat fat ≤8g, sodium ≤700mg, fiber ≥10g, added sugar ≤6g. Quantities appear before items; fresh meat/poultry/fish use weights. Condiments/spices/seasonings are listed name-only (no tsp/tbsp in Ingredients); all measurements are in Steps. No olives, no squash, no Brussels sprouts, no zucchini, no yogurt in dinners.

Step-by-step Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 475°F.
  2. Cook quinoa: rinse 1/2 cup dry quinoa. Simmer with 1 cup water (water referenced in Steps only) for 15 minutes; rest 5 minutes. Fluff. (Yields ~1 1/2 cups cooked.)
  3. Edamame: microwave 1/2 bag frozen shelled edamame 4–5 minutes until hot. Measure 1 cup for salad; freeze remaining.
  4. Chicken: season 14 oz chicken breast with 2 tsp Italian seasoning + 2 tsp garlic powder + 1 tsp black pepper + 2 tsp salt substitute. Cook in a skillet with 2 tsp olive oil 6–7 minutes per side to 165°F. Slice thin.
  5. Veg toppings: slice pepper, onion, and mushrooms. Sauté mushrooms 4–5 minutes to drive off moisture (optional).
  6. Shape dough into 12-inch pizza. Spread 1/3 jar sauce thinly. Add mozzarella. Top with chicken and vegetables. Bake 10–12 minutes. Cut into 10 slices.
  7. Quinoa-edamame salad: mix 1 cup cooked quinoa + 1 cup edamame + lemon juice (2 tsp) + vinegar (1 tsp) + olive oil (2 tsp) + black pepper + salt substitute.
  8. Portion: Frank 4 slices + ~1 1/2 cups quinoa-edamame salad. Susan/Elise 3 slices + ~1 cup salad each.
