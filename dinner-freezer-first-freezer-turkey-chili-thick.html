Freezer Turkey Chili (Thick)

Nutrition (approx)

Frank: 645 kcal • P 58g • C 55g • F 16g • Sat 4g • Na 700mg • Fiber 14g
Susan/Elise (each): 484 kcal • P 44g • C 41g • F 12g • Sat 3g • Na 525mg • Fiber 10g

Ingredients (purchase-clean)

  • 1.75 lb 93% lean ground turkey
  • 1 can kidney beans
  • 1 can black beans
  • 2 cans no-salt-added diced tomatoes
  • 1 each onion
  • 1 bag frozen fajita-style pepper blend
  • kosher salt
  • black pepper
  • garlic powder
  • onion powder
  • smoked paprika
  • Italian seasoning
  • ground cumin
  • chili powder
  • red pepper flakes
  • extra-virgin olive oil
  • low-sodium chicken broth
  • cornstarch

Steps

  1. Turkey: brown ground turkey with diced onion in a pot with olive oil, then season with chili powder, cumin, garlic powder, smoked paprika, black pepper, and a modest pinch of kosher salt (8–10 minutes).
  2. Veg: add frozen fajita pepper blend; cook 6–8 minutes until water evaporates and edges start to brown.
  3. Tomatoes: add 2 cans diced tomatoes (14.5 oz each).
  4. Beans: drain/rinse kidney beans and black beans (15 oz each); add 1 1/2 cups of each to the pot.
  5. Simmer: simmer uncovered 25–35 minutes until thick (stir every 5 minutes).
  6. Cool + freeze: cool 30 minutes, portion into freezer containers, label, and freeze.
  7. Reheat: thaw overnight in fridge; reheat to a steady simmer on stovetop or microwave until piping hot.

Notes

Big-batch chili designed to freeze cleanly and reheat thick (not watery). Portions (per person): Frank: About 2 1/2 cups (heaping bowl) Susan: About 2 cups (bowl) Elise: About 2 cups (bowl) Tips (recipe-specific): Simmer uncovered so it freezes thick and reheats like chili, not soup. Freeze in flat 2-cup portions so it thaws fast on work nights.
