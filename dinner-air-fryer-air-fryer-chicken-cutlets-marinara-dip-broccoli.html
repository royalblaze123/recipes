Air Fryer Chicken Cutlets + Marinara Dip + Broccoli

Nutrition (approx)

Frank: 640 kcal • P 60g • C 35g • F 22g • Sat 6g • Na 700mg • Fiber 10g
Susan/Elise (each): 480 kcal • P 45g • C 26g • F 16g • Sat 4g • Na 525mg • Fiber 8g

Ingredients (purchase-clean)

  • 1.75 lb chicken breast cutlets
  • panko breadcrumbs
  • egg whites
  • 1 jar marinara sauce
  • 1 bag frozen broccoli florets
  • 6 oz grated Parmesan cheese
  • kosher salt
  • black pepper
  • garlic powder
  • onion powder
  • smoked paprika
  • Italian seasoning
  • ground cumin
  • chili powder
  • red pepper flakes

  • low-sodium soy sauce
  • hot sauce
  • Dijon mustard
  • balsamic vinegar
  • apple cider vinegar
  • cornstarch

Steps (Cosori dual-basket)

  1. Preheat Cosori to 400°F. Basket A: chicken cutlets. Basket B: broccoli (later).
  2. Breading: whisk 1/2 cup egg whites. Mix 1 cup panko with 2 tsp Italian seasoning, 2 tsp garlic powder, 1 tsp smoked paprika, 1/2 tsp kosher salt, and 1/2 tsp black pepper.
  3. Cutlets: dip cutlets in egg whites then panko. Lightly oil basket. Air fry 10–12 minutes at 400°F; flip at reminder; cook to 165°F.
  4. Marinara dip: warm 1 1/2 cups marinara (measured) and simmer 6 minutes to thicken.
  5. Broccoli: microwave, drain/pat dry; toss with 2 tsp olive oil, 1 tsp garlic powder, 1/2 tsp kosher salt, black pepper; crisp 6 minutes at 400°F.
  6. Finish: sprinkle 3 oz Parmesan across plates (measured). Dip cutlets into marinara per bite.
  7. Serve per Notes.

Notes

Chicken parm vibes without heavy cheese layering—crisp cutlets + measured marinara dip. Portions (per person): Frank: 7 oz cutlets + — + 1 cup broccoli Susan: 5 oz protein + — + 3/4 cup veg Elise: 5 oz protein + — + 3/4 cup veg Tips (recipe-specific): Serve marinara as a dip so cutlets stay crisp. Measure Parmesan as a finish, not a blanket.
