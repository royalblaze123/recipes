Sheet Pan Garlic-Lime Pork Tenderloin with Broccoli and Carrots over Brown Rice

Prep: 18 min   Cook: 26 min   Total: 44 min

Ingredients

  • 1.5 lb pork tenderloin
  • 1 bag frozen broccoli florets
  • 1 lb carrots
  • 0.5 cup dry brown rice
  • olive oil
  • lime
  • low-sodium soy sauce
  • rice vinegar
  • garlic
  • ginger
  • black pepper
  • salt substitute
  • cornstarch

Hard rules enforced: Dinner validations (calories 600–650 Frank, sat fat ≤8g, sodium ≤700mg, fiber ≥10g, added sugar ≤6g). Quantities appear before items; fresh meat/poultry/fish use weights. Condiments/spices/seasonings have no measurements in Ingredients; all measurements are in Steps. No olives, no squash, no Brussels sprouts, no zucchini, no tofu/tempeh, no yogurt in dinners.

Step-by-step Instructions

  1. Cook brown rice: cook 1/2 cup dry brown rice per package directions (water in Steps only).
  2. Preheat oven to 425°F. Line a sheet pan.
  3. Broccoli: microwave frozen broccoli 3–4 minutes to thaw; pat dry. Measure 4 cups; refreeze remainder.
  4. Carrots: peel and slice on a diagonal into 1/4-inch pieces.
  5. Sauce: whisk 2 tbsp low-sodium soy sauce + 1 tbsp rice vinegar + 2 tsp lime juice + 1 tsp cornstarch + 2 tbsp water + minced garlic + grated ginger + black pepper.
  6. Pork: slice tenderloin into 1-inch medallions. Toss pork with 2 tsp olive oil + half the sauce. Arrange on pan. Toss broccoli and carrots with 1 tsp olive oil + black pepper + salt substitute; spread around pork.
  7. Roast 14–18 minutes until pork reaches 145°F and rests 3 minutes (carryover).
  8. Warm remaining sauce in a small skillet 1 minute until thick; drizzle over pork and veg.
  9. Serve over brown rice. Portion: Frank largest pork/rice share; Susan/Elise ~75%.
