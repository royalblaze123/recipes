Greek-Herb Meatloaf Plate

Nutrition (approx)

Frank: 620 kcal • P 54g • C 34g • F 24g
Susan/Elise (each): 465 kcal • P 40g • C 26g • F 18g

Ingredients (purchase-clean)

  • 1.50 lb 93% lean ground beef
  • 1 each yellow onion
  • 2 each large eggs
  • 3/4 cup whole wheat panko breadcrumbs
  • 2 tsp oregano
  • 2 tsp garlic powder
  • 1 each lemon
  • 1 tsp black pepper
  • 1 tsp kosher salt
  • 1.50 lb baby potatoes
  • 1 bag spring mix
  • 1 each cucumber
  • 8 tsp light salad dressing
  • 4 tsp extra-virgin olive oil

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 400°F. Line a sheet pan with parchment.
  2. Dice the yellow onion small.
  3. Meatloaf mix: combine ground beef, onion, eggs, panko, oregano, garlic powder, black pepper, and kosher salt. Mix just until combined.
  4. Form loaf on the sheet pan and bake 35 minutes until 160°F in the center. Rest 10 minutes.
  5. Potatoes: halve baby potatoes if large. Toss with 2 tsp olive oil, 1 tsp lemon zest, and 2 tsp lemon juice; roast 25–30 minutes, flipping once, until browned and tender.
  6. Salad: slice cucumber and toss with spring mix and light dressing right before serving.

Notes

Herb-forward meatloaf with lemony roasted potatoes and a crisp salad. Portions (per person): Frank: Meatloaf: 2 thick slices (about 10 oz cooked total) • lemon potatoes: about 6 oz cooked • Vegetables: at least 1 1/2 cups Susan: Meatloaf: 1–2 slices (about 7–8 oz cooked total) • lemon potatoes: about 4–5 oz cooked • Vegetables: at least 1 cup Elise: Meatloaf: 1–2 slices (about 7–8 oz cooked total) • lemon potatoes: about 4–5 oz cooked • Vegetables: at least 1 cup
