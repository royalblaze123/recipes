Turkey & Veggie Yogurt-Mustard Sandwich + Carrot Sticks (RBM Lunch)

Lower-sodium deli turkey on whole-grain bread with a yogurt-mustard spread, crisp veggies, and carrot sticks on the side.

Servings

3 servings (Frank, Susan, Elise).

Ingredients

Sandwich

  • 6 slices whole-grain sandwich bread (about 1 oz per slice), lightly toasted if desired
  • 7.5 oz lower-sodium deli turkey breast, sliced (clearly labeled deli meat)
  • 2 slices reduced-fat cheddar or provolone cheese (about 0.7 oz each)
  • 3 tbsp nonfat plain Greek yogurt
  • 1 1/2 tsp Dijon mustard (no added sugar)
  • 3 leaves fresh romaine or leaf lettuce
  • 1 small fresh tomato, sliced
  • 1/4 medium fresh cucumber, sliced

Side

  • 7.5 oz fresh baby carrots

Step-by-Step Instructions

  1. In a small bowl, stir together the plain Greek yogurt and Dijon mustard until smooth to make the yogurt-mustard spread.
  2. Lay out the whole-grain bread slices on a cutting board. If you prefer, lightly toast them first to help them hold up for packing.
  3. Spread the yogurt-mustard mixture evenly over one side of each bread slice.
  4. On three of the slices, layer the lower-sodium deli turkey breast, a portion of the reduced-fat cheese (cut or torn to divide it for Frank, Susan, and Elise as per portions), a lettuce leaf, tomato slices, and cucumber slices.
  5. Top each sandwich with the remaining bread slices, spread side down. Press gently and cut in half if desired for easier packing.
  6. Divide the baby carrots into three portions in small containers or snack bags so that Frank, Susan, and Elise each receive their portion size listed below.
  7. Serve immediately, or wrap each sandwich tightly (and pack the carrot sticks separately) for an easy grab-and-go lunch.

Portion Sizes (Per Person)

Frank: 2 slices whole-grain bread, 3 oz deli turkey breast, 1 slice reduced-fat cheese, 2 tbsp yogurt-mustard spread, 1 leaf lettuce, 2 tomato slices, 4 cucumber slices, 3 oz baby carrots.

 

Susan: 2 slices whole-grain bread, 2.25 oz deli turkey breast, 3/4 slice reduced-fat cheese, 1 1/2 tbsp yogurt-mustard spread, 1 leaf lettuce, 2 tomato slices, 3 cucumber slices, 2.5 oz baby carrots.

 

Elise: 2 slices whole-grain bread, 1.5 oz deli turkey breast, 1/2 slice reduced-fat cheese, 1 tbsp yogurt-mustard spread, 1 leaf lettuce, 1–2 tomato slices, 2 cucumber slices, 2 oz baby carrots.

Nutrition (Per Person)

  • Frank: 410 kcal, 38 g protein, 38 g carbs, 11 g fat, 7 g fiber, 630 mg sodium
  • Susan: 380 kcal, 34 g protein, 33 g carbs, 10 g fat, 6 g fiber, 580 mg sodium
  • Elise: 350 kcal, 30 g protein, 30 g carbs, 9 g fat, 5 g fiber, 530 mg sodium
