Parmesan Garlic Tenders + Roasted Broccoli (Sheet Pan)

Nutrition (approx)

Frank: 630 kcal • P 62g • C 45g • F 18g • Sat 6g • Na 690mg • Fiber 10g
Susan/Elise (each): 472 kcal • P 46g • C 34g • F 14g • Sat 4g • Na 518mg • Fiber 8g

Ingredients (purchase-clean)

  • 1.75 lb chicken tenderloins
  • 1 carton egg whites
  • 1 package whole-wheat panko breadcrumbs
  • 2 oz grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 bag frozen broccoli florets
  • 1 each lemon
  • kosher salt
  • black pepper
  • garlic powder
  • onion powder
  • smoked paprika
  • chili powder
  • ground cumin
  • Italian seasoning
  • crushed red pepper
  • cornstarch
  • low-sodium soy sauce
  • rice vinegar
  • hot sauce
  • Dijon mustard
  • extra-virgin olive oil
  • parsley

Steps

  1. Oven: heat to 425°F. Line a sheet pan and lightly oil.
  2. Broccoli: spread frozen broccoli florets on the pan. Toss with 2 tsp olive oil, 1/2 tsp kosher salt, 1/2 tsp black pepper. Roast 16–20 minutes, stirring once.
  3. Breading bowls: Bowl 1: 1/2 cup egg whites + 1 tsp Dijon. Bowl 2: 1 cup panko + 2 oz Parmesan + 2 tsp garlic powder + 1 tsp Italian seasoning + 1/2 tsp black pepper.
  4. Coat chicken tenderloins with egg whites then panko mix; place on a second sheet pan.
  5. Bake tenders at 425°F for 12–14 minutes. Flip at 7 minutes. Cook to 165°F.
  6. Finish broccoli with 2 tsp lemon juice and a pinch of parsley.
  7. Serve: portion tenders + broccoli per Notes.

Notes

Garlic-parm comfort tenders without frying. Portions (per person): Frank: 6 tenders + 1 cup broccoli side + dip Susan: 4 tenders + 3/4 cup broccoli side + dip Elise: 4 tenders + 3/4 cup broccoli side + dip Tips (recipe-specific): Lemon at the end makes the parm flavor pop without extra salt. Flip halfway so both sides crisp.
