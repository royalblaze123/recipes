BBQ Chicken Protein Mac (Pulled-Style)

Nutrition (approx)

Frank: 645 kcal • P 60g • C 60g • F 16g • Sat 6g • Na 700mg • Fiber 10g
Susan/Elise (each): 484 kcal • P 45g • C 45g • F 12g • Sat 4g • Na 525mg • Fiber 8g

Ingredients (purchase-clean)

  • 1 box Barilla Protein+ pasta
  • 1.75 lb boneless skinless chicken breast
  • 1 bottle low-sugar BBQ sauce
  • 1 bag frozen green beans
  • 1 carton skim milk
  • 1 container reduced-fat shredded cheddar cheese
  • kosher salt
  • black pepper
  • garlic powder
  • onion powder
  • smoked paprika
  • Italian seasoning
  • chili powder
  • ground cumin
  • crushed red pepper
  • cornstarch
  • extra-virgin olive oil
  • Dijon mustard
  • hot sauce
  • Worcestershire sauce
  • low-sodium soy sauce
  • rice vinegar

Steps

  1. Pasta: cook Protein+ pasta al dente. Drain.
  2. Chicken: poach chicken in a pot with 2 cups water and a pinch of kosher salt 12–14 minutes to 165°F. Shred with forks.
  3. Green beans: roast 1 bag frozen green beans at 425°F with 2 tsp olive oil and black pepper for 14–18 minutes.
  4. Sauce base: whisk 2 tbsp cornstarch into 2 cups skim milk. Simmer 3–5 minutes until thick.
  5. Cheese: stir in 6 oz reduced-fat cheddar until melted.
  6. BBQ: stir in 3 tbsp low-sugar BBQ sauce (measured). Fold in shredded chicken.
  7. Combine: fold in pasta. Serve with green beans per Notes.

Notes

BBQ comfort mac, but sauce is measured and the plate includes veg. Portions (per person): Frank: About 2 1/2 cups mac + 1 cup green beans Susan: About 2 cups mac + 3/4 cup green beans Elise: About 2 cups mac + 3/4 cup green beans Tips (recipe-specific): Poached-then-shredded chicken gives that ‘pulled’ feel without extra fat. Serve BBQ mac with roasted green beans to keep the plate balanced.
