Taco Turkey Pizza with Black Bean Cabbage Slaw

Prep: 24 min   Cook: 18 min   Total: 42 min

Ingredients

  • 1 pizza dough ball
  • 0.33 jar no-salt-added pizza sauce
  • 5 oz reduced-fat mozzarella cheese
  • 14 oz lean ground turkey 93%
  • 1 onion
  • 1 bell pepper
  • 0.5 can no-salt-added black beans
  • 0.5 bag shredded cabbage
  • 1 lime
  • olive oil
  • cumin
  • chili powder
  • smoked paprika
  • black pepper
  • salt substitute
  • vinegar

Hard rules enforced: Dinner calories (Frank 600–650), sat fat ≤8g, sodium ≤700mg, fiber ≥10g, added sugar ≤6g. Quantities appear before items; fresh meat/poultry/fish use weights. Condiments/spices/seasonings are listed name-only (no tsp/tbsp in Ingredients); all measurements are in Steps. No olives, no squash, no Brussels sprouts, no zucchini, no yogurt in dinners.

Step-by-step Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 475°F.
  2. Cook turkey: brown 14 oz lean ground turkey in a skillet 7–9 minutes. Season with 2 tsp cumin + 2 tsp chili powder + 1 tsp smoked paprika + 1 tsp black pepper + 2 tsp salt substitute. Set aside.
  3. Shape dough into 12-inch pizza. Spread 1/3 jar sauce thinly. Add mozzarella.
  4. Top pizza with turkey, thin-sliced onion, and thin-sliced bell pepper. Bake 10–12 minutes. Cut into 10 slices.
  5. Slaw: drain and rinse 1/2 can black beans. Toss cabbage + beans with 2 tsp olive oil + 2 tsp lime juice + 1 tsp vinegar + black pepper + salt substitute.
  6. Portion: Frank 4 slices + ~1 1/2 cups slaw. Susan/Elise 3 slices + ~1 1/4 cups slaw each.
