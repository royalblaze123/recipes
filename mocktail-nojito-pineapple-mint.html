Nojito Pineapple Mint

Yield: 1 drink • Prep: 5 min • Total: 5 min

Ingredients (AnyList-ready)

  • 10 mint leaves
  • 1 lime
  • 3/4 oz simple syrup
  • 3 oz pineapple juice
  • 4 oz club soda
  • 1 cup ice

Steps

  1. Add 10 mint leaves and 3/4 oz simple syrup to a tall glass.
  2. Squeeze in about 1 oz lime juice (about 1 lime) and gently muddle 8–10 presses.
  3. Fill with 1 cup ice.
  4. Add 3 oz pineapple juice, then top with 4 oz club soda.
  5. Stir once, gently.

Tip: Use plenty of ice and a quick, gentle stir to keep carbonation lively.
