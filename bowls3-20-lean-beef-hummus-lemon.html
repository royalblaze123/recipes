Composed Lean Beef Bowl with Brown Rice, Cabbage, and Hummus Lemon

Servings

3 bowls (Frank 1.0×, Susan 0.75×, Elise 0.75×).

Times

  • Prep time: 22 minutes
  • Cook time: 28 minutes
  • Total time: 50 minutes

Ingredients

  • 2.0 lb lean beef steak (top sirloin or top round), trimmed
  • 1.0 cup dry brown rice
  • 1 head green cabbage
  • 1 pack microgreens
  • 11 tsp olive oil
  • 6 tsp minced garlic
  • 2 tsp black pepper
  • 2 tsp salt substitute
  • 2 tsp dried parsley
  • 12 tsp hummus
  • 4 tsp lemon juice
  • 2 tsp black pepper

Steps

  1. Brown rice: Cook 1 cup dry brown rice to yield ~3 cups cooked. Keep warm.
  2. Vegetables: Roast per steps below to get browned edges (texture contrast matters).
  3. Cabbage: Slice into wedges or thick ribbons. Toss with 4 tsp olive oil + 1 tsp pepper + 1 tsp salt substitute. Roast at 425°F for 16–20 minutes, flipping once, until browned edges.
  4. Lean beef: Slice across the grain into strips. Sear in a hot skillet with 3 tsp olive oil in 2 batches, 2–3 minutes per batch to brown.
  5. Sauce: In a small bowl, whisk the Sauce ingredients (hummus lemon). Keep it measured—this is a finish, not a flood.
  6. Build bowls (assembly order): Add warm starch first, then vegetables, then sliced protein. Spoon sauce on top. Finish with microgreens after heat (they’re the ‘gourmet’ signal).
  7. Plate portions per Notes.

Notes

Portions (per person):

Frank – 1 bowl (8 oz cooked protein, 1 cup cooked brown rice, 1 1/2 cups cabbage, 1/2 cup microgreens, 2–3 tbsp sauce).

Susan – 1 bowl (6 oz cooked protein, 3/4 of Frank’s starch portion, 1 1/4 cups cabbage, 1/3 cup microgreens, 2 tbsp sauce).

Elise – 1 bowl (6 oz cooked protein, 3/4 of Frank’s starch portion, 1 1/4 cups cabbage, 1/3 cup microgreens, 2 tbsp sauce).

Tips (recipe-specific):

Build order matters: warm base → dry veg → protein → sauce → microgreens last so nothing turns soggy.

Keep sauce measured; the bowl should taste ‘finished,’ not ‘dressed.’

Storage + Reheat

  • Cool promptly; store components in airtight containers in the fridge up to 3–4 days (typical safety guidance).
  • Reheat grain/beans + protein 60–90 seconds in the microwave; reheat roasted veg 4–6 minutes at 350°F in the air fryer for texture; add sauce and microgreens after reheating.
