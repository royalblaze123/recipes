Mediterranean Chicken & Feta Pizza

Whole-wheat pizza with chicken, spinach, tomatoes, and a light sprinkle of feta and mozzarella for a Mediterranean-style dinner without olives.

Servings

3 servings.

Times

  • Prep time: 12 minutes
  • Cook time: 16 minutes
  • Total time: 28 minutes

Ingredients

  • 1 large rectangular whole-wheat thin pizza crust
  • 13 oz cooked chicken breast
  • 3 cups lightly packed fresh baby spinach
  • 1 1/4 cups halved grape tomatoes
  • 1 cup thinly sliced bell peppers
  • 1/4 cup thinly sliced red onion
  • 1/2 cup no-salt-added tomato sauce or crushed tomatoes
  • 1/2 cup 2% plain Greek yogurt
  • 1 tbsp extra-virgin olive oil
  • 1 tsp garlic powder
  • 1 tsp dried oregano
  • 1/4 tsp ground black pepper
  • 1/4 tsp kosher or sea salt
  • 3.5 oz part-skim mozzarella cheese
  • 1.5 oz reduced-fat feta cheese
  • Olive oil spray

Preparation Steps

  1. Heat the oven to 425°F (about 220°C). Line a large sheet pan with parchment paper and lightly spray with olive oil.
  2. If the chicken is not yet cooked, season about 1 lb raw boneless skinless chicken breast lightly with salt, pepper, and garlic powder and bake or sauté until cooked through (165°F internal), then chop to make 13 oz cooked chicken breast. Set aside.
  3. Rinse and dry the spinach, grape tomatoes, bell peppers, and red onion (if using). Roughly chop the spinach, halve the tomatoes, slice the bell peppers, and thinly slice the onion.
  4. In a medium bowl, toss the spinach, tomatoes, peppers, and onion with 1 tsp olive oil and a light sprinkle of salt and pepper. Spread the vegetables on a separate parchment-lined pan and pre-roast for 5–6 minutes until just starting to soften.
  5. Make the light sauce: In a small bowl, whisk together the tomato sauce, Greek yogurt, remaining 2 tsp olive oil, garlic powder, dried oregano, black pepper, and a pinch of salt until smooth.
  6. Place the whole-wheat pizza crust on the prepared sheet pan. Spread the yogurt-tomato sauce evenly over the crust, leaving a small border at the edges.
  7. Sprinkle half of the shredded mozzarella over the sauce. Scatter the chopped cooked chicken evenly over the crust. Add the pre-roasted spinach and vegetables on top of the chicken.
  8. Sprinkle the remaining mozzarella and the crumbled feta evenly over the pizza.
  9. Bake at 425°F for 10–12 minutes, or until the crust is crisp, the cheese is melted, and the edges are lightly browned.
  10. Remove from the oven and let rest for 2–3 minutes. Cut into 10 equal squares.
  11. Place 4 of the 10 squares on a plate for Frank, making sure his slices include a good amount of chicken, spinach, tomatoes, and feta.
  12. Place 3 squares each on plates for Susan and Elise, redistributing any loose toppings evenly.
