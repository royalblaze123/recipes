Protein Waffle Sandwich Plates + Fruit

Nutrition (approx)

Frank: 640 kcal • P 55g • C 65g • F 16g • Sat 5g • Na 700mg • Fiber 10g
Susan/Elise (each): 480 kcal • P 41g • C 49g • F 12g • Sat 4g • Na 525mg • Fiber 8g

Ingredients (purchase-clean)

  • 1 box protein waffle mix
  • 1 carton egg whites
  • 6 slices reduced-fat cheese slices
  • 12 slices deli turkey
  • 1 bunch bananas
  • kosher salt
  • black pepper
  • garlic powder
  • onion powder
  • smoked paprika
  • Italian seasoning
  • hot sauce
  • Dijon mustard
  • extra-virgin olive oil
  • salsa
  • cinnamon

Steps

  1. Waffles: make protein waffles per box, using egg whites for the liquid portion. Add cinnamon.
  2. Build sandwiches: each person gets 2 waffles as a sandwich with turkey + cheese.
  3. Toast sandwich in a dry skillet 1–2 minutes per side to melt cheese.
  4. Serve with banana per Notes.

Notes

Sweet-and-savory waffle sandwich comfort with controlled portions. Portions (per person): Frank: 1 waffle sandwich + 1 banana Susan: 1 waffle sandwich + 1/2 banana Elise: 1 waffle sandwich + 1/2 banana Tips (recipe-specific): Dry-skillet melt gives diner feel without butter. Cinnamon in the waffle batter adds flavor without sugar.
