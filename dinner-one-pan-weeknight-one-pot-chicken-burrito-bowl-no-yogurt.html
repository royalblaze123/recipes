One-Pot Chicken Burrito Bowl (No Yogurt)

Nutrition (approx)

Frank: 650 kcal • P 60g • C 65g • F 16g • Sat 4g • Na 700mg • Fiber 12g
Susan/Elise (each): 488 kcal • P 45g • C 49g • F 12g • Sat 3g • Na 525mg • Fiber 9g

Ingredients (purchase-clean)

  • 1.75 lb boneless skinless chicken breast
  • 1 cup dry brown rice
  • 1 can black beans
  • 1 jar salsa
  • 1 bag frozen mixed vegetables
  • low-sodium chicken broth
  • 1 each avocado
  • kosher salt
  • black pepper
  • garlic powder
  • onion powder
  • smoked paprika
  • Italian seasoning
  • ground cumin
  • chili powder
  • red pepper flakes
  • extra-virgin olive oil
  • low-sodium chicken broth

Steps

  1. Chicken: dice chicken. Sauté with 2 tsp olive oil, 1 tsp chili powder, 1 tsp cumin, 1 tsp garlic powder, 1/2 tsp onion powder, 1/2 tsp kosher salt, 1/2 tsp black pepper 6–8 minutes.
  2. Rice: stir in 1 cup dry brown rice.
  3. Liquid: add 2 1/4 cups low-sodium broth plus 1/2 cup salsa. Bring to a boil.
  4. Simmer: cover and simmer 35–40 minutes until rice is tender. Rest 5 minutes.
  5. Veg + beans: stir in 2 cups frozen mixed vegetables and 1 1/2 cups drained/rinsed black beans. Cover 5 minutes to heat through.
  6. Top with avocado slices. Serve per Notes.

Notes

Burrito-bowl comfort without creamy sauces—salsa + avocado do the work. Portions (per person): Frank: About 2 1/2 cups bowl + 1/3 avocado Susan: About 2 cups bowl + 1/4 avocado Elise: About 2 cups bowl + 1/4 avocado Tips (recipe-specific): Rest after simmering so the bowl thickens naturally. Add avocado at the end for richness without extra saturated fat.
