Lemon-Garlic Greek Yogurt Sauce

Category: Sauce • AnyList-ready HTML

Ingredients

  • 3/4 cup 2% plain Greek yogurt
  • 2 tsp lemon juice
  • 1 clove garlic
  • 2 tsp extra-virgin olive oil
  • 2 tsp Dijon mustard
  • 2 tsp fresh dill
  • 1/4 tsp black pepper

Steps

  1. Mince or microplane 1 clove garlic.
  2. Whisk 3/4 cup Greek yogurt, 2 tsp lemon juice, 2 tsp Dijon mustard, 2 tsp olive oil, garlic, and 1/4 tsp black pepper until smooth.
  3. Stir in 2 tsp fresh dill. Chill 10 minutes before serving.
Recipe Info (AnyList description): Creamy lemon-garlic yogurt sauce for chicken, fish, bowls, and wraps. Portions (per person): Frank – Start with 2 tbsp; add up to 3 tbsp if calories/sodium allow. Susan – Start with 1 1/2 tbsp; add up to 2 tbsp as needed. Elise – Start with 1 1/2 tbsp; add up to 2 tbsp as needed. Tips (recipe-specific): For GERD-sensitive dinners, keep lemon at 1 tsp and add 1 tsp water to thin. Holds 4 days refrigerated in a sealed container.
