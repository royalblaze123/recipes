Air Fryer Garlic Herb Pork Medallions with Potatoes and Asparagus

Juicy pork medallions with crispy potatoes and asparagus, timed with Cosori Sync Finish for an easy weeknight dinner.

Servings

3 servings.

Times

  • Prep time: 20 minutes
  • Cook time: 18 minutes
  • Total time: 38 minutes

Ingredients

Main Dish

  • 1 lb pork tenderloin
  • 1 bag baby potatoes
  • 1 bunch asparagus
  • 1 each lemon
  • 6 cloves garlic
  • 1 bottle extra virgin olive oil
  • 1 container nonfat Greek yogurt
  • 1 jar Italian seasoning
  • 1 jar garlic powder
  • 1 jar black pepper
    • 

    Preparation Steps

    1. Prep potatoes: halve baby potatoes. Measure out about 3 cups potatoes for this recipe; refrigerate remaining potatoes. Toss with 1 tbsp olive oil, 1 tsp garlic powder, 1 tsp Italian seasoning, and black pepper.
    2. Prep pork: slice pork tenderloin into 12–15 medallions. Toss with 1 tbsp olive oil, 1 tsp Italian seasoning, 1/2 tsp garlic powder, and black pepper.
    3. Cosori setup: preheat both baskets to 390°F for 3 minutes. Add potatoes to Basket A in a single layer. Add pork medallions to Basket B in a single layer (cook in two batches if crowded).
    4. Sync Finish: set Basket A to 390°F for 18 minutes (shake reminder ON). Set Basket B to 390°F for 12 minutes. Press Sync Finish.
    5. Shake reminder (Basket A): shake/flip potatoes when prompted.
    6. Flip pork: at the 6-minute mark, flip medallions. Cook until pork reaches 145°F, then rest 3 minutes.
    7. Asparagus: trim asparagus. Toss with 1 tsp olive oil and black pepper. When pork is done, cook asparagus in Basket B at 390°F for 6–7 minutes, shaking once.
    8. Yogurt lemon drizzle: stir 1/4 cup Greek yogurt with 1 tsp lemon juice and a pinch of Italian seasoning (thin with 1 tsp water if needed).
    9. Serve (40/30/30): plate pork with potatoes and asparagus, then add a light yogurt drizzle.

      Notes

      Portions (per person):


      Frank – Pork: 6–7 medallions. Potatoes: about 1 1/4 cups. Asparagus: about 1 1/2 cups. Yogurt drizzle.


      Susan – Pork: 4–5 medallions. Potatoes: about 1 cup. Asparagus: about 1 1/4 cups. Yogurt drizzle.


      Elise – Pork: 4–5 medallions. Potatoes: about 1 cup. Asparagus: about 1 1/4 cups. Yogurt drizzle.


      Tips (recipe-specific):


      If Basket B is crowded, cook pork in two quick batches and keep the first batch warm under foil.


      Cook asparagus after pork so it stays crisp-tender and bright.


      Keep potato pieces large so they don’t dry out before the center softens.
