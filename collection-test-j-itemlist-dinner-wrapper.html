Dinner Test Recipe 1 — Turkey Taco Bowl

Ingredients

  • 1 package lean ground turkey
  • 1 bag frozen cauliflower rice
  • 1 can black beans
  • 1 jar salsa
  • 1 jar chili powder
  • 1 jar cumin
  • 1 bottle olive oil

Preparation Steps

  1. Heat a skillet over medium-high. Add 2 tsp olive oil.
  2. Cook 1 package lean ground turkey 10–12 minutes, breaking up, until no pink remains.
  3. Season with 1 tsp chili powder and 1 tsp cumin; stir 30 seconds.
  4. Microwave 1 bag frozen cauliflower rice per bag directions; fluff.
  5. Drain and rinse 1 can black beans; warm in skillet 2 minutes.
  6. Serve over cauliflower rice with 1/2 jar salsa; refrigerate remaining salsa.

Dinner Test Recipe 2 — Lemon-Garlic Chicken + Green Beans

Ingredients

  • 1 package chicken breast tenderloins
  • 1 bag frozen green beans
  • 1 bottle olive oil
  • 1 jar garlic powder
  • 1 jar black pepper
  • 1 lemon

Preparation Steps

  1. Preheat air fryer to 390°F for 3 minutes.
  2. Measure out 12 oz chicken tenderloins; toss with 2 tsp olive oil, 1 tsp garlic powder, and black pepper.
  3. Air-fry chicken 10–12 minutes at 390°F, flipping at 6 minutes, to 165°F internal.
  4. Microwave frozen green beans per bag directions; drain.
  5. Toss green beans with 1 tsp olive oil, 1/2 tsp garlic powder, black pepper, and juice from 1 lemon; serve with chicken.
