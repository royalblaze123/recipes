Taco Burger Plates + Corn-Bean Side

Nutrition (approx)

Frank: 650 kcal • P 58g • C 58g • F 18g • Sat 7g • Na 690mg • Fiber 12g
Susan/Elise (each): 488 kcal • P 44g • C 44g • F 14g • Sat 5g • Na 518mg • Fiber 9g

Ingredients (purchase-clean)

  • 1.75 lb 93% lean ground beef
  • 4 each whole-wheat burger buns
  • 1 can corn
  • 1 can black beans
  • 1 each lime
  • 1 jar salsa
  • 1 each avocado
  • 1 bag shredded lettuce
  • kosher salt
  • black pepper
  • garlic powder
  • onion powder
  • smoked paprika
  • dried oregano
  • extra-virgin olive oil
  • mustard
  • ketchup
  • pickle relish
  • hot sauce
  • Worcestershire sauce
  • chili powder
  • ground cumin

Steps

  1. Patties: season beef with 2 tsp chili powder, 2 tsp cumin, 1 tsp garlic powder, 1/2 tsp kosher salt, 1/2 tsp black pepper. Form 6 patties.
  2. Skillet: cook patties 4–5 minutes per side to 160°F.
  3. Side: drain 1 can corn (15 oz). Measure 1 1/2 cups corn into a bowl; refrigerate remainder.
  4. Side: drain/rinse 1 can black beans (15 oz). Measure 1 1/2 cups beans into bowl; refrigerate remainder.
  5. Side dressing: add 2 tsp lime juice and 2 tbsp salsa to the corn/beans; add black pepper.
  6. Assemble: buns + lettuce + salsa; top with sliced avocado. Portion per Notes.

Notes

Taco night in burger form, with a fast corn/bean side for fiber. Portions (per person): Frank: 2 patties + 1 cup corn/bean side side + toppings Susan: 1 patty + 3/4 cup corn/bean side side + toppings Elise: 1 patty + 3/4 cup corn/bean side side + toppings Tips (recipe-specific): Use salsa as your ‘sauce’ instead of mayo. Corn/beans side eats like chips-and-salsa vibes but with fiber.
