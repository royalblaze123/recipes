Air Fryer Chicken Tender Tacos

Nutrition (approx)

Frank: 525 kcal • P 42g • C 38g • F 18g
Susan/Elise (each): 394 kcal • P 32g • C 28g • F 14g

Ingredients (purchase-clean)

  • 1.25 lb boneless skinless chicken breast
  • 4/5 package whole-grain small tortillas
  • 1 bag spring mix
  • 1 each cucumber
  • 1 jar salsa
  • 1 tub 2% plain Greek yogurt
  • 4 tsp extra-virgin olive oil
  • 2 tsp chili powder
  • 2 tsp garlic powder
  • 1 tsp black pepper
  • 1 tsp kosher salt

Steps

  1. Preheat air fryer to 400°F.
  2. Chicken tenders: cut chicken breast into 1-inch thick strips. Rub with 4 tsp olive oil. Season with 2 tsp chili powder, 2 tsp garlic powder, 1 tsp black pepper, and 1 tsp kosher salt.
  3. Air fry chicken strips 10–12 minutes, flipping at 6 minutes, until 165°F. Rest 3 minutes, then slice into bite-size pieces.
  4. Tortillas: warm tortillas 20–30 seconds per side in a dry skillet.
  5. Fresh topping: slice cucumber and toss with spring mix.
  6. Sauce: use Greek yogurt as a drizzle (2 tbsp per taco) and add salsa to taste (start 1 tbsp per taco).
  7. Assemble 7 tacos and portion per Notes.

Notes

Air-fryer chicken tender tacos for a fast, high-protein lunch. Portions (per person): Frank: 3 tacos (use 3 tortillas) + 1/3 of the filling + 1/3 of the toppings Susan: 2 tacos (use 2 tortillas) + 1/4 of the filling + 1/4 of the toppings Elise: 2 tacos (use 2 tortillas) + 1/4 of the filling + 1/4 of the toppings
