Protein Pancake Plates + Turkey + Berries

Nutrition (approx)

Frank: 645 kcal • P 55g • C 75g • F 14g • Sat 3g • Na 650mg • Fiber 10g
Susan/Elise (each): 484 kcal • P 41g • C 56g • F 10g • Sat 2g • Na 488mg • Fiber 8g

Ingredients (purchase-clean)

  • 1 box protein pancake mix
  • 1 carton egg whites
  • 12 slices deli turkey
  • 1 pint strawberries
  • kosher salt
  • black pepper
  • garlic powder
  • onion powder
  • smoked paprika
  • Italian seasoning
  • hot sauce
  • Dijon mustard
  • extra-virgin olive oil
  • salsa
  • cinnamon
  • sugar-free syrup

Steps

  1. Pancake batter: mix protein pancake mix per box, using egg whites instead of water for extra protein (measure per box serving guidance). Add cinnamon.
  2. Cook pancakes on a nonstick skillet over medium heat 2–3 minutes per side.
  3. Turkey: warm turkey slices in the same pan 30 seconds per side.
  4. Fruit: slice strawberries.
  5. Serve per Notes with a measured drizzle of sugar-free syrup.

Notes

Breakfast-for-dinner pancakes that still hit protein, with turkey and fruit. Portions (per person): Frank: 3 pancakes + 4 slices turkey + 1 cup strawberries Susan: 2 pancakes + 4 slices turkey + 3/4 cup strawberries Elise: 2 pancakes + 4 slices turkey + 3/4 cup strawberries Tips (recipe-specific): Use egg whites in batter for fluff + protein. Keep syrup measured; fruit provides the sweet.
