BBQ Pulled Chicken + Sweet Potato + Vinegar Slaw

Nutrition (approx)

Frank: 645 kcal • P 60g • C 55g • F 16g
Susan/Elise (each): 484 kcal • P 45g • C 41g • F 12g

Ingredients (purchase-clean)

  • 1.75 lb boneless skinless chicken breast
  • low-sugar BBQ sauce
  • 2.00 lb sweet potatoes
  • 1 bag coleslaw mix
  • apple cider vinegar
  • kosher salt
  • black pepper
  • garlic powder
  • onion powder
  • smoked paprika
  • Italian seasoning
  • ground cumin
  • chili powder
  • red pepper flakes
  • extra-virgin olive oil
  • low-sodium chicken broth
  • low-sodium beef broth
  • low-sodium soy sauce
  • hot sauce
  • Dijon mustard
  • balsamic vinegar
  • apple cider vinegar
  • cornstarch

Steps (slow cooker)

  1. Chicken + 1/2 cup broth; season garlic powder, pepper; modest salt.
  2. LOW 4–5 hr or HIGH 2–3 hr to 165°F; shred.
  3. Stir in BBQ sauce 5 tbsp total (measured); HIGH 10 min.
  4. Roast sweet potato cubes 425°F 22–26 min with olive oil + smoked paprika + garlic powder + modest salt.
  5. Slaw: coleslaw mix + apple cider vinegar + Dijon + olive oil + pepper + small pinch salt.
  6. Plate per Notes.

Notes

Measured BBQ + crisp vinegar slaw = big flavor without calorie creep. Portions (per person): Frank: 7 oz pulled chicken + 1 cup sweet potatoes + 1 cup slaw Susan: 5 oz chicken + 3/4 cup sweet potatoes + 3/4 cup slaw Elise: 5 oz chicken + 3/4 cup sweet potatoes + 3/4 cup slaw Tips (recipe-specific): Measure BBQ; 5 tbsp is plenty when chicken is juicy. Vinegar slaw holds texture for leftovers.
