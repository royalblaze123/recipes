Mild Curry Chicken Salad Sandwich + Snap Peas

Greek-yogurt curry chicken salad with celery and scallions, plus crisp snap peas for a full, portable lunch.

Servings

3 servings (Frank, Susan, Elise).

Times

  • Prep time: 12 minutes
  • Cook time: 15 minutes
  • Total time: 27 minutes

Ingredients

Sandwich

  • 6 slices whole wheat bread
  • 1 lb boneless skinless chicken breast
  • 1 cup nonfat Greek yogurt
  • 1 bunch celery
  • 1 bunch scallions
  • 1 each lemon
  • 1 tsp curry powder
  • 1 tsp garlic powder
  • 1 tsp ground black pepper

Side

  • 1 bag snap peas
  • 1 pint cherry tomatoes

Step-by-step Instructions

  1. Cook the chicken: Season chicken with curry powder, garlic powder, and black pepper. Air fry at 380°F until 165°F (about 10–14 minutes depending on thickness) or bake at 425°F for 15–18 minutes. Rest 5 minutes, then chop.
  2. Make the salad base: In a bowl, mix 1/2 cup Greek yogurt with 1 tbsp lemon juice. Finely chop celery and scallions; stir into yogurt.
  3. Combine: Fold chopped chicken into the yogurt mixture until evenly coated. Measure out per-person portions below; refrigerate any extra.
  4. Assemble sandwiches: Spoon chicken salad onto bread and close into 3 full sandwiches.
  5. Prep side: Rinse snap peas and tomatoes. Portion per person. Pack tomatoes separately if transporting to keep bread drier.

Portion Sizes

Portions (per person):
Frank: 2 slices bread, 3.0 oz cooked chicken, 1/4 cup Greek yogurt, 1/3 cup chopped celery, 2 tbsp chopped scallions, 1 cup snap peas, 1/2 cup cherry tomatoes

Susan: 2 slices bread, 2.25 oz cooked chicken, 3 tbsp Greek yogurt, 1/4 cup chopped celery, 1 1/2 tbsp chopped scallions, 3/4 cup snap peas, 1/3 cup cherry tomatoes

Elise: 2 slices bread, 2.25 oz cooked chicken, 3 tbsp Greek yogurt, 1/4 cup chopped celery, 1 1/2 tbsp chopped scallions, 3/4 cup snap peas, 1/3 cup cherry tomatoes
