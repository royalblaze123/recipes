Lean Roast Beef Veggie Sandwich & Orange Slices

Thin-sliced lean roast beef on whole-grain bread with mustard and vegetables, served with juicy orange slices.

Servings

3 servings.

Times

  • Prep time: 10 minutes
  • Cook time: 0 minutes
  • Total time: 10 minutes

Ingredients

Sandwich

  • 6 slices high-fiber whole-grain sandwich bread
  • 9 oz thin-sliced lean roast beef
  • 3 tbsp Dijon mustard
  • 3 tbsp low-fat plain Greek yogurt
  • 1 cup lettuce
  • 1/2 cup tomato
  • 1 red onion

Side

  • 2 medium oranges, peeled and cut into segments

Preparation Steps

  1. Dice the red onion and measure out 1/4 cup for the recipe. Refrigerate any remaining onion.
  2. In a small bowl, stir together the Dijon mustard and Greek yogurt to make a creamy mustard spread.
  3. Lay the roast beef slices on a cutting board and separate them so they are easy to portion.
  4. Lay out the six slices of whole-grain bread. Spread the creamy mustard mixture evenly over one side of each slice.
  5. Divide the roast beef among three slices of bread, giving Frank a slightly larger pile and Susan and Elise slightly smaller piles as described in the portions section.
  6. Top the roast beef with lettuce or spring mix, sliced tomato, and sliced red onion.
  7. Close each sandwich with a remaining slice of bread and press gently. Cut in half if desired.
  8. Peel the oranges, remove any large seeds, and cut the oranges into bite-size segments.
  9. Divide the orange segments onto three plates according to the portions listed.
  10. Serve each person one roast beef sandwich with their portion of orange slices.

Notes

Portions (per person):

Frank – 1 full roast beef sandwich made with about 3.5 oz lean roast beef and vegetables on 2 slices whole-grain bread, plus about 2/3 of a medium orange in segments.

Susan – 1 full roast beef sandwich made with about 3 oz lean roast beef and vegetables on 2 slices whole-grain bread, plus about 1/2 of a medium orange in segments.

Elise – 1 full roast beef sandwich made with about 2.5 oz lean roast beef and vegetables on 2 slices whole-grain bread, plus about 1/2 of a medium orange in segments.

Nutrition (per person)

  • Frank: 420 kcal; 38 g protein; 36 g carbs; 11 g fat; 7 g fiber; ~580 mg sodium.
  • Susan: 370 kcal; 32 g protein; 32 g carbs; 9 g fat; 6 g fiber; ~540 mg sodium.
  • Elise: 370 kcal; 32 g protein; 32 g carbs; 9 g fat; 6 g fiber; ~540 mg sodium.
