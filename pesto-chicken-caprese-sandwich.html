Pesto Chicken Caprese Sandwich + Carrots

Caprese-style chicken sandwich with a measured pesto layer and mozzarella, paired with carrot sticks for a clean, chefy lunch.

Servings

3 servings (Frank, Susan, Elise).

Times

  • Prep time: 12 minutes
  • Cook time: 15 minutes
  • Total time: 27 minutes

Ingredients

Sandwich

  • 6 slices whole wheat bread
  • 1 lb boneless skinless chicken breast
  • 3 slices part-skim mozzarella cheese
  • 1 bunch fresh basil
  • 1 pint cherry tomatoes
  • 6 tsp basil pesto

Side

  • 1 lb bag carrots
  • 2 hearts romaine hearts

Step-by-step Instructions

  1. Cook the chicken: Air fry at 380°F until 165°F (about 10–14 minutes) or bake at 425°F for 15–18 minutes. Rest 5 minutes, then thin-slice.
  2. Assemble sandwiches: Spread pesto on bread (measure per person). Layer chicken, mozzarella (cut slices for 0.75 portions), basil leaves, and halved cherry tomatoes. Close into 3 full sandwiches.
  3. Prep side: Chop romaine for a quick salad and portion carrot sticks per person. Pack salad separately with no dressing, or dress lightly at serving.

Portion Sizes

Portions (per person):

Frank: 2 slices bread, 2.5 oz cooked chicken, 1 slice mozzarella, 2 tsp pesto, 1/2 cup cherry tomatoes, 6 basil leaves, 1 cup romaine, 2 oz carrots

Susan: 2 slices bread, 2 oz cooked chicken, 3/4 slice mozzarella, 1 1/2 tsp pesto, 1/3 cup cherry tomatoes, 4 basil leaves, 3/4 cup romaine, 1 1/2 oz carrots

Elise: 2 slices bread, 2 oz cooked chicken, 3/4 slice mozzarella, 1 1/2 tsp pesto, 1/3 cup cherry tomatoes, 4 basil leaves, 3/4 cup romaine, 1 1/2 oz carrots
