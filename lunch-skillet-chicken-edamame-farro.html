Warm Lunch Skillet: Chicken, Edamame, and Farro with Lemon-Garlic

Prep: 18 min   Cook: 16 min   Total: 34 min

Ingredients

  • 1.1 lb chicken breast
  • 0.5 cup dry farro
  • 0.5 bag frozen shelled edamame
  • 1 bag spinach
  • 1 lemon
  • olive oil
  • garlic powder
  • black pepper
  • salt substitute
  • Italian seasoning

Rules enforced: Clean AnyList Ingredients (quantities before items; fresh meat/poultry/fish use weights; packaged items use fractional containers when partial). Condiments/spices/seasonings have no measurements in Ingredients; all measurements are in Steps. No olives, no squash, no Brussels sprouts, no zucchini, no tofu/tempeh. Lunch validations: Frank calories 500–550, sat fat ≤6g, sodium ≤650mg, fiber ≥8g, added sugar ≤6g.

Step-by-step Instructions

  1. Cook farro: cook 1/2 cup dry farro with 1 1/2 cups water per package directions (about 20 minutes).
  2. Edamame: microwave 1/2 bag edamame 3–4 minutes; set aside; refreeze remainder.
  3. Chicken: dice 1.1 lb chicken breast. Season with 2 tsp Italian seasoning + 1 tsp garlic powder + 1/2 tsp black pepper + 1/2 tsp salt substitute.
  4. Sauté chicken with 2 tsp olive oil 6–8 minutes until 165°F.
  5. Stir in 1 bag spinach and cook 1–2 minutes until wilted.
  6. Add cooked farro and edamame; heat 2 minutes.
  7. Finish: add 2 tsp lemon juice per plate.
