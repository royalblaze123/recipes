Freezer Lentil Bolognese Sauce + Barilla Protein+

Nutrition (approx)

Frank: 640 kcal • P 55g • C 70g • F 14g • Sat 3g • Na 700mg • Fiber 14g
Susan/Elise (each): 480 kcal • P 41g • C 52g • F 10g • Sat 2g • Na 525mg • Fiber 10g

Ingredients (purchase-clean)

  • 1 lb 93% lean ground turkey
  • 1 cup dry lentils
  • 1 jar marinara sauce
  • 2 cans no-salt-added diced tomatoes
  • 1 each onion
  • 1 box Barilla Protein+ pasta
  • kosher salt
  • black pepper
  • garlic powder
  • onion powder
  • smoked paprika
  • Italian seasoning
  • ground cumin
  • chili powder
  • red pepper flakes
  • extra-virgin olive oil
  • low-sodium chicken broth
  • cornstarch

Steps

  1. Sauce base: sauté diced onion with olive oil; add turkey and seasonings (Italian seasoning, garlic powder, black pepper, modest salt). Brown 7–9 minutes.
  2. Lentils: add 1 cup dry lentils, 2 cans diced tomatoes (14.5 oz each), and 2 cups marinara (measured). Add 1 cup low-sodium broth if needed to keep lentils just submerged.
  3. Simmer: simmer 28–35 minutes until lentils are tender and sauce is thick (uncovered last 10 minutes).
  4. Freeze: cool fully; portion sauce into freezer containers and freeze.
  5. Pasta night: cook Barilla Protein+ pasta to al dente; reheat sauce until bubbling; toss and serve per Notes.

Notes

Freezer-first sauce: make once, freeze, then pasta nights are automatic. Portions (per person): Frank: About 2 1/2 cups pasta + sauce Susan: About 2 cups pasta + sauce Elise: About 2 cups pasta + sauce Tips (recipe-specific): Freeze the sauce separately so pasta texture stays perfect. Uncovered simmer at the end keeps it thick after thawing.
