Creamy Verde Shrimp Enchiladas + Roasted Broccoli

Nutrition (approx)

Frank: 620 kcal • P 56g • C 36g • F 22g
Susan/Elise (each): 465 kcal • P 42g • C 27g • F 16g

Ingredients (purchase-clean)

  • 1.50 lb raw shrimp
  • 1 package whole-grain small tortillas
  • 1 jar salsa verde
  • 1 tub 2% plain Greek yogurt
  • 1/2 bag reduced-fat shredded Mexican cheese blend
  • 12 oz fresh broccoli florets
  • 4 tsp extra-virgin olive oil
  • 2 tsp garlic powder
  • 1 tsp black pepper
  • 1 tsp kosher salt

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 400°F. Lightly coat a 9x13-inch baking dish with nonstick spray.
  2. Roasted broccoli: toss broccoli florets with 2 tsp olive oil, 1 tsp black pepper, and 1/2 tsp kosher salt. Roast on a sheet pan 12–15 minutes until browned at edges.
  3. Shrimp: pat shrimp dry. Season with 2 tsp garlic powder, 1 tsp black pepper, and 1/2 tsp kosher salt. Sauté in a hot nonstick skillet with 2 tsp olive oil for 2–3 minutes total, flipping once, just until opaque.
  4. Creamy verde sauce: heat salsa verde in a saucepan over low heat 3–4 minutes until warm. Remove from heat and whisk in 1/2 cup Greek yogurt until smooth.
  5. Tortillas: warm tortillas 15–20 seconds per side in a dry skillet.
  6. Assembly: spread 1/2 cup creamy verde sauce in the baking dish. Fill tortillas with shrimp plus a pinch of cheese; roll and place seam-side down.
  7. Top: pour remaining creamy verde sauce over enchiladas and sprinkle remaining cheese over the center line.
  8. Bake 12–14 minutes until bubbly (shrimp is already cooked; you’re mainly heating through).
  9. Portion: Frank 4 enchiladas; Susan 3; Elise 3. Serve with roasted broccoli.

Notes

Creamy verde shrimp enchiladas using Greek yogurt for richness without heavy cream. Portions (per person): Frank: 4 enchiladas (4 tortillas) + 1/3 of the side/veg Susan: 3 enchiladas (3 tortillas) + 1/4 of the side/veg Elise: 3 enchiladas (3 tortillas) + 1/4 of the side/veg
