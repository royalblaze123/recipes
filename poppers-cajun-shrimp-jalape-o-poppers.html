Cajun Shrimp Jalapeño Poppers

Air Fryer or Sheet Pan • Yield: 24 popper halves

Ingredients (purchase-clean)

  • 12 each fresh jalapeños
  • 16 oz fresh shrimp
  • 6 oz reduced-fat cream cheese
  • 1 oz grated parmesan
  • cajun seasoning
  • kosher salt
  • black pepper

Steps

  1. Preheat air fryer to 370°F (or oven to 425°F).
  2. Chop 16 oz shrimp small. Sauté 2–3 min until just opaque with 1 tsp cajun seasoning, 1/2 tsp kosher salt, and 1/2 tsp black pepper; cool 5 min.
  3. Halve and seed 12 jalapeños; pat dry.
  4. Mix filling: soften 6 oz cream cheese; stir in shrimp; add 1 oz parmesan.
  5. Stuff halves evenly.
  6. Cook: air-fry 370°F for 7–9 min (or bake 425°F for 12–15 min) until hot.

Notes

Flavor-forward holiday poppers designed for snacking or a light meal. Portions (guidance): Do not overcook shrimp before stuffing—carryover heat finishes it. Snack portion: 4–6 halves.
