Chicken & White Bean Tostada Plates

Nutrition (approx)

Frank: 640 kcal • P 60g • C 50g • F 20g
Susan/Elise (each): 480 kcal • P 45g • C 38g • F 15g

Ingredients (purchase-clean)

  • 1.25 lb boneless skinless chicken breast
  • 1 can cannellini beans
  • 1 package baked tostada shells
  • 1 each avocado
  • 1/2 jar salsa
  • 4 tsp extra-virgin olive oil
  • 1 each lime
  • 2 tsp chili powder
  • 2 tsp cumin
  • 2 tsp garlic powder
  • 1 tsp smoked paprika
  • 1 tsp black pepper
  • 1 tsp kosher salt

Steps

  1. Tostadas: warm in a 350°F oven for 3–4 minutes to crisp and heat through.
  2. Chicken: pat dry and cut into 1/2-inch pieces. Season with the spices.
  3. Cook chicken in a skillet 8–10 minutes until browned and 165°F.
  4. Beans: can size note—use a standard 15 oz can cannellini beans. Drain and rinse. Measure out 1 1/2 cups beans; refrigerate any remainder.
  5. Combine: add measured beans to the chicken with 2 tsp lime juice and warm 2 minutes.
  6. Avocado: halve, pit, and dice. Use about 1/2 avocado total; refrigerate remaining avocado with the pit to slow browning.
  7. Serve: top tostadas with chicken/bean mix, salsa, and avocado. Portion per Notes.

Notes

High-fiber tostadas using white beans + chicken, finished with salsa and avocado. Portions (per person): Frank: 2 tostadas + 1/3 of the protein topping + 1/3 of the avocado + salsa Susan: 1 tostada + 1/4 of the protein topping + 1/4 of the avocado + salsa Elise: 1 tostada + 1/4 of the protein topping + 1/4 of the avocado + salsa
