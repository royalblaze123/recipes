Ginger-Garlic Turkey Lettuce Wrap Plates + Rice

Nutrition (approx)

Frank: 640 kcal • P 58g • C 70g • F 14g • Sat 3g • Na 700mg • Fiber 10g
Susan/Elise (each): 480 kcal • P 44g • C 52g • F 10g • Sat 2g • Na 525mg • Fiber 8g

Ingredients (purchase-clean)

  • 1.50 lb 93% lean ground turkey
  • 1 head lettuce
  • 1 bag frozen mixed vegetables
  • 1 cup dry brown rice
  • low-sodium soy sauce
  • sesame oil
  • kosher salt
  • black pepper
  • garlic powder
  • onion powder
  • smoked paprika
  • Italian seasoning
  • ground cumin
  • chili powder
  • red pepper flakes
  • extra-virgin olive oil
  • low-sodium chicken broth

Steps

  1. Rice: cook 1 cup dry brown rice with 2 cups water 35–40 minutes; rest 5 minutes.
  2. Turkey: brown turkey with 2 tsp olive oil, garlic powder, black pepper 7–9 minutes.
  3. Veg: add mixed vegetables; cook 6–8 minutes until water evaporates and edges brown.
  4. Season: stir in 4 tbsp low-sodium soy sauce (measured) and 1 tsp sesame oil.
  5. Wraps: separate lettuce leaves; spoon filling into leaves.
  6. Serve with rice per Notes.

Notes

Comfort lettuce-wrap dinner with rice on the side (big flavor, low heaviness). Portions (per person): Frank: 3 lettuce wraps + 1 cup cooked rice Susan: 3 lettuce wraps + 3/4 cup cooked rice Elise: 3 lettuce wraps + 3/4 cup cooked rice Tips (recipe-specific): Brown the veg first so the filling tastes ‘wok’ style. Sesame oil is strong—use 1 tsp and let it carry the dish.
