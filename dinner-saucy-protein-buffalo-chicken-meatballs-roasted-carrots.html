Buffalo Chicken Meatballs + Roasted Carrots

Nutrition (approx)

Frank: 625 kcal • P 60g • C 45g • F 18g • Sat 5g • Na 690mg • Fiber 10g
Susan/Elise (each): 469 kcal • P 45g • C 34g • F 14g • Sat 4g • Na 518mg • Fiber 8g

Ingredients (purchase-clean)

  • 2.00 lb 99% lean ground chicken
  • 1 package whole-wheat breadcrumbs
  • 2 each eggs
  • 1 bottle buffalo sauce
  • 2 lb carrots
  • 1 bag shredded lettuce
  • kosher salt
  • black pepper
  • garlic powder
  • onion powder
  • Italian seasoning
  • smoked paprika
  • chili powder
  • ground cumin
  • crushed red pepper
  • extra-virgin olive oil
  • ranch seasoning

Steps

  1. Oven: heat to 425°F. Line two sheet pans and lightly oil.
  2. Carrots: peel and cut into sticks. Toss with 2 tsp olive oil, 1 tsp garlic powder, 1/2 tsp kosher salt, 1/2 tsp black pepper. Roast 22–26 minutes, flipping once.
  3. Meatballs: mix chicken with 1/2 cup breadcrumbs, 2 eggs, 1 tsp garlic powder, 1 tsp onion powder, 1/2 tsp kosher salt, 1/2 tsp black pepper. Form 18–20 meatballs.
  4. Bake 13–15 minutes to 165°F.
  5. Sauce: toss meatballs with 1/4 cup buffalo sauce.
  6. Serve: bed plates with shredded lettuce, top with meatballs, serve carrots on the side. Portion per Notes.

Notes

Buffalo comfort without wings—meatballs keep it easy and lean. Portions (per person): Frank: 8–9 meatballs + 1 cup carrots + 1 cup lettuce Susan: 6–7 meatballs + 3/4 cup carrots + 3/4 cup lettuce Elise: 6–7 meatballs + 3/4 cup carrots + 3/4 cup lettuce Tips (recipe-specific): Use a measured 1/4 cup buffalo for the batch; add more at the table if needed. Roast carrots hard for the ‘bar food’ side vibe.
