Egg Muffin Sandwich Plates + Hash + Fruit

Nutrition (approx)

Frank: 640 kcal • P 55g • C 60g • F 16g • Sat 6g • Na 700mg • Fiber 10g
Susan/Elise (each): 480 kcal • P 41g • C 45g • F 12g • Sat 4g • Na 525mg • Fiber 8g

Ingredients (purchase-clean)

  • 6 each whole-wheat English muffins
  • 6 each eggs
  • 6 slices reduced-fat cheese slices
  • 12 slices deli turkey
  • 1.50 lb baby potatoes
  • 1 bunch bananas
  • kosher salt
  • black pepper
  • garlic powder
  • onion powder
  • smoked paprika
  • Italian seasoning
  • hot sauce
  • Dijon mustard
  • extra-virgin olive oil
  • salsa

Steps

  1. Oven: heat to 425°F.
  2. Hash: dice baby potatoes; toss with 2 tsp olive oil, smoked paprika, black pepper, modest kosher salt. Roast 22–26 minutes, stirring once.
  3. Eggs: cook 6 eggs into round patties (ring mold if you have it) 2–3 minutes per side.
  4. Assemble: each person gets 2 muffin halves (1 sandwich) with egg + turkey + cheese.
  5. Warm assembled sandwiches in oven 2 minutes to melt.
  6. Serve with hash and banana per Notes.

Notes

McMuffin-style comfort plate that still fits the macros (portion-controlled). Portions (per person): Frank: 1 sandwich + 1 cup hash + 1/2 banana Susan: 1 sandwich + 3/4 cup hash + 1/2 banana Elise: 1 sandwich + 3/4 cup hash + 1/2 banana Tips (recipe-specific): Roasted hash gives the fast-food feel without deep frying. Make egg rounds so the sandwich eats like the real thing.
