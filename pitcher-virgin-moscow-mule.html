Virgin Moscow Mule (Pitcher Size)

Yield: 8 drinks • Prep: 10 min • Total: 10 min

Ingredients (AnyList-ready)

  • 8 oz lime juice
  • 6 oz simple syrup
  • 48 oz ginger beer
  • 8 cups ice

Steps

  1. In a pitcher, combine lime juice and simple syrup. Stir 10 seconds and chill.
  2. To serve: fill each glass with ice, pour in 2 oz base, then top with 6 oz ginger beer. Stir once, gently.

Batch rule: keep carbonation separate until serving for the best fizz.
