Chicken & White Bean Taco Salad Bowls

Nutrition (approx)

Frank: 640 kcal • P 60g • C 44g • F 20g
Susan/Elise (each): 480 kcal • P 45g • C 33g • F 15g

Ingredients (purchase-clean)

  • 1.25 lb boneless skinless chicken breast
  • 1 bag spring mix
  • 1 each avocado
  • 1/2 jar salsa
  • 1/2 tub 2% plain Greek yogurt
  • 1/2 bag bean-based tortilla chips
  • 1 can cannellini beans
  • 1 pint cherry tomatoes
  • 4 tsp extra-virgin olive oil
  • 2 tsp chili powder
  • 2 tsp cumin
  • 2 tsp garlic powder
  • 1 tsp smoked paprika
  • 1 tsp black pepper
  • 1 tsp kosher salt

Steps

  1. Greens: add the spring mix to a large serving bowl.
  2. Chicken: pat dry and cut into 1/2-inch pieces. Season with taco spices.
  3. Cook chicken in a nonstick skillet over medium-high 8–10 minutes until browned and 165°F.
  4. Beans: can size note—use a standard 15 oz can cannellini beans. Drain and rinse. Measure out 1 1/2 cups beans; refrigerate any remainder.
  5. Combine: add measured beans to the chicken and warm 2 minutes.
  6. Tomatoes: halve cherry tomatoes.
  7. Avocado: halve, pit, and dice. Use about 1/2 avocado total; refrigerate the remaining avocado with the pit to slow browning.
  8. Salsa-yogurt: in a small bowl, stir 6 tbsp Greek yogurt with 6 tbsp salsa until smooth.
  9. Crunch: portion chips by weight. Frank 1.0 oz; Susan 0.75 oz; Elise 0.75 oz.
  10. Build bowls: divide greens, then add hot protein mix, then cold toppings. Drizzle salsa-yogurt and finish with avocado and measured chips. Portion per Notes.

Notes

Chicken + white beans taco salad: extra filling with the same measured chip crunch. Portions (per person): Frank: Salad base: 1/3 of the greens + 1/3 of the hot protein mix + toppings (salsa + avocado + yogurt). Crunch: chips 1.0 oz Susan: Salad base: 1/4 of the greens + 1/4 of the hot protein mix + toppings (salsa + avocado + yogurt). Crunch: chips 0.75 oz Elise: Salad base: 1/4 of the greens + 1/4 of the hot protein mix + toppings (salsa + avocado + yogurt). Crunch: chips 0.75 oz
