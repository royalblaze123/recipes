One-Pan Shrimp Fajita Tray + Brown Rice

Nutrition (approx)

Frank: 640 kcal • P 55g • C 60g • F 16g • Sat 3g • Na 680mg • Fiber 10g
Susan/Elise (each): 480 kcal • P 41g • C 45g • F 12g • Sat 2g • Na 510mg • Fiber 8g

Ingredients (purchase-clean)

  • 1.50 lb shrimp
  • 2 each bell peppers
  • 1 each onion
  • 1 cup dry brown rice
  • 1 each lime
  • kosher salt
  • black pepper
  • garlic powder
  • onion powder
  • smoked paprika
  • Italian seasoning
  • ground cumin
  • chili powder
  • red pepper flakes
  • extra-virgin olive oil
  • low-sodium chicken broth

Steps

  1. Rice: cook 1 cup dry brown rice with 2 cups water 35–40 minutes; rest 5 minutes.
  2. Oven: heat to 450°F. Line sheet pan.
  3. Peppers/onion: slice and toss with 2 tsp olive oil, 1 tsp chili powder, 1 tsp cumin, 1 tsp garlic powder, black pepper, modest kosher salt. Roast 10 minutes.
  4. Shrimp: toss shrimp with 2 tsp olive oil, 1 tsp chili powder, 1/2 tsp cumin, black pepper, and 2 tsp lime juice.
  5. Add shrimp to pan; roast 6–8 minutes until pink.
  6. Serve over rice with extra lime per Notes.

Notes

Fast fajitas with high heat and lime—big flavor, minimal cleanup. Portions (per person): Frank: About 6 oz shrimp + 1 cup peppers/onions over 1 cup cooked rice Susan: About 4 oz shrimp + 3/4 cup veg over 3/4 cup cooked rice Elise: About 4 oz shrimp + 3/4 cup veg over 3/4 cup cooked rice Tips (recipe-specific): Roast veg first so shrimp doesn’t overcook. High heat gives restaurant-style char without extra oil.
