Chicken Broccoli Cheddar Bake (Divan-Style)

Nutrition (approx)

Frank: 645 kcal • P 60g • C 55g • F 16g • Sat 6g • Na 690mg • Fiber 10g
Susan/Elise (each): 484 kcal • P 45g • C 41g • F 12g • Sat 4g • Na 518mg • Fiber 8g

Ingredients (purchase-clean)

  • 1.75 lb boneless skinless chicken breast
  • 1 bag frozen broccoli florets
  • 1 carton skim milk
  • cornstarch
  • 1 container reduced-fat shredded cheddar cheese
  • 1 box Barilla Protein+ pasta
  • kosher salt
  • black pepper
  • garlic powder
  • onion powder
  • Italian seasoning
  • smoked paprika
  • cornstarch
  • extra-virgin olive oil
  • low-sodium chicken broth

Steps

  1. Oven: heat to 425°F.
  2. Pasta: cook Protein+ pasta al dente; drain.
  3. Chicken: sauté diced chicken with 2 tsp olive oil, garlic powder, onion powder, modest kosher salt, black pepper 8–10 minutes to 165°F.
  4. Broccoli: roast frozen broccoli at 425°F with 2 tsp olive oil for 16–20 minutes; stir once.
  5. Sauce: whisk 2 tbsp cornstarch into 2 cups skim milk; simmer 3–5 minutes until thick.
  6. Cheese: stir in 6 oz reduced-fat cheddar until melted.
  7. Assemble: combine pasta + chicken + roasted broccoli + sauce in dish.
  8. Bake 10 minutes until bubbling. Rest 5 minutes. Serve per Notes.

Notes

Cheesy chicken-broccoli bake comfort with roasted broccoli flavor. Portions (per person): Frank: About 2 1/2 cups (1/3 of pan) + veg side Susan: About 2 cups (1/4 of pan) + veg side Elise: About 2 cups (1/4 of pan) + veg side Tips (recipe-specific): Roasting broccoli gives ‘cheesy casserole’ flavor without extra cheese. Rest after baking so portions hold together.
