Turkey Taco Skillet Tacos

Nutrition (approx)

Frank: 525 kcal • P 42g • C 38g • F 18g
Susan/Elise (each): 394 kcal • P 32g • C 28g • F 14g

Ingredients (purchase-clean)

  • 1.25 lb 93% lean ground turkey
  • 4/5 package whole-grain small tortillas
  • 1 can black beans
  • 1 bag spring mix
  • 1 each lime
  • 1 tub 2% plain Greek yogurt
  • 1 jar salsa
  • 2 tsp Worcestershire sauce
  • 2 tsp chili powder
  • 2 tsp cumin
  • 2 tsp garlic powder
  • 1 tsp smoked paprika
  • 1 tsp black pepper
  • 1 tsp kosher salt

Steps

  1. Heat a large nonstick skillet over medium-high.
  2. Turkey: add ground turkey and cook 7–9 minutes, breaking up, until browned and cooked through.
  3. Beans: can size note—use a standard 15 oz can black beans. Drain and rinse. Measure out 1 1/2 cups beans for this recipe; refrigerate any remainder.
  4. Season: add 2 tsp chili powder, 2 tsp cumin, 2 tsp garlic powder, 1 tsp smoked paprika, 1 tsp black pepper, 1 tsp kosher salt, and 2 tsp Worcestershire. Stir 60 seconds to toast spices.
  5. Add beans to the skillet with 2–3 tbsp hot water from the tap to help spices coat (do not add water to Ingredients). Simmer 2–3 minutes.
  6. Tortillas: warm tortillas in a dry skillet 20–30 seconds per side.
  7. Yogurt-lime: mix 6 tbsp Greek yogurt with 2 tsp lime juice.
  8. Greens: use spring mix as the ‘shredded lettuce’ layer. Add a small handful to each tortilla.
  9. Assemble 7 tacos and portion as listed in Notes.

Notes

Classic taco-night flavor with turkey + black beans for a higher-fiber lunch. Portions (per person): Frank: 3 tacos (use 3 tortillas) + 1/3 of the filling + 1/3 of the toppings Susan: 2 tacos (use 2 tortillas) + 1/4 of the filling + 1/4 of the toppings Elise: 2 tacos (use 2 tortillas) + 1/4 of the filling + 1/4 of the toppings
