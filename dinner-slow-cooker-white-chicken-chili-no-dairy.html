White Chicken Chili (No Dairy)

Nutrition (approx)

Frank: 650 kcal • P 60g • C 60g • F 14g
Susan/Elise (each): 488 kcal • P 45g • C 45g • F 10g

Ingredients (purchase-clean)

  • 1.75 lb boneless skinless chicken breast
  • 1 can white beans
  • 1 bag frozen corn
  • 1 can green chiles
  • low-sodium chicken broth
  • kosher salt
  • black pepper
  • garlic powder
  • onion powder
  • smoked paprika
  • Italian seasoning
  • ground cumin
  • chili powder
  • red pepper flakes
  • extra-virgin olive oil
  • low-sodium chicken broth
  • low-sodium beef broth
  • low-sodium soy sauce
  • hot sauce
  • Dijon mustard
  • balsamic vinegar
  • apple cider vinegar
  • cornstarch

Steps (slow cooker)

  1. Add chicken + broth (5 cups) + beans (15 oz; add 1 1/2 cups) + corn (2 cups) + green chiles (4 oz).
  2. Season cumin, chili powder, garlic powder, pepper; modest salt.
  3. LOW 4–5 hr or HIGH 2–3 hr; shred chicken.
  4. Thicken: mash 1/2 cup beans against cooker wall OR cornstarch slurry 10 min.
  5. Serve per Notes.

Notes

Creamy-feeling white chili without dairy (beans do the work). Portions (per person): Frank: About 2 1/2 cups (heaping bowl) Susan: About 2 cups (bowl) Elise: About 2 cups (bowl) Tips (recipe-specific): Mash beans for thickness; no dairy needed. Cornstarch only if you want it extra thick.
