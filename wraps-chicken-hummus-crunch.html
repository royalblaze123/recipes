Chicken Hummus Crunch Wrap

High-fiber whole-wheat wrap with grilled chicken, hummus, and crisp veggies for a full, packable lunch that fits your Frank Household targets.

Servings

3 servings.

Times

  • Prep time: 15 minutes
  • Cook time: 0 minutes (uses pre-cooked chicken)
  • Total time: 15 minutes

Ingredients

  • 1 large high-fiber whole-wheat tortilla
  • 2 medium high-fiber whole-wheat tortillas
  • 10 1/4 oz cooked skinless chicken breast
  • 6 tbsp hummus
  • 3 tbsp 2% plain Greek yogurt
  • 1 tsp extra-virgin olive oil
  • 1 1/2 cups shredded romaine lettuce
  • 1/2 cup matchstick carrots
  • 1/2 cup thinly sliced cucumber
  • 3 tbsp diced red bell pepper
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice
  • 1/2 tsp garlic powder
  • 1/2 tsp dried parsley or dill
  • 1/4 tsp ground black pepper
  • 1/8 to 1/4 tsp kosher or sea salt

Preparation Steps

  1. Rinse and pat dry the romaine, carrots, cucumber, and bell pepper. Shred or slice as directed so they are ready for layering in the wraps.
  2. In a small bowl, whisk together the Greek yogurt, olive oil, lemon juice, garlic powder, dried parsley or dill, black pepper, and a pinch of salt until smooth. Taste and adjust seasoning if needed. This will be drizzled over the chicken and vegetables.
  3. Warm each tortilla in a dry skillet over low heat for 15–20 seconds per side, or microwave for about 10 seconds, just until pliable. Keep them covered with a clean towel while you assemble.
  4. Place the large (10-inch) tortilla on a cutting board. Spread about 2 tbsp hummus in a wide strip down the center, leaving a 1–2 inch border. Layer about 4.25 oz sliced cooked chicken over the hummus. Top with a generous handful of shredded romaine, some matchstick carrots, cucumber slices, and diced red bell pepper. Drizzle about 1 tbsp of the yogurt sauce over the filling.
  5. Fold the sides of the large tortilla toward the center, then roll up tightly from the bottom into a burrito-style wrap. Wrap in foil or parchment for an easy grab-and-go lunch and slice in half if desired.
  6. Place one medium (8-inch) tortilla on the board. Spread about 1 1/2 tbsp hummus down the center. Add about 3 oz sliced chicken, a moderate handful of romaine, carrots, cucumber, and bell pepper. Drizzle about 1 tbsp yogurt sauce over the top.
  7. Fold in the sides of the medium tortilla and roll up firmly from the bottom. Wrap in foil or parchment and slice in half if desired.
  8. Repeat the same process with the remaining medium (8-inch) tortilla, using about 1 1/2 tbsp hummus, 3 oz chicken, romaine, carrots, cucumber, and bell pepper, plus about 1 tbsp yogurt sauce.
  9. Serve immediately for best texture, or refrigerate the wrapped sandwiches for up to 24 hours. Store tightly wrapped in foil or parchment in a lidded container for work or school lunches.
