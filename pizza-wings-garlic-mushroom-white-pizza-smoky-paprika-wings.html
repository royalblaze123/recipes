Garlic Mushroom White Pizza with Smoky Paprika Wings

Prep: 20 min   Cook: 30 min   Total: 50 min

Ingredients

  • 1 pizza dough ball
  • 6 oz reduced-fat mozzarella cheese
  • 2 oz grated parmesan cheese
  • 1 package baby bella mushrooms
  • 1 bulb garlic
  • 1 bag spinach
  • 2.25 lb chicken wings
  • 1 lemon
  • olive oil
  • black pepper
  • salt substitute
  • smoked paprika
  • garlic powder

RBM rules: quantities appear before items; fresh meat/poultry/fish use weights. Condiments/spices/seasonings are listed name-only (no tsp/tbsp in Ingredients); all measurements are in Steps. Water is referenced in Steps only. No olives, no squash, no Brussels sprouts, no yogurt in dinners.

Step-by-step Instructions

  1. Pizza: preheat oven to 475°F. Stretch dough into 12-inch round on parchment.
  2. Mushrooms: slice thick. Sauté 4–5 minutes to reduce moisture (recommended).
  3. Make garlic oil: mix 2 tbsp olive oil with 2 minced garlic cloves + black pepper.
  4. Top pizza (white): brush crust with garlic oil, add mozzarella + parmesan, add mushrooms, then bake 10–12 minutes. Add spinach after baking to wilt. Cut into 10 slices.
  5. Wings: pat dry. Toss with 2 tsp olive oil + 2 tsp salt substitute + 2 tsp smoked paprika + 1 tsp garlic powder + 1 tsp black pepper + zest of 1 lemon.
  6. Air fry wings: 380°F for 24–26 minutes; then 400°F for 4–6 minutes. Finish with lemon squeeze.
  7. Portion: Frank 4 slices + 8 wings; Susan/Elise 3 slices + 6 wings.
