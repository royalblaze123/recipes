Chopped Salad: Chicken Pesto Chop with Tomatoes and Cucumber

Prep: 18 min   Cook: 10 min   Total: 28 min

Ingredients

  • 1.1 lb chicken breast
  • 0.5 bag shredded cabbage
  • 1 cucumber
  • 1 pint cherry tomatoes
  • 0.5 can no-salt-added chickpeas
  • 1 lemon
  • olive oil
  • basil pesto
  • black pepper
  • salt substitute
  • vinegar

Rules enforced: Clean AnyList Ingredients (quantities before items; fresh meat/poultry/fish use weights; packaged items use fractional containers when partial). Condiments/spices/seasonings have no measurements in Ingredients; all measurements are in Steps. No olives, no squash, no Brussels sprouts, no zucchini, no tofu/tempeh. Lunch validations: Frank calories 500–550, sat fat ≤6g, sodium ≤650mg, fiber ≥8g, added sugar ≤6g.

Step-by-step Instructions

  1. Chicken: cook 1.1 lb chicken breast in a skillet with 2 tsp olive oil 6–8 minutes until 165°F; cool and dice.
  2. Chickpeas: drain and rinse 1/2 can chickpeas; measure 3/4 cup for salad; refrigerate remainder.
  3. Base: combine 1/2 bag shredded cabbage + diced 1 cucumber + halved 1 pint cherry tomatoes.
  4. Dressing: whisk 1 tbsp basil pesto + 2 tsp vinegar + 2 tsp lemon juice + 2 tbsp water + 1/4 tsp black pepper + 1/4 tsp salt substitute.
  5. Toss salad with chicken and chickpeas.
  6. Portion: Frank largest bowl; Susan/Elise ~75%.
  7. Meal-prep: keep dressing separate if you want the crispest texture.
