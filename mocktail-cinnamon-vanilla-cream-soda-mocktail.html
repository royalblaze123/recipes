Cinnamon-Vanilla Cream Soda Mocktail

Yield: 1 drink • Prep: 5 min • Total: 5 min

Ingredients (AnyList-ready)

  • 6 oz club soda
  • 2 oz unsweetened vanilla almond milk
  • 1 oz simple syrup
  • 1/4 tsp ground cinnamon
  • 1 cup ice

Steps

  1. Fill a tall glass with 1 cup ice.
  2. Add 1 oz simple syrup and 2 oz unsweetened vanilla almond milk.
  3. Add 1/4 tsp ground cinnamon and stir 10 seconds.
  4. Top with 6 oz club soda.
  5. Stir once, gently.

Notes

If you want more dessert-style body, increase almond milk to 3 oz and reduce club soda to 5 oz.

Tip: Use plenty of ice and a quick, gentle stir to keep carbonation lively.
