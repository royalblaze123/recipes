Freezer Turkey & Egg White Breakfast Sandwiches

High-protein breakfast sandwiches with whole grains for slow carbs—built for grab-and-go mornings.

Servings

12 sandwiches (freezer batch).

Times

  • Prep time: 20 minutes
  • Cook time: 20 minutes
  • Total time: 40 minutes

Ingredients

  • 12 each whole wheat English muffins
  • 1 carton egg whites
  • 24 slices deli turkey breast
  • 12 slices reduced-fat cheddar cheese
  • 1/2 tsp ground black pepper
  • 1 tsp avocado oil

Preparation Steps

  1. Oven: Preheat to 350°F.
  2. Pan: Brush a 9x13 pan lightly with the 1 tsp avocado oil.
  3. Egg slab: Measure 4 cups (32 fl oz) egg whites (this uses 1 full 32 fl oz carton). Pour into the pan. Sprinkle with 1/2 tsp ground black pepper. Bake 18–20 minutes until set. Cool 5 minutes.
  4. English muffins: Split and toast 12 whole wheat English muffins.
  5. Deli: Ask for deli turkey breast thin sliced for sandwiches (not shaved).
  6. Assemble: Cut the egg slab into 12 squares. Build each sandwich: 1 English muffin + 1 egg square + 2 slices deli turkey + 1 slice reduced-fat cheddar.
  7. Freeze: Wrap each sandwich tightly and freeze in a labeled bag for up to 1 month.
  8. Breakfast use: Thaw overnight in the fridge or microwave from frozen until hot; eat with your morning shake.

Notes

High-protein breakfast sandwiches with whole grains for slow carbs—built for grab-and-go mornings.

Portions (per person):

Frank – 1 sandwich with your morning shake (slow-carb add-on).

Susan – Optional: 1 sandwich with breakfast if desired.

Elise – Optional: 1 sandwich with breakfast if desired.

Tips (recipe-specific):

To keep sodium down, pick lower-sodium deli turkey and reduced-fat cheese.

If microwaving, wrap in a paper towel so the muffin stays soft, not rubbery.

If lunch is late, add 1 piece of fruit with the sandwich and shake.

Nutrition (per person)

  • Frank: 230 kcal; 24 g protein; 24 g carbs; 6 g fat; 4 g fiber; 420 mg sodium
  • Susan: 230 kcal; 24 g protein; 24 g carbs; 6 g fat; 4 g fiber; 420 mg sodium
  • Elise: 230 kcal; 24 g protein; 24 g carbs; 6 g fat; 4 g fiber; 420 mg sodium
