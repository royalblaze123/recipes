Freezer Turkey & Egg White Breakfast Sandwiches

High-protein breakfast sandwiches with whole grains for slow carbs—built for grab-and-go mornings.

Servings

12 sandwiches.

Times

  • Prep time: 20 minutes
  • Cook time: 20 minutes
  • Total time: 40 minutes

Ingredients

Main Dish

  • 1 package whole wheat English muffins
  • 1 carton egg whites
  • 24 slices deli turkey breast
  • 12 slices reduced-fat cheddar cheese
  • 1 jar black pepper
  • 1 bottle nonstick cooking spray
    • 

    Preparation Steps

    1. Preheat oven to 350°F. Lightly spray a 9x13 pan.
    2. Make egg slab: pour 4 cups egg whites into the pan, season with black pepper, and bake 18–20 minutes until set. Cool 5 minutes.
    3. Split and toast English muffins. Freeze remaining muffins if the pack contains more than 12.
    4. Ask deli for turkey breast thin sliced for sandwiches (not shaved).
    5. Cut the egg slab into 12 squares. Assemble 12 sandwiches: muffin + egg square + 2 slices turkey + 1 slice reduced-fat cheddar.
    6. Wrap each sandwich tightly and freeze in a labeled bag for up to 1 month.
    7. Breakfast use: thaw overnight in the fridge or microwave from frozen until hot; eat with your morning shake.

      Notes

      Portions (per person):


      Frank – 1 sandwich with your morning shake (slow-carb add-on).


      Susan – Optional: 1 sandwich with breakfast if desired.


      Elise – Optional: 1 sandwich with breakfast if desired.


      Tips (recipe-specific):


      To keep sodium down, pick lower-sodium deli turkey and reduced-fat cheese.


      If microwaving, wrap in a paper towel so the muffin stays soft, not rubbery.


      If lunch is late, add 1 piece of fruit with the sandwich and shake.

      • Susan: 230 kcal; 24 g protein; 24 g carbs; 6 g fat; 4 g fiber; 420 mg sodium
      • Elise: 230 kcal; 24 g protein; 24 g carbs; 6 g fat; 4 g fiber; 420 mg sodium
