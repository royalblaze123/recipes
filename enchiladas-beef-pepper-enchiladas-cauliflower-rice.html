Beef & Pepper Enchiladas + Cauliflower Rice

Nutrition (approx)

Frank: 630 kcal • P 58g • C 38g • F 22g
Susan/Elise (each): 472 kcal • P 44g • C 28g • F 16g

Ingredients (purchase-clean)

  • 1.50 lb 93% lean ground beef
  • 1 package whole-grain small tortillas
  • 1 can red enchilada sauce
  • 1/2 bag reduced-fat shredded Mexican cheese blend
  • 2 each bell peppers
  • 1 each yellow onion
  • 1 bag frozen cauliflower rice
  • 4 tsp extra-virgin olive oil
  • 2 tsp chili powder
  • 2 tsp cumin
  • 2 tsp garlic powder
  • 1 tsp smoked paprika
  • 1 tsp black pepper
  • 1 tsp kosher salt

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 400°F. Lightly coat a 9x13-inch baking dish with nonstick spray.
  2. Veg: dice bell peppers and yellow onion into 1/2-inch pieces.
  3. Cook beef: brown ground beef in a nonstick skillet over medium-high 7–9 minutes. Drain any excess liquid if needed.
  4. Cook peppers/onion: add diced peppers and onion to the beef. Cook 5–7 minutes until tender-crisp.
  5. Season: add chili powder, cumin, garlic powder, smoked paprika, black pepper, and kosher salt. Stir 60 seconds.
  6. Warm enchilada sauce: heat the can of red enchilada sauce over medium-low 4–5 minutes until steaming.
  7. Tortillas: warm tortillas 15–20 seconds per side in a dry skillet.
  8. Assembly: spread 1/2 cup sauce in the dish. Fill tortillas with beef-pepper mixture plus a pinch of cheese; roll and place seam-side down.
  9. Top: pour remaining sauce over enchiladas, sprinkle remaining cheese, and bake 14–18 minutes until bubbly.
  10. Cauliflower rice: microwave the frozen cauliflower rice per bag directions. Drain any excess moisture; season with a pinch of black pepper.
  11. Portion: Frank 4 enchiladas; Susan 3; Elise 3. Serve with cauliflower rice.

Notes

Beef-and-pepper enchiladas with a light cauliflower-rice side to keep the plate balanced. Portions (per person): Frank: 4 enchiladas (4 tortillas) + 1/3 of the side/veg Susan: 3 enchiladas (3 tortillas) + 1/4 of the side/veg Elise: 3 enchiladas (3 tortillas) + 1/4 of the side/veg
