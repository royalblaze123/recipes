Shrimp & Avocado Salad + Roasted Sweet Potato

Nutrition (approx)

Frank: 650 kcal • P 55g • C 55g • F 18g • Sat 3g • Na 620mg • Fiber 12g
Susan/Elise (each): 488 kcal • P 41g • C 41g • F 14g • Sat 2g • Na 465mg • Fiber 9g

Ingredients (purchase-clean)

  • 1.50 lb shrimp
  • 1 each avocado
  • 2.00 lb sweet potatoes
  • 1 head romaine lettuce
  • 1 each lime
  • kosher salt
  • black pepper
  • garlic powder
  • onion powder
  • smoked paprika
  • Italian seasoning
  • ground cumin
  • chili powder
  • red pepper flakes
  • extra-virgin olive oil
  • balsamic vinegar
  • apple cider vinegar
  • Dijon mustard

Steps

  1. Oven: heat to 425°F.
  2. Sweet potatoes: cube; toss with 2 tsp olive oil, smoked paprika, black pepper, modest kosher salt. Roast 22–26 minutes.
  3. Shrimp: sauté shrimp with 2 tsp olive oil, garlic powder, black pepper, and lime juice 2–3 minutes until pink.
  4. Dressing: whisk 2 tbsp lime juice, 2 tbsp apple cider vinegar, 2 tbsp olive oil, Dijon mustard, black pepper, and a small pinch of kosher salt.
  5. Salad: chop romaine; toss with dressing; top with shrimp, avocado slices, and sweet potatoes.
  6. Serve per Notes.

Notes

Shrimp salad that feels like dinner—avocado and sweet potato make it satisfying. Portions (per person): Frank: 4 cups salad + 6 oz shrimp + 1/3 avocado + 1 cup sweet potato Susan: 3 cups salad + 4 oz shrimp + 1/4 avocado + 3/4 cup sweet potato Elise: 3 cups salad + 4 oz shrimp + 1/4 avocado + 3/4 cup sweet potato Tips (recipe-specific): Roast sweet potatoes until browned for maximum flavor. Add avocado right before eating so it stays fresh.
