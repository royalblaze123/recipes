Baked Protein+ Ziti (Lean Turkey) + Big Veg

Nutrition (approx)

Frank: 650 kcal • P 58g • C 75g • F 16g
Susan/Elise (each): 488 kcal • P 44g • C 56g • F 12g

Ingredients (purchase-clean)

  • 1.50 lb 93% lean ground turkey
  • 1 box Barilla Protein+ pasta
  • 1 jar marinara sauce
  • 6 oz part-skim mozzarella cheese
  • 6 oz grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 bag frozen spinach
  • kosher salt
  • black pepper
  • garlic powder
  • onion powder
  • smoked paprika
  • Italian seasoning
  • ground cumin
  • chili powder
  • red pepper flakes
  • extra-virgin olive oil
  • low-sodium chicken broth
  • low-sodium beef broth
  • low-sodium soy sauce
  • hot sauce
  • Dijon mustard
  • balsamic vinegar
  • apple cider vinegar
  • cornstarch

Steps (oven)

  1. Oven: heat to 400°F.
  2. Pasta: cook Protein+ pasta 2 minutes less than al dente; drain.
  3. Turkey: brown with garlic powder, Italian seasoning, black pepper, modest salt (8–10 minutes).
  4. Spinach: microwave spinach; squeeze dry; stir into turkey.
  5. Assemble: mix pasta + marinara (2 1/2 cups measured) + turkey/spinach in baking dish. Top with measured mozzarella + Parmesan.
  6. Bake: 400°F 18–22 minutes until bubbly. Rest 10 minutes before serving.
  7. Serve per Notes.

Notes

Baked ziti comfort without heavy cheese: measured topping + spinach volume. Portions (per person): Frank: 2 1/2 cups baked ziti portion Susan: About 2 cups portion Elise: About 2 cups portion Tips (recipe-specific): Undercook pasta slightly so it doesn’t get soft in the bake. Squeeze spinach dry so the bake stays thick.
