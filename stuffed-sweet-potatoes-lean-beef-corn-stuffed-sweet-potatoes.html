Lean Beef & Corn Stuffed Sweet Potatoes

Nutrition (approx)

Frank: 650 kcal • P 58g • C 58g • F 18g
Susan/Elise (each): 488 kcal • P 44g • C 44g • F 14g

Ingredients (purchase-clean)

  • 2.25 lb sweet potatoes
  • 1.50 lb 93% lean ground beef
  • 1 can corn
  • 1 each avocado
  • 1/2 jar salsa
  • 4 tsp extra-virgin olive oil
  • 2 tsp chili powder
  • 2 tsp cumin
  • 2 tsp garlic powder
  • 1 tsp smoked paprika
  • 1 tsp black pepper
  • 1 tsp kosher salt

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 425°F. Line a sheet pan with parchment.
  2. Sweet potatoes: scrub and pierce each potato 6–8 times with a fork. Rub with 2 tsp olive oil and a pinch of kosher salt.
  3. Roast sweet potatoes 40–55 minutes until very tender (knife slides in easily).
  4. Beef: cook ground beef in a skillet with 2 tsp olive oil 7–9 minutes until browned. Stir in spices for 60 seconds.
  5. Corn: Can size note—use a standard 15 oz can corn.. Drain. Add 1 1/2 cups corn; refrigerate remainder.
  6. Combine: warm 2 minutes.
  7. Split: slice each potato lengthwise, fluff the insides with a fork, and create a well for the topping.
  8. Stuff: spoon beef-corn mix into potatoes.
  9. Avocado: halve, pit, and dice. Use about 1/2 avocado total; refrigerate remaining avocado with the pit to slow browning.
  10. Serve: top with salsa and measured avocado. Portion per Notes.

Notes

Lean beef + corn over roasted sweet potatoes; salsa and avocado keep it ‘Mexican bowl’ vibes. Portions (per person): Frank: 1 stuffed sweet potato (largest) + 1/3 of the topping mix + 1/3 of the avocado + salsa Susan: 1 stuffed sweet potato (medium) + 1/4 of the topping mix + 1/4 of the avocado + salsa Elise: 1 stuffed sweet potato (medium) + 1/4 of the topping mix + 1/4 of the avocado + salsa
