Instant Pot Chicken Tikka-Style Tomato + Rice (No Yogurt)

Nutrition (approx)

Frank: 650 kcal • P 60g • C 70g • F 14g
Susan/Elise (each): 488 kcal • P 45g • C 52g • F 10g

Ingredients (purchase-clean)

  • 1.75 lb boneless skinless chicken breast
  • 2 cans no-salt-added diced tomatoes
  • 1 each onion
  • 1 cup dry brown rice
  • low-sodium chicken broth
  • cornstarch
  • kosher salt
  • black pepper
  • garlic powder
  • onion powder
  • smoked paprika
  • Italian seasoning
  • ground cumin
  • chili powder
  • red pepper flakes
  • extra-virgin olive oil
  • low-sodium chicken broth
  • low-sodium beef broth
  • low-sodium soy sauce
  • hot sauce
  • Dijon mustard
  • balsamic vinegar
  • apple cider vinegar
  • cornstarch

Steps (Instant Pot)

  1. Rice side: cook brown rice on stovetop (35–40 minutes) or Instant Pot pot-in-pot (High 22 minutes; NR 10).
  2. Sauté: olive oil + diced onion 3–4 minutes. Add cumin, smoked paprika, chili powder, garlic powder; bloom 30 seconds.
  3. Load: add tomatoes (2x 14.5 oz) + broth (1 cup) + chicken.
  4. Pressure cook: High 10 minutes; NR 10, then Quick Release.
  5. Shred chicken. Sauté 5 minutes to reduce.
  6. Thicken: cornstarch slurry (1 tbsp + cold water) 2 minutes.
  7. Serve per Notes with rice.

Notes

Thick tomato-spice sauce without dairy; great as leftovers over rice. Portions (per person): Frank: 7 oz chicken + 1 cup cooked rice + 1 cup sauce/veg Susan: 5 oz protein + 1 cup cooked rice (smaller) + 1 cup sauce/veg (smaller) Elise: 5 oz protein + 1 cup cooked rice (smaller) + 1 cup sauce/veg (smaller) Tips (recipe-specific): Bloom spices on Sauté so you don’t need extra salt. Reduce after shredding so it stays saucy, not watery.
