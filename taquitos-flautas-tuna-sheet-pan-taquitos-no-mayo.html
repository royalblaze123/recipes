Tuna Sheet-Pan Taquitos (No Mayo)

Nutrition (approx)

Frank: 625 kcal • P 60g • C 48g • F 18g
Susan/Elise (each): 469 kcal • P 45g • C 36g • F 14g

Ingredients (purchase-clean)

  • 3 cans tuna
  • 1 package corn tortillas
  • 1 each avocado
  • 1/2 jar salsa
  • 1 bag spring mix
  • 1 each lime
  • 6 tsp extra-virgin olive oil
  • 1 bag reduced-fat shredded Mexican cheese blend
  • 2 tsp garlic powder
  • 1 tsp black pepper
  • 1 tsp kosher salt

Steps

  1. Tuna: Can size note—use a standard 5 oz can tuna.. Use three 5 oz cans tuna. Drain very well.
  2. Season tuna with garlic powder, black pepper, and kosher salt.
  3. Filling: mix tuna with 3/4 cup shredded cheese. Cool 5 minutes.
  4. Tortillas: warm corn tortillas by microwaving under a damp paper towel for 25–35 seconds so they roll without cracking.
  5. Assemble taquitos: 2 tbsp filling per tortilla; roll tight; seam-side down on sheet pan.
  6. Preheat oven to 425°F. Line a sheet pan with parchment.
  7. Brush taquitos lightly with oil and bake 14–18 minutes, turning once, until crisp and browned.
  8. Side salad: add spring mix to a bowl. Drizzle with 2 tsp lime juice and 2 tsp olive oil; toss.
  9. Avocado: halve, pit, and dice. Use about 1/2 avocado total; refrigerate remaining avocado with the pit to slow browning.
  10. Serve with salsa and portion per Notes.

Notes

Pantry taquitos that still hit: tuna + measured cheese, baked crisp, served with salsa and avocado. Portions (per person): Frank: 6 taquitos + 1/3 of the side + 1/3 of the avocado + salsa Susan: 4 taquitos + 1/4 of the side + 1/4 of the avocado + salsa Elise: 4 taquitos + 1/4 of the side + 1/4 of the avocado + salsa
