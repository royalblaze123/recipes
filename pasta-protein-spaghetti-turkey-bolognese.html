Protein+ Spaghetti Turkey Bolognese

Nutrition (approx)

Frank: 779 kcal • P 58g • C 71g • F 32g
Susan/Elise (each): 584 kcal • P 44g • C 53g • F 24g

Ingredients (purchase-clean)

  • 1/3 box Barilla Protein+ spaghetti
  • 1.25 lb 93% lean ground turkey
  • 0.62 jar no-sugar-added marinara sauce
  • 4 tsp extra-virgin olive oil
  • 1 each yellow onion
  • 8 oz fresh mushrooms
  • 2 tbsp grated parmesan
  • 2 tsp garlic powder
  • 2 tsp Italian seasoning
  • 1 tsp black pepper
  • 1 tsp kosher salt

Steps

  1. Bring a large pot of water to a boil for the Barilla Protein+ spaghetti. (Use enough water to fully cover pasta; do not add water to Ingredients.)
  2. Heat a large nonstick skillet over medium-high. Add 4 tsp extra-virgin olive oil if listed. Add ground turkey and cook 7–9 minutes, breaking it up, until browned and cooked through.
  3. Dice the yellow onion. Add to the skillet and cook 4–6 minutes until softened.
  4. Chop the mushrooms. Cook 6–8 minutes until moisture cooks off and mushrooms brown.
  5. Jar size note: assume a standard 24 oz jar marinara. Measure out what you need for this recipe; refrigerate the remainder.
  6. Add marinara to the skillet and simmer 5–7 minutes until hot and slightly thickened.
  7. Cook Barilla Protein+ spaghetti to al dente per box time. Reserve 1 cup pasta water, then drain.
  8. Toss pasta into the sauce/skillet. Add reserved pasta water 1–2 tbsp at a time until the sauce clings well.
  9. Portion into bowls using the Portions guidance in Notes.

Notes

Rich turkey marinara with browned mushrooms and onions. Built on Barilla Protein+ for a higher-protein pasta plate with close-approx nutrition. Portions (per person): Frank: 2 oz dry Barilla Protein+ spaghetti + 1/3 of the skillet/sauce Susan: 1.5 oz dry Barilla Protein+ spaghetti + 1/4 of the skillet/sauce Elise: 1.5 oz dry Barilla Protein+ spaghetti + 1/4 of the skillet/sauce
