Low-Sodium Teriyaki-Style Glaze

Category: Sauce • Yield: About 1/2 cup (6 tbsp) • AnyList-ready

Ingredients

  • 1/3 cup low-sodium tamari
  • 2 tsp rice vinegar
  • 1 tsp sesame oil
  • 1 tsp fresh ginger
  • 1 clove garlic
  • 1/2 tsp cornstarch

Steps

  1. Grate fresh ginger to measure 1 tsp. Mince 1 clove garlic.
  2. In a small pot, whisk tamari, rice vinegar, sesame oil, ginger, and garlic.
  3. In a cup, whisk cornstarch with 2 tsp water until smooth.
  4. Bring pot to a gentle simmer. Whisk in slurry and cook 60–90 seconds until glossy. Cool before using.
