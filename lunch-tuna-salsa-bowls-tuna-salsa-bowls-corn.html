Tuna Salsa Bowls + Corn

Nutrition (approx)

Frank: 525 kcal • P 50g • C 50g • F 14g
Susan/Elise (each): 394 kcal • P 38g • C 38g • F 10g

Ingredients (purchase-clean)

  • 3 cans tuna
  • 1 bag spring mix
  • 1 each avocado
  • 1/2 jar salsa
  • 1 package whole-wheat tortillas
  • 1 each lime
  • 4 tsp extra-virgin olive oil
  • 1 tsp black pepper
  • 1 tsp kosher salt
  • 1 can corn
  • 1 tsp smoked paprika

Steps

  1. Tuna: Can size note—use a standard 5 oz can tuna.. Use three 5 oz cans tuna. Drain very well.
  2. Tuna mix: in a bowl, combine drained tuna with 1/2 cup salsa, 2 tsp lime juice, 2 tsp olive oil, black pepper, and kosher salt.
  3. Corn: Can size note—use a standard 15 oz can corn.. Drain. Stir 1 1/2 cups corn into the tuna mix along with 1 tsp smoked paprika; refrigerate remainder.
  4. Greens: divide spring mix into three bowls.
  5. Top: spoon tuna salsa mixture over the greens.
  6. Avocado: halve, pit, and dice. Use about 1/2 avocado total; refrigerate remaining avocado with the pit.
  7. Finish: add measured avocado on each bowl and add an extra spoon of salsa if desired.
  8. Tortillas: warm tortillas 15 seconds. Serve 1 tortilla on the side (measured) for scooping. Portion per Notes.

Notes

Tuna salsa bowls with a little sweet corn for balance, still portion-controlled. Portions (per person): Frank: 1 large tuna salsa bowl + 1 tortilla on the side + 1/3 avocado Susan: 1 medium tuna salsa bowl + 3/4 tortilla on the side + 1/4 avocado Elise: 1 medium tuna salsa bowl + 3/4 tortilla on the side + 1/4 avocado
