Citrus Herb Cod with Potatoes & Broccoli (RBM Dinner)

Mild baked cod with lemon-herb seasoning, served alongside roasted potatoes and broccoli.

Servings

3 servings.

Times

  • Prep time: 15m
  • Cook time: 25m
  • Total time: 40m

Ingredients

  • 18 oz cod fillets
  • 18 oz baby potatoes
  • 1 head broccoli
  • 1 1/2 tbsp extra-virgin olive oil
  • 1 medium lemon
  • 1 tsp garlic powder
  • 1 tsp dried parsley
  • 1/4 tsp kosher
  • 1/2 tsp ground black pepper

Step-by-Step Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 400°F (205°C). Line a large rimmed sheet pan with parchment paper.
  2. In a small bowl, combine the olive oil, lemon zest, half of the lemon juice, garlic powder, dried parsley (or Italian seasoning), salt, and pepper.
  3. Add the halved baby potatoes to a large bowl. Spoon about one-third of the citrus herb mixture over the potatoes and toss to coat. Spread the potatoes over one-third of the sheet pan in a single layer, cut sides down.
  4. Add the fresh broccoli florets to the same bowl. Spoon another third of the citrus herb mixture over the broccoli and toss to coat. Spread the broccoli over another third of the sheet pan.
  5. Place the cod fillets on the remaining third of the sheet pan. Brush or spoon the remaining citrus herb mixture over the cod pieces.
  6. Bake for 15–20 minutes, depending on thickness of the cod, until the fish flakes easily with a fork and reaches 145°F (63°C), and the potatoes are fork-tender (stir potatoes and broccoli once halfway through).
  7. If desired, squeeze the remaining lemon juice over the cod and vegetables right after baking for a fresh finish.
  8. Turn off the oven. Divide the cod, potatoes, and broccoli into portions as listed below and serve hot.

Portion Sizes (Cooked Approx.)

  • Frank (F · 1.0×): ~7 oz cooked cod, ~7 oz potatoes, ~1 1/2 cups broccoli
  • Susan (S · 0.75×): ~5 oz cooked cod, ~5 oz potatoes, ~1 cup broccoli
  • Elise (E · 0.5×): ~3.5 oz cooked cod, ~3.5 oz potatoes, ~3/4 cup broccoli

  • Susan: 460 kcal, 42 g protein, 26 g carbs, 14 g fat, 4 g fiber, 400 mg sodium
