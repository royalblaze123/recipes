BBQ Chicken + Roasted Broccoli + Sweet Potatoes (Measured Glaze)

Nutrition (approx)

Frank: 650 kcal • P 60g • C 55g • F 18g • Sat 3g • Na 700mg • Fiber 10g
Susan/Elise (each): 488 kcal • P 45g • C 41g • F 14g • Sat 2g • Na 525mg • Fiber 8g

Ingredients (purchase-clean)

  • 1.75 lb boneless skinless chicken breast
  • 2.00 lb sweet potatoes
  • 1 bag frozen broccoli florets
  • low-sugar BBQ sauce
  • kosher salt
  • black pepper
  • garlic powder
  • onion powder
  • smoked paprika
  • Italian seasoning
  • ground cumin
  • chili powder
  • red pepper flakes
  • extra-virgin olive oil
  • Dijon mustard
  • balsamic vinegar
  • apple cider vinegar
  • low-sodium soy sauce
  • cornstarch
  • hot sauce

Steps

  1. Oven: heat to 425°F. Line a sheet pan.
  2. Sweet potatoes: cube. Toss with 2 tsp olive oil, smoked paprika, garlic powder, 1/2 tsp kosher salt, black pepper. Roast 12 minutes.
  3. Chicken: season with garlic powder, smoked paprika, 1/2 tsp kosher salt, black pepper. Add to pan and roast 10 minutes.
  4. BBQ glaze: brush chicken with 3 tbsp BBQ sauce total (measured). Return pan to oven 3 minutes.
  5. Broccoli: microwave 4–5 minutes; drain and pat dry. Toss with 2 tsp olive oil, 1/2 tsp kosher salt, black pepper, garlic powder. Add to pan; roast 10 minutes to brown edges.
  6. Serve per Notes.

Notes

BBQ flavor without the calorie hit: glaze is measured and baked on. Portions (per person): Frank: 7 oz chicken + 1 cup sweet potatoes + 1 1/2 cups broccoli Susan: 5 oz protein + 3/4 cup starch + 1 cup veg Elise: 5 oz protein + 3/4 cup starch + 1 cup veg Tips (recipe-specific): Pat broccoli dry before roasting so it caramelizes. Glaze at the end so you use less sauce.
