Smoky Pork Tenderloin with Sweet Potatoes & Green Beans (RBM Dinner)

Roasted pork tenderloin with smoky seasoning, paired with roasted sweet potatoes and green beans for a lean, filling dinner.

Servings

3 servings (Frank, Susan, Elise).

Times

  • Prep time: 20m
  • Cook time: 30m
  • Total time: 50m

Ingredients

  • 18 oz pork tenderloin, trimmed of visible fat
  • 18 oz sweet potatoes, peeled and cut into 3/4-inch cubes
  • 12 oz fresh green beans, trimmed
  • 1 1/2 tbsp extra-virgin olive oil
  • 1 tsp smoked paprika
  • 1 tsp garlic powder
  • 1/2 tsp dried oregano
  • 1/4 tsp kosher or sea salt (or less, to taste)
  • 1/2 tsp ground black pepper

Step-by-Step Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 400°F (205°C). Line a large rimmed sheet pan with parchment paper.
  2. In a small bowl, mix together the smoked paprika, garlic powder, dried oregano, salt, and pepper.
  3. Place the trimmed pork tenderloin on the center of the sheet pan. Drizzle with about 2 teaspoons of the olive oil and rub with half of the spice mixture until evenly coated.
  4. Add the cubed sweet potatoes to a large bowl. Drizzle with about 2 teaspoons olive oil and sprinkle with half of the remaining spice mixture. Toss to coat, then spread the sweet potatoes on one side of the sheet pan in a single layer.
  5. Add the fresh green beans to the same bowl. Drizzle with the remaining olive oil and sprinkle with the remaining spice mixture. Toss to coat, then spread the green beans on the other side of the sheet pan.
  6. Roast for 25–30 minutes, flipping the pork tenderloin once halfway through and stirring the sweet potatoes and green beans, until the pork reaches 145°F (63°C) in the thickest part and the sweet potatoes are tender.
  7. Transfer the pork to a cutting board and let rest for 5 minutes, then slice into 1/2-inch medallions.
  8. Divide the sliced pork, roasted sweet potatoes, and green beans into portions as listed below and serve.

Portion Sizes (Cooked Approx.)

  • Frank (F · 1.0×): ~7 oz cooked pork tenderloin, ~7 oz sweet potatoes, ~5 oz green beans
  • Susan (S · 0.75×): ~5 oz cooked pork tenderloin, ~5 oz sweet potatoes, ~4 oz green beans
  • Elise (E · 0.5×): ~3.5 oz cooked pork tenderloin, ~3.5 oz sweet potatoes, ~3 oz green beans

Nutrition (Per Person)

  • Frank: 630 kcal, 55 g protein, 45 g carbs, 18 g fat, 6 g fiber, 570 mg sodium
  • Susan: 470 kcal, 41 g protein, 34 g carbs, 14 g fat, 4.5 g fiber, 430 mg sodium
  • Elise: 315 kcal, 28 g protein, 22 g carbs, 9 g fat, 3 g fiber, 285 mg sodium
