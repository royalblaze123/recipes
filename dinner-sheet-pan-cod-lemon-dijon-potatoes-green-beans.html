Cod + Lemon-Dijon Potatoes + Green Beans

Nutrition (approx)

Frank: 640 kcal • P 55g • C 55g • F 18g • Sat 3g • Na 700mg • Fiber 10g
Susan/Elise (each): 480 kcal • P 41g • C 41g • F 14g • Sat 2g • Na 525mg • Fiber 8g

Ingredients (purchase-clean)

  • 1.50 lb cod fillets
  • 1.50 lb baby potatoes
  • 1 bag frozen green beans
  • 1 each lemon
  • kosher salt
  • black pepper
  • garlic powder
  • onion powder
  • smoked paprika
  • Italian seasoning
  • ground cumin
  • chili powder
  • red pepper flakes
  • extra-virgin olive oil
  • Dijon mustard
  • balsamic vinegar
  • apple cider vinegar
  • low-sodium soy sauce
  • cornstarch
  • hot sauce

Steps

  1. Oven: heat to 425°F. Line sheet pan.
  2. Potatoes: halve; toss with 2 tsp olive oil, 2 tsp Dijon mustard, 2 tsp lemon juice, garlic powder, 1/2 tsp kosher salt, black pepper. Roast 22 minutes.
  3. Green beans: microwave; drain. Toss with 2 tsp olive oil, garlic powder, 1/2 tsp kosher salt, black pepper. Add to pan; roast 10 minutes.
  4. Cod: pat dry; season with black pepper, garlic powder, 1/2 tsp kosher salt, and 2 tsp lemon juice.
  5. Add cod for last 10–12 minutes until flaky.
  6. Serve per Notes.

Notes

Bright, non-boring cod: lemon + Dijon keeps it moist and flavorful. Portions (per person): Frank: 6 oz cod + 1 cup potatoes + 1 1/2 cups green beans Susan: 5 oz protein + 3/4 cup starch + 1 cup veg Elise: 5 oz protein + 3/4 cup starch + 1 cup veg Tips (recipe-specific): Add cod late so it doesn’t dry out. Toss potatoes with Dijon + lemon so they taste ‘sauced’.
