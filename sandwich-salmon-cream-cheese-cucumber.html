Salmon Cream Cheese Cucumber Sandwich

Servings

3 sandwiches (Frank 1.0×, Susan 0.75×, Elise 0.75×).

Times

  • Prep time: 14 minutes
  • Cook time: 0 minutes
  • Total time: 14 minutes

Ingredients

  • 6 slices whole wheat bread
  • 2 cans salmon
  • 20 tsp light cream cheese
  • 1/2 avocado
  • 1 cucumber
  • 1 lemon

Recipe

  1. Open 2 cans salmon and drain well. Flake into a bowl.
  2. Add to the bowl: 20 tsp light cream cheese + juice of 1 lemon; mix into a spread.
  3. Slice 1 cucumber into thin rounds.
  4. Slice 1 avocado; use 1/2 of it for this recipe. Portion avocado: Frank 1/4 avocado; Susan 1/8 avocado; Elise 1/8 avocado. Store the remaining 1/2 avocado tightly wrapped.
  5. Frank sandwich: use 2 bread slices; spread about 1/2 of the salmon mixture + add cucumber rounds + 1/4 avocado slices; close and wrap.
  6. Susan sandwich: use 2 bread slices; spread about 1/4 of the salmon mixture + add cucumber rounds + 1/8 avocado slices; close and wrap.
  7. Elise sandwich: use 2 bread slices; spread about 1/4 of the salmon mixture + add cucumber rounds + 1/8 avocado slices; close and wrap.

Notes

Portions (per person):

Frank – 1 sandwich (about 1/2 of salmon spread, 1/4 avocado).

Susan – 1 sandwich (about 1/4 of salmon spread, 1/8 avocado).

Elise – 1 sandwich (about 1/4 of salmon spread, 1/8 avocado).

Tips (recipe-specific):

If packing overnight, keep cucumber in the center of the filling so the bread edges stay dry.

Drain salmon thoroughly to prevent a watery spread.

  • Susan: 367 kcal; Protein 20 g; Carbs 33 g; Fat 18 g; Fiber 8 g; Sodium 552 mg
