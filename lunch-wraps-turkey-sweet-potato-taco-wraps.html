Turkey & Sweet Potato Taco Wraps

Nutrition (approx)

Frank: 545 kcal • P 48g • C 56g • F 15g
Susan/Elise (each): 409 kcal • P 36g • C 42g • F 11g

Ingredients (purchase-clean)

  • 1.25 lb 93% lean ground turkey
  • 1.25 lb sweet potatoes
  • 1 package large whole-wheat tortillas
  • 1 bag shredded lettuce
  • 1 each avocado
  • 1/2 jar salsa
  • 1 each cucumber
  • 1 bag mini sweet peppers
  • 1 tub plain nonfat Greek yogurt
  • 1 each lime
  • 2 tsp chili powder
  • 2 tsp cumin
  • 2 tsp garlic powder
  • 1 tsp smoked paprika
  • 1 tsp black pepper
  • 1 tsp kosher salt

Steps

  1. Sweet potatoes: small dice. Toss with 2 tsp olive oil and roast at 425°F for 18–22 minutes until browned and tender.
  2. Turkey: cook ground turkey 7–9 minutes until browned. Season with spices.
  3. Combine: fold roasted sweet potato into the turkey and warm 1 minute.
  4. Sauce: mix 1/2 cup Greek yogurt with 2 tsp lime juice and 1 tsp smoked paprika.
  5. Wrap build: warm tortillas 15 seconds so they roll easily. Spread the sauce on each tortilla. Add the protein, then add shredded lettuce or slaw mix if listed.
  6. Roll: fold in the sides and roll tightly. Slice in half for serving if desired.
  7. Crunchy side: slice cucumber and sweet peppers into sticks. Sprinkle with black pepper. Serve alongside the wrap.
  8. Avocado: halve, pit, and slice. Use about 1/2 avocado total; refrigerate remaining avocado with the pit to slow browning.
  9. Serve with salsa and portion per Notes.

Notes

Turkey + roasted sweet potato taco wraps with yogurt-lime sauce and crunchy sides. Portions (per person): Frank: 1 large wrap + 1/3 of the crunchy veg side + salsa + avocado Susan: 3/4 wrap + 1/4 of the crunchy veg side + salsa + avocado Elise: 3/4 wrap + 1/4 of the crunchy veg side + salsa + avocado
