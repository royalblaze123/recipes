Chicken Brown Rice Bowl

High-protein chicken and brown rice bowl with broccoli, designed as a grab-and-go microwaveable lunch.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb chicken breast, cooked and diced
  • 2 cups cooked brown rice
  • 1 cup steamed broccoli florets
  • 1 tbsp extra-virgin olive oil
  • 1 tsp garlic powder
  • 1/2 tsp paprika
  • 1/2 tsp kosher salt
  • 1/4 tsp black pepper

Instructions

  1. Cook the chicken breast until done, then dice into bite-size pieces.
  2. Steam the broccoli florets until crisp-tender.
  3. Reheat or cook the brown rice.
  4. Combine chicken, rice, and broccoli.
  5. Drizzle with olive oil and seasonings.
  6. Divide into 3 microwave-safe containers and reheat 60â€“90 seconds.

Serves: 3 servings
