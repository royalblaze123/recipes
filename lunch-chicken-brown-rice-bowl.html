Chicken Brown Rice Bowl

Notes

  • Grab-and-go, microwaveable high-protein bowl.
  • Serves 3 (Frank, Susan, Elise) using household ratios: F=1.0×, S=0.75×, E=0.75×.
  • Portions (approx): F ≈ 40% of total, S ≈ 30%, E ≈ 30%.
  • Approx nutrition per generic serving: ~480 kcal, ~42 g protein, ~40 g carbs, ~15 g fat.
  • Portion mapping: Total yield split F≈40%, S≈30%, E≈30% by volume.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb chicken breast, cooked and diced
  • 2 cups cooked brown rice
  • 1 cup steamed broccoli florets
  • 1 tbsp extra-virgin olive oil
  • 1 tsp garlic powder
  • 1/2 tsp paprika
  • 1/2 tsp kosher salt (or to taste)
  • 1/4 tsp black pepper

Instructions

  1. Cook the chicken breast until fully done, then dice into bite-size pieces.
  2. Steam the broccoli florets until crisp-tender.
  3. Cook or reheat the brown rice according to package directions.
  4. In a large bowl, combine chicken, brown rice, and broccoli.
  5. Drizzle with olive oil, then sprinkle with garlic powder, paprika, salt, and pepper. Toss to coat evenly.
  6. Divide into 3 microwave-safe containers. Reheat each portion in the microwave for 60–90 seconds before eating.
