Creamy Tomato Basil Sauce

Category: Sauce • Yield: About 1 3/4 cups (28 tbsp) • AnyList-ready

Ingredients

  • 1 can no-salt-added tomato sauce
  • 3/4 cup 2% plain Greek yogurt
  • 2 tsp extra-virgin olive oil
  • 2 tsp dried basil
  • 1 tsp garlic powder
  • 1/4 tsp black pepper
  • 1 tsp cornstarch

Steps

  1. Open 1 can of no-salt-added tomato sauce. Use a 15 oz can.
  2. In a small saucepan over medium heat, warm olive oil. Add tomato sauce, dried basil, garlic powder, and black pepper; bring to a gentle simmer.
  3. Whisk cornstarch with 2 tsp water until smooth; whisk into the sauce and simmer 60–90 seconds.
  4. Remove from heat and cool 2 minutes. Whisk in Greek yogurt until smooth. Do not boil after adding yogurt.
