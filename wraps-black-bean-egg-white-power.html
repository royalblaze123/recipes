Black Bean & Egg White Power Wrap

High-protein black bean and egg white wrap with veggies and a light yogurt salsa sauce for a meat-light, full lunch option.

Servings

3 servings.

Times

  • Prep time: 15 minutes
  • Cook time: 5 minutes
  • Total time: 20 minutes

Ingredients

  • 1 large high-fiber whole-wheat tortilla
  • 2 medium high-fiber whole-wheat tortillas
  • 1 cup liquid egg whites
  • 3/4 cup canned black beans
  • 6 tbsp 2% plain Greek yogurt
  • 2 tbsp mild salsa
  • 1 tsp extra-virgin olive oil
  • 1/2 tsp ground cumin
  • 1/2 tsp smoked paprika or sweet paprika
  • 1/4 tsp garlic powder
  • 1/4 tsp ground black pepper
  • 1/8 to 1/4 tsp kosher or sea salt
  • 1 1/2 cups shredded romaine lettuce
  • 1/2 cup diced red bell pepper
  • 1/4 cup thinly sliced green onion

Preparation Steps

  1. Rinse and pat dry the romaine and bell pepper. Shred the romaine, dice the bell pepper, and thinly slice the green onion. Set aside.
  2. Lightly coat a nonstick skillet with the olive oil and warm over medium heat. Add the liquid egg whites and cook, stirring gently, until just set and still tender. Sprinkle with a pinch of salt and pepper. Remove from heat and let cool slightly.
  3. In a small bowl, combine the drained, rinsed black beans with the ground cumin, smoked paprika, garlic powder, and a pinch of salt and pepper. Microwave for 20–30 seconds or warm briefly in the same skillet used for the eggs until just heated through.
  4. In another small bowl, stir together the Greek yogurt and mild salsa until smooth. Taste and adjust with a little extra salsa if you want more flavor while keeping heat and acidity modest.
  5. Gently warm each tortilla in a dry skillet over low heat for 15–20 seconds per side, or in the microwave for about 10 seconds, until flexible.
  6. Place the large (10-inch) tortilla on a cutting board. Spread about 2 tbsp yogurt salsa sauce down the center. Add about 1/2 cup warm black beans and about 3/4 cup cooked egg whites. Top with a generous handful of shredded romaine, diced bell pepper, and some sliced green onion.
  7. Fold the sides inward, then roll up tightly from the bottom into a burrito-style wrap. Wrap in foil or parchment and slice in half if desired.
  8. Place one medium (8-inch) tortilla on the board. Spread about 2 tbsp yogurt salsa down the center. Add about 1/3 cup black beans, about 1/2 cup egg whites, a moderate handful of romaine, bell pepper, and green onion.
  9. Fold in the sides and roll firmly from the bottom. Wrap in foil or parchment and slice in half if desired.
  10. Repeat the process with the remaining medium (8-inch) tortilla, using about 1/3 cup beans, 1/2 cup egg whites, romaine, bell pepper, and green onion plus about 2 tbsp sauce.
  11. Serve immediately or chill the wrapped sandwiches for up to 24 hours. Keep tightly wrapped in foil or parchment for easy grab-and-go lunches.
