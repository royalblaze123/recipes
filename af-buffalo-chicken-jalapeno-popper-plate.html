Buffalo Chicken Stuffed Jalapeño Popper Plate (Air Fryer)

Dinner • Air Fryer • Cook time: 25 min

Ingredients (purchase-clean)

  • 12 each fresh jalapeños
  • 24 oz fresh chicken breast
  • 12 oz 2% plain Greek yogurt
  • 3 oz part-skim shredded mozzarella
  • 1.32 lb fresh broccoli florets
  • hot sauce
  • garlic powder
  • onion powder
  • smoked paprika
  • black pepper
  • kosher salt
  • extra-virgin olive oil

Steps

  1. Heat air fryer to 370°F. Line basket with perforated parchment if you have it (optional).
  2. Broccoli side: Toss 1.32 lb broccoli with 3 tsp extra-virgin olive oil, 1 tsp garlic powder, 1 tsp smoked paprika, 1/2 tsp kosher salt, and 1/2 tsp black pepper. Air-fry at 370°F for 9–11 minutes, shaking at 5 minutes. Transfer to a plate.
  3. Chicken: Cut 24 oz chicken breast into 1-inch pieces. Season with 1 tsp kosher salt, 1 tsp garlic powder, 1 tsp onion powder, and 1/2 tsp black pepper. Air-fry at 370°F for 10–12 minutes, shaking once, until the thickest pieces reach 165°F.
  4. Jalapeños: Slice 12 jalapeños lengthwise to make 24 halves. Remove seeds and ribs (leave a little rib for heat if you want).
  5. Buffalo filling: In a bowl, stir together 12 oz Greek yogurt + 6 tbsp (18 tsp) hot sauce + 1 tsp garlic powder + 1/2 tsp smoked paprika. Fold in the cooked chicken pieces and 2 oz mozzarella.
  6. Stuff: Pack the filling into the 24 jalapeño halves. Sprinkle the remaining 1 oz mozzarella across the tops (lightly, not buried).
  7. Cook poppers: Air-fry stuffed jalapeños at 370°F for 7–9 minutes until the peppers are tender-crisp and the filling is hot. Serve immediately with the broccoli.

Notes

Air Fryer dinner built to be high-protein and satisfying. Portions (per person): Frank: 12 jalapeño halves (6 whole jalapeños) + 8 oz broccoli Susan: 9 jalapeño halves (4–5 jalapeños) + 6.5 oz broccoli Elise: 9 jalapeño halves (4–5 jalapeños) + 6.5 oz broccoli Tips (recipe-specific): For less heat, remove all ribs/seeds and rinse the pepper halves under cold water, then pat dry. If you want extra crunch, broil the poppers 1–2 minutes after air frying (oven broiler) until lightly browned.
