Ginger-Scallion Sauce

Category: Sauce • Yield: About 1/4 cup (3–4 tbsp) • AnyList-ready

Ingredients

  • 2 tbsp scallions
  • 2 tsp fresh ginger
  • 2 tsp rice vinegar
  • 2 tsp extra-virgin olive oil
  • 1/4 tsp black pepper

Steps

  1. Slice scallions finely to measure 2 tbsp.
  2. Grate fresh ginger to measure 2 tsp.
  3. Stir scallions, ginger, rice vinegar, olive oil, and black pepper.
  4. Optional: add 1–2 tbsp water to mellow and make it more spoonable.
