Air Fryer Sesame Salmon with Sautéed Snap Peas and Lime-Black Pepper Edamame

Prep: 10 min   Cook: 18 min   Total: 28 min

Ingredients

  • 1 package salmon fillets (~1–1.25 lb total)
  • 1 bag frozen sugar snap peas
  • 1 bag frozen shelled edamame
  • 1 lime
  • sesame seeds (tsp) — pantry
  • low-sodium soy sauce (tsp) — pantry
  • sesame oil (tsp) — pantry
  • olive oil (tsp) — pantry
  • garlic powder (tsp) — pantry
  • black pepper (tsp) — pantry
  • salt substitute (tsp) — pantry

Ingredient list is purchase-clean (AnyList aggregation). All measuring/used amounts and remainder handling are in Steps.

Step-by-step Instructions

  1. Measure soy sauce: use 8 tsp total. Mix 8 tsp soy sauce + 4 tsp sesame oil + 1 tsp black pepper. Brush over salmon (reserve 2 tsp of the mixture for finishing).
  2. Apply sesame crust: measure sesame seeds 8 tsp. Press seeds onto the top of each salmon piece (the brushed side).
  3. Cook snap peas: in a skillet over medium-high, add 4 tsp olive oil. Add frozen snap peas and cook 6–8 minutes, stirring, until crisp-tender and lightly blistered. Season with 2 tsp salt substitute and 1 tsp garlic powder.
  4. Cook edamame: microwave per package directions. After heating, season with lime juice 4 tsp and 1 tsp black pepper.
  5. Air fry salmon (Cosori): preheat 390°F for 3 minutes. Cook salmon skin-side down 8–11 minutes. The sesame top should toast.
  6. Finish: drizzle the reserved 2 tsp soy-sesame mixture over Frank’s plate; Susan and Elise get 1 tsp each.
