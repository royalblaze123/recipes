Air Fryer Sesame Salmon with Sautéed Snap Peas and Lime-Black Pepper Edamame

Prep: 10 min   Cook: 18 min   Total: 28 min

Ingredients

  • 1.25 lb salmon fillets
  • 1 bag frozen sugar snap peas
  • 1 bag frozen shelled edamame
  • 1 lime
  • sesame seeds
  • low-sodium soy sauce
  • sesame oil
  • olive oil
  • garlic powder
  • black pepper
  • salt substitute

RBM rules: quantities appear before items; fresh proteins use weight. Condiments/spices/seasonings are listed name-only; all measurements are in Steps.

Step-by-step Instructions

  1. Mix glaze: whisk 8 tsp low-sodium soy sauce + 4 tsp sesame oil + 1 tsp black pepper. Brush onto salmon; reserve 2 tsp of the mixture for finishing.
  2. Press sesame crust: use 8 tsp sesame seeds; press onto the top of each salmon piece (the brushed side).
  3. Cook snap peas: in a skillet over medium-high, add 4 tsp olive oil. Add frozen snap peas; cook 6–8 minutes until crisp-tender and lightly blistered. Season with 2 tsp salt substitute + 1 tsp garlic powder.
  4. Cook edamame: microwave per package directions. Season with 4 tsp lime juice + 1 tsp black pepper.
  5. Air fry salmon (Cosori): preheat 390°F for 3 minutes. Cook skin-side down 8–11 minutes; sesame top should toast.
  6. Finish and portion: drizzle reserved soy-sesame mixture on plates (Frank 2 tsp; Susan 1 tsp; Elise 1 tsp). Frank gets ~1 1/2 cups snap peas + ~3/4 cup edamame; Susan and Elise each get ~1 1/4 cups snap peas + ~1/2 cup edamame.
