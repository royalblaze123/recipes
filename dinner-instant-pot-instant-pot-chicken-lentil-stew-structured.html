Instant Pot Chicken + Lentil Stew (Structured)

Nutrition (approx)

Frank: 640 kcal • P 58g • C 55g • F 14g
Susan/Elise (each): 480 kcal • P 44g • C 41g • F 10g

Ingredients (purchase-clean)

  • 1.75 lb boneless skinless chicken breast
  • 1 cup dry lentils
  • 1 lb carrots
  • 2 stalks celery
  • 1 each onion
  • low-sodium chicken broth
  • kosher salt
  • black pepper
  • garlic powder
  • onion powder
  • smoked paprika
  • Italian seasoning
  • ground cumin
  • chili powder
  • red pepper flakes
  • extra-virgin olive oil
  • low-sodium chicken broth
  • low-sodium beef broth
  • low-sodium soy sauce
  • hot sauce
  • Dijon mustard
  • balsamic vinegar
  • apple cider vinegar
  • cornstarch

Steps (Instant Pot)

  1. Sauté: olive oil + onion 3 minutes. Add carrots/celery 2 minutes.
  2. Load: add lentils (1 cup dry) + broth (6 cups) + chicken. Season garlic powder, smoked paprika, black pepper, modest salt.
  3. Pressure cook: High 12 minutes; Natural Release 10 minutes, then Quick Release.
  4. Texture: shred chicken lightly into larger pieces (don’t over-shred).
  5. Reduce: Sauté 5 minutes if you want thicker body.
  6. Serve per Notes.

Notes

Stew that stays structured—lentils hold up and chicken stays in pieces. Portions (per person): Frank: About 2 1/2 cups (heaping bowl) Susan: About 2 cups (bowl) Elise: About 2 cups (bowl) Tips (recipe-specific): Natural release keeps lentils intact. Shred lightly for better texture.
