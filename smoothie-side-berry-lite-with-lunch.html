Berry Lite With-Lunch Smoothie

NOTES: Designed as a lighter, with-lunch smoothie to sit beside your main meal—extra hydration, fiber, and modest protein without blowing calorie targets. All use water/unsweetened almond milk base and whole fruits/veggies only, no added sugar. PORTION SIZES (serves 3): Blend one batch, then divide into three small glasses or bottles. Frank receives the largest side portion (≈1/3 of the total volume); Susan and Elise receive slightly smaller portions (≈1/4 of the total volume each). Frank = 1.0×; Susan = 0.75×; Elise = 0.75× portions.

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 cups cold water
  • 1/2 cup unsweetened almond milk
  • 1 cup mixed fruit or vegetables
  • 1 tbsp ground flaxseed or chia seeds
  • Ice cubes as needed

Step-by-Step Instructions

  1. Add 1 1/2 cups cold water, 1/2 cup unsweetened almond milk, 1 cup mixed fruit or vegetables, 1 tbsp ground flaxseed or chia seeds, and Ice cubes as needed to the blender, placing the cold water and unsweetened almond milk in first so the blades catch easily.
  2. Blend on low at first, then increase to high and blend for about 30–45 seconds until the drink is smooth and lightly frothy.
  3. Adjust the consistency with a splash of extra water for a thinner sipper or a few more ice cubes for a thicker texture. Keep the drink unsweetened; only add a small amount of non-nutritive sweetener if truly necessary.
  4. Pour into three small glasses or bottles. Give Frank the largest side portion and divide the remaining smoothie between Susan and Elise. Serve alongside lunch and refrigerate any leftovers, shaking before drinking.

Nutrition (approximate per person)

Frank (largest side portion): ~120–180 kcal, 8–15 g protein, 15–25 g carbs, 3–6 g fat
Susan (medium side portion): ~90–140 kcal, 6–12 g protein, 12–20 g carbs, 2–5 g fat
Elise (medium side portion): ~90–140 kcal, 6–12 g protein, 12–20 g carbs, 2–5 g fat
