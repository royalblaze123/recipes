Salsa Verde Chicken & Bean Stuffed Poblanos (Sheet Pan)

Dinner • Sheet Pan • Cook time: 30 min

Ingredients (purchase-clean)

  • 4 each fresh poblano peppers
  • 24 oz fresh chicken breast
  • 8 oz canned black beans
  • 8 oz 2% plain Greek yogurt
  • 3 oz part-skim shredded mozzarella
  • 1 each fresh lime
  • 1/2 heart romaine hearts
  • 1/2 pint cherry tomatoes
  • salsa verde
  • garlic powder
  • cumin
  • black pepper
  • kosher salt
  • extra-virgin olive oil

Steps

  1. Heat oven to 425°F. Line a sheet pan with parchment.
  2. Chicken: Cut 24 oz chicken breast into small cubes. Season with 2 tsp kosher salt, 2 tsp cumin, 2 tsp garlic powder, and 1/2 tsp black pepper. Sauté in a nonstick skillet with 2 tsp olive oil for 8–10 minutes until 165°F.
  3. Beans: Warm 8 oz drained/rinsed black beans in the same skillet for 2 minutes. Stir in 8 tsp salsa verde and 1 tsp cumin.
  4. Creamy verde binder: In a bowl, whisk 8 oz Greek yogurt + 12 tsp salsa verde + zest of 1 lime. Fold in cooked chicken and beans. Stir in 2 oz mozzarella.
  5. Poblanos: Slice 4 poblanos lengthwise and remove seeds/ribs. Arrange 8 halves on the sheet pan.
  6. Stuff & bake: Fill poblanos evenly and top with remaining 1 oz mozzarella. Bake 14–18 minutes until peppers are tender and filling is hot.
  7. Salad: Chop 1/2 heart romaine hearts (about 4 oz). Halve 1/2 pint cherry tomatoes. Toss with 1 tsp olive oil, 1 tsp lime juice, 1/2 tsp kosher salt, and black pepper.
  8. Serve: Plate poblanos with the simple salad.

Notes

Sheet Pan dinner built to be high-protein and satisfying. Portions (per person): Frank: 4 poblano halves (2 whole poblanos) + 1.5 cups salad Susan: 3 poblano halves + 1 cup salad Elise: 3 poblano halves + 1 cup salad Tips (recipe-specific): If you’re sodium-sensitive, choose a lower-sodium salsa verde and rinse beans thoroughly. For less heat, roast poblanos 6 minutes first, then stuff and finish baking.
