Turkey Bacon-Turkey Avocado Sandwiches with Crunchy Pepper Side

Prep: 12 min   Cook: 8 min   Total: 20 min

Ingredients

  • 6 slices whole wheat bread
  • 12 slices deli turkey breast
  • 12 oz turkey bacon
  • 1 avocado
  • 1 tomato
  • 1 bag microgreens
  • 2 bell peppers
  • light mayonnaise
  • black pepper
  • salt substitute

RBM Sandwich rules: 3 full sandwiches total (6 slices bread). Deli meats/cheeses listed as slice counts; slicing guidance lives in Steps. Condiments/spices are name-only in Ingredients; all measurements are in Steps. Produce is listed in shoppable units (fractional OK). Microgreens preferred.

Step-by-step Instructions

  1. Cook turkey bacon: bake at 400°F for 10–12 minutes (flip at 6), or pan-cook 6–8 minutes until crisp. Pat dry.
  2. Slice tomato. Mash avocado with a pinch of salt substitute + black pepper.
  3. Assemble 3 sandwiches: spread a thin layer of mayo (about 1 tsp per sandwich) on one bread slice, spread avocado on the other. Layer turkey, turkey bacon, tomato, and microgreens. Close and press.
  4. Portioning: Frank gets 6 turkey slices + 4 turkey bacon slices + 1/2 avocado. Susan and Elise each get 3 turkey slices + 2 turkey bacon slices + 1/4 avocado.
  5. Veg side: slice bell peppers into strips. Frank gets ~2 cups pepper strips. Susan and Elise each get ~1 1/2 cups.
