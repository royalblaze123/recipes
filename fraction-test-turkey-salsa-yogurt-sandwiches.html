Fraction Test – Turkey Salsa-Yogurt Sandwiches + Air-Fried Broccoli

Lunch • Prep 12 min • Cook 12 min • Total 24 min

Ingredients

  • 6 slices whole wheat bread
  • 14 slices deli turkey
  • 4 slices provolone
  • 1/3 tub 2% plain Greek yogurt
  • 1/4 jar salsa
  • 1/2 bag spring mix
  • 1/2 bag frozen broccoli florets
  • 1 tsp olive oil
  • 1/2 tsp garlic powder
  • 1/2 tsp kosher salt
  • 1/4 tsp black pepper
  • 1 each lime

Step-by-step Instructions

  1. Air-fry broccoli (side): Preheat air fryer to 400°F. Add the frozen broccoli florets to a bowl. Drizzle with 1 tsp olive oil and toss. Sprinkle with 1/4 tsp kosher salt, 1/4 tsp black pepper, and 1/2 tsp garlic powder. Air-fry in a single layer for 10–12 minutes, shaking at 6 minutes, until browned at the edges. (This uses about 1/2 of a standard 12 oz bag; reseal and freeze the rest.)
  2. Make the salsa-yogurt spread: In a bowl, combine 2% plain Greek yogurt and salsa. Squeeze in juice from 1/2 the lime. Stir until smooth.
  3. Lay out bread and greens: Lay out 6 slices bread. Divide spring mix into 3 equal piles and place on 3 bread slices.
  4. Portion turkey by person: Add deli turkey to each sandwich: Frank gets 6 slices, Susan gets 4 slices, Elise gets 4 slices.
  5. Portion cheese by person: Add provolone: Frank gets 2 slices, Susan gets 1 slice, Elise gets 1 slice.
  6. Add spread and close: Spoon the salsa-yogurt spread onto the top bread slices (Frank gets about 2 tbsp; Susan and Elise get about 1 1/2 tbsp each). Close each sandwich.
  7. Plate and serve: Serve each person 1 sandwich. Divide the air-fried broccoli into 3 equal portions. If you have extra spring mix, add a small side salad and squeeze the remaining lime over greens if desired.
  8. Deli note (for shopping consistency): Order the deli turkey thin sliced for sandwiches (not shaved).
