Air Fryer Lemon Herb Chicken with Sweet Potato Wedges and Broccoli

Juicy lemon-herb chicken with sweet potato wedges and crispy-tender broccoli using the Cosori dual-basket Sync Finish workflow.

Servings

3 servings.

Times

  • Prep time: 20 minutes
  • Cook time: 22 minutes
  • Total time: 42 minutes

Ingredients

Main Dish

  • 1 lb boneless skinless chicken breast
  • 2 each sweet potatoes
  • 1 head broccoli
  • 1 each lemon
  • 6 cloves garlic
  • 1 bottle extra virgin olive oil
  • 1 bag reduced fat shredded cheese
  • 1 container nonfat Greek yogurt
  • 1 jar Italian seasoning
  • 1 jar paprika
  • 1 jar black pepper
    • 

    Preparation Steps

    1. Prep (chicken): pat chicken dry. Slice into 3 even cutlets. In a bowl, whisk 2 tbsp olive oil with lemon zest, 2 tbsp lemon juice, minced garlic, Italian seasoning, paprika, and black pepper. Coat chicken and rest 10 minutes.
    2. Prep (sweet potatoes): cut sweet potatoes into wedges. Measure out about 2 1/3 cups wedges for this recipe; refrigerate remaining sweet potato for another meal. Toss wedges with 1 tbsp olive oil, paprika, and black pepper.
    3. Prep (broccoli): cut broccoli into florets. Measure out 1/2 head broccoli for this recipe; refrigerate remaining broccoli. Toss florets with 1 tsp olive oil and black pepper.
    4. Cosori setup: preheat both baskets to 390°F for 3 minutes. Put sweet potato wedges in Basket A in a single layer. Put chicken cutlets in Basket B.
    5. Cook (Sync Finish): set Basket A to 390°F for 18 minutes. Set Basket B to 390°F for 10 minutes. Press Sync Finish so both baskets complete together.
    6. Shake reminder (Basket A): when the shake reminder beeps, shake/flip the sweet potato wedges for even browning.
    7. Mid-cook (Basket B): at the 5-minute mark, flip the chicken cutlets.
    8. Add broccoli: when Basket B finishes, remove chicken to a plate and tent with foil. Add broccoli to Basket B and cook 390°F for 6–7 minutes, shaking once.
    9. Check doneness: chicken must reach 165°F internal. If needed, return chicken to Basket B for 1–2 minutes.
    10. Yogurt drizzle: stir 1/4 cup nonfat Greek yogurt with 1 tsp lemon juice and a pinch of Italian seasoning (thin with 1 tsp water if needed).
    11. Serve (40/30/30): plate chicken with sweet potato wedges and broccoli. Sprinkle 3/4 oz reduced-fat shredded cheese total across all plates and drizzle lightly with yogurt sauce.

      Notes

      Portions (per person):


      Frank – Chicken: the largest cutlet. Sweet potato wedges: about 1 1/4 cups. Broccoli: about 1 1/2 cups. Yogurt drizzle + a light cheese sprinkle.


      Susan – Chicken: medium cutlet. Sweet potato wedges: about 1 cup. Broccoli: about 1 1/4 cups. Yogurt drizzle + a light cheese sprinkle.


      Elise – Chicken: medium cutlet. Sweet potato wedges: about 1 cup. Broccoli: about 1 1/4 cups. Yogurt drizzle + a light cheese sprinkle.


      Tips (recipe-specific):


      Use Sync Finish with sweet potatoes in Basket A and chicken in Basket B so both land hot at the same time.


      Keep broccoli in a single layer and cook it at the end so it stays crisp-tender (not soft).


      If you want fewer carbs one night, reduce sweet potato on the plate and add more broccoli.
