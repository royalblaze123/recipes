Lean Beef & Corn Tostada Plates

Nutrition (approx)

Frank: 650 kcal • P 58g • C 52g • F 22g
Susan/Elise (each): 488 kcal • P 44g • C 39g • F 16g

Ingredients (purchase-clean)

  • 1.50 lb 93% lean ground beef
  • 1 can corn
  • 1 package baked tostada shells
  • 1 each avocado
  • 1/2 jar salsa
  • 4 tsp extra-virgin olive oil
  • 2 tsp chili powder
  • 2 tsp cumin
  • 2 tsp garlic powder
  • 1 tsp smoked paprika
  • 1 tsp black pepper
  • 1 tsp kosher salt

Steps

  1. Tostadas: warm in a 350°F oven for 3–4 minutes to crisp and heat through.
  2. Beef: cook ground beef in a skillet over medium-high 7–9 minutes until browned. Stir in the spices for 60 seconds.
  3. Corn: can size note—use a standard 15 oz can corn. Drain well. Measure out 1 1/2 cups corn; refrigerate any remainder.
  4. Combine: stir measured corn into the beef and warm 2 minutes.
  5. Avocado: halve, pit, and dice. Use about 1/2 avocado total; refrigerate remaining avocado with the pit to slow browning.
  6. Serve: top tostadas with beef-corn mix, salsa, and avocado. Portion per Notes.

Notes

Lean beef tostadas with a controlled corn add-in, plus salsa and avocado. Portions (per person): Frank: 2 tostadas + 1/3 of the protein topping + 1/3 of the avocado + salsa Susan: 1 tostada + 1/4 of the protein topping + 1/4 of the avocado + salsa Elise: 1 tostada + 1/4 of the protein topping + 1/4 of the avocado + salsa
