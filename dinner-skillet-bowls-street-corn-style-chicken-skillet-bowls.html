Street-Corn Style Chicken Skillet Bowls

Nutrition (approx)

Frank: 645 kcal • P 58g • C 60g • F 18g • Sat 7g • Na 690mg • Fiber 13g
Susan/Elise (each): 484 kcal • P 44g • C 45g • F 14g • Sat 5g • Na 518mg • Fiber 10g

Ingredients (purchase-clean)

  • 1.50 lb boneless skinless chicken breast
  • 1 bag frozen corn
  • 1 can black beans
  • 1 bag shredded lettuce
  • 1 cup dry brown rice
  • 2 oz cotija cheese
  • 1 each avocado
  • 1/2 jar salsa
  • 1 each lime
  • kosher salt
  • black pepper
  • chili powder
  • ground cumin
  • garlic powder
  • smoked paprika
  • extra-virgin olive oil
  • cayenne pepper

Steps

  1. Rice: cook 1 cup dry brown rice with 2 cups water (35–40 minutes), rest 5 minutes, fluff.
  2. Chicken: cook 1.50 lb diced chicken in skillet with 2 tsp olive oil 8–10 minutes to 165°F.
  3. Season with 2 tsp chili powder, 2 tsp cumin, 2 tsp garlic powder, 1/2 tsp kosher salt, 1/2 tsp black pepper, and a pinch cayenne.
  4. Corn: cook 3 cups frozen corn in the same skillet 6–8 minutes until browned. Stir into chicken.
  5. Beans: drain/rinse one 15 oz can black beans. Add 1 1/2 cups beans; warm 2 minutes.
  6. Finish with 2 tsp lime juice.
  7. Bowls: divide rice + chicken mix over shredded lettuce. Top with salsa, diced avocado, and 2 oz cotija total across bowls (about 2 tsp each). Portion per Notes.

Notes

Comfort bowls that taste like street corn + tacos (without the mayo). Portions (per person): Frank: 1/3 rice + 1/3 chicken mix + 1/3 avocado + salsa + 2 tsp cotija Susan: 1/4 rice + 1/4 chicken mix + 1/4 avocado + salsa + 1 1/2 tsp cotija Elise: 1/4 rice + 1/4 chicken mix + 1/4 avocado + salsa + 1 1/2 tsp cotija Tips (recipe-specific): Brown the corn in the skillet for the ‘street corn’ flavor. Cotija is salty—keep it measured and let salsa do the rest.
