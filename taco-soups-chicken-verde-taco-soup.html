Chicken Verde Taco Soup

Nutrition (approx)

Frank: 640 kcal • P 58g • C 44g • F 20g
Susan/Elise (each): 480 kcal • P 44g • C 33g • F 15 g

Ingredients (purchase-clean)

  • 1.50 lb boneless skinless chicken breast
  • 1 can cannellini beans
  • 1 jar salsa verde
  • 1 carton low-sodium chicken broth
  • 1 bag frozen fajita-style pepper blend
  • 1 each avocado
  • 1/2 jar salsa
  • 1 each lime
  • 2 tsp extra-virgin olive oil
  • 2 tsp cumin
  • 2 tsp garlic powder
  • 1 tsp black pepper
  • 1 tsp kosher salt

Steps

  1. Chicken: cut into 1/2-inch pieces. Brown in a pot with 2 tsp olive oil 8–10 minutes until 165°F.
  2. Veg: add frozen fajita pepper blend and cook 6–8 minutes until browned at edges.
  3. Beans: Can size note—use a standard 15 oz can cannellini beans.. Drain/rinse. Add 1 1/2 cups; refrigerate remainder.
  4. Verde: jar size note—use a 16 oz jar salsa verde. Add 1 1/2 cups verde; refrigerate remainder.
  5. Broth: carton size note—use a 32 oz carton low-sodium chicken broth. Add 2 cups broth; refrigerate remainder.
  6. Season: stir in cumin, garlic powder, black pepper, and kosher salt.
  7. Simmer: simmer 12–15 minutes.
  8. Acid: add 2 tsp lime juice.
  9. Finish: taste and adjust seasoning with additional black pepper if needed.
  10. Toppings: dice avocado and serve salsa on the side. Top each bowl with measured avocado + salsa. Portion per Notes.

Notes

Green taco soup (verde) with chicken + white beans—bright, filling, and topped with avocado + salsa. Portions (per person): Frank: 1/3 of the pot + 1/3 of the avocado + 1/3 of the salsa Susan: 1/4 of the pot + 1/4 of the avocado + 1/4 of the salsa Elise: 1/4 of the pot + 1/4 of the avocado + 1/4 of the salsa
