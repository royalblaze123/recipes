Greek Lemon-Herb Chicken Plates (Sheet Pan)

Nutrition (approx)

Frank: 640 kcal • P 58g • C 55g • F 16g • Sat 4g • Na 700mg • Fiber 10g
Susan/Elise (each): 480 kcal • P 44g • C 41g • F 12g • Sat 3g • Na 525mg • Fiber 8g

Ingredients (purchase-clean)

  • 1.75 lb boneless skinless chicken breast
  • 1.50 lb baby potatoes
  • 1 bag frozen broccoli florets
  • 1 each lemon
  • 1 jar roasted red peppers
  • kosher salt
  • black pepper
  • garlic powder
  • onion powder
  • smoked paprika
  • Italian seasoning
  • ground cumin
  • chili powder
  • red pepper flakes
  • extra-virgin olive oil
  • low-sodium chicken broth

Steps

  1. Oven: heat to 425°F. Line a sheet pan.
  2. Potatoes: halve baby potatoes. Toss with 2 tsp olive oil, black pepper, garlic powder, Italian seasoning, and a modest pinch of kosher salt. Roast 15 minutes.
  3. Chicken: toss chicken with 2 tsp olive oil, 2 tsp lemon juice, garlic powder, Italian seasoning, and black pepper. Add to pan; roast 10 minutes.
  4. Broccoli: add frozen broccoli tossed with 2 tsp olive oil and black pepper; roast 10–12 minutes until chicken is 165°F.
  5. Finish: slice roasted red peppers and scatter over plates; add extra lemon squeeze.
  6. Serve per Notes.

Notes

Greek-style comfort plate built on lemon, herbs, and roasted edges (no creamy sauce). Portions (per person): Frank: 7 oz chicken + 1 cup potatoes + 1 cup broccoli Susan: 5 oz protein + 1 cup potatoes + 3/4 cup veg Elise: 5 oz protein + 1 cup potatoes + 3/4 cup veg Tips (recipe-specific): Roast potatoes first so they brown. Lemon at the end makes the whole plate taste brighter without extra salt.
