Chicken Avocado Crunch Wrap & Veggie Sticks

Whole-wheat tortilla wrap with lean chicken, avocado-yogurt spread, and crunchy vegetables, served with carrot and cucumber sticks on the side.

Servings

3 servings.

Times

  • Prep time: 15 minutes
  • Cook time: 0 minutes
  • Total time: 15 minutes

Ingredients

Sandwich

  • 3 medium whole-wheat tortillas
  • 10.5 oz cooked skinless chicken breast
  • 3 tbsp low-fat plain Greek yogurt
  • 1 small ripe avocado
  • 2 tsp lime juice
  • 1/4 tsp garlic powder
  • 1/4 tsp ground cumin
  • 1/4 tsp black pepper
  • 1/8 tsp salt or salt substitute
  • 1 1/2 cups shredded romaine or lettuce mix
  • 1/2 cup thinly sliced red bell pepper
  • 1/4 cup thinly sliced red onion

Side

  • 1 cup carrot sticks
  • 1 cup cucumber sticks

Preparation Steps

  1. Place the cooked chicken breast on a cutting board and slice it into thin strips if not already sliced.
  2. In a small bowl, mash the avocado with a fork until mostly smooth. Add the Greek yogurt, lime juice, garlic powder, ground cumin, black pepper, and salt or salt substitute. Stir until a creamy avocado-yogurt spread forms.
  3. Lay the whole-wheat tortillas flat on a clean work surface.
  4. Spread a thin, even layer of the avocado-yogurt mixture over each tortilla, leaving a small border around the edge.
  5. Divide the sliced chicken among the three tortillas, giving Frank a slightly larger amount and Susan and Elise slightly smaller amounts, as described in the portions section.
  6. Top the chicken on each tortilla with shredded lettuce, sliced red bell pepper, and sliced red onion.
  7. Starting from one side, roll each tortilla tightly into a wrap, keeping the filling tucked in as you roll. Slice each wrap in half on a slight diagonal if desired.
  8. Arrange the carrot sticks and cucumber sticks on a cutting board if they need trimming, then divide them onto three plates in the amounts listed for each person.
  9. Place one wrap on each plate with the veggie sticks and serve immediately.

Notes

Portions (per person):

Frank – 1 full wrap made with about 3.75 oz cooked chicken, 1 medium whole-wheat tortilla, about 1/4 of a small avocado in the spread, and a generous layer of vegetables, plus about 3/4 cup total carrot and cucumber sticks.

Susan – 1 full wrap made with about 3 oz cooked chicken, 1 medium whole-wheat tortilla, about 1/6 of a small avocado in the spread, and a moderate layer of vegetables, plus about 2/3 cup total carrot and cucumber sticks.

Elise – 1 full wrap made with about 2.75 oz cooked chicken, 1 medium whole-wheat tortilla, about 1/6 of a small avocado in the spread, and a moderate layer of vegetables, plus about 2/3 cup total carrot and cucumber sticks.

Nutrition (per person)

  • Frank: 455 kcal; 43 g protein; 40 g carbs; 14 g fat; 9 g fiber; ~560 mg sodium.
  • Susan: 410 kcal; 36 g protein; 37 g carbs; 12 g fat; 8 g fiber; ~520 mg sodium.
  • Elise: 410 kcal; 36 g protein; 37 g carbs; 12 g fat; 8 g fiber; ~520 mg sodium.
