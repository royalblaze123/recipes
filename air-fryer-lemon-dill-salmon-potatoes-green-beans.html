Air Fryer Lemon-Dill Salmon with Roasted Baby Potatoes and Garlicky Green Beans

Prep: 12 min   Cook: 28 min   Total: 40 min

Ingredients

  • 1 package salmon fillets (~1–1.25 lb total)
  • 1 bag baby potatoes
  • 1 bag fresh green beans
  • 1 lemon
  • 1 bunch fresh dill
  • 1 package garlic cloves
  • olive oil (tsp) — pantry
  • Dijon mustard (tsp) — pantry
  • black pepper (tsp) — pantry
  • salt substitute (tsp) — pantry

Ingredient list is purchase-clean (AnyList aggregation). All measuring/used amounts and remainder handling are in Steps.

Step-by-step Instructions

  1. Heat the oven to 425°F. Line a sheet pan with parchment. Rinse and dry the baby potatoes; cut any large potatoes in half.
  2. Measure olive oil: use 12 tsp total for this recipe. On the sheet pan, toss potatoes with 8 tsp olive oil, 2 tsp salt substitute, and 1 tsp black pepper. Roast 22–26 minutes, flipping once at ~12 minutes.
  3. While potatoes start roasting, trim green beans. Measure olive oil: use 4 tsp reserved for beans. Mince 2 cloves garlic.
  4. Add green beans to the sheet pan after the potatoes have roasted ~12 minutes. Toss beans on-pan with 4 tsp olive oil, the minced garlic, and 1 tsp black pepper. Continue roasting 10–12 minutes until beans are crisp-tender and potatoes are browned.
  5. Prep salmon: pat fillets dry. Zest the lemon and then juice it. Measure Dijon: use 6 tsp total. In a small bowl whisk 6 tsp Dijon + 6 tsp lemon juice + 1 tsp black pepper + 1 tbsp chopped dill (chop from the dill bunch). Brush over the salmon (discard any unused brush-bowl mixture).
  6. Air fry salmon (Cosori): preheat to 390°F for 3 minutes. Place salmon skin-side down in a single layer. Cook 8–11 minutes depending on thickness, until it flakes easily and is 125–130°F for medium or 135°F for well-done. No flipping needed; check at 8 minutes.
  7. Portion and serve: Frank gets 6 oz cooked salmon + ~1 1/4 cups potatoes + ~1 1/2 cups green beans. Susan and Elise each get ~4.5 oz salmon + ~1 cup potatoes + ~1 1/4 cups green beans. Finish each plate with extra dill and a lemon wedge.
