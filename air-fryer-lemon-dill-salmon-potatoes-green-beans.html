Air Fryer Lemon-Dill Salmon with Roasted Baby Potatoes and Garlicky Green Beans

Prep: 12 min   Cook: 28 min   Total: 40 min

Ingredients

  • 1.25 lb salmon fillets
  • 1 bag baby potatoes
  • 1 bag fresh green beans
  • 1 lemon
  • 1 bunch fresh dill
  • 1 bulb garlic
  • olive oil
  • Dijon mustard
  • black pepper
  • salt substitute

RBM rules: quantities appear before items; fresh proteins use weight. Condiments/spices/seasonings are listed name-only; all measurements are in Steps.

Step-by-step Instructions

  1. Heat the oven to 425°F. Line a sheet pan with parchment. Rinse and dry the baby potatoes; cut any large potatoes in half.
  2. On the sheet pan, toss potatoes with 8 tsp olive oil + 2 tsp salt substitute + 1 tsp black pepper. Roast 22–26 minutes, flipping once at ~12 minutes.
  3. Trim green beans. Mince 2 garlic cloves.
  4. Add green beans to the sheet pan after the potatoes have roasted ~12 minutes. Toss beans on-pan with 4 tsp olive oil + minced garlic + 1 tsp black pepper. Continue roasting 10–12 minutes until beans are crisp-tender and potatoes are browned.
  5. Prep salmon: pat dry. Zest the lemon and then juice it. Chop dill (use 1 tbsp chopped). In a bowl whisk 6 tsp Dijon + 6 tsp lemon juice + 1 tsp black pepper + 1 tbsp chopped dill. Brush over the salmon.
  6. Air fry salmon (Cosori): preheat to 390°F for 3 minutes. Cook salmon skin-side down 8–11 minutes (thickness-dependent) until it flakes and reaches 125–130°F medium or 135°F well-done.
  7. Portion and serve: Frank gets 6 oz cooked salmon + ~1 1/4 cups potatoes + ~1 1/2 cups green beans. Susan and Elise each get ~4.5 oz salmon + ~1 cup potatoes + ~1 1/4 cups green beans. Finish with extra dill and lemon wedges.
