One-Pot Barilla Protein+ Turkey Bolognese

Nutrition (approx)

Frank: 650 kcal • P 60g • C 70g • F 14g • Sat 4g • Na 700mg • Fiber 10g
Susan/Elise (each): 488 kcal • P 45g • C 52g • F 10g • Sat 3g • Na 525mg • Fiber 8g

Ingredients (purchase-clean)

  • 1 box Barilla Protein+ pasta
  • 1.50 lb 93% lean ground turkey
  • 1 jar marinara sauce
  • 1 can no-salt-added diced tomatoes
  • 1 each onion
  • low-sodium chicken broth
  • kosher salt
  • black pepper
  • garlic powder
  • onion powder
  • smoked paprika
  • Italian seasoning
  • ground cumin
  • chili powder
  • red pepper flakes
  • extra-virgin olive oil
  • low-sodium chicken broth

Steps

  1. Turkey: brown turkey with diced onion, 2 tsp olive oil, 1 tsp garlic powder, 1 tsp onion powder (optional), black pepper, and a modest pinch of kosher salt (7–9 minutes).
  2. Liquids: add 1 jar marinara + 1 can diced tomatoes (14.5 oz) + 2 cups low-sodium broth. Bring to a simmer.
  3. Pasta: add 1 box Barilla Protein+ pasta (broken if needed). Stir well.
  4. Simmer: cover and cook 10–12 minutes, stirring every 2 minutes, until pasta is al dente and sauce is thick.
  5. Rest 3 minutes. Serve per Notes.

Notes

Weeknight pasta without a second pot—Protein+ pasta and a thick bolognese sauce. Portions (per person): Frank: About 2 1/2 cups (heaping bowl) Susan: About 2 cups (bowl) Elise: About 2 cups (bowl) Tips (recipe-specific): Stir frequently so one-pot pasta doesn’t stick. Rest 3 minutes so the sauce clings before serving.
