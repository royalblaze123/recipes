Chicken Piccata-Style Plates (No Butter Bomb)

Nutrition (approx)

Frank: 645 kcal • P 58g • C 55g • F 16g • Sat 3g • Na 700mg • Fiber 10g
Susan/Elise (each): 484 kcal • P 44g • C 41g • F 12g • Sat 2g • Na 525mg • Fiber 8g

Ingredients (purchase-clean)

  • 1.75 lb boneless skinless chicken breast
  • 1 each lemon
  • 1 carton low-sodium chicken broth
  • cornstarch
  • 1.50 lb baby potatoes
  • 1 bag frozen green beans
  • kosher salt
  • black pepper
  • garlic powder
  • onion powder
  • smoked paprika
  • Italian seasoning
  • ground cumin
  • chili powder
  • red pepper flakes
  • extra-virgin olive oil
  • low-sodium chicken broth

Steps

  1. Oven: heat to 425°F.
  2. Potatoes: halve and roast with 2 tsp olive oil, black pepper, garlic powder, and modest kosher salt 22–26 minutes.
  3. Chicken: season chicken with black pepper, garlic powder, and a modest pinch of kosher salt. Sear in a skillet with 2 tsp olive oil 3 minutes per side.
  4. Sauce: add 1 cup low-sodium chicken broth and 2 tbsp lemon juice. Whisk 1 tsp cornstarch into 2 tbsp cold water; stir in. Simmer 2–3 minutes to thicken.
  5. Green beans: microwave green beans; drain; toss with black pepper and a squeeze of lemon.
  6. Serve per Notes.

Notes

Piccata vibe from lemon and a glossy pan sauce—without lots of butter. Portions (per person): Frank: 7 oz chicken + 1 cup potatoes + 1 cup green beans Susan: 5 oz protein + 1 cup potatoes + 3/4 cup veg Elise: 5 oz protein + 1 cup potatoes + 3/4 cup veg Tips (recipe-specific): Thicken with cornstarch so you don’t need butter for body. Add lemon at the end to keep it bright.
