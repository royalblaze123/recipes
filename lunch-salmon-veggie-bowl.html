Salmon Quinoa Veggie Bowl

Heart-healthy salmon with quinoa and green beans.

Ingredients

  • 12 oz cooked salmon, flaked
  • 1 cup cooked quinoa
  • 1 cup steamed green beans
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice
  • 1/4 tsp garlic powder
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 1/8 tsp black pepper

Instructions

  1. Flake cooked salmon.
  2. Cook or reheat quinoa.
  3. Steam green beans.
  4. Combine quinoa and beans with oil, lemon, and seasonings.
  5. Top with salmon and reheat 45â€“60 seconds.

Serves: 3 servings
