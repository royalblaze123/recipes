Chicken Fajita Skillet Rice Bowls

Nutrition (approx)

Frank: 625 kcal • P 58g • C 55g • F 18g • Sat 6g • Na 650mg • Fiber 12g
Susan/Elise (each): 469 kcal • P 44g • C 41g • F 14g • Sat 4g • Na 488mg • Fiber 9g

Ingredients (purchase-clean)

  • 1.50 lb boneless skinless chicken breast
  • 1 bag frozen fajita-style pepper blend
  • 1 can black beans
  • 1 cup dry brown rice
  • 1 each avocado
  • 1/2 jar salsa
  • 1 each lime
  • kosher salt
  • black pepper
  • chili powder
  • ground cumin
  • garlic powder
  • smoked paprika
  • extra-virgin olive oil

Steps

  1. Rice: rinse 1 cup dry brown rice. Cook with 2 cups water: bring to a boil, cover, reduce to low, simmer 35–40 minutes. Rest 5 minutes, fluff.
  2. Skillet: heat 2 tsp olive oil over medium-high. Add 1.50 lb diced chicken; cook 8–10 minutes to 165°F.
  3. Season chicken with 2 tsp chili powder, 2 tsp cumin, 2 tsp garlic powder, 1 tsp smoked paprika, 1/2 tsp kosher salt, and 1/2 tsp black pepper.
  4. Add 1 bag frozen fajita pepper blend; cook 6–8 minutes until browned and hot.
  5. Beans: use one 15 oz can black beans. Drain/rinse. Stir 1 1/2 cups beans into the skillet; cook 2 minutes. (Refrigerate remaining beans.)
  6. Finish with 2 tsp lime juice.
  7. Bowls: divide rice and skillet mix into 3 bowls. Top with salsa and diced avocado. Portion per Notes.

Notes

One-pan fajita bowls that eat like takeout but stay portion-controlled. Portions (per person): Frank: 1/3 of rice + 1/3 of skillet mix + 1/3 avocado + salsa Susan: 1/4 of rice + 1/4 of skillet mix + 1/4 avocado + salsa Elise: 1/4 of rice + 1/4 of skillet mix + 1/4 avocado + salsa Tips (recipe-specific): Use a hot skillet and let the pepper blend brown before stirring—browning = flavor. Keep salsa on the table and add a fresh spoon right before eating to prevent sogginess.
