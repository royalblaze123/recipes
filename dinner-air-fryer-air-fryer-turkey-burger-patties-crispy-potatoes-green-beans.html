Air Fryer Turkey Burger Patties + Crispy Potatoes + Green Beans

Nutrition (approx)

Frank: 650 kcal • P 58g • C 55g • F 20g • Sat 6g • Na 700mg • Fiber 10g
Susan/Elise (each): 488 kcal • P 44g • C 41g • F 15g • Sat 4g • Na 525mg • Fiber 8g

Ingredients (purchase-clean)

  • 1.75 lb 93% lean ground turkey
  • 1.50 lb baby potatoes
  • 1 bag frozen green beans
  • whole-wheat burger buns
  • reduced-fat cheddar cheese
  • kosher salt
  • black pepper
  • garlic powder
  • onion powder
  • smoked paprika
  • Italian seasoning
  • ground cumin
  • chili powder
  • red pepper flakes
  • extra-virgin olive oil
  • low-sodium soy sauce
  • hot sauce
  • Dijon mustard
  • balsamic vinegar
  • apple cider vinegar
  • cornstarch

Steps (Cosori dual-basket)

  1. Preheat Cosori to 400°F. Basket A: potatoes. Basket B: patties later. Use Sync Finish.
  2. Potatoes (Basket A): cut potatoes into wedges; toss with 2 tsp olive oil, 1 tsp garlic powder, 1 tsp smoked paprika, 1/2 tsp kosher salt, black pepper. Air fry 18–22 minutes with Shake Reminder.
  3. Patties: form 3 large patties. Season with 1 tsp garlic powder, 1 tsp onion powder, 1/2 tsp kosher salt, and black pepper.
  4. Air fry patties (Basket B) 10–12 minutes at 380°F, flipping once, to 165°F. Add 1 slice reduced-fat cheddar per patty in the last 1 minute (measured).
  5. Green beans: microwave, drain; toss with 2 tsp olive oil, black pepper, 1 tsp garlic powder; crisp 5–6 minutes if space or roast 425°F 10 minutes.
  6. Assemble: use 1 whole-wheat bun per person (measured). Serve with potatoes and green beans per Notes.

Notes

Burger-plate comfort: air-fryer patties with measured bun and cheese, plus real sides. Portions (per person): Frank: 1 burger + 1 cup potatoes + 1 cup green beans Susan: 1 burger + 3/4 cup potatoes + 3/4 cup green beans Elise: 1 burger + 3/4 cup potatoes + 3/4 cup green beans Tips (recipe-specific): Lower basket temp keeps turkey from drying out. Add cheese only at the end so it melts without overcooking the patty.
