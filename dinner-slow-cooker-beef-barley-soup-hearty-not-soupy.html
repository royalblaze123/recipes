Beef + Barley Soup (Hearty, Not Soupy)

Nutrition (approx)

Frank: 650 kcal • P 55g • C 65g • F 16g
Susan/Elise (each): 488 kcal • P 41g • C 49g • F 12g

Ingredients (purchase-clean)

  • 1.25 lb lean sirloin steak
  • 1 cup dry pearl barley
  • 1 lb carrots
  • 2 stalks celery
  • 1 each onion
  • low-sodium beef broth
  • kosher salt
  • black pepper
  • garlic powder
  • onion powder
  • smoked paprika
  • Italian seasoning
  • ground cumin
  • chili powder
  • red pepper flakes
  • extra-virgin olive oil
  • low-sodium chicken broth
  • low-sodium beef broth
  • low-sodium soy sauce
  • hot sauce
  • Dijon mustard
  • balsamic vinegar
  • apple cider vinegar
  • cornstarch

Steps (slow cooker)

  1. Cube sirloin; season pepper, garlic powder, onion powder; modest salt. Sear optional.
  2. Add beef + onion + carrots + celery + broth (7 cups) + barley (1 cup dry).
  3. LOW 5–6 hr or HIGH 3–4 hr until barley is tender but not mushy.
  4. Reduce HIGH lid ajar 15 min if you want thicker body.
  5. Serve per Notes.

Notes

Hearty barley soup with chew and structure (not mush). Portions (per person): Frank: About 2 1/2 cups (heaping bowl) Susan: About 2 cups (bowl) Elise: About 2 cups (bowl) Tips (recipe-specific): LOW keeps barley pleasantly chewy. If done early, switch to Warm to avoid mush.
