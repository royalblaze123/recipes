Egg Salad Avocado Rye Sandwich & Carrot Sticks

Light Greek yogurt egg salad with mashed avocado on rye bread, served with crisp carrot sticks.

Servings

3 servings (Frank, Susan, Elise).

Times

  • Prep time: 10 minutes
  • Cook time: 0 minutes
  • Total time: 10 minutes

Ingredients

Sandwich

  • 6 slices thin-sliced rye or whole-grain rye bread
  • 6 large hard-boiled eggs, peeled
  • 3 tbsp low-fat (2%) plain Greek yogurt
  • 1 small ripe avocado, peeled and pitted
  • 1 tsp Dijon mustard
  • 1/4 tsp garlic powder
  • 1/4 tsp black pepper
  • 1/8 tsp salt or salt substitute
  • 1/4 cup finely chopped celery
  • 1/4 cup finely chopped green onion or chives
  • 1 cup fresh lettuce leaves

Side

  • 1 1/2 cups carrot sticks

Preparation Steps

  1. Place the hard-boiled eggs in a medium mixing bowl and chop them into small pieces using a knife or fork.
  2. Add the Greek yogurt, Dijon mustard, garlic powder, black pepper, and salt or salt substitute to the eggs.
  3. In a separate small bowl, mash the avocado with a fork until mostly smooth, then add it to the egg mixture.
  4. Add the finely chopped celery and chopped green onion or chives, then stir until the egg salad is evenly combined.
  5. Lay out the rye bread slices. Place lettuce leaves on three of the slices to create a base.
  6. Divide the egg salad evenly among the three lettuce-topped slices, giving Frank a slightly larger share and Susan and Elise slightly smaller shares as described in the portions section.
  7. Top each sandwich with a remaining slice of rye bread, press gently, and cut in half if desired.
  8. Place the carrot sticks on a cutting board if they need trimming, then divide them onto three plates according to the portions listed.
  9. Serve each person one egg salad avocado sandwich with their portion of carrot sticks.

Notes

Portions (per person):

Frank – 1 full egg salad sandwich made with about 2 1/2 eggs worth of filling on 2 slices rye bread, plus about 2/3 cup carrot sticks.

Susan – 1 full egg salad sandwich made with about 2 eggs worth of filling on 2 slices rye bread, plus about 1/2 cup carrot sticks.

Elise – 1 full egg salad sandwich made with about 1 1/2 eggs worth of filling on 2 slices rye bread, plus about 1/2 cup carrot sticks.

Nutrition (per person)

  • Frank: 430 kcal; 36 g protein; 34 g carbs; 15 g fat; 7 g fiber; ~540 mg sodium.
  • Susan: 380 kcal; 31 g protein; 30 g carbs; 13 g fat; 6 g fiber; ~500 mg sodium.
  • Elise: 380 kcal; 31 g protein; 30 g carbs; 13 g fat; 6 g fiber; ~500 mg sodium.
