Baked Salmon + Dijon-Balsamic Glaze + Asparagus + Sweet Potatoes

Nutrition (approx)

Frank: 650 kcal • P 55g • C 55g • F 28g
Susan/Elise (each): 488 kcal • P 41g • C 41g • F 21g

Steps (oven)

  1. Oven: heat to 425°F.
  2. Sweet potatoes: cube; toss with olive oil, smoked paprika, garlic powder, modest salt, black pepper. Roast 425°F 22–26 minutes.
  3. Glaze: whisk Dijon (2 tsp) + balsamic vinegar (2 tbsp) + black pepper.
  4. Salmon: pat dry; season modest salt/pepper/garlic powder. Brush with glaze; bake 10–12 minutes to 125–130°F and rest.
  5. Asparagus: toss with olive oil, black pepper, modest salt; roast 425°F 8–10 minutes.
  6. Serve per Notes.

Notes

Simple but flavorful salmon plate with a tangy glaze and roasted sides. Portions (per person): Frank: 7 oz salmon + 1 cup sweet potatoes + 1 1/2 cups asparagus Susan: 5 oz protein + 3/4 cup starch + 1 cup veg Elise: 5 oz protein + 3/4 cup starch + 1 cup veg Tips (recipe-specific): Glaze is strong—measure it so calories stay controlled. Rest salmon 3 minutes for juiciness.
