Instant Pot BBQ Pulled Chicken + Sweet Potatoes + Slaw

Nutrition (approx)

Frank: 645 kcal • P 60g • C 55g • F 16g
Susan/Elise (each): 484 kcal • P 45g • C 41g • F 12g

Ingredients (purchase-clean)

  • 1.75 lb boneless skinless chicken breast
  • low-sugar BBQ sauce
  • 2.00 lb sweet potatoes
  • 1 bag coleslaw mix
  • apple cider vinegar
  • kosher salt
  • black pepper
  • garlic powder
  • onion powder
  • smoked paprika
  • Italian seasoning
  • ground cumin
  • chili powder
  • red pepper flakes
  • extra-virgin olive oil
  • low-sodium chicken broth
  • low-sodium beef broth
  • low-sodium soy sauce
  • hot sauce
  • Dijon mustard
  • balsamic vinegar
  • apple cider vinegar
  • cornstarch

Steps (Instant Pot)

  1. Pressure: add chicken + broth (1/2 cup). High 10 minutes; NR 10 then Quick Release.
  2. Shred; stir in BBQ sauce (5 tbsp measured). Sauté 2 minutes to thicken.
  3. Sweet potatoes: roast cubes 425°F 22–26 minutes with olive oil, smoked paprika, garlic powder, modest salt, black pepper.
  4. Slaw: coleslaw mix + apple cider vinegar (2 tbsp) + Dijon mustard (2 tsp) + olive oil (2 tsp) + black pepper + small pinch salt.
  5. Plate per Notes.

Notes

Holiday-friendly comfort: juicy pulled chicken with measured BBQ and crisp slaw. Portions (per person): Frank: 7 oz pulled chicken + 1 cup sweet potatoes + 1 cup slaw Susan: 5 oz protein + 1 cup sweet potatoes (smaller) + 1 cup slaw (smaller) Elise: 5 oz protein + 1 cup sweet potatoes (smaller) + 1 cup slaw (smaller) Tips (recipe-specific): Measure BBQ; flavor comes from smoke/spice, not sugar. Keep slaw vinegar-based so it stays crisp.
