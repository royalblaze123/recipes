Turkey + Sweet Potato Burrito Bowls

Nutrition (approx)

Frank: 545 kcal • P 50g • C 56g • F 15g
Susan/Elise (each): 409 kcal • P 38g • C 42g • F 11g

Ingredients (purchase-clean)

  • 1.25 lb 93% lean ground turkey
  • 1.25 lb sweet potatoes
  • 1 can black beans
  • 1 bag shredded lettuce
  • 1 each avocado
  • 1/2 jar salsa
  • 1 each lime
  • 4 tsp extra-virgin olive oil
  • 1/2 cup dry brown rice
  • 2 tsp chili powder
  • 2 tsp cumin
  • 2 tsp garlic powder
  • 1 tsp smoked paprika
  • 1 tsp black pepper
  • 1 tsp kosher salt

Steps

  1. Rice: cook 1/2 cup dry brown rice with 1 cup water: bring to boil, cover, simmer 35–40 minutes, rest 5 minutes, fluff.
  2. Sweet potatoes: small dice. Toss with 2 tsp olive oil and roast at 425°F for 18–22 minutes until browned and tender.
  3. Turkey: cook ground turkey 7–9 minutes until browned. Stir in spices for 60 seconds.
  4. Beans: Can size note—use a standard 15 oz can black beans.. Drain/rinse. Use 1 1/2 cups beans; refrigerate remainder.
  5. Combine: fold roasted sweet potato and beans into turkey and warm 2 minutes. Finish with 2 tsp lime juice.
  6. Assembly: divide shredded lettuce into three bowls. Top with turkey mix and the cooked rice.
  7. Top: add salsa and measured diced avocado. Portion per Notes.
  8. Avocado: halve, pit, and dice. Use about 1/2 avocado total; refrigerate remaining avocado with the pit to slow browning.

Notes

Turkey burrito bowls with roasted sweet potato (more volume, great reheat), finished with salsa + avocado. Portions (per person): Frank: 1 large burrito bowl (about 1/3 of the batch) + 1/3 avocado + salsa Susan: 1 medium burrito bowl (about 1/4 of the batch) + 1/4 avocado + salsa Elise: 1 medium burrito bowl (about 1/4 of the batch) + 1/4 avocado + salsa
