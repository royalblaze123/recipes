Turkey Taco Pitas (Salsa + Avocado Inside)

Nutrition (approx)

Frank: 535 kcal • P 52g • C 50g • F 16g
Susan/Elise (each): 401 kcal • P 39g • C 38g • F 12g

Ingredients (purchase-clean)

  • 1.50 lb 93% lean ground turkey
  • 1 package whole-wheat pitas
  • 1 each avocado
  • 1/2 jar salsa
  • 1 bag shredded lettuce
  • 1 each cucumber
  • 1 bag mini sweet peppers
  • 4 tsp extra-virgin olive oil
  • 2 tsp chili powder
  • 2 tsp cumin
  • 2 tsp garlic powder
  • 1 tsp smoked paprika
  • 1 tsp black pepper
  • 1 tsp kosher salt

Steps

  1. Turkey: cook ground turkey 7–9 minutes until browned. Season with spices.
  2. Pitas: warm and split.
  3. Stuff: add shredded lettuce, then turkey.
  4. Avocado: halve, pit, and dice. Use about 1/2 avocado total.
  5. Salsa: spoon salsa inside each wrap/pita (not just on the side) so every bite has flavor.
  6. Serve with veggie sticks. Portion per Notes.

Notes

Turkey taco stuffed pitas; salsa + avocado inside keeps it moist all shift. Portions (per person): Frank: 2 stuffed pitas (with salsa + avocado inside) + veggie sticks Susan: 1 stuffed pita + veggie sticks Elise: 1 stuffed pita + veggie sticks
