Chicken Enchilada Bake (Layered, No Tortillas)

Nutrition (approx)

Frank: 640 kcal • P 60g • C 45g • F 18g • Sat 6g • Na 700mg • Fiber 12g
Susan/Elise (each): 480 kcal • P 45g • C 34g • F 14g • Sat 4g • Na 525mg • Fiber 9g

Ingredients (purchase-clean)

  • 1.75 lb boneless skinless chicken breast
  • 1 can black beans
  • 1 jar enchilada sauce
  • 1 container reduced-fat shredded cheddar cheese
  • 1 bag frozen fajita-style pepper blend
  • 1 bag shredded lettuce
  • kosher salt
  • black pepper
  • garlic powder
  • onion powder
  • Italian seasoning
  • smoked paprika
  • cornstarch
  • extra-virgin olive oil
  • low-sodium chicken broth
  • chili powder
  • ground cumin

Steps

  1. Oven: heat to 425°F.
  2. Chicken: sauté diced chicken with 2 tsp olive oil, chili powder, cumin, garlic powder, onion powder, modest kosher salt, black pepper 8–10 minutes to 165°F.
  3. Beans: Can size note—use a standard 15 oz can black beans.. Drain/rinse. Use 1 1/2 cups beans; refrigerate remainder.
  4. Peppers: sauté frozen fajita pepper blend 6–8 minutes until browned and water evaporates.
  5. Layer: spread 1/2 cup enchilada sauce in a baking dish. Add chicken/beans/peppers. Pour 1 cup enchilada sauce over top (measured).
  6. Cheese: sprinkle 6 oz reduced-fat cheddar.
  7. Bake 12–15 minutes until bubbly. Rest 5 minutes.
  8. Serve over shredded lettuce. Portion per Notes.

Notes

Enchilada casserole comfort without tortillas—still saucy and cheesy, with lettuce crunch. Portions (per person): Frank: 2 cups bake over lettuce Susan: 1 1/2 cups bake over lettuce Elise: 1 1/2 cups bake over lettuce Tips (recipe-specific): Brown peppers before baking so the casserole isn’t watery. Serve over lettuce for a ‘loaded plate’ feel without chips.
