Buffalo-ish Toss Sauce

Category: Sauce • Yield: About 3 tbsp • AnyList-ready

Ingredients

  • 2 tbsp hot sauce
  • 2 tsp extra-virgin olive oil
  • 1 tsp apple cider vinegar

Steps

  1. Whisk hot sauce, olive oil, and apple cider vinegar until emulsified.
  2. Toss wings or tenders lightly. Start with 1 tbsp sauce per serving and add more only if needed.
