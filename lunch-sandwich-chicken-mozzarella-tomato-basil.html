Lunch Sandwich Chicken Mozzarella Tomato Basil

Chicken mozzarella tomato basil sandwiches with spring mix for volume—Caprese-inspired but calibrated for your lunch band.

Servings

3 servings.

Times

  • Prep time: 14 minutes
  • Cook time: 0 minutes
  • Total time: 14 minutes

Ingredients

Main Dish

  • 6 slices whole wheat bread
  • 18 slices deli chicken breast
  • 3 slices mozzarella cheese
  • 1 bunch basil
  • 2 each tomatoes
  • 1 bag spring mix
  • 1 bottle extra virgin olive oil
  • 1 each lemon
  • 1 jar black pepper
    • 

    Preparation Steps

    1. Veg: measure out 6 packed cups spring mix for these sandwiches; refrigerate remaining spring mix.
    2. Slice: slice 1 tomato for these sandwiches; refrigerate remaining tomato.
    3. Basil: tear basil leaves (use about 12 leaves total).
    4. Light drizzle: in a small bowl, mix 2 tsp olive oil with 1 tsp lemon juice and black pepper. Use this drizzle across all sandwiches; store remaining oil.
    5. Build (Frank): 2 slices bread + 8 slices deli chicken + 1 slice mozzarella + basil leaves + ~2 cups spring mix + tomato slices + part of the drizzle.
    6. Build (Susan): 2 slices bread + 5 slices deli chicken + 1 slice mozzarella + basil leaves + ~2 cups spring mix + tomato slices + part of the drizzle.
    7. Build (Elise): 2 slices bread + 5 slices deli chicken + 1 slice mozzarella + basil leaves + ~2 cups spring mix + tomato slices + part of the drizzle.
    8. Close sandwiches and wrap.
      9. 

      Notes

      Portions (per person):


      Frank – 1 whole sandwich: 8 slices chicken, 1 slice mozzarella, basil, ~2 cups spring mix, tomato, light olive oil lemon drizzle.


      Susan – 1 whole sandwich: 5 slices chicken, 1 slice mozzarella, basil, ~2 cups spring mix, tomato, light olive oil lemon drizzle.


      Elise – 1 whole sandwich: 5 slices chicken, 1 slice mozzarella, basil, ~2 cups spring mix, tomato, light olive oil lemon drizzle.


      Tips (recipe-specific):


      Keep the olive oil drizzle light—just enough to carry flavor.


      Basil bruises easily; tear it right before assembling.


      If you need more crunch, add extra cucumber (optional) without changing the protein.
