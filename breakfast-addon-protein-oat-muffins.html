Protein Oat Muffins (Freezer Batch)

Grab-and-go oat muffins engineered as a slow-carb add-on to your shake for rushed mornings.

Servings

12 muffins.

Times

  • Prep time: 10 minutes
  • Cook time: 22 minutes
  • Total time: 32 minutes

Ingredients

Main Dish

  • 1 container old-fashioned oats
  • 1 carton egg whites
  • 1 tub nonfat Greek yogurt
  • 2 each ripe bananas
  • 1 container whey protein isolate
  • 1 container baking powder
  • 1 jar ground cinnamon
    • 

    Preparation Steps

    1. Preheat oven to 350°F. Line a 12-cup muffin tin.
    2. Mash 2 bananas in a bowl. Stir in 1 1/2 cups oats, 1 scoop whey, 2 tsp baking powder, and 2 tsp cinnamon.
    3. Whisk in 1 1/2 cups egg whites and 1/2 cup Greek yogurt until the batter is uniform.
    4. Divide into 12 muffin cups. Bake 18–22 minutes until set. Cool completely.
    5. Freeze muffins on a tray 1 hour, then move to a freezer bag. Freeze up to 1 month.
    6. Breakfast use: grab 1 muffin and eat with your morning shake.

      Notes

      Portions (per person):


      Frank – 1 muffin with your morning shake (slow-carb add-on).


      Susan – Optional: 1 muffin with breakfast if desired.


      Elise – Optional: 1 muffin with breakfast if desired.


      Tips (recipe-specific):


      If lunch will be 2–3 PM, grab 2 muffins instead of 1.


      If you want less sweetness, use 1 banana and add 1/2 cup applesauce in its place.


      Store 2–3 muffins in the fridge as a 3-day backup so you never run out.
