Sheet-Pan Tofu: Asparagus + Mushrooms + Roasted Sweet Potatoes with Worcester Dijon Pan Drizzle

Servings

3 plates (Frank 1.0×, Susan 0.75×, Elise 0.75×).

Times

  • Prep time: 18 minutes
  • Cook time: 27 minutes
  • Total time: 45 minutes

Ingredients

  • 2 blocks firm tofu
  • 2 sweet potatoes
  • 2 bunches asparagus
  • 24 oz mushrooms
  • 9 tsp olive oil
  • 4 tsp minced garlic
  • 2 tsp black pepper
  • 2 tsp salt substitute
  • 2 tsp dried parsley
  • 3 tsp Worcestershire sauce
  • 4 tsp Dijon mustard
  • 2 tsp black pepper

Steps

  1. Base: Peel and cube sweet potatoes into 3/4-inch pieces (~6 cups). Toss with 3 tsp olive oil + pepper + salt substitute. Roast at 425°F for 20–25 minutes, stirring once, until browned and tender.
  2. Oven: Heat to 425°F. Line a large sheet pan with parchment or foil for faster cleanup.
  3. Vegetables (Asparagus + Mushrooms): Toss with 4 tsp olive oil + pepper + salt substitute. Roast at 425°F on a sheet pan. Start tougher veg first (carrots/cabbage) 10 minutes; add quicker veg (broccoli/asparagus/green beans/mushrooms) later so everything stays browned-tender.
  4. Aromatics: Add minced garlic during the last 2 minutes of roasting (or it burns and turns bitter).
  5. Protein: Press tofu 15 minutes, cube. Toss with 3 tsp olive oil + pepper. Roast at 425°F for 22–25 minutes, flipping once, until browned edges.
  6. Finish sauce: Whisk finishing sauce ingredients. Measured umami; do not freestyle-pour unless you enjoy swelling.
  7. Plate: Base first, then vegetables, then protein. Drizzle finishing sauce. Add parsley + black pepper last.
  8. Portions per Notes.

Notes

Portions (per person):

Frank – 1 plate (12 oz roasted tofu, 1 cup roasted sweet potatoes, 2 cups roasted vegetables, 2–3 tbsp finish sauce).

Susan – 1 plate (9 oz roasted tofu, 3/4 of Frank’s base portion, 1 1/2 cups roasted vegetables, 2 tbsp finish sauce).

Elise – 1 plate (9 oz roasted tofu, 3/4 of Frank’s base portion, 1 1/2 cups roasted vegetables, 2 tbsp finish sauce).

Tips (recipe-specific):

Stagger timing: tough veg first, quick veg later. If everything goes in at once, one thing will be mush and one thing will be raw.

Finish sauce goes on after roasting (or last 2 minutes max). That’s how you keep it ‘gourmet’ instead of ‘cafeteria’.

Storage + Reheat

  • Cool promptly; store in airtight containers in the fridge up to 3–4 days (typical safety guidance).
  • Reheat protein + veg at 350°F in the air fryer 4–6 minutes for texture; reheat base in microwave 60–90 seconds; add finishing sauce after reheating.
