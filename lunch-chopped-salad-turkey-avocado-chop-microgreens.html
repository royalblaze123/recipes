Chopped Salad: Turkey, Avocado, and Microgreens Chop Salad

Prep: 16 min   Cook: 0 min   Total: 16 min

Ingredients

  • 12 slices deli turkey
  • 1 avocado
  • 0.5 bag shredded cabbage
  • 1 cucumber
  • 1 pint cherry tomatoes
  • 1 clamshell microgreens
  • 1 lemon
  • olive oil
  • black pepper
  • salt substitute
  • vinegar
  • Dijon mustard

Rules enforced: Clean AnyList Ingredients (quantities before items; fresh meat/poultry/fish use weights; packaged items use fractional containers when partial). Condiments/spices/seasonings have no measurements in Ingredients; all measurements are in Steps. No olives, no squash, no Brussels sprouts, no zucchini, no tofu/tempeh. Lunch validations: Frank calories 500–550, sat fat ≤6g, sodium ≤650mg, fiber ≥8g, added sugar ≤6g.

Step-by-step Instructions

  1. Chop base: combine 1/2 bag shredded cabbage + diced 1 cucumber + halved 1 pint cherry tomatoes + 1 clamshell microgreens.
  2. Turkey: cut 12 slices deli turkey into bite-size ribbons and add to bowl.
  3. Avocado: dice 1 avocado. Add to salad right before eating (or store separately for meal prep).
  4. Dressing: whisk 2 tsp olive oil + 2 tsp vinegar + 2 tsp lemon juice + 2 tsp Dijon + 1/4 tsp black pepper + 1/4 tsp salt substitute.
  5. Toss and portion: Frank largest bowl; Susan/Elise ~75%.
  6. Meal-prep: keep avocado and dressing separate until serving.
