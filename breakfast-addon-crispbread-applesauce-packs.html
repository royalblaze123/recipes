Crispbread + Applesauce Breakfast Packs (No-Cook Bulk)

The simplest slow-carb breakfast add-on: pre-packed crispbread plus no-sugar-added applesauce.

Servings

14 packs.

Times

  • Prep time: 10 minutes
  • Cook time: 0 minutes
  • Total time: 10 minutes

Ingredients

Main Dish

  • 1 box multigrain crispbread
  • 1 box no-sugar-added applesauce pouches
    • 

    Preparation Steps

    1. Build 14 packs: place 3–4 crispbreads plus 1 applesauce pouch into a bag/container.
    2. If you expect a later lunch, pack 4 crispbreads; if lunch will be early, pack 3.
    3. Store packs at home, in the car, and at work so you always have a backup.
    4. Breakfast use: grab 1 pack and eat with your morning shake.
      5. 

      Notes

      Portions (per person):


      Frank – 1 pack with your morning shake (3–4 crispbreads + 1 pouch).


      Susan – Optional: 1 pack with breakfast if desired.


      Elise – Optional: 1 pack with breakfast if desired.


      Tips (recipe-specific):


      If your stomach is sensitive, eat the applesauce first, then the crispbread.


      If lunch is 2–3 PM, default to 4 crispbreads in the pack.


      Keep at least 3 packs at work at all times.

      • Frank: 190 kcal; 4 g protein; 40 g carbs; 1 g fat; 6 g fiber; 120 mg sodium
      • Susan: 190 kcal; 4 g protein; 40 g carbs; 1 g fat; 6 g fiber; 120 mg sodium
      • Elise: 190 kcal; 4 g protein; 40 g carbs; 1 g fat; 6 g fiber; 120 mg sodium
