Crispbread + Applesauce Breakfast Packs (No-Cook Bulk)

The simplest slow-carb breakfast add-on: pre-packed crispbread plus no-sugar-added applesauce.

Servings

14 packs (no-cook bulk).

Times

  • Prep time: 10 minutes
  • Cook time: 0 minutes
  • Total time: 10 minutes

Ingredients

  • 56 each multigrain crispbread
  • 14 each no-sugar-added applesauce pouches

Preparation Steps

  1. Build 14 packs: Place 4 multigrain crispbreads plus 1 no-sugar-added applesauce pouch into each bag/container.
  2. Storage: Keep packs at home, in the car, and at work so you always have a backup.
  3. Breakfast use: Grab 1 pack and eat with your morning shake.

Notes

The simplest slow-carb breakfast add-on: pre-packed crispbread plus no-sugar-added applesauce.

Portions (per person):

Frank – 1 pack with your morning shake (4 crispbreads + 1 pouch).

Susan – Optional: 1 pack with breakfast if desired.

Elise – Optional: 1 pack with breakfast if desired.

Tips (recipe-specific):

If your stomach is sensitive, eat the applesauce first, then the crispbread.

If you expect a later lunch, keep the pack at 4 crispbreads (don’t reduce).

Keep at least 3 packs at work at all times.

Nutrition (per person)

  • Frank: 190 kcal; 4 g protein; 40 g carbs; 1 g fat; 6 g fiber; 120 mg sodium
  • Susan: 190 kcal; 4 g protein; 40 g carbs; 1 g fat; 6 g fiber; 120 mg sodium
  • Elise: 190 kcal; 4 g protein; 40 g carbs; 1 g fat; 6 g fiber; 120 mg sodium
