Garlic-Parmesan Style Chicken Plates (Measured Cheese)

Nutrition (approx)

Frank: 650 kcal • P 58g • C 55g • F 18g • Sat 6g • Na 700mg • Fiber 10g
Susan/Elise (each): 488 kcal • P 44g • C 41g • F 14g • Sat 4g • Na 525mg • Fiber 8g

Ingredients (purchase-clean)

  • 1.75 lb boneless skinless chicken breast
  • 6 oz grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1.50 lb baby potatoes
  • 1 bag frozen broccoli florets
  • kosher salt
  • black pepper
  • garlic powder
  • onion powder
  • smoked paprika
  • Italian seasoning
  • ground cumin
  • chili powder
  • red pepper flakes
  • extra-virgin olive oil
  • low-sodium chicken broth

Steps

  1. Oven: heat to 425°F.
  2. Potatoes: roast halved potatoes with 2 tsp olive oil, black pepper, garlic powder, and modest kosher salt 22–26 minutes.
  3. Chicken: coat chicken with black pepper, garlic powder, Italian seasoning, and 3 oz Parmesan total (measured). Roast on a sheet pan 12–15 minutes to 165°F.
  4. Broccoli: roast broccoli tossed with 2 tsp olive oil and black pepper 12 minutes until browned.
  5. Finish: sprinkle remaining 3 oz Parmesan over plated chicken and broccoli (measured).
  6. Serve per Notes.

Notes

Garlic-parmesan comfort with cheese measured so it stays in range. Portions (per person): Frank: 7 oz chicken + 1 cup potatoes + 1 cup broccoli Susan: 5 oz protein + 1 cup potatoes + 3/4 cup veg Elise: 5 oz protein + 1 cup potatoes + 3/4 cup veg Tips (recipe-specific): Use Parmesan as a finish, not a heavy sauce. Roast broccoli hard so it’s crispy and ‘restaurant’.
